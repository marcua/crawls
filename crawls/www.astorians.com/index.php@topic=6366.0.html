Saskia, anyone who has had the glory that is norte√±o Mexican food (that which you can get in CA, AZ, and to some extent NM, but they have their own fab cuisine), has to do a mental and palate shift when it comes to the Mexican food here.  The food here comes from a different region, that of central Mexico.  I've come to appreciate the corn-based style of cooking here, after six years in the east (i'm from California originally).  I used to get so frustrated that I couldn't get the Mexican food I was used to.  It's almost impossible to find good CA/AZ-style of Mexican food here.

That being said, I hear Kitchen Market makes good burritos. I also like Burrito Box in the city.  Some like Chipotle and Burritoville.  Here in Astoria, tasty Mexican can be had at the El Rey Del Taco Truck on 30th Ave and also the tacos at Los Portales on Broadway are pretty good.  And cheap!  Tacos Mexico also has its fans and it's worth trying.

Good luck!
