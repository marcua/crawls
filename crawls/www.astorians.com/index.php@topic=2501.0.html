Well, you actually can beat the price by going with Lingo or SunRocket. I use SunRocket myself after being a long time Vonage customer. Ends up being about $17/mo, and that includes taxes. Comes with many of the same features. I liked my Voange service, but wanted something less expensive.

SunRocket also comes with e911, it makes it so that your home address shows up on the screen if you call 911. Vonage still doesn't have e911, the 911 operator doesn't know where you are calling from instantly, but has to look it up (though they can still get it, just takes a couple extra minutes).
