Thats a tough one because it just doesnt exist here, YET.

1.  irish rover. 28th ave 38th street.  good local irish pub, good drinks and cheap. pleasant and friendly bartenders.  live music friday nights.  lots of good classic american rock, pop, 80's, etc...  (no food though).  the only bar to just hang at and have a few.  easy relaxed vibe.

2.  Mccanns. ditmars & 37th street. Wings, food, good beer selection. pool tables. 

3.  rapture.  28th ave 34th street?  the only lounge worthy so far.  not a euro lounge, no euro techno music, no smoking.  decent drinks.

4. quays.  30th ave 46th street? just good classic pub.  great guinness on tap.

5.  cronins on bway & 38th/steinway.  excellent food and great guinness on tap. But not a great bar to hang at.
