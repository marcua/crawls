Astoria/LIC Waterfront Bike Tour
« on: September 18, 2007, 02:08:12 PM »
Join us for a leisurely 5 mile ride of the waterfront parks in Queens and learn about the history of this great neighborhood from Richard Melnick of the Greater Astoria Historical Society. Sponsored by Partnerships for Parks.

Saturday, October 6 at 10am - 1pm

Meet at Gantry Plaza State Park (48th Avenue & East River) and end at Ralph de Marco Park.

Please register with Karen at (718) 706-8044 or Karen.Overton@parks.nyc.gov
