Moving Update:

As I mentioned way back before I lived in Astoria (it seems like another lifetime...), I made a reservation with Shea Moving to haul my just-a-bit-less-than-1-bedroom's worth of junk from Jamaica Queens to my new digs...

These guys rock so hard I'm still a bit overwhelmed. This fact is well-known, as has already been noted here by their staunch supporters, but *damn*.  Three men and truck arrived exactly on time, and two of them proceeded to just haul a$$ up and down three flights of stairs while their wing-man in the truck arranged everything to make room for the incoming.  It truly is a remarkable sight to see a seemingly wee skinny man line up five or six boxes full of hardcover art books (IE: The densest sort of book known to man - they should be published with warning labels: May Cause Severe Muscle Strain) that were packed by your Greek shove-as-much-crap-in-as-big-a-box-as-you-can boyfriend (the sweetie), and tie them seemingly oh-so-casually with some straps TO HIS BACK, and just *walk* down three flights of stairs.  After I got over my initial shock, my first thought was to document the phenomenon with my digital camera, but couldn't find it, seeing as how it had been packed somewhere a week earlier.  :roll:

Indeed: I am a Shea Moving convert. And once we saw how quickly and efficiently they were working, the BF just turned to me and said "Thank GOD you hired movers. It not only would have taken me and your brother all day just to load a truck, but my back is twitching just looking at that pile of boxes."  To which I repressed the urge to make a snarky reply about his self-professed "Greek Style" of packing those boxes... but I was in no place to complain: he flew up from Florida to pack me because I was in class from 8 to 4 everyday and doing homework every night. But that's another story.
