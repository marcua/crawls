Make sure you find out if the rent you'll be paying is the "preferential" rate, or the full amount they can charge.  If it's the preferential rent you agree to, which is any amount lower than what they can legally charge, they can jack up the rent to the full amount at the time you renew your lease.  Not a lot of protection for renters in that situation.  So for example, if the full amount they can legally charge is $1500, but they offer a preferential rate of $1100, at the time you renew your lease, they can jack it all the way up to $1500.  So it depends greatly on what the market dictates at any time.  So for those of us who have preferential rates, we need to pray that the market stabilizes come renewal time.
