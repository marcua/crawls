Uh, there actually isn't a sign. There's a plaque, nothing says "FreezePeach" that I'm aware of. The plaque looks like cutout paper dolls. Don't feel bad about not being able to find the place. I heard about it and walked past 3 times before seeing it.

Here are directions, as specific as I can make them:
-FreezePeach is just off the southwest corner of Ditmars Blvd and 29th St.
-It is 1 storefront away from Ditmars Blvd. A nail salon is on the corner.
-On sunny days there's a sandwich sign on the sidewalk with amusing chalked messages about the benefits of drinking tea.
-The cafe looks like a renovated garage. (It may be a renovated garage, for all I know...) It has big glass windows, though.
-If you are on 29th St and get to the alleyway or houses, you just missed it.
-If you are still totally lost, try their phone number: (718) 877-6545. Maybe we can all pile out and wave.
