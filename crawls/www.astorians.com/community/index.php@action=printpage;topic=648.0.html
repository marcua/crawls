Title: CUP - I need to vent
Post by: Dizzy on August 27, 2005, 11:11:01 PM
So tonight my boyfriend and I ended up ordering dinner from CUP. Since we tend to keep strange hours, working late at times, CUP was always a good spot to order from since they delivered later and had a somewhat unique menu from the usual places around us. Basically, CUP was the spot to order from when you just wanted to pig out on really bad for you food and watch some movies for the night.

We haven't ordered from CUP for a few weeks now, so tonight when I called in our order I was informed that their menu had changed and some of the items we might want could have been taken off. Some items? Everything we liked was gone. No crab cake wrap, no chicken salad sandwiches with apples and raisins, no mac & cheese. We finally found something they did still offer (Havana corn on the cob & turkey burgers), and they sent over a new menu with the delivery.

Now, I am going to assume that the same people that own CUP also own Lite Bites (on Steinway & B'Way) because the menu, except for maybe 2 or 3 items, is completely identical.  I understand that restaurants will change their menus to better serve their customers..get rid of dishes not selling, update a bit, etc. But why, why, why, would a somewhat successful establishment just copy and serve the exact same thing that a smaller cafe (known more for take out and only 2 avenues away) has been serving for a few years now? And the kicker?  Lite Bites' prices are lower than CUP, anywhere from $.25 to a whole dollar lower!

Ugh, it's so annoying. I just want more selection for take out around this area and now having yet another glorified sandwich shop /cafe here is just depressing. I just had to vent....


Oh and even though I've only just gotten around to registering here, I've been lurking quite awhile now. I've learned more about Astoria in the past few months than I have in the 6 years I've been living here! Thanks everyone!
