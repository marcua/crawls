Title: Re: Semi-Basement Living Conditions
Post by: purplecurlygirl on May 26, 2009, 12:02:48 PM
From what I understand, these apartments are illegal because they can be dangerous to live in. It might not be up to code. There could be problems with flooding, mold, carbon monoxide poisoning, not enough exits in case of emergencies/fires, etc. You wouldn't get into trouble for living there, but the landlord would get into trouble for renting it out, and you'd probably be required to move. You might have difficulties with rental insurance or other services (if the apartment isn't legal, it might not be registered at the address and therefore "doesn't exist" in terms of being insurable).

Good luck on your new place!  Maybe I'm chiming in a little too late, but I think that is an AMAZING price for that apartment when you consider the parking spot.  The going rate to rent a parking spot around here appears to be $150 month.  I'm not sure which utilties are are talking about, but if it's electricity/heat/gas, that would be another 100-200 per month.  If cable/internet is included, that's another $100+.
