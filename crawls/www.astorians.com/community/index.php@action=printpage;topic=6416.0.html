Title: Brazilian Music, this Friday @ Waltz-Astoria!!!
Post by: anthonylanni on May 20, 2007, 09:07:36 PM
Hello Everybody,

Anthony Lanni and Dan Kendall will be performing Brazilian music as a duo this Friday, May 25th at Waltz-Astoria. The styles will include Bossa Nova, Samba, Baiao, and Brazilian Pop, while featuring music by composers, such as Antonio Carlos Jobim, Caetano Veloso, Jorge Ben Jor, Luiz Gonzaga Jr., and many more! The performance will mix instrumental tunes, played on two guitars, and vocal tunes sung by Dan Kendall in both Portuguese and Spanish.  Also, we will have two special guests sitting in during the performance: Mike Sapirstein on sax and Dave Martinez on percussion. 

For more information, go to: http://www.anthonylanni.com
