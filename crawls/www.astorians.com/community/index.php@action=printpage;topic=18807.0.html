Open Mic
Saturday, July 10th
6pm to 8pm
@ Seaburn Bookstore
33-18 Broadway Astoria, NY
Phone: 718-267-7929

Come sample of bit of Brazilian Culture with homemade Brigadeiro chocolates. If you'd like, sign up for the Open Mic.  And, yes, there will be cake as we celebrate poet, songwriter Diane Ott’s birthday and the upcoming publication of her book “International Love Transplant” a book in Portuguese and English.
