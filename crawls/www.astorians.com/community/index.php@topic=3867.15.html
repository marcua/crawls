Whole Foods is a shopping Extravaganza, for sure - but their prices are outrageous on most things.  Sai Organics has great prices and I have to say a pretty good variety considering the size of their store - which, since I've been shopping there in the last 2.5 years has grown to bursting (hence the need for a 2nd location I assume). 

One of the things I like best is that the owner, Sayeed (sp?), stocks his store based on suggestions of his patrons.  I do almost all of my shopping there now - very rarely will I venture to Key Food or that other health food store over on Broadway.

Occasionally, I'll drag myself to Whole Foods at Columbus Circle because the N/R takes you to 57th which isn't too far, for the items I just can't get at Sai (greater variety of organic produce, and meat/fish).

Since I haven't seen so much as chicken broth at Sai - are they vegetarian?
