I grew up in Queensview and my parents and two of my sisters still live there.

The apartments are really amazingly sized for the price.

The area to the south isn't the best because you border Ravenswood Houses, but in 24 years of living there before moving out, I can't say I ever had any problems at all.

Maintenance charges have ballooned recently, as others have stated.

I wish that my wife and I didn't have two dogs, because I would totally look into getting a place there now.  For the price you can't really go wrong and I am used to the neighborhood.

My only major complaint would be that the cooperators can be stubborn about things that make no sense.  Recently there were plans to install new laundry machines that used magnetic cards rather than quarters.  For some reason people were against this and new machines were installed that use quarters.  This would be fine if there were change machines in the laundry rooms, but there aren't, so you have to save all your quarters or go to the bank for change whenever you need to do laundry.  I think that many of the older cooperators are just resistant to change and want to keep things going the same old way.
