Title: Community Supper at Trinity Lutheran (31st Avenue at 37th Street)
Post by: mcdirk on November 02, 2013, 06:56:37 AM
Community Potluck Supper
November 8 (The Second Friday of every month), 6:30p.m. to 8 p.m.

Trinity Lutheran Church, 31-18 37th Street at 31st Avenue, Astoria, NY

Bring a dish and a can of food for our pantry.
No food to bring? Don’t worry: Just bring yourself.
Make new friends of all ages — Meet your neighbors — Network a bit.
Enjoy your Friday night quality time with folks from Trinity and the neighborhood.
