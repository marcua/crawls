I am currently living in Astoria near 30th Ave & Crescent. I love that area and would love to either stay in the 30th Ave/Astoria Boulevard area. I am open to almost anywhere in Astoria, though.

I have a reliable job and I've never been late with bills or anything like that. I'm not a neat freak, but I do the dishes and I don't mind cleaning a bathroom. I'm a bit cluttered, but I like to have common areas clean.

I am pretty social, but am still very new to New York. I'm a pseudo-writer (I try to do it often, and I enjoy it, but I'm no JD Salinger) and it would be really great to live with someone that does "art things" and that would be up for going out with me every now and then or inviting me to tag along when they've got cool plans. My favorite housemates have been people who were filmmakers, painters, musicians--mostly, I really like people who have something that they do that they really care about.

I'm probably going to post on craigslist, but I was hoping for an Astorian (yay!) and I would like to meet people and find a living situation that would be good for all involved. Ideally, I would want to find a place in the beginning of July, but I could wait until later or could go earlier. If I can find a good place, person and situation, I can make pretty much any date this summer work out.

So, if you're interested, or if you have a friend who might be interested, or you hear someone read a really awesome poem at a sweet open mic and finish by saying that they really need a hip roommate, tell me and tell them:
