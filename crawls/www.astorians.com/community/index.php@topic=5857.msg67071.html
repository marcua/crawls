The Internet made me into a hypochondiac!
« on: April 05, 2007, 10:52:08 AM »

Not to make light of people who have this condition, but I swear to god the Internet has made me a hypochondriac because of Web MD and other health related websites!  Recently, I've been experiencing horrible eye aches and neck pains.  Both could easily be explained by the way that I sleep (very restless and with crappy pillows that need to be replaced) and the fact that I spend the majority of my day in front of a crappy monitor, staring at the screen.  But OH NO! Your boy, Astoria Luv, has determined that according to Web MD, I probably have a brain aneurysm!  Now I can't work because I feel like I should drag my uninsured butt to the nearest emergency room and have this checked out before my aneurysm ruptures.

I just needed to vent, but I am wondering if I'm the only one!  I called my friend, who is a doctor in Minnesota and he just hung up on me because I relied on the Internet to diagnose myself!
