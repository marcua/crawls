Mussels ‘N Sausages
« on: October 08, 2015, 11:43:25 AM »
Per Astoria Post, the owner of Francis Cafe will relaunch the restaurant in three weeks, under the new name Mussels 'N Sausages.  The food will be inspired by the Alsace region of France (near Germany).   Sauces to accompany mussels will come from French as well as Thai and other cuisines; German, French and Spanish sausages will be available.  So with 2 Lizards, the corner of Ditmars and 35th is going to be hoppin.
