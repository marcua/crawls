QUEENS LOGIC

I also saw the MUNICH last night and the last scene was shot by the new buildings in LIC...I had to chuckle at the very '00's style of the fence at the river, BUT note the added silhouette of the WTC (for the period and to the point of the scene.).

And yes, even though there's no scenery, you have to count that most of the early Marx Brothers movies were filmed at Kaufman Astoria Studios while they were performing on Broadway at night. It was there that one director got so tired of their shenanigans that he had them caged.

As for tv, 36th street is (literally) SESAME STREET.

NOTE: if any of you are near to the area, don't miss the museum at the studio...Great!
