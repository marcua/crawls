I work in the insurance industry (lovely world of No-Fault) and I see some good chiropractors and some that are just out to enrich themselves at all costs. As a general rule I would go to someone who has their own practice as opposed to one that is in a facility that also employs other specialists (Acupuncture, Physical therapists) as they tend to be less reputable.

I would specifically stay away from Sports Medicine & Orthopedic Rehab (38-25 Astoria Boulevard)...their reputation isn't exactly stellar.
