Yes. Which is why you see so many young people drinking beer through a straw in a paper bag on the street. If you carry an open container of any alcoholic beverage on the street, or any open container of anything on public transportation, you are subject to a fine. Open simple means not sealed, or it is considered unopened. If you can drink from the contents of the container, it is opened.
