Title: Re: Renting a car in LA
Post by: lanseaux on January 23, 2008, 11:35:23 AM
I've rented cars from LAX several times for several hours when we've had something like an 8-hour layover.  I've always returned them to the airport, though.

My experiences were with Fox rental and with National, I think, or possibly Enterprise.  The first I rented through the Fox rental website, and I can't remember exactly how much it was, but it was probably around $30.  We drove it to Santa Monica and back.  The second time I scored an incredible deal through Orbitz -- a six hour rental cost me all of $12.  We drove it to visit friends who couldn't drive because it was Shabbos, and back.  The second time, we drove for a short enough distance that we were able to return the car without refueling or getting charged for a less-than-full gas tank, but I think we put a couple of gallons in the Fox rental.

Both rentals were off-site, so that took some time, but it wasn't terrible.
