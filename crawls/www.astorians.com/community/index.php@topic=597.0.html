Timarimba, are you also from Ithaca?

Yes, native Ithacan speaking here, if you don't mind the drive or the bus ride Ithaca is perfect for hiking. There are many trails there along water falls, streams, gorges and such. I love walking along them and getting my nature shots. My personal favorite was walking in the bogs in little McLean where you walk on the vegitation that grows on water. You feel like your walking on water, it is a little scary like you are going to fall through but it is a fun experience overall. Then for a cool off you can go swimming in Buttermilk falls or one of the other swimming areas. Ithaca is perfect for that. Make sure you take a light jacket or sweater however, while the days are almost as warm as here the nights due tend to get chilly up there. I was up there visiting my family a few weeks ago and one night the temp got down to 58 degrees. So nice compared to our nights that don't really cool down here.

Ithaca also has a lot of nice little shopping places including Greenstar, an organic health food store across from the greyhound bus terminal down there.
