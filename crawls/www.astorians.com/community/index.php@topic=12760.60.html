It's a sad situation to admit that in any neighborhood, in any town, and in any part of New York, (Ican't speak for other parts of the country) you have to have a healthy bit of paranoia in order to be safe.

I don't like to think the worst of anyone, but in order to stay safe, you have to look at anyone as being a potential threat....especially if the prevailing conditions i.e. time of day...weather conditions....isolated situation, etc. present a threat to your safety.

It's best to be aware of your surroundings and always have an escape or self defense plan prepared...it's just the way it is.  I've had many close calls....in what would be expected to be safe areas and times of day.  I have never been anyone who supports violence...i hate to think of inflicting pain on another human being..or an animal...but in this day and age, what must be...must be.  If you have to protect yourself or a loved one from harm...anything goes.

Male or female...your life is in danger...learn to protect yourself at all costs. No one has the right to inflict pain, injury or death on another living being. It's strange and horrible times we live in, but the animal world, of which we are a part, learn to adapt to their surroundings. 

Just never intentionally put yourselves in dangerous situations....use good sense....and if you find yourself in peril....react as if your life depends on it...because it probably does.
