A girl was just attacked - 29th b/w Bway and 31st Ave.
« on: December 16, 2007, 09:47:59 PM »
A young woman was attacked tonight (Sunday) on my street at about 9:00PM.  She was only a few doors down from her own building.  As near as I could make out (she was sobbing terribly and bleeding badly from her nose), a group of guys tried to take her purse, but she wouldn't let it go, so they punched (?) her in the face and, probably, broke her nose.  She screamed and screamed at the top of her lungs, "HELP!  HELP! I NEED HELP!"  That's when we got to her.  By that time, the guys took off running SOUTH on Broadway, but got away well before the cops showed up. 

I am seriouly disturbed and PISSED OFF that this is happening in our neighborhood.  That young man was shot at the Broadway stop, two people were mugged and attacked on Broadway in October. . . now this.  What is going on in this world?!?!

I live on 33rd & Broadway & I walk to the HealthFood Store and C-Town all the time at night. I never felt unsafe because there are so many people, lights, and cops there all the time.  A cop car usually sits right outside the bakery on Broadway & 31st.

There are a lot of dark and unlit areas about three blocks down broadway either direction, and that is where they are running towards.  I have seen groups running (away?) several times over this past year.

I always keep my eyes and ears open.
