Title: Re: employee benefits
Post by: aquaecny on August 17, 2007, 04:08:46 PM
As lanseaux said, it depends on the Agency.  I am a city employee working for the Dept of Education.  We have plenty of options, but I went with the basic default, GHI, for medical.  I think GHI has a good medical benefit program for city employees.  I do not know about dental and vision as the DOE uses the UFT (United Federation of Teachers) Welfare Fund for them.  I guess the Dental and Vision are okay, however, many providers have chosen NOT to join the UFT Network because they do not pay much to the provider.
