I think the walkie talkies and text messaging are big with teenagers.  Other people use them too, but they're definitely "cool" among teenagers.

I took text messaging off my phone.  I was getting spam for fat pills, naked pics, etc - on a brand new phone!!  I figure people can talk to me, I like to hear people's voices anyway.  For the time they spend trying to type in their message, they could have called and told me already.  I understand when you are somewhere where you should be quiet, but to tell me you're late or to ask me for coffee - hey, just call.  I get charged for these, so maybe I'm just a cheapskate for wanting phonecalls instead.  But I guess I'm old-fashioned with wanting to hear people's voices.
