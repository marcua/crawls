You guys realize what rent prices in the neighborhood are like, right? You know that the Fast Break went out of business because of a huge rent increase. Or you can shop at chain stores that eat the cost of the high rents around here in order to have market saturation and don't have to worry about cost of goods because they buy in huge quantities.
