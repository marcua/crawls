Keep in mind that a lot of the violations aren't necessarily gross or scary.

My best friend from high school is an inspector, and I once tagged along for one of her visits. Stuff like shelves in the walk-in freezers: The bottom one has to be at least six inches or so off the ground, and you can't store cases of stuff unless they're on a shelf. If there's more than three flies flying around, that's points off (she once counted in her own kitchen, "one.....two......three there I just failed my own inspection!"  :-D ) Our own kitchens probably wouldn't stand up to the scrutiny.

She did say that people probably get food poisoning more often than they realize. Times when you don't feel quite right, or attribute it to something else. Hell, last weekend I was sick after a lunch for a christening. Ruined me for the rest of my day. Nobody else that I spoke with got sick, but then they didn't order the salmon!  :roll:
