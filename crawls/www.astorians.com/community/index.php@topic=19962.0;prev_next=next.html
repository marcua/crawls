Missing UPS Package
« on: December 19, 2010, 01:05:36 PM »
I ordered a cheap electric razor online. It came with a fogless mirror and i could not resist. I checked on the status of its delivery yesterday morning, and UPS claims to have delivered the package to the front door of my building at 3:30 pm on Friday. When I got home from work on Friday around 8, there was no package and no UPS sticky note stating that they attempted to deliver me a package. I'm working with the vendor to get a replacement, but am more perturbed by the thought of someone taking my package. I live on a busy block near 30th Ave and 34th street, and I am surprised that UPS would leave a package on the steps of a 5 story walk up.

Yesterday I posted a hand written note in my lobby along the lines of, "Dear neighbors, UPS claims that they delivered me a package to the front door of our building, but it was not here when I got home. If you picked the package up by mistake or saw anyone else do so, please let me know."

Would you find this offensive?

I went out last night about 8 and when I got home around 2 the note was gone. I posted another note along the same lines.

Now I'm going to check if that one is still up.

Meanwhile,

Dear neighbors,

You know my story. Have you seen my package? If you have, please let me know. And you happen to see anyone with a crappy shave, let me know too, because they may be using my crappy electric razor.

Yours,
AAPA
