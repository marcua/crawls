I've been looking for the very same thing.  You can find listings of community theaters that hold auditions at http://www.communitytheaterny.com/ but so far I've found that the places that are auditioning for traditional musicals are a little far away.  I'd definitely be interested in participating in something if you want to put a musical together around these parts. 

Also, Astoria Performing Arts Center does do musicals - they just staged Ragtime a month ago - but (and please correct me if I'm wrong, anyone) I feel that they are geared to more professional performers. I'll still audition next time they have a musical - I think they do one or two per year.

Let me know if you find anything else!
