I was thinking of running a workshop pretty much for anybody who wanted to attend, on certain areas. It would be like a meetup with a few people leading the discussion on their specialty.

It would go something like this.
Tuesday night. Topic: using your DSLR. 
Two weeks from that, Topic: Creative Composition, exercises in creativity.
Two weeks from that, Topic: Portraiture (my topic)
Two weeks from that, Topic: Shooting products for websites (this is huge in Astoria)

This would go on for a few months, peppered with the monthly photo contest. Winners from each week will have their picture grace local establishments.

Places I was thinking of talking too were Cafe Bar, Freeze Peach, the Waltz, and other local hangouts.
