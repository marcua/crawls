Title: How Unlucky Can one Get???
Post by: elkelorraine1 on July 10, 2008, 01:33:23 AM
I know this may be a silly thread, but I thought it might be interesting to share any unlucky occurrence of the day. I'll start mine now.

I parked my car this morning on 28th street. I then walked toward the Ditmars station on this sunny summer morning, minding my own business, chatting up with my husband when the wierdest thing happened. A sudden boom filled the air, everyone looks around since it sounded like a shot. In that instant I fall to the floor after feeling the sharpest pain on the outside of my right calf.

Well here's the thing. Apparently, a van began to pull out of it's parking spot and it ran over a plastic water bottle. The weight of the van caused so much pressure that the bottle broke and the top portion with the cap in tack shot out with such force and shot right into my leg. When I pulled my hand away from the painful area, there appeared a perfectly round purple burning welt on my leg. And right next to me was lying the evidence itself. (Oh, of all days I wore a skirt).

I mean, what are the chances that this bottle would break, the cap pointing in my direction, move with such lightning speed and still hit a target onto my leg while I was actually moving. It burned all the way to work. Thankfully, all that's left is a red spot on my leg which is sure to disappear in a couple of days.

My husbands words to me? "Wow, now that's really bad luck". I mean, really, you think?
