Currently, that is not a problem, as we have been on the same page regarding what we have hated at the weddings we've been to together. 

As for some of the things I detest, here are some things:
1. Songs that have a dance already / any song that is line dancable.  That means no chicken dance, no electric slide, no Soulja Boy.
2. The Dollar Dance.  I don't understand this tradition and I think it's tacky.
3. That clanging on your glass thing to make the couple kiss.
4. Too many speeches. 
5. "Games." At my friend's wedding there was a point where the bride had to grope the behinds of a number of dudes and figure out which one was my friend.  The horror.
6. Bridal Registry.  I simply don't like the idea of this. 

On top of that, the location (which I already have an idea about), food and music will all be very closely monitored, and aside from the food, which I plan to have catered, everything else will be tended to by me.
