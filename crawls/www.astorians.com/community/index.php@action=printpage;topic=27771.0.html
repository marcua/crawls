Title: ASTORIA IS ORGANIZING - Missing Cats!
Post by: mmissinglink on March 23, 2014, 04:35:08 PM
 ATTENTION!    ASTORIA IS ORGANIZING!   COMMUNITY EVENT!
The seriousness of Astoria's missing cats has grown since the tragic news reports in January. As citizens who care about the community, Astorian's are urgently invited to join for this breaking event.


This Thursday, March/27 from 6:30-9:00 PM there is a community event in Astoria focusing on the now 60+ missing cats. Location of the event: Enigma Books, 33-17 Crescent St (off B'way).
Speakers from the District Attorney's office scheduled at this event.


MAKE YOUR VOICE HEARD. Join the rest of us.


As a gesture of appreciation to the local small business facilitating the evening's event and to help contribute to a collection for the cats, please bring a can of cat food and a book (in good condition) as a donation. Thank you.
