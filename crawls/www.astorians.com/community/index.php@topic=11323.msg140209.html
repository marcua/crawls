I am still on record with being fine with the nods. Yes, I use them because sometimes I'm simply lazy and don't want to just post "I agree".  Often I contribute to threads if I have something thoughtful and well-thought out.  I stay away from the more controversial threads (read: politics) because it's not my bag (and I'm not a great debater), so I don't really know what goes on in there with regard to nods.

I can't agree with NYCMacUser in her colorful expression of her distaste for the nods.  I disagree with the assertion that this place is devoid of fun and that it's not informative.  I find a lot of helpful and informative things here, and I think there are a lot of good natured, fun people here!  To paint this place with such a broad stroke of negativity does an injustice to those that do spend time here contributing to the community, and enjoying each other. 

I also can't relate to the idea that nods might rigidly control what one says or does.  I've said this before - why give them so much power?  I know others disagree (by writing it out or by nodding), and that's ok. 

And meeting at a cafe or restaurant to discuss serious issues.  Why not??  It's fun and speaking for myself, I much prefer to discuss serious issues face to face than on a messageboard.  Plus there's food and drink involved... mmm....
