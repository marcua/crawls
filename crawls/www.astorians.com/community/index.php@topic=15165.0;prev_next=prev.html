I've had a few custom pieces done by Mimi.  To make pieces 'custom' and because I was interested in buying more than 1 piece, she did what she called a fully body pattern for me.  that cost $100 and is on file w/ her forever.  she takes tons of measurements, writes them all down and then asks what you want.  you then look at her rack & say I like this style dress and these pants and she pulls out fabric swatches or you can just go with the fabrics the samples on the rack are in.  Little things like pocket style, belt loops (or no loops) length of sleeves, etc can also be 'customized'.  cost for each piece is pre-set based on the piece & fabric & is on the tag of the item, so no suprises which is great.  Can't remember exactly, but I think pants were like $80 and dresses were something like $130 each.  She takes about 2 weeks to create the items & then you go in for a 'fitting' to be sure all is just right for you & she tweaks whatever is necessary.  come back in a few days and you walk out with a perfect fit & totally custom items.  It was a great experience, she made you feel like a celebrity and I got my 3 pieces soon after she opened & they're each in great shape years later.  It may be a splurge for some, but she made you feel really good about splurging.
