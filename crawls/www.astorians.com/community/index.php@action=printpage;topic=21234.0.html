Title: 1 year transit memorial: Monday June 27
Post by: costa01 on June 23, 2011, 11:28:16 PM
Last June, the MTA instituted draconians cuts that shelved the W and V trains as well as our express bus to Manhattan the QM22 and a fare increase.  1 year later we are gathering to remind the MTA we remember what they have done and that we deserve better!!
 
Join:
NYS Senator Michael Gianaris
NYS Assemblymember Aravella Simotas
NYC Councilmember Peter Vallone Jr.
Democratic District Leader Costa Constantinides
Long Island City Alliance
Transportation Alternatives-Rider Rebellion
& Other concerned Astorians and transit riders
 
Monday June 27
6:15pm
Ditmars Blvd Train Stop N/Q train
 
To date, we have collected almost 1000 signatures in support of the MTA restoring service to the QM22 express bus and expanding its service into downtown Manhattan.  Astoria is a growing, thriving community that needs more options into Manhattan not less.  We deserve to be treated with the same respect that communities in Brooklyn have been shown having their service restored.  We deserve better long term transit! Come stand with us to fight for better options for our community!!
