I agree thumbs up on Golden Kitchen (Astoria Blvd stop) but you have to order carefully. Their noodle soup and wonton soup I like because the broth seems to be homemade. Too salty tho. The wontons in it are great.

I also really like their plain rice, which may sound strange, until you compare how their jasmine rice tastes compared to most joints'. It makes a huge difference.

The fried rice is good in all its incarnations and the shrimp egg roll is the best in Astoria, easily.

Some of the other stuff is just average. I haven't had anything that was awful--just the usual goop and grease that usually signifies fast-food chinese.

The people at the counter are nice and so are the delivery persons.

There used to be a coupla places in Astoria that had a special "chinese" menu on its menu--as crazy as it sounds. It meant dim sum and other stuff (like jellyfish, beef tendon, etc) that wouldn't fly for most non-Asians. I mourn the passing of those places, and if anybody knows of a restaurant that is still doing that, please post. It's been a long time since I've had drunk chicken delivered to my door.
