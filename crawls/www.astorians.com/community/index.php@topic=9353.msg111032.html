Most museums have free days or nights - check websites - Also some museums you can donate minimumn like a dollar and go - Museum of NH is one.

Bronx Zoo is free on Wed, I believe - double check that day. Libraries have books and movies to rent for free.

The Staten Island Ferry is free - check out the Statute of Liberty w/o waiting on line.

Parks are free - jog on the track - do the workout stations - ride a bike - roller blade - in summer the pool is open and free.

Sat Afternoon 12N to 5 - free tour of Brooklyn Brewery - free beer. :lol:

Many things to do - just a few off the top of my head - I bet people here have more - and if you did a search on it - you would find plenty
