DETAILS:

Il Bambino
34-08 31st Ave
Friday, February 16
8pm
$20 prix fixe before tax/tip
BYOB - the Happy Liquor Store is a couple doors down. Wine tasting starts at 6:30, so if you like you can go on over, have a drinky tasty and buy a bottle.  The selection is decent, and I was happy to see South African wines there, as well as some interesting Chilean, Austrailian and NZ wines.  They also have some ok CA wines.  The restaurant has italian sodas, water, coffee, tea, and the like.

This is very exciting!  Those of you who have not been are in for a real treat.  The food is so delcious!

I'll bring my camera, and I expect I'll see the regular papparazi there, too.  ;)
