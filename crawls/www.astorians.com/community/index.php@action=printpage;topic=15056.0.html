Title: Exit 44 Music Festival w/ Tigercity and Project Jenny, Project Jan @ Wonderland
Post by: naohuth on May 23, 2009, 03:55:40 PM
June 13th 3:00pm-2:00am
Exit 44 Music Festival w/ Tigercity and Project Jenny, Project Jan @ Wonderland, 38-01 23rd Ave, Astoria, Queens
$10/$13; Exit 44 is an all-day independent music festival featuring some of the best emerging acts in the city. Headlined by Tigercity and Project Jenny, Project Jan, the lineup also includes Action Painters, The Shondes, My Teenage Stride, Schwervon!, and many more. DJ sets by Caps (Caps & Jones). Full lineup and details at http://www.myspace.com/exit44festival. Advance tickets at http://www.brownpapertickets.com/event/65889.
Check out the event on Why Leave Astoria: http://www.whyleaveastoria.com/events/exit-44-festival
