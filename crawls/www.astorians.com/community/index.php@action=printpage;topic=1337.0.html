My favorite way to cook spinach:

Slice some garlic, and sautee it in olive oil. When it's very lightly browned, add the spinach and cover so it wilts. Season with some salt, pepper and nutmeg. (Nutmeg compliments dark green veggies very well)

Yummmm :)
