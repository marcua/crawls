What I used to do with excess change:  Stop by the grocery store when you need to pick up a few items and there isn't a line at the self check-outs.  Use up your change whenever you can.  I don't think I ever put in more than $10 in change, but when you just have tons and tons of pennies and small coins, it's a good way to get rid of it.  Plus, you're not spending your "real" money on groceries, so it doesn't feel like you're spending.  And you're not paying fees at the machines.
