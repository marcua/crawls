We tried it the other night for delivery. Our order was incorrect, and despite being told they'd credit us, we never were, even though it rendered the meal inedible for one of us (bacon on a vegetarian's meal). Mistakes happen, but it's poor customer service to say it'll take another hour, on top of the one we already waited, to have the correct food delivered. Even worse, though, was the burger - it just wasn't good. There's nothing I love more than a good, juicy burger, but this wasn't it. It was oddly dry, though not overcooked. It was flavorless, unseasoned and left a bad taste. I ate 1/4 of it and the best part was the bun. For a bad, tasteless burger, I thought it was overpriced. I liked 5 Napkin, and will miss them, though their quality dropped over the last year. I like Burger Club - probably my new favorite, but my wife isn't a fan (no good vegetarian option that she likes). Not a huge fan of Bare Burger, but I do like their black bean burger.

So who has the best beef burger in the neighborhood?
