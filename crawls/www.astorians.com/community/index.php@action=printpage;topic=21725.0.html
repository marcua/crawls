Title: Learn how to make natural dyes at the Socrates Sculpture Park Greenmarket!
Post by: Markella on September 29, 2011, 09:35:35 AM
Laura Sansone is bringing her mobile Textile Lab to the market this week and will be demonstrating how to create dyes from local vegetables and flowers as well as spinning, knitting and crocheting with fibers from local sheep. The Textile Lab creates a link between textiles and food, and uses this connection as a way to explore sustainable systems of community based production. 9am-2pm at the market info tent.

October is full of fun activities at the market, stay tuned!

 Socrates Sculpture Park Greenmarket
Saturdays 8-4
Vernon Blvd and Broadway
www.grownyc.org/socratesgreenmarket (http://www.grownyc.org/socratesgreenmarket)
