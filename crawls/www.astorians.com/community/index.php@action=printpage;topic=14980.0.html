Title: Dutch Kills Bar
Post by: RoverNYC on May 15, 2009, 01:11:56 PM
The other night I tried a brand new bar called Dutch Kills Bar, down on Jackson Ave near Court Square. It's an absolutely beautiful bar with classic cocktails and is definitely worth the trip if you're a fan of the cocktail.
I had the Astoria Martini (Plymouth Gin, dry Vermouth, Orange Bitters) and the Pisco Sour (Pisco, lemon/lime, bitters, frothy egg white) and both were amazing. I think they were $12 each - pricey but worth it.

It's a strange location, but it would be cool if they made it. The owners used to run Milk & Honey on the LES, if that tells you anything. The back room has 2 pianos and I believe they have live music on weekends.

here is their website: http://www.dutchkillsbar.com/
