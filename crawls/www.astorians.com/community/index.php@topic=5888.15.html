We drop off our laundry and pick it up ourselves and tip each time we pick it up. I never used to at other laundromats but then again this laundromat is a vast improvement over ones we have used in the past. I usually just tip around $2 or $3 nothing big- just a little something to say thanks. The woman are very appreciative and I think they make an extra effort for myself and my husband if we have any specials requests (i.e. please don't put my jeans in the dryer etc.)

That being said- I don't think its imperative that you tip- I think its more of a choice. Since being able to drop off my laundry and not have to worry about doing it myself makes my life much easier I don't mind tipping a few bucks. Anything that keeps me from having to do laundry makes me very very happy.  :-D

On a separate note for those who do drop off your laundry- how much do you pay per pound? We are currently paying 65 a pound. I was wondering if that was pretty standard?
