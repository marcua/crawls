I made a salad last night with this week's greens (I can't remember the name, but it was the greens option with small yellow flowers).  I mixed it with the red lettuce, about 1 part greens - 4 parts red lettuce.  I sliced up a few radishes, a store-bought cucumber, & some sliced almonds.  Served with a ranch dressing.

I also cooked the Jerusalem artichokes with a lemon-pepper chicken recipe.  And I used the peppercress to liven up a minty rice pilaf.
