Re: 5 boroughs Ice Cream Tasting
« Reply #9 on: January 07, 2007, 04:28:31 PM »
Hey everyone! Sorry I haven't responded to your questions before now, but I just got an email prompting me to do so. My bad.

We are currently in 18 locations, 5 are in Queens. Check out the website for all of them- www.5bicecream.com

The closest one to Ditmars is Best Yet Market. If you'd like to see it closer to where you shop, please print out the info sheet pdf on the site and hand it to the manager of the store you want it in. It has all the info they need to either place and order with our distributor, or to contact us for samples. And, no, Victory and Rose & Joe's do not sell our stuff there-we'd love them to, though. I think it has alot to do with limited space. It's funny becasue when we first started years ago- they were like, "you're gonna do what with our stuff?" But now we've all gotten to know each other better.

And, yeah, several people want us to bring back the Holy F'n Cannoli flavor-including us!! We want to, but this will take some time and research on our part. Unfortunately, the costs to make this flavor right now are more than we can do. We are planning on at least 3 new flavors this summer, though and I'm very excited about them. They are of course TOP SECRET, but I can tell you one will be our first fruit flavor, and one will have a portion of it's procedes go to a flavor specific charity.

I don't know when I can get to doing another demo at Bravo. Kim and I are trying hard to get to each store at least once before the spring time.

Thank you guys for all the interest, and positive feedback we have been getting! It's such a cool thing to have the support of our neighbors!
