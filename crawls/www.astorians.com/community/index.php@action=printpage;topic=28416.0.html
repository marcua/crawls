ABOUT

Susan Lee has worked with artists and freelancers in New York City for over twenty years. As a Certified Financial Planner, Susan has presented on tax and financial issues facing artists at organizations such as Volunteer Lawyers for the Arts, Columbia University, The Center for Book Arts, Bronx Museum, and the New York Foundation for the Arts among others. For many years, she hosted and produced a weekly holistic financial radio show, You and Your Money, on WBAI-FM in NYC. Susan has a website dedicated to tax and financial issues for artists and freelancers at www.freelancetaxation.com (http://www.freelancetaxation.com/)
