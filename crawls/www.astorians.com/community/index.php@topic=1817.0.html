Try Nina at Casablanca on 30th Avenue. I have used her for 4 years for highlights (blonde on my dark hair) and for blow-outs. She is amazing. Two of my friends in NYC come out for color by her and for cuts. I get blow-outs weekly during the season, and get lots of compliments about her work. Having tried a lot of different types of stylists, I've found the really experienced, often older stylists are best. They also have the most experience with color. So explore options like that and don't necessarily go for the glitzy interiors, etc.
