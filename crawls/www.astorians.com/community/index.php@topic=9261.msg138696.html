I have used FatWallet.com to find some great deals. Another tip is never buy something online (travel, Amazon, Target, etc.) without a coupon code which is entered at check out. Usually you can Google "Amazon Coupon" or "Target promotion" or something like that and you will get a ton of links to promotions. I recently used a coupon code to book a hotel down in New Orleans. With the coupon and my AAA discount, I knocked off about 20%. A little homework can save you a bundle.
