Sandwich Bar
« on: June 01, 2013, 11:30:47 PM »
Open for business.


I got a pressed sandwich and it was delicious. The carved roasted turkey one. I also tried some of one of the cold sandwiches and I thought the bread was WAY too hard. Sticking with the pressed sandwiches for the foreseeable future.


The beer menu is decent, but doesn't even begin to touch what they have over @ MP or down the road @ C&V.


The new layout is nice and the outdoor seating area is obviously a huge improvement from when it was Just Burgers. Speaking of the burgers, my friend tried the burger and said it wasn't as good as it was when they were Just Burgers, which I thought was odd, because has the ownership changed??


I like that it seems like this place will be a good quick takeout option, which is what I liked about Just Burger and was afraid would have changed with the redo. There are too few decent quick takeout places in the Ditmars area so I'm glad that element is still intact.
