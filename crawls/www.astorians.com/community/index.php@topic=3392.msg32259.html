www.eddietorres.com  Legends of salsa!

I take salsa with Eddie and Maria Torres, of The Palladium Dancers in Tito Puente's heyday.  Maria teaches the beginner classes and she is extremely patient, friendly, and encouraging.  And might I mention fun.  Here's the schedule.  I like it because I don't have to sign up, I can show up whenever I want, and I still learn something new.  And it is such a workout.  Especially Eddie's advanced class.  I may be there at thursday's class this week.  I would also recommend DanceManhattan with Rodney Lopez for salsa.  And heels are unnecessary.  Some take classes in socks, sneakers, flip-flops, and jazz shoes.  As long as your footwear can slide easily on the floor (flipflops and sneakers suck) then you should be fine.
