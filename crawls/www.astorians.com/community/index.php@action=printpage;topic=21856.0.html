Title: Re: Psari seafood restaurant is yummy
Post by: TJT on October 24, 2011, 05:32:10 PM
I think Psari is the best Greek seafood restaurant in Astoria.  It's a little underrated and a little off the beaten path on 36th Ave.  But it's worth the trip.  They do a really good raw bar.  They definitely have the best raw oysters I've had anywhere in town.  Clams good too.  It's not easy to talk about the menu because it's exactly the same as Telly's, Elias Corner, Kyklades, Okeanos, etc.  But I think their grilled shrimp are exceptional.  I think their fish always seems the freshest to me.  I tend to get what they say is recommended that day.  If you like the traditional style of Greek seafood restaurants in town, then try this and see what you think.  I think it comes out on top without a doubt.  It used to be called Greek Captain and was much more rustic.  More like Elias.  But they renamed the place and remodeled.  It's very nice inside and yes - the prices are not exactly cheap.  But you can't touch a meal like this in Manhattan for what they charge.  I've recommended Psari to others and everyone is really happy they went.  They also have a full bar and a very nice staff.  It's also a nice spot for dinner if you are planning to hit Studio Square after.
