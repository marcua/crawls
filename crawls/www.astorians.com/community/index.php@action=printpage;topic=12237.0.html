Title: Do You Stay Up or Get Up?
Post by: Starwind51 on September 13, 2008, 05:22:36 AM
Anybody else listen to Hour of the Wolf?  Do you stay up or get up?  There was a time when I would get up at 4, take my shower, and be ready at 5 to walk out the door with walkman & headphones to listen as I strolled the neighborhood.  If I didn't have memories of doing this, even I wouldn't believe myself capable of it.  For years I didn't listen my Saturday morning radio shows because I couldn't get up early enough.  Recently I started listening again, but it's about 50/50 stay up/ get up.
