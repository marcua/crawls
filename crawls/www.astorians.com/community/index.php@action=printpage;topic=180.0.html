Title: Inclusive St. Patrick's Parade
Post by: brendan on December 13, 2004, 08:11:58 PM
In 10 weeks the 6th inclusive St Patricks Parade and fair will take place in Queens. The parade with street theater, color and pipes and jimbe drums will celebrate women, be welcoming to all, remember our African & Latino connections


We need puppeteers,stiltwalkers, community organizers,activists, kids of all ages, musicians,teachers and funds,...No you don't have to be Irish!!

Have fun, make freinds join in a rare New York celebration

Help organize this unique event...meetings over mugs of tea at the Freeze Peach Cafe in Astoria  and Sunnyside

Check us out at www.Stpatsforall.com.
