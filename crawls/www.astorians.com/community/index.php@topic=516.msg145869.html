While I can't speak for other people's experiences, I've lived on both the R and the N/W lines in Astoria for many years.  Neither line has been more uncomfortable than the other for me.  You get the same amount of drunks and homeless after midnight on the trains, and the walks home have been quiet but not any more dangerous than anywhere else.  Astoria is a mostly safe area, but you should always be aware of your surroundings as you would anywhere alone at night.  Crime happens here, and near either line.  But I wouldn't categorize Astoria as dangerous at all.  I'd be more concerned about how long your walk is to the train, than which line it is on.  As far as wait time goes, unfortunately, 20 mins wait time on the weekends and late nights is common on both lines, and is common for a lot of the NYC trains, not just ours.
