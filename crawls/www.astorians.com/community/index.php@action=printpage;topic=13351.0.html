Title: Re: Out Astoria December Dinner
Post by: mcdirk on December 10, 2008, 10:38:33 PM
What a wonderful night!  We had a small but friendly group (I expect the rain kept our numbers down) and the service was amazing.  We tried five of the cheeses and three of the meats, and they were top-rate across the board.  We also tried all four dips - the olive dip was intense and tasty, the humus delicious and garlicky and the chimichurri was out of this world - so cheesy and addictive.  We tried an array of the larger plates - I had the chicken which was very nice (and came with spinach, cheese and capers in a lemon sauce, yum!).  Others had the crab cakes, the scallops, the Mac and cheese (the best I've found in NYC) and the beef - rave reviews across the board.  The wines and desert were also brilliant - it was one of the nicest Out Astoria events we have had and we salute Winegasm!  Kudos!
