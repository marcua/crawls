If you're going to have poutine, you might as well go all the way and have Au Pied de Cochon's version.  It is not for the faint of heart (and I mean that literally as well as figuratively!) - it is topped with foie gras.  Ahhhh.

Au Pied de Cochon is a wonderful restaurant - I highly recommend it for the food and atmosphere.  They specialise in traditional Quebecois food, with a focus on meat, including offal, and seafood.  You can read more about it here and here.

Enjoy!  I adore Montreal.  It reminds me of a wonderful mishmash of New Orleans, Brooklyn and Paris - the best parts of three of my favourite places that make it into its own unique place.
