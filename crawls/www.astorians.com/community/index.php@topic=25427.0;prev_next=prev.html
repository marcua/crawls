Hi there.  Good luck with your coffeehouse endeavor!  I myself had been wishing there was a nice coffee place in Astoria....I'm not really aware of any specialty coffee-only places in Astoria...
Anyway, the only spaces I know off-hand are....on 21st Street....on the East side of 21st....around maybe 32nd Ave??....there is an unused storefront on the corner with a sign for Pinoy Eats.  That space is quite large though....maybe too large for a coffee only house?....but also I believe on that same block is an old space from what used to be I think a cheesecake bakery?  Looks like it's been out of use for a mighty long time, but clearly the space is already zoned for commercial use.... worth a look-see.
Ah.....also on 30th Avenue.....on the North side of 30th Ave.....about two blocks West of Crescent....there is Pita Hot....and right next to Pita Hot is their former, now-empty cafe space.  That might be a perfect size for a little coffe house!!  And I can tell you this much...that end of 30th Ave is sorely in need of a cute little cafe for people to just sit over a cup of Joe!  ;-)
Let me know any other input or advise I can give....I'm a sort of business-minded, entrepreneurial, love-Astoria type, and would love to help in any way I could.  I live on 21st near 30th Ave so I know that particular area pretty well, as well as what establishments are already in the vicinity and what might be your competition versus complimentary to your business....
