Personally I don't think that there is such a thing as perfect marriage or perfect relationships. There can be good marriages and good relationships, but I don't think perfection exists hardly anywhere. It's so cliche, but so true - you have to work at marriage to make it work. You're always negotiating, compromising, etc. Any time you live with someone, even if there isn't a romantic relationship there, you have to learn how to live with each other.

Without knowing the details of your grandparents' relationship it is hard to know what went "wrong." In fact, maybe nothing went wrong. Maybe this is what is the best for them, although clearly difficult for those around them.

Good luck as you're trying to figure out what this means, and I hope your grandparents are happy with the decision they've made.
