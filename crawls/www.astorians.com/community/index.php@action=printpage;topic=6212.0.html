If you are looking to get published there is a great magazine called JPG.  They have three themes each issue that come up (although there are a ton more themes that you can submit to even before they come up for issue).  Once you submit for a theme people can vote on your picture and how well it suits that theme.  You can vote as well on yours and everyone elses.  It's also a good way to look at some really good photography (and some not so good  :-()  When the themes are closed the editors take into account the voting but they eventually pick what will go in the magazine for that theme.  I have an issue of this magazine and it's a gorgeous art magazine printed on thick paper stock.
I have been submitting and i highly recommend it.
http://www.jpgmag.com/
