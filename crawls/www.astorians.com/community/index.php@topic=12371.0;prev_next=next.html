I vote for Thai Elephant. Everytime I've asked for it extra spicy, it's actually been _extra_ spicy. And I have a pretty high tolerance for spiciness. I particularly like the Tom Kha Gai (or however you spell it), and ask for it spicy with extra mushrooms and they actually make it the way you want. It's awesome. I also recommend the chicken Pad Kee Mao extra spicy. Also, I've now been inside the actual restaurant once (as opposed to getting delivery all the time) and it's actually a nice sit down restaurant as well. I'd bring a date there, nice atmosphere and food.

I'd avoid the appetizers as they are tasty, but just doesn't seem worth the $ vs. amount of food.

I ordered from Wave Thai as a lot of people recommended it to me. Apparently bizarre mutant people with low tolerance for spice and yet still lacking tastebuds. I was sorely disappointed in the spiciness level and it's not like the food was particularly flavorful to make up for the lack of spiciness.

The best thing about Leng Thai is it's byob atm. My friend really liked their seafood soup. I don't know how "thai" that particular dish is. I'd say the two dishes I tried were definitely better than Wave Thai.

To each their own however.
