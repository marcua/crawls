Not to be a bearer of bad news, but unless you already have a Section 8 voucher in hand, it has become impossible to get one due to a lack of funding and budget cuts.  To my knowledge, vouchers are only being provided to households that have experienced domestic violence, have a member that is an intimidated witness in a criminal investigation, or have an open ACS case.  Just throwing that out there...

Section 8 vouchers can usually be used in any apartment, as long as it passes inspection by the agency that is issuing the voucher.  I think there is also a cap on how much the rent of the apartment (based on bedroom size) can be.  You can even transfer them between different counties/states.
