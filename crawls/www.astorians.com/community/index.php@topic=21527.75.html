Quote from: FoxyWiles on January 08, 2012, 08:49:18 PM
Can anyone tell me what kind of cuts/meats they have for sale? In the butcher section I mean, not what's available on the restaurant menu.

Miss Foxy, the cuts and availability change daily.  You should call them directly and ask.  Matthew and Cathy (owners) can answer any question.  Generally they have: eye round, roasts, rib eye steaks, beef shank (osso bucco), two types of burger, 2 types of sausage, house cured bacon, skewers of beef and pork kabobs, assorted steaks and most recently giant on the bone tomahawk steaks. 

Meat usual comes in on Monday or Tuesday so Tuesday afternoon is best chance for biggest selection. 

Let me say that a farm raised grass fed piece of beef is a glorious tasting thing.  Cook it simple and as rare as possible. The flavors might just make you cry.  You will feel better too.  So much better than a supermarket bought steak.  The only condiment you might want is some good sea salt.
