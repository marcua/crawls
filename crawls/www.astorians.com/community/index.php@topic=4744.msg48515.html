Okay- so I know this doesn't fit into the Astoria/LIC area, BUT I didn't feel like going into Chinatown to the Pearl Paint store one day- so my boyfriend and I were looking around for other Pearls- and we found one in Long Island- and if you have a car or a friend who does- it's not that far away! Great prices like the Canal store and it's pretty big! It's not spread out on all the floors like the Canal St. store, but I found everything I needed (i.e. paints, brushes, surfaces, etc....) It's definitely worth the drive if you're able to make it :mrgreen:
Here's the location/number:

Pearl Paint
East Meadow
2000 Hempstead Tpke., East Meadow,
NY 11554
Phone: 516-542-7700
Store Hours:
Monday to Saturday: 10 am to 9 pm
Sunday: 11 am to 6 pm
