Ok -- I went to Sakura last night, and it's the best sushi in town. I've been to Watawa four times, and Linn (on B-way between 29th/30th) once. Linn was good, but very pricey, and Watawa has some decent stuff, but the fish always seems bland.

I sat at the Sushi bar at Sakura and was awarded with two free pieces of Sashimi, and a delicious sashimi seaweed salad to start. And the best part is that the food is *extremely* inexpensive. Most pieces of sushi are 2 dollars? A special combo with 18 pieces of fish, and 2 rolls is 35 dollars? That's absurd. Anyway. I recommend it, great service, huge cuts of fish, just all around good.
