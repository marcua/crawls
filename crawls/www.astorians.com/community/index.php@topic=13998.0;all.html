Hi all,
I am KittyKat's sister, and the secretary at Trinity Lutheran Church.
Our food pantry works on donations only  (which is not alot) so the church pays for the food that is given out
to people from the community.  Anyone can come down and sign up, you just need ID and proof of children if any.  Powell Griggs distributes the food 2 fridays a month we wish it could be more but we can't afford it.
The pantry starts at 6:30pm and runs til about 7:30pm.  Powell looks for volunteers to help pack the bags usually 6pm he starts bagging. He knows everyone that comes down to pick up and if clothes are available we let the ladies and gentleman look at them and take what they need.

He takes a short history on each applicant he likes to know if they are on a special diet or medical problems
and makes the bags accordinly example :  high blood pressure, diabetic etc.

If interested in helping out or donating please call the church office @ 718-278-0036 9:30am - 12:00 and speak to me Rose-Ann or you can email Powell   ( Powelleon@earthlink.net ).  Just tell him I gave you his email.

Hope you can all help us out.

Rose-Ann    :-)
