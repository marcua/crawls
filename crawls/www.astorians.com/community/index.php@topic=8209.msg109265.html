I've only had a mani/pedi there, but I would recommend Spa Hydra too. I was very impressed
with the space and the staff. I have a regular person I've been going to in the city for 15 years for
facials, peels and brow waxing, but if something came up that she wasn't available and I needed my skin
cleaned, I'd give Spa Hydra a try.

And just FYI, booking ahead is the norm for appointments at day spas - I don't see why a previous poster would
say that's a problem. It's the same as a hair appointment - you don't just walk in and expect them to have
a free hour for you. I actually booked my mani/pedi appointment just a couple of days in advance w/Spa Hydra
and they were able to accommodate me.

I've also heard good things about Anasa but haven't been there yet.
