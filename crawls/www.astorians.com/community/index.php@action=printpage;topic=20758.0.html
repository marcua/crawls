Title: Milky Way Midnight Minis
Post by: ladywithpans on March 27, 2011, 09:28:15 PM
Hi everybody,

Milky Way Midnight Minis are a key ingredient in my signature dessert, and when they started disappearing from stores I looked in every store in Queens and Manhattan that I happened upon.  I also did Web searches to locate possible dealers.

The Associated on 31st Avenue and 38th St. had the last 3 bags, all with a pull date of Jan. 2011.  After I told the assistant manager I wanted these candies, I bought the last three bags, and resigned myself to not having them anymore unless I ordered them online, or went to Kmart perhaps, or wrote to the Mars company begging them to bring them back.

About 2 weeks ago I went to the candy aisle at the Associated on 31st Avenue and there they were!  A whole new supply!

So if anyone likes these candies, either to eat by themselves or as an ingredient, go to the Associated on 31st Avenue!  Show the store that it made the right decision!
