After nearly two years in my apartment, I am giving it up to go see the world. Otherwise, I'd stay here another few years -- this place is really fantastic.

I'm in a small one bedroom on 31st Ave by 29th St. It's a 5 minute walk to the 30th Ave stop or Broadway stop. Close to grocery stores, restaurants (including Il Bambino and Bareburger), post office, convenience stores. There is a laundomat in the complex. 15 minute train ride into midtown Manhattan.

The apartment is located on the first floor of the building. It's quiet here with a fountain in the courtyard. Super is helpful and responds quickly. Bedroom comfortably fits a full-size bed and there's room for more furniture (desk, etc). Living room currently fits a loveseat but could fit a larger couch if so desired. Cute kitchen located on the side of the room (I have a kitchen cart for extra cooking space.) Compact bathroom with full bathtub. Closet space with extra storage.

Sign a one- or two-year lease. Rent is only $1150, plus utilities. Pet friendly. You need excellent credit and a salary of $40k+ to qualify. If you want the place to be furnished, I am willing to sell most of my things.

I haven't listed this apartment on Craigslist yet -- giving my fellow Astorians first crack. Looking for someone reliable who will commit/sign paperwork in a timely manner. You'll love it here. If you are interested, know anyone who is or have any questions, please PM me. Thank you!
