I love both summer and winter olympics.  I get teary eyed every time they play the winner's national anthem.  Doesn't matter who wins.... I just love it.

I don't care about the corporate bulls..t, I don't care about the politics while I'm watching the athletes compete. I just look at the amount of training each individual puts into their sport and it blows my mind.  Then the pressure each athlete is under "representing their country" also blows my mind.  It amazes me that they can perform at all.  I don't like China's history on human rights but I don't let that take anything away from the accomplishments of the athletes, including the Chinese athletes.  I am so moved by them all, I can't even describe it.  I am the biggest sap when it comes to this stuff.
