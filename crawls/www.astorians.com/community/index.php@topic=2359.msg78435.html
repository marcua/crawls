It's presumptuous to think that just because you are a certain way means you deserve someone else's seat. What if that person has had a long day on their feet, or if they have bad joints, or if they're feeling dizzy or sick? You can't tell these things by looking at them. No one should assume that just because someone's young means they're healthy or feeling great at any given time.

Generally what I like doing is, if I'm sitting between two empty seats and a couple people get on that are friends (or mother and child or something) I'll move over so they can sit next to each other. That way we're all happy. But I don't think anyone should "expect" a seat just because they appear one way and someone else appears another way.
