Gotham Stat Industries Inc

We are the right company for you!


Gotham Stat Industries is a NYC based construction and environmental remediation company. We specialize in the design and construction of residential interiors, beautiful bathroom and kitchen designs as well as the removal of Asbestos and Lead paint. Our staff takes the time to understand your needs whether you are a multi-unit landlord, a Coop sponsor or a single unit owner looking for that special product that identifies your individual style. If you are looking for an Asbestos removal firm Gotham Stat is the firm for you. Our principals have been in the business since 1988 and have been involved with lead paint removal since before current regulations were in place. If you are an Architect or GC looking for a demolition company to undertake large Asbestos roof rips or the abatement of friable Thermal Systems Insulation (TSI) under stringent NYCDEP and NYSDOL rules you will find our approach enlightening and affordable.

Gotham Stat is active in multiple unit renovations for landlords interested in marketing rental units for above market and for Coop sponsors renovating and selling individual units after recently purchasing coop packages. We can design and renovate your Coop units and even work with your RE Broker to facilitate marketing. Our unique sense of style and trends as well as a deep understanding of the Coop and rental market can increase your units’ values quickly. Some of our clients have seen a 100% increase in sales and 50% value in as little as 18 months from the time we began a business relationship. We believe that even in a softer market we can design your project so that it works efficiently for you, your lifestyle and your budget. Whether your project involves a small upgrade to an existing rental unit or a complete renovation of several Coops’ for sale Gotham Stat will be your one stop contractor. Our firm guarantees your complete satisfaction, so much so, that we believe you will gladly recommend us to your friends and family. That’s why “We are the right company for you”!

Asbestos & Lead

Gotham STAT Industries has the experience and equipment to eliminate asbestos, lead and mold from any site, thus meeting the re-occupancy requirements project after project. Our project managers and supervisors are experts in what today has become an art in the removal of Lead and Asbestos Containing Material in both residential and commercial projects. Gotham STAT Industries specialist has worked on a wide variety of friable and non-friable asbestos removal projects as well as numerous lead health violations. Gotham STAT Industries is ready to handle the most unusual situations and only know how to do the job one way; the right way!
Our policy is to adhere to the most stringent regulations set forth by the New York City Department of Environmental Protection (DEP), the New York State Department of Labor (DOL), United States Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), the Department of Health (DOH), Housing Preservation and Development (HPD) and the Housing and Urban Development (HUD) rules and regulations.

With its serious environmental hazards, the presence of asbestos, lead and mold can turn any property from an asset to a major liability. With state and local legislation getting tougher, the importance of finding a reliable environmental remediation company has never been greater. With this in mind, we believe that the question should not be whether you can afford to do business with Gotham STAT Industries, the questions is “can you afford not to do business” with GOtham Stat
Bathroom & Kitchen Design and Trends

Bathrooms and kitchens have dramatically changed over the last decade and will continue to evolve, as your costumer and consumer demands more. Translation, more selection, more options, more customization and not just more for them but more for you as well. Just as our cities, automobiles and fashion change with time, the bathrooms and kitchens have taken a life of their own and look different now than they did years ago and will look very different than they do today.
These spaces should incorporate more lifestyle features according to current industry leaders from the manufacturing, retail, product and interior design sectors. Gotham STAT Industries will help you create a custom design from conception thru completion of the project. Gotham STAT Industries will provide a custom array of simple, efficient and affordable designs or fabulous and extraordinary kitchens and bathrooms.


Flooring

Whether is hardwood, bamboo, ceramics, porcelain, stone or laminates, understanding of the basics of design and installation of flooring is imperative. Working with Gotham Stat will increase your knowledge and enhance your confidence in our relationship. Installing flooring is hard work, exacting and detailed. While any handyman can do a simple ceramics installation on a new and level floor we recommend professionals do the job if you are looking for a quality product that will produce true craftsmanship and even a work of art. Just as lighting, floors represents an important component of any property and can make a home beautiful or not. However, with all the abuse that floors take, especially hardwood and bamboo, they’ll occasionally need to be refinished. Gotham Stat can refinish and repair any hardwood flooring and can even change the over all appearance to tie in the finish look of your property.
“Beautiful” is what comes out of our client’s mouth when they first lay eyes on our work. Even today our own employees continue to be pleasantly surprised of the finished product their own hands produce. Whether you hire your own designer or architect this too is what you’ll say when you hire Gotham Stat to renovated or restore your floors.
