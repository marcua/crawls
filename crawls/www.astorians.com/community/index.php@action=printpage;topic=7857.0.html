Welcome to the neighborhood.

Unfortunately, both Astoria CSA (located in southern Astoria) and Hellgate CSA (Ditmars-area) are sold out for the summer season. Sunnyside CSA *might* still be taking on more members, but this late in the season, I doubt it.

Astoria CSA is working on getting a winter share together, but we have no word yet from our farmers on the numbers they can hold. There is no waitlist for this until we get more details.

There are plenty of great organic produce stores and a lot of those 24 hour veggie stands sell local (but not organic) produce this time of year. For organic stores, there is Sai (30th ave b/w 36/37 and new locale at 30th ave west of 31st) also B'way Organics on b'way and 29th or 30th. Also I know the Key Foods at 30th and 32nd sells some organic produce, it's pretty expensive though. Not sure of what's near Ditmars or where you're located.

Oh, and the greenmarket on Wednesdays on 31st ave and 14th street.
