Title: Ayazamana: Traditional Music and Dances from Ecuador
Post by: ayazamana on June 26, 2012, 01:53:56 AM
 Saturday July 14, 2012, 7:45 pm
Frank Sinatra School of the Arts, 35-12 35 Ave., Astoria, NY 11106
[font=]Join us for a delightful night of traditional music and dances from Ecuador. With over thirty performers on stage, Ayazamana will showcase various dances with colorful costumes from the different regions of Ecuador while Andes Manta will perform a wide array of traditional melodies. Bring the whole family to this spectacular show so you can learn about the richness and diversity that exists in Ecuador.[/font]
[font=]Tickets can be purchased online at www.EcuadorianTraditions.com (http://www.ecuadoriantraditions.com/) or on the night of the show. Box office will open at 7 PM. Information: 646-229-5867[/font]
[font=]$12 Adults / $10 Children[/font]
