Socrates Sculpture Park offers free yoga classes every Saturday from 11-12 between May 21st and August 27th.  They teach the Kripalu system and you have to bring your own mat.  They also offer Tai Chi on Sundays from 11-12, same dates by someone from the Taoist Tai Chi Society.

here's the link:
http://www.socratessculpturepark.org/Education/Education05.htm#yoga

SSP also has great films outdoors on Wednesday nights in July and August, also free.

Re finding space, approaching the churches is a good idea.  (my two cents)
