Yes, I am married to the *original* Matt, who was one of the original founders. But my Matt is not the same Matt that bought FP - two different people. We are not involved with FP anymore like we used to be (i.e. we don't live it and breathe it) - our involvement is minimal nowadays, so "THEY" really means "THEY". Yes, I understand FP's issues probably more than the regular patronizer (although I have no idea what the current deal is now), but if it goes out of business it won't affect us any differently than any other customer. That's not to say that it would not be disappointing on a certain personal level (I saw it grow from a twinkle in Matt's/Meesh's eyes ;) ), but I really am approaching this issue from an outsider standpoint. I honestly don't know how these types of businesses survive.

I'm not advocating donating or not donating. I am of the mind, however, that FP, despite it's for-profit status, has had a valuable and positive impact on the Ditmars-area community. I'm proud to have been a minor part of its beginnings (it has never been "my" cafe), and we'll do what we can to support it.
