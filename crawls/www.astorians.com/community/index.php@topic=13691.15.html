Delivery services don't have to give a damn about us because they know we don't have an alternative.  Whadya gonna do?  Drive across country ya self to get the package?

Even when you pay for shipping, it's the company you've bought something from that's the client.  I've tried and tried to call the companies I've purchased from and convince them to offer a choice in shipping.  Some very small companies will go the extra step of taking a single package to the post office to mail it, and while no bigger company has offered on the spot to change their ways, more companies are now offering a choice of UPS or USPS.  The advantage to us in tight urban areas is that our post offices are closer and easier to access than the UPS depot, and we usually have places near the post office that we want to go to anyway, so we're not going out of our way if we do have to pick up our packages ourselves.

It's the companies we shop from that have the voice.  We just have to keep yelling in their ears!
