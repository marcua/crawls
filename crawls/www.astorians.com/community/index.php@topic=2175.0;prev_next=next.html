ASTORIAN-PRODUCED SHAKESPEARE
« on: June 13, 2006, 12:03:21 PM »
Dear fellow Astorians,

I am proud to invite you to the inaugural production of The Hipgnosis Theatre Company. Our producers - myself, Justin Steeve, Margot Newkirk, and Demetri Bonaros - are all your fellow Astorians. You may have seen us at FreezePeach, Waltz, or Igloo hatching plots and cooking up our first production:


Shakespeare's Measure for Measure.

When: June 15 - July 2, Thursdays - Sundays, at 8pm

Where: the Flamboyan Theatre at the Clemente Soto Velez Center (107 Suffolk St, between Rivington and Delancey).

Tickets: order via SmartTix over the phone (212-868-4444) or online (http://www.smarttix.com/show.aspx?showCode=MEA0).

Tickets are just $14 when you use the discount code "FRIEND".

A difficult, thorny, gripping play, as provocative and eerily comical as anything Shakespeare ever concocted.

Help us build an audience! Please come out and support this new company - we've assembled a terrific cast and crew (a good chunk of whom are Queens/Astoria residents) and we look forward to a long future performing for you.

If you have any questions or would like more information, please email me at info@hipgnosistheatre.org or give me a call at 718-274-7477. I can't wait to see you at the theatre!

Peace,

John
