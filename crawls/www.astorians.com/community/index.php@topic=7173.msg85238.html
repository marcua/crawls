Out to Work - the LGBT Career Fair
« on: July 20, 2007, 01:38:32 PM »
As some of you know, I started a new job this week (I'm the new Executive Director of the Greenwich Village and Chelsea Chamber of Commerce).  One of our projects, we are launching the first LGBT Career Fair in NYC.  It will be on September 19, from 10 a.m. until 6 p.m. at the Metropolitan Pavilion - 125 West 18th Street.  It's called Out to Work and we have a website - outtowork.com

I'm looking for contacts in HR departments to get folks to sponsor the career fair and take tables to take resumes and talk with possible applicants.  We have over 400 folks already registered and expect a few thousand. Currently we have Google, Sovereign Bank, HSBC, Village Care and Verizon Wireless sponsoring the event - so it should be pretty successful.  Any contacts at companies would be welcome, especially if they're able to get me to the right folks in the marketing or HR departments - company affinity groups for the LGBT community are often my best way in.

If you know anyone to whom I should be talking, please pm me, email me or post it here (though without their contact information, so maybe PMs or emails would better).
Thanks!
