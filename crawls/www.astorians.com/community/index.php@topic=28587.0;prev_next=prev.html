Rizzo's Pizza
« on: December 22, 2014, 10:09:24 AM »
I heard good things about this Pizza Place so I decided to give it a try on Saturday and I was pleasantly surprised.  Now I see why people rave about this pizza.  It's thin crust pizza and the crust is so light and crispy.  I didn't even attempt to fold my slice like I usually do because I felt like the crust would break in half so I ate in without folding.  It was very hot, just the way I like it and the sauce was perfect and also the amount of cheese.  I enjoyed it so much and couldn't believe how good it was and while I was sitting there eating my slice, a lady who works there came around handing out complimentary mini cannollies.  That was delicious too.  I will definitely be going back to Rizzo's whenever I'm on Steinway
