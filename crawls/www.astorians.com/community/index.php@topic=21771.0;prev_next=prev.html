Re: Shirley Mae's Soul Food Diner
« Reply #6 on: September 20, 2011, 12:24:43 PM »
Holy cow - was the food good!   

So, atmosphere is really bad inside but they can definitely make some good food. 

I did take-out and went in to place the order, but would do delivery next time for sure.  Really nice young guys doing the cooking & running the place.  Ordered the shrimp/fish family meal which came with choice of 3 large sides, like a dozen pieces of cornbread & 2L bottle of soda.  Shrimp were excellent, really excellent with a light cornmeal & beautifully seasoned crust.  I like the fried fish a lot, my husband wasn't too crazy for it.  It was whiting fillets, also very nicely seasoned with light breading & not greasy at all.  he's just not a fan of small whiting fillets, prefers a more solid bigger piece of cod or something more substantial.  Sides were killer - collards, lima beans and green beans.  Great portions too.  Wanted to try some meat dish, but they didn't have meatloaf and didn't feel like ribs.  Guys directed me to what he called flank ribs, but I think they were shortribs - super meaty, tender, not too fatty and literally just fell off the little bones without a hitch.  got some sweet potatoes with that dish and they were excellent too. 

Biggest critique is that they didn't have everything on the menu (no turkey, meatloaf, black eyed peas, soups, etc) so don't get your hopes up for a particular dish until you call or stop in.  They're still getting things going, but hopefully will be at full speed soon. 

...and Sally Mae is a real person - mom to one of the guys and all the recipes are hers - sweet touch.
