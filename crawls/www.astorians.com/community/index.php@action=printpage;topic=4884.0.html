Title: Re: What's the Crescent Lounge like?
Post by: lanseaux on January 26, 2007, 11:46:37 AM
The only problem with the Crescent Lounge is that it's pretty small.  I really like the atmosphere, though.  When it's full it can be very loud (not unlike many bars!).  I've never been on a Friday night but I would say get there as early as possible to snag a comfortable seat.  You can also order food in there -  I did that recently and ordered from the new Indian place, Mughal Dinner (not mind-blowing but tasty enough), so I know they have that menu behind the bar.

Crescent Lounge is a very nice spot, very laid back and you get a good mix of locals and peeps who are just out and about and who happen to stop in for a drink (such as yourselves). I also enjoy the decor, you almost feel like you are in someones private parlor. Enjoy the chilly night out wherever you may end up :wink:
