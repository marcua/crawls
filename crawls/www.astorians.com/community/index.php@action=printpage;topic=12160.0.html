I had a birthday party at Break on Broadway between 32nd and 33rd Street a couple of years ago.  I don't know if they still have the same special, but we got free pool (I could have had ping pong) on two tables for two hours and a complimentary bottle of Absolute vodka with mixers.  They have a full bar and a lounge like area for guests to hang out, if they don't shoot pool.  The staff was very helpful and attentive.  A fun night was had by all!

3208 Broadway
Astoria, NY 11106
718-777-2400
