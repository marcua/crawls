You will need a driver on your Mac to use the drive if it is formatted by Windows.

Windows uses NTFS by default, but you could use FAT32 (which I'm pretty sure OS X can read/write) by choice. Problem with FAT32 is you can't have files over 2gb (like ripped DVDs etc.) OS X uses HFS+, which Windows 2k supported, but XP does not.

Format the drive on your XP machine, and install NTFS-3G on your Mac. Once you have that installed, you can attach the external drive to your Mac and should be to read and write to the drive. Make sure you properly remove the drive (using the "Safely Remove Hardware" option in XP, or drag to trash in OS X) before unplugging from the machine or there may be problems mounting the drive or (worst case) you could lose data.

Good luck!
