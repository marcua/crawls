Life is 100% unexpected   :-o

My father passed away two weeks ago  and as I sat Shiva at my parents' home, I decided it might be good to start looking for a job in California in order to be closer to my mom.

What happened next is: I sent a resume sunday night, I got a call this past monday to interview, I interviewed monday afternoon, the job was mine tuesday afternoon

when opportunity beckons, even under the worst of circumstances, you take what you're offered.

so it's sudden, it might seemed rushed, but it's a good thing right now to be closer to home.

That said, I've loved my five months here in Astoria. I've had opportunity to meet wonderfully warm, vibrant and creative people working hard to create a thriving online and offline community. I've enjoyed this neighborhood and discovering its haunts through the eyes of the many online posters here at astorians.com. I've enjoyed stuffing my face to food goodness from the Taco Roll to EATS to the social events we've celebrated. It's been short but it's been very very sweet.

thanks for understanding,
