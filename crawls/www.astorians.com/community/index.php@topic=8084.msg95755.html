Regarding "no" and what means "no, i'm not interested so stop talking to me" from the perspective of flirter to receiver:

1. The ipod earphones stay in the receiver's ear. (not interested in a long convo.)
2. The flirter is the only one asking questions. If the receiver isn't reciprocating with a question, they aren't interested.
3. Checking the phone.
4. Still glancing at their reading materials occasionally to show "i'd like to get back to my reading."
5. Response with "mhmmm" instead of meaningful response to a question.
6. Decreased eye contact


Just a couple of things I've tried doing to get the point across. Doesn't always work.
