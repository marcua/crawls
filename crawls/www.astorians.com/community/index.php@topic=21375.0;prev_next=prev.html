Steak in Astoria
« on: July 21, 2011, 04:59:43 PM »
Lived here over ten years and with one exception I haven't a good steak.  There used to be a greek restuarant on Broadway and 33rd street.  They renovated, went upscale and in Astoria that's a recipe for disaster.   They opened a resturant in the Murray Hill section of Manhattan.  But before they renovated, they had the best rib eye streak with bone in Astoria at a reasonable price.  Since then, every place I've tried the meat is like Beef Jerky.   
 
I've tried the steak at Michael's Diner.  It was well seasoned but overcooked and/or just too chewy.  Opa Opa, the worst steak in all of the city.   I've tried a 1/2 dozen other places over the years and not one good steak.  Plus if you say medium, they cook it well.   
 
Anybody know a place that cooks a juicy tender steak.  It can't be brain surgery.  All you need is a hot grill and tender piece of meat.
