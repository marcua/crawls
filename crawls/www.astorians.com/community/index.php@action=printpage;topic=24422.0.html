Title: Level 2 Baby Sign Language Class
Post by: My Smart Hands on February 06, 2013, 05:04:57 PM
 Due to popular demand, My Smart Hands is now offerering a level 2 course, which will run from March 3 - April 28th at 11:00 a.m. at babyNOIR (http://www.babynoir.net) (on 23rd Avenue, across the street from Raising Astoria).  The class will meet every Sunday for 8 weeks (no class on Easter, March 31st), so bring Dad too!
 
Like level 1, we will learn over 100 new signs through themes such as colors, feelings, food, animals, and people (like teacher and fireman). We will add more signs to the songs we already know and learn new songs too.  The class comes with a manual, like the first class.
 
I look forward to seeing you and discovering how much your little ones are signing since we last met!
