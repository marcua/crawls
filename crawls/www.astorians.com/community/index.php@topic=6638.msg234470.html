Was Gus' tavern where the butcher is now on the south side of 30th Ave. between 31st & 32nd Streets? I know that was a bar. If you go into the meat market you can see the *cool* tin ceiling. When they built that building on the southwest corner of 30th Ave. & 32nd St., Pinochio's restaurant moved across the street where the hamburger place is now. The butcher moved a few doors west to where a bar once was. I remember when this move happened -- it wasn't all that long ago -- but I can't remember the bar where the butcher is now.


Speaking of bars, I noticed that Jimbo's, a long-time Astoria bar on Astoria Blvd. just west of 31st St./the Astoria Blvd. subway stop, is renovating. It is morphing into a Greek restaurant/bar, according to the sign outside the place.
