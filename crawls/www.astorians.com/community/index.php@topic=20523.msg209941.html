Nods are the dumbest part of this board and people would do well to just ignore them.  They serve no useful purpose whatsoever.

My father in law used to own a small coffee shop in Manhattan and was pushed out by the building so they could combine the spaces and get a big name store in the building.  So I understand the difficulties that small businesses face.  One day you might think everything is fine and you can run a profitable business...then when it comes time to renew your lease then BAM you could just lose your livelihood in an instant.
