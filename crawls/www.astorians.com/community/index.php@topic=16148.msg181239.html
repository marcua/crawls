Anyway, having begun to take taxis from LGA to the vicinity of Steinway & Broadway, I wanted to figure out the most direct route to request.  I know it won't have much of an effect on the fare, but I'd like to minimize travel time and look like less of a rube.  Last time, the driver took the GCP to the 31st/Hoyt exit and doubled back on Astoria Blvd to Steinway.  This wasn't too much of a detour, but involved lots of traffic at 31st.  Google Maps suggests the BQE to Broadway.  I was thinking perhaps just Astoria Blvd directly to Steinway - and I believe a few people alluded to this route in the previous thread.  Any thoughts?
