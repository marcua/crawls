I'm an edamame freak! I just bought 4 bags of frozen edamame in their pods at Trader Joe's for 1.69 per pound. They're from Taiwan and I've found that the Taiwanese edamame are tastier and sweeter than the ones from China. Not meaning to China bash.

I once purchased frozen "chamame" (also from Taiwan) from the Japanese store ("Sunrise" or "Shunshine") downtown, across the street from Cooper Union. The pods are green and the beans have brownish skin. They were so delicious, different from edamame, a nuttier taste. When I went back to purchase more they were out of stock and I haven't seen them since then. That was about 2 years ago. I'd like to grow them in my backyard, but with my appetite I'd need a much bigger yard!
