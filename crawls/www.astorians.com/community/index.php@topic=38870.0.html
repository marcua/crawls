Hi everyone! I'm new to this board, but I've lived in Astoria since 2010 and I absolutely love it! Sorry to start my first post off so negatively, but my friends and I are fed up.



I can't begin to describe my feelings when I saw that the former home of the short lived Granos restaurant was getting turned into another trashy "lounge." I honestly don't understand why we would need another one of these places, when Steinway street has enough for the entire city. These lounges bring in the wrong sort of people and make our neighborhood unsafe. I don't know what can be done to keep these sort of places from opening up. Hopefully it will close soon. From a business perspective, this just doesn't seem like a place that will thrive with the direction the neighborhood is moving.
