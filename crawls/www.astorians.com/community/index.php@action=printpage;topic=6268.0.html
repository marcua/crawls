Title: Re: Spitzer's gay marriage bill
Post by: mcdirk on May 08, 2007, 04:03:24 PM
It will pass, but not this year.  The best we can hope for this year is passage by the New York State Assembly.  I think we have a solid 66 Assemblymembers ready to vote for marriage, and we need 76 to pass.  There are just over 50 members on the fence.  Once we get the numbers of committed yes votes up to 80, we will see a hearing in the Assembly Judiciary Committee, followed by a vote by the full Assembly.  In the Senate, the Republican leadership will not allow the bill a vote. 
As to what you can do, join Marriage Equality New York for the Wedding March over the Brooklyn Bridge on Saturday, May 19, 11 a.m. congregation in Foley Square, followed by a March over the Bridge and a rally/show in Cadman Plaza in Brooklyn.  It will be fun and will help raise awareness.  Letters to Assemblymember Michael Gianaris and State Senator George Onorato asking them to co-sponsor the bill are also in order!
