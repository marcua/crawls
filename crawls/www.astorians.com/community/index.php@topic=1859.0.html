Cooking With Jazz to reopen
« on: April 01, 2006, 02:58:31 PM »
Ok, so I'm straying from Astoria to Whitestone with this, but I think it deserves a mention.

Cooking With Jazz, a really great Cajun restaurant that was in Whitestone for over 10 years, lost its lease last year and had to close.  But an email from chef-owner Steve Ven Gelder reveals that they are close to finding a new location, and he hopes to be reopened by Mother's Day.  He says that it will still be in Whitestone.
