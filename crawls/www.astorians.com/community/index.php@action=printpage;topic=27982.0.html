Title: Electronic Waste Recycling Event - Today (May 3rd) from 10 a.m.-4 p.m.
Post by: nsutaria on May 03, 2014, 10:40:54 AM
Hi folks,

I know this is last minute, but there's an electronics and e-waste recycling event today, Saturday May 3rd, at the Broadway Library from 10 a.m.-4 p.m.. Most importantly, they take alkaline and rechargeable batteries (tape over the ends of the rechargeable batteries).  See below and this link:

http://lesecologycenter.org/index.php?option=com_jevents&task=icalrepeat.detail&evid=3143&Itemid=1&year=2014&month=05&day=03&title=electronic-waste-recycling-event-astoria&uid=dcde68267fe914e17e2461c4dfed376a&catids=86  (http://lesecologycenter.org/index.php?option=com_jevents&task=icalrepeat.detail&evid=3143&Itemid=1&year=2014&month=05&day=03&title=electronic-waste-recycling-event-astoria&uid=dcde68267fe914e17e2461c4dfed376a&catids=86)
Saturday, May 03, 2014 @10:00am - 04:00pm
Broadway Library, 4020 Broadway between Steinway and 41st Streets, Queens, NY 11103

Start your spring cleaning by dropping off your unwanted or broken electronics during our spring e-waste collection event at Broadway Library in Astoria.

We will accept working & non-working computers, monitors, printers, scanners, keyboards, mice, cables, TV’s, VCR’s, DVD players, phones, audio/visual equipment, cell phones, and video gaming equipment. We do not accept any household appliances including microwaves and other kitchen appliances.

For more program and data security information, please check our FAQs (http://lesecologycenter.org/index.php/e-waste/faq). If you have additional questions, please email us (info@lesecologycenter.org) or call (212) 477-4022.

We would like to thank Broadway Library for hosting this event. The spring e-waste collection events are sponsored by Tekserve.
