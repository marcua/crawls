Hello fellow Astorians.  I've lived on and off in Astoria for years.  I came from East Harlem back in the 70's where my mother moved us to 41st and Bway.  Anyone remember the EE train?  That's our current R train.  It would only go to Lexington and 59th up until 7:30.  You would have to walk to the elevated stop on Bway and 31st st if you wanted to get into Manhattan.  I don't want to explain how you'd get there otherwise.  It was just too long of a trip!

At any rate, after that I've lived all over the place but I'd find myself back in Astoria.  I had gotten married and lived in Alexandria, VA, but when I came back to NY, you guessed it, Astoria.  Then I lived in Manhattan for a while (yeah, the ex-wife has the condo, oh well), and after that I lived in NJ.  But when it came to getting another apartment in NY?  Astoria!  So I've been back since '99 and unless I can get back into Manhattan, this is the place to be!

Hi everyone! I am new to the board, and thought this was a good (super ongoing thread) to post an introduction myself as well.

My name is Andrea, I'm 29 years old, and I have lived in Astoria for just over 3 years. Having said that, I feel like I've just gotten out of a coma!! I never found these boards before, and I never made any local friends in my 3 years here, to my dismay. I lived with my roommate, who was also my friend from school days, and hung out every place but Astoria. Tired of feeling isolated, (and hearing that Brooklyn is "the place to be"...sigh), I am determined to find people here, and an artist community too.

I just moved to 48th street and 31st ave with my boyfriend, and after both being laid-off and both desperately needing a place, we settled for this one.....though I miss the N/W line like crazy! I loved looking out the window in the mornings of that crowded subway car to work.... (speaking of, does anyone know why the R train stops running after 11pm?!?)

I am an artist/illustrator....looking for FT work, but freelancing and taking a design class at SVA in the meantime. I am addicted to vegetarian food, HGTV(!), crafts, and my cat Smush.
