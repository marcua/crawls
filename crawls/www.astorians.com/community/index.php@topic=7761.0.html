I belong to Matrix Fitness on 45 ST and Ditmars Boulevard.  The club is very clean and it is big.  It isn't quite as nice at the NYSC on Steinway but it is about $30-40 cheaper per month and you don't have to commit for a year.  It is month by month.  I joined in October and the plan I chose was an initiation fee of $179 and a monthly fee of $49.95. As long as you continue your membership the price will never go up.  The personal trainer I am working with here is great also.  I used a personal trainer at NYSC who was very immature and treated me like I was in my early 20's not my late 40's (I think he was trying to kill me!)

I know this can't help everyone, but if you have HealthNet insurance (and possibly other insurance providers as well) look at your membership book or google WellQuest. I get my membership at the rock through this option and it is only $57 a month. A good discount on the rumored $80 a month or prepaid contract. I think there was a one time fee that was around $50 as well. Everything goes through WellQuest and apparently the fee won't change even if I switch jobs or insurance providers.
I believe there are even cheaper options at other gyms around the city using WellQuest. But convenience is really important in determining how often I workout so I chose the Rock since it is close to my apartment.
