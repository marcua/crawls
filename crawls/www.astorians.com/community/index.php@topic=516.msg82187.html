Hey everybody!  My name is Vinny and I'm 20.  I've lived in New York almost 2 years now but just recently moved to Astoria with my girlfriend Kaytee Sue.  We are high school sweethearts both born and raised in CT but NYC and particularly Astoria has always been my second home for as long as I can remember.  I'm so happy to be living in the neighborhood now.  Crazy, it seems like just yesterday I'd come to Astoria for a few weeks during the summer and play basketball on the same courts that now are only footsteps away. 
I love, love music.  Especially punk rock but also jazz, experimental, trance, rap and just straight up rock.  I play guitar and am trying to get a band together with my friend Pixie from the last neighborhood I lived in.  She's a poet/lyricist/vocalist with astonishing talent and we've started playing some open mics trying to find other people to jam with.  Perhaps you may catch us at the Waltz one of these upcoming Wednesdays.  I also like writing, reading, people-watching and just enjoying everything NYC has to offer.  If you see my girl and I walking along Ditmars please stop and say hey - we'd really like to meet some people in the neighborhood.  Cheers and beers guys.  God bless.

- Vinny

Welcome, Sintex.  I feel like one in every five people I meet are originally from Ohio  :-D

You came to the right spot to get connected to the neighborhood!  Whether you want to know which restaurant to go to (or avoid, but you can make your own decision), events in the neighborhood, whether political or social, or just to chat, Astorians is definitely the place to be.

If you get a chance, you should just get out and start walking around.  This area is unique because it's so close to Manhattan, but it has single family homes with quiet, tree lined streets and a nice big ole park on the northwest corner. 

Again, welcome to the area and if you need any help trying to find anything, let us know.
