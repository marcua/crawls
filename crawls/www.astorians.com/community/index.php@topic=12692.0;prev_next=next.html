A group of friends and I tried this place out the other night.  All of us have walked by over the years and wondered about the place.  The gates around it and the old door can be a bit off-putting.  It reminds one of the many "social" clubs in the area.  It was a very dead night and we were one of 3 parties dining there that evening.  The restaurant has a certain amount of charm to it - it's very dark, but it's like walking into Grandma's living room from the 70's.  It's really interesting and in a good way.   The staff was very friendly and nice.  The food was really great and I loved my chicken franchese with artichokes and asparagus.  Everyone seemed to love their food and I wanted to try everyone's :)  Apparently the eggplant lasagna might be a little too much eggplant.  But otherwise, no complaints were heard.  We had a few bottles of wine and had a really good night!
