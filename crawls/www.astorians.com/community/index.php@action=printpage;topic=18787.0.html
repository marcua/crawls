Title: Greater Astoria Historical Society Annual Lecture and Beer Tasting
Post by: greentiger on July 08, 2010, 07:00:47 AM
The Greater Astoria Historical Society (GAHS) is hosting their annual Lecture and Beer Tasting on Wednesday, July 21 at 6:00 pm.  In honor of the Centennial of Bohemian Hall, join us for an armchair tour of the Beer Gardens in Queens. Learn about the history of beer gardens and how they became a favorite past time in the 1900s.  This lecture will include a beer tasting with a discussion of what makes each brew unique and the harvesting of hops around the world. 

The event takes place at the GAHS, located at 35-20 Broadway, 4th Floor
Please call 718-278-0700 or email astorialic@gmail.com by Saturday, July 17 for reservations.
$20.00 per person ($12.00 for GAHS members)
Must be 21 or over
