Well my foodie friends, its never to early to think about food huh?  :mrgreen:

Carrying on with our "help save local businesses" theme from August (it went sooo super) i am proposing we hit Ali's Kabab Cafe. Some have tossed the idea around and i couldn't be happier with that option. So what that we get yummy exotic Egyptian cuisine 2 months in a row! Its for the greater good of the hood yo  :-P
hahaha
I am writing this with a full belly of some loverly filet of sole, grilled veggies and rice from Ali right now,so heck yeah i'm pro going there!!!!!

I spoke to him tonight about it. Sunday Sept. 24th 2pm. i think we max  out the space at 20 we can maybe push it to 22-24? $30 each and he'll feed us till he runs out of food! hahaha no really its a cut and dry $30 no adding tax and tip-its all in there. Again its byob, so you know the drill. I was even thinking of talking to him about bringing plastic plates (i don't want to make it cheesey or anything) so he wouldn't have to wash so many dishes before his dinner rush, you know? We can bring our own music even, party central!!!!

So yeah, its nothing set in stone- but its a go if we decide on a yes. Would love to love him that day big time.
