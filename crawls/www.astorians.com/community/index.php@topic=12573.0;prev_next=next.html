I went here last night to get dinner around 8:45 and it was GREAT.  While the wait was a little on the long side (20 min or so), that was only because the very nice woman (owner?) was working alone and there were quite a few orders coming in, both for take-out and delivery.

She was very sweet and apologetic, explaining to everyone that her other chef didn't show up so she was cooking solo. 

But most importantly, my pasta was FANTASTIC.  I got a pretty basic order (whole wheat penne with bolognese sauce) but others around me got all sorts of combinations and everything looked and smelled great.  I will definitely go back.  And I have plenty of leftovers for lunch today, since the serving sizes are pretty big.

Only cons (and these are excusable, IMO) were that the wait was long and she accepts cash only.
