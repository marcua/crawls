Fage Tzatziki Sauce
« on: November 01, 2007, 12:15:20 PM »
Multiple trips to the C-Town on Broadway have turned up empty. Out of all the grocers in the Broadway area they were the only ones who had it. Associated, Bravo, Pathmark and Stop & Shop have never had it. I'm slightly addicted to it since it's tasty and somewhat healthy (yogurt cucumber garlic, how can you go wrong?)

At this point, as long as I know the store in question has it, I'm willing to leg it up to Ditmars. I haven't tried any Greek shops because quite frankly I am unfamiliar with most. I'm willing to go there as well, as long as I know where it is and that folks have been happy with the store.

The catch is that is has to be the Fage one. I bought some other version from the Associated by my office in Manhattan and the consistency is quite different and is certainly not as healthy as the Fage (30 calories for 2 tablespoons, which is more than enough for one warm piece of pita, as opposed the random one which is about 100 + calories for the same serving size. For that many calories I basically have one whole grain cracker, which makes it a less enjoyable eating experience).

Between me and John we go through a container a week; faster if I use it as part of a meal or have friends over. I'm in withdrawl!! And that other brand just isn't cutting it (I'd tell ya what it is but I'm too lazy to go downstairs to the fridge and identify it).

Help me....please.....
