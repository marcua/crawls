 Let’s Get Motivated!
Why a community Garden?
   o Many of us rent and don’t have much space, if any, to grow anything!
      Some facts taken from CommunityGarden.org:
      + Gardens create opportunities for people to work together to improve communities in many ways.
      + Studies have shown that for humans, just looking at a plant can reduce stress, fear, and anger, and lower blood pressure and muscle tension.
       + Visiting a small garden can give a person the feeling of “getting away” from stressful places such as work, etc.
          Gardens can bring communities benefits such as, reduced crime, nutritious food, and increased business activity
    o Gardeners themselves gain a sense of accomplishment and success from caring for their own garden.
