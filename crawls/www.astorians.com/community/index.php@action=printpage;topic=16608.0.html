Title: Grandma Pizza From Porto Bello
Post by: Yaxpac on November 08, 2009, 12:44:33 PM
I recently ordered a Grandma pizza From Porto Pizza in the shopping center on 43rd and ditmars.  What a great pie.  Thin crust square pizza with a great garlicky sauce on top of the cheese.  Just wanted to spread the word about this delicious pie.  Their other pizzas are good too but this one really stands out.  The sauce is what makes it.  It has lots of garlic and you can tell it is a homemade sauce, not that generic sweet pizza sauce like most mediocre places. 

www.portobellopizzeria.com
