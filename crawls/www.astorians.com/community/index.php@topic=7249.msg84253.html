Touch Football in Queensbridge Park!
« on: July 25, 2007, 04:19:53 PM »
ZogSports, the charity-focused, social, co-ed sports league is coming to Queens for the first time this fall and we'll be playing co-ed Touch Football in Queensbridge Park!

Join fellow young professionals in their 20s and 30s for 8-on-8 Touch Football, followed by organized Happy Hours at local bars after each game!  This is a great way to meet your neighbors and make new friends or spend time with old ones!

No need to put together an entire team (though many people do!) - you can sign up as an individual or a small group and we'll place you on a team with awesome other people!

Registration is Now Open for the fall league!  Early registration runs until July 27, so sign up now to get the lower registration fee!  For more information and to register, visit us at www.zogsports.org, or go directly to the Football league page here: http://www.zogsports.org/Football/Queens%20Football.htm.

Play For Your Cause:
ZogSports encourages each team to select a charity to play for.  We ask each captain to designate an officially sanctioned 501(c)3 not-for-profit organization before the end of the season.  Instead of prize money, we make charitable contributions to the winning teams' charities of choice.  In addition, ZogSports' happy hour partners donate 10-15% of what you eat & drink to Play For Your Cause.
