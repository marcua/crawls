Title: Re: Solid Blues Bar In Astoria?
Post by: snz333 on May 08, 2008, 11:02:10 AM
Welcome to the area!  I've never been to any spots in Astoria that feature blues.  However, Waltz Cafe is my favorite place for live music in Astoria, and the bands there sometimes cross over into blues.  It's on Ditmars between 23rd and 24th.  Even the open mic night is great.

Also, one time I was at Sunswick at 35th and 35th and they were featuring a blues singer.  But I haven't seen live music there in a while.

Not that anyone asked, but in Manhattan, my favorite blues spots are Terra Blues on Bleeker (lousy atmosphere but GREAT music), and the Rodeo Bar on 3rd Ave (more rockabilly, but definitely a lot of blues, great food, great atmosphere).

Also Dinosaur BBQ up on 131st on the west side is def worth checking out.  The food is great, and they host a lot of great blues music.

Hope this helps.  I like blues music a lot and if there were a blues bar in Astoria I would LIVE there.
