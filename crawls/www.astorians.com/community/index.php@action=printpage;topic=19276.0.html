Since taking office three years ago, Comptroller Tom DiNapoli has worked hard to cut waste, restore accountability and secure the financial well-being of New York State.  His efforts to curb spending and end fraud and pay-to-play have been lauded by Democrats and Republicans, editorial boards, and citizens from Montauk to Niagara.

Come out and support our outstanding NYS Comptroller, Tom DiNapoli, and help him continue to work on our behalf.  Please consider attending a fund-raising reception we are hosting in support of his reelection campaign.
