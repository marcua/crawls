There are some types of noise that can be mitigated and other types of noise that can be abated.  Car alarm noise can be be abated; there are better technologies such as silent pagers that can alert the owner directly if the alarm is tripped.

Most businesses can effectively mitigate noise if it proves to be a problem for neighbors, that includes better policies on music playing, improved insulation, and following existing noise codes in place.  Better legislation and enforcement on loud motorcycles and boom cars is another means to reduce noise.
