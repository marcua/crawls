It's on the northwest corner of Astoria Blvd and 31st street.  There's the Dunkin' Donuts / Baskin Robbins on 31st, then a couple bodegas, then you turn the corner and the restaurant is right there.  Little hole in the wall, usually filled with hispanic people and very little English is spoken inside.  Despite what the awning says, they don't deliver.

But be warned: I'm not snobby about my food.  I don't care if it tastes like the food in mexico as long as it tastes good in my mouth.

Los Amigos was nothing great, nothing bad. A solid mexican place doing your run of the mill authentic mex.  It gets very busy late at night with local Spanish people coming home from working at other places. I think they come more for the comraderi, cheap but solid eats and the juke box than for culinary delights.   

I like all the basics there and the salsa too.  But it is nowhere as good as Tacos Mexico on 38th st and bway.  They get the same type of crowd for the same reasons but its so much better.
