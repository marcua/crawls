I have recently moved to Astoria from Brooklyn (bayridge).  Over the weekend, my wife and I went to Vesta which was recommended by one of our new neighbors.  The place was very crowded so we had a drink at the bar and with in 15 min we were able to have a table.  The place has a very relaxing and cozy feel to it.  I worked in many different restaraunts in Brooklyn for over ten years plus and the menu selection was a bit small for my liking but I did like the quality of their carfully selected dishes.   Prices were reasonable, slightly inexpensive for the quality of food that they serve.  My wife had the Bronzino and I had the Pork Milanese.  Both dishes were amazing and fullfilling. I do wish they also had a wider selection of beers but like their food they're choices of beer are great, particularly the Hofbrau.  The staff was extremely helpful and attentive but not overbearing like some places could be.  Last but not least the baby Jesus cakes OMG amazing!! Something this heavenly definitely has it's proper name.  

Since we just moved to Astoria a month ago, we really can't judge this place as one of Astoria's best restaurants...we're going to continue to explore...but for now this is definitely our favorite local spot!
