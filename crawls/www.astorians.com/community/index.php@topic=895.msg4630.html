Laundromat Rant
« on: October 07, 2005, 11:34:16 AM »
I know its so silly, but I am so disgusted with the condition of my local laundry. Its the one on Crescent between Broadway and 34 Ave. Firstly, I have to bring my own cleaning supplies because there is so much mildew growing in the detergent compartments and I always see roaches crawling around on the machines and the tables. Secondly, the machines keep using less and less water. My last trip there was so disasterous that the clothes I supposedly "washed" in the machines smelled like dirty underwear when they came out. Is this a board of health issue? My son's blankets smelled literally of feces after being washed there. I use good soaps, hot water, etc. I ended up taking everything to another laundry and thankfully, everything got cleaned right. Whenever I complain, I get answers like "You overloaded the machine." or "I'll tell the owner." (I've gone for the bigger machines with less clothes and they do the same thing.)
