I have been a member of Fitness Point for about three years now. It is not the fanciest gym around and sometimes the wait time for the machines can be a pain in the neck- but in general it is a decent gym. It is very inexpensive and the sales staff isn't overly pushy or aggressive which I really like.

I have never tried Synergy but I have friends that had memberships there and said that the sales staff was very aggressive to the point of almost harassment. They are also more expensive.

If you are looking for a bare bones, inexpensive gym I would say Fitness Point is a good option.

My other suggestion is that there are two new gyms being built in the area. I don't know when they will be completed but if you are not in a rush you could find out a bit more information about them. Try searching this board - there is definitely a thread regarding this (sorry I am not exactly sure how to attach a thread otherwise I would have).

Goodluck!
