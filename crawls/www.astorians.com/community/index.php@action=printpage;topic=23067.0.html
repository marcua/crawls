Exclusive Early Bird Tickets to Cask Ale Fest at Alewife on July 13, 14 and 15

http://ny-offers.timeout.com/deals/cask-ale-fest

Get Real Presents (https://getrealpresents.com/) - brings their 'Cask Ale Festival' to the boroughs! 1st stop is Alewife NYC (http://alewifequeens.com/) in Long Island City -
This excellent Beer Hall is just about perfect to host our beloved Cask Ale (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cask_ale) - showing off 2 floors and a back patio -
Get real Presents (https://getrealpresents.com/) will be running 4 sessions through out the weekend of July 13th - 15th showcasing many many great breweries foreign and domestic.
The centerpiece of the festival will be the Cask Bar, which will showcase 50+ cask ales from some of the nation's best craft breweries and homebrewers. Attendees can expect rare cask finds like Sierra Nevada Harvest, Green Flash Double Stout, Lagunitas Brown Shugga, Ballast Point Sculpin IPA and Sixpoint Diesel.  The beer lineup will be  available on the Get Real Presents website soon and is being updated daily as new beers come in.
The festival will also feature local beers from Brewery Ommegang, Brooklyn Brewery, Greenport Harbor Brewing, Kelso of Brooklyn, Empire Brewing Company & Sixpoint Craft Ales.
