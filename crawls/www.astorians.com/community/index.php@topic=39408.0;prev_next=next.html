Quote from: mm on April 25, 2016, 10:28:40 PM
I think most tracks are 400 meters. How is making it longer making runners more happy?


Tracks are 400 meters or 400 yards, just like swimming pools can be 25 meters or 25 yards. Changing the size beyond the 400 m/y is something that would throw a lot of people off - walkers and runners. They know the size and can determine their workout accordingly, whether they are doing short distances, long distances, sprints/drills, etc. A "custom" length track or pool automatically lowers the perceived quality of the space. Whoever is saying "size doesn't matter" when it comes to a track is uninformed at best and dismissive at worst.


Many more people will use the track vs the soccer field. It's a huge draw in Astoria Park. I think it should stay as-is, except for a refinish on is surface.
