Re: Cirque Du Soleil
« Reply #4 on: October 23, 2009, 11:21:29 PM »
I've seen most of the shows in Vegas and every one that has come through NYC since 1997 (including the arena shows).  The one at the Beacon looks interesting but if you bide your time you will likely be able to get discount tix.  I know a lot of people on the board (including myself) were able to get really good seats for "Kooza" when it was on Randall's Island next year (FYI - "OVO" - their latest touring show hits Randall's Island in May 2010!).

The only show of theirs I would not recommend is "Wintuk" which is playing at the WaMu Theatre at MSG.  I saw it the first year and it was just boring boring boring.

The shows at MSG and the Beacon are a different concept than what tours under the grand chapiteau on Randall's Island as they are offshoots of the original plan.  That said, if you go in with an open mind you will be amazed at what you see before you - almost as if being awake while dreaming.
