On Saturdays when construction is being done they will sometimes depart and land runway 22.  Meaning they will liftoff right over the Grand Central Parkway and do a climbing left hand turn to the northeast.  This isn't usually done due to "noise abatement".  But if the winds call for it safety > noise.  Doubt this was at 5am as the airport doesn't really open itself to flights until 5:30-6am the earliest.  Just a heads up.
