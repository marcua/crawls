Hi just passing this info along...

Qville, an Astoria based non-profit dedicated to strengthening
community through the arts, is having its second Open Meeting at
8:00 pm on Thursday, September 23, 2004 at the Bohemian Beer
Garden.  We want to meet you!  It's an informal affair, and a
nice excuse to meet up and have a few with your friendly
neighborhood
artists.

Bohemian Beer Garden
29-19 24th Avenue
Astoria, NY
(We'll have a red balloon tied to our table)

Directions:
Subway # N/W to Astoria Blvd. (one stop before the last - Ditmars
Blvd.). Walk 1 block to the intersection of 24th Avenue and 31st
Street. The Bohemian Beer Garden is located between 29th Street and
31st Street.

And visit our website for more information about our Art Fundraiser
on October 2nd!  www.Qville.org

join the mailing list info@Qville.org

Qville respects the privacy of all our neighbors - if you do not
wish to receive future e-mail announcements, please send an e-mail
to unsubscribe@Qville.org
