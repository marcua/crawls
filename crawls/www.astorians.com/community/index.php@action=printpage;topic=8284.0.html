Title: Re: jogging to/from and in/around astoria park
Post by: kaitlin on November 14, 2007, 08:26:07 PM
Hellgate Road Runners meets in the park 4x a week. I just moved here a few months ago and joined up with them. There are some pretty intense runners in the group (not me) but they are completely open and accepting of beginners. They have a website where you can find out where they train and how to contact the coach. I really like running with them because its motivating to know that there are a bunch of people out there, its much safer than going by myself or with my roommate (she and i run with the group together), and they don't care if you dont show up... they are just happy when you do!

to get involved you can either just show up at a practice or email Jared, the coach. I just happened upon them during a walk in the park and thats how we started running with them. Good Luck!

http://www.hrr-online.org/
