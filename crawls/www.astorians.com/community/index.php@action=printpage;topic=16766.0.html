Title: THE QUEENS PLAYERS PRESENTS OPENING GALA FOR PREMIERE OF NEW PERFORMING SPACE!
Post by: dmart912 on November 30, 2009, 12:39:42 PM
The Queens Players & The Secret Theatre cordially invite you to the Grand Opening Gala Celebration for the premiere of its new performing space, "The Big Secret Theatre." The event will take place Saturday, December 5 from 5-7PM. General admission for the event are only $10.00.The night includes music, live performance by THE MAIN SQUEEZE ORCHESTRA,cocktails, and hors d'oeuvres with a special champagne toast, a silent auction, a raffle, followed by a celebrity ribbon cutting ceremony. The Gala takes place at The Big Secret Theatre (4402 23rd St LIC, NY 11101).

As part of our opening Gala, we are holding a silent auction.  We will be auctioning off everything from original paintings by local renowned artists to personal fitness training sessions, photo packages, acupuncture sessions, couture fashion items, and many more. The Big Secret Theatre’s inaugural production is Edmond Rostand’s hilariously witty comic- romance, Cyrano De Bergerac. Set in modern day France, the play follows Cyrano (Dan Wolfe), the charismatic swordsman-poet with the absurd nose, who hopelessly loves the beauteous Roxane (Sarah Bonner); she, in turn, confesses to Cyrano her love for the handsome but tongue-tied Christian (Anthony Martinez). The chivalrous Cyrano sets up with Christian an innocent deception, with tragic results. The production will be directed by Artistic Director, Richard Mazda. The show is up and running until December 5. General Admission will be $15. Tickets can be purchased online at www.secrettheatre.com or via box office (718) 581-6477.

Please join us on December 5th in celebrating this momentous occasion in our theatre company’s history. All proceeds for the event go to The Queens Players not-for-profit account sponsored under Fractured Atlas, a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that serves as a national community of artists and arts organizations.

For all inquiries about the Queens Players Gala, please contact Artistic Director, Richard Mazda, @ Gala@thequeensplayers.com or call (718) 581-6477.
