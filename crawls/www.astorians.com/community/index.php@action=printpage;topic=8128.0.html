Title: Last Tuesday's Tomato Share
Post by: photomill on October 01, 2007, 01:05:47 PM
Hello!  It's been a while but I thought this is where to unload this one.

Did anyone else have a LOT of bad tomatoes in their tomato share?  Last year, i got my share, then started production on Saturday with my large box of tomatoes.  In the end, there were a few bad ones but I got 16 jars of tomato-based product.

This year, I only got 12.  4 jars may not sound like a lot but when you think of it in terms of diced tomatoes it's considerable.  I threw out a LOT ot tomatoes.  Here's the sick part - there were maggots on a lot of them.  Based on my wife's science background and labwork, she estimated that the maggots were hatched 12-16 days prior to their discovery on Saturday.  YIKES!!

I still have a dozen jars of delicious summery goodness to be cracked open in winter time, but the rot issue added a lot of frustration and time to the whole job.
