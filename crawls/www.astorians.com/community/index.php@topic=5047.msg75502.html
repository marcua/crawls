I think 2-5 pages is really too short for any kind of workshop. I also think that limiting a group to memoir writing would probably mean missing out on some great works!

The last workshop I ran met twice a month. We were limited to 3 mid-length poems/1 long poem or no more than 15 pages of prose. We would start off with a 10 minute free write before taking turns editing. the group had 8 people, including me, and it worked pretty well. We were all serious writers intent on publishing and it worked well. We talked about editing, but also about where to send work, what magazines fit other's styles, things like that. If anyone would be interested in a format like this, I could easily be persuaded to organize.
