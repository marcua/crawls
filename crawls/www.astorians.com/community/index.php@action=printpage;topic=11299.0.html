Title: CSA & Greenmarket Cooking Class
Post by: skoullias on July 12, 2008, 10:55:03 PM
When: Saturday, July 19th. 2pm to 5pm.




Where: <a href="http://www.saiorganics.com/retailer/store_templates/shell_id_1.asp?storeID=6B30A4C706774E65AF516A034403B081" rel="nofollow">Sai Organics[/url] - 30-21 30th Ave. Astoria, NY.




What: A sumptuous meal featuring farm fresh produce from a local farms, prepared by you, your friends, other CSA members, and those that share your palette and predilections toward local, sustainable, equitable agriculture.




Why: Aside from making the commitment to support the farmers that feed us, you want to share your love of cooking with fellow community members. OR you have no idea how to make good use, let alone pronounce, all that produce you're receiving in your share.




Who: Steph K. (graduate of the Natural Gourmet (http://www.naturalgourmetschool.com), NYC personal chef, former baker at Hawthorne Valley Farms (http://www.hawthornevalleyfarm.org/bakery/bakery.htm)).




Tickets are $80 each or $150 for two. Ten dollars of every ticket to benefit The Hunger Action Network of New York State and The New York City Coalition Against Hunger. For more info email skoulliasATgmailDOTcom. Tickets on sale starting Friday, July 4th, at Sais Organics on 30th Ave between 30th and 31st St;  30th Ave between 36th and 37th St; and <a href="mailto:skoullias@gmail.com?subject=CSA/Greenmarket Cooking Class" rel="nofollow">online[/url].
