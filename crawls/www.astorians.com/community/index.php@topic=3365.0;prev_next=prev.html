Green Mountain Energy - Beware!!!
« on: October 10, 2011, 01:33:51 PM »
Beginning of August, a Green Mountain Energy rep was canvassing the building. Ordinarily I am quite the cynic but he was very sincere and assured me that I would save money.  Just like everyone else, that's what I'm aiming to do and agreed to try it. 
 
I received my first bill this past Friday and almost fainted...the bill is almost twice as much as any I have EVER received at this time of year!!!! The cost of "supplying" the energy cost as much as the energy itself ($75/81).  I just got off the phone with their customer service department to cancel and was informed that it will be "one or two more billing cycles" before I am back to Con Edison for good.  Jeeezzz...just putting it out there so others don't make my mistake...don't fall for their promises of saving because it is complete BS!!!!
