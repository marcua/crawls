After reading all of the great reviews here, I decided to take my bf to Vesta last night for his birthday. And I have to say, the meal definitely lived up to (if not exceeded) all of the hype.  :mrgreen:

We started off with the dish of olives & caper berries. Then he had the cracked wheat salad, I had the fennel & orange salad. Both salads were great (and the portions were a bit more generous than I was expecting, which was nice). The only thing that perplexed me was that I couldn't detect any fennel in my salad (it has a pretty distinct flavor). When I asked about it, I was told that the fennel was "shaved very thin", but for the life of me I couldn't taste it. But it was a very good salad anyway.

For entrees, my bf had the gnocchi with mushroom cream sauce, and I had the salmon with lentils and prune reduction. WOW-- both were fantastic. The gnocchi was tender and fluffy, the mushroom sauce very mushroom-y, and it had some toasted pine nuts on top which gave it a nice texture. The salmon was cooked perfectly-- tender, with a nice crispy crust around the edges. The sweetness of the prune sauce balanced nicely with the lentils. For sides, we got the grilled asparagus and creamy polenta-- both very nice. With all of these great dishes around me on the table, I couldn't decide where to stick my fork :)

Then of course, we had to get the jesus cake... and oh. my. god. YUM. I've never had a dessert quite like it. It was kind of like a spice cake, but not overly spiced. Nice toffee flavor. The cream on the side was perfect-- not overly sweet, which was a nice balance with the sweet toffee sauce. The cake was moist and warm, and had a nice little crust on top. Had a cappuccino too, which was really good.

Also, nice wine list, and I dig the "shot" option... you can order by the bottle, glass, or "shot" (a 2 oz. portion for about half the price of a glass). I often can't drink 2 full glasses of wine, so I love that I have the option to have just a little more, without having to buy a second full glass that I know I won't finish. Or, if you want to taste a bunch of different wines, that gives you a good option. Maybe Vesta should look into introducing flights?

Very happy to have this place in the neighborhood. We will definitely be back!
