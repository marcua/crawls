There is a roasted chicken sandwich (pressed, I believe) that has corn in it -- and I've gotten that several times and enjoyed it. I've tried a couple other sandwiches and enjoyed those (brisket maybe?), but don't get food from here much anymore. And definitely don't dine there because I assume it's more of a bar than restaurant (e.g. somewhere I can take my kid). I understand a lot of people like this, though, and don't like kids in restaurants. But I sure do appreciate places (e.g. Bareburger, Beer Garden) that serve booze and appeal to adults while still feeling very family-oriented. I am very curious how the new expanded Butcher Bar space might fill this niche too (will it serve booze now too?).
