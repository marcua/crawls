There are a lot of good bars around 34th St.  Niles, on 7th Ave, between 30th and 31st is really kind of sleek and has good food as well. Or Tir Na Nog on 8th between 33rd and 34th.  33rd st between 6th and 7th has a few pubs; Stout, Blarney Rock, Joe O's.

One of my favorite restaurants is on 28th between Park and Lexington, The Black Duck.  Its a cool space with good food and good people.  Check out their menu :http://www.menupages.com/restaurantdetails.asp?areaid=0&restaurantid=3097&neighborhoodid=0&cuisineid=0

Or if you're looking to stay further north, Dos Caminos is a good Mexican option on 50th and 3rd.

Ate with sis and her husband @ Candelight 79 (just off Lex on 79th St.) last night. Vegetarian but not vegan. Gorgeous, cozy setting, delicious and filling food. Not cheap though!

I had Moroccan chickpea cakes with apricot (?) chutney which was dry but nice, and the chutney was to die for. We shared some mashed potatoes (typical), sauteed mushrooms (okay), polenta fries (looked like mozzarella sticks), organic wine (very nice), veggie risotto (yum!), dumplings (droolll) and then some Kahlua flan

The dishes that were good were very very good; the others were a bit bland BUT I could've been more generous with my use of the accompanying sauces so maybe I wasn't doing them right! Truly a delightful dinner experience.
