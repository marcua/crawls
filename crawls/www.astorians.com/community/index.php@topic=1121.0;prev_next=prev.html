The Astorians Underground Reading Group (AURG!) gathers weekly at the Freeze Peach Cafe to appreciate the written word.

We are a group of passionate readers (and not necessarily writers) who wish to share the ideas, influences, and words we find compelling.  Participants encourage each other to bring short passages from all kinds of writing- fiction, non-fiction, poetry, prose- to read aloud to the group.

By doing this, we hope to find new and exciting ideas to discuss, to get inspired, and to learn different ways of expression.

Come one, come all!  Tea will be drunk!  Words will be spoken!  8:30pm, every Tuesday night! :P  :P :P
