The Warwick Flea Market Committee is pleased to announce that the Warwick co-op on 7612 35th Ave. will hold its first flea market on Saturday, May 9th!  We are welcoming vendor applications now, so please email warwick.flea.market@gmail.com for an application.

    * Cost per Table:  $30.00
