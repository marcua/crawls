Why must I love you so?

I had been eyeing you at Natural Market for a few weeks now, resisting your purchase because I already had a couple chocolate bars in my cabinet. Recently, though, I ran out of chocolate. I could no longer justify not purchasing you.

Yeah, enough of that, anyway, I bought these cookies called Jimmy's Cookies that are sold at Natural Market. I got the Dutch Chocolate Chocolate Chunk kind. They are everything I could ever want in a cookie. I was hoping they wouldn't be as delicious as they are, so in that respect they are disappointing. And at $3.99, they aren't even that expensive compared to other all-natural cookies, and they are definitely much more delicious than a lot of other ones I've tried.

Also, Natural Market carries Theo brand chocolate bars which are similarly delicious as far as chocolate bars go, and they're cheaper there than anywhere else I've seen them. I recommend the Bread & Chocolate kind, the Hazelnut Crunch kind, and the Curry kind if the idea of a chocolate & curry bar intrigues you. It is very potent. I do not recommend the kind with figs and fennel.

It is the Natural Market on Broadway and 46th Street, right outside the 46th St R/V stop. These cookies are located towards the front of the store, not in the cookie aisle.
