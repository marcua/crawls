Hello everyone!

As some of you already know, I am a Music for Aardvarks teacher with classes in Astoria, Sunnyside/Woodside and Forest Hills.  Music for Aardvarks is a hip music class for babies and toddlers aged 6 months through 4 years.  Our Spring semester begins in mid-April and I would like to invite anyone who is interested to come to a free trial class.

In Astoria:
Steinway Reformed Church
Corner of Ditmars and 41st Street.
Monday, March 31: 9:30, 10:30, 11:30
Friday, April 4: 9:30, 10:30
Monday, April 7: 9:30, 10:30, 11:30
Friday, April 11: 10:30, 11:30

Classes for the Spring semester begin the week of April 14.

For more information about the program, as well as a complete Spring 2008 schedule and registration information, please visit http://www.musicforqueens.com.

Please RSVP by phone (212-769-6812) or email (info@musicforqueens.com) if you would like to attend one of the trial classes and let me know which day and time you are interested in.  Feel free to contact me with any questions you may have as well.

Many Thanks!

Allison
