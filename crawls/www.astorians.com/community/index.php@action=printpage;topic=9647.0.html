Title: Re: Marino's Fish Place
Post by: Pulled pork on February 28, 2008, 06:26:14 PM
Just a bit more information on this topic:  It is not because of retiring.  They were talking about a few things. 
The bad economy and how it is affecting their retail and wholesale business.
How the old timers that used to line up at the end of the week for weekend fish dinners are mostly gone.
The younger people moving in mostly eat out or want pre-prepped dishes (like Whole foods). Or want single serving portions only.
And of course leases are going to sky rocket when the come up for renewal.

Most of the great old school markets in Astoria will tell you the same thing.  It's a shame, these markets and people are half the reason I want to live here.

So next time you pass one of the old family run food shops, remember this, go buy some great products and cook your heart out.
