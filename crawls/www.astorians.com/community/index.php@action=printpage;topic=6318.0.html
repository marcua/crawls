Title: Wedding March for Marriage Equality - Saturday, May 19, 11 a.m.
Post by: mcdirk on May 12, 2007, 12:30:26 PM
Join Marriage Equality New York (MENY) for the Wedding March, going over the Brooklyn Bridge from Manhattan's Foley Square to Brooklyn's Cadman Plaza.  We will have brief remarks before we start the march followed by a rally/show in Cadman Plaza following the march (featuring Astoria's own Candy Samples and the Gender Offenders!).  The festivities start at 11 a.m. in Foley Square (near the Courts) in Manhattan - take the 4,5,6 to Brooklyn Bridge/City Hall, the march commences at 12 noon and we should be done before 3 p.m.
Hope you can join us!
