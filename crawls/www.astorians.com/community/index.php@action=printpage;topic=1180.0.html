Title: 24 Parking w/trailer
Post by: merm on December 01, 2005, 01:41:18 PM
You'll get a ticket if you park a commercial vehicle on the street overnight. But you might want to try the public school parking lot if it's the weekend, or the parking lots of one of these churches around here. Of course you'd need permission to do so, but you might be able to arrange something for free or a reasonable price. Since it's private property you won't get a ticket (unless you don't have permission and they don't want you there.)
