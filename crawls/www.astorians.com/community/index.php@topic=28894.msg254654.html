The two locations are related - at least according to the takeout bags.


That said, I ate there twice over the long weekend (takeout both times) and both times the food was delightful!  On Saturday I did the "Market Table" with grilled chicken, mac-n-cheese and haricot vert with mushrooms.  The owner-type was there and threw in potato wedges and squash for me to try and both were as good as the other items.


Yesterday we ate there again and I had the Cuban Bowl with brown rice and grilled chicken.  It. Was. Amazing.  The portion is huge so I ended up having the second half for lunch today and it was equally as tasty the next day.  My husband had "The Skinny" bowl with the turkey burger and he thought it was good as well. 


I can't say much for the atmosphere since I did takeout each time but it's a nice addition to the neighbourhood.  Hopefully people find it more to their liking over time.
