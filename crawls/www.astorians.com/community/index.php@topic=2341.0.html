How funny that three of us from this board were all there this Sunday.  I got there at around 12:30, waited about 25 minutes for a table, and then about 45 minutes for the food to arrive.  I enjoyed the food and the atmosphere (although quite loud inside), but I wouldn't go back under the same circumstances.  I'm wondering if it's any better at around 9 or 10 AM.
