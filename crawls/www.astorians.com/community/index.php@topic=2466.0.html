The Best Local Car Service?
« on: June 27, 2006, 02:49:21 PM »
We use a car service from our home fairly regularly on the weekends.  No cabs (yellow or otherwise) are ever near our building.

We most often call Hoyt car service.  They've generally been very reliable.  However, the last few times we called, we dealt with drivers that spoke little to no English.  Had no clue of street numbers or well-known local businesses.  And drove recklessly.  It's bad enough we have to listen to their dispatcher speaking exclusively in Spanish over their radios.  But usually WE don't need to speak Spanish in order to get where we need to go!  And not to get into the whole illegal immigrant thing - but it leads you to wonder if these drivers belong here and if they have valid licenses and insurance.

We tried Olympia at a friends suggestion.  We had both good and bad experiences there as well.  Usually the biggest problem with them is the long wait.

Can I hear from some people who use a service that they like and trust?  And why?  I'm happy to regularly pay a higher fare to a car service that employs drivers who can communicate well in English and who are familiar with Astoria streets and local major businesses.  Honestly, some of the best drivers we've experienced are the ones we just hailed on Broadway after a night out.  Unfortunately we can't call them from home.
