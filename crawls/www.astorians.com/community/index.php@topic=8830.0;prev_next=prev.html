A lot of health plans also offer a refund for gym memberships.  I have had this under both Aetna and Cigna.  You must go to the gym at least twice a week and have your gym sign a paper stating you were really there.  Under each of those programs that refund has ranged from $150-$200 2 times a year.  Check the additional benefits area of your policy to see if you are covered under your plan.
