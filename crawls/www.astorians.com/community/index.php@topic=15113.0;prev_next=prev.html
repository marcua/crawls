Price differences - Fresh Start charges high prices
« on: June 01, 2009, 04:16:37 PM »
At the great fruit stand underneath the Ditmars station I found organic eggs, milk and cheese for half the price of the Fresh Start organic mart on 23rd Ave. Eggs were $6.79 at Fresh Start and I got them for about $2.49 at the fruit stand, and the same Applegate Organic cheese was only $3 something at the fruit stand and almost $6 at Fresh Start. I really like Fresh Start and you can find stuff there that you can't find elsewhere in the neighborhood, but if there's something you can get at another place, it's probably cheaper.

Some of the local organic markets really gouge you on the packaged products. I feel like these places (Natural Market on Broadway is another one) are like corner delis, where you can get the fresh food and sandwich-type stuff prepared by the store for a good price, but everything on the shelves is a rip-off. Sai Organics doesn't seem to do this as much. Their cereal is very reasonably priced. I get Nature's Path cereal there for $3.99 whereas the same box is over $5 at Natural Market. Even Trade Fair sells the cereal for more as their organic prices are fairly high as well.

There are two on 30th Ave - the one I go to is between 36th & 37th Street. The other one is between 31st and 29th St. The prices are all over the place at all these stores (certain things are cheaper at Natural Market), so it's best to compare between stores for the specific items you buy if you have time. Oh and don't bother buying any Japanese products at any of these stores. If you want something like soba or seaweed, just go to a Japanese store. You will get a lot more for a lot less.
