Happy Gay Pride
« on: June 27, 2010, 12:18:02 AM »
Well, it's that time of year where I get super excited and cry tears of joy. I never imagined before I moved to NYC of being so happy that I am gay. Being here makes me feel so free and that I can express myself in ways that I never imagined.

It feels like my anniversary, my day of freedom. My day to say that I am all that I can be. That I am beautiful. And nothing can take away that joy.

I work at the Barnes and Noble on Sixth Ave and Eight St. The heart of the parade. Today, I watched them set up the barricades and thought.....this is for me and my brothers and sisters.

I know there are some people that hate us......but today, I don't care what they say. It is my day to stand up and be proud. And proud I am.

HAPPY GAY PRIDE!!!!!!!!!!

I love you all!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
