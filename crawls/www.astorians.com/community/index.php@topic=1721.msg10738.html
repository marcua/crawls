There's a really good meditation class at Grace Lutheran Church on 21st Ave @32nd St on Sundays at 12 noon.  Its based on the Kadampa school of Buddhism, the most accessible to most Westerners, and led by a former Wall Street trader who's very funny.  Normally we go in, sit for a while (1/2 hour or so) to clear our heads, then the leader speaks for a while on that topic, and then we sit again for 1/2 hour or so.  I know he's got another class at Grace Lutheran on Mondays at 7pm, but I haven't been to that one, and I believe its more of a classy-class with books and all.  Hope that helps.
