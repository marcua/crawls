We just had custom window treatments designed by www.CLScustom.com.  Very professional service.  The designer comes to you with ideas and tons of samples and books.  You decide on what you want.  They place the order.  And it takes about 8 weeks for them to custom make the materials if you get draperies and curtains.  Blinds may be different.  They have all the major blind manufacturers.  I asked here and looked around town.  Could not find a single local business who would do this.  These people are in Nassau.  Not cheap.  But if you want top quality and custom fit, I would give them a call.  And if you like the idea of professional installation.
