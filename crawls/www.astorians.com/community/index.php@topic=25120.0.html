What do we do, folks? Strand's noise level is out of control. Every night this week there has been live bands blaring music well past the 10pm noise ordinance limit. Total disregard for the rules and the residents of the neighborhood. I call 311 but we can see how much good that does. Or maybe, eventually, it will do some good? After enough complaints, will some action be taken? Or is the only option to move?

But Strand's noise is totally unacceptable. It angers me that they continue to get away with it.


What do we do?
