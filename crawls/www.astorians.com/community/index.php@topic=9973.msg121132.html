I'm sorry to hear about your doggie. I've had dogs and cats in my life since I was five and have had to say goodbye to quite a few. The very worst experience was having my 4-year old cat die suddenly in the middle of the night while she was sleeping next to me. The veterinarian said it was probably an aneurysm of some sort and there was nothing that I could have done to prevent it, but I still obsessed about her behavior in the previous month, searching for clues. In my grief, I adopted a kitten almost 2 months later thinking it would make me feel better. In hindsight, that was not a good idea. We never really bonded, though she got very attached to my then-boyfriend.

In the end it worked out: the 2nd cat stayed with my ex-boyfriend when we broke up, and now I have a very sweet pair of cats, brother and sister, living with me. They came along at just the right time and I am so glad I took them in. You will know when the time is right, when the right dog licks your hand and gives you the "take me home with you" look.

-Deborah
