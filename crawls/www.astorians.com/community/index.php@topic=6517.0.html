Brokers have plenty of apartment buildings to show, believe me, they'll have everything there is to show you.  Fee should only be one month - some will try and bs you into paying more than that.  Credit reports are only 20-30 dollars, don't let them charge you more.  Seems $1300 is the going rate for a one-bedroom these days, but there are deals out there to be found. I and all my friends have found one-bedrooms under $1000 in the last two years.  It's hard, but they are out there. Be persistent.  I think you can find something for $1200.
