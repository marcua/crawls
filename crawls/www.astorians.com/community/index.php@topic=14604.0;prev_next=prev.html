Marriage Equality Bill
« on: October 21, 2009, 05:00:32 PM »
If you live in Astoria, your State Senator is Senator George Onorato.  Who knew??  He is on the fence when it comes to marriage equality.  A fence can't be a very comfortable thing to sit on these days.  Please call or write your district 12 senator because the only way New York will allow same-sex marriage is if we get our leaders off the fence.  We can march on Washington until we're blue in the face, but it's more important to press our local officials to make the big changes in Albany.  Call Senator Onorato's office at 518-455-3486 and tell him to vote YES on the Marriage Equality Bill 4401.  Or contact him online at http://www.nysenate.gov/senator/george-onorato

Pick up the phone or e-mail today.  Let's all do our part to move New York state toward full equality. 

Thank you.
