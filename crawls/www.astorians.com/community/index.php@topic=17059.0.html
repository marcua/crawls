Very Large 1BR available in 3BR share!
« on: January 06, 2010, 09:09:01 PM »
Our roommate is off to travel before heading to grad school and we are looking for someone to take his place starting asap.

Specifics:

The available room is the largest in a 3BR, 1 BA apartment located on the first floor of a recently-renovated house in a safe, quiet residential part of Astoria, Queens. Currently there are two guys in the apartment - one is a grad student, the other is a night DJ and salsa instructor. We are looking for a third guy (though we might consider a cool girl) who is a) gainfully employed (and can provide proof), b) clean (very important! we try to keep the apartment in good order), c) not a smoker, hardcore drug user, or crazy, and d) willing to chip in to pay for communal apartment expenses (i.e. cleaning supplies, toilet paper, etc. - we also buy some food products in bulk from Costco, but you don't have to opt in for that if you don't want).

The apartment features a spacious and fully furnished living room with beautiful couches, coffee table and wireless internet, as well as an awesome bathroom and a solid kitchen with all your cooking necessities. Sorry, no pets. You can bring a TV if you want, but you'll have to keep it in your room and pay for the cable or satellite yourself.

- Rent for the room is 850.00 per month (includes heat, hot water, gas).

- Internet comes out to $10.00 per month (wi-fi)

The room is LARGE (15ftx15ft). The guy in there now has a full size bed on a wooden frame, a desk, etc. One drawback: the room has no closet, but the guy who is moving out will probably be willing to sell you the portable closet he's been using (or you could just bring your own).

-It's a 12 minute walk to the NW trains, a little bit longer to the R,V, and about a 6 minute walk to catch the M60. Laundromats are on every block (the closest one right on the corner 30 ft from the house). We're near everything from the grocery stores to gyms to fun nightlife, but also far enough away from the loud business of the main streets. The nearest main street is Steinway Street, so if you like Middle Eastern food, hookah, and the like, you're golden.

If you have a car:

This is also an ideal spot if you have a car and like the option of both driving or taking a train/cab as finding parking in this area is pretty easy and you would only have to move your car one day per week for street cleaning.

RECAP - About you:

We are looking for someone (1 PERSON) who is 21-30 years old, easy going, respectful, courteous, and considerate. You must pay rent and utilities on time (last day of every month for the following month). There is NO SMOKING in the apt, and we currently aren't allowed to have pets, so NO CATS, DOGS, ETC. We are looking for someone to move in asap, ready to pay first months rent and the security deposit. There is no lease to be signed. So, if you're interested, email and tell us a bit about yourself. If we like what we read, we'll get back to you asap.
