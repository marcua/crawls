Title: Re: Jackson Heights
Post by: daisy on December 06, 2006, 09:44:50 PM
I went looking for to buy there a year ago.  The apartments are really big and for the most part very nice.  Views can be lacking if you don't face the street.  I probably would have bought if they weren't all coops with high mtn fees.  But I liked the areas especially 74th St area and 81st Elmhurst.   81st reminded me of Steinway St with the shopping, and there are tree lined streets away from the main drags in both areas, and the developments have nice courtyards.  Honestly, it reminded me of Astoria and Woodside alot.  Lots of people are moving there and the commute can be pretty good.  Good restaurants too.  Time Out had some articles on it recently, maybe you should do some research online, and go looking at apartments - there's a lot to see, ads abound.  I beleive the area will pick up and already is.  Good luck.
