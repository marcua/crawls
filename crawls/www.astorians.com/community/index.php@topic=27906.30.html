I know a lot about these schools from being an active parent in the community and I moved my gen ed child from 122 into a G&T program at another school.    All these school have their issues but wanted to address a few things people mentioned. The PA is phenomenal. 122 is a good school but the administration is a bit more set in their ways as opposed to 166 where there is a younger, more up and coming principal who is more receptive to parental involvement.
