I went here for the first time last Friday.  Very nice experience.  The order location may be changed because the bagels, cashier, etc was all to the back of the shop.  The entire middle area is not yet in use.  I think there will eventually be a grill and hot food.  They had the most amazing looking homemade crumb cake.  I didn't buy it but it looked so good.  One variety had a raspberry jam incorporated under the thick layer of crumbs.  My whole grain bagel with lox spread was delicious.  Great lox spread (not phony smoke flavor but the real thing).  The bagel was also truly whole grain, with a lot of visible flecks of bran throughout.  Very nice people.  Fun, pleasant, and very interested in how I liked everything.  Great iced coffee too.  I'll be back.  They have limited seating so this is not a place for someone looking to sit for hours with a laptop.  To me, that's a good thing.
