Trains leaving Ditmars w/out announcements or warning
« on: January 08, 2009, 11:29:01 PM »
I have had an ongoing issue with the subway trains, the N & W, at the Ditmars platform for some time now.

It was to a new degree of absurdity earlier this week.

I got on the platform about 1:00 PM and there was a W train there with the doors open. I got on and heard the auto voice announcement that the doors were closing.

Goody! I'm kinda running late here.

The doors close and an N train pulls in. Doors open, doors shortly there after close. The train leaves.

Damn!

Not one word was spoken on the W train I was on....who knows about the platform, there are no speakers at the end of the platform.

Another N train pulls in, doors open... I stick my head outside and see people getting on the train from the mid platform.

Oh no, not again.

I walk across to look inside. The doors close on the W train I just stepped off of ... no announcements ...and it leaves the station. I now stay on the N train for about 10 minutes before it finally leaves.

I've just spent about 20 minutes on three different trains on the Ditmars platform and have gone NOWHERE!

Who do I report this to.

The civil servant "20 year attitude" is alive and well at the Ditmars Blvd. Station!


Grrrrrrr!  :x
