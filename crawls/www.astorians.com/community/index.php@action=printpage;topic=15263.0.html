Title: Citywide music festival comes to Astorias largest community garden!
Post by: skoullias on June 16, 2009, 04:40:14 AM
Hello Friends and Neighbors,
 
Please join us for a music event in Two Coves Community Garden this weekend.  The event is part of Make Music New York, a unique festival of
free concerts in public spaces throughout the five boroughs of New York City.  The event will be:

Sunday, June 21 from 4-7PM

Musicians include
the Rude Mechanical Orchestra,
Hilary Hawke,
Two Coves Community Garden's own Mimi LaValley and Hubby Jenkins

The music will span many genres including marching band and dance troupe, kids songs with banjo and traditional American blues guitar.

Please note that we have limited seating available, so it would be appropriate to bring blankets or small portable chairs to rest your legs. For more info about the garden and how to get there, please visit www.twocovescommunitygarden.org

Hope to see you there!
