It might be hard to find such a bank... (Although I see some people have suggestions for accounts that require a large minimum balance.) You might be better off trying to find the other way around. I bank through a credit union. They charge $1 for all outside-the-network transactions (the CU is in central Illinois, so I don't make any inside-the-network transactions anymore), but they're on the "Co-op Network" of ATMs, and so as long as I can find such an ATM, I know I'm just paying $1, and not $1.75 or $2.50 or whatever other ridiculous ATM fee there might be. And all credit union ATMs are on that network, as well as all of the ATMs in McDonald's restaurants! If anyone sees me ducking into a McDonalds, it's to use their ATM, not to ruin my foodie cred. :)
