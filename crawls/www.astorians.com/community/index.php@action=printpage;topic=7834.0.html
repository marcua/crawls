Title: September Concert Brings Free music to Astoria Tomorrow 9/11.
Post by: AllisonTartalia on September 10, 2007, 04:51:28 PM
Hello everyone!

Here at long last is a full listing of all the September Concert events taking place in Astoria!  All of these events are free and open to the public.  Many thanks to the local businesses who have offered their spaces for these concerts and to the excellent musicians who have donated their performances.  Please be sure to check them out!

 
Background
On September 11th, hundreds of musicians will fill the skies with music by participating in THE FIFTH ANNUAL SEPTEMBER CONCERT, a music festival launched in 2002, with the goal to bring people together, to reaffirm our hope for peace and to celebrate life and our universal humanity.

The September Concert adhere to the following three principles:
FREEDOM: All venues and musicians are free to design and organize their own concert.
EQUALITY: All music and all musicians are treated equally, regardless of the genre and background.
ACCESSIBILITY: All music must be offered free of charge - Venues donate their space, and musicians play for free.
