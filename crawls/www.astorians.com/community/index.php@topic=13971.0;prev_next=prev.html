Some decent, modest restaurants I've frequented on the Upper West Side that I recommend include Arte Cafe, Carmines, and Coppolas (Italian); Isabellas (American); Roppongi  (Japanese); Land Thai (Thai/Asian); Arties (Jewish deli, best for lunch, huge sandwiches and great soup); Cafe Con Leche (Cuban); Good Enough to Eat (American comfort food).  This is only the tip of iceberg; there a dozens of restaurants up and down Amsterdam, Broadway and Columbus.
