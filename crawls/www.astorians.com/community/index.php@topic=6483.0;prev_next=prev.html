So- I've ventured into the new seafood place!
My boyfriend and I bought a LOT of tilapia fillets there last night- for a dinner party and they were wonderful- so fresh that they didn't even have that fishy fish smell.....if ya know what I mean. The store itself was a bit much for me smell wise- in fact I walked out after about one minute to grab some fresh air and left my boyfriend to fend for himself!
But the fish looked wonderful and ours tasted great! Plus the prices seemed really reasonable for the quality of the fish. The only thing that really got to me was the tank with the large live fish....I know, I know- I do eat the stuff, so I'm being a hypocrite but I got all weepy seeing the fish in a tank, directly below their um, siblings above......but you don't get much fresher than live fish I suppose! They had the fillets, whole fish, a frozen section, and also the fish n' chips section where you could order fried seafood:)
I think there was a deal when we went- something like 30popcorn shrimp for $4.99- not too shabby!

anyway- I'll definitely go back and get more fish! I recommend it if you like seafood- think you'll be really happy with the shopping there
