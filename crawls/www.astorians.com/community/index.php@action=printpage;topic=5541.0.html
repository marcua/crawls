Title: Re: A bank that doesn't charge for ATM use
Post by: merm on March 17, 2007, 10:55:49 AM
Yeah. Just to reiterate:

Every time you use an ATM you can get hit with two possible fees:
1. YOUR bank may charge a fee.
2. The bank who owns the ATM may charge a fee.

Usually banks don't charge their own customers to use their own ATM's. But, most banks do charge one or both fees to use OTHER bank's ATM's.

I was with Commerce for about 2 years when they changed their policy and stopped reimbursing the fees charged when I used other bank's ATM's. So I switched to WAMU as they have lots of ATM's throughout the city that are close to where I live/work so I can just use their own ATM's and not pay any fees at all.
