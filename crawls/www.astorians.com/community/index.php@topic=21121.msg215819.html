Had a great meal here last night, went with a Pimm's Cup for a drink which was really refreshing (as promised) along with the pork Boudain balls (work of art, excellent), fried chicken (very juicy, lots of flavor) and an extra side of collards (which were also tasty). My lady got the broiled oysters with mac and cheese which were also excellent. Good to see they had Abita on tap, but they were out of the amber unfortunately.


My one critique would be the french fries I got with the fried chicken were just average french fries you could find anywhere, nothing really special at all, will probably stick with the collards or maybe okra in the future.


Our waitress was great, a total character, also, I really enjoyed the spicy peanuts!


Our waitress mentioned they are closing next week to revamp the menu a little and transition into a 6 day a week schedule with brunch! Can't wait, I will be back!
