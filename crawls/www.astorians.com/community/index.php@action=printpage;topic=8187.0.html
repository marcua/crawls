Title: Pizza Siciliana at Michaelangelo's II
Post by: round2 on October 05, 2007, 04:32:25 PM
Okay, this has turned into something of an addiction - the Sicilian salad (celery, tomatoes, onions, basil) and the Pizza Siciliana with eggplant, tomatoes, ricotta (what am I leaving out?).  Sitting outside in the back and having this combo with an occasional glimpse of an Amtrack train wafting by, what a life!

The pasta specials have been very good, too by the way.  We will have to push ourselves to venture outward on their menu.  Haven't tried the take-out or delivery yet - I think that menu's a little different.
