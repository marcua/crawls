Hells, Hells Gate Social Yeah!

That's right!  CRIMINOSO is back at the Hell Gate Social, this Thursday, with their super electro-jazz freak-out music!  Come hear some great music at a great venue.  All original compositions by Mr. Dan Kendall, and we'll have a great trombonist, Andrew Hunter, who plays with the Charles Mingus band!

2 Sets: 9PM & 10PM, NO COVER!!

Take the N or W train towards Queens to the Astoria Boulevard stop.  Once you get off of the train, walk West on Astoria Boulevard until you get to 12th St. (about a 7 minute walk).

Criminoso is a Nu-Jazz band, blending Jazz, Brazilian, and Electronic Music.
The Band: Dan Kendall - bass, Anthony Lanni - guitar, Mike Sapirstein - sax, Sam Kulik - trombone (Andrew Hunter will be subbing for Sam on Thursday's gig)
