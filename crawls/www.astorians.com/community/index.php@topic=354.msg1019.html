I'd be willing to sign a petition.

Another option is to file a complaint by dialling 311.  There used to be a direct phone number to file complaints, but it's been pretty much taken over by 311 in the past year or so.  If for some reason 311 doesn't work, you can try the City DEP at (718) 699-9811.

I filed at least three complaints against the ice cream trucks on our block last year, with a result of at least one ticket being issued.  A short time later, the most annoying driver (started in February; would come at 11:30pm) disappeared, and the truck was softer and much more tolerable.  It seems louder and more persistent this year, but still not as bad as the worst.

The story you get when you call to complain is mixed, though - one noise control guy told me that technically, the trucks are only supposed to play their tune once per block and not repeat it.  Another time I called, and the guy wouldn't even take the complaint, telling me that they couldn't write a ticket for an ice cream truck.  That, clearly, is not true.
