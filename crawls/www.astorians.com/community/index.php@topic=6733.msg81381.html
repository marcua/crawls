Yup, $4.50. A bit pricey in the standard soda fountain tall tulip shaped glass. But tasty. I miss the "extra / leftover shake" in a small side glass from the blender cup that some places give you. It always irked me to see my presentation glass filled and the left over just dumped down the sink.

The decor reminds me more of the kind of establishment you'd see in a college town like New Paltz. I agree that if it was another nail salon or bank I'd just scream.

Good luck to them, they appear to be off to a good start.
