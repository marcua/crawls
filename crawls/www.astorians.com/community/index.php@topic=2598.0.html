Break

Anybody else been to this pool hall on Broadway between 32nd and 33rd?  I made a second visit tonight and thought I should point it out for those of you who aren't aware of it.

Because it was previously a club (I forget the name of it - Clic?) the decor is hip and amazing - walls that change color, stone-tiled bathrooms, etc.  Only now the place has been turned into a giant wall-to-wall game hall, with over a dozen pool tables, ping pong, darts, etc.

Drinks are reasonable, ranging from beers starting at $3.50 for an off-night, and up to $10 or so for a cocktail.  They provide food from one of the cafes downstairs, though I didn't catch which one, and you rent the tables by the hour.

After time spent waiting for and hogging pool tables in crowded bars in Manhattan, this one is a nice change - every time I've been there it's been fairly laid back, spacious, uncrowded, and with a friendly staff. 

Not sure if people even realize this little gem is up there, but it's definitely something else to make our neighborhood unique!  I highly recommend it - it also might be fun for a group outing sometime.
