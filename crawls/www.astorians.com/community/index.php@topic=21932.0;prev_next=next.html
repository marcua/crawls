   BodyTalk First Aid:  Tapping Out Cortices
 
 Monday, November 7, 2011, 6:15 PM
      Discovery Lane Daycare:  2717 27th Street, Astoria, NY 
        Tap Out Cortices is a self-healing technique from the BodyTalk System that is simple to learn and apply. School-aged children and their parents have found that this brain-balancing technique helps students concentrate, feel more peaceful, and get over colds more quickly. They have also use it to reduce stress, relieve pain, improve sleep, and heal more quickly from injuries and sickness.

Join Jean Menges, a licensed acupuncturist and certified BodyTalk practitioner, in a discussion and demonstration of using the Tap Out Cortices technique. You and your children will enjoy having this wonderful tool to help yourselves any time, anywhere!

Topics Covered:

    * Brief introduction of the brain/body connection
    * How parents and children can balance their brains every day
    * Demonstration of the Tap Out Cortices technique
    * Achieving instant reduction in stress and relief of pain
    * The benefits of the BodyTalk System for children
 
 Please note that we have extended the time of the monthly evening meetings to accomodate more schedules, so if you need to come early and leave early, or arrive late and stay til the end that is no problem.  Coming for a part of it is better than not coming at all.
 
 Child care will be provided in an adjacent area at $4 per child.  Children may alternatively sit with their mother during the meeting (e.g. in-arms babies or children over 12) or attend with an adult dedicated to their care.
   



The Holistic Moms Network is a 501(c) 3 non-profit organization connecting parents who are interested in holistic health and green living. Our mission is to generate national awareness, education, and support for holistic parenting and green living by providing nurturing, open-minded, and respectful local community networks that encourage families to share these ideals and learn from each other.  To learn more about the Queens Chapter of the Holistic Moms Network, become a member, become a local sponsor, or check our calendar for upcoming meetings and events, please visit http://queensny.holisticmoms.org, To RSVP for one of our events, or for more information, please call 917-683-6062 or email queensholisticmoms@gmail.com.
