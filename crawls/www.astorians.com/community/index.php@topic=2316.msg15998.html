New Hellgate CSA Flickr group

Hello all,

I've started a new group on Flickr for Hellgate CSA.  Feel free to join and post pictures -- pictures of your share, pictures of the distribution, pictures from the farm trips, pictures of how you're cooking the food, pictures of the finished meals, and whatever else you think we'd like to see!

The group address is http://www.flickr.com/groups/hellgatecsa/

Flickr is a photo-sharing/social-networking website.   You can host your photos, blog them, get prints and other merchandise, and share your photos.  (To get a free account, go to http://www.flickr.com/signup/ )

Hope to see you there!
