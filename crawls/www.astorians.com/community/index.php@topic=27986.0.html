Astoria has so many options for date night restaurants for mixed vegetarian and non-vegetarian couples (like me and my hubby). We rarely can't find decent options for a vegetarian anywhere, though the new oyster places and bbq places may not work too well for date nights.


Some places that come to mind (in addition to Seva) include:


  • Maizal - Mexican on 34th Av near 32nd St
  • eTaverna - a favorite of ours, Greek on 23rd Av at 27th St
  • Vesta - Italian on 30th Av at 21st St
  • Trattoria L'Incontro - Italian on 31st St near Ditmars
  • Queens Comfort - quirky, on 30th Av near Steinway
  • Kebab Cafe - funky, special place on Steinway near 25th Av where the chef/owner, Ali, shops daily and makes Alexandrian/Egyptian inspired food magic based on what's fresh and good
  • Watawa - sushi that's a cut above the Astoria norm, with a good veg sushi combo plate, on Ditmars near 33rd St
  • Piccola Venezia - old school Italian, well done, on 28th Av near 42nd St
  • Ovelia - modern-ish Greek on 30th Av near 34th St
  • Milkflower - serious pizza and great salads on 31st Av near 34th St
Lots more, but that's a start. I'm leaving out plenty of good ones I've tried, and some I'd like to try...

Over on We Heart Astoria we recently published this guide to global vegetarian food in Astoria, written by Anne Noyes Saini. I think it's a fantastic list! One of my favorites on the list is La Cabana's queso taco, which sounds simple and basic (and maybe boring) but is truly amazing. One of the best vegetarian dishes (or at least snacks) in Astoria, IMO. Their meaty tacos are also wonderful.
