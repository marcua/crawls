========================================================
“Figure al fresco”
May 21, 2:30PM to 4:30PM
   
A traditional figure drawing class for beginners and advanced students. Models strike a series of poses, beginning with short, minute-long gestures that students capture in fast, impromptu sketches. Poses lengthen to five and ten minutes, and each session ends with a 20-minute pose. Students work in black and white using pencil and/or charcoal. Artist Enid Braun provides instruction and critiques.

Materials are provided.

Price: free

This event takes place at:
Rector Park East
- access: Rector Place 

Directions: Take the 1 train to Rector Street; 2, 3, 4, 5, J, M, or Z to Wall Street; 4 or 5 to Bowling Green; W or R to Rector Street or Whitehall Street.

===========================
Elements of Nature Drawing    
May 21, 11:30AM to 1:30PM
   
Participants work in color and/or black and white, rendering subjects of their own choice in a variety of media: pencil, charcoal, watercolor, and pastel. Artist Enid Braun provides one on one instruction and guidance as well as botany instruction. These sessions provide the perfect opportunity for people who have always wanted to draw, but have been hesitant to do so, as well as for those wanting to enhance their skills. A perfect way to spend the lunch hour and escape the concerns of work.

Materials are provided.

Price: free

This event takes place at:
Robert F. Wagner, Jr. Park
- access: Battery Place.
1-212-267-9700 

Directions: Take the 1 train to Rector Street; 2, 3, 4, 5, J, M, or Z to Wall Street; 4 or 5 to Bowling Green; W or R to Rector Street or Whitehall Street.

=======================================================
Drawing in the park
May 31, 10:30AM to 12:00PM
   
Students work in pencil, charcoal, watercolor and pastel, and are encouraged to explore subjects ranging from detailed flower studies to vast panoramas of the Hudson River and New York City skyline. Instruction and critiques are provided by artist/educator Larry Dobens.

Materials are provided.

Price: free

This event takes place at:
South Cove
- access: West Thames Street 

Directions: Take the 1 train to Rector Street; 2, 3, 4, 5, J, M, or Z to Wall Street; 4 or 5 to Bowling Green; W or R to Rector Street or Whitehall Street.
