Yes, I'm still alive.  Sometimes these small, unfancy places are better than the places with higher prices and lots of atmosphere.  Before this I used to go to Yajai Thai on 30th Avenue.  Of all the Thai restaurants on 30th Avenue, that one is the least fancy, but in my opinion, it has the best food.  I went to Wave Thai when they opened and wasn't impressed.  I got the lunch special shrimp with curry and got a tiny bowl of curry with 4 shrimp.  I was still hungry when I finished eating.  This places gives a good size portion and the food was delicious.  I can't wait to go back and try something else.  I recommend that you try it and don't judge it by the way it looks. :-)
