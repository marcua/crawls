It's pretty big, and the wi-fi is free. They may be having financial issues, though, because the last time I was there I asked how late they stay open and they said, "Normally, 11pm, but right now, 5-6 pm."

Doesn't surprise me, though, because their location is a little weird.

When I stopped by a few days ago the interior had been reorganized. They had some books in a shelf, a few tables with chess/checker sets (nice ones, looked like glass pieces) set up, and even had some canaries somewhere (although I didn't see them - could have been an audio tape for all I know).

Their prices were pretty reasonable - a mocha, for example, was $2.15, I think. The one I had was pretty good, too, although I'm no connoisseur.

They also seemed to be ok with people bringing in pets - one customer had a medium-to-large dog sitting next to her as she worked on her laptop.
