Title: "THE" Broker to use - Astoria Apartments!
Post by: Aimprescia on September 12, 2011, 07:22:28 PM
I usually do not post reviews but based on the outstanding customer service I received at Astoria Apartments - located on 31st street btwn. Ditmars & 21st Ave - I felt it was the honorable thing to share my experience!

I just graduated college and was looking for a 2 bedroom with my roommate - something close to the train and in a quiet neighborhood.  I specifically told Tasos Makedon, the realtor with whom I worked (and recommend!) that I did not want another college dorm and that I wanted to be near the Ditmars train station.  Sure enough, he showed me a BEAUTIFUL, newly renovated apartment with the sweetest landlords, just a 5 minute walk from the Ditmars train.  Unfortunately, my roommate was not able to view the apartment with me that first day and I knew an apartment this nice was going to go quickly.  Luckily, Taso was able to hold that apartment for me with just a small sum deposit I put down.  As soon as my roommate was able to come and view the place, we signed!

Too good to be true, right??  The first day of apartment hunting I find EXACTLY what I was looking for?  Well - it gets better.  Taso has continued to work with me - AFTER we have paid the security, his fee & signed the lease.  Any other realtor would be done with until it was time to give you the keys.  This is especially true because I know I bothered Taso every other day prior to coming down and viewing an apartment (I like to call it persistence!).  He keeps me updated daily as to the progress of the renovations in our apartment and has served as an integral communication link between the landlord and me. 

I would HIGHLY recommend going to see Taso over at Astoria Apartments.  His office gets the best inventory of apartments and ALL of the agents truly go above and beyond for their clients!!!  Just ask him about the 4 girls & the ladder story!!
