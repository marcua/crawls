Post by: bennyt on November 06, 2006, 10:13:15 AM
One addendum- you asked where the closest place to go would be.  Part of that depends on whether by "Jewish deli," you also mean "kosher deli."  If non-kosher works for you, I suspect that there is something good on the Upper East Side.  Kosher-wise, I've had fantastic sandwiches at Mendy's at 34th and Park: see my post here http://www.astorians.com/index.php?topic=3710.0 and also at Mr. Broadway (38th and Broaday) is quite good, if a bit expensive.  Both places have excellent corned beef, pastrami, chopped liver, etc.  Also, Kosher Marketplace on the Upper West (Broadway and 90th, I think) sometimes has half-price sandwich days, and it's pretty good (not as good as the other two places I mentioned, though).
bennyt
