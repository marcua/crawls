Title: Re: Place to get a digital camera repaired?
Post by: Harlan on November 03, 2008, 06:58:34 AM
This said, cameras (or other consumer electronics) in the $250 range are not really designed to be repaired. If it were under warranty, the company would likely just confirm that it's broken, then give you a new (or refurbished) unit. In fact, you might be able to find a refurbished unit of the same model on eBay for less than the cost of any repair. FWIW, I agree with the Photo-Tech recommendation, if you do decide to get an estimate...
