Title: Indian Restaurant on 30th and 36th--Kurry Qulture
Post by: CrystelH on November 28, 2015, 04:12:23 PM
I walked into this place yesterday and got way more than I expected. The food and drinks are nothing like the usual indian meals I've had before.  They say it's a three course menu for $33 but there's a lot more than just three courses. The mushroom soup I started with wasn't even on the menu. It's "just because". Rice and naan is included and it ends with three indian desserts. I got the goat curry with turnips--never seen it on an Indian menu before and it was delicious. I hope this place is here to stay...Did I mention beer on tap and the bar is open till late?   :cheesy: :cool: :cool: :cool: :grin: :grin:
