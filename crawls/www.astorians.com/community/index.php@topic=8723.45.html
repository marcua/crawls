I want to order Buffalo Wings from somewhere in Astoria tonight. Any suggestions? I like 'em spicy - preferably not breaded... want them delivered so sitting at a bar isn't an option.

I'll pass along my recipe.

Get yourself some chicken wings.  I like to use Western Beef on Northern and Steinway.
Bake at 450 for about an hour.
In a saucepan, heat a stick of butter and a cup of Frank's Red hot.

When the wings are done, toss and yum!!!

Good wings really are that simple and are always a hit at the superbowl party.

I have to thank KidMurdock for this brilliant plan.  It made my Super Bowl a lot more fun last night.  Here's how I did it with a few minor modifications. 

Step 1:  Stop by local supermarket and pick up 1 bottle of Jim Beam's Hot Wing Sauce™ and blue cheese dip.
Step 2:  Also pick up some loose gorgonzola cheese to give the blue cheese dip a little more wallop.
Step 3:  Stop at local Chinese delivery joint on way home, indicate the number of chicken wings I'd be interested in consuming, exchange currency for chicken flesh, transport said chicken flesh and aforementioned groceries home.
Step 4:  Sit down, pop open a beer. 
Step 5:  Get a large bowl from the kitchen, deposit chicken wings into said bowl, slather with Jim Beam's Hot Wing Sauce™.
Step 6:  In a sauce pan, melt down the gorgonzola (add a little milk so it doesn't burn).  Mix in with blue cheese dip.
Step 7:  Return to La-Z-Boy.  Have some beer.  Dig into the wings and watch the game.  Repeat.

Seriously, that was a great plan.  Didn't have to bother with frying the wings and they came out all nice and crispy like I prefer my hot wings to be, then all I had to do was sauce 'em up.  A minimum of effort while still producing the vaunted Wing Effect™ that makes me do little happy dances inside.  Thanks!
