From whyleaveastoria.com

"The Brooklyn Grange rooftop farm project have finally broken installed ground in their home borough Queens atop the Standard Motor Products building  on Northern Blvd.

The one-acre urban farm contains over six-hundred tons of soil and will supply fruits and vegetables to local residents and establishments. They anticipate their first harvest in June and will hold an on-site farmer's market each week. Local restaurant, Vesta Trattoria has also committed to using the vegetation.

The founders are using the social investing website, Kickstarter to help fund the project.
If you're interested in helping fund the project:
http://www.kickstarter.com/projects/1909670623/brooklyn-grange-rooftop-farm
