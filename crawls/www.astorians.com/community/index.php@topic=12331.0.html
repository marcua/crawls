THIS THURSDAY: Welcome Home "Mike Jones", The PHH After Party.

MIKE JONES! HE'S BACK AND ON THE ATTACK. Ok, that was a cheesy rhyme but come to Bungalo this Thursday and share in our good time.

Ok, so seriously. Instead of having half of Astoria (and Beyond) try to fit into a restaurant for a welcome back dinner, we're going to celebrate it over at Bungalo (where we both have a residency) in proper style.

Look for us in the back of Bungalo where the Grey Goose Bottles will be flowing.

When? This Thursday...
After hitting up PHH after work (to get re-enforcments), we're going to head back to Bungalo in Astoria,Qns for Thirsty Thursdays. The music starts the 6pm, party kicks off at 10 and we're going to be fashionably late at 11pm. We go till 4am.

Satisfy your senses and quench your every thirst with us...

See: Astoria has such great diversity that there is something that will catch everyone's eye. wink

Hear: house, electropunk, mashups and funky beats. s___, maybe even 80's and disco. Just no hiphop, no reageton, no salsa, no R&B, no requests. (I mean we love that music and if you do too, you should check out our friday and saturday night parties)

Taste + Smell: Ask for 'A Tall 10' {gin}, a Mojito {rum}, Z-Martini {vodka} or a Long Island Ice Tea {???}. We've got a wide range of wine, beers and a fully stocked bar. For your tummy our kitchen is open till 1am. The menu includes fresh salads, quesadilla, fried calamari, shrimp cocktails and pasta dishes. If you get hungry after the kitchen is closed closed, dont fret! You can get the best chicken and beef kebabs in the city on the corner till 5am.

Feel: At home with us. Manhattan Quality. Astoria Location. It's a place where you can dance like no one is watching. The service smiles back and the guests are welcoming.

We're located 10min from midtown on the N/W (Broadway), 15min on the V,R,G (Steinway).
