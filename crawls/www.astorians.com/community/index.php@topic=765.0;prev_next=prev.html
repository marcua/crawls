how a book exchange could work:

seriously, folks, i have the answer:  www.bookcrossing.com

you can register your books there and give them a unique id number.  you can make a note as to who you are giving the book to and whether or not it is a gift or a trade, or whether you expect them to return the book to you or pass it along to someone else. when the recipient has the book they can log on, read your notes, make their own notes, say what they thought of the book, etc.  

i myself am a huge fan of the "wild release" - leaving a book to be found and enjoyed by a stranger.   check out my bookshelf to see if there's anything you're interested.  please note the status of the book - if it is "traveling" or has been marked by another user i do not have the book.

http://www.bookcrossing.com/mybookshelf/enigmacat/page_1/statusfilter_0
