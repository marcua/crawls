The chase sequence takes place beneath the West End subway line, whose proper letter marking in 1971 was a B and as of 2004, D. When equipment for the movie was chosen, the producers insisted on clean cars, and the only available clean cars were normally assigned to the N line and did not have B signs. Consequently, they operated during the movie with an N displayed in the front slot.

quoted from imdb--http://imdb.com/title/tt0067116/trivia

We just watched the "classic" (-ly campy and bad, but funny) medical thriller Extreme Measures (1996), starring Hugh Grant and Gene Hackman and about 15 familiar character actors.
