March Dare to Care sessions
Come and learn more about New York Cares at a 'Dare to Care' session
Dare to Care is a one-hour introductory session that provides a detailed insight into the challenges facing disadvantaged New Yorkers, and New York Cares' unique approach to addressing these challenges. Attendees will hear stories about the people New York Cares has helped, meet our visionary leaders and staff and share their thoughts and ideas with them. This is a great opportunity to learn more about our work and the many ways you can help us address the needs of our city.

If you would like to attend, please RSVP to Amy Wickstein at amy_wickstein AT nycares.org or 212-402-1154.

Monday, March 5 at 7:00 p.m.
Tuesday, March 13 at 12:00 p.m.
Wednesday, March 21 at 8:00 a.m.

Hands On New York Day
Registration is now open for the 13th Annual Hands On New York Day; we're looking for thousands of volunteers to help take spring cleaning to a new level. Volunteer with your fellow New Yorkers to clean, paint and revitalize our city's public spaces including: parks, gardens, playgrounds, community centers, homeless shelters and schools.
