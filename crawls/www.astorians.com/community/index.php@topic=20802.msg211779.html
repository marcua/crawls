 Hi! I am a maternal child Rn who has worked with Dr.Chris and Dr.Mike from Astoria Pediatrics.They are both excellence pediatricians .What some of the other posters have said is true.Dr.Mike is warm,smart and responses well to all children.Dr.Chris is more serious but very capable. I have been going to AP since my daughter was born (12 years old) I usually stick with Dr.Chris and Dr.Mike since Ive worked with them.My daughter have not had visits with the others two MDs so I can not relay any information about them.One very importance point is that these mds have admitting rights to Long Island Jewish Hospital (Schneider Children's Hospital) which is a excellent hospital for children.
    It sounds like your son has hydronephrosis which is more common in boys.After you deliver at the hospital,the nurses and  mds will watch your son closely to see when he urinates  and will most likely do a sonogram to see if there is urine backflow into his kidneys.Hopefully it will resolve before he is born.
   There are many doctors who have graduated from "foreign " medicals school who are just as talented as a American graduates.As a nurse I can honestly say that isn't a special consideration for me  and I don't base my choice of doctors on whether they went to an American school. That being said  ,I can tell you Dr.Bloom and Dr Costin are also excellent pediatricians but I would stick with the group that covers your insurance.If any problem arise with your son ,it could become very expensive if you are "out of network"
    I wish you good luck and a safe happy delivery.
