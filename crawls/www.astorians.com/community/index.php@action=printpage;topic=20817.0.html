Title: Free Baby Sign Language Demo
Post by: My Smart Hands on April 08, 2011, 06:27:06 PM
My Smart Hands will be having a free baby sign language demo on Wednesday, April 13th at 11:00 a.m. at Queensview Coop Apartments' community room, 21-50 33rd Road (use the entrance on the west side of the building, next to the Queensview Nursery School).

Come with your pre-verbal baby (recommended for parents of babies 5 months and up)! We will go over some of the benefits of signing to your hearing baby, learn some everyday signs you can use right away, and sing a couple songs with signs.

RSVP to me at rebecca@mysmarthands.com

The next session of My Smart Hands will begin Wednesday, May 4th at 11:00 a.m. and run through June 29th (no class on May 18).
Find out more about My Smart Hands, the only baby sign language course that has received the "Parent Tested, Parent Approved" Award, at www.mysmarthandsnyc.com (http://www.mysmarthandsnyc.com).
