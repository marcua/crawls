« on: June 11, 2015, 01:01:10 PM »
FYI - I'm saying it here first.  We have company in Astoria.  Have you seen them?  It's the Quaker/Monk Parrots.     I find it exciting. For those who don't know about them, they been around for ages in sections of Bklyn (Bklyn college campus; Greenwood Cemetary to name a few); JFK area and, well basically all over.  They are tough parrots and now making their home in Astoria !  You can Google it to read up on them.


As a person who likes all birds (pigeons included), I started hearing their sounds late January walking to catch the train in the morning. Finally I got to witness them carrying nesting materials (twigs, branches,etc.,).  So my thoughts were confirmed.  Their nests are getting larger ~ there are two of them now.


Why they choose a certain spot for their home remains a mystery.  Do they actually think Astoria has cheaper renting space?
