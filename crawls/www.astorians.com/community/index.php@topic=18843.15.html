I'm really happy to see a post about this - so happy that I finally officially signed up instead of just lurking.

I was telling my bf the other day how awful the park looked.  I went down to read on one of the benches around the pool and was horrified at not only how much trash there was but the number of times I saw others continuing to throw it on the ground or leave it behind.  A lot of it was teenagers and also kids whose parents didn't scold them for littering.  Makes me sad that so many people don't believe in caring for the place that they live. Too many people feel entitled and take it for granted. I'd love to help out in some kind of clean up - I'm also available Aug 1 and could probably get a few more people involved.

One other thing - not only does the trash situation really upset me, but the behavior of some of the young people in our neighborhood is just atrocious.  A weekend or two ago, I witnessed a group of teens on their way to the pool steal ice cream from a vendor who had stepped away for one second to go park his car, when he chased after them they threw the stolen ice cream at his face and a fight nearly broke out.  I know there is little I can do to change these kids ways, but geez, between that, the trash and the spitting incident I just read about on 30th - I'm sad for the next generation.
