I use Newark Airport a lot for international flights.  Mostly because I hate the ordeal it is to get to JFK, and the fact that flights are cheaper to Newark - and heck so is the transportation.  Besides the New Jersey transit train right to the airport which is really easy, there is also a bus that will take you from Port Authority on a 20 minute ride to the airport as well.  I would recommend both options.  http://www.coachusa.com/olympia/ss.newarkairport.asp
