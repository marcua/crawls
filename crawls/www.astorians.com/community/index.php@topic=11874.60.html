I belong to the Rock now, but am switching to Life Health & Fitness (formerly known as Evolution) on 35th Avenue and 36th St.


I just went there Sunday to check on fees. They have different plans - with and without contracts. You can also pay cash upfront for a year or six month stint. I'm planning to do either the six months for $300 or the year for $550. Very clean gym, good equipment. Open 24 hours Monday - Friday, which was important to me.
They also have classes - spinning, yoga, etc.


And no heavy sales pitch. I asked for info, they gave me a printout with the fees clearly listed, no games, which I liked.
