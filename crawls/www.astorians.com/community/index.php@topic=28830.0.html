We've been enjoying Palermo since they opened.  Da Franco's was so bad in so many ways, including the hideous décor.  These people added a nice wine bar up front and tastefully redesigned the entire space.  The food is delicious and as you say, awesome pizzas.  Very reliable delivery too.  They're very strong with seafood.  Try their clams and any fresh fish special.
