The moderators are discussing this right now. Before we create any rule though we might like to see if brokers posting in multiple places becomes a problem. We're of the opinion that it's better to not make rules unless necessary. That being said, I think we're generally okay with brokers participating in the discussion as many of them will have helpful advice. It's when someone just comes in and posts an advertisement in the middle of a conversation that really doesn't speak to the conversation that it's perceived as being in bad taste. Hopefully we'll all find a good balance for the new "Apartments" board that stays true to the "Astorians.com" format of conversation and minimizes the advertisement/spam mentality.

Of course anyone is welcome to post a blatant advertisement in the advertising section. But the general rule here so far is that if someone wants to post outside the business section, it's better if the post be conversational, and less promotional.

A good way to "advertise" I think is to just put your website using a simple text link, (or name, or phone number) in the signature of your profile - which shows at the bottom of every post.
