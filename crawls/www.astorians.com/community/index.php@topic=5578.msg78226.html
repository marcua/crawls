This is outside the neighborhood, but may be of interest to families here.

The Free Synagogue of Flushing's Tot Shabbat program provides a fun, low-key introduction to Jewish traditions and culture for families with small children.

• Classes celebrate Jewish holidays and traditions over the course of the
year through crafts, stories, songs, and foods

• Geared for children 2-5 years old (and their parents!)

• Ten sessions, held Saturday mornings, monthly September 2007 – June 2008

• All are welcome, including interfaith and non-religious families

• Attendance at the first session is free

The Free Synagogue is located at 41-60 Kissena Blvd. in Flushing --- steps away
from the Flushing Library, near the #7 subway. Free parking is available.

For more information, including meeting dates, please contact Lee at lcgould@med.cornell.edu, Julie at julie.inthemix@gmail.com, or the synagogue office at 718-961-0030.
