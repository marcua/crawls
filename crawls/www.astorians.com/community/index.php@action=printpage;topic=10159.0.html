Title: Kick It Old School(Yard) with Wiffleball this Summer!
Post by: ZogSports on April 15, 2008, 04:44:53 PM
WIFFLEBALL (you know,the awesome game with the yellow plastic bat) is coming to Astoria this summer!  Join us for all the retro recess fun - without having to go back to class afterwards!

This weeknight league will kickoff in June and run through August with games one night a week.  We'll put a new twist on the old favorite and add an organized post-game happy hour after every game to add to the fun!

Sign up as a full team, or join as an individual or small group and ZogSports -
the charity-focused, co-ed, social sports league - will place you on a team!

Registration Starts April 21 and we expect this league to sell out FAST!

All the details will be up soon at www.ZogSports.org so check back to get all the info!
