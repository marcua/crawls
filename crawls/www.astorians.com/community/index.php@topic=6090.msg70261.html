There is a flower shop on 38th and Ditmars that sells flats of flowers and vegetables in the spring and summer, its a small selection but nice to be able to pick up locally.  I've bought jalapeno plants and basil from them for the past two years, and they are always healthy and grow well (and taste great).  The vegetable shop across the street sells a similar selection of flowers and vegetables as well.  I typically get everything else I need from Union Square like everyone else.  There is another thread on here somewhere from last year that talks about a nearby nursery that a bunch of people would drive to that had an excellant selection.  Might be worth renting a zip car and loading up.
