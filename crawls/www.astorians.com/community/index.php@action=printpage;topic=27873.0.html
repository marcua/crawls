Title: Re: Seeking advice about G&T programs
Post by: BoredTemp on April 05, 2014, 07:19:27 PM
Welcome!


All the programs are very similar.  My daughter is at 150 and is very happy.  The program at 150 moves the kids ahead by a full grade in math, offers them chess in the second grade, and has a full complement of art, music and drama. 


I encourage you to tour all the schools and see which seems to be the best fit for you and your family.
