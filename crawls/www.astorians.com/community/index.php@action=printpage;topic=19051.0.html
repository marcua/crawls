Post by: doglover on August 20, 2010, 03:00:23 PM
No, a PBA card is something you give to a cop when you get pulled over to let them know you have family on the force. Each cop is given a certain amount of PBA cards each year and they are allowed to give them to immediate family. It's not a "get out of jail free card" or a bribe. It's something that shows them that you have family on the force and it's one of the perks of being a cop and having family on the force. It's not fair that some people have one and others don't, but it's a reality. That said, when I get pulled over, my NYPD family members have told me to always give them my card.
