Title: Re: Ginger?
Post by: sabrina on June 21, 2014, 03:31:44 PM
I just discovered this place today, was getting hair done in Saloon and someone came in with a fresh juice and a yogurt parfait and was talking about it.
Checked it out, it's a very very nice stylish looking place and they have a small bar with only organic liquor and wines.  They let me sample their fresh juices.  I  ended up buying a vegan wrap with ginger dressing that was very tasty, i think it was $6 or 7.  The menu is mostly fresh juices and smoothies, which they divide into 'old' school and new school.  I didn't ask them the details of this distinction, but their take out menu says "centrifugal juicing" under the Old school Juice and the other stuff is "organic cold press."  They share a hallway that leads to Grand Cafe also so I guess same owners?   There were nice tables to sit at, but the AC was too high and the music was on a Sirius station that made me want to fall on a sword, every song was a  syrupy emo-ballad like  Five For Fighting.   I like relaxing music when I eat but this was totally annoying. The  staff was friendly and overall it's a thumbs -up.
