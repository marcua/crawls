Title: How much would YOU pay for your chocolate fix
Post by: jennifer on September 08, 2010, 10:59:35 PM
Coming home today I was in dire need of a chocolate fix... where to go while walking down Broadway????

it occured to me that i'd seen chocolate by the register at Bakeway - figured - they're a bake shop (mostly) and so they must have some good chocolate. 

.... there it was .... lined up by the register.... in it's recycled paper wrapper.... 3 oz give or take a couple of tenths.... organic....dark..... chili pepper with ginger.... stopped me from even checking the other flavors... handed it to the cashier and she says.....











$7.50.   
WHAT!!!!!!!????????


who in the hell would pay $7.50 for a bar of chocolate in Astoria?????  7.50. 

I told her that surely she was mistaken.  Just one bar.  Then I asked - do you sell any of these??? The manager looking fellow behind the counter scowled at me.  I didn't mean to insult.  I even saw the price tag - yet assumed it was for a different item. 

so i gave back the chocolate bar and continued down the street to .... associated?  trade fair???  I don't know ... I mix up my supermarkets.... got my Green & Blacks Organic Dark chocolate (spicy orange) ... it was $2.99.  It was just okay.  I wonder if the $7.50 bar is 2.5 times better.
