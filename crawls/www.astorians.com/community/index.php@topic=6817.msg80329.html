I just last month got a new toaster oven from BedBathAndBeyond using a 20%-off coupon! It's a Black and Decker, and it seems to work quite well. Looking through the options at the store, it definitely seemed like you get what you pay for. The $200 ones with digital thermostat looked quite nice, and they were additionally heavier, which presumably means better temperature regulation. Also, there were differences in the number of heating elements. Presumably more is better there too...

I love using my toaster oven in the summer. It keeps the kitchen much cooler than turning on the regular oven. My sister is the queen of toaster oven baking. I think her most creative was baking slice-n-bake cookies in the toaster oven. There is enough room to bake about 6 cookies at a time and this way she won't eat the whole batch after making them in a traditional oven. When I have time, I use it to bake a potato.

A tip about the BBB coupons, they usually still accept expired coupons for up to one month after the expiration date.
