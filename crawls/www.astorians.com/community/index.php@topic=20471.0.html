ok I know this is old, but I have to chime in here. After being on the hunt for a burger close to In n Out in California for YEARS, Petey's nails it. For those not familiar with In N Out, this isnt huge thick juicy type of burger. Its a delicious different style all together. No, no one can actually duplicate In n Out in NYC despite how much we try because In n Out only opens restauraunts within a certain proximity to where they get their beef. BUT this is the closest I have tried. I had one yesterday and am craving again today.

Yeay Petey's!
