the new flea market in town
« Reply #1 on: June 06, 2009, 10:46:26 PM »
I got back from Summer Lovin Market a lil while ago. Whoever hasn't been by there yet, I encourage you to go!  The market features local independent designers that sell handmade jewelry, paintings, accessories, candles, bath and body products, t-shirts and clothing, its located at the newly renovated Astoria Walk and is hosted by Astoria Events. You all need to see what this has turned into, its a mini shopping center and there will be different vendors there every weekend. The market is set to run all summer long!  Stop by next weekend June 13th and 14th to see what I am talking about...
