What I heard about Zodiac, which I've posted here previously, is that it's closed because two stories will be added to the building where it is located, which means that additional support columns will need to be constructed inside the existing structure, including the ground floor where Zodiac is, and the basement parking garage.  Perhaps something has changed, but from what I had heard, the plan was for Zodiac to reopen when construction on the ground floor was completed.

I would take a guess that the Bosnian place will move to the corner where there was a print shop for a long time, and more recently, a convenience store (which never seemed to have much business).  The corner opposite from that, where Byzantio was, is now Alma Bank (owned by the same man who owns the building which houses Zodiac), so I doubt that will be going anywhere anytime soon, especially as the bank only opened a few months ago.
