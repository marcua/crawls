Title: directions to Green Thumb Farm
Post by: kickingchicken on October 02, 2006, 11:53:43 AM
Hi all,

Just re-posting the directions to Green Thumb Farm.  I'm not a driver, so if the drivers out there have better directions, please post here.
It's about a 2 hour drive to the farm.

Before you go, you need to call the farm in advance to let them know how many are in your party. 

631-726-1900

From Queens:

Get onto the Long Island Expressway.  Go to Exit 70S.  Follow South (bear right) and get onto Route 27 Sunrise Highway.  It becomes Country Road 39.  Follow to the end.  There will be a traffic light, with a car dealership nearby.  Turn left at the light onto Montauk Highway, Route 27.  Pass the Princess Diner on your right.  Go through the town of Water Mill.  There will be a windmill on your right.  Go a quarter of a mile east, and on the right hand side will be the farm.

By Train:

Take the Long Island Railroad to Southampton.  Take a cab from Southampton to Green Thumb Farm, in Water Mill.  If the driver doesn't know where it is, say that it's a quarter of a mile east on Montauk Highway.  It will cost about $10-15 one way. 

I don't know how many people will be in my party, so I don't know how I'll get there.  I'm leaning towards the public transportation.
