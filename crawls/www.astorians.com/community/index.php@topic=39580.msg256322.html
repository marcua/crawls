With all due respect, it seems it is you who isn't understanding how the system (of "democracy") functions in the US. Nobody is asking for rules to be set aside in the above petition, the petition is for the electoral voters to vote not for the candidate of their party but for someone else. In most states they have the right to do so, in some states their votes won't be counted if they do, and in some states there's a moderate fine as a result.
