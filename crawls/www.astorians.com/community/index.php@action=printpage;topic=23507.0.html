There are bars, restaurants, and cafes of all shapes, sizes, and flavors on 30th from 31st to Steinway and beyond.  If you are new to the area, do what most of us did.  Clear a Friday night and bar hop up and down the street.  You will soon learn which are your favorites.  Bar 30 has live music some nights.  Not sure about open mic.  Domnies as well.
