Title: Candlelight Vigil to mark 4th anniversary of the start of the Iraq War.
Post by: angledge on March 08, 2007, 08:49:33 AM
Democracy for America (http://www.democracyforamerica.com/) is asking for people to hold candlelight vigils on or around March 19th.  The idea is to have thousands of Americans hit the streets from March 17 to 20 in honor of fallen soldiers, and to demand our troops come home.

I would like to hold one in Astoria, preferably in the evening on Monday, March 19th.  Can anyone think of a good, high-visibility spot?  Would anyone want to come?

I think a lot of people have forgotten that it's now been FOUR YEARS that we've been in the Iraq War.  Thousands of Americans & Iraqi & Afghanis are dead.  Let's take an hour out of our schedule to lament these losses & try to help bring this war to an end.
