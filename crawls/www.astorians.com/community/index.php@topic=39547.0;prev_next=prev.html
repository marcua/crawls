Astoria Park Car Break Ins?
« on: October 11, 2016, 06:12:05 PM »
Hi all - I've been living between 19th st & 21st streets near Ditmars for the past 3+ years and haven't had problems with my car being parked on the street until recently. Twice in the past 3 weeks I've had my car broken into while parked along the park on 19th street. My car was locked at the time with my windows up, yet the robbers were able to get into my car without breaking any of my windows.


The only evidence of a break in was my glove box papers and maps + CDs in my car thrown about throughout the inside of my car and my stash of coins missing.


Has anyone else in the area had similar experiences? Clearly the folks doing these breakins have some sort of tool to get into locked cars without breaking windows.
