Title: Performance/Rehearsal Space

Greetings!

Thanks so much for lending an ear!

My nascent theater company is looking to perform Measure for Measure sometime in mid-June (opening night date).

We're trying some new approaches to the show and we're obviously on a tight deadline for space. Rehearsals begin mid-May(!).

So: what are we looking for? Well, don't laugh at the following list:

Cool/non-traditional atmosphere - doesn't have to be in a theater
Flexible seating, so we can structure our own playing areas
Location - local, or in Manhattan, or on the way
Affordable - $1000 a week
Exclusivity - we'd like to have a key and leave our stuff there
Technical resources - a nice grid, instruments, and board would be great

<pause>

Now that you've recovered from the laughter - these are our "druthers": the perfect space. We don't expect to find it. But some combination of these ideals with the inevitable compromises would be awesome.

What do you all think? Any notions? We're really hungry to get out there and work; any suggestions will be greatly appreciated!

Many thanks,

Antinomic
