Title: Re: Recommendations for jewelry store
Post by: Starwind51 on May 19, 2010, 02:13:28 AM
With the bad luck you've had at JC Penney, you might have a hard time trusting Sears (on Queens Blvd) but I've bought a lot of gemstone gold jewelry from them and I don't believe I've ever had a problem.  Try to shop on weekends, things really do go off sale during the week, unless stated in an ad/posted at the counter.  I've also done well with my stuff from Macy's.  You might also be interested in the 22k gold jewelry sold at the Indian stores in Jackson Heights.

As for Penney's, try to find a corporate phone number and tell someone higher up about the problems you've been having; they might get you a new chain.
