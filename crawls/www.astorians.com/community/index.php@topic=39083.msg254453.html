Hello Astorians! I'm excited that I heard about this thread from a neighbor of mine...Thanks for being supportive of our restaurant. You can find out more about my husband and me from this link: http://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20151125/astoria/astoria-couple-opens-indian-eatery-with-seasonal-fare


About the name: A google search for Curry Culture takes you to Singapore, Kurry Kulture takes you to Kansas...and Kurry Qulture takes you to Queens. Online presence is everything these days. So we've opted for the "cute" Q for Queens. Yes, I slightly cringe as my background is teaching high school...


To answer TJT: You can sit at the bar and order anything you like (sides, bar bites). If you want the familiarity of an alu gobi, naan, and dal, we've got that for you. We also have some great drinks and beer on tap that have been selected to complement Indian flavors. Bo, our bartender is super friendly and helpful. For sit-down service, we have the simplicity of the pre-fixe menu.


Anything else I can help with?


Check us out on social media. We're on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter...and have been mentioned by Astoria Post, DNAinfo, Eater NY, and WeHeartAstoria since we opened less than two weeks ago. Last but not least, we've been Astorians for 13 years now (same block, same building) and I wouldn't be surprised if you've seen us running around with our dog or schlepping our two kids. The restaurant is on 30th Avenue and 36th Street. Thanks neighbors, we love that we've brought Kurry Qulture close to you.

braun: Great question. Of course we don't have a $33 price for everyone who walks in! We have a full service bar with beer on tap, wine, and specialty cocktails.  If you sit at the bar, you can order off the bar bites menu AND/OR you can order any of the sides on the pre-fixe menu. People are really appreciating the creamy black lentils, alu gobi, butter chicken, chicken tikka, prawns, and various breads available at the bar. There are more options, I'm just naming a few. So yes, it is completely possible to relax at the bar (open till late) and have a complete meal for less than $33.


Hope that helps and we hope to see you soon! If you do go, I'd love to hear what you think! Have you seen the article written by WeHeartAstoria today?
