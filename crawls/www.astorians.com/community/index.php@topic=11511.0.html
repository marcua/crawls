Terra cotta pots, soil, and herb seeds are super cheap and if you've got sunny windows, the kids will enjoy seeing the seedlings grow into basil, parsely, chives, and other delicious things. Could also make a fun project for them; each kid gets his or her own pot and gets to choose the herb to grow in it. If you say they're for your class, you might even be able to coax a donation out of Home Depot or Target or whatever. And if the kids are older, they might like to cut them to take home and cook with. Herbs grow quickly, smell fantastic, and require little care (just water!). Most even get flowers. That's what I would do if I had a classroom full of windows.
