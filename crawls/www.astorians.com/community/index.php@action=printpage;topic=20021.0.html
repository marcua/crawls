Title: TOT SHABBAT at Astoria Center of Israel
Post by: eblack on December 17, 2010, 12:38:25 PM
Jews with kids!  Want to hang out on Shabbat with a bunch of other parents with kids?  Tot Shabbat at ACI is usually the first Saturday of each month, but is being bumped to January 8th, due to New Year's Day.

Tot Shabbat has been very successful and there are typically about 10 or more kids in attendance from newborns to about 5 years old.  Any age kids are welcome, though!  The more the merrier.  Hope to see you there.  Info is below:

TOT SHABBAT: Astoria Center of Israel, 27-35 Crescent Street, Astoria
Saturday, January 8, 10:45 am - 11:30 am
 
Join us for ACI's monthly Tot Shabbat, where children come together for a fun Shabbat experience with songs, stories, and a kiddush snack. Families with young children of all ages -- infants and up -- are welcome. Come be a part of our wonderful, growing community. No charge and no pre-registration necessary.
 
For more information, contact astoriashul@gmail.com, 718-278-2680, www.astoriacenter.org, facebook.com/astoriashul.
