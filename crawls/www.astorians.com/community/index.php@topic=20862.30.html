After all this time I finally tried this place.  I'm glad I did and was sorry I didn't give it a shot sooner.  If you ask me, the burgers here are quite good.  We got delivery.  Organized and prompt.  I really like their veggie burger.  Mostly quinoa, then it seemed yellow lentils, carrots and other veggies.  The beef burger is also great.  I think their whole grain rolls are actually better than Bareburger's.  The Pesto Aioli is very delicious.  The onion rings and sweet potato fries were fried to a perfect crisp, but there was no sign or oil or greasiness at all.  Really good stuff.  Anyway, I think they have  good thing going.  Limited menu, quality items, fast and friendly service.  And open nearly 24 hours with delivery until about 4am.  I'm glad I gave them a shot and will be back often.  I will also visit Bareburger.  They are very different places.
