Okay - so after posting my Michael's dislike post again, I gave it some thought and figured if there were some folks here that really liked the place I should give it another try.  So my guy and I went last night.  We both had a cup of Manhattan clam chowder and I had the corned beef panini and he had a burger. 

I have to say, the manhattan clam chowder was awesome!  My guy is somewhat obessed with the soup, so we tried several in the neighborhood.  He still prefers Sanford's, but I thought Michael's was the best.  It was very slightly spicy and had tons of vegetables in it.  Nice and chunky and awesome!

My panini was awesome as well.  The corned beef was perfectly cooked and almost no trace of fat. And I asked for well done fries .  They didn't initially didn't come out well done but the waiter caught the mistake before I did and took them back for some well done ones. 

My guy's burger and fries were average but good.


So anyway, we'll be going back.  And a plus, it's next to my favorite bodega!
