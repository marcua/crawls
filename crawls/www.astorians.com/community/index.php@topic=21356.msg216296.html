New Listing at Jac Towers in Jackson Heights

CHARMING PREWAR ONE BEDROOM STEPS FROM THE HISTORIC DISTRICT
 
Prewar one bedroom steps from The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights. Prime location! This sunlit one bedroom, one bath, brimming with charm, is situated on a lovely block. The spacious entry foyer easily accommodates bookshelves and a home office area. The windowed eat-in kitchen has generous cabinet space and is fully equipped with stainless steel stove and oven.  Its well proportioned living/dining room is ideal for entertaining. The large master bedroom provides plenty of room for substantial sleep and sitting areas.  Prewar details include nine foot ceilings, original crown moldings, beautiful arches, and hardwood floors throughout. The lobby is elegant with terrazzo hallways.  This intimate, well-run, and pet-friendly co-op is convenient to everything.  Jackson Heights has been named among the most desirable neighborhoods to live in with Zagat rated restaurants, great shopping, weekend farmers market, close to major transportation and approximately a 25 minute subway ride to Grand Central Station.  This truly is a great Jackson Heights opportunity!
 
 ASKING: $195K, MAINTENANCE: $774, DOWNPAYMENT: 20%
 
 EXCLUSIVELY LISTED BY: MARLENE FLORES, REALTOR®, 917-208-6709
