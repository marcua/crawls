In my opinion, there is absolutely no comparison between Kyclades and Oceano.

One is established with a clientele base and classic, fresh, well prized, consistent Greek cuisine.

The other is completely lost and has no clue how to run a restaurant. To begin with – Oceano doesn’t know who they want to be.
