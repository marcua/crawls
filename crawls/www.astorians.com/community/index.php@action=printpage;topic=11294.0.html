What a fantastic restaurant – from the owner, manager, assistant manager, bartenders, hostess and staff – we had an amazing experience for our wedding!  The owner is a masterful planner and took all the worry of planning off our hands.  The food was exceptional and we had almost everything on the menu from chicken, fish, to my favorite, the filet mignon – it all measures up to the finest served in Manhattan.  Riverview has a tasty drink menu as well – try the mojitos.  To top it all off, the location is perfectly situated on the water which allows for a view Manhattan that can not be rivaled.  Thank you Riverview for making our wedding such an amazing experience; one which we will forever be thankful to you for.
