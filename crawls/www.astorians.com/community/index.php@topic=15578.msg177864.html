Best Bang for the Buck
« on: July 16, 2009, 10:08:32 AM »
So, I'd thought I'd start a thread with the best value deals you've been finding when you dine around Astoria, Manhattan, and beyond.

I'll throw out two to start. One I've just discovered, and the other was an oldie but goodie.

DiWine: $10 for an individual brick oven, thin crust pizza. This pizza is about 10". It is really really good if you like thin. The topping choices on their menu are awesome combinations (The breakfast pizza and the "Gigi," are two of note) and when you make your own it still costs the same. Another great thing-Happy Hour is 2 for 1!!!! Sun-Thur 5-8 pm.

Spice Market (yes, of the Jean George): $48/pp for a 5 course tasting menu. And I don't mean a 1" morself of food for most of these things. They were good size portions for each of the items. The only thing is the whole table has to order, but it's an incredible feast that we ended up taking part of home. And it's 5 courses, but 10 different dishes. Just incredible. If you like to be adventurous with your food and like the Thai/Asian/ Indian fusion stuff, this is just a great meal. Highly recommend it. As soon as I was done eating it, I wanted to go back for another night. The space is also really beautiful and would make for an awesome group get together/special night out.

Last night I went to Bel Aire for a cheeseburger (regular, not "deluxe").  I thought the waiter was bringing me a dish of coleslaw, but it turned out to be a dish of beets & onions and a dish of chickpeas & onions.  Then came the big dish of coleslaw (and a pickle).  The burger was large.  I had a rootbeer, and was thinking about asking if they gave free refills when the waiter refilled it without my even having to ask!  That was a very filling, very satisfying meal!  The bill came to $8.13 with tax (not including the tip).  You could easily run up a bill like that at nearby McD or White Castle and not get nearly the same amount of food or nutrition!

(Bel Aire a diner on 21st Street at Broadway.  They are open 24 hours a day.)
