There were, in fact, only 2 translations of that line.

The Ogden translation: The world is everything that is the case.

and

The Pears/McGuinness translation: The world is all that is the case.

In both instances the sentence punctuation was eliminated. Had either translation included it, the structure of the thought might have been more evident.

For example: The world is everything, that is the case. OR The world is all, that is the case.
