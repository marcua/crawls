Title: New Dishes At Bistro Les Minots
Post by: Les Minots Bistro on September 08, 2009, 06:27:09 PM
Bonjour a tous,

Bistro Les Minots is celebrating the end of the summer with some new dishes:

First, there is “La Salade de Foie de Volaille”, which is composed of baby spinach and warm chicken liver confit in a balasamic reduction sauce.

Second, we are pleased to introduce “Les Rougets A La Nage”, which are pan seared fillets of red mullets with tomatoes, zucchini, and an arugula & mussel pesto nage.

Third, “Le Poulet Basquaise” is replaced by “Le Coq Au Vin” that is dark chicken meat cooked in red wine with bacon, mushrooms, onions & carrots over spaghetti served in a red wine sauce.

Fourth, we added “Les Travers de Bœuf Braisés”, which are braised short ribs in red wine and served with polenta gratinée and a fricassé of carrots & mushrooms. The short ribs will also be available in a sandwich served with fries and salad as a variation of “Le Citadin”, our hanger steak sandwich.

Finally, we have a new type of mussels: “Les Moules A La Normande”, steamed mussels with bacon, mushrooms, shallots and an apple brandy sauce.

Our $19.95 Dinner Prix Fixe including 3 courses, appetizer, entrée and dessert is still available from 5 to 10 pm, 7 days a week.
As always we value your comments and feedbacks and hope to see you all very soon,

A bientot,
