As we've strayed from Astoria, I feel compelled to add a few suggestions...

Corner Bistro in the West Village - can't go wrong at all.  Just have to deal with the lines, which usually move pretty quick.  Probably in the top 5 burger joints in the city.

Cornelia Street Cafe - Whenever I go to check out a jazz show, I order a burger.  Mmm... and they serve the burger on an English Muffin, and with baby green salad. 

Pete's Tavern - One of NYC's oldest bars, really great burgers and other bar grub

Peter Luger - how could you possibly go wrong when they use the same beef for the porterhouse in their hamburger?  Not difficult to get a seat during lunch, and not overly expensive, either.

Balthazar - for a great dining experience, and lets not forget the poms frites.  Aaaarrrgggg....

That's about all I have for now.
