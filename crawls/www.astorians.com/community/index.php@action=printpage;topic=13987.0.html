Title: Zlata Praha Venison Festival 2009 = HUGE dissapointment
Post by: Spaulding on February 13, 2009, 10:11:10 PM
Yikes.

Just went to the opening night of the annual Venison Fest.  It was really, really horrible.  I have been to Zlatha Praha before and had an excellent Goose entre.  But this was bad. 

Ordered Venison loin in a garlic sauce and should have been tipped off when the server walked off without asking how I wanted it cooked.  We also had to remind him that our meal came with soup.  He didn't know what kind of soup they had to serve.  Thought it was goulash, it wasn't.  When the entree came, the Venison seemed like it had been simmering in broth for about 5 days.  So overstewed it was dry.  The garlic, which was a major component of the meal, was clearly minced garlic from a jar.  You can taste the difference.

Waitstaff were very confused except for a 10 year old girl who was bussing tables and expediting things.  She seemed to be helping the adult staff work the computers/register.  She was very sweet and was the one to finally come by our table and ask if we wanted a check.  The place was not even full, so it wasn't that busy.

We were never offered the pastry and coffee that came with our meal.

The Pilsner Urquell was good.


We spent $40 a person.  Petey's, Tastee Corner, or the The Taco King on 33rd (my Bro) would have been much better and much cheaper.
