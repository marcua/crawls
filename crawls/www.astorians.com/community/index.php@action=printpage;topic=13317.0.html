Title: END OF DECEMBER ASTORIA/LIC MUSIC FESTIVAL!
Post by: audreyLICmag on December 05, 2008, 09:24:10 PM
HEY EVERYONE!

Why not bring in 2009 with a bang and spend the end of December rocking out with music lovers and the extremely diverse and talented bands/artists of your very own neighborhood?!

Ins&Outs Magazine (licmagazine.com (http://licmagazine.com)) is teaming up with Astoria Music & Arts (myspace.com/astoriamusiciansunite (http://myspace.com/astoriamusiciansunite)) to create an end of December (26th-29th) music festival comprised solely of QUEENS musicians in various QUEENS venues in Astoria & LIC! We want to take the time to invite you all and ask for your support as we promote the growing LIC/Astoria music scene that has been so long eclipsed by that of Manhattan and Brooklyn. Join us and let's show NYC what QUEENS has to offer! Seriously!

Please stay posted with AM&A and Ins&Outs as the details are being worked out (look out for postings, fliers, etc.), and if you are a band or a solo musician who would like to participate PLEASE don't hesitate to get in touch with us! audrey@licmagazine.com

THANKS SO MUCH!!  And if you can, please spread the word and keep the dates in mind! We know the holidays can be a hectic time, but if you're in Astoria/LIC, PLEASE join us!! :mrgreen:
