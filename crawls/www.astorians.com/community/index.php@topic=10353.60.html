Freeze Peach - my last post
« Reply #67 on: May 13, 2008, 07:39:22 PM »
Hi,

This is my last post on Freeze Peach. First of all, I do come here very, very often with my laptop but it has not kept me from talking to people.  I am an artists career coach and got an awesome client by talking to someone at Freeze Peach in the Astoria Krafty Knitty group.  You'll see that my screenname is Women's Studio Center - I'm changing that soon. I was the Founder and Executive Director and it went out of business recently. 

During this time Freeze Peach was incredibly helpful to me.  I would have been hiding out in my house being a hermit if I hadn't a place like this to come to.   Mostly it's the interaction with people that was of interest to me.   Or being with others around while I worked.

Lastly, when I walked in I found wonderful new additions.   There are now two large tables with tall chairs and many  more arm chairs.  So the changes they promised are taking root. 

They're also talking about bubble teas - tea with tapioca pearls - and other exciting drinks.  Alev, one of the baristas here invented a new drink which I intend to try when it gets a bit warmer.  Iced Mango Green Tea.

So if you haven't been here drop on by and if you have, give it another try.  You'll find a whole new atmosphere.    Just start a conversation with someone and you'll see what I'm talking about.  It's a warm and friendly place.

Yes thank god that real estate (if that's what he was) guy is gone.

That's it,
Melissa
