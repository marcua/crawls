Title: New Image Hair Salon - A Great Find in Astoria - 3617 Ditmars Blvd.
Post by: judy771 on July 31, 2008, 09:03:53 AM
If you are one of the many women (or men) living in Astoria who have struggled to find a great salon for cut, color and great customer service, look no further.

No, I am not being paid to post this message.  I am simply a woman who has tried just about every salon in Astoria, spent all kinds of money, only to have my hair burnt, broken and cut into unrecognizable layers.

Let's face it without a great cut (or color) it does not matter what you wear - you will look awful.

Well, on someones recommendation, I happened upon New Image Salon and it was the best decision I ever made.  But don't take my word for it.  Go in there, sit down and just watch the stylists in action.  See the great color, cuts and styles that all the stylists turn out.

Now I happent to use someone by the name of "Jen".  She took my haircut, previously butchered by a stylist in another salon, and transformed it.  I was completely amazed at what she was able to do with such a mess.  For the record, I have short, very curly hair, one side more than the other, cowlicks in the front top of my head and some at the nap of my neck.  Yet, when I walked out of the salon I looked amazing.  I got compliments at work all day long the next day.  Jen is talented beyond words.  This is true of all their stylists. 

You can just tell from watching the stylists in action that they stay on the cutting edge of what's out there.  I sat there one day and watched Demetri put long extensions on a woman and was amazed at how beautiful they looked.  In addition, the quality of their products for color, shampoo/conditioners and styling products, etc. is amazing. 

How great does it feel to have someone shampoo your hair and massage your head with precision?  That's what you get from Rosie and Gina.

And what about customer service?  The best customer service I have gotten in just about any store in Astoria is at this salon.  You feel appreciated and welcome.  It is clear that the owners care about their customers.

Now some of you might be asking, well for this quality their prices must be through the roof.  Absolutely not true.  I was amazed at their prices.  So reasonable.  They have packaged prices for combinations such as cut, color and styling.  What I paid for all of that is what I paid just for color in SPA Salon.

Going to a hair salon should be a fun, relaxing experience.  For me, New Image lives up to their name.  You will always walk out looking better than you ever expected, feeling happy and relaxed.

Hope to see you there.

Judy B
