In case you missed the buzz, the hot new ice cream was created by a couple right here in the neighborhood. We were able to get a couple of the flavors at C-town on 28th Ave. The Amaretto (for Bayside, I think?) was great, but the Bronx Cha-Cha Chocolate was unbelievable!!! Great chocolate with (yes, people do this) a bit of Cayanne Pepper. I think I'm gaining about 5-pounds just thinking about it.

Definately my new ice cream of choice!!!!

The couple from the neighborhood who came up with this stuff deserve every bit of good stuff coming to them. BUT, when and where will we see the Astoria Bakla-Wha??? I can't wait for that one.
