Title: "All You Can Eat Pasta Dinner" to benefit The Wounded Warrior Project
Post by: Ditmartian on February 06, 2008, 07:51:59 PM
The Lions Club of Port Washington presents The Annual All You Can Eat Pasta Dinner to benefit WWP. At The Manhasset Bay Sportsman's Club. 36 Matinecock Ave. Manhasset Isle, Port Washington, NY.

Featuring Spaghetti, Rigatoni, Meatballs, Sausage, Salad, Bread and Beverages!

$12.00 per person - Kids 5 - 12 $5.00 - Kids under 5 eat free! Meals are being served at 6:00 p.m. and the last plate going out at 9:30 p.m.

Contact Info:
For more info on this event please contact Chris Avazis at 767-5108 or Sandi Nunziatta at 516-676-1336.
