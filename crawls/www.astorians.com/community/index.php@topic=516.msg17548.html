Hi everyone, I'm Abby. I have lived in Astoria for 5.5 years now, but have only recently realized what a great community is growing here, and am very excited to be more a part of it!  I discovered the board a few months ago, but didn't post til recently (to tell a not-so-pleasant story here: http://astorians.com/index.php?topic=2143.0 ).

I love Astoria and am so happy I ended up here randomly when I moved to New York.  My apartment overlooks the Beer Garden, so it's getting a little loud these days - especially when the Czech Republic gets a goal in the World Cup!  And I love the Beer Garden, but the lines have been getting riDICulous in the past year or so.

What else do I love...  I love cheese!  And exploring new foods and restaurants, summer picnics and winter sledding in Astoria Park, snowboarding, samba, salsa, world music, travel, languages, dolphins, kitties, playing guitar, songwriting, singing, dancing, making lists.  Also my boyfriend Tom (who I invite to introduce himself here!), who stays with me here most of the time and cooks me burgers and Filipino food, and has made an impromptu garden on the fire escape.

What I do...  I currently work as a financial literacy trainer & counselor at a community development credit union in Washington Heights, but am looking for a full-time job in the field of international development/microfinance. 

What have I done...  Studied music and anthropology (ethnomusicology) in undergrad, then lived in Ecuador for a couple years after college, then moved to Astoria and worked at World Music Institute for a couple years, then got my degree at the New School in International Affairs/Socio-Economic Development last year, and here I am ... still in Astoria.

I really hope to meet all of you soon around Astoria, as it seems like such a wonderful, welcoming community!  Though I'm relatively new to this board, I extend my warm welcome as well to those who are newcomers to Astoria!

-Abby
