Happy to see that our work has not gone unnoticed and we thank all.

We are 4 senior citizens striving to create an affordable venue where neighborhood artists and artisans can show their wonderful creations.
 
Astoria has always been a cultural center and from the start it has been our continuing goal to maintain high standards.

I for one am highly impressed as to the quality and uniqueness of the designs. Their talents and creativity inspire us to continue.

Our next event on September 9 is at Queensview Housing and this promises to be the best show ever.

We have implemented some of your suggestions and will be distributing thousands of hand-out flyers, posting notices on web bulletin boards, sending 1000 emails, making hundreds of telephone calls, mailing postcards, posting on hundreds of poles etc etc.

We invite all of you and hope that you win the $50 cash door prize or any one of the other prizes.
