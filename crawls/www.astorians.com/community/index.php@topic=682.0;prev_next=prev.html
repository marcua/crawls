It's open now
« Reply #5 on: October 17, 2005, 09:32:52 AM »
The Eckerd on 31st St. is open again.  I went there yesterday and bought a magazine and an Ove Glove.

The story that the roof caved in seems the plausible one.  On the door of the store is a notice from the Queens Borough Commissioner of the NYC Dept. of Buildings, saying that the order to vacate has been lifted, because temporary shoring is in place.  In the store itself there is a hole in the ceiling.

The health-violation story sounds like a rumor to me, akin to the anxiety aroused by the dirty-restaurants listings in the Times in the 80's.

I still think it strange that this store is the only holdout in the area from ExpressScripts, but that's another story.
