Title: bags bags bags
Post by: Christine on October 07, 2006, 04:02:21 PM
I have this *thing* about plastic bags. Drives me nuts the more I obtain.

Like everybody else, I hoard them and use them for small wastebaskets in the house. But I'm also a little obsessed about avoiding them whenever possible.

For example, when I know I'm going to Fairway or for a bunch of groceries, I bring the granny bag (you know, that big plastic monstrosity w/the zipper that fits in the granny cart.) That thing is amazing- like ten little bags' worth of space, at least. I love seeing how much I can cram in there.  :evil:

But like everybody else, I don't always plan ahead and often find myself at the store sans granny bag. If I'm getting a couple of small items that fit in the purse, I have to practically jump up and down and yell, "NO BAG! NO BAG!" Some cashiers think I'm nuts, guess they don't understand how I could walk out of a store with a naked item in hand. Won't people think I stole it??  :roll: That's what the receipt is for.

Today I got stuck without a bag and bought a loaf of bread. Figured I had more stuff to buy anyway so I took it out in a bag. Turns out she had double-bagged it......one stupid loaf of bread!  :-o

Anyway. How many paragraphs is that about bags?  :-P Needed to rant about this, tired of feeling like a freak just b/c I DON'T WANT TEN THOUSAND PLASTIC BAGS PER ITEM!!!!

Oh yeah, to add insult to injury: I see that my roommate has a box of small trash bags  :| For the love of all that is good and holy, WHY?!

Thank you.
