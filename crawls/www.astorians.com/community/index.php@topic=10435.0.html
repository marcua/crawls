Nuisance marketing phone calls from American Express or a scam?
« on: May 11, 2008, 07:09:43 PM »
For the last couple of weeks I've been getting nuisance phone calls from a 877 number that claims to be American Express and states that they're a non solicitation phone call. However, I am not a American Express card member, so it doesn't make sense that they're calling me. When I pick up the phone I only get a recording telling me to call, and when I call the number they ask me to leave my name and phone number.

My question is, can this be some scam? Why would AmEx be calling me every day. I'm reluctant to leave them my name and number. Does anybody know an agency I can call to file a complaint?
