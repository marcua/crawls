You know what we need to do?  Cancel Valentine's Day.  No, hear me out on this one.  I think it screws with people.  Whether you realize it or not, facing another Valentine's Day as a singleton - and let me qualify this by saying for some people - can get depressing.  You can't swing a dead cat without running into signs hollering at you to buy truckloads of flowers and candy for your sweetie.  And don't get me started on the jewelry commercials.  You have to buy your lady a big fat diamond to show your love once a year?  If you are fortunate enough to be coupled, you then have to stress about finding a gift of some sort in order to participate in this Hallmarkian, Godiva-fueled, 1800Flowers-laden excuse for a holiday.  Down with that, I say!  If you love someone, you should make it known every damn day. 

That said, please make sure you buy my truffles as Valentine's gifts.   :-)

(Just because I'm on the capitalistic bandwagon doesn't mean I approve).
