EMAIL:  roomastoria718@yahoo.com

One of my roommates is moving to Seattle.  He was a great roommate and I hate to see him leave.  So, we have a room that will be available August 15th.

About us:
We are friendly, down-to-earth, and easy-going.  We are clean, respectful, and don’t mind going out for a drink every now and then.

The Area:
Astoria is a nice neighborhood.  It has a variety of restaurants and plenty of shops.  It is about 6 subway stops from a huge mall and a Target.  There are three grocery stores in the area and a nice laundramat across the street, as well as a dry cleaner.  There is also a dry cleaner across the street.  On 30th Avenue there is a plethora of Greek Cafes.  There is a decent nightlife here on the weekends.  Some places worth mention are The Beer Gardens, Rapture, The Irish Rover, and Café Bar.  There is a big movie theatre, and video rental store.  There are a few gyms in the area…a big NY Sports Club.  And a Staples on 31st Street.  There are several 99 Cent stores and bargain stores too….  As well as a 7-11 if you are in need of Big Gulp or Slurpee (just kidding).

To get here one would take the N or W to either at the 30th Ave stop or Astoria Blvd stop.  You can also take the R to Steinway, but it’s a longer walk.  There are two bus lines that run into the city as well.  One goes across Steinway and crosses over the 59th Street Bridge ending at 2nd Ave.  The other crosses the Triborough Bridge and then crosses 125th Street and ends just south of Columbia University on Broadway.

The Apartment:
It’s a four bedroom apartment.  We use the fourth bedroom as storage.  We have a kitchen, bathroom, living room, and dining room.  Surprisingly it is pretty quiet and it rarely gets messy.  I would like to keep it that way.   The apartment is Non-Smoking apartment.  The apartment is fully furnished as well.

We have pots, pans, dishes, glasses, etc in the kitchen.  So there is no need to bring any of that.

The Room:
The room is $800 a month.  Utilities are split four ways, and include:  electric, cable, and our cable modem.  ,It is 13.5’ X 9.5’. The room has large windows.  It has a walk-in closet that is 5.5’ X 4’ and 9’ high with built in shelves.  Perfect for storage.  Our roommate is taking his furniture and thus the room itself will not be furnished.

The type of person we are looking for:
We are looking for a friendly, quiet, and easy going roommate.  The most important aspect of what we are looking for in a person is that they are respectful, and clean.  We are looking for someone who cleans up after themselves, doesn’t leave dishes laying around, and doesn’t mind helping clean up the apartment.  We all do our part to keep the apartment looking nice, and want to find someone that will do his/her part as well.

We will consider straight males or possibly a female.  We are looking for someone long term….NOTshort term.

EMAIL: roomastoria718@yahoo.com
