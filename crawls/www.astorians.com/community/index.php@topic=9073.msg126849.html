I have a new favorite Mexican place!  I'm very excited to add Guacamole Restaurant as my BEST MEXICAN Restaurant in Astoria.  I had dinner there last night and the Carne Asada blew me away.  The place is relatively new, I believe only 6 months old.  It is located on Broadway between 38th and Steinway.  The staff was very friendly.  The guacamole was great.

This place is very authentic Mexican.  The dinning is casual, the interior is nothing fancy, but the food is better than the setting would suggest.  They take care to make a nice presentation on the plate, they do not just slop the food on it.

My friends' only complaint was that the music was out of place, and I think this might be a big reason why the place was empty on a Saturday night even though the food was great.  They have to decide what crowd they are catering to.  Also, there was a flat screen TV in the middle of the wall, yet the place does not have a bar, so it seemed unnecessary.

Overall, you must check this place out if you like Mexican food.
