Title: Re: Farmers Market in Astoria?
Post by: lanseaux on May 10, 2006, 08:42:50 PM
Ramps are a member of the onion family that grow wild in the Northeast (and as far south as South Carolina) in early to mid spring.  They are also called wild leeks.  Here are a few links to information about ramps:

Ramps (http://www.econetwork.net/~wildmansteve/Plants.Folder/Ramp.html)  (this first link is from New York's own Wildman Steve Brill (http://www.econetwork.net/~wildmansteve/edible%20wild%20plants.html), who leads walks in NYC parks (and other places) to discover edible plants.  Here's (http://www.econetwork.net/~wildmansteve/Public%20Tour%20Stuff/'06%20Tour%20Calendar.html) a link to this year's calendar of hikes, including hikes in Central Park, Prospect Park, and several Queens parks.

Ramps (http://southernfood.about.com/cs/ramps/a/ramps.htm)

Ramps (http://www.main.nc.us/yancey/Ramps/ramps_or_wild_leek.htm)
