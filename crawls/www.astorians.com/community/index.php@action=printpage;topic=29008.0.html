Title: Queens Arts Connection
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on March 31, 2015, 11:11:16 AM
The QAC is a professional development event for artists to learn more about the business of art, get feedback on their work, and build networks within the artistic community.
 
Saturday, April 25, 2015, 2:00-7:00pm
Hosted at Queens Museum
Flushing Meadows Corona Park, Flushing, NY 11368
#7 Train to Mets-Willets Point
 
Schedule of Events:
2 – 5 PM     Tabling by leading arts organizations
2 – 3 PM     Registration for speed consultations and portfolio reviews
3 – 5 PM     15-minute portfolio reviews with QM curators
3 – 5 PM     15-minute speed consultations with experts in the arts and media
5 – 7 PM     Slide Slam of artwork slideshows, video, and readings
5 – 7 PM     Cocktail party
 
To RSVP for the Event follow this link: http://bit.ly/1ETH8Xc (http://bit.ly/1ETH8Xc)
 
Registration for the consultations will be done onsite the day of the event
 
Registration and submissions for the Slide Slam will be done on a first-come, first-serve basis and 20 spaces are available.
 
To submit work for the Slide Slam please follow this link: http://bit.ly/1HFvL8W (http://bit.ly/1HFvL8W)
 
Participating art service organizations include:
 
New York Foundation on the Arts, Kickstarter, Volunteer Lawyers of the Arts New York, Fractured Atlas, Jamaica Center for Arts and Learning, Poets and Writers, Women Makes Movies, Green Space Studio, Dance NYC, Taiwanese-American Arts Council, Indo-American Arts Council, Local Project, Laundromat Project, Space works, Topaz Arts, Flux Factory, The Field, Harvestworks Digital Media Center
 
For more information visit: http://www.queensmuseum.org/events/queens-arts-connection (http://www.queensmuseum.org/events/queens-arts-connection)
