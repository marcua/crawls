I've been a member of HSBC for years and years and years.  Up until recently, they've been great.  They are not located in the Ditmars area, but because of the type of account I had with them, I avoided ATM fees when using other bank ATMs.  Sometime in the fall, they changed their fee policies and now all accounts are being charged $1.50 per use.  Plus my online savings interest rate keeps going down every two weeks, with the failing economy.  So it is no longer useful to have a checking account with them.
