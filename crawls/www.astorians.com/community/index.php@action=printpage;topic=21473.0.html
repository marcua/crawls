Title: Auditions - Astoria Symphonic Choir 2011-12 Season
Post by: adamegg on August 13, 2011, 10:45:31 AM
ASC 2011-12 Season Auditions
August 29th-30th, and September 6th-7th, 7-9:30 PM
The Astoria Symphonic Choir works in collaboration with the Astoria Music Society to provide world class choral music experiences to singers and audiences in the community of Astoria, New York.
 
The choir was established in 2010 to perform major choral works with the Astoria Symphony in Astoria, Queens, NYC. The choir's inaugural season included lauded performances Handel’s Messiah and Haydn’s Lord Nelson Mass. The 2011-12 season promises continued partnership with the orchestra and may include works by Vivaldi, Mozart, Lauridsen, and more. Singers with all levels of experience and from all walks of life are welcomed to sing with this community-based choir. 
Join this young and exciting choir for its second season of performances with the Astoria Symphony! Singers from a variety of backgrounds and levels of experience are welcomed to sing in this community based choir.
Email astoriachoir@gmail.com (astoriachoir@gmail.com) for an appointment.
 
No prior choral experience is required for singers.
All voice parts are needed. 
Rehearsals are held Monday evenings from 7-9:30 PM in Astoria. 
Please see visit www.astoriachoir.org (http://www.astoriachoir.org) for more info.
