Title: Re: Fishing Spots In Astoria
Post by: lanseaux on October 01, 2008, 06:25:24 PM
There are definitely spots around Astoria where you can catch fish, but people should be careful about eating fish pulled from the East River.  Here (http://www.health.state.ny.us/environmental/outdoors/fish/fishengl.htm) are the health advisories from the New York State Department of Health, including a statement that because of environmental contaminants,  NO women of childbearing age and NO children under the age of 15 should eat any fish pulled from the waters around Astoria.  Women not of childbearing age, children older than 15, and all men, according to the chart in the link above, should eat only one meal per month of several of the most common fish, including bluefish and stripers.
