If you watch Heroes on NBC you know Sylar, the brain eating, nostrils flaring, watch repairing main villain.  Or at least he was the main villain until the season finale last nite.  Anywho I saw him, walking along Astoria blvd, right by 37th St, when I was heading home 11am Saturday morning.  He must live around here, otherwise why would he be hanging out along scenic Astoria Blvd.

THAR BE SPOILERS BELOW
