Title: Re: Stained shirt advice needed
Post by: NYCMacUser on September 07, 2006, 12:21:40 PM
Gotta supply a little more information about the shirt.
1) Fabric contents?
2) Color?
3) Pattern or print?
4) Buttons?

When most stains are washed in, they usually need a stronger treatment to remove. All of the above matter because the stronger treatments frequently require overall soaking. When I have a stain on a non-patterned, washable fabric, I make a paste of vinegar and a powdered detergent. Gently rub it on the front and back of the stain and let sit overnight, then wash. This usually works.
