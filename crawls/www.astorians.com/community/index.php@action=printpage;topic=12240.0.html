Title: Free Trial Classes in Art, Drama, Dance, Music and Spanish for ages 5-9
Post by: ssewell on September 13, 2008, 09:11:23 AM

Hi parents:

Just to let you know Serious Fun, Steinway Church's our new after school arts program in Astoria, is offering free trial classes this week, September 15-19, so if you'd like to have your child try a class while you observe, we would love to have you.  We are a registered School Age Child Care, so tuition for classes is tax deductible.

The classes are offered each day, M-F, 3:30-5:00 pm, at Steinway Reformed Church, 41-01 Ditmars Blvd (co of 41st/Ditmars):

Monday, Sept 15, Hip Hop Dance, 3:30-5:00 pm: children have fun learning basic dance technique set to an urban beat.

Tuesday, Sept 16: Hands on Art, 3:30-5:00 pm: children play with paint, mask-making, mural-making, printmaking, puppetry, & sculpture.

Wednesday, Sept 17 Theater Arts, 3:30-5:00 pm: children use improvisation, storytelling, playwriting, musical theater, & set design to make their own play. 

Thursday, Sept 18, World Music Jam, 3:30-5:00 pm: children play instruments from around the world while learning new songs & cultures. 

Friday, Sept 19:  Spanish Language Immersion, 3:30-5:00 pm: children learn Spanish using original music, songs, games and with native language speakers.

The information is also on our website: www.SeriousFunNYC.com, or give me a call at 646-415-8901.

Since our class sizes are small, we just ask that you let us know how many children for how many classes this week before you come, so that we can confirm that we have space. In response to parents' feedback, we are offering one way pick-ups from local schools and if there are enough parents interested, we could also offer an extended day to 6:00 pm.

I hope we see you there!

Sylvia Ramsaywak
Serious Fun
