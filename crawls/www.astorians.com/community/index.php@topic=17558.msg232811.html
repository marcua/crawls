As time goes on, I become more and more interested in spending my food dollars in Astoria's amazingly diverse and vital locally owned small businesses. Let the chains get their support elsewhere!:
  • Sparrow (serving up some really tasty food here, along with some good beers)
  • Queens Comfort (long hold-out here, surprised by how much I liked this place)
  • Astoria Bier and Cheese (surprised by how delicious and fun their beer/cheese pairings were)
  • Ukus (I regularly crave and get a slice of their awesome Montenegran savory pies, a delicious chopped salad; my meat-eating hubby loves their soup, their goulash and other meat dishes)
  • Lorusso Pizza and Focaccia (check out the remarkable Focaccia wizards located right here in Astoria!)
