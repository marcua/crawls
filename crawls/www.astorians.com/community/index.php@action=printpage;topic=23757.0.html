You should have more than that unless you know for sure your parents will back you up in case of an emergency .   Someone can lose their job and not have success right away finding something new . 1 month security deposit
1 month rent
If you are gonna pay a broker have money available for that
min 6 months rent and extra emergency money avail on hand .
