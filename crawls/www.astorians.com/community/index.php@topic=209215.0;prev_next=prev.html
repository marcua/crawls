I recently picked up couponing for whatever I can save on. I found the CVS has the most valuable sales by using coupons and their in store sales every week. I would like to start a thread about how I and others save money by shopping at CVS.
In the past two weeks, I've used $42.43 in cash (including tax) to get $186.82 worth of stuff from CVS. CVS is well known for it's overprice items but even cut the total price in half, I still did pretty well in saving.
I used to only print out coupons online but now I also buy one or two newspapers on Sunday and occasionally buy coupons from some website to save more.
