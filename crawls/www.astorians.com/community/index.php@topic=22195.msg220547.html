Homeowners aren't allowed to block the alley. There's a document called "Declaration of the Right of Way" that should be attached to everyone's deed which restricts the owners from obstructing other owners and occupants of the block to their property, garages, parking spaces. You should call DOB, tell them your problem. Go to ACRIS website or the office in Kew Gardens to see if the Declaraton of Right of Way is attached to the deed-info is public access. And if a vehicle belonging to a resident of the block is constantly and for long periods of time blocking the alley -not just loading, unloading-you most definitely can call 311. It's a priority A complaint and someone will be sent out there that very day. Problem with that is that if the car isn't there when the inspector comes no violation can be issued. You should also talk to other residents of the block who I'm sure are just as frustrated as you are. Maybe some community agitation is needed to resolve this problem. Too many self-entitled inconsiderate a-holes get away with it because they know they can.
