http://www.wliw.org/whatsup/uncategorized/digital-tv-transition-still-not-getting-wliw21/27/

They are broadcasting from Plainview LI. If they were to have a repeater broadcast locally, that would be great. If you are getting digital over the air reception, they suggest a rescan everytime you adjust your antenna.
