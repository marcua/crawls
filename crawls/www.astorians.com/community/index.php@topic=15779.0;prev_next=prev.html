Bistro Les Minots is welcoming Joe Kurauchi as their new Chef de Cuisine
« on: August 03, 2009, 01:35:43 PM »
Bonjour a tous,
Willing to elevate the quality and consistency of our food, we decided to make drastic changes by taking on board the famous Joe Kurauchi as a chef de cuisine. Trained in Japan, where he learned kaiseki technique, Joe Kurauchi embarked on a worldwide culinary tour, eating and cooking in 120 countries, and working in such far-flung kitchens as El Bulli in Spain, the Fat Duck in London, Stella Maris in Paris, and Morimoto in Philadelphia, Bombey, India & NYC.

While Joe made his debut at Bistro Les Minots the last week of July, we right away took advantage of his presence to make some changes to our menus. A better quality and consistency of our food is what we thrive for. Among the new comers, three new sandwiches, “Le Marocain” with spicy lamb sausages, “Le Normand” with ham & melted brie cheese, and finally “L’Atlantic” with deep fried monkfish over coleslaw in a wasabi sauzan sauce. We also adjusted prices and quantity of existing dishes while modifying garnishes to adapt to the ever coming summer.
Another great deal that we are now offering for the summer is the addition of a dessert to our $19.95 Dinner Prix Fixe. This 3 course meal is now available 7 days a week from 5 to 7 pm.

As always we value your opinion and feedbacks so as to keep improving and better ourselves.
Come and join us on this new adventure.

A bientôt,


Les Minots
