here's my short list (because the list is still VERY short - ie there aren't that many)



Volo cafe/bistro -  on Ditmars just past 29th st. (Open until pretty late. panini sandwiches, pasta dishes, (excellent couscous)nice pastries, cookies, good espresso, take-out quick italian dinner on the way home from work when im too lazy to cook



Fatty's Cafe/ Bar/ Bistro -  also on Ditmars. Cool bar, beergarden in back, serves brunch on the weekend, haven't tried food there, hear its good/ oh yeah, the bartender (Dino) is developing quite a reputation for his 'mojito'...



McCanns - huge wood sign, Ditmars (on the less cool side of 31st. St.) near Thirty One the new French restaurant I haven't tried yet/ Irish pub-ish place, tvs running with sports on if you like that kind of thing...



i guess that's about it - near Ditmars, at least, that i know of - but that isnt saying much
