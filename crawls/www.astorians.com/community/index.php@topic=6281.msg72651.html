hey everyone! we'd love to have you next week. membership has been a little low in the past few months... some new ideas and new people would be awesome!

i will warn you, i'm moving next saturday, so i might be a little frazzled on thursday nite, but i will be there.

if anyone wants to be put on the ACK email list, PM me and you'll be set.

just some things i want to throw out there -- ACK has always been a very community oriented group. people drop in, drop out, come in when they can. people who have just been sitting in the cafe see us and join, or tell others to join, and that's awesome too. i have always offered to teach knitting or crocheting for free. i'd like to keep everything at ACK for free as well.  we have done other small things like make valentine's for each other and people would bring construction paper, scissors, glue, etc to share with the group.  but i always ask that you purchase something from the venue (drink, snack, etc). 

now, please dont get me wrong, i'm not knocking Freeze Peach, but sometimes i feel other crafts may not be conducive to the location. it's a bit tight.... so things like painting, maybe even sewing, can be cumbersome in that space without a good table, lighting, maybe even some patrons may become a little frustrated if they are close by needles or paints, or other crafty substances.  that's why the yarn and needlecrafts have been doing well there i suppose.

i am not going to cancel ACK. but i would welcome discussion of craft mafia like things and participate in them, so long as it doesnt conflict with ACK dates.  a location that might have more space, and bigger tables where we can spread out would be awesome.  when i read all this, i immediately thought of that other thread that is going around about 'hanging out space' if that ever comes about, that would be awesome place to do things like this.  (or at least, it seems like it...)

yes, i do agree that there's a lot of crafty stuff going on in brooklyn, and not enough here in Astoria/LIC.... i KNOW there's lots of crafty people here.... let's all get together and make stuff! yay!

can i get a show of hands (hahahah) of how many of you are planning to come out next week? if you could just post up by next wednesday (or pm me to let me know if you want) that would be great! that way i can try to give a heads up to Freeze Peach and see if we can clear out a space..... thanks!
