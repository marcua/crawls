I'm from Bridgeport, CT (it's on the water, about 1/2 way between Stamford and New Haven). Not NYC, or even NY State, but I still consider it part of the NYC area (its 45-60 minutes in the car). And, I would get New York and Hartford/New Haven tv channels - so CBS is 2 and 3, NBC is 4 and 6, ABC is 7 and 8, FOX is 5 and 25...I think we might have even had 3 UPNs. And yes, I've had conversations with my dad about which ABC is "better", even though they're exactly the shows (news was different though, obviously).
