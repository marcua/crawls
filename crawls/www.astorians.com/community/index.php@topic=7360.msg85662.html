So I went by on Friday and took a look at a 1 bedroom apartment that was about ready to be occupied.  As NTrain mentioned, the building (elevator, hallways etc..) and the grounds looked to be very well maintained.  The apartment itself had a really good layout with lots of light.  The current price for a 1 bedroom is $147,000, $568.50 monthly rent, a minimum 20% down payment and minimum $55,000 annual income.  All in all not a bad deal when you think of the high prices that their asking for apartments in new construction these days.

As for the whole dog debate - I grew up on Roosevelt Island and dogs had been prohibited from there since the start.  My parents knew about it going in and it was something that you lived with no matter how much you wanted a pup.  I understand that as soon you start making exceptions for one person, you're going to have people breaking down your door looking for the same.
