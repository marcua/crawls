Have any of you heard about the hacking going on at Flickr? Wanted to know what you all thought about it.

Here is some info:

Dear Flickr friends.

During the last several weeks I have seen numerous friends stalked on flickr by a very determined hacker. Several of them had accounts hacked or spoofed and one even had a home computer broken into and files removed.

One of my best friends quit flickr over this. I won't name her, but many of you know whom I'm talking about. When she gathered information about the hacking, and tried to give this information to flickr, they blew her off. Several times. This scumbag is somehow involved in child pornography. I'm not sure of the details, but my friend was scared enough that she quit flickr. It was a great loss to me, and I cannot even begin to imagine her pain and anger over this.

This person is determined. Today, they posted a message to several hundred flickr people from a hacked account. This is being talked about at:
flickr.com/forums/help/34075/
flickr.com/forums/help/34219/
flickr.com/forums/help/34168/


This message said:

(I have deleted the name of the person who was being harrassed)

Hello,

I Was just wondering, somebody told me you sell naked
images of XXXXXXXXXX (XXXXXX's
www.flickr.com/people/XXXXXXXXXXX/ ) daughter(s)
and that he has bought some off of you before. I Was just
wondering how much you charge, and do you accept paypal? If
not then what methods of payment do u accept? Thx and hope
to hear from u soon.


Enough. Flickr needs to act. They need to identify this person and have their attorneys assist the FBI on criminally prosecuting this person. This person is hurting friends that we care about.

The only way I can think of to get Yahoo to take this seriously is either to shame them into it via a viral email/photo campaign and/or to attract the press.

Please email flickr. Tell them that enough is enough.

Thank you.

p.s.
