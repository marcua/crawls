The nice thing about this gentle community of LPW is that we all win, we just win at different times.  And for different reasons.

I win because I finally got it together to go some place before work, now that I'm back to my regular hours (I start work in the late afternoon).  I went to the last day of the NY400 Old Amsterdam/New Amsterdam event in Bowling Green.  In miniature old-style Dutch houses vendors sold things.  One man was making tiny sculptures from edible flowers.  They stay preserved indefinitely, so I bought one to keep and one to eat.  At the wooden shoe vendor I bought a small deflt shoe that holds a clothes brush, and the shoe maker carved my name on it.  I had a beef kroket (and figured out it's a croquette) and poffertjes (everybody has a zeppole).  I debated buying any cheese because I would be carrying it on the train to work, but I did buy vacuum sealed smoked gouda and it's in a fridge here at work (with dire warnings about stealing it).  While I was eating I listened to contestants answer quiz questions about the Netherlands.  Prizes were discounts at Dutch restaurants in our area.  I passed up tryng fish sandwiches, as they smelled like fish sandwiches.  Other vendors were selling crystal sculptures, tulip bulbs, potted flowers and other plants, hot house grown vegetables, other cheeses, photos of Amsterdamn, and a cookbook of Dutch foods.  And there was a windmill.

NY400:
http://www.ny400.org/

the Wiki on Dutch cuisine
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Dutch_cuisine
 
En nu, win ik!

En ik mis ook manusje van alles.
