Greater Astoria Historical Society - two events for June

SATURDAY, June 15, 2013 @ 1 P.M. CIVIL WAR ROUNDTABLE: GETTYSBURG
The History Roundtable at G.A.H.S.
The five-year remembrance of the American Civil War continues by commemorating the 150th Anniversary
of the Battle of Gettysburg (July 1 – 3, 1863). The event features a blue ribbon panel of past and current Civil War
Roundtable presidents and a commander of the Sons of the Union Veterans.
. William F. Finlayson ~ The 150th Anniversary of the Battle of Gettysburg
Bill is the Current President of the Civil War Roundtable of New York and his direct ancestor was the commander of the
‘Monitor’
. George Weinmann ~ 1938: The 75th Anniversary of the Last Gettysburg Reunion
George is New York Commander of the Sons of the Union Veterans and a Trustee of the Greenpoint Monitor Museum.
 . Patrick Falci ~ The 20th Anniversary of the making of ‘Gettysburg’
Patrick is the past President of the Civil War Roundtable of New York. He played the role of “General A.P. Hill” in the cast
and was historical advisor to the movie.  Fee: $5, free to GAHS, Greenpoint Monitor Museum and Civil War Roundtable members
************************************************
SATURDAY, June 22, @ Noon 12:00 P.M.
CORONA - Forgotten NY Walking Tour
Join Forgotten NY's Kevin Walsh and GAHS’ Rich Melnick in exploring Corona, Queens, visiting several historic
locales such as the former Linden Lake, the site of a former racetrack, the highlights of the western end of
Flushing Meadows Corona Park, several historic homes and churches. Have an ice at the Lemon Ice King!
Start Location: Meet on plaza at Roosevelt Avenue and National Street, below 103rd Street/Corona Plaza
station, #7 train.
Start Time: Noon 12 P.M. Length of Tour: approx. 3 HRS
Fee: GAHS members $15, $20 to the public.
