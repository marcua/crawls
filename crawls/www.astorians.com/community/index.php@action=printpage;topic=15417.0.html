Title: Re: IS 126 - what's your opinion?
Post by: Eliza5 on June 29, 2009, 08:39:53 PM
To be honest, we applied to other schools to avoid having our son having to go to 126, which is also our zoned school.  (Fortunately he was accepted to a citywide school in Manhattan and will be starting there in September for 6th grade.)  I'm sorry to tell you that I.S. 126 still has a bad reputation but several of my son's classmates are going there in September and they are good kids who just didn't get any of their choices on the middle school application process.  We also know some parents who are putting their kids in Catholic school to avoid 126.

The good news is that 126 has a new principal who came last year and he is really trying to turn things around.  He visited my son's school and talked to the kids and then also came and spoke to the parents and he seemed sincere about wanting to improve the school and change its reputation.  Maybe your friend can contact the principal and see if what he has to say eases his concerns.  Also, if the child has good grades, there are several 7-12 public schools that he can apply to if he can stick it out at 126 for one year.

Hope this helps!
