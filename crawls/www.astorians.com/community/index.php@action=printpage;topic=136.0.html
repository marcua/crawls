Flux Factory is pleased to present...





Cute and Scary



an exhibit showcasing works by emerging artists raised within a milieu of comics and Japanese animation,

exploring the dark side beneath the Hello Kitty exterior.



Something in the work of these artists remains childlike and innocent. But a deeper look reveals a poignant sinister aspect.

An unsolved dichotomy makes the work feel both empowered and vulnerable.



This ambiguity translates into works that come across as fun and disturbing, dark and light, cute and scary without really favoring one or the other. Subverted clich√©s of feminism, pop culture or eroticism mingle with nightmarish visions.

 

This show will entice kids and make grownups shiver.
