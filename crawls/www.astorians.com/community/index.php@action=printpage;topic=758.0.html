Title: Thinking of consolidating "Food" catagory...
Post by: merm on September 15, 2005, 11:24:09 AM
Greetings all,

I'm thinking of merging this catagory with "Where can I find ___ in Astoria". (Of course I will update that catagory to include a description that includes the title that food, festivals, and restaurants should be posted there).

The reasons being:

1 - Because it has the lowest post count. People are posting things related to food in the "general" area, or in the "Where can I find" area.

2 - I'm finding that most of the posts that are related to "food, restaurants, or festivals" are not even being posted here. Most of them are in the "where can I find..." or in the "general discussion" sections. This means that "food" related posts are scattered throughout the board and that this catagory isn't getting used. At the same time, most of the posts in this catagory, could just as well fit in another. I believe that a streamline of catagories is in order.

3 - Because consolidation will mean that more people will see the individual posts and potentially respond to them by having more posts in less catagories.

I'm open to thoughts on this.
