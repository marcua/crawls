Hi Jonathan
Its not tacky and its only a little casual, but I wanted to tell you about our Valentine's Day dinner date at Lil Bistro 33!! It was upon your esteemed recommendation that we try it, so what better evening than when they're offering an innovative tasting menu? Well I must tell you it was super-sweet kickass and sumptuous and great all around. Thanks for the tip! Despite the overwhelmed wait staff (they only had two servers and people were knocking down the doors) the food was served promptly and without fault. The tasting menu was exemplary, with their fried goat cheese the stuff dreams are made of! Their sushi course was strong and the dessert, while not heart-stopping, was comforting and solid (puff pastry square, chocolate-BATHED strawberry). The wine pairings were also inspired but not pretentious. Thanks for turning us on to such a neighborhood prize (secretive as it is-- in the daytime you wouldn't even know they were there!)

cheers
annie
