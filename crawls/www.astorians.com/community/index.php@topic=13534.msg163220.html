Its great for things you use a lot of. If you are single, you should definitely consider sharing the membership with someone and maybe splitting the stuff you buy. Their meat and veggies are very fresh-but honestly, whenever I buy fresh stuff from there part of it ends up going bad because we just can't eat it all. Cleaning supplies, vitamins, ibuprofen, shampoo, meds, batteries, office supplies, frozen foods, coffee, cereal are great buys at Costco though. You can also find nice gifts there and seasonal items too. Nice ready made platters or cook at home items as well. Good place to buy food when you are having a party.
