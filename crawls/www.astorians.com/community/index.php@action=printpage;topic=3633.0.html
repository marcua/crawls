Hi all,
 
Just a quick note that we've received inquiries from people who'd like to attend just Kol Nidre and/or Yom Kippur services at the Astoria Center of Israel. I spoke to the president and she suggested we offer a reduced rate on tickets now that Rosh Hashanah has passed.
 
If you don't already have a place to go and would like to attend, we've reduced the full price of $150 High Holiday tickets to just $50pp, which includes tickets for Kol Nidre services tonight and Yom Kippur services all day tomorrow. There's also a $15pp charge if you'd like to come to the Break-the-Fast meal.
 
As always, everyone is most welcome! If you do wish to attend, please contact us at: info@astoriacenterofisrael.org to get your name on the list for tickets and the meal. Our Break-the-Fast is a light kosher meal, including dessert, from a wonderful local caterer.
 
The schedule of events can be found here: http://www.astoriacenter.org/events.htm (http://www.astoriacenter.org/events.htm) .

Astoria Center of Israel is an egalitarian synagogue that maintains a conservative Jewish tradition and is built around a warm, wonderful group of people that embraces a wide variety of perspectives on Judaism in a joyful and inclusive community.
Everyone is welcome!
