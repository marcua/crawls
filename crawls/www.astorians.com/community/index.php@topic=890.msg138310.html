I think murph is talking about a flash drive - something like this.  They are much sturdier and more compact than a floppy disk, and any computer that has a USB port (which is all of them now) can use one.  You use it just like a floppy, to copy individual files to backup or transfer from computer to computer - but it even fits on your keyring.  Not only that, it holds much, much more data (the one I linked to holds 4 GB -- that's 4096 MB, whereas a floppy holds only 1.44 MB of data).
