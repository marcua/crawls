Ahh Newtown Farmer's Market.  Slightly overpriced, a bit random, and in desperate need of better management.  And yet, I love it.  The produce is wonderful and inexpensive.  Staff members are courteous and generally give you a lower than retail price for items that are not scanned .  Newtown carries tofu and frozen vegetarian delights that prior to their existence, one would have to venture to Ditmars for.  I still do shopping elsewhere, but for a neighborhood littered with bodegas, take-out chinese, and auto shops its a godsend.  I'll patronize as much as possible just to keep a decent grocery store in the area.
