Title: Re: 21 St. and 29th Ave Beer Distributor
Post by: Cotto on December 17, 2011, 04:03:51 PM
Tri-boro Beverage on Astoria Blvd (South) and 42nd St. has seriously stepped their game up. I walked by yesterday and noticed a completely revamped beer room, which they said happened at the end of the summer. The selection is much improved and different than Euro Market; Euro Market of course is heavy on the imports, while most of Triboro's selection was domestic beers. The prices were reasonable to slightly on the cheap side (I find Euro Market to be on the slightly pricey side).

Examples for any beer geeks out there: 750mL Rodenbach Grand Cru for 8.99, 6-packs of Left Hand Milk Stout for 10.99.
