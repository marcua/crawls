Title: This Sunday, May 6, Trinity Lutheran Church Welcomes Artists
Post by: ArtsMarketing on May 03, 2007, 07:15:12 PM
This Sunday, May 6, Trinity Lutheran Church will celebrate the Arts in Worship.
We invite you to join us for worship services at 10:30 a.m. and enjoy a performance
of Liturgical dancers.  After the worship service, join us for fellowship in the
Undercroft for refreshments.  Visual and performing artists are welcome to bring
materials about their upcoming events to share with the Trinity Community.  Please
call 718-278-0036 for more info. Trinity Lutheran Church is located at 31-18 37th
Street, Long Island City.
Web site: http://home.earthlink.net/~trinityastoria/
