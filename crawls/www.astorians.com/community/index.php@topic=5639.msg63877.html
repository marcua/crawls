I'm starting a little jewelry company. It's called Firelight Jewelry.
I'm making earrings right now and am planning on expanding when i have a better set up to work with.
I started a MySpace page to get the word out and guage interest. I know I know, doing this on MySpace is kind of cliche but it's an easy way to start and show what i have going on.
So please check out the page:
http://www.myspace.com/firelightjewelry
and if you like what you see, friendquest me, leave me a comment and spread the love to your friends.
And post here!
If you want to buy something (i will love you forever) email me through myspace or at firelightjewelry@gmail.com

I'll be shopping my collection around Astoria soon, to hopefully place a few pieces in boutiques. I'll also be starting a shop on etsy.com but i'm taking this one small step at a time.

Also, my arists, anyone want to help me design my logo and mascot? I know what i want the mascot to be/look like. Unfortunatly my hands cannot replecate the picture in my head. And i have no graphic design skills whatsoever. Gals will be rewarded with a free pair of earrings of your choice. Dudes either get earrings (for your woman, your mom, you, whatever floats your boat) or a homemade dinner.

Thanks for the support everyone!
Jenn
(PS-my, as of now, 1 friend is me. if there's more than 1 then I'm numero uno. in case inquiring minds wanted to know. and if we aren't myspace buddies, hit up miss jennifer with some love).
