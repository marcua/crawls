Post by: veatnomeat on September 29, 2006, 10:21:05 PM
I boiled the tomatoes for a few minutes and peeled the skin over a bowl since it gets liquidy.  Once I collected enough tomatoes in the bowl, I put them in a ziploc freezer bag (1 quart size) with a few leaves of basil and a couple shakes of salt.  It came out to be about 8 bags.  There was excess tomato liquid -- I just put in a cup, added some lemon and salt (yummy fresh tomatoe juice.) sorry i didn't see this thread earlier.

Each bag is perfect as tomatoe sauce, or soup base, etc.

Title: Re: Tomato Share
Post by: merm on September 30, 2006, 09:07:04 PM
I usually put my leftover tomatoes in my Vitamix and blend for about 30 seconds.

Then I put this into a pan with olive oil and let it cook for about 4 hours on low.

Then I add a bunch of other veggeis (onions, olives, squash whatever), season (basil, oregano, salt, sugar, whatever) and a bunch of red wine and leave it go for another 30 minutes or so.

If you want it to taste like store bought sauce just add ketchup.

Makes a great sauce. Not sure if it's good for canning, but great for spaghetti.
