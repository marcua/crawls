THE STEPHEN HALL HUGE TWO BEDROOM CO-OP UNIT

The Stephen Hall, located in The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights, has a huge two bedroom, which is available to sell immediately.

Both bedrooms are considered master bedrooms, with an attractive freshly painted formal foyer; enormous living room/dining room combo; renovated and freshly painted eat-in-kitchen with granite counter-tops and stainless steel appliances; renovated bathroom with mosaic tiles; parquet floors; elegant arches; and minutes to all trains, which include the 7, E, F, M, N, & G.

ASKING: $359K, MAINTENANCE: $915, DOWNPAYMENT: 20%

EXCLUSIVELY LISTED BY: Marlene Flores, 917-208-6709, Fenwick Keats Goodstein Real Estate, http://www.fenwick-keats.com/
