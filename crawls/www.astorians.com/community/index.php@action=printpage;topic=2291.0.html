Post by: mcdirk on June 22, 2006, 02:44:54 PM
Howard Dean is doing a very smart thing with his 50 state strategy.  I think it will bear fruit in the long-term.  I still contend, however, that abolishing the "constituency desks" will end up hurting us.  The idea that anyone and everyone should be doing outreach to all Democratic constituents is a good thing, and one that I think everyone would agree with - but the without people who know their communities and are charged with performing set tasks (targeted literature, fundraising, parade contingents, lists, endorsements, press conferences) in each of the big Democratic constituencies, I think things will fall through the crack.
Great article though - and a good topic.
