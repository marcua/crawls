Sorriso's and Gian Piero
« on: February 16, 2008, 05:15:27 PM »
I know that both of these places have been briefly discussed in other threads, but I just wanted to show some appreciation for both of these places.  Sorriso's is a small, old school Italian meat shop. They still make thier own sausages, meatballs, sauces, mozzarella, and lots of other stuff, and they have TONS of imported Italian product.  Gian Piero is the bakery that I've gone to since I moved here 5 years ago.  Big selection, incredibly delicious...well...everything (especially the ricotta cheesecake). 
I bring these two places up, because it's places like this that I would hate to lose in the neighborhood, and I feel like across the country these are the amazing, local establishments that are disappearing, and one of the biggest reasons I absolutely love Astoria.  Just walking into either of these places is enough to turn my frown upside down (eating speck on fresh Italian bread doesn't hurt either).
Both places are located on 30th Ave between 44th and 45th.
