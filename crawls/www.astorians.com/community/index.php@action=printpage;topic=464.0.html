Title: tai chi
Post by: jayme on June 22, 2005, 10:30:52 AM
So the group was finally finishing at the perfect time to ask for info.

The Tai Chi happens daily (weather permitting) at 6:30.  Warm ups last 25-20 minutes, with 25-30 minutes of flow/practice/pose following.  The informal class is taught by "an old chinese dude" and I think it might be free (not entirely certain on this point).  Certainly people are free to join in but it is recommended that you show up at 6:30 for warm up.
