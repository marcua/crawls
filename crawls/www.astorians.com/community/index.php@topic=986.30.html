I hate the grocery stores heres, especially the trade fair on Ditmars and 36 or 37th? The smell of the meats in the meat section makes me want to vomit.

I rather like the Trade Fair on Ditmars.  Haven't had too much of a problem, but then again I don't buy meats there.  I buy a lot of produce right now at the Top Tomato - they have had some really nice things lately, including tasty tangerines for 10 for a dollar.  I bought great broccoli and spinach there, too.  When the CSA starts up again, of course I'll get my produce that way and from the greenmarket as much as possible.  I feel a sense of adventure in the Trade Fair, as there is always something interesting to try.  Of course, there are things to avoid and I don't buy meat there.  I love that you can find multiple kinds of dulce de leche, arepas, latin cheeses, and two kinds of organic non-gmo tofu!  There are other things I love there, including the Portuguese bread, Sabra hummus (aka "crack"), a whole slew of pita, the dried apricot roll, and bulk halvah of all sorts.  Not to mention any kind of Goya product ever made!

After living in whitewashed Long Island, having this grocery store with so many interesting products is so refreshing.  So is having a choice - I can get my produce one place, good cheese another (Rosario's), bread another (various), meats at meat markets (and freshly killed in certain places), and organic/healthy/alternative foods another (Fresh Start).  There's even Fresh Direct. Astoria has so many choices!  It's just great.
