Title: Re: Cooking ideas
Post by: lanseaux on July 12, 2007, 02:35:45 PM
I often make a summer entree of a chicken breast, avocado and Belgian endive salad, served chilled or at room temperature.  The original recipe was an avocado and endive salad from the Barefoot Contessa, and I found that adding cubed grilled or poached chicken breast gives it enough heft to make it an entree.  I'd use 1/2 chicken breast, 1/2 avocado, and 1/2 head endive per person.

Here's (http://www.foodnetwork.com/food/recipes/recipe/0,1977,FOOD_9936_23572,00.html) the recipe for the original salad from the Food Network that includes the mustard vinaigrette.
