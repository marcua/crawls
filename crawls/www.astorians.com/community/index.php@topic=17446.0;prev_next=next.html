I'm not sure how great Tastee Corners' breakfasts are; but their wraps are always great (esp the tastee corner wrap & the corned beef wrap) and so is the deluxe grilled chicken salad. I still can't believe that I can get a huge wrap and a baked potato for $6.95 and the salad for just under $10 is good for 2 meals. The portions are huge and they are insanely cheap for this area. For diner food it's cheap and hits the spot. It's located on 30th ave right next to the train on 31st st and has free and relatively fast delivery, check it out. They should have takeout menus at the front counter.
