Title: 2nd ANNUAL ASTORIA WATER WALK - Sundays, August 1, 8 and 15
Post by: DomNation on July 28, 2010, 03:13:28 AM
   

The Astoria Park Alliance Presents the 2nd Annual Astoria Water Walk!
August 1th, August 8th and August 15th from 1-7pm
Shore Boulevard in Astoria Park, Queens
Three Days of FUN on a Temporary Pedestrian Street

Astoria Water Walk brings the DOT’s successful Summer Streets Program to the Western Queens Waterfront. Spontaneous relaxation, as well as fun & games on Shore Blvd is expected and encouraged. All activities are FREE.

Astoria Park is Western Queens’ largest park and features tennis courts, a running track, playgrounds, basketball courts, bucolic lawns and the City’s largest outdoor swimming pool. Its East River shoreline provides breathtaking views of the skyline and two bridges. It is heavily used by children, families, boaters, cyclists, runners, and motorists and is home to fish, crabs, birds, and aquatic plants.

Shore Boulevard will be closed to motorized vehicles during the Astoria Water Walk.

Partners include: Green Shores NYC, the NYC Department of Transportation, Edible Queens, Astoria Music & Arts, Partnerships for Parks, NYC Department of Parks & Recreation, BIG! NYC, the Rock Health & Fitness, GrowNYC

August 1: Kids and Family Fun!
The lineup for this day includes:
The Astoria Pet Parade, FDNY Smoke House, Curious Frog presents A Midsummer's Night Dream, puppet show for the kids, Red Storm Drum Dance Troupe, Games and more!

August 8: Taste the Neighborhood!
The lineup for this day includes:
Representative food establishments provide free samplings, Music by Cat Gut, Clothes and Recipe Swap, Environmental Education, Nature Walk, A Midsummer's Night Dream, puppet show for the kids!

August 15: LIVE MUSIC and Clothing Swap!
The lineup for this day includes:
An eclectic mix of live music co-sponsored by Astoria Music and Arts. Opera meets Irish Rock, ukulele meets metal. Something for everyone! Plus a clothing swap! Bring your gently used old clothes and find ones that are new to you! Books and other small items welcome too. Anyone can participate.
