OK, I have to chime in because I LOVE Martha's. Now, I only get one thing there (raspberry cupcakes!), so I can't testify to many other items on their menu. However, I don't think I've seen raspberry cupcakes anywhere else and theirs are delicious. The frosting somehow tastes just like fresh raspberries! Raspberries are my favorite fruit, so that's a major part of the appeal for me, and obviously someone who didn't like raspberries as much as I do wouldn't enjoy the cupcakes as much.

That being said, I did have a red velvet cake cupcake once and did not enjoy it. I also had a Napoleon and it was good, but overly sweet for my taste. Since those two trips, I've gotten nothing but the raspberry cupcakes and been very happy.

I, too, have wondered how often they change the cupcake display. Actually, all the baked goods. They can't all be fresh every day!

My husband likes to get the gelato during the summer and seems to enjoy it very much (the toffee coffee flavor).
