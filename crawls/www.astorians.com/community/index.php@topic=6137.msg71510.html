Weekend update on Twiggy:

She seems to have adjusted to her new room (my bathroom), but she often enjoys her time to get out and explore a bit.  Twiggy has to alternate apartment free time with the dog of the apartment (who is very jealous and curious about the new beast in the bathroom).

While we had taken her towel out for washing, she was enjoying looking out for hiding spots in her new room, but our kitty fort seems to be maintaining its barriers well.  She has made her bed in her original carrier box with one side folded down and her towel inside.  She has an orange nightlight so she doesn't get scared of the dark.

Not quite sure why, but she has had a bit of a rough time with kitty "morning sickness" both last night and today.  She is not keeping down wet or dry food or water.  I have called the vet and we will be visiting tomorrow to find out what's going on.  Will post update/major developments.  For tonight, she's getting some extra TLC and seems ok- still purring and laying around. 

Will be posting some new pictures from today- tried to get more head/profile shots.  Overall, she's been a lovely houseguest and she seems to find the accommodations suitable.
