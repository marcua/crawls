Marine Tank Lovers of Astoria[/glow]

In my list of loves of my life I must include both Astoria and my marine tank.  I have worked for high end custom built marine tanks (shark tanks, lion fish tanks... (200+ gal)) on Madison Ave. and loved every day. I have my own tank at home in Astoria, although not as large as the Madison Ave. Shop... but I love it anyways.  I have also had the honor of working with David Blaine on his Lincoln Center feat in which he lived for a week inside a customized sphere shaped tank. One of my closest friends is one of NYC top marine tank installers and maintainers.

Since I am no billionaire (or millionaire for that fact) I thought I would reach out to my fellow Astorians to find like minded persons who also have a love of marine tanks.  I would like to be able to connect with other marine tank owners, get tips, learn about places to get supplies and go from there.  I hope that this community board also has marine lovers, like me.

I started my marine tank experience with my former boyfriend over 8 yrs ago.  While he may be gone (not really gone, just dating a guy who is not me) my love of marine tanks, reef tanks is still with me.

I've never started a forum topic before, but hopefully some one will chime in and we go from there.  I would love to hear about the tanks my fellow Astorians have.  If enough members chime in and have tanks, I would love to create a web page or on line group where we can post photos of our tanks and species we care for.

Lets go Astoria!

Cheers,

Grimace Bingers
