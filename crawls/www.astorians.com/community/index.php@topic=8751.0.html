Aces is still open, last time I was down there.  There's also a well-received Thai restaurant on 36th Ave, and El Sitio de Astoria is on 31st and 36th Ave (Cuban food).  A little further is Malagueta (Brazilian) on 36th Ave near 29th, and the new Rest-Au-Rant wine bar on 36th Ave and 30th Street.  Sunswick has decent bar food, yep.  Oh, and there is a new Arepas Cafe on 36th Ave near 34th Street.  Venezuelan food.  Napoli on 35th Ave near 34th, I think.  And don't forget the crepe place on 36th Ave near 33rd Street, too, Cafe Triskell!
