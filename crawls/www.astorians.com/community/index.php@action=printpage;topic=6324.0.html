Title: Crescent and Vine
Post by: rjk on May 12, 2007, 07:13:57 PM
I went to Crescent and Vine (located at Ditmars and Crescent) Tuesday night for it's opening and thought that this board might like to know about the place as I felt very comfortable there with my boyfriend.

It is a really great atmosphere, a nice tapas selection, paninis, great wine selection and beer on tap as well. It is a nice addition to the neighborhood and gay clientele will definitely feel comfortable there.
