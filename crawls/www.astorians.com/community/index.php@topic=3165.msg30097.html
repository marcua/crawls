Here's what you do...  you have to "rip" that particular CD with i-Tunes.  Left click on the begining song so that it's highlighted and then go to the ending song, but before you click on it, hold down the shift key.  When you left click the last song while holding down the shift key, the songs in between that and the first will highlight and i-Tunes will rip it as all one song.   I used this for Queen's "We Will Rock You-We Are The Champions" and a few Boston tunes.  When I get around to ripping "The Wall", I'm sure I'll use it several times there.
