Title: Re: new hooka place on ditmars?
Post by: cabanird on January 08, 2006, 12:25:12 AM
hey!

just hung out at this place with some fellow Astorians this evening.

the  place is called Tutankhamun Cafe.
28-09 Ditmars Blvd.
Astoria, NY 11105

we smoked some shisha, i had a "saloop" (abbey called it by another name, but it's creamy, milky hot drink, with nuts on top, and honey.)

they have a big list of different flavored shisha, assortment of juices which my two buddies enjoyed thoroughly, and hot drinks. (they do not have the desserts in yet, which was dissappointing, but i have hope.)

i like the atmosphere. there were some older arabic men smoking all together, but then there was a nice group of non arabic people too.

apparently they are open till 5am every single day! and i've heard rumors that belly dancing starts at some point in the evening.

i'm glad this cafe landed so close to us!
