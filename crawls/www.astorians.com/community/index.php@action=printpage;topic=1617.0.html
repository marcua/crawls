I taught myself the basics of HTML and style sheets from this site:

http://webmonkey.com

There's a beginners section that I found helpful. Page layout was always a huge headache for me until I just found a style sheet sample I liked and used that.

http://harpold.com/homepaged

I don't think these styles have been updated in years, and they're certainly on the earlier side of HTML development, but maybe something there inspires you.

If you don't have a web page program like Dreamweaver or GoLive, you can still use the Composer utility from Mozilla.

On the main page of Wikipages (http://wikipages.com/index.php/Home), you'll see a blue box in the top at the center. Inside this is a text field. Enter the name of the business in this field, and press the "Create new entry" button.
