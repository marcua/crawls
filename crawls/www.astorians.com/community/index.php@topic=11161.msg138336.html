Ok I went to Agnanti the other day and we got the stuffed kalamari. It sounds delicious, but BEWARE! If you are squeamish about weird looking food then do NOT get this.

It actually tasted pretty good, but it is not the calamari Americans are used to. It is a huge piece of calamari that looks like a mini version of Jaba the Hutt. And when you cut it, all of the "stuffing" (that looks gross but tastes good) comes oozing out in a very unappetizing manner.

Overall it tasted fine, but it really looks nasty. Not only were we grossed out by the way it looked, but two other tables ordered this dish and they reacted the same way. Luckily that kind of stuff doesnt bother us too much, because we got past it and enjoyed the appetizer. But we were with a friend who almost vomited when we cut into it. It was funny.

Why is this place packed???   I need to rant a little about our dinner there....

My MIL order a fish special ($32!) and it was good, but that is crazy especially since all it came with were some cold greens.
