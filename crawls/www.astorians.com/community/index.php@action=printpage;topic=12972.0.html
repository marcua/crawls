Title: NY Cares - Winter Wishes Program
Post by: Mer on December 02, 2008, 01:19:11 PM
Hey fellow Astorians -

My friend emailed a bunch of us about the need for more volunteers for this year's NY Cares' Winter Wishes Program.  Below I've directly quoted his email to us.  This is one of my favorite volunteer/charity activities and it's a bit sad (although, expected) that they are also hurting for volunteers during this slow economic season.  Anyway, I thought I'd help get the word out:



Quote
NYCares receives letters from families that are not otherwise able to afford holiday gifts. Then, they look for volunteers who are willing to accept one of those letters and fulfill the wishes of one or more people, whether they be a child, adolescent, adult or senior. The giver gets to select an age group and number of people for whom he or she will buy gifts.

The point is that, because the economy is suffering, this charity drive is too. However, I believe that giving in times of scarcity is more urgent, not less. As it stands, there are still about 5000 letters waiting to be answered.

Please, give some consideration to taking part in this charity. Even if you don't want to take part, please tell others in the NYC area about it.

You can follow the link below to learn more.

http://nycares.org/volunteer/annual_events/winter_wishes/index.php

Thank you for taking the time to read this, and happy holidays to you all.
