Seeking Roommate April 1st - August 31st
« on: March 23, 2009, 03:33:30 PM »
Come live with a fun young couple in a sunny furnished bedroom! Our place is between the Steinway St. stop on the R/V/G trains and the Broadway stop on the N/W trains...

The apartment is a very spacious, renovated, ground-floor 3-bedroom apartment. The room we're renting is on the opposite side of the apartment from our bedroom (and railroad music room), which provides a nice level of privacy. It has a large kitchen and living room, all-new appliances (including gas range and a DISHWASHER!), tile floor in the kitchen, and new hardwood floors in the living room and "your" bedroom. It’s a cozy and well-appointed place to live! Steps to laundromats, grocery store, coffee shop, and dry cleaners. Short walk to many of Astoria's best restaurants and cafés.


We’re looking to fill the "move-in ready" room April 1st. We'd like our prospective roommate to stay through August with the potential to continue on a month-to month basis through the end of the year. The sunny, spacious, 135 sq. ft. bedroom is FULLY FURNISHED with a full-size bed, night table, table lamp, dresser, storage bench, bookshelf, mirror with hooks, full length mirror, laundry hamper, power strip, window A/C unit, 27" TV, DVD player, cable with DVR, HBO and Showtime. Rent also includes a large closet, located in the apartment's main hallway. Just bring your clothes and stuff and you are set up!

The shared living room has a large and comfy couch, dinner table, and a full home theater/entertainment center with flat-screen HDTV, HD cable/DVR, up-converting DVD player, iPod/iPhone dock, wireless iTunes, all in full digital surround sound. Wireless high-speed internet is provided throughout the apartment.

We’re looking for a roommate (male or female) that is responsible, respectful, adult, and personable. We like to keep the place clean and want someone who is of the same mindset. Common areas should stay picked up, dishes should be put in the dishwasher, and regular cleaning of the kitchen/bathroom/living room will be expected and duties shared. We both work Monday through Friday from 8am & 9am to 5pm in Manhattan and will need to make sure that morning schedules are not an issue as the bathroom is shared. We don't mind the occasional visitor, but please, no extended-stay guests or live-in boyfriends/girlfriends.

Rent for the FURNISHED room is $975, and includes all utilities and amenities: electric, gas, internet, cable/DVR/HBO/Showtime/etc. We require one month's rent security deposit at time of move-in. We can provide more info, measurements, details, photos, whatever, upon request.

email us: pax.and.mary@gmail.com
