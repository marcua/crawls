Title: Re: Where to eat on Easter?
Post by: sassyjass on March 30, 2009, 04:51:36 PM
My parents and I have gone on quite a few holidays to Trattoria L'Incontro.  The place isn't cheap but the food is always delicious.  I believe the restaurant is serving a prix fixe menu on Easter.  If you do plan to go there, be sure to make reservations as soon as possible, since Trattoria gets very crowded even on regular Sundays.
