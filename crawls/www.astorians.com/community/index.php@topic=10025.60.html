YES!!!! I'll always remember how at the end of Forever (spoiler alert), Kathy's father tells her, "Theo called"--it really brings it home that her relationship with Michael is really over, and that life goes on. I always had it in the back of my mind when I was growing up, so it really prepared me for reality. Oh, and for the record, I read Forever when I was about 13 or so, and by then I had already read my share of bodice rippers, which I read on my grandmother's front porch, and FYI Forever wasn't worse than any of those...if anythign it was realistic, informative.
