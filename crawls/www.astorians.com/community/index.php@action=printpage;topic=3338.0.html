Title: Customer service phone departments! ARGHHH!
Post by: Jonathan on September 11, 2006, 11:32:07 AM
Ive been dealing with lots of customer service recently for my new business space.  Why why why do they waste so much time with useless talk?  Its part of this whole new "how can we serve you" crap campaign.

You call to get info on something with their service and half the call is them asking:
what they can do to beter serve you
how can they be of assistance over and above what they are already helping you out on
now that we have provided you the requested info what else can we do for you
do yuou feel that I have provided you with superior service and what else can i do for you

Just today one rep said to me: I will be placing you on hold to connect you with the proper dept. here is the dept's # just in case you get disconnected. If you are disconnected let me apologize in advance and tell you that it is not our intention to disconnect you. We pride ourselves on good customer service and would never want to disconnect you and provide bad customer service... this went on and on. 

Wouldnt it be better if they just provided help and requested info immediately, thanked you for the call and hung up?  I dont want to talk with them, I dont want to waste my time chatting with them about the weather, war or how they have never been to ny and its so different where there are in Kansas.

Just want to yell at them to shut up, shut up and say good bye.  Even worse is you dont want to be rude when someone is smiling at you through the phone and thanking you for speaking with them today but ARGHHHH, enough already.


Thank you all for this ranting space.  I feel better now.  God bless America.
