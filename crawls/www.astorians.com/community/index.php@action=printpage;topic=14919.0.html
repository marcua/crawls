Title: Lentini's Pizza II
Post by: Midnight_Highway on May 09, 2009, 09:19:01 PM
Great pizza place just opened on 34th ave and 31 street.  The place is still new, but it's one of the best pizza experiences I've had in NYC.  My gf's pizza was tasty and non-greasy; garlic knots great.  I ordered a meatball + penne platter, and it was likewise excellent.  Delivery was free.  Cannot  recommend this place highly enough.

Title: Re: Lentini's Pizza II
Post by: jenriet on June 29, 2009, 06:34:46 PM
We just got their menu delivered outside our door with a coupon so we decided to keep the oven off tonight and order instead. I have to say I was pretty pleased by the pizza - I consider it on par with Grand Avenue pizza. The sauce was garlicky, but also had a touch of sweetness, and the cheese was quite generous. It wasn't too greasy. The dough was fresh, but a tad inconsistent. Part of it was nice and crispy and part of it was soft and less cooked. On the whole, however, it hit the spot for delivery pizza. Not on par with Michael Angelo's or Vesta's pizza, but I consider those to be in a different realm. As far as good solid delivery pizza goes, I think Lentini's may be our go-to place from now on, especially given the reasonableness of the prices. An extra-large cheese pie is $11! All in all, I'd recommend checking it out.  :mrgreen:
