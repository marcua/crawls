Congrats on the boyfriend's big move!

I don't know if there are any krav instructors in the Astoria area, but Commando Krav does have at least three level III instructors with clubs in the New York City area: http://www.commandokravmaga.com/html/locations/ny.html

You also might want to check out Premier Martial Arts.  I have never trained with them, but my friend trained in krav for a little while at one of their Connecticut locations.  They have at least two NYC locations that I am aware of, and there is a good chance they have a krav instructor here.  Again, not in Astoria.

Your boyfriend, being a krav enthusiast, might have an interest in training in Brazilian Jiu Jitsu or some of the other combatives these guys train in: http://www.combinedmartialarts.com/
I don't know much about them, but they are right on Steinway Street by the R stop, and from what I hear, they train hard.

Finally, if he has any interest in a more traditional art, here is the website for a local Shotokan style karate dojo: http://www.astoriakarate.com/
That's where I go.  I've been going there for a little over a year now.  I am an uncoordinated mess and terrible at karate, I admit, but I love going there.

Ugh, I had a long-ish post on here but I guess it got wiped out when the forum went Ker-plooey.

In short, I train under the Krav Maga Federation. No Astoria locations, but they do have a few places in the city. I train at the John Jay college location (58th and 10th), which offers free a intro class on Wednesday nights @ 5:45. You just have to call to reserve a spot: 212 781-7918.

I've been there for a little over a year. The instructors are awesome... skilled, intense, yet friendly. The student body is very diverse, focused and also rather friendly. There's a number of fellow Astoria people who train there too!

KMF's main site is here: http://kravmagafederation.com

Anyway, that's my spiel (again).   :-D
