Title: Queensview Nursery (and PreK) OPEN HOUSE for 2013-2014
Post by: jennifer on October 04, 2012, 06:12:54 PM
Queensview Nursery School (and PreK) is having its open house for the 2013 - 14 school year. OCTOBER 24 9:30 - 11am  OR 1pm - 2:30.  You need to call to reserve a space at the open house.  718-728-4164

Queenview accepts children 2.9 - 5 years.
They have just two classrooms with 2 teachers in each.  Last year as a "3" my son was in a morning class with 11 kids and afternoon had 9 (I think).  The "4s" (UPK) is generally bigger.  The class lists from last year show 14 in each class.
The school has been around since 1951 and the teachers have been there for generations.  They have an enormous wealth of experience between them.
The school has a small private playground and it's on the grounds of Queensview Coops, so it's quiet and shady.
They offer full or half day and 3 and 5 day options.

For the past couple of years I believe they have reached full enrollment for the next year by the end of October (I think they used to run a 2nd open house - but I don't think they need to any more).
