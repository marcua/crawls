Title: Queens is Green Fashion Show
Post by: robynsklar on February 06, 2009, 10:53:44 AM
Attention All Fashion Designers, Sewers, and Crafters
Participate in the second Queens is Green Fashion Show
Queens is Green Fashion Show
Long Island City/Astoria
April 18 from 6-8pm (tentative)

Green Fashion isn't just clothing made with sustainable fabrics like hemp
or bamboo silk, but also includes items that are handmade or refashioned
from recycled or reusable products -- and we would like to highlight designs
that have a local Queens connection.

To be eligible, please send us an email with the following items:

• A picture of your clothes or accessories – we need to see 3 items
either sketched or finished
• A short paragraph on how your items are environmentally friendly
• A short explanation on your connection with Queens, NY (i.e. resident,
work, born in Queens)

Deadline to enter is March 15th. If you have any questions about your
eligibility, please contact Robyn at queensisgreen@gmail.com

The catwalk is scheduled during the week of Earth Day to highlight clothing
and accessories that not only look good, but are good for the planet.
