Hey All -

I met a bunch of you at the Astorians Mixer this week (hi, again!) but for those I didn't get a chance to chat with, I wanted to share with you a great way to meet fellow Astorians!

Playing Dodgeball or 3-on-3 Basketball with fellow Astorians is a great way to meet people in your area.  You can join the charity-focused league as a whole team (an Astorians.com team maybe?) or as an individual/small group and we'll place you on a team.

Then after the games, everyone meets up for organized happy hours at local bars!  Pretty awesome, huh?

Anyway, I'd love to see an Astorians team (or at least a bunch of individuals) join the league, so feel free to reply to this post, or email me at alyssa@zogsports.org for more info.  You can register for both leagues at www.zogsports.org

Hope to see you all out there this winter!

Actually, it's not!  In fact, I'll print the prices right here so everyone can see them.

For a FULL season of Dodgeball (which is 6 regular season games and more if you make playoffs), it's $800/team (for a roster of 12 players, which works out to about $66 each) and $75 for an individual.  As  a member of a team, that works out to about $11 a game.

For a FULL season of 3-on-3 Basketball (which is 8 regular season games and one scrimmage - more games if you make playoffs), it's $745/team (for a roster of 6-7 players, which works out to about $106 each) and $130 for an individual.  As a member of a team, that works out to about $11.70 a game.

Hope to see you all out there this winter!  Please feel free to write me with ANY questions.

Again, you can register at www.zogsports.org.

Of course, and we are sad that you will not be joining us! 

For everyone else, it should also be added that a portion of this goes to charity.  In fact, it goes to charities our teams select themselves!  Each team picks a charity to play for at the beginning of the season.  During the season, we cull together a portion of what they pay to be in the league, as well as money from our partner bars where we send teams for happy hours.  At the end of the season, that money is given to winning teams in checks made out to their chosen charities.

To explain further, the rest of the cost goes to renting field and gym space, paying referees, and obtaining gym equipment.  To date we have more than 35,000 people playing with us in all 4 locations.  We have a lot of Astorians who are already planning to join us, many who joined us in the fall for our inaugural season, and we hope many more of you to come!
