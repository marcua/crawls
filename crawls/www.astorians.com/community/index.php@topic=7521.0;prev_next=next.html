Mastodon this coming Thursday night at Roseland. It may be sold out, but do whatever you can to see this band on this tour. There's heavy metal and there's hard rock. Mastodon are something new and special. They've transcended any specific genre utilizing aspects of metal, prog rock and even country-style guitar licks whenever necessary. Their musicianship is outstanding as is their songwriting. They are HEEEEAAAA-VEEEEE but intelligent as well. They shred but never lose focus of the broader composition. Just a truly great band most likely near the top of their game. I saw them in Saerville, NJ a couple of months ago and was floored. They really needed no opening act. At the end of their encore they looked physically and emotionally spent--like they had nothing left to give. Easily the best concert I've been to in years. Check em' out:

http://www.mastodonrocks.com
