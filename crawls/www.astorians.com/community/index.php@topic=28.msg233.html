We just moved to Astoria in August with our then-3 month old (from Seattle area), so i have no local information or resources, but am also desperate to hang out with other parents of young babies.

I just registered for the website because i was specifically interested in finding cool people to hang out with in the afternoons. (Evenings, i've got a husband to hang with, but daytime is one long, boring diaper change...)

As the weather gets warmer, there will be more parents in the park--but most of them have mobile kids.
i have found very little for non-mobile babies.

(ok, so sam is now "cruising"... but the fall was very lonely)

There's Bumbolee, but they are kind of expensive (imho)... but if i could afford it, I'd go to them.
they were doing stuff for kids at least as young as 6 months, maybe younger.
(i think i get a discount if you mention my name!)

For 2-4 yr olds, there's a place (Kid Crazy?) on 19th street i was impressed by-- but again, not helpful for under 2s.

I hate my doctor. So does my one friend-with-child, who i met waiting for the same doctor. But neither of us have heard of anything better, locally. You may want to stay with the manhattan ones. (And who do you like, since i expect my baby will get a sibling eventually, and i keep hearing horror stories of local births.
has no one heard of a doula around here???)

I would suggest meeting this tuesday afternoon, 3 pmish myself.

Or some other day of the week... but afternoons are really best for me...
