Title: Happy 50th Anniversary, Rizzo's Pizza!
Post by: ThePinkSuperhero on May 30, 2009, 01:34:35 PM
Stopped by Rizzo's on Steinway near 30th Ave on the way back from the gym for the 50th anniversary celebration.  They've got slices for 50 cents (limit 2 per person), sodas for $1 (one per person), and italian ice for 50 cents.  The line moved very quickly.  On a street where restaurants come and go in the blink of an eye (we noticed that Arunee Thai near Astoria Blvd seems to have closed down for good), 50 years is a great accomplishment.  Here's to 50 more, Rizzo's! :mrgreen:
