There is a place on Broadway and 37th, on the North side of the street in between Bai Sushi and the window repair place.  It's called Broadway Animal Clinic.  I recently brought my cat in for a checkup and they were very nice and professional.  They have some package deals on shots and weren't overly expensive.  They just opened in the fall, so the place is brand new and very clean.  When I was there, there was a family there gushing to the main vet/owner about how grateful they were to him for saving their dog- I guess he did some work for free because they couldn't afford it and the dog would have been put down otherwise.  That won a few points with me.

On the flip side, the only review I can find online is from someone who had a horrible experience, so your mileage may vary.  Since you're so close, you might as well stop in and see if you think they'd be a good fit for you and your cat.
