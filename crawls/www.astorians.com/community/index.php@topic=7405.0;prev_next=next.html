Okay, so I went to the King last night for dinner with my girlfriend, and it was totally different than what I was expecting.  I eat chicken and rice plates at least once a week for lunch while at work, and this was a pleasant surprise.  I got the chicken & shwarma plate.  First off, the shwarma tasted like well, lamb as opposed to the huge rolls of meat that you typically get from food carts.  They addition to the pickles and pickled turnips was great.  The guys working the cart were hysterical, and super friendly, and seemed like they genuinely enjoyed what they were doing.  And then there is the falafel.  I can say, without hesitation, that they were the best falfel I've ever tasted.  My girlfriend got the falafel pita, and I ended up eating half of hers.  They're so fresh, spicy, and crunchy yet not greasy at all.  My only complaint was that I was hoping for hot sauce on my platter, but I didn't get it.  There's always next time, though.
I will definitely be going back, and I can't wait to see Freddy at the Vendies.
