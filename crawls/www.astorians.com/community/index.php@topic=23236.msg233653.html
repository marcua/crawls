I'm sorry, Illegal Alien, that I can't remember more. Oh, speaking of IMMAC, you do remind me that Frankenstein and Dracula came out about that time, 1934/35, and my folks wouldn't let me go or take me (9 years old?). The theater was somewhere in the area, 28th Ave?, or somewhere near. Just to close the circle; we settled in Maine in the early 40's, I went into the service and when I got out I didn't want to stay in that "hick" state, Maine, so I took off for  New York City in 1947, instead of going to college like I should have. Remembering Woolworth, and with my father's help, I got a job at good old FW, the Times Square store, but was called back when the Korean war started. No, I didn't return to Woolworth after I got out of the service the 2nd time but I did go to college. Thanks for bringing some of those great times back to me.
Kindest regards, Chuck B
