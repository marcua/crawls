OK, here it is.  We made a reservation at Christos.  The Porterhouse for 2 is $67, which is expensive...but can get much pricier at other places.  Add on an app and their cheapest bottle of wine ($34), however, and it's getting up there.  Of course, I said in my original post that price wasn't a big deal, and it wasn't, but when it came time to go...we felt like something more casual. 

We ended up at Tournesol on Jackson Ave.  It's a great, small French place we've been to a couple times.  Good news is the food was excellent.  We each got the steak frittes, and they do it perfectly!  Bad news is that our 8:30 reservation got us seated at 9:30.  We spent the extra hour at their wine bar (same owner, I believer) two doors down.  Truthfully, it was really quite nice - I'd just prefer it on my terms.  I don't mind waiting 20 minutes or so after a reservation, but an hour is a bit much.  A great meal, nonetheless.

We'll definately try Christos another night when we're feeling a bit more, um, classy.  I also want to check out Jackson Steakhouse.

Thanks so much everyone for the recommendations!
