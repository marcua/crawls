CBS News has just reported that an Air Conditioner Fell Out of Window on the Lower East Side of Manhattan and a man living in the building has been injured while walking the street.  There are air conditioner brackets, mounting rails or similar supports available to safely secure these from falling out the window.

Here is an article that is helpful.  http://www.habitatmag.com/Publication-Content/Habitat-s-Purchasing-Primer-News-for-New-Buyers/Installing-Window-Air-Conditioners-in-Your-Apartment

I believe that all residents should have their air conditioners safely installed, and that all buildings should include this in the house rules.
