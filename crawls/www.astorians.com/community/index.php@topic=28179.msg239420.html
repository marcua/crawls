Socrates Sculpture Park -- Outdoor Cinema -- FREE!
« on: June 23, 2014, 09:04:04 AM »
Given that the one thing all Astorians can agree on is how expensive it can be to live here, I'd thought I post some things to do this summer that are LOCAL AND FREE!

In case you didn't know, there's a wide range of events at Socrates Sculpture Parks that are absolutely free below  is the schedule for the outdoor film series.

Check out other stuff at:
http://socratessculpturepark.org/

Outdoor cinema
Eight-week international film festival celebrating the cultural diversity of Queens
WEDNESDAYS, JULY 2–AUGUST 20, 2014 -- 7 PM

Pre-screening performances at 7 pm, films begins at sundown: http://socratessculpturepark.org/programs/films/

Schedule:
Tuesday, June 24, 2014 -- When Harry Met Sally
Wednesday, July 2, 2014 -- pansy riot: a punk prayer (Russia)
Wednesday, July 9, 2014 -- La Pirouge (Senegal)
Wednesday, July 16, 2014 -- The Deadly Ponies Gang (New Zealand)
Wednesday, July 23, 2014 – Umberto D (Italy)
Wednesday, July 30, 2014 – Viola (Argentina)
Wednesday, August 6, 2014 – Forest of the Dancing Spirits (Central Africa, Sweden)
Wednesday, August 13, 2014 – 13 Assassins (Japan)
Wednesday, August 13, 2014 – Microcosmos (France)
