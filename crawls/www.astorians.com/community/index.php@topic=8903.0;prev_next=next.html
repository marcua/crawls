We had a fantastic dinner at Mezzo Mezzo that night... Funny, I've only been there once for brunch -- I always thought the place looked too pricey and/or old school. Not at all.

The food was excellent (gi-normous portions,   :-o ) but above all, the service was the absolute best I've ever encountered around here. Even though the prix fixe menu kicked in @ 9pm, we didn't feel rushed, the waiter and the maitre'd were attentive and friendly (had a nice chat w/ the maitre'd afterwards). Food was delish -- made for great leftovers....  :mrgreen:

I think we found a good substitute for Anna's Corner as well. Yeah, we're definitely gonna be regulars.
