Title: Young Adult Mass ~ Astoria Bohemian Hall Beer Garden
Post by: cfnyc on May 03, 2010, 09:40:13 PM
This is one of our popular events before we enter Summer, and take a break with our Young Adult Mass.

Join the members of The Catholic Fellowship of NYC for the Young Adult Mass on Sunday May 16th at 5pm at Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church (www.mountcarmelastoria.org) located at 23-25 Newtown Avenue, Astoria, NY 11102.

Following the mass, please meet the group in front of the church to head to the beer garden. The beer garden is located at:

The Bohemian Hall Beer Garden
29-19 24th Avenue
Astoria, NY 11102
www.bohemianhall.com

The beer garden has food to order and has a wide selection of drinks on its menu. Pay as you go at this event.

Directions to the church: Take the N subway uptown to Astoria, and get off 30th Avenue Station. Walk down 31st Street, and turn left on Newtown Avenue. The church is located on the corner of Newtown Avenue and Crescent Street.

If you have any questions, please contact: Toan Chu at catholicfellowship@ymail.com or at 917.653.2140.

This event is hosted by The Catholic Fellowship.  To learn about us, go to www.cfnetwork.org.
