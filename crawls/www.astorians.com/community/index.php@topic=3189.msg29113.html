If you guys are selling CDs or DVDs, I've had good luck selling used on amazon.com, and I have a friend who has done well on half.com. Its pretty easy to set up, and you get a notice saying when something sells and you mail it to the person. CDs and DVDs are under a pound, so I just stick a few 39 cent stamps on a padded envelope and throw it in the corner mailbox. I'm probably losing a few cents on postage that way, but its totally worth it not to have to stand in that post office line!

I also buy all my CDs used now too - it might take a week to get them, but you're still paying a lot less than even iTunes (which I hate), and I usually resell them after I rip them to my computer, unless its something I really want a hard copy of. I buy DVDs used too, and I guess I could make a copy and resell the original, but I like having my DVDs :)
