Don't care about your silly warning, Astorians!  I am adding my post to this 1+ year old thread! 

I just got back from Cronin and Phelan's where I had theee most amazing cheeseburger.  I don't know much about food, but I do know about burgers.  My friend and I make it a point to try just about every renowned burger joint in Manhattan.  I would have to say that the cheeseburger that I just had at C&P beats them all.

I ordered a cheeseburger rare with fries (deluxe).  It was juicy, but not so much so that you got that sort of icky feeling afterwards that I sometimes get when there's too much grease.  In other words, it was cooked just right.  They piled raw onions, a big tomato slice and lettuce somewhere in there.  The bun was simply something that "held it all in" and helped me guide the big patty into my mouth without too much of a mess. The fries were lightly seasoned and well-done, almost crispy, like I ordered them. 

The atmosphere didn't hurt the experience either.  There were a couple of people at the bar and the bartender was very pleasant.  An older gentleman, who I believe was either the owner or a manager, told me that I would be back for another one.  People get hooked on them, he told me.  He's right.  Member's have written about them on the board before, but I finally can report that it was one of the best burgers in Astoria.

Oh and by the way, that deluxe and a soda was all for $10.00!  Not bad at all.
