For the first time in the history of the Vendy Awards, a vendor has won the 2010 Vendy Awards Peoples' Taste Award AND the Judges Pick.  Congratulations to Freddy Zeidaies and the King of Felafel & Shawarma gang, regularly found at Broadway & 30th Street in Astoria:

http://newyork.seriouseats.com/2010/09/a-look-at-the-2010-vendy-awards.html

http://midtownlunch.com/2010/09/27/freddie-the-falafel-king-of-astoria-sweeps-the-2010-vendy-awards/

A lot of hard work rewarded!
