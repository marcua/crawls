Title: Re: Hellgate CSA
Post by: lanseaux on May 31, 2008, 05:24:49 PM
The 2008 season runs from June 10-November 25, so our first pickup will be on Tuesday, June 10, in front of Freeze Peach.  You can also find information about the dates in the email you received about signing up, as well as on our website (http://www.hellgatecsa.com/), specifically this post (http://www.hellgatecsa.com/2008/04/).  Additional information about distribution dates and times will be posted on the website or sent to members via email as well. 

If you are sharing a share and are not sure whether the CSA has your contact info, send an email to info@hellgatecsa.com, specifying that you are splitting a share and with whom, and ask to have your email put on the list to receive notifications from the CSA.
