I know it's only my 3rd post, but I bring news of FREE BEER!

But more importantly, we are celebrating the success of the internet sitcom, "We Need Girlfriends", produced and filmed entirely in Astoria, and now being developed as a pilot for CBS!!!

Please come down and support something cool coming out of Astoria.

Here is the full Press Release:


WE NEED GIRLFRIENDS SCREENING EVENT
Sponsored by S.K.I. Beer Corp, & Radeberger Pilsner

Thursday, July 24th
8pm

FREE RADEBERGER PILSNER 8-9pm!

LIC Bar
45-58 Vernon Blvd (cnr of 46th Ave), Long Island City, NY 11101
http://www.longislandcitybar. com/

Join us for this very special event where we salute the creators of the internet phenomenon known as "We Need Girlfriends." The series is being made into a pilot for CBS and been featured in publications like ROLLING STONE and THE NEW YORK TIMES.

We'll be screening some of their best episodes, all of which were produced right here in Astoria, Queens!

The show's creator Steven Tsapelas and series lead actors Evan Bass, Patrick Cohen & Seth Kirschner will be also on hand for the event!

To learn more about the show visit the "We Need Girlfriends" website:

http://www.weneedgirlfriends.tv/
