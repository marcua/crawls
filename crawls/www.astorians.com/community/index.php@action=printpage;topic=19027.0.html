Title: Re: Any Interest in a Kitten/Puppy adoption party?
Post by: KurtLove on August 06, 2010, 08:52:39 PM
Okay, just to clear things up- we DO screen our adopters, and we DO make home visits. If you don't have screens on your window, forget it. If you heave a balcony without a fence, forget it. we require 2 forms of ID, photo and proof of address. And WE are NOT a non-profit group-we simply take sick and very young kittens from Animal Care and Control and foster them until they are old and healthy enough to be adopted. We DO NOT solicit donations, although many times people will make a donation in lieu of adopting an animal from us.

And the $25 adoption fee goes directly to Animal Care and Control, NOT us. The shelter provides all of the animals medical needs and surgery, and we provide the rest-food, litter, and TLC and socializing. AND we will provide paperwork for the kitten so you know where you are actually adopting it from. I know it seems like an odd concept, giving our money and time away to help save animals that are going to be adopted by total strangers, but once you look into the eyes of a sick little kitten, and they stare back at you with nothing but love and trust, it's kind of hard to just turn your back on them when saving them is an option.

Whatever, we will do it or we won't, we'll see what happens.
