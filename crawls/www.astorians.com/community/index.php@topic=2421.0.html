elitist fireworks
« on: June 22, 2006, 01:35:07 PM »
For years I've gone down to the piers in LIC to watch the fireworks there. I think it's called Elmer Gantry Park? It's in front of those huge Citylights buildings. Anyway, it was always the best place to go. I didn't go last year 'cause I was out of town, but a friend told me that he went down there as usual to get a spot on the piers and they were closed off to the public! You could only get a spot on them if you had a pass from Citylights, meaning you were a resident or guest. THIS IS SO ELITIST! Apparantly, the riff-raff (all of us) had to sit farther back in the park. Does anyone know if this is going to be the case this year? I was appalled when I heard about it. Does anyone know someone who lives in Citylights that we could ask, or better yet, someone who is involved in management there? I think if they do this this year, we should protest. These are supposed to be public waterfronts. This is why I'm suspicious of all these big plans that are being made for Silvercup west and the like. They say they'll improve the waterfront for everyone, but then they restrict it. If Independence Day isn't the time for activism, when is?
