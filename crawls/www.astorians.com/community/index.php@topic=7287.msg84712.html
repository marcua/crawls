I'm not speaking from any personal knowledge, but it seems that the nicknames are actually from the phonetic alphabets used by police and fire agencies, and other organizations which use radio communications.  There are various phonetic alphabets in use, but it's likely that the D is called "David" the Q "Quebec", etc.  My grandpa used to be really into HAM radio, and his call sign is Whiskey-Alfalfa-2-Oscar-Quebec-Oscar (WA2OQO).
