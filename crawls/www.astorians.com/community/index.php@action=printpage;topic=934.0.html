The Path of Love
Buddhist Meditations on Love
with Luis Villalon
Sundays 12:00-2:00pm
($10 per class)

The secret to harmonious relationships lies in learning to love others unconditionally. On this course, we will learn profound methods for developing a deep and stable love for others that will lead to happiness and balanced, peaceful relationships.


October 16    Learning to Love
October 23    The Power of Love
October 30   Removing the Source of Pain
November 6  The Wisdom of Love

Talks and guided meditations suitable for everyone. The emphasis is on integrating Buddha's teachings into our lives. Beginners are welcome. Although taught as a series each class is self-contained.

About the Teacher
Luis Villalon is a senior student on the Teacher Training Program at  Chakrasambara Buddhist Center. Of Cuban heritage, Luis was born and raised in Queens. He greatly assists with the running of the Center and teaches a number of branch classes throughout the city. Luis is known for his clear and heartfelt presentation of the teachings and for his warm and generous nature.

Location
At Grace Lutheran Church
31-20 21st Ave (at 32nd Street)
Astoria, NY
(Please use office entrance to the left of the main Church entrance)

By Train:
Take the N train to Ditmars, walk north on 31st Street to 21st Ave and make a right.

About these Buddhist Meditation Classes:

Based in the traditions of Kadampa Buddhism, each class is structured to include a teaching, two meditations, a group discussion, and a questions and answers session.

No special clothing is required, and pre-registration is not necessary. This class is a branch of Chakrasambara Buddhist Center in New York.[/i]
