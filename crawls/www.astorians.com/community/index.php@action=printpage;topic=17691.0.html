Title: Free Music for Aardvarks Trial Classes in Astoria
Post by: AllisonTartalia on March 18, 2010, 07:48:49 AM
Hello Families!

I’m a music for Aardvarks teacher, and just wanted to spread the word about some free trial classes we’re holding over the next week in Astoria. Music for Aardvarks and Other Mammals is a class geared toward children aged 6 months through 4 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, rhythm training and instrumental jam sessions. Written by David Weinstone, songs like Taxi, City Kid and Subway provide children with a positive image of their world and help them relate to their surroundings. Please email info@musicforqueens.com, to reserve your spot in a free trial class! More information and a complete schedule of our Spring semester classes can be found at http://www.MusicForQueens.com.
