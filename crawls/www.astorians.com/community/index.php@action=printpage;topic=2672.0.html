you pronounce both C not as in "cat" but as C in Italian "Ciao" ( its a Slavic sound that Americans do not have ) and you pronounce dz in the middle as G in "gin", and also you do pronounce all the letters in the word.
Ok I have no idea if I explained this well, but it is hard to pronounce!   :|
