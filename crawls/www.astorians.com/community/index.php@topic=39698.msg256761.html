Where in Astoria for best (Brunchpancakes or French toast but without long waits

I'm really picky about my pancakes and French toast.  Don't want pancakes made from instant batter.  I want real pancakes, with real maple syrup.  Ditto for French toast.  I want thick French toast made with challah bread or similar....not some thin mushy 'white bread'.

In the past, Astor Bake Shop was my go to place when I wanted pancakes.  There's were the best!!  ;-(

Now with Astor gone, I'm looking for a new place where I can just pop-in when I want, for good pancakes or French toast.  But I'm not looking for a place like The Bonnie, where I need to wait in line forever.  I want an 'easy' place similar to how Astor Bake Shop was....where I can just show up and get a seat either immediately, or waiting just a few minutes for a table to open up.

Any suggestions?

Tx!
