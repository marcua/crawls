McDonald's certainly, and Blimpy's too I guess, don't do much food preparation in their restaurants. Mostly they are heating and assembling food that was trimmed, portioned, shaped, mixed, etc. at some commissary and packaged for transport and storage. And fast-food chains each have a "system" of standard procedures - a competently run store is probably at low risk of a significant violation by its design.
