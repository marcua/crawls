Bookstores are expensive to start b/c you have to buy all your inventory upfront, you can't get a few things that look nice and expand from there.  If you go to a bookstore and they don't have the book you want, you're less inclined to return, so it takes a lot of space and a lot of cash to fill that space, all before you open.

If you really really want a bookstore like B&N in astoria you'd probably do best to come up with a petition or something that shows a high demand in the area for a bookstore, then send that onto B&N.
