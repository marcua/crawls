Honestly I love the Trade Fair on Ditmars- they have such a fascinating collection of stuff. They just obviously don't have enough room to sensibly stock and organize their shelves, and the store feels claustrophic and dusty. And there are categories of stuff I don't buy there just because they're stacked up near the ceiling and I don't want to have to ask the employees to get them for me, mainly stuff like paper towels, tissues, and toilet paper. Their produce is pretty good (albeit jam-packed) and all their stuff is noticeably cheaper than Key Food.

Also, I usually go there late at night, and the store used to smell foul around that time of day.. but it's been much better recently. That smell has pretty much prevented me from ever buying meat there, though. I dunno what the smell is but it ain't good.

I've been to the fancy Wegman's up in Rochester. Gorgeous store. And we have Stop 'N' Shops in Connecticut and those are usually decent and clean, though the selection is certainly nothing special. I guess they had a decent cheese selection.

Anyone been to the Trade Fair website? http://www.tradefairny.com/
I'm wondering where all those pictures were taken; I've been to the Trade Fair on 30th Ave also and, while that one's much nicer than the Ditmars one, I didn't see a big olive or cheese section like the ones pictured on the site.
