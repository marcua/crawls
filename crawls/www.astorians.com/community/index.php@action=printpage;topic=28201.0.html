Title: 2015 Queens Arts Fund: Funding for Queens Artists and Arts Organizations
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on June 30, 2014, 01:13:22 PM
Queens Council on the Arts (QCA) supports the hard work and contributions of the borough’s creative citizens with our Queens Arts Fund (QAF) grant program. These QAF grants help build the creative community in Queens.
 
The online submission deadline for ALL applications is Thursday, October 9, 2014. The QAF application must be submitted using QCA’s online application will be available after July 1st at http://queenscouncilarts.org/about-the-qca-grant-programs (http://queenscouncilarts.org/about-the-qca-grant-programs). Individual artists and non-profit organizations producing cultural programming in all arts disciplines are encouraged to apply for DCA Greater New York Arts Development Fund or NYSCA Community Grants Program.
 
All applicants are welcome to attend any of the application info session and take advantage of the QAF resources on the QCA website, www.queenscouncilarts.org (http://www.queenscouncilarts.org/).  This includes new guidelines and eligibility, the video tutorials, the downloadable resources, and the FAQs section.

In 2014, QCA awarded over $180,000 in grants to 31 individual artists and 47 organizations for the creation of arts and cultural programming in the borough of Queens. The Queens Art Fund is made possible in part by the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs in partnership with the City Council and the New York State Council on the Arts with the support of Governor Cuomo and the New York State Legislature.
                                                           
Queens Council on the Arts is the borough-wide arts council of Queens. The mission of the Queens Council on Arts is to foster, and develop the arts in Queens County and to support individual artists and arts organizations in presenting their cultural diversity for the benefit of the community.
