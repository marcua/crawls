I have been getting kabobs and falafel now and then from the Cedars Meat House, which is a Lebanese grocery & take out place on 30th near 41st, (and hence, just round the corner from me.) I had just assumed for some time that it was a meat market, then lately noticed the grill. It's very convenient to hide out while my clothes tumble round in the excessively compact laundromat next to it.

The guy there is nice. It's never very busy. The falafel has been tasty, neither overcooked nor mooshy, when I had it. But I am no expert on the stuff. If the difference between Lebanese, Moroccan, and Egyptian means something to you, you might want to include it in the rotation.
