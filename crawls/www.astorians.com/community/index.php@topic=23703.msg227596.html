restaurants can you DONATE food???
« on: November 09, 2012, 10:18:22 AM »
hi all,
a group of us working with astoria recovers and occupy sandy are hoping to find hot food for the communities in far rockaway, specifically beach 19th street area.  if you're interested, contact christina at 212-684-3068.  or read more...
it is a wasteland there.  they normally don't live lavishly so they are especially in dire need and completely underserved.  very little is reaching them, none of the help or donations you see on tv or from other efforts.  the only official presence i see are the 'prison lights' the nypd shines at dusk to prevent looting. they are working class people with babies and children whose schools are closed and who don't have power.  they can't do laundry so they come daily to pick up clothes and whatever food we have.  i have slowly been able to get them 'on the map' for supplies.
so we're ready to open up our personal kitchens to make the food and then drive it over.  but before we did that we thought we'd reach out to our community. 
williamsburg and greenpoint restaurants have been coordinating to donate with churches in they're neighborhoods, like fette sau!  they gave us 75lbs of their delicious pulled pork yesterday!!!
can we pull together some thoughtful restaurants here?  we'll pick up and in advance, thank you from the bottom of all our and their hearts.
