Title: Churrascaria Tropical
Post by: megc on October 19, 2005, 10:18:17 AM
I got up this morning early specifically to run off some of that meat I ate last night!  Wow, it was good.  Really delicious and of course my favorite was that which was wrapped in bacon (no surprise there).  The caiphirihas (did I spell that correctly?) were awfully strong but awfully tasty.  Nice sides of fried yucca, plaintain, and fantastic beans and rice!  I thought there were just out of this world, and paired with the cucumber/pepper/vinegary condiment, it made for a really fantatastic side.  The farofa (sp?) was really interesting and I liked it very much with the meat.  There were these little balls of yummy bread, too, that I thought were really great.  Nice salad bar and everything was pretty fresh there.  And dessert was fun!   Flan, etc.  I loved hearing all the Brasilian Portuguese being spoken here and there during the meal (I think it's a lovely sounding language).  All in all a really delicious meal, and the experience was just amplified by being there with a group of friends.  It was wonderful to see you all and share a meal together.  Looking forward to the next one!
