Re: McCann's Pub and Grill
« Reply #1 on: June 25, 2008, 09:51:59 AM »
The tap Guiness is good. Not the best in the city, but still quality.

The food is great bar food. It *is* bar food, but even their fried calamari is pretty darn good.

It is a sports pub and most people go there alone or with friends to watch the games (baseball, football and boxing seem to be the most popular). It's not always the best place to just have a pint because of this. There are pool tables in the back which is where my friends and I end up hanging out if we're there after a game or not there to watch a game at all.

The staff and the crowd are really great though. You can sit and yell bloody murder at a Yankees fan there all night, but if you turn around and go "Hey, I'm afraid to walk back to the subway alone" they will walk you. John who tends bar gives me shiat for my Red Sox fandom, but will always put the game on and their wooden chip policy is great.
