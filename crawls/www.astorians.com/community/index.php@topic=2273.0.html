Summer Solstice Celebration
Wednesday, June 21, 2006, 5:00 pm - 9:00 pm

Celebrate the summer solstice and the longest day of the year with Socrates Sculpture Park and Materials for the Arts. This fun family event will feature live music and Solstice DJ Jonny Fego, special performances by Free Style Arts Association and Recycle A Bicycle's BMX Stunt Rider Jeff McIntyre, eco-friendly artmaking workshops with The Queens Museum, The Noguchi Museum, Materials for the Arts, Recycle A Bicycle and Socrates Sculpture Park.
Don't miss: Transformation! Facepainting by Agostino Arts, and delicious down home picnic fare from Jonathan Forgash and Film Chefs Restaurant to celebrate the beginning of summer!
