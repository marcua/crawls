Honestly, when I was 18 I had a terrible relationship with my parents, too. I wanted to get out of the house and move somewhere (anywhere!) else. But I didn't have the money, the job or the means to get housing anywhere. It wasn't fun to stay with my parents until I got the means to go to college and get out of my annoying hometown, but it was the best choice I could make. We're closer now; I have a place of my own, a job and friends that I like. I also got a pretty okay education.

I know it sucks, but you don't want to move to NYC without a college degree and without savings. Try looking at colleges in NYC and see if you can apply to one or if there are any that look good to you. Or stay where you are and save more money. You'll need at least first month's rent and a security deposit for most sublets, which will be the easiest to get if you arrive without already having a job (that's what I did when I came here).
