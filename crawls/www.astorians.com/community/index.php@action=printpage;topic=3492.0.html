You can renew it at the main post office at 33rd Street and 8th Avenue, as well as at the big post office on 3rd Avenue at 54th Street.  Make sure you have all your identification paperwork in order.  I had to sign an affidavit for a friend as he didn't have a drivers license (he does have valid ID) - I don't think he's received his passport as yet.
