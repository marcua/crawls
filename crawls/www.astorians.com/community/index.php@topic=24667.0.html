CHARMING ONE BEDROOM AT THE GREYSTONES WITH PRIVATE GARDENS

Located in The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights, Queens and on one of the most beautiful blocks in New York City, The Greystones is a fifteen unit original co-op.  This third floor unit is in move-in condition; with sunlight shining from eastern and western exposures; original hardwood floors; renovated eat-in kitchen with all new stainless steel appliances, which includes dishwasher and gas stove; renovated bathroom with textured glass door; hall pantry with plenty of storage space; dumbwaiter for garbage disposal; and original transoms above all doors.  Your storage unit and washer/dryer are in the basement.  And of course the beautiful private gardens are available for your quiet enjoyment.  Unfortunately, the building is not pet friendly.  This property is close to all neighborhood shopping, restaurants, weekend farmers market, transportation and only 20 minutes away from mid-town Manhattan.
