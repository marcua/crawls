Hello Fellow Astorians!

My name is Betty and I've been living here in Astoria for over 2 years.  I'm 25 years old and plan on staying that age for a few more years.    I'm a real estate agent and an amazing pastel artist. 

A little about why I moved to Astoria:

1.  To be closer to Manhattan

2.  Because of the wide range of tastes and feelings that Astoria has to offer.  From the great Thai (Ubols) food, to traditional Egyptian (Ali's Kabob Cafe) delicacies, to Greek lemon filled dishes (I can't spell any of the restaurant names!) I'm constantly left wanting more!  Then you have such artistic spots as The Museum of the Moving Image, P.S. 1 (Technically in LIC, but who's mapping?), the Namaguchi Museum and all the little galleries. 

3.  Astoria offers such a great melting pot of people that actually get along and create a community of citizens not desperate enough to be criminals and not rich enough to have sticks up their butts.  (Sorry to those who love Chelsea, Park Slope and the rest of you know who you are)

Anywho, that's a bit about me.  Hopefully I'll see you all around. 

P.S.

I offer the following services:

Art instruction
Manhattan Real Estate (residential sales and rentals)
Pet and Human portraits
