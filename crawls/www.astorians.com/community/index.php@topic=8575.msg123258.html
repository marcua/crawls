Im a native floridian, where we have cockroaches/waterbugs LITERALLY 4" long. terrifying. 1 There ARE ways to stop them...namely

1) get steel wool and plug up ALL your pipes leading to and from the radiators and under all your sinks. Shove the steel wool in the holes. 9 times out of 10 this is where they come from.

2) have either you or your landlord caulk all the seals of your apartment, including along the windows, baseboards, underneath sinks and behind toilets. COMBAT makes a caulk/insect repellent that worked pretty well in my old apartment. once my landlord used it in the bathroom, we saw considerably fewer bugs (they werent eliminated entirely but we maybe had 1 a month)

3) NEVER EVER EVER LEAVE ANY water whatsover in sinks or tubs! Get a squeegie for your shower and wipe down the water when you leave the shower.  Keep your sinks DRY; wipe down with a towel when not using! Do dishes IMMEDIATELY and NEVER EVER let them sit in the sink!

4)make sure your screens are tight and fit your windows properly. Use that weather stripping to seal up any cracks (thsi will also help to keep heat in)

5) COMBAT TRAPS. Everywhere.  Use boric acid...but only if you dont have pets (toxic!)

6) never ever keep any cardboard in your house; bugs DO hide in there. Curb it immediately. Get rid of any cardboard you may be using as storage and switch them out for clear plastic containers or duffell bags.


good luck...
