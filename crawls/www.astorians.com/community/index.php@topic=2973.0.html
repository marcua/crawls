8pm Sign up, 8:30 Start

Take the N or the W to Ditmars, turn west/left onto Ditmars, and Waltz's is about 7 blocks down on your left.

www.waltz-astoria.com


Mars

On folded legs Jeffery pricks the Milky Way
across a black sheet of paper, twists the point
to ream each hole, his feet in pins and needles.
Sunlight, turns orange, darts around the page.

Absorbed in the chatter of the shaft
sinking through fiber, Jeffery closes his eyes,
nibbles out a cosmos, gets stung. The sky spins
off the pick. He squeezes his finger, sees blood,
tastes its iron, opens the hole brighter.


Bio:
Glenn Werner is a graphic designer who lives and works in the Hudson Valley. A native New Yorker, born in Queens, he was first inspired to create from visits to the city's museums and the Greenwich Village art shows of the early 1960s. There he was also introduced to the mysteries of used book stores where he learned to love the smell of dusty old tomes, the blanketed thud of a book clapped shut, and the overwhelming beauty of words. He has been writing poetry for eight years. Many of his poems are pictures drawn with words that illustrate the emotion, attitude, or condition of their subjects presented as a frozen moment or event, reflecting his visual art background.
