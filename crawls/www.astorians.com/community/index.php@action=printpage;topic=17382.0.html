Title: Young Adult Mass - Dinner/Drinks - Dr. Phil Mango on "Male & Female Sexuality"
Post by: cfnyc on February 15, 2010, 11:26:53 AM
Young Adult Mass on Sunday March 21st at 5pm at:

Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church
23-25 Newtown Avenue
Astoria, NY 11102
(Cross Street is Crescent Street & Newtown Avenue)
http://www.mountcarmelastoria.org

After mass, meet the group in front of the church to head to dinner to:

Brewhouse (formerly Indigo)
28-50 31st Street, 2nd Floor
Astoria, NY 11102
Between 30th Ave & Newtown Ave
http://www.indigobarnyc.com

During dinner, our guest speaker will be Dr. Phil Mango, and he will talk about "Male & Female Sexuality."

Directions to the church:
***Subway***
Take the N train uptown to Astoria, and get off 30th Avenue Station. Walk down 31st Street and turn left on Newtown Avenue. The church is located on the corner of Newtown Avenue and Crescent Street

***Car***
From Westchester & NJ, take the Triboro/RFK Bridge and exit 29th Street (first exit in Queens). From 29th Street, turn right on Astoria Blvd.

From Long Island, take the Grand Central Park Way and exit Hoyt Avenue (last exit before Triboro/RFK Bridge). Turn left on 31st Street (below elevated subway line) and right on Astoria Blvd.

The entrance to the church parking lot is on Astoria Blvd. (pass Crescent Street).
