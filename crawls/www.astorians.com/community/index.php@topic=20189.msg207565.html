Artopolis is an amazing bakery if you ask me.  We are tremendously fortunate to have it in our neighborhood, and it would be hard to find many places comparable to it in the U.S.  I've had SO many wonderful things from there, both sweet and savory.  Occassionally there's been something less impressive, but a wide range of truly wonderful stuff. 

Also, the staff has been pretty wonderful in all of my experience there (which is probably too much - ask my waistline).  I always enjoy going in the place.  I'm partial to the cookies, the savory pies, the breads, the custardy phyllo treats (galaktoboreko?) and especially the amazing tsoureki.  If you ever catch that cardamon-orange scented bread when it's still hot from the oven, better find someplace to sit down when you taste it.
