Los Portales
« on: March 19, 2008, 11:19:33 AM »
Went there for the first time with the BF, and we were very pleased. It's on Broadway just off Crescent St.

I had the vegetable quesadilla; he had skirt steak something-or-other. Everything tasted fresh and delicious, only complaint was that his steak was a bit dry.

But fresh cilantro, tortilla chips (though a few more than three would've been nice!), lightly-crisp tortilla, fresh vegetables- you could tell by the details this was good-quality cheap eats.

Anyway, I believe it's been discussed before, just thought I'd add my own little review.
