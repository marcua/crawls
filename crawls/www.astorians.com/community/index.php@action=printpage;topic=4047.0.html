I asked my roommate about the website.  This is what she had:

Quote
The public version of Medicare Interactive online until December 31, 2006, and our new PDP comparison tool, MI Drug Plan Compare, available at www.medicarerights.org/help.html :  Be sure you have all the facts.
 
You can get to this page from the www.medicarerights.org homepage. It is the first blurb below the Question of the Week, and you can click on either the MI Counselor logo or the text, Need Help Understanding Your Medicare Plan Options this Enrollment Period? to get to the tools.
While access to this public version of MI is FREE, users must register for access.   When clicking into MI, they are prompted to create an account, which requests general contact information (name, address, email; NO phone) and allows them to pick a username and password and subscribe to MRC e-newsletters.
