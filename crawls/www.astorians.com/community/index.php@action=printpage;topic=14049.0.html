Hi-  we pay $50 per day for someone to come to our house from 11-7.  I know someone else who pays around $70 for someone to go to their house from 7 am - 7 pm.  Don't know if that helps, but I think it kind of depends on flexibility and experience of the caregiver.  We do give her more if she stays late, but if we get home early we don't normally take away.  I thinks she's more flexible because of that, which is great for those nights when you get stuck late or want to grab a quick drink!
