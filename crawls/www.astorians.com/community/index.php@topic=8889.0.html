Craft Fair at Waltz Dec 16
« on: December 11, 2007, 06:53:37 PM »
Hello all,

Due to the huge success of this Astoria Craft Collective event on Dec. 2nd, L.I.C. and Astorian crafters are coming out once again to share their creations.

Holiday Craft Fair at Waltz-Astoria
Sunday December 16, 2007
11am-5pm
Waltz-Astoria http://www.waltz-astoria.com/
Ditmars Blvd, between 23rd and 24th Streets, Astoria Queens

This craft fair will feature artisans and crafters from the Astoria/LIC area.
Gorgeous and unique jewelry, cards, crochet, knitted items and much more will be featured.   Come out and support local crafters!

Joey in Astoria article ...

 http://astorianyc.blogspot.com/2007/11/handmade-holiday-astoria-craft.html

Astoria Craft Collective

http://www.astoriacraftcollective.blogspot.com/
