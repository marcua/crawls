Oh well. I always figure around a hundred dollars a year is just the cost of parking from tickets. Stupid me for being too excited about saving a couple of quarters.

It could have easily been a mistake/error - maybe a new, inexperienced person was out today writing tickets.  Usually federal holidays are included in meter suspension.  Definitley check the website, but appeal it anyway, as you had honest intentions in the situation (you weren't trying to avoid paying for any other reason that it was a federal holiday).  For sure appeal!  Appeal, appeal, appeal!  I used to work as a Hearing Officer and I read all sorts of parking ticket appeals.  If it were me, I would dismiss the ticket. 

Also, first thing to do is always check the ticket to see if an error was made on the part of the issuing officer.  If there is, then the ticket is immediately void.

Good luck, Christine!
