2 PM causasian guy 5'8 age approximately 28-35, bald head goatee beard gets into building hawking another electric service provider (badge around neck looked like it said FLM Marketing, not sure).  He's in rapt conversation with the dodos in 4e whose English is frankly not great.  Saying he's from New York State Public Service Commission, and wants to see their electric bill.  I yell through the paper-thin door here on 34th St around 36th Avenue "no, don't fall for it."  He wants me to have the nerve to show myself So I Do.  I ring 911 in his presence, we wait in Mexican Standoff mode for 10 minutes and he finally leaves when dispatch calls my phone back to confirm the address.  PD gets here after he's departed, and I give the description and apologize for pulling them in this direction.

The guy made his way directly to the top floor, so I've done 15 other families a service.  I'd love to see the security footage as to who let him in.  I have funny conversation with neighbor on 2 that he was a vampire looking for a bite!
