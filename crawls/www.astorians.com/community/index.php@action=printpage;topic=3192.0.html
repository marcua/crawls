But, again, allow me to reiterate -- for all my whining, I'm still going to stick with these guys.  Most of the gyms are reasonably well kept, they're located all over the place, and going there numerous times a week is a mainstay in my life I have no intention of giving up, no matter how much I grind my teeth when I get my monthly credit card bill.
