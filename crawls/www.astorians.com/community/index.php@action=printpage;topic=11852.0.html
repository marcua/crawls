Post by: Bowbridge on August 20, 2008, 07:24:37 PM
It's better to come in on the Q, you get a nice view after you make the turn into West 8th Street station.  If you look to the right, you can see where Luna Park used to be, and where the Aquarium is, that was Dreamland.  I rode the Cyclone a few times and went on the Wonder Wheel.  It's probably the last year for Astroland, what with Thor Equities jerking the place around.  This might be the last chance anyone has of seeing Old Coney before the place is...upgraded, as they say.
