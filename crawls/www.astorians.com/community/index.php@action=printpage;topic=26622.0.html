Title: Astoria First Presbyterian Hosts St. Valentine's Day Program
Post by: arthouse on February 06, 2014, 11:42:19 AM
Join us and our old friends Will and Paul, the Bard and the Apostle, and their musings on love.

The Apostle Paul wrote: "Love is patient; love is kind..." Shakespeare says, “The course of true love never did run smooth. Love is a familiar. Love is a devil. There is no evil angel but Love.”

It will be an evening of theater, music, food and interesting conversation. The more the merrier!

Directed by Alex Carney, Artistic Director of Raised Spirits Theatre.
Astoria Presbyterian Church
23-35 Broadway, Astoria, NY 11106
