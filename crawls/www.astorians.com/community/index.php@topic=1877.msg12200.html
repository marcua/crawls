April 22nd Saturday 8pm - Midnight
Zenon Taverna
34-10 31st street (between 34&35th streets) Astoria, NY
www.zenontaverna.com

After much consultation with executive board member Flooz & the owner of Zenon, The following menu and pricing is offered:

BUFFET DINNER   $35.90 per person
Available for 25 people minimum.
(Menu #3 on website plus the salmon dish)
(Includes unlimited beer, house wine and soda)

Cold Appetizers
Greek Salad   
Tzantziki
Seafood Salad

Hot Appetizers
Halloumi
Char-grilled Octopus
Spinach Pie

Buffet Table
Roasted Leg Of Lamb
Chicken Francaise
Salmon dish (TBD by owner)
Fried Calamari
Keftedes
Sheftalies
Pasboobsio
Roasted Potatoes
Rice

Also included: Coffee and Fresh Fruit for Everyone

Extra expenses: taxes and a minimum tip of $100 to our waiter for the evening. 
That shouldnt cost more than about $44 per person.

Comments:
Can everyone be happy with this menu and pricing?
We will have our own private room and sound system. Who will bring the music?
You can order hard alcoholic drinks from the waiter (pay as you go).
We can remove the unlimited beer and wine ($10.95 per person) but it seems pretty cheap.
A 1/3 deposit must be paid in advance (about $350 cash or check, no CC).
Final group size will be phoned in on wednesday April 19th.

I hope you all say yes to this proposal.
