Title: Buddhist Meditations in Astoria
Post by: andres21 on May 12, 2005, 07:25:49 PM
Meditation in Astoria

on Eight Steps to Happiness
In this series based on Geshe Kelsang Gyatso's book, Eight Steps to Happiness, we will look at how to train the mind in loving-kindness. The more we are capable of opening our hearts to others, the more a natural happiness will arise inside. Luis Villalon will guide us in this step-by-step method for bringing about a transformation of the heart ($10 per class).

Sundays 12:00-2:00pm
with Luis Villalon

May 8 Identifying the Source of our Problems
May 15 The Pain of Putting Yourself First
May 22 The Third Step: Completing Cherishing Love
June 5 Exchanging Self with Others

Each class is self contained and can be taken individually. Anyone can benefit from these techniques regardless of spiritual background.

At Grace Lutheran Church
31-20 21st Ave (at 32nd Street)
Astoria, NY
(Please use office entrance to the left of the main Church entrance)

By Train:
Take the N train to Ditmars, walk north on 31st Street to 21st Ave and make a right.
