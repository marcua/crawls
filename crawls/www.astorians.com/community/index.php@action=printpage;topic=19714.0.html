Title: Tot Shabbat
Post by: jennifer on October 31, 2010, 10:25:22 PM
Looks like ACI (conservative temple on Crescent) is making a come-back-to life.
We checked it out years ago and found most of the members to be in their 70s, 80s and probably older. 
There's a new rabbi (Rabbi Pearl) and he's doing his darndest to breathe life back into the place - and I have to say - YEAH!!!

Yesterday we went to their first Tot Shabbat (it's like a mommy and daddy and me for an intro to Jewish culture and celebration of Shabbat) and there were 9 little ones there!! That's 7 more kids than have been in the temple in probably 30 years or more.  The congregation has been around for over 75 years and it will be wonderful if it comes back to life enough to be a significant cultural institution in the neighborhood. 

First a synagogue - next thing you know maybe there will be smoked white fish, chopped liver, kugel, gifilte fish, challah and shmaltz available at the markets.  After that - Purim might be a big hit at the beer garden. 

They're organizing events aimed at 20s, 30s and families with children.  Check out their events page:
http://www.astoriacenter.org/events.htm

Tot Shabbat will be a monthly event - usually held the first Saturday of the month.  The next one is next week Nov 6th from 10:45 - 11:30.
