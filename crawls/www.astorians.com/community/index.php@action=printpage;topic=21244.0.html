Title: Need someone to walk your pup? Waggin Tails Dog Walking Service is here to help
Post by: waggintails on June 26, 2011, 07:38:34 AM
Specialties
My name is Patrick Barragan, and I specialize in professional, courteous and individual dog walks in the Astoria/LIC area (other areas on a case-by-case basis). I provide care, attention, exercise, basic training reinforcement, socialization and plenty of play time for your pooch. I will ride my bike to your home every day, cutting my business's carbon emissions and use nothing but 100% biodegradable baggies made from recycled material. I also offer a free professional puppy portrait for every new client, and am available on a case-by-case basis for pet-sitting and walks on the weekends. Please feel free to call, text or e-mail me with any questions or inquiries!


History
Established in January, 2010
I have worked as a dog walker in the Fairfield County in Connecticut, as well as in the Upper East Side of Manhattan and have recently moved my business to the Astoria/LIC area.


About the owner:
I am a professional dog walker and photographer. I have had and been exposed to dogs my entire life. Waggin Tails Dog Walking Service is a combination of my three passions in life: dogs, being outdoors, and photography -- the service I provide you and your canine companions cannot be found anywhere else!


I am also a volunteer at the Heavenly Angels Animal Rescue in Astoria, a no-kill shelter providing care and love to a handful of cats and dogs near Astoria Park South. I offer 5 free 60-minute walks to anyone who adopts from HAAR, or can prove they've recently adopted their dog from a no-kill shelter in NYC (a $100 value, no-obligation!)
