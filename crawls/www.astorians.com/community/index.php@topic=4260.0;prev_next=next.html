I went recently and it was great!  That chestnut pasta that they make in house was awesome!  Everything was good and they are generous.  I see nothing wrong with the Manhattan prices comparison.  If one transplanted Ornella or Trattoria L'Incontro in Manhattan, they could EASILY charge nearly double, and get away with it.  Luckily it's here in Astoria, so it's moderately priced.  I think their prices for the quality and amount of food are spot on.  This isn't 1972 after all.  Those ingredients cost money and they have a staff to pay while also making a profit.
