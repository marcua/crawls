Greetings to those interested ~


I have been all moved and settled for a few months now    I am quite happy with my place although I am almost adjusted going from the first floor, 3 family home (with backyard and facing), to a building complex with much more tenants.  I do have a terrace which is nice.  Having not to leave the bldg to do laundry is great.


I miss taking the "N/W" train and having a seat all the time in the mornings.  Now it's the "7" - no seat and crowded.  There is several bus lines along my new neighbor for the just in case, so that's not a problem (i.e., Q60,Q32,Q18,Q72 to name a few).  While I do switch at QBP for the N/W, I occasionally do get a seat there due to the closures along that line (for now).


Having A/C at my disposal this past summer was wonderful.  More windows/more light    .. very happy with that.  Now to see how the cold-weathered months work out.


I still visit the Ditmars area.  The ambiance/stores/restaurants, etc., different too.  Still adjusting to all that too and getting acquainted.  It's all good ....


C'iao for now ~

Greetings yippee ~  in reply, the Towers are Mitchell-Lama (moderate-middle income).  The waiting list takes a long time to open up depending on the size of apartment you sign up for.


Once you're moved in, I doubt if anyone moves out.  Building/complex is kept clean. If you own a car you sign up and placed on wait list for that.  I understand the monthly fee is very reasonable as well.


I don't know about renting with options to buy.


We are shareholders and pay maintenance, not rent.  It is definitely much more reasonable and affordable.  There are rules but that is expected and, do have Board meetings for everyone to attend. Not sure how often.. remember, I'm the new person!
