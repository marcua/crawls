In an America where everyone already owns whatever they want, the gift card is the best gift you can get someone.  I do wishlists from Amazon, but despite my encouraging my family members and friends to do this, I only got my Mom to send me hers.  And when I ask people what they want, they never know or want anything.  Who wants another candle, lotion set, or clothing you won't wear?  And how do you know the person already doesn't have what you bought?  What do you get the kids, who have plenty of toys, and you're not sure of their clothing size?  Gift cards to Best Buy.  Get the parents a gift certificate to their favorite restaurant.  Your friend's a gift card to the spa, bookstore, or Amazon.  Your dad a gift card to Home Depot.  Everyone loves gift cards!  (geez, I sound like a commercial!)
