Title: Jackson Heights Flea Market - Sat. Nov. 4th!
Post by: TBM on November 02, 2017, 10:23:00 AM

The Saxony Towers Playground (34th Ave. at 88th St., Jackson Heights, NY) is hosting a flea market on Saturday, Nov. 4th from 9AM to 4PM.  There will be new clothes, toys, household appliances, furniture, jewelry and lots more

There are still spots available ($35 for a 9'x6' spot) so vendors are encouraged to reserve their spots now.  Contact SaxonyPlayground@Gmail.com or call (347 766-2780).
