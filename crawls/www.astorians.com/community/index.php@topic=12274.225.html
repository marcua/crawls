To my fellow Greek Americans - let's stop the drama.  Astoria has changed and we have to accept it.  There are still plenty of Greeks here but we certainly cannot claim ownership.  I want to share a story with you -

When I moved out of Astoria and went into Manhattan, I convinced my husband to move back and purchase a house here because it was becoming more 'hip', more youthful, a bit more cutting edge etc.  I liked what I saw, young people from elsewhere moving in.  Nine years later, I'm not sure I like it anymore.  I think I prefer the Astoria with unsophisticated, rough around the collar, working class immigrants who invested in Astoria years ago.  I think I prefer people who did not complain constantly but were grateful to own a little bit of New York, who were happy with a fresh loaf of bread and a strong coffee, who did not criticize endlessly.  I never thought I would agree with Gleason, but I miss the old days of families and people who seemed content with what they had.
