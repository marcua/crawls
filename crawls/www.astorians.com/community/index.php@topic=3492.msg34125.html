You can renew it at the main post office at 33rd Street and 8th Avenue, as well as at the big post office on 3rd Avenue at 54th Street.  Make sure you have all your identification paperwork in order.  I had to sign an affidavit for a friend as he didn't have a drivers license (he does have valid ID) - I don't think he's received his passport as yet.

I hate dealing with the post office- its always a disaster. So the last time I renewed my passport I used a place called "Its Easy Passport". I was in a bit of a rush to renew my passport and they were able to sort me out really quickly.  They take the pictures there for you and sort out all of the paperwork. I had my passort in about a week.I don't remember the exact cost- but it wasn't too bad and it was worth it for me to not have any hassle. They also do Visas and other travel documents. If you don't want to go through the post office- I highly recommend this place!
