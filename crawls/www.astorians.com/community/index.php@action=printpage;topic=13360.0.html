Title: Bakery on 31st North of Ditmars
Post by: luchamos on December 11, 2008, 09:22:47 AM
Hey all, just wanted to plug a local bakery.  The bakery, which is next to the library and Wave Thai on 31st Street, has changed ownership (I guess it was a while ago, but I didn't notice).  Anyway, they had a sign up for Churros with Dulce de Leche, and I had never had that before, so I tried them this morning.  Oh. My. God.  Seriously transporting -- warm and crusty on the outside and filled with gooey dulce de leche.  I can see this is going to be habit-forming and blow my Christmas diet. Ah well.

Anyway, I just wanted to post it here because it is a little bit out-of-the-way, and now that I have enjoyed these churros I really want them to stay in business. 

Rachel.
