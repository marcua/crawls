Title: Community Townhall - Thursday, February 25
Post by: mcdirk on February 18, 2010, 04:02:20 PM
Please come out for a community townhall with Assemblymember Michael Gianaris and the United Community Civic Association (UCCA) led by the wonderful Rose Marie Poveromo.  I have been to a number of their past events and they are very impressive.  Rose Marie and Mike get a large number of representatives from city and state agencies to attend and answer questions directly.  It's always helpful and informative and I encourage people to attend.
