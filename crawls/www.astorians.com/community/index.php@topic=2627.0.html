Con Ed Urges
Business Customers
in Northwest Queens to
Immediately Turn Off All Electric Service

Due to continuing equipment problems in the Northwest Queens area, Con Edison is asking all of its business customers in that area of Queens to IMMEDIATELY discontinue their use of all electric service. The affected areas are Long Island City, Sunnyside, Woodside, Hunters Point and the Astoria neighborhoods of Queens. The affected area is bounded by the East River on the west and north, the Brooklyn-Queens Expressway on the east and Newtown Creek on the south.

***

LICBDC encourages you to share this important alert from Con Edison widely within the Queens business community, and to help by conserving electricity as they request.
