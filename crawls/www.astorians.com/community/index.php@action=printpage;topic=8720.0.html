Title: Free Trial Classes Music for Aardvarks (Babies and Toddlers) 11/26 & 12/3
Post by: AllisonTartalia on November 26, 2007, 08:43:12 AM
Hi,

This is just a note to let those of you with babies and toddlers know that there will be free Music For Aardvarks trial classes this Monday (11/26) and next Monday (12/3) at 9:30am, 10:30am and 11:30am. 

Music for Aardvarks and Other Mammals is a class geared toward children aged 6 months thru 5 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, and instrumental jam sessions. The original material used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.  To hear some of the songs and learn more, visit www.MusicForQueens.com!

Our Winter 2008 session starts January 7 and will run for 10 weeks .  Classes will be held on Mondays and Fridays.

Classes are held at Steinway Reformed Church
21-65 41st Street
(at Ditmars Blvd.)

Please rsvp to reserve a spot, as space is limited by emailing info@MusicForQueens.com or leaving a message at 212-769-6812.

For more information, to hear songs, or register for classes, please visit www.MusicForQueens.com.

Many Thanks!

Allison
