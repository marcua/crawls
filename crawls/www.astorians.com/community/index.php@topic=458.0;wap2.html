enigmacat:
on 30th Ave there are two good Thai restaurants.  Thai Pavilion, near 30th and Steinway, is more of a sit-down place where you can have wine with your meal.  Yum Thai is near 32nd and 30th and is great cheap fast-food Thai.  I recommend both.

Thai Angel is across the street from Yum Thai but I have not been impressed with them.
