Don't know what area of Astoria you're interested in, but I belong to the Rock on 31st Street, just off Ditmars Blvd. - right
near the Ditmars train station.

They're running some specials now for the new year. It's much pricier than PF, but it's a better gym. Lots of cardio and weight
machines along with free weights, a rock climbing wall, boxing ring and two studios. Classes range from belly dancing to zumba.
Trainers are all certified and it's very clean and well maintained.

You mentioned PF is crowded. Every gym is crowded now with the new year's resolution crowd. That'll thin out in a few weeks.
And no offense to anyone who just joined or wants to join a gym now -  I was one of those January beginners too!

If you go to the Rock, they'll usually give you a three-day pass to try it out. I think most gyms will. k

I used to be a member of the NYSC and was very happy with it, but it got too expensive for me.  For the last year I've been a member of Astoria Sports Complex (ASC) which is $99 per year (that's right just $99 per year).  ASC has decent machines and is clean.  It doesn't have  personal entertainment systems (i.e., TVs on machines), and the locker room is beyond bare bones and you have to give a dollar deposit to get a towel (which you get back when you return the towel).  It is a decent and cheap gym. I go most week mornings from 7AM-8AM and unlike NYSC, it isn't crowded (yay!).  All in all, you get more than what you pay for at ASC.
