Title: Alegria
Post by: m_bart on February 01, 2011, 11:51:02 PM
Wanted to give a quick shout out for Alegria. We ate here last Friday night and were pleasantly surprised.  The original plan was to hit up MexiQ but there was a wait and we were starving. We had past this place on the way over and decided to give it a shot.  There is a decent size dining area in the back not visible from the street.  We order a few tapitas that were all pretty good - winner was the empanadas.
