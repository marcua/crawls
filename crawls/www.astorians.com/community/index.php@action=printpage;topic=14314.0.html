Title: Spring Fling Flea Market in Astoria 5/3/2009
Post by: Astoria Events on March 16, 2009, 07:04:58 PM
Join us on Sunday May 3rd 2009 from 10am-5pm for a magnificent event!  Astoria Events presents a market consisting of independent designers, arts and crafts, handmade products such as: clothing, jewelry, bags, purses, cosmetics, natural bath products, ethnic foods and delicacies and fresh baked foods.  Entertainment will include a DJ that will play a variety of music, and there will be live folk music.  We are now accepting vendor applications. For further information please  respond to this ad or send a message/email with:  your name, company name, product sold, website or link, email and phone number (optional). Tables and chairs will be provided for all vendors, table sharing is allowed up to 2 people per table.  Table size is 8 x 2.5 feet long.  Apply now to reserve a space as vendors will be selected according to product sold.
