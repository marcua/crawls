Title: Re: Zorba's Souvlaki?
Post by: liman73 on May 17, 2010, 12:32:27 PM
I know this is an old topic, but just wanted to say I tried Zorba's for the first time last evening for dinner. Been in Astoria for 2.5 years and finally went there, and it was so nice!

We ate outside, had basic Greek meats/salad, and you can't beat the 3.50 house wine and imported beers!

The service was good and the waitress was extremely friendly. She seemed to be buddies with all the regulars, so who knows, maybe I will become a regular myself!

Great place for an inexpensive but nice date, or to go alone and have a nice meal outside. I definitely recommend Zorba's!
