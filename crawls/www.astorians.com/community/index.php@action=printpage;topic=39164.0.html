Title: Valerie Green/Dance Entropy presents Impermanent Landscape
Post by: Dance Entropy on February 15, 2016, 12:24:16 PM
Green Space Presents Valerie Green/Dance Entropy In Impermanent Landscape

March 17-19 at 8pm, March 20 at 5pm
Green Space
37-24 24th Street, Suite 301
Long Island City, NY 11101
In celebration of Green Space’s 10-year anniversary Valerie Green/Dance Entropy presents Impermanent Landscape (world premier) is an evening length performance in the round inspired by the ideas of cubism, perspective, perception, and impermanence. Breaking the 4th wall and moving choreography outside of traditional formats, Impermanent Landscape features the geometry and architecture of overlapping bodies, creating it's own personal landscape surrounded by the visual art installation Fragile City.  A live feed triggered by movement sensors creates an auditory experience alongside an existing sonic environment.
 
"Green’s artistry is fully compelling" says Molly Marinik, Theater is Easy
 
Choreography by Valerie Green
Performance by Frank Leone, Kristin Licata, Jonathan Matthews, Yayoi Suzuki and Hana Ginsburg Tirosh
Fragile City Art Installation by Priscilla Stadler
Music by Martyn Axe
Text by Stephanie Davis
Lighting Design by Nessa Rodriguez
 
Admission: Presale tickets $20 available on-line at www.DanceEntropy.org (http://www.danceentropy.org/).
Tickets at the door $25.
 
Impermanent Landscape is supported, in part, by public funds from the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs in partnership with the City Council and The Mertz Gilmore Foundation Late-Stage Production Stipend
