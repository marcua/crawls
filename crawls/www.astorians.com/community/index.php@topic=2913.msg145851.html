Hey
I'm a total Astorians.com newbie, having lurked for a bit, and then built up the courage to post.

I'll just throw out some of my experiences, to add to the mix.

Angelica at, what is it, Capital? Or Angelica Realty? I don't know--I think the place is hers.
I had a tough time with her. It's probably a personal thing, but I'm a pretty patient lady, and her flightiness was too much for me. She made an appointment with me to see a place a couple blocks from my current apartment, had me come to her office only to find she forgotten our appointment. After an hour and a half of waiting, (she actually sent me home, since the wait was going to be so long) she finally picked me up at my apartment (which I thought was very nice, actually), and took me to an apartment she had listed as being on Broadway, but was in truth between 30th and 31st Avenues.

She then drove me back to her office, claiming that we would be seeing another apartment, but when we arrived she felt tired (it WAS 8:30 at this point) and offered to show it to me another day. This would have been fine, except that I was frustrated that she drove me away from my apartment, only to cancel the next viewing when we arrived at her office. I mean, I don't mind the walking, I just thought it was very strange, and rather annoying.

She also kept forgetting which client I was, what I was looking for, and what my price range was. At one point she became "playfully" pushy and said "go higher, go higher!!" in regards to my upper limit for a one bedroom. She continued to pressure me to spend more, and was really unprofessional. It was a bit too much for me, although I suppose if she has a place you like, and you can handle her personality for a short time, she might be useful.

Grace Realty is not quite my cup of tea either. I actually had a lovely conversation with Grace (once she found out I was studying to become a therapist) during which she disclosed some surprisingly personal information. She was very sweet, trusting, and certainly has a lot on her plate, but when it came to actually finding a place, she showed me one apartment and I never heard back from her again. My feeling is that she's not a malicious person by any means, but real estate might not be her biggest strength. Which is tough when the Realty is named after you.

I'll keep you folks posted as to whom I next encounter! Or I may very well just vent--I make no promises
