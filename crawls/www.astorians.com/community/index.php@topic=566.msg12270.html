I swear!  I swear!  We're working on it.

Yesterday we met with our farmer (farmer Bill) from the Green Thumb Farm to confirm and discuss details for the upcoming season.  We're finalizing our membership materials and sign up schedule.

More details will be revealed NEXT WEEK but here are a few teasers to tide you over:

1) The Hell Gate CSA will distribute from the Freeze Peach Cafe starting in JUNE.
2) Organic vegetable shares will consist of 5-6 fresh veggies distributed weekly for 25 weeks (June - November), with a cost per week in the $10 - $11 range.
3) This year we will offer a TOMATO, FLOWER, and HONEY share in addition to the regular veggies.

Green with envy yet?

Just wait until next week when we show you pictures!
