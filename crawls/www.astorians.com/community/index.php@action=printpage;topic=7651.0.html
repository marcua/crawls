Title: Riding the Kissena Corridor
Post by: Christine on August 23, 2007, 11:40:47 PM
Tonight I finally rode from Astoria to Cunningham Park and back, through the Kissena Corridor that I've read so much about but never actually attempted in its entirety.

Did a write-up about it in our local bike board: http://www.visionmtb.com/forumvb/showthread.php?p=134337#post134337 For those who want a terrific, challenging but not impossible bike ride, I highly recommend it.

By continuing over the LIE instead of heading to Cunningham Park as indicated in the story, keep going straight if you want to ride the former Vanderbilt Parkway. You can get all the way to Alley Pond Park, or even a little beyond that to Belmont Park and the racetrack!

From here to Cunningham took me 1hr 25min on a Trek mountain bike. Should take less time on a bike w/skinnier, smoother tires. Grab a free map at any bike store and go for it!  :mrgreen:
