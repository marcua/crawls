I am so happy you kids kept yourselves busy posting today. I've had a really crappy day. Not that any of you care! But, since you are my captive audience, and if I don't vent I will lose it, this was my day:

As many of you know, Wednesday is Puppy Playgroup in my house. Today I had a full house. Let's see, I had my Bichon, my Affenpinscher, 2 mini Dachshunds, a Maltese, a Yorkie, a Min Pin, a Lhasa Apso and a Miniature Schnauzer. They all know each other and play nicely together, so when a girlfriend called and suggested an afternoon of Scrabble I said sure.

I put down some carrots and celery stick for the pups, and made us fresh coffee. We both love playing Scrabble and we figured we could get in 3 really fast games. We heard what sounded like rain, and I told her she could borrow an umbrella to get home. It never occurred to either of us that rain hadn't been forecast. When I went to the kitchen to wash out the coffee pot, I opened the window to see if it had gotten any warmer—figuring that since it was raining it must be above 32°—and the sun was shining, and it was still just as cold as it had been this morning. WTF! Then my day turned crappy. There was a HUGE BOOMING NOISE. I ran and counted the pups to be sure that no-one was hurt, and then I looked for the source.

My bathroom ceiling, or should I say, what once was my bathroom ceiling was now my bathroom floor; and the water was pouring in. I live in a building with a live-in super and 2 live-in porters. I bet you can't guess who was at home. NO EFFING ONE! The water is coming down in buckets and there is no-one around to turn off the source. So, my friend runs upstairs and rings the bell of my upstairs neighbor who, can you believe it, answers the door wrapped in a towel showing off his manly goodness. Oooops. He was running a bath and forgot! FORGOT? How the hell can you forget when your own damn house must be also flooded by now?

Whew. I'm glad I got that out. Now I just have to wait for everything to dry to assess the damage and schedule the repairs.

I probably should have posted this in rants, but I feel much closer to you guys than to all the other strangers around here.

Now back to topic.

I win.
