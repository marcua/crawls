I'll add that the kinds of messages you should absolutely report are if someone is saying "F U" or "get off this board" or "get back to [insert country here]". But if someone is PM'ing you just because they are expressing an opinion, or want to continue the conversation this is hardly abuse, even if they are not as friendly as you'd like. You can always ignore them if it's a problem (there's an ignore feature under profile settings for personal messages).

That being said I think it's a good idea for us to just remember that newbies don't always understand the dynamics of online community forums. For a lot of people this is the first one they've participated in. Once you've been around for a while it's easy to shrug off many of the negative comments because you know there are lots of other people out there that agree with you. Some of the newcomers I don't think realize that and zero in on the negative comments. If you disagree with a newbie, try and go easy on them until they learn the ropes and adjust to the community.
