Oooh this is a great puzzle, aparently this was asked to one of my friends who interviewed for Microsofts DirectX team.

Albert is on an island, and is very sick.  There is nothing on this island that he can use, except he has a unique Lock and unique Key for this lock.

Ben is on a different island, too far away for Albert to reach the Ben's island, and vice-versa.  Ben has nothing on his islant also, except, unique key for his unique lock, and also the Medicine that Albert needs.

Now Strangely enough, there is an evil boatman that travels in between the two islands.  And on this boat is a chest that is attached to the boat.  This chest can be locked with the locks that both albert and ben have, but anything not locked in the chest will be eaten by the strange boatman during the trip over.

So the question is how does Ben get the medicine to Albert?
