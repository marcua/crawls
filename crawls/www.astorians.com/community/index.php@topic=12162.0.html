I just stayed at the Quality Inn on 40th Ave. last week for five days. It is acceptable, clean, and a place to sleep. Nothing fancy and next to the NW at 39th so the trip out to Ditmars wouldn't be too bad. There is also the La Quinta on Queens Blvd near 35th that isn't bad either.

I guess it depends on what you are looking for, but all I needed was a clean place to sleep, reasonable rates, close to the train.

There is also a Hotel Verve in the area that looks a bit more upscale and its has higher prices also.
