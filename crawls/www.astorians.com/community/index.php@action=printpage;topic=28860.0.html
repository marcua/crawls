Title: Community Blood Drive this Sunday
Post by: GersonK on January 21, 2015, 11:23:05 AM

The New York Blood Center will be taking blood donations this Sunday, January 25 from 10:30am - 4:30pm at Astoria Center of Israel, 27-35 Crescent Street.

Please come and help save lives.And if you need more motivation, all blood donors will receive 2 free tickets to a Mets game this spring!

Requirements:
Blood Donors must be between ages 16 and 75
Age 16 must have written parental permission (please request form prior to donation)
76 and over need doctor's note
Minimum weight 110 pounds.

Contact the ACI office (718-278-2680; acioffice18@gmail.com) for more information.
