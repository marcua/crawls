Free Financial Planning Round-table Meeting Hosted by Daniel D’Ordine, CFP

The demands of balancing work, family, and social obligations along with the responsibilities of raising your child can cause us to push aside the extremely important financial aspects of having a family. 

Join me for a free round-table meeting where you will learn how to identify and address the unique financial responsibilities related to starting a family.  We will cover the following topics:

• How to Protect Your Family:  What are the benefits of life, disability, and personal liability insurance, and how much do you need?
• Your Estate Plan:  Why you need an estate plan, a look at the basic estate documents, and assigning guardians, executors, and beneficiaries.
• How to Save for College:  And high school, and middle school, and grammar school, and pre-school, and day care…
• Saving for Retirement vs. College Funding:   Which to fund first, and how much.
• Reviewing Your Benefits at Work:  Health insurance, dependent care, tax withholding rates.

Attendees will receive a structured, organized guide to getting your financial plan off the ground, plus a useful check list of action items.  Questions and discussions will be encouraged.

Date:  Wednesday, November 17, 2010
Time:  7:30 – 9:00 p.m.
Location:  TBD, depending on number of attendees.
