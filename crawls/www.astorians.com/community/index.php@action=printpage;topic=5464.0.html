Title: Daylight Saving Time on Sunday, March 11
Post by: Astoria Luv on March 07, 2007, 06:36:35 PM
Hey everyone, don't forget that:

Beginning in 2007, most of the United States begins Daylight Saving Time at 2:00 a.m. on the second Sunday in March and reverts to standard time on the first Sunday in November. In the U.S., each time zone switches at a different time.

So check your clocks, computers, and all other devices that you have that keeps time and make sure it actually changes!
