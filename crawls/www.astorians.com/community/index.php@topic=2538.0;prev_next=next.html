One other factor to consider: people are shopping more and more online. Given the prices in store(where most things are full price, very few sales, etc), its cheaper to shop online.

For example, today I ordered 4 DVD's online, rather than even bother to check out a store. Why? Because I knew that there was no way I'd pay less than $85 for the Perfect Storm, Studio 60 on the Sunset Strip, The Thing(HD-DVD), and For Love of the Game(HD-DVD). At least $85. But I paid less than $60 with shipping. Until brick and mortar stores do something to combat this, its going to be a growing trend.
