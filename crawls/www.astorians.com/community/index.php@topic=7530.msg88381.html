I used to go to All Pets and I really liked them but their hours can be a little erratic.  I had problems picking up my cats' food and medicine because they would be closed at odd hours (like 2pm).  I switched to Astoria Veterinary Group and I really like them.  Very professional and caring.  But if you have a fairly flexible schedule or only need to appointments a few times a year, All Pets is great and affordable. 

Whatever you do, stay away from Astoria Animal Hospital on Steinway between 25th and 28th Ave!!!  I've heard quite a few scary stories from people about being ripped off or pushed into unnecessary surgeries, etc.
