Title: Queens Holistic Moms Network Chapter Kick Off Meeting/Celebration March 11th!
Post by: angelmama on February 11, 2009, 09:28:54 PM
Who says Brooklyn and Manhattan Mamas have all the fun?

Finally, Queens has it's own Holistic Moms Network Chapter! Hooray!!!

We're holding our Kick-Off Meeting/Celebration on Wednesday, March 11th from 6:30-8pm at Arrow Community Center at 35-30 35th Street (between 35th & 36th Aves) here in Astoria. It's going to be a ton of fun, with goodie bags, a raffle, plenty of info about our upcoming topic discussions at our monthly meetings - around all holistic themes.

Families with children are definitely welcome. This meeting is free and open to the public. We just ask that you RSVP so we can have enough goodie bags on hand.

For more info on our chapter and the national not-for-profit organization "Holistic Moms Network", go to http://queensny.holisticmoms.org
We're also on facebook (search for Queens Holistic Moms Network)

RSVP to queensholisticmoms@gmail.com.

If you are a Queens business or someone who provides a service in Queens and would like to donate a raffle item or something to our goodie bags, food or decorations for the event or anything else we would LOVE to hear from you. We're also looking for volunteers to help us spread the word with flyers, and via word of mouth. Please use our email address above to contact us for more information on how to get involved.
