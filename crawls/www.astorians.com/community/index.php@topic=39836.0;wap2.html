Come welcome 5778 at the Free Synagogue of Flushing, as we greet our new Rabbi and celebrate our centennial year!


The Free Synagogue of Flushing stands proudly in the midst of one of the most ethnically diverse neighborhoods in the world, an enduring center of Jewish culture. Founded in 1917, it is the oldest Reform Synagogue in Queens. True to the ideals of its founders, FSF is an all inclusive, vibrant community dedicated to equality, freedom, social action and commitment to Torah.


Join us in worship in our beautiful, historic sanctuary surrounded by the beauty of stained glass windows. Be welcomed by our open congregation.
Join us in our holiday celebrations and take part in the many educational, cultural and social events The Free Synagogue of Flushing has to offer.


- No tickets needed for High Holiday services... just call the office to reserve your seats (718-961-0030).
- Join us on Sundays for our Religious/Hebrew School and Family Education programs.  School begins on September 10th. Please call the office (718-961-0030) for more information.
- Just three blocks from the #7 train at Main Street.
