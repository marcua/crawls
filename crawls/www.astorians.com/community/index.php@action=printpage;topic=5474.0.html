OK, so being a fan of the GEICO commercials with the cavemen, I came across their website.  "www.cavemanscrib.com".

It's an interactive site so while taking a time out from actual work I started to navigate.  When you enter the living room there is an end table with a lamp on it.  Next to the lamp are magazines.  The top one is clickable.  I believe the name of the mag is "HIM".  Approximately 5 clicks into the magazine there is a page that says "The 5 Best cities of 2007". 

Number 1 says:  "When you're visiting New York, be sure to eat where the quality is guaranteed.  Stop by Hays' Deli in Astoria, Queens for the mouth-watering and famously ironic Bronto Burger."

Of course I got all giddy and stuff.  Then I wondered...is there really such a place?  I've lived here all of my life (going on 30 years) and the name does not ring a bell..AT ALL. 

Anyone?
