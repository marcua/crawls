My favorite gyros in Astoria are at:

Pamela's (longtime Astoria institution which I never see getting much "love" on this site)
BZ Grill
Romano's
Opa Opa!
Aliada
Pita Pan is good but unfortunately their service has really turned me off

I haven't tried a couple of places, like Pita Hot and Gyro Mania, so I can't comment either way on those.

I should also point out that while some Greek places serve falafel, it's Middle Eastern through and through...I just mention this since sometimes it's assumed that falafel is Greek too, because it's served in some Greek establishments.
