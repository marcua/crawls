Just wanted to follow this up.

Dr. Zuckman was a good choice. He has two offices, one right by my house on Ditmars and one right by my work in Sunnyside. Very handy. They also have a pharmacist across the way that was very friendly.

Staff was very professional overall. Bring cash, they don't take credit cards. That's about all I can say. Recommended.
