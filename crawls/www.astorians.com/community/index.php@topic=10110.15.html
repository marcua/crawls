Friends of mine have stayed at two of the newer hotels that are very close to Queenboro Plaza.  Trip Advisor links with reviews, plus my notes:

Comfort Inn -- It's been around for a few years, and several friends of mine from out of town have stayed there when visiting me.  They've all said it's been fine, and I've been in several of the rooms -- they are not large, but they are clean and the lobby is also clean and bright.

Clarion Hotel -- I think it's been renamed the Verve.  A friend of mine stayed here in the beginning of August and said it was fine and a great value (compared to hotels in Manhattan).  She didn't mention issues with noise.
