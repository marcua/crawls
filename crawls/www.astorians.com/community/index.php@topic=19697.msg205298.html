Hotel alternative in Astoria/Ditmars
« on: October 28, 2010, 04:21:37 PM »
With the holidays coming up, many of you may be making plans for family visiting Astoria. There aren't a lot of hotels in the area, and you may be looking for something more convenient than the ones near La Guardia or LIC.

My husband and I have just furnished a two-bedroom apartment on the 3rd floor of our house that's now available for short-term rental. It's small, but it sleeps four people and is sunny, private, and pleasantly furnished. We're less than a 10-minute walk from the Ditmars stop (the house is visible in the banner at the top of this page, in fact!), so it's probably quite convenient to many of you and to getting into Manhattan.

Pictures and detailed information available here: http://www.astoriaguesthouse.com. Please get in touch if you have any questions!
