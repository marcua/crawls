Crowded gyms!!! Gaaaah!
« on: January 31, 2011, 05:53:19 PM »
Hi all, apologies in advance if this comes off as too "complain-y," which to some, I'm sure it will.  Let the neg nods commence....

I have been a member at the NYSC Astoria (30th Ave and Steinway) for almost 4 years now.  I enjoy the climate, location, staff, clientele, etc.  The crowds generally ebb and flow throughout the day/year depending on lots of different factors, and I know that January is high season for all the New Years resolution gym-goers.  I am fine with the occasional wait for a treadmill, crowded group class, and/or packed locker room from time to time. 

But HOLY moly...these past couple weeks it has been a MOB SCENE.  Classes with as many as 50-60 (!!!) people in a studio that comfortably holds 35, no free lockers, lines for machines, etc.  During a class I took this past weekend, women were bumping into one another and arguments ensued.  It was highly unpleasant and enough to make me want to stop and comment to one of the managers on duty, which I didn't do, so I chose to vent my frustrations here.  :) 

I also noticed multiple people checking in with "free" 30-day passes that looked like computer print-outs.  Does anyone know if these passes are being circulated to non-members to cultivate new clients during the winter slump?  Are other gyms in the area experiencing the same thing?  Am I just acting crazy and come next week, all these newbies will be gone and sanity will restore itself? 

Any info would be appreciated!!
