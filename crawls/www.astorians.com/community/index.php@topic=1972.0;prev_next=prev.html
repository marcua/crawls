My great-grandmother used to make something like this, she used dandelions, but it works with broccoli rabe, too:

olive oil
chopped onion
minced garlic cloves
chopped serrano chile pepper (or hot red pepper flakes)
anchovies
salt
1 pound orecchiette ("little ears", so cute!)
chopped broccoli rabe

To taste: Romano cheese, black pepper. You can add ricotta cheese as a side with it, too (though, I'm not a fan ...)

cook the pasta, saute broccoli rabe with all other ingredients, mix together. Nice and simple! :)

Its good Indian style too.  

Heat ghee or clarified butter. Add black mustard seeds and cook til they start to pop. Add ground cumin, coriander, tumeric, and some cardamom pods, cook until fragrant.  Add sliced onion, sauteeing until transparent, then add washed and chopped broccoli rabe.

You can add many different things at this point-- cooked chick peas, boiled skinned potatos, paneer, cubed meat, etc.  Cook covered until done.
