don't know if you'll have the time, but there's a really nice restaurant i think everyone should go to if in Paris, it's called "La Coupole"... just looked it up and it's located at 102 Boulevard du Montparnasse, looks like it's also in the center of Paris between the 6th and 14th arrondissements. Anyway, this place has amazing food... it's a little pricey, but it's worth it, and you're in Paris!  when it's someone's birthday they turn down the lights in the entire restaurant (it's very large and spacious) and they light a sparkler on a cake and sing happy birthday loudly in French as they walk to the table.. it's just so nice.  :smitten: ahh wish I was there...
