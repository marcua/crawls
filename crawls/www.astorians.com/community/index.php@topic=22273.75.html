The terminology is a bit confusing, but a basement and a cellar are very different in nyc. A basement is defined as having at least 50% of it above ground. It is possible to legalize a basement and rent it, by applying at the buildings department to convert from 1 family to 2 family. If you dont do that, then it is illegal to rent it. A cellar is defined as being more than 50% below ground. A cellar can never be rented or occupied legally in nyc, it is used only for utilities and storage.

Either way, that was an illegal apartment being offered by a con artist who had no jurisdiction over it anyways.

~Daryl
