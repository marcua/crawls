I saw "After Innocence" - I highly recommend it as well. There are a LOT of people who serve terms and are later proved innocent through DNA evidence.

At the screening for "After Innocence" one of them men in the film came to talk about his experiences. I was amazed at the (seeming) lack of bitterness that this guy had over serving years in prison over being at the wrong place at the wrong time.

The Innocence Project is heavily involved in getting innocent people out of jail. It's based here in NYC, and you can find out about volunteering here:
