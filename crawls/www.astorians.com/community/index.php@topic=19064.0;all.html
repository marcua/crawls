New farm stand on 21st street & 28th avenue
« on: August 09, 2010, 05:09:51 PM »
Driving down 21st street today we saw a guy selling produce.  Pulled over and found out we have something new in the neighborhood.

Farmer Louis of J&L farms sets up shop every Monday (10-6) & Saturday (8-6) on 21st street (west side) @ 28th ave. More days to come. All natural foods from their family farm in NJ. No pesticides, no chemicals, no govt labeling. Just delicious foods. Today I picked up bright yellow squash, musk melon, grape tomatoes, Hungarian peppers, white peaches, eggplant and sugar corn.  He also had tomatoes, watermelon, zucchini, cucumbers. 

A very nice guy.  Born and raised in Astoria with a family farm in Monrie, NJ.  Just started this farm stand and hopes people will come.  Was happy to talk Astoria, farm, food and cooking. 

Tonight's dinner:
Musk melon, grape tomatoes, feta, flaky sea salt and fresh ground black pepper
Wild cod with corn, squash & butter succotash
Peaches with raw sugar rum caramel
