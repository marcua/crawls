Title: Peter Vallone Q&A on Thursday at the Aldos
Post by: Frappe on June 18, 2007, 09:36:34 AM
Council Member Peter Vallone Jr. shall be speaking and then doing a Q&A at the Aldos Independent Democratic Club this Thursday (6/21).  Event begins at 7:30.  All are invited to attend and then stick around for the greek food catered in from Telly's Taverna downstairs.  There is no fee for the event or the food.

The Aldos is located on 23rd Avenue, between 28th and 29th Streets at 28-13 23rd Avenue.  It is located directly above Telly's Tavern.

The Aldos Independent Democratic Club is one of the three democratic clubs in Astoria (along with the Taminents and Powhattans).  Its probably the smallest of the three, however it has begun to recognize the changing character of the neighborhood and is actively looking to combine the interests of the club and the new neighborhood together, in particularly those who have recently moved into Astoria, be it from Mumbai, Michigan or Manhattan.  Annual memberships cost $10.
