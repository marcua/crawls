Out of fairness, I have to resubmit to this thread because we gave the place another try.  Our first experience here was really, really bad.  It was very soon after they had opened.  So we went back last weekend for lunch.  This time the Margherita pizza was much better.  Not the greatest but it wasn't bad at all and we'd order pizza here again.  The fillet of sole Francese was very good and a healthy portion of fish.  Fresh and not over/under cooked.  The Veal Marsala was also very good.  The veal was very tender.  The sauce was a bit starchy, but the mushrooms were fresh and crisp and the flavor was good.  We requested that both come with rigatoni on the same plate with the same sauce as the entree.  The pasta was quite good and cooked perfectly al dente.  They also bring you a basket of fresh baked bread that is actually made from the pizza dough.  It's pretty delicious! 

As for the place.   Our waiter was very friendly and service was very good.  The time frame was just fine.  The window next to our table was pretty filthy.  The paint job has got to be the absolute worst example of faux finish painting I've ever seen in my life.  It can spoil your appetite if you focus on it too long.  Is there better pizza and Italian food in the area?  Definitely.  But I think this is not a bad choice for families and casual dining, if you're in the immediate area.  I get my hair cut at the Thai Spa next door.  I'll probably pop in to DeFrancos each time I go there.
