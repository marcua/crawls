As the deliveree, it may seem easy to say that minimums are inconvenient, but as the business owner they are pretty necessary. You don't want your delivery staff out delivering orders for $9.95 when a $30 order comes in. Being a stickler about it might mean they are having a normal volume of business or heavier that night. Conversely, if a restaurant delivers for you at under their minimum, they might be having a slow evening and can justify letting one of their delivery staff go for a small order to build goodwill.

Doubt they want to lose anyone's business. They probably just can't afford to send someone out for less than the minimum.
