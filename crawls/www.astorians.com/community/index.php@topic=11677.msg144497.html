The last Olympics that I watched with great interest was Barcelona '92.  I don't even remember anything from Athens '04 because I was working 60 to 70 hours a week and drinking my self into a coma when I wasn't working.

I've sort of been caught up in the political, social, and environmental issues with Beijing '08.  Besides the big names like Phelps, the US basketball team, and that 40-year-old swimmer, I really don't know about the participants.  I'm sure NBC will show the personal side of many of the athletes, but I just want to see them compete!

I used to be a big track fan.  Whether it was disc or marathon, I was glued to the television.  But now we just don't know who's taking what or which one of our childhood "heroes" were on the juice.  I used to like Marion Jones and Ben Johnson, two of the biggest names in the sport, but we all saw what happened to them.

Sorry for being negative.  I know that I will probably plop in front of my television and watch like everyone else.  I just will never have the same enthusiasm as I did like the '84 LA or the '88 Seoul games.
