Les Moules seems overpriced to me, but maybe that's just because I know how cheap mussels are.  Their mussels entrees are in the $17 to $19 price range, if I recall.  They have a website with menus, but I can't locate it offhand.  I have their card at home but not here at work.  If I figure out the website, I'll post it.

The other thing I find odd about their menu is that their non-mussel section has a kind of "one of everything" approach - one chicken dish, one salmon dish, one steak dish, etc.  I also don't think there are many, if any, vegetarian options on the menu.  Also, their menu doesn't have a tapas section, even though they describe themselves as "mussels and tapas".  Instead, you are apparently served "a selection of tapas" either before or with your meal.

None of my comments relates in any way to the quality of the food, by the way - I haven't eaten there.  For all I know it's great and worth every penny.

My weird vibe about the place may just be that it seems to be cursed as a restaurant spot.  Who else remembers Ditmars Video?
