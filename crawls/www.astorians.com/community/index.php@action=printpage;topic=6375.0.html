Title: Queens Parks photo contest
Post by: Christine on May 17, 2007, 09:31:42 AM
PERFECT WEATHER FOR TAKING PICTURES IN A PARK

The peak of park season is upon us and there's less than four months left to capture Queens' parks and submit entries to A Salute to Queens Parks Photography Contest. Amateur photographers are invited to submit photos to the photography contest through September 4. Winning entries will be exhibited at the Queens Museum of Art and the Arsenal Gallery, as well as published in the Queens Courier and a book.

All photos must be taken in a Queens park or playground, at a Parks operated facility or a Parks sponsored event. A panel of experts in photography, art and parks will judge entries in four categories and three age groups. Categories include Black and White, People, Nature/Landscape, and Places/Landmarks. Age groups are divided into Youth (ages 12 and under), Teen (ages 13-17), and Adult (ages 18 and over). One winner from each age group in each category will be selected.

For further information, please visit www.nyc.gov/parks.
