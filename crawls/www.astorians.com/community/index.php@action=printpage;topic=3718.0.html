Title: A call to all bloggers and webmasters
Post by: merm on October 06, 2006, 05:57:55 PM
Hi all,

A couple people have asked about ways they can help support the site, so I though I'd share this. Linking to us helps in more ways that one. It lets people that visit your site know about Astorians, but also helps the search engines find us easier. Here are a few things you can do to help out.

1. If you are a blogger and you see a conversation you enjoyed on Astorians, mention it in your blog with a link to the thread.

2. Check out some of the banners (http://www.astorians.com/index.php?topic=3718.msg37812#msg37812) created by wasabisam below and add one to your site.

3. If you're a webmaster, consider adding a link back to astorians in your blogroll or "links" section.

4. Tell people about astorians! Use the "Send This Topic" button and let others know when you find an interesting discussion here.

If anyone else has any suggestions please post them here. And thanks to anyone who helps get the word out about the site.
