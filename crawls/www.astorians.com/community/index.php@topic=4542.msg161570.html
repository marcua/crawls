I put the words in pale gray because it wasn't a serious call for sympathy.  If it were a serious call for sympathy I would have said in bold letters "This is a serious call.  I need sympathy."  It was my attempt at poignant art work - the lighthearted child-like artwork, contrasting with the would-be sad reality - the cold apartment, missing Mr. Bunny.  I had been planning for a few days to wear the hat in the apartment - I'd taken winter holiday decorations out of the closet - and it really was a practical thing to wear that day, but I wasn't really feeling bad at the time I wrote the words.  I do sometimes feel bad about him not being here, but I wasn't having an especially deep moment at the time.
