Title: Custom Wedding Invitations
Post by: Twinky on July 09, 2009, 04:43:14 AM
You have probably chosen your dream dress, determined your wedding location, and perhaps selected your color palette of have been dreaming and fantasizing about this day for along time.  You have visualized everything in you mind many times over.   This is an exciting time which can also be overwhelming. 

Now you need to attend to all the details of your fabulous wedding and introduce those details to your guests via your ideal wedding invitation.  We make invitations the easiest and most stress-free part of the planning process.  Whatever you decide to for your wedding, your invitation should reflect both the event and your personality. 

Invitations are all about first impressions, and each should be as unique as your event.  The first thing any prospective guest knows about an event is what they can gather from looking at the invitation sent to them.  It should speak VOLUMES about what one can expect by attending.

Going Postal custom and handmade invitations are conceived, styled and designed to fit each event they are announcing precisely, so guests are inspired… not just obligated… to attend.  We design invitations which suit the needs, style and  budget  of every client we work with.
 
We believe in more than ivory cardstock and ebony type.  We work side by side with you to create an invitation that will make you and your guests party inspired!  Our mission in simple:  add some beauty to the world by creating truly unique and elegant handmade invitations and announcements.  An invitation is advertising for your event… and the best advertising is to be UNFORGETTABLE.

We believe in being unforgettable, in sending your guest an invitation they will talk about for years, or keep on the fridge for good because it’s just to pretty to toss.  We want to make them  feel like the most special person in the world when they see your invitation.  Sweet or sophisticated, elegant or playful, traditional or modern — we can customize your invitation or announcement, choosing your favorite colors, papers and fonts.  No invitation is like any other and each sets the perfect tone for your wedding.
