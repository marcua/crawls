Funding for Queens Artists and Arts Organizations

(1/1)

QueensCouncilArts:
Queens Council on the Arts (QCA) announces the 2016 Queens Arts Fund (QAF) funding cycle that supports the hard work and contributions of the boroughâ€™s creative citizens with grants for their programming. In 2015, QCA awarded over $180,000 in grants to 39 individual artists and 40 organizations for the creation of arts and cultural programming in the borough of Queens.

New guidelines and the online grant application for the 2016 grant cycle are available on the QCA website http://www.queenscouncilarts.org/qaf/.

Queens-based individual artists and nonprofit organizations producing cultural programming in all arts discipline and meeting eligibility and requirements are invited to apply to the 2016 QAF grant program.

ALL APPLICANTS MUST ATTEND A QAF APPLICATION INFORMATION SESSION, which begins Wednesday, July 29th.

Application Deadline - October 8, 2015

For more information visit www.queenscouncilarts.org/qaf or contact Lynn Lobell, Grants and Resource Director, llobell@queenscouncilarts.org or 347.505.3015 or Sam Dalsheimer, QAF Grants Associate, at qcagrants@gmail.com or 347-505-3019.
