I have curly hair and never had luck with anyone until I found Casey @ the Beehive Salon in Williamsburg, 4 years ago.  She does my cut & color and I haven't gone to anyone else since.  She cuts the curls dry which really made the biggest difference.  Then you can actually see what you are cutting vs. when curly hair is wet, as we all know isn't the same.

Sadly, she moved back home but she will be at the Salon on weekends about every 6 - 8 weeks, which is perfect.  She did recommend Elieen, should I need to get a cut in between her weekends.

The salon is right off of the L @ Bedford and is totally reasonably priced.  Everyone is great there.
