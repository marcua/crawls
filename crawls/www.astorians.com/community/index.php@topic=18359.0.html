We took our son to Dr. Bloom and Dr. Costin at Metro Pediatrics. In general, they are pretty good but we've decided to switch pediatricians due to their preferred vaccination schedule and my particular insurance plan.

They are great if you are interested in spreading out your child's vaccinations because that's what they strongly prefer to do. However, they are opposed to the use of combination vaccines such as Pentacel except in the cases where your child needs to catch up on vaccinations. Not exactly sure why. In the end, they seem very conservative with their vaccination approach, preferring to ensure the child has a healthy response to each vaccine. Again, if this is what you're looking for, that's great because it's hard to find pediatricians who will do this.

Unfortunately, my particular insurance plan has a deductible and I can't afford to make all of those extra visits for something I don't feel that strongly about.
