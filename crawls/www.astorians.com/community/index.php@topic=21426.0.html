Hello...
Well, it's Thursday, the day when my Dad drives into Astoria to take me out to dinner.

We had become addicted to Emilio's cooking at Hellgate and were really sad to hear that the "kitchen is under renovation".

Some of the locals mentioned that the owner of Hellgate didn't treat Emilio very fair and let the business fall into disarray.

The health department closed the kitchen because of violations, which is truly sad because Emilio really put his heart into every delicious meal he's prepared for us.

Does anyone have any suggestions on how Hellgate's kitchen can get back up and running?
Perhaps there is a better suggestion as to another establishment Emilio can bring his culinary talent and be treated well?

Either way, my Dad is really bummed that he's not having Emilio's Eggplant Parm tonight for dinner...
