My boyfriends' parents have stayed at the Fairfield Inn on Astoria Blvd a few times, and they've always had a nice room.  I'm not sure what they charge per night, but Fairfield Inn's are usually pretty reasonable.  There are also some nice hotels closer to LaGuardia--the Wingate by Wyndham, a Crowne Plaza, and a Courtyard Marriot, I believe.  I've been inside all 3 for work, and they're all nice hotels.  I would Google "Laguardia Airport" and "hotels" and see what comes up.  Your friend should be able to find a variety, and something will fit his price range.

Enjoy your visit!
