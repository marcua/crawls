Post by: lanseaux on October 11, 2006, 10:49:04 AM
We got cilantro, so they must have run out.  That's too bad.

I made a salsa verde similar to the recipe in the newsletter -- it is fantastic.  I broiled about a pound of tomatillos in two batches.  Burned the second batch a little, which turned out to be a good thing.  Pureed them (stick blender) and added them to 1 minced jalapeno (those jalapenos are amazing!), bunch of cilantro, minced, two minced garlic cloves, some minced onion.  Added a few drops of yuzu.  Let it sit for awhile.

We had it two ways.

First with porgie fillets - dipped in flour mixed with Mexican spice, then egg.  Pan-fried golden in olive oil.  Slathered with the salsa.  I've never had tomatillos be sweet like this, and it is the first time I've ever understood tomatillo salsa as a really perfect spicy-sweet combination.

Then I took an egg, beat it, heated the oil until nearly smoking, and poured the egg in very thin.  We wrapped the egg with the salsa inside like a tortilla.  The spicy sweet salsa was heaven with the egg.
