Isn't there anything more important going on in the world right now than this?  So they got the guy... Big *%$*## deal!!!  I don't mean to be disrespectful or anything, but why is this the biggest news story right now?  Is it because she was a rich little white girl?  Why don't we hear about minorities that are killed at a young age, and the murders never get solved, much less even investigated?  There are so many issues of such greater significance than this that don't even get touched on.  Ugh.  The state of the news media makes me want to hurl.

My father was a journalist back in the early 80's with the PBS affiliate in Rochester.  He left journalism, because he could see, even all the way back then, where the news media was going, and no longer wanted to be associated with it.

How can we as a society make proper, informed decisions about anything, when the top news story is about the murder of a single rich girl that took place 10 friggin years ago?

This has nothing to do with liberal or conservative slant in the media.  It has to do with $$$.  I tend to see this as just another symptom of the downfall of society as we know it.  Profit reigns supreme, and the people are left in the dark.
