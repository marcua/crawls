I love Thai food and was used to getting from Sripraphai before I moved here which means I got terribly spoiled by the best Thai food in NYC being a walk away. Bangkok Tasty is spicy to a fault sometimes and it's pretty inconsistent with the spiciness. I've ordered things mild and they've been inedible and spicy is just ridiculous. I love spicy food and my husband eats habenero peppers like a snack and puts the hottest hot sauces on things but he's been unable to eat some things from there, which means it's just crazy spicy. It's a shame too because most of the underlying flavors are great but are at times overwhelmed by the heat. I'm hoping this place has the awesome Thai food I miss from living in Woodside. Wave is ok but not amazing and the same goes for Thai Elephant. Thai Pavillion is pretty damn good though and they're opening that new on on 21st St. and 24th Ave. in the Pistilli building.
