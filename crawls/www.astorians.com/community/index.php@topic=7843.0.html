Reasons NOT to bring your neighbors homemade pie
« on: September 10, 2007, 10:13:36 PM »
Jonathan knocks on the neighbor's door.  He can hear them inside whispering and shuffling around.  He knocks again.  They say "who is it?'  He says - "Jonathan from downstairs."   No response.  Still whispering and they turn off the music.  But they don't open the door. 

Jonathan comes back in - he's baffled.  The neighbors won't open the door. 

15 min later - there's cop cars out front - cops jumping the fence - cops coming in the front door.  "do you have a roof?"  "how do we get to your roof?" 

Apparently - unused to friendly neighbors bearing home baked goods - the upstairs folk called the cops b/c there was a man at their door (or was he on the fire escape?  as they reported).
