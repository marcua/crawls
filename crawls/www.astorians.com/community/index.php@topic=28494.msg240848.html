I work near a Caffe Bene (2 Fs) in Chelsea and I enjoy their coffee.   It is worlds better than Starbucks. 

I went to 60 Beans last weekend and tried one of their pour over coffees.  I like the atmosphere of the place and it was comfortable.   I was completely disappointed by the coffee though and it was $4+.   It was bitter and not even hot.   I am not familiar with the ins and outs of pour over coffee but it took the barista a good ten minutes to slowly pour the coffee (not exaggerating).  So by the time she was done the liquid was luke warm for obvious reasons. 

I would like to support the local places too but I was really disappointed with the coffee at 60 beans and it was expensive! 

I did buy some beans at OK Cafe and they were wonderful.   I prefer the coffee there but the atmosphere/space at 60 Beans.  Oh and I completely agree that the people at OK Cafe are incredibly nice and I will probably patronize the place for that reason alone.   

I wish that it was not the case, but I find the coffee at Caffe Bene to be on par with either place and a solid $1-2 cheaper for a single cup of Joe.
