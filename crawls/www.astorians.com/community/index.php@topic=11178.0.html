I was speaking to one of the tree trimmers this morning, on the way back from the park, and I was wondering if they were going to be trimming all the trees or just what they were doing this week. This will be ongoing throughout part of the summer. The branches were trimmed so high because of branches interfering and being close to snapping the overhead lines which include: Con Edison power lines, Time Warner Cable lines, Verizon copper lines and RCN cable lines. He told me that if they hadn't done the trimming this week, a lot of those cables would have been snapped and there would have been an even bigger mess. Some of the trees, he said, also had pest problems and that they're trying to do some damage control on them. Hopefully everything works out. Also some of the trees were trimmed to allow for light from the street lights to be able to filter through to the street. You may notice certain areas will be brighter now that the trees have been trimmed.
