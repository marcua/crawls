Holidaze Book Swap * Sunday 12/16/07 * 2 pm

Special...Post-Thanksgiving/Hanukkah...Pre-Christmas/Kwanzaa/New Year's...Book Swap!

Sunday, December 16th 2007 at 2 pm
Freeze Peach Cafe: 29th St off Ditmars Blvd

Bring books to give or lend...take books to borrow or keep. If you want yours back, put your name and contact info inside the cover. At the end of the swap, we bring the remaining (unwanted) volumes to a nearby thrift shop.

Can't make it this time? No problem, we meet on the last Sunday of every month!1 The next regularly scheduled book swap will be on January 27th, 2008.

1 Except around the holidaze.  :lol:
