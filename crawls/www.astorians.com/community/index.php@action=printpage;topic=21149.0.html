Blue Danube Wine Company and Oenocentric Present....


Winemakers of Croatia
at
Veslo Restaurant
32-11 Broadway {Between 32nd & 33rd Streets}
Astoria, NY
718-728-0549


Tuesday June 14, 2011 from 7:00PM to 10:00PM


• Sample the widest variety of Croatian wines ever presented to American consumers - unlimited tastings!


• Meet & greet visiting Croatian Winemakers


• Learn about the rapidly evolving Croatian wine industry


• Taste hors d'ourves prepared by Veslo's expert Chef!


$35 per person


Tickets available online at - http://www.showclix.com/event/52080/


FEATURED WINES | Bibich • Coronica • Degrassi • Dingac Winery • Daruvar Winery • Karaman • Korta Katarina • Krajancic • Matosevic • Milos • Saints Hills • Terzolo • Toljanic • Tomic • Trapan


Wineries that will be represented by owners are Korta Katarina, Matosevic, Saints Hills, Toljanic, and Trapan. Blue Danube Wine Company and Oenocentric will also be represented pouring wines from respective portfolios. Wines from 15+ wineries will be poured, and attendees will have the chance to sample well over 50+ different wines with authentic Croatian cooking at a truly authentic Croatian event in New York City.


Advance reservations recommended. by calling the restaurant or buying online.
