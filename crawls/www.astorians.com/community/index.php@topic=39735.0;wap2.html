C-J:
Walked by this new place on Ditmars this past Saturday while running errands and the server came outside and chatted us up while we were looking at the menu.


They serve brunch all day with a full dinner menu as well so we returned on Saturday evening for dinner.


In short, the food is really terrific!  I had the pancakes and chicken and my husband had the duck sandwich.  The skewer and deviled egg appetizers were also great! 


Service is on point and they will have their liquor license soon. 


This is a welcomed new addition to the 'hood and worth checking out!  http://www.chopchopgrubshop.com/restaurant
