Mugged on Crescent between Ditmars and 23rd
« on: June 24, 2008, 03:03:52 AM »
I was mugged about an hour ago three doors from my house on Crescent between Ditmars and 23rd. It was two men in their late 20s or early 30s, white, who came up on me and my boyfriend from behind. They took my wallet and money from his.

I don't really know what I hope to accomplish by posting this, other than to warn people that these things do happen in safe neighborhoods and to always watch who is behind you.

I am a wreck right now and my boyfriend is outside waiting for the police to finish writing the report.

I want to thank all of the neighbors who came outside when they heard me screaming.
