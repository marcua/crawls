Title: Re: Physical Therapist Recommendations
Post by: geowrld on February 24, 2014, 09:23:48 PM
I have only ever had one physical therapist - but I've "used" her for many different injuries (broken bones, plantar fascitis).  I have watched other people get PT from others (not her) and I was appalled.  I HIGHLY recommend Tamar Amitay of Thrive PT.  Her office is in the Cable Building on Houston and Broadway. 


She's very hands on - which is important - painful, but important.  Other PT's I've watched put on a heat or cold pack or maybe do a little stim or ultrasound.  Tamar is really into ergonomics and fascial release and whatever else you need. 


Susan is a PT who works at Thrive and she's very good, too.  My dad saw her after back surgery and she really helped him.
