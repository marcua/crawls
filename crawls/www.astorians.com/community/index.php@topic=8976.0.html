If you're near the N/W, you can go to Broadway Station, a neighborhood sports bar.  It's between 31st Street and 30th Street, on the north side of Broadway.

Cronin and Phelans near the corner of Broadway and Steinway has the NFL Network.  At least that's what they told me when I was trying to find a place to watch the Green Bay vs. Dallas game.

I ended up at Dillinger's, which is on 30th Avenue and 47th Street that evening.  The food was pretty good and the drinks were cheap.  If there weren't so many Packer's fans there (I'm a Vikes fan), it would have been a perfect evening!
