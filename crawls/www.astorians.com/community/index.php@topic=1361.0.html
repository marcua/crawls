Starlite Karaoke Open Casting Call

Dear Starliters:
 
 PLEASE READ the entire post  it will answer most questions, if not, any questions can be e-mailed to me Richard at starlite1karaoke@aol.com
 
   First let me wish everyone a blessed and prosperous New Year!!!  May I say how thankful I am to all of you for making this our 5th consecutive year with Starlite Karaoke as our show keeps getting stronger and stronger.
Our big contest at Doyle's Corner ($3000.00 For First Place) is getting better and better each week as the talent level is through the roof.  The big final show on March 11 is being hyped as nothing short of incredible. So far we have:
Johnny Rambo, Jeremiah Cebulski, The Risque Carla,
Nathalie Matthews (http://www.nathaliematthews.com/),
Johnny Hollywood, Meaghan Farrell (http://skeegersketch.com/) Richie The Bartender (http://www.croninandphelans.homestead.com/)
Beth Soderbergh, Meghan Heddrich, and Arthur are all in the finals, as they each won their week. There are 10 weeks left and it's real simple to qualify for a chance to win the Grand Prize of $3000.00. Just join us any of the remaining Saturday's leading up to March 11, pay the bartender $10.00 (which we are giving all back by using it for second & third place prizes) and win the weekly contest that night and you are in the finals for a chance to win one of the cash prizes. Each week the contestants will draw numbers to see which place they will sing and will be judged 
on four categories with one to ten star's being given for each category. The categories are as followed, 1. originality, 2. song choice, 3. your ability to sing and 4. your stage presence or showmanship.This contest is opened to Anyone and Everyone. I feel if you really think about everything we are judging you on this contest can be won by anyone with a great imagination and a deep desire to perform. Who will be next to qualify. I wish everyone the best of luck...
 
Everyone knows that a big part of our success and longevity is because of the AMAZINGLY Talented Astorians that so religiously attend our shows. Most of you are hard working people that are trying very hard to break in The Business as singers dancers and actors. This can sometimes be a real pain in the ass and I always applaud you in your relentless pursuit to just be a working actor. I always try to promote you in any way I can and now  I will be able to offer you a place to hone your craft and have a place to perform as well as grow.  I am in negotiations with
St. Sebastian Church www.stsebastianwooside.org  and will soon be opening
The Woodside Performing Arts Center (WOPAC). The facility is just amazing with a big stage and a room that has a 1000 seat capacity... I was planing on having 4 productions a year with adult actors and then incorporating a 5th show with the children of St. Sebastian and the dance studio of June Parodi across the street. I also want to have an after school program where students can learn all the in's & out's of the dramatic arts and musical theater. I will be needing everyone from actors, singers dancers as well as writers, directors, stage managers and producers, as well as qualified teachers who are wanting to teach our children about the precious gift of The Dramatic Arts... Please E-mail me Richard at starlite1karaoke@aol.com if you are interested in joining our production company.
 Our first show will be "Grease" so if you want to be a part of this show please e-mail me. I will not need any resumes or head shots at this time, I just want to see who wants to be a part of this great opportunity. This is going to be a really great thing as St. Sebastian is arguably one of the biggest parishes in The Brooklyn/Queens Dioceses with over 10,000 parishioners so I am predicting many SOLD -OUT shows...
 
Kelly and I will be rocking the New Year at Doyles Corner this Saturday and it will be a blast, so come down as Barry the Bartender told me to tell everyone there will be no better place to be in the city then Doyles. We will be doing karaoke and playing all the great dance hits that will keep you moving and shedding off all of that great food you ate all week long...
 
It looks like we are going to have our good friends
The Getaway Drive (www.thegetawaydrive.com) to finally play Astoria again. We are working all the details out and it looks like the end of January and I will confirm some dates real soon...
 
Our good friend Sarah Mclellan, (www.sarahmclellan.com) who didn't make it through on The American Idol, just shows how when one door closes one door opens as she was just hired as the new lead singer for Lez Zeppelin an all girl Led Zeppelin cover band getting rave reviews. Check them out at
www.lezzeppelin.com and

This Friday Doyles Corner welcomes Astoria's Favorite New Band "Tosser " back again as they will bring a new meaning to Sex, Hugs and Rock & Roll. Check out Tosser at www.tossermusic.net
See you at the show as this is one you won't want to miss!!!!
 
Our good friend Dani Rhodes just got finished playing piano on tour for "The Three Irish Tenors" (Yes, she plays piano too, she's classically trained) She has assures me she will be a big part of The Woodside Performing Arts Center which is just super great news!!! I  just can't wait to just hear her sing again... See you soon Dani.
 
Well, that's about it for now.
 
Check us out on the web at
www.starlitekaraoke.com
 
PS. I will be making updating the web site my biggest priority this new year, so please keep checking back with us and please bare with me. With working construction full time in the day, doing karaoke 2 to 4 nights a week, doing side work all the time, raising two young girls and starting WOPAC, I have had a lot on my plate, but I wouldn't change it for anything and I am so grateful for it all.
 
Happy New Year to All of You!!!
