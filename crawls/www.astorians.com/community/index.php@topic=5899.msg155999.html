Hey

I'm also a female in my 20's... i live on ditmars blvd just a couple of blocks from the subway... i really like it over here.  there's tons to do over there, yet it's not too noisy at night.  I especially like it because there are always people around and still has that 'city' feel, yet it's not the center of the party crowd, where as over by steinway and the area you mentioned is very residential, and then over on broadway and 30th ave is where all the bars and party scene is.

i recommend touring the areas at both the day and night time so you get a better idea of where is best for you.

finding a pet friendly apt is harder... but i was able to do it.  i have a 3 bedroom, 2 bath apt for 2400 that is pet friendly.  we have a small dog and 2 cats.
