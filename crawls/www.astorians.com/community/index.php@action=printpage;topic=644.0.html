Title: Where can I find a hand vac?
Post by: lanseaux on September 01, 2005, 02:28:04 PM
Check out King Penny next to (or nearly so) the Dunkin' Donuts on Ditmars between 31st/33rd.  They've got all sorts of little appliances, although you might have to root around for awhile.

It's fun just to go in there and look around like you're in a cluttered museum.  They've still got things like new original Mr. Coffee machines from the 1970s.
