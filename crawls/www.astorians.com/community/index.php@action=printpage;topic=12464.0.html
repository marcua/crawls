Title: Wine Tasting Trip to North Fork of Long Island!
Post by: ZogSports on September 30, 2008, 12:57:41 PM
Join a great group of NY young professionals for Wine Tasting trips this Fall!  Buses leave from and return to Midtown Manhattan.

Both trips are on Saturdays (October 11 & October 25)
 
While both trips are currently Sold Out for Women, there is still room for Men to sign up!  (Women can join the waitlist).
 
Included in the trip:
•   Transportation
•   Tastings at 3 vineyards
•   At least one winery tour
•   Hayride and Pumpkin picking
•   Lunch outside at a vineyard
•     Discounts on wine purchased at vineyards
     
Registration is on Now Here: http://www.zogsports.org/activity/trips (http://www.zogsports.org/activity/trips)! 

Questions?  Email info@zogsports.org
