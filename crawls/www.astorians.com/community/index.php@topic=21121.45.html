Went there today to try their new brunch menu.  They're such nice folks, I felt bad leaving without eating, but... the options are all priced to include a cocktail, and meat (sausage, and bacon).  Since I didn't want alcohol, and don't eat meat, it wasn't going to work too well.  I could have had the Bananas Foster French Toast, possibly, which I don't think comes with sausage or bacon, but I was interested in savory breakfast food first.
 
Will work great, though, if you do eat meat and want a cocktail.  I bet it's all great, and with all that, reasonably priced.
 
I appreciate that they did add into their new dinner menu more options for vegetarians - super appreciative.  I'd bet that they tinker with their brunch over time too, and wouldn't rule out checking it out again sometime.
