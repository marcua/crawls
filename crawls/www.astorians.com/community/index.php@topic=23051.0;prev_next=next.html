Car Tires Stolen & Burglaries Upper Ditmars
« on: June 23, 2012, 11:17:25 AM »
Car thieves and Burglars have moved to Upper Ditmars.  This morning an Accura had all four tires stolen on 78th St. near Ditmars.  Last week a Honda Accord was missing all four on 79th St. near 21st Ave.  About two months ago a Toyota Camry had two stolen again on 78th St. close to 21st Avenue.  I spoke to the police officer this morning and he said wheel locks are somewhat of a deterrent but he has seen cars have tires stolen with wheel locks on too.  If you park close to the curb sometimes it is hard to get the tires off.


About two months ago two houses on 79th St. between Ditmars and 21st Ave were burglarized.  Also on the same block a new house that is being constructed was broken into several times and fixtures were stolen. 


I didn't used to see or hear much about this in this area. I hope it is not a trend although crime happens everywhere.
