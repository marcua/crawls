Features                                                  December 13, 2006


Manhole Fire Causes Small Blackout

On December 6, a manhole fire at 7:20 p.m. occurred at three locations on 45th Street between Broadway and 34th Avenue, resulting in a power outage to approximately 198 customers.

Power to most customers was restored by 3 a.m. on December 7, with the exception of four customers whose power was restored by 3:40 p.m. later that day.

Con Edison crews built shunts— above ground cable—to restore power to customers as quickly as possible. Shunts were necessary to replace cables damaged by the fire.

The cause of the fire is being investigated.
