Hi Yippee 


I was smiling that someone out there also enjoy feeding the birds/bluejays.  I myself feed the winter cardinals/bluejays/sparrows/mourning doves/chickadee's/other smaller birds and squirrels.  I don't discriminate and feed pigeons whenever possible (that's me).


Once in a while, I will have a visiting pigeon and they are so quick to pick up the seeds I buy for the other feathered birds.  I have been doing this for years in my backyard.  I keep a fairly medium size bowl w/water ... they all drink from it and even witness some bathing in it - even in this cold weather!   Gotta change it when it gets really cold overnite since it freezes.


Oh yeah to give you my comments on the peanuts ...  you mention you didn't care for Best Market (43rd Str?) - last checked, they have the best deal.  I always have brought the UNSALTED Roasted with no problem. They also carry the raw peanuts (Hampton Farms brand, yellow bag).  Personally, I find the raw nuts are harder for them to break into.  Cost: $3 for 2, 16oz bags.  Even at the $1.99 price, it's still not bad.  I won't go into further details, LOL, but this is the short version.
