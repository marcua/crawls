Title: Plaza Lounge
Post by: alyssa726 on November 11, 2007, 11:42:04 PM
i had another great experience at Plaza Lounge this weekend, i thought i would share.

our wraps and sandwiches were great, but i REALLY enjoyed the crab and shrimp fondue, and i would highly recommend it.

my father loved the coffee and he is a very picky coffee guy.

it's also a great value.

i don't think they do brunch, because it was saturday afternoon and they just had the regular menu.

Title: Re: Plaza Lounge
Post by: alyssa726 on November 13, 2007, 05:27:26 PM
yea, i don't think it is fondue like fondue is supposed to be, with the dipping stuff in other stuff.  it's like a big caserole of melted cheese with little shrimps and crab in it, served with garlicky pieces of bread.  i don't know if it was real crab, but everything they serve is really fresh and delicious so i would say... probably.

i asked what cheeses were in it because i was so taken with the dish and they were mozzarella, gouda and.... damn, i forgot the third one, but there were three.  it's also available without the seafood, if you're not into that kind of thing.

plaza lounge is located at 2902 30th Ave, right across the street from the park.
