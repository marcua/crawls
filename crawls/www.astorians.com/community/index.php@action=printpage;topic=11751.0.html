Crown Jewels and Hood Ornaments - Queens vs. Bronx Rematch!
Bronx beat Manhattan (159-79) in the most recent installment of the 2008 GGRD season, and damn! It was one hell of a fierce crashing, smashing, derby fiesta! Manhattan 2 time game MVP, Em Dash, scored a whopping 52 of Mayhem's 79 points. Bronx captain Kandy Kakes took home the MVP title for her team after spending the night picking off anything in orange she could see.

The season continues Saturday, 8/16 when the Bronx Gridlock cabbies battle the Queens of Pain at Hunter College Sportsplex at Lexington Avenue and 68th Street in Manhattan.

Last year, the Gridlock stole the championship trophy away from the 'Pain (95-90) in a total nailbiting season finale, and both teams go into this much awaited re-match undefeated for the 2008 season. Existing Gotham fans know this matchup promises to be even more thrilling and new fans will be hooked from the first whistle.
