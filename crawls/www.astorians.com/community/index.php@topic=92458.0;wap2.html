In need of Greek-speaking volunteers

(1/1)

michelleinqueens:
I have had the fortune of working as a volunteer and employee at Sunnyside Community Services for the past 2 years. We are currently in search of volunteers who speak another language, especially Greek. We also usually have a high need for male volunteers, as we try to pair older men with male volunteers.

The program is one that is close to my heart, as I first learned of Sunnyside Community Services through this program. The commitment is only for one hour per week. I can personally speak to the good it does in the lives of seniors to have someone who visits them consistently.




If you would like to learn more, you can visit us online here [https://www.scsny.org/programs/senior-programs/friendly-visiting-pet-pals][/size], email me, or call me at 929.335.7800. Thanks.
