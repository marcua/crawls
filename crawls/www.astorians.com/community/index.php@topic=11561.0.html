DON'T buy from Jennifer.  Worst customer service I've ever experienced.  For the money, go elsewhere.  Their leather "protection" plan is a joke and falsely advertised.  Do a google for jennifer furniture to see...

http://www.consumeraffairs.com/furniture/jennifer_lifetime.html

As most people reported, when I called customer service, I had to wait 45 minutes before speaking to anyone.  I was told one thing at the store, and completely contradictory info from customer service (when I could finally speak with someone).

From Wikipedia...

"On July 29, 2004 New York State Attorney General Eliot Spitzer announced a consent order and judgment that will significantly reform the sales practices of Jennifer Convertibles and provide restitution to consumers. The judgment permanently enjoined Jennifer Convertibles from engaging in deceptive, fraudulent or illegal business practices, and requires the company to make substantial reforms to its sales practices and customer service operations. The judgment also requires Jennifer Convertibles to make restitution to consumers for its failure to replace or repair defective goods. In addition, Jennifer Convertibles agreed to pay $275,000 in penalties, and $2,000 in costs."

But if you do buy from them, stay away from leather.

You're much better off going to Macy's or La-Z-Boy.

We got our couch from Jennifer's.  We ordered it from the one on 3rd Ave just north of 57th or 58th street.  The sales guy there was very good and patient.  It took us several visits before deciding.  Not the best quality in the world, not the best service in the world, but very good for the price.

As for internet service - I've had Time Warner and hated it.  I switch to RCN and love it!  I have their top tier 20Mbps plan and it is very fast.  I don't think (though I'm not sure) that TW offers tiered service this fast.  Really, though, it can be a crap shoot.  I've heard of people having problems with RCN and loving TW.  It really depends on the very neighborhood you're in and how well the installation goes.

Just a personal plug - if you do happen to go with RCN and feel generous - send me a PM first.  If you give them my information as a referral, I think I get a $100 credit.  We can split it if you like.  Anyway, don't make your decision based on that...just an aside.
