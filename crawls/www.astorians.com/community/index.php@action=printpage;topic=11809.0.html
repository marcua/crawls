Post by: mronda on August 15, 2008, 10:07:13 AM
If you're looking near Broadway/Steinway, and depending on your aesthetic standards, and political orientation, there are at least 2 options about which I know:

The VFW Post on 41st Street near Bway rents their space at affordable prices:
Post 2348 Long Island City Post 3135 41st; (718) 728-6220

The Chian Federation rents their space and does catering (corner of 44th Street and Broadway): http://www.chianfed.org/mos/index.php

Up north, Bohemian Hall has indoor rooms they rent for private parties, and offer catering: http://www.bohemianhall.com/home.htm

There's also a website that lists more options (including restaurants): http://www.eventective.com/USA/New+York/Astoria/

Happy brunching!
