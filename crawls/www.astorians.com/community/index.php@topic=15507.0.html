We had another ridiculously pleasurable experience tonight eating at Hellgate on the Park.  Perfect night tonight, delicious eggplant parmagiana, glass of red wine, and the jukebox ringing out with some of my favorite Sinatra hits.

How many restaurants in this country, from sea to shining sea, strain painfully to try to duplicate this experience, and fail miserably?  I left with a strong feeling that this is only for now, we're lucky to have a place like this happening now.  It's the kind of place that people wish was still open.
