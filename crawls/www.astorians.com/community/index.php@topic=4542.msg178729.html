My place is small.  That's part of the problem - no room to put furniture meant no place to store things properly.  I have to use half my living room as a dining area.  I do need to do clean more often, but I've got seriously painful permanent injuries and that gets in the way.  I did all this after a week of taking Celebrex.

It turns out that the most time consuming thing about that tv cabinet was packing up the miscellanea.  After it was out of the way and I dusted and washed the cabinet I saw the better ways to use it.  A big decorative storage box, in a floral pattern belonging to a design scheme I know longer have an interest in, none the less occupies well much of the space where a tv formerly was, and a similarly patterned big square fabric vanity organizer now sits on the box.  The narrow shelves that formerly held VHS tapes now hold light weight books.  My DVD player and cable box are still on the upper shelves, and I my all-in-one volumes of Beatrix Potter, Madeleine, and Winnie the Pooh are laying flat on one of the lower shelves, and there is room for big decorative storage baskets on the lowest shelf.  I dusted off my best beloved big screen tv and the bureau it sits on and today still had enough brain cells left to clean the window and the air condtioner.  I had four panels of curtains that I bought long ago and never used - two pale sky blue and two in the floral theme, so even though floral is no longer my thing, it was nice to have something new to put up.  The floral curtains came with tie backs - there are little rings on the ends that are supposed to go over a hook on the wall, but lacking the hooks I simply put little gold safety pins through the rings.  They hold the curtains back mainly so that they don't block the air conditioner.  So, all the screaming madness paid off.  The room is nearly done.  Everythng still needing to be done can be done in small manageable chunks of time.  And then I can do the living room.  AAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAAHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHHH!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!!
