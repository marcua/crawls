I made tapenade today with my rockin' professional-ish Cuisinart food processor.  Man, I love that thing!  It's quiet, has 11-cup capacity, and it's just my new favorite gizmo. As for the tapenade, well, I hate to brag, but it rocked.  Hard to tell in the pic, but it has an almost purple tint to it.  I totally invented a recipe.  Olives, sauteed mushrooms, lemon juice, fresh parsley, basil, and thyme, garlic, salt, pepper, and olive oil.  Yum scrum!  I also made a white bean hummus, too, but it's not as pretty.
