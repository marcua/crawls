"Come Over For Coffee" is a storytelling home concert in the historic district of Jackson Heights.
If you've never been to a storytelling home concert before (and most people haven't!), it's about 20 people sitting around in a living room (no campfire, sorry!), having refreshments, and listening to people tell true, personal stories from their lives (we'll also have a folklorist this month.)


We have networking so that neighbors can meet one another, and eating, from 5:30pm to 6pm. Then stories from 6:00pm to almost 8:00pm. We request a $20/person donation.


Email heightshub@gmail.com for a reservation and our address.
