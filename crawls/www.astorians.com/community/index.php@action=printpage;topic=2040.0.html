Title: Runners in Astoria
Post by: megc on May 01, 2006, 01:53:17 PM
So, a few of us here get together on a semi-regular (hopefully more regular now that it's warmer) basis and run together in Astoria, usually in the Ditmars/Astoria Park area.  We also run at the Central Park Reservoir when possible.  Sometimes early mornings, sometimes evenings and also on some Saturday mornings.  The runs are pretty low-pressure and right now are around 3 miles or so; some of us are trying to get back into said regular running and up the mileage. Right now is great running weather!  If anyone would like to join in the fun, post here or let me know.  I know that it helps me to have other people to run with.  Oh, one more thing - Saturday morning runs usually incorporate the goal of eating pancakes afterwards, and that is worth the run all in itself!

We have a great time and would love to see more local runners!  Come join us!
