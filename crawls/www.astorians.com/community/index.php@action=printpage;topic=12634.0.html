Title: Re: Laundromats near the Broadway N/W Stop
Post by: paratactical on October 13, 2008, 04:20:57 PM
In my limited experience, most places are pretty equivalent in terms of price and quality of the machines. The best thing to do is go to the one closest to you and try it out. If the dryers don't get hot enough or there's some other problem, you can try a different one next time. It's easiest to do this with a load at a time and save big trips until you have a preference.

When we lived over there, my wife and I used to go to Rhodes Laundry (I think that was the name).  It's on Broadway between Crescent and 29th Streets right near the Blockbuster.  It's usually kept pretty clean and has good tables in the back for folding.
