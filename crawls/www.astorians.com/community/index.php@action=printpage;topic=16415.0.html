Title: Nutrition Services: for restauranteurs and individuals
Post by: LauraK on October 20, 2009, 07:29:22 PM
Registered Dietitians (RD) help people learn about staying healthy and selecting the right foods.  We are your most credible and reliable source of accurate nutrition information.  Balance, variety and moderation are the keys to healthful eating!  RDs can help you find a total eating plan that works for you and your lifestyle.

As an RD working in Astoria I offer counseling to all members of the community: individuals and food service businesses alike.  So whether you are looking for ways to offer your customers healthier fare, or just want to shop smarter (save money!) and prepare healthier meals for yourself and your roommates or family, I can help.  Other nutrition therapy counseling topics include but are by no means limited to:

Weight Control
Diabetes
Kidney Disease
Heart Disease
High Cholesterol
Hypertension
Celiac Disease/Gluten intolerance
Vegetarian living
Food Allergies
Food Safety
Sports Nutrition
Food Labels

Remember, in New York State just about anyone can call themselves a “Nutritionist.”  For nutrition care and guidance it is important to always ask for an RD.  The credential indicates that the practitioner has completed an accredited 4-year educational program, a minimum of 900 hours of supervised practice experience, and a national licensing examination.
