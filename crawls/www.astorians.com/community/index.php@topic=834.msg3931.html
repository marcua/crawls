Whoever did their web site doesn't know bread, but they do. Their best bread, I think is their six-grain bread, available with or without sesame seeds on top. I've also tried their Greek village bread, which is squishy for my tastes. I'm not sure that I've tried any of their other breads.

They also have really good jams (try the sour cherry!) and some good cookies...
