Title: Used Bike Sale Starts Monday May 4th
Post by: SpokesmanCycles on April 24, 2009, 12:57:26 PM
With warm weather, a transit fare hike, and new Queens bike lanes all around the corner, it's the perfect time to buy a bicycle!

Our annual sale of "slightly used bikes" starts Monday May 4th! All of these almost new bikes have been assembled and maintained by our mechanics and some have only been ridden once. We're selling some of them for less than half of what they'd sell for brand-new.

Quantities are limited and the selection varies by size. Among the models available are: Jamis Ranger SX for  $199; Fuji Montery 3.0 for $250 and Fuji Crosstown 1.0 for $300

Spokesman Cycles
49-04 Vernon Boulevard
Long Island City, NY
(#7 train to Vernon/Jackson or G train to 21st Street)
718-433-0450
