Title: Re: window gates -- worthwhile?
Post by: KidMurdock on June 03, 2011, 12:44:18 PM
A far less expensive option would be a wooden dowel cut and insert between the top of the bottom window pane and the top of the frame, to prevent someone from sliding the window up from the outside and renter's insurance. I've been in Astoria for almost 10 years, and haven't heard of many burgalries, but with dowel that I mentioned, the burglar would have to smash through the window, which if they're dedicated enough to do that, the gates aren't going to stop them.
If they're really that dedicated, your renter's insurance (mine comes out to be under $20 a month) will cover any losses, something a gate won't do.
That said, if the gate makes you feel safer, go for it, but personally, I don't see them as necessary.
