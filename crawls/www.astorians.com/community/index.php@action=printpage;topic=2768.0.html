Title: Astoria Annual September Concert needs musicians, venues, volunteers
Post by: AllisonTartalia on July 27, 2006, 06:41:42 PM
Hi!

As some of you may know, for the past 5 years, I've been the Astoria organizer for an event called September Concert.  September Concert happens every September 11 and the goal is to bring people together on September 11 through the power of music.  For more information on September Concert please visit the website at http://www.septemberconcert.org.  On the website, you'll also be able to view documentary clips and photos from concerts in past years.  This started as a city-wide event, but has become international in scope, with cities participating as far away as Japan.

In years past, I've placed volunteer musicians at about 10 small businesses in the neighborhood, mostly along Ditmars and Steinway.  Once again, I'm looking for volunteer musicians, and would also like to extend an invitation to any local business owners on this list who'd like to get involved. 

Although I'll continue to do some placement of musicians at venues, this year, we'd really like to encourage people to arrange their own events as well.  The only requirement is that all events must be free and open to the public.  This is an all-volunteer effort, so no one is getting paid.  It's really about the spirit of community, and since this is such a terrific neighborhood, I'd made it my mission to make sure Astoria is well-represented each year. 

Venues do not have to be traditional music venues.  Past venues have included cafes, restaurants, a take-out noodle shop, bagel shops, and a haircutting place, where the owner each year plays outside between hair appointments!  It's really open for all kinds of involvement, whether you play classical, jazz, bluegrass, pop or are a singer-songwriter; whether you make music for a living, or as a hobby.  Since many of these untraditional "venues" don't have a PA system, anything unplugged is especially welcome.

This year, September falls on a Monday.  We will schedule music events anywhere from noon to midnight.

If you'd like to be involved in any way, please send me an email at SeptemberAstoria@allisontartalia.com.

Thanks so much for reading.  I look forward to hearing from you!

Best,
Allison Tartalia
Astoria Organizer
