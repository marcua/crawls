Colombian arepas are typically simply served with a little butter and white cheese. They are usually eaten for breakfast but are also an accompaniment for a plate of rice, beans, chicken, etc...

The Venezuelan arepas are kind of a twist on a sandwich...they are stuffed with you name it....shredded beef, sweet plantains, roast pork, black beans.....I'm getting hungry!!!!!

IMHO I much prefer the Venezuelan kind over the Colombian's....if my Colombian wife could hear me now
