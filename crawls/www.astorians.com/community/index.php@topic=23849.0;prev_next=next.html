It is just that.  And I actually think that's pretty cool.  I think it gives them some staying power in the winter.  I had the peanut butter frozen yogurt.  It was delicious.  The place is really nice.  It's huge and bright and cheery.  I will be back for sure.  And it was mobbed with a constant flow of traffic yesterday.  It's more like 16 Handles than Red Mango but it doesn't have the loud music and the teen vibe of 16 Handles.   Which I think is probably a good thing for this place.  And they have a great variety of toppings from natural and healthy to cereal and candy.  I really enjoyed it.
