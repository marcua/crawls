After giving New China House a few months to step into Mei Jia's shoes, I have to admit that I've been pretty much underwhelmed by both of their locations on 30th Ave. and now Broadway.
It's not so much that they are a typical neighborhood Chinese takeout, but they just don't seem very generous with their serving size.
Lately, I've taken the advice of several earlier posters and have been patronizing King's Chef on 28th Ave.
Huge portions, pretty tasty, very fresh and a pretty fair price.
For $12 (food plus a good tip), I get four meals
No complaints so far.

Delivery is lightening fast, but then I'm only a block away.

For the "best" Chinese in Astoria, you probably have to go to Nuevo Jardin de China on Broadway.  It is pretty expensive, as it's more of a sit down restaurant than a take-out spot.


For the next best thing in Chinese take out, I recommend Golden Dragon on 31st Avenue (next to Il Bambino) or Fatima's on Broadway (in between Bakeway and The Strand).  Fatima's is Halal, so there's no pork on the menu (their beef wontons are yummy; and they have mock duck/seitan options).
