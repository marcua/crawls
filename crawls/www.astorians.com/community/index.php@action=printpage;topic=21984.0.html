Title: Photos with Santa, Saturday, December 3 @ Trinity LIC/Astoria
Post by: Laughing Lion on November 27, 2011, 11:30:38 PM
Saturday, December 3, Santa Claus is coming to town! At Trinity Lutheran Church, Long Island City, 31-38 37th Street (corner of 31st Avenue and 37th Street, Astoria), from 12 noon to 4pm, Santa will be visiting Trinity’s Christmas Craft Fair! On Saturday December 3 stop by to take a picture with Santa, and also pick up wonderful personal homemade gifts for the special people in your life.  Enjoy crafts, baked goods, and a raffle sponsored by local business as well! The Craft Fair opens at 10am, Santa stops by hourly, starting at 12noon. Call: 718-278-0036 for more info or email office@trinitylic.org

http://www.trinitylic.org/events.php

Directions:

·       R or V Train
Take the R or V train to the Steinway street stop, walk one block North to Broadway, turn left, and turn right onto 37th Street. Trinity will be at the end of that long block, near 31st Ave.

·       N or W Train
Go to the Broadway stop and walk East on Broadway to 37th St. Turn left onto 37th St. Trinity will be at the end of that long block, near 31st Ave.
