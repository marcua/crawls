I can't swear to it - but I think the reason there isn't differentiated information is because all schools are supposed to offer the same services, based on what is outlined in a child's IEP. 
Some may have ICT and some self-contained, but in general they are supposed to be the same across schools.

You have to call each school individually and inquire.  Most of them will say they are not touring or doing open houses until Jan/Feb.   The local school is supposed to fulfill the IEP, but most schools don't have special programs - they just have an ICT class or 12:1:1 and related services according to what is on the IEP.
