The Blimpies (and Twin Donuts) on Crescent and Broadway are long closed.  I believe there was a Carvel there at one point as well.  The store closed and opened and then finally closed again for good.  It sat vacant for a few months and then the neighboring Trade Fair took it over and added the space to their supermarket.

I believe there was also a Blimpies on Steinway, but that closed a long time ago.
