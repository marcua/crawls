I will motion to you at specific times. When I motion,
you give her a good poke in the leg with the pin."

In church the following Sunday, Mrs. Jones dozed off.
Noticing this, the preacher put his plan to work.
". . . And who made the ultimate sacrifice for you?" he
said, nodding to Mr. Jones.

"Jesus!" cried Mrs. Jones as her husband jabbed her in
the leg with the pin.
