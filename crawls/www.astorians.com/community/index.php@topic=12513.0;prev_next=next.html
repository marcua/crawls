I've never heard of dog litter.  The cedar chips are also toxic to rabbits and are meant for areas under the cage, when there is a wire floor between the chips and the rabbit.  Plus they get wet and gross.  They sell this stuff called "yesterday's news", which works really well (same idea as the woodstove pellets--pellets that soak up smell and are easy to clean up), but it's very expensive--$10+ for a bag that will barely last a week.  If I find woodstove pellets, the price is about $5 and it will last months.
