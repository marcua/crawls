what a fantastic collection of furry- and not-so-furry companions!

i bring my dog to All Pets on Ditmars @35th St.  The doc is no-nonsense - she is affectionate and kind to the animals and matter-of-fact to the humans.  my dog adores the place: if we walk by, he'll tug the leash and sit in front of the office, whining, until i bring him in (they give him treats).  he's a strong dog - makes it difficult to yank him away when they're closed; he'll stick his nose in the gate and whimper, whimper, whimper.

several months ago my dog was hit by a car - Dr. H and her staff took such good care of him and also took good care of me - i was with him when he got hit and it terrified me.  i'm sold.

incidentally, i've heard good things about the vet group on Steinway at Astoria Blvd - and one of their staff does dogsitting, too.
