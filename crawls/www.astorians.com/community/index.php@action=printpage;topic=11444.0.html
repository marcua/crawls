SHIVS OR SKIDS?
Bronx Gridlock cabbies battle the inmates on the Manhattan Mayhem on Saturday, 7/26 at Hunter College Sportsplex at Lexington Avenue and 68th Street, Manhattan.

The Bronx Gridlock started the season strong with a 126-53 victory over the Brooklyn Bombshells. The ladies in checkers will be looking to keep the pedal to the metal to make roadkill of the rest of the league.

The Manhattan Mayhem will try to shake off their season opening 152-71 loss to the Queens of Pain on May 10th, and they have a plan to put a wrench in the Gridlock's plans for a second undefeated season. Mayhem, which boasts eight rookies on its roster of 13, brings an infectious level of enthusiasm to the track. Gotham fans should not forget that the reigning champion Gridlock girls debuted in 2006 as a team composed almost entirely of rookies - and after a rough start, the team quickly gelled to string together an undefeated 2007 campaign.

Get in on the action early and take advantage of the advance ticket discount online at www.gothamgirlsrollerderby.com or through www.brownpapertickets.com. Don't miss the best season of flat-track roller derby action in New York City yet!
