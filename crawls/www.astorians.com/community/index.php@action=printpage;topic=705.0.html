hmmm -- good question.  most of what I've seen has been in private gardens.  some cool ones from the train -- balconies, rooftops, that are inaccessible from the street but still pretty to see looking down are my favorite.

I'm also not sure where to find great and cheap flowers to buy (thinking fresh bunches).

the Ravenswood CSA has a flower share available in addition to the veggies.  something like $60 for the season (pro-rated for late joiners) and since I love fresh flowers, I signed on.  each week I pick up my veggies and my bouquet.  so far I've had 3 funny/cool bunches (red elephant ears? and something that looked like strawberries on sticks and something that was like a decorative flowery wheat stalk thing), a bunch of zinnias, and sunflowers.  while they aren't necessarily what I would have picked (I'm more traditional than I thought) I love my flower "subscription".
