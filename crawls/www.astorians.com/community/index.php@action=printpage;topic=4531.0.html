Title: Socrates Sculpture Park application guidelines
Post by: erpotown on December 29, 2006, 01:07:20 PM
Socrates Sculpture Park 2007 application guidelines are now available online
at http://www.socratessculpturepark.org (http://www.socratessculpturepark.org). 

If you know of artists who would be interested in working at the Park, and
could benefit from the grants that we offer, we hope that you will encourage
them to apply.

The applications are due on Monday, January 22, 2007.

In addition to the exhibition opportunities listed on the application
guidelines, we will also be producing a show about artists working in Long
Island City. If you know of artists whose studios are in the area, please
give us their contact information and we will make sure that they are
notified of the exhibition opportunity.

If you have any questions about our exhibition program or would like more
information about the application process, please feel free to call us at
718-956-1819 or contact us by e-mail: info@socratessculpturepark.org.
