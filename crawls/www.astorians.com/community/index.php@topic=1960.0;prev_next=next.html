Meg, check out the pass books offered by the American Health and Fitness Alliance.  There is a gym pass book and a yoga/pilates/dance pass book.  They're not free -- the gym passbook is $69 -- but the passes are good for the (calendar) year, for gyms all over NYC.  Basically, for the (rest of the) year, you can use these passes at the appropriate gym, and not pay anything else in addition.  Some passes are good for one or two trips, some a full week or I think even month.  My sister-in-law has used the yoga one before, and says it's great.
