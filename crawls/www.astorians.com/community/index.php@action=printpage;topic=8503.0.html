Title: Dodgeball and Basketball in Astoria this Winter!
Post by: ZogSports on November 01, 2007, 04:00:21 PM
When it gets too cold out this winter, come inside to meet, socialize, and engage in friendly competition with fellow Astorians - playing Dodgeball or 3-on-3 Basketball!

ZogSports, the charity-focused, co-ed, social sports league for young professionals, is back in Queens this winter to bring you two awesome new sports leagues, as well as a way to give back to the community, meet new people, reconnect with friends, and share a pint or several at post-game happy hours!

Sign up a whole team, or as an individual/small group and we'll place you on a team!  Your team will then pick a charity to play for this season, and hopefully win some money to donate to its cause.

Queens residents gave us a great response to our inaugural fall leagues (Soccer and Touch Football) and we're excited to be back for another season!

Early Registration is on now and will continue until November 9.  You can register and get lots of other info at www.zogsports.org, or email info@zogsports.org with questions.

Hope to see you out there!
