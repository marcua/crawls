Eating Astoria through the Alphabet- Suggestions Welcome!
« on: April 11, 2011, 09:51:48 PM »
My wife and I, and a group of our friends have been Astoria residents for 3-4 years now, and came up with the idea to "eat Astoria through the alphabet." Basically this means every month we go to a different restaurant, and each time the restauarant starts with the next letter of the alphabet we are up to. We want to do some classy and expensive places, some dirt cheap delicious grub, and everything in between.

We started our journey with the letter A this past Sunday and had an amazing meal at "Astor Room" by Kaufman Astoria Studios. Up next is B, and so far the favorite is Bareburger. But we vote before a final decision is made, and I thought good old Astorians.com would be a good place to post updates and have people give suggestions. Keep in mind we are looking for a place a little less expensive than Astor Room, but does anyone have a good suggestion for an Astoria restaurant that begins with the letter "B"????
