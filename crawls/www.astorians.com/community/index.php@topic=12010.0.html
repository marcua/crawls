That is very right, my friends!
 
After a long break with some serious musical/mental retooling, regimented fitness program, and gluten-free diet, Criminoso is back playing at the hands down, coolest bar in Astoria: THE Hell Gate Social, Thursday, Sept. 11th @ 9:00 PM. 
 
We've fixed up our tunes, made our drum loops better, controllable, and more dynamic, 75 sit-ups per day, washed behind our ears, and are ready to rock Astoria the way Astoria deserves to be rocked.
 
Also, you can hear some new recordings the band has been patiently working on, on our website and myspace: http://www.criminosomusic.com and http://www.myspace.com/anthonylanni.  I just posted them up, but please note that these recordings are still subject to final tweakings, but they'll give you a good idea of what we've been working on.
 
So...come check us out!  The Hell Gate is one of our favorite bars with very cool owners, so you should check it out if you haven't already. Info below:
 
Thursday, September 11th, 2 Sets: 9PM & 10PM
 CRIMINOSO at:
 Hell Gate Social
 12-21 Astoria Boulevard
 Btwn. 12th & 14th St
 Astoria, NY
 718-204-8313 
 NO COVER!

Hope to see you there!

Come check out the new, improved, upgraded Criminoso at the Hell Gate Social, Thurs. Sept. 11 @ 9PM.  Once again we'll be back playing electro-jazz at our favorite and the most coolest Astoria bar, The Hell Gate Social!!!  We just had our rehearsal yesterday and the band and new loops are sounding great!  Thursday's band will be Dan Kendall - bass, Sam Kulik - trombone, Jonas Labhart - saxophone, and myself - guitar.
 
You get the following improvements when you upgrade to Criminoso 2.0 Enterprise Edition:
 
Virus Protection
Strict band personal hygiene regiments are enforced.  Band will wash hands before and after gig.
 
Security Updates
I promise we will not steal anything.  Seriously.  But if we find it on the floor, too bad for you.
 
Performance Enhancements
Buffed, waxed, speed, power.  Each band member is required to complete intense physical training, equivalent to that of a 5th grade field day exam (rope climb is not mandatory).
 
Stability Improvements
Band members have been thoroughly mentally screened and/or heavily medicated.  On stage "freak-outs" will be kept to a min.   Tazers are at the ready in the event of an "attack".
 
Parental Control
Yes, we have full control of your parents.
 
Identity Theft Protection
Only Dolly Parton, Richard Simmons, and Macho Man Randy Savage are at risk here.
 
Details:
