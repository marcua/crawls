Freezepeach is amazing. Great quiet vibe, nice people and a huge selection of teas that are brewed upon order. Create your own or go with a conconction they've already created.  Hot chocolate, chai and fresh brewed iced teas are also available.
Indeed this is 2 subway stops from where you will be living but not terribly far.

Another place is Cafe/Bar on 34th Avenue.  It's a combo, cafe, restaurant/ bar and has a funky feel.  I enjoy the cheese platters, nescafe frappe or a Stella Artois.

Welcome to the neighborhood in advance.
