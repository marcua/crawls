Our daughter is at Learning Odyssey and I can't say enough good things about it.  Yes, you pay more, but that is because you get so much more.  They have more providers per child than any place I've seen.  All their teachers have a great deal of experience and training.  The facility is gorgeous and you will NOT find any place cleaner or more concerned with maintaining the kids' health.  They wash hands frequently.  They help the children brush their teeth after meals.  They do educational activities.  They have a great outdoor play area attached to the facility.  They have cameras, excellent security, its convenient to transportation, etc.

I've looked at other places in the neighborhood and there really is no comparison.
