I guess I am just confused as to how the government is handling their records when not everyone uses the same technology when communicating with people. Of course, if they are just not telling us about it, then my confusion may only have to last for a very long time...  :roll:

I call on you, as leaders of a co-equal branch of our government, to investigate this matter fully. In order to fully understand the breadth of this program, all witness testimony should be delivered under oath.
