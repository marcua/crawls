Because of this thread, my bf & I went to Sparrow for dinner last night. I also assumed it was more of a dive-y bar, not so much for food—and boy is that wrong!


We shared the pork tacos to start. AMAZING. Tender, flavorful and smokey.
I had the duck (came with roasted asparagus and mashed potatoes), and he had the chicken pot pie. Both were wonderful. Chicken pot pie is a dish that can have a tendency to become very bland, but Sparrow's was very nicely seasoned throughout. No need to add salt.


Blueberry creme brûlée for dessert. Perfect way to end the meal.
