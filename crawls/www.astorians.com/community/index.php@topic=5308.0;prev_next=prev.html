If you live in Community Board 2, and are interested in bicycling issues, consider attending the upcoming CB 2 Land Use meeting:

From Transportation Alternatives:

Bike Parking on CB2's Agenda
If you're free, please attend the CB2 Land Use meeting on Wednesday, February 21st at 7pm. It's located at the Community Board office, 43-22 50th St. in Woodside. Caroline, our bike coordinator, will be presenting a proposal for secure indoor bike parking at the municipal parking garage at Court House Square. Even a few voices of support for the proposal, and how it would help promote biking in the area would be a huge help. If interested in attending, give Caroline a call at 646-873-6021 or bike@transalt.org and let her know to expect you.
