When I was called for jury duty in Kew Gardens (for 2 days which felt like 8 days!) they gave us a 90 minute lunch break. If you cross Queens Blvd and walk up the hill the area turns into a nice, quiet residential neighborhood with single family homes. Continue walking for a few blocks and you end up on Austin Street! I was so thrilled to find a little Chinese restaurant to have lunch in. That was the highlight of the day! After having spent a whole morning in their factory green painted waiting room, sitting on a wooden bench and getting stir crazy that little walk in beautiful Spring weather saved the day!
