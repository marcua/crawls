Sunswick was okay. Lots of screens for such a small place! We had a drink, appetizer then we (moi, lanseux, justrachel, enigmacat, cary119, anzubird, victorine, and jonathan) moved on to Cup where we chowed down on yummy burgers and quesadillas. (I hope in my 2-Corona state I'm not leaving anyone out.)  :lol: Telesphoros, despite his advanced age, and Duluoz Cats joined us there. Later Jayme and her sis stopped by  - quite an Astorian showing. My first time at Cup and I really liked it! We had a lot of fun. Jonathan kept us laughing! Then some of us moved on to Crescent Lounge, which was too crowded, so we went to Kelly's Bar on... 26th and 31st Ave?

For those who haven't been....we gotta go back to this place. Talk about vintage... next to the pool table (.75 a game) they still have an old wooden phone booth with a working door and light! Imagine the drunken tales those walls have soaked up! LOL I'm surprised the phone wasn't still rotary!

To get to the ladies room one passes through the kitchen with this stove from what, the 1920s or 1930s, THAT THEY STILL USE. Throwback music on the somewhat modern and free (it just keeps getting better) jukebox. They got all the hits - Sinatra, Dean Martin, Barry Manilow, Neil Diamond, Patsy Cline and more. Some Karaoke stars to go right along with it! We could tell the headliner was a little ticked that we interrupted her set when we first walked in.....LMAO

A real dart board, in a wall case! But no darts - barkeep said she would throw them at the customers - however, if you have a set...... 

The woman might have been the owner as well as bartender, the license on the wall said "Mary Kelly". I'll have to ask her next time, maybe the place has been in her family for a while or something and that's why the old, original (?) equipment.

Some real characters in there.  :-) Bar area certainly cheerful and bright, looks like they decorate for all the major holidays. The current motif is July 4th, with some remnants of Christmas past hanging around. From Cary119's description, the Easter finery is not to be missed!

We played pool, had a couple of drinks, sang a little, laughed a lot and had a great time!

Thought about megc, justdesserts and mandaT and others that said they were coming. Hope none of you showed up at Sunswick after we left. We all thought about you...we should exchange numbers more so nobody feels stood up in cases where the original meeting place doesn't work out. I would be pissed if that happened to me...

Thanks for introducing us to Kelly's!  :-D
