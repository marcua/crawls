Hi everyone,


Thanks for the interest in the shop, and for taking the survey. I am hoping very soon to contact all of you kind enough to have left your email addresses on the survey.


I'm glad to hear a lot of your comments on the idea. We think a cafe should be a space that harmoniously accommodates everyone from laptop campers to the on-the-go coffee person; a few existing places are certainly making that effort. It's our sincere hope that it will be the kind of place that neighborhood people can meet, converse, and share some moments in the day over drinks-- but we also understand the appeal of a place where you can comfortably do some work in a place that's not your apartment. And of course we'll have great coffee; I'm glad someone mentioned the extent to which cafes elevate coffee...Simply because we're coffee people we want to offer the "good stuff" made in ways that we think taste the best. But have no fear, we want you to enjoy coffee the way you like it. And thanks for the tea comments as well...we plan on doing loose leaf!


I wanted to shoot a quick reply and thank you to those of you who have been generous with your time in taking the survey and contributing to the discussion. Hopefully, I'll get to meet some of you soon through a coffee salon (in the works)!


In the meantime please shoot me an email at astoriacoffeeshop@gmail.com if you'd like to get in touch.
