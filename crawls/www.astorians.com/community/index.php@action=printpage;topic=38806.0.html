Title: Habitat for Humanity ReStore Grand Opening!
Post by: amandahabitat on September 10, 2015, 05:12:51 PM
Location: 62-01 Northern Blvd, Woodside, NY 11377
Time: 10am to 5pm

Join us as we cut the ribbon on NYC's first ever ReStore! Customers will enjoy refreshments, music, raffles and great deals on our latest products. The ribbon cutting and program will take place at 10:30 AM so don't miss the fun!

]About ReStore:
This 3,500 square foot retail space is an affordable home improvement center offering a variety of building materials including appliances, furniture, hardware and other discounted merchandise - typically 50-80% percent off the original retail value. Profits from the store directly support Habitat NYC in its mission to build affordable homes in New York City.
