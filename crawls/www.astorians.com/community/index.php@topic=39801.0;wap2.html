Live Music & Free Beer Samples

(1/1)

oliversastoria:
Allagash Brewing is coming to Oliver's, pouring samples of new rare beers. Live music and tastings start at 8pm.

 - Émile – sour ale fermented in French aperitif barrels with notes of black currants and elderberries, dark chocolate and dates

 - Avancé – strong sour aged in oak bourbon barrels for a year, then aged with a pound of fresh strawberries for another 6 months.

 - Hugh Malone - Citra & Denali dry hopped Belgian IPA on tap

Come get down in Astoria with one of the best breweries in the northeast!
