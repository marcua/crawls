Woolworth Sign in view on 31 and Ditmars
« on: September 02, 2010, 09:31:24 AM »
So CVS is tearing up that building on the corner to move the CVS. Well last night I was in TD Bank talking to customer service rep wheN I saw it. The old Woolworth sign. I got so excited. Everyone should go check it out before CVS covers it up. Such a little treat and peek into the history of our hood. As I stood outside taking the pictures countless people were telling their old woolworth stories of how their grandmothers and mothers took them there. Apparently there was a soda fountain inside.

You can see the old pressed tin ceilings inside. A shame it will probably all get replaced with some dull fugly cvs monstrosity.

Anyway, here it is! Enjoy!
