« Reply #7 on: February 05, 2011, 03:32:11 PM »
The stairways on the triboro ped/bike way have metal chute-thingies laid over them at the side (maybe 6" wide) to allow bicyclists to walk their bikes up & down the stairs. I believe (could be wrong!) that cycling is legal on the foot bridge, but you're supposed to walk your bike on the part between the stairways (which is fairly narrow and has tight spots where you go around the stanchions). However I have never seen anyone do this (walk their bike through that part), everyone rides.

Regarding the Hell Gate path - from the pretty limited amount of info in the news article, it sounds to me like the planned pathway is only on the Randall's Island side of the bridge. I think  (again, I'm not really an expert on this, so could be wrong) that the city got a bunch of $$ from a consortium of private schools in exchange for the schools having the use of playing fields on the island, and that's where the renovations over there in recent years have come from. There's a newish bike/ped path that goes over around by the stadium & connects with the 103rd street bridge to Manhattan - maybe this one will link with that somehow?
