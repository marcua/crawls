Powhatan/Pocahontas Democratic Annual Dinner Dance May 14
« on: May 01, 2010, 02:11:14 PM »
The Powhatan - Pocahontas Democratic Club will hold their 2010 Annual Dinner Dance at Ricardo’s by the Bridge, located at 21-01 24th Avenue, in Astoria, New York.

Ricardo's by the bridge
21-01 24th Avenue
Friday May 14th
7pm

This year’s honorees are our good friends New York City Councilmember Jimmy Van Bramer who represents the 26th Council District, Judy Trilivas, the Chief Operating Officer for the Mt. Sinai Hospital of Queens and Marie Torniali, Executive Director Central Astoria Local Development Coalition and Executive Director Steinway Street BID. We are delighted to recognize these three outstanding leaders.

The Cocktail Hour begins at 7 p.m. and is followed by Dinner and Dancing at 8 p.m. Tickets are $65 per person and can be purchased by calling Ann Favicchio at 718-274-7775. Please make all checks payable to the Pocahontas Democratic Club and mail them to Ann at 31-35 46th Street, Long Island City, New York 11103.

In conjunction with the Dance, the Club will once again publish a Souvenir Journal. We encourage you to submit your greeting or advertisement for inclusion. The funds raised by the Journal enable us to maintain our clubhouse and to continue our work in the community. Enclosed please find a Journal Submission Sheet and a return envelope. The Journal Deadline is May 1, 2010.

Gold Page $250
Full Page $100
Half Page $60
Quarter Page $40

On behalf of the Officers and Members of the Powhatan - Pocahontas Club, thank you for your past and continued support.
