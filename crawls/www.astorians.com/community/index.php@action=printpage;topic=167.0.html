Title: ACK: Astoria Crafty/Knitty Group
Post by: cabanird on August 05, 2005, 08:02:29 PM
the group is meeting!

not many of us are there, but i am there with a crochet hook.

last nite, august 4, myself and allison were there with our crochet hooks.

for those who would like to join in the crafty goodness, be at the freeze peach sometime on thursday evenings.  the original time for the group was 8pm. but i since have been getting there later from about 830pm-9pm.

for those who already enjoy a craft, bring it along and we can all sit together. it's really informal. but it's great to sit with other crafters. it's almost like a support group.... can ask other members-- what color would look good with this? is this too much? should i take something away? how many more stiches should i do? things like that.

for those who do not enjoy a craft yet and would like to learn... i'm a very bad teacher at crocheting---i'm left handed so it throws right handers off. i do not knit very well. i can teach you the basic knit and purl stitches. but that's all i know. increasing, decreasing, and other fancy things i cannot do.  our ACK knitter hasnt been to this group for a little bit. :(

if you are on a budget-- the bargain stop at 30th ave sometimes has yarns at a very good discount price (limited selection and colors). there's a few fabric/craft stores on ditmars blvd that have yarns too. dont forget to hit Micheal's at the northern boulevard stop on the R train. :)

for those who enjoy other crafts such as jewlry making and paper crafts, bring that along. i'm sure we are all curious to see your work and admire it.

that's another cool thing about the crafty group-- we can show off our things. it's very cathartic. :)

contact me if you have anymore questions!

Rosemary

rock on!

please join us tomorrow.

if you plan on crocheting or knitting start off by buying a skein (ball of yarn) that is not all bumpy and funky looking, use worsted weight yarn. it looks like a normal ball of yarn. :) red heart super savers are great for beginners. and be sure to get yourself a color that is not too dark. it will be easier to see you stitches in a lighter color. (grays, pastels)

if you are crocheting, be sure to purchase a J (6.5mm) hook.

if you are knitting, get size 10 needles.

get yerself a great cup of tea, and join us! :)
