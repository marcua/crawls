Dance Entropy:
Valerie Green/Dance Entropy - New York City Summer Dance Intensive
 July 10-16, 2017
 
 Immerse Yourself in Movement
 
 All classes are taught by Valerie Green and Dance Entropy company members - Featuring modern dance technique, choreography, peak performance, ballet - plus a performance lab!

This intensive is open to all dancers, choreographers, actors, directors, and movement lovers of every kind!

The Intensive is Monday - Friday 10am to 4pm, Saturday 10am to 2pm and Sunday 10am to 3pm.  Each day the students take a variety of classes and a performance lab. This culminates in a Final Showing and Reception for all participants and their families on Sunday July 16th.

For a complete schedule and registration information, please visit: http://www.danceentropy.org/NYCSummerDanceIntensive.html.

We are looking forward to dancing with you this summer!
