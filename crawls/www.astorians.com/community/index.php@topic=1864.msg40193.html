I took my family to Michael's for brunch on Sunday. There were 6 of us and our bill came to $68, not including tip. The brunch special is $9.95 per person and included two drinks (we had mimosas), coffee or tea, and an entree. There were a ton of omelets to choose from, burgers, salads, wraps, etc. It is definitely worth the price! The special runs until 3pm and even though we got there at 2:50pm, they honored the brunch prices.
