Title: Marine Terrace apartment complex up for sale
Post by: 28Grand on June 13, 2016, 09:54:20 AM
Another example of big developers moving into the Queens/Astoria property market: The Related Companies are in talks to purchase the Marine Terrace Apartment complex between on Shore Boulevard and 21st Street.

As part of the plan, 100 garages will be demolished and replaced with two rental buildings that will include housing for the homeless and veterans.

Funny, I thought the developer behind the Time Warner Center, Hudson Yards and 70 Vestry Street buying what is probably one of the last affordable, mixed income rental complexes in the neighborhood would raise more eyebrows than the provision to add affordable housing as a proposition to sweeten the deal with the city and state.
