Title: Turkish honey cakes
Post by: Christine on December 02, 2006, 05:26:16 PM
I used to think that baklava was the love of my life. To the point where I had to severely restrict consumption.  :| Tried to arrange it so that I couldn't eat baklava unless it was from the Neptune Diner. This works fairly well.

The other night, I was downtown, and a friend took us to her local haunt right around the corner from Katz's. It's a little Turkish take-out place, highly acclaimed it seems for its cheap, fresh, authentic fare. My kinda place!

Anyway, decided to try something different, so I bought one pistachio/honey cake. Got an extra for free. And now I see why they gave me the extra one: It's addictive  :-)

Yeah so now I just ate the last morsel (they're dense so I've been taking pieces of it) and am jonesin' pretty bad. Where in Astoria can I obtain a fix? Probably that Art Cafe but it's so expensive in there.....and a minefield of other temptations.

Maybe it's best I *don't* know  :-P
