Title: Re: What to do in Freeport, Grand Bahama Island.
Post by: NYCMacUser on August 15, 2007, 09:23:45 PM
Swim with the dolphins. It's an experience you'll never forget. It used to cost around $150 for the trip, but I'm sure the price went up since it is such a popular thing to do. Ask your hotel concierge for details. BTW, where are you staying? I have fond memories of a restaurant called either Stoned Crab or The Crab Stoned. Most places don't take credit cards, so keep a lot of cash in the safe. Be sure to load up at the duty-free shops, take a breathtaking stroll through the botanical gardens (The Garden of Groves) and if you love golf, you'll be in heaven. The Fortune Hills golf course is stunning. You are overlooking the ocean on some holes. Just magnificent. If you are going alone they will pair you up with someone. If you are a water-sport enthusiast you're not going to contain your enthusiasm for all the activities available; windsurfing, para-sailing, jet skiing . . . a virtual water paradise. Also, if you still have some time, visit the Perfume Factory. It is a wonderfully unique experience. Have a ball, and report back here when you return.
