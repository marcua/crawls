I had that happen to my here in Bayside. I had gone on vacation for 10 days and when I came back, I noticed they were paving the street. There were signs posted but of course how I could I know that right? My husband and I were walking around all the blocks near us looking for the car and we finally found it about 4 blocks away. It was a Saturday morning and while we were looking I called 311. They were about to give me phone number to call but then I found my car.

Call 311 and just walk the blocks and avenues near your house. They shouldn't have towed it too far. Good luck.
