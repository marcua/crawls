it was such a beautiful day in astoria park.

this was a pretty cool event considering it was the very first one, and there was absolutely no budget at all.
it can only get better each year.

and all the bands that played for free, and brought lots of peeps, thank you!

although the PA was total crap, we made the best of the situation, and all the bands were really cool about it.

pie boys flat is such a great band, but the technical difficulties kind of hampered their set, and I felt for them.

i think they should be one of the headliners next year, since they also brought a ton of people just to see them.

i really liked the tarantinos too.

and the capoiera group that showed up and did a showcase was really cool also. they had just heard about the festival 2 days before, and showed up in full force.

and another shout out to all the very well-behaved kids playing with all of chalkdusts hoops, and their great parents!

the seed was planted on saturday.
