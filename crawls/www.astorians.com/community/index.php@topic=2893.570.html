I also live in Riverview East and feel like only bad things are ever said about this building, which is a shame and a bit misleading, IMO.  I think the complaints of a smattering of people in a large building, while definitely justified, are being blown out of proportion.

Hi,

I just came across this thread. I've been in the river view east since 2008 when they started rentals, been renting for about roughly 2 years. I love the area and I love the people that reside in the building along with great access to famous Astoria park! I must say I have a pet, so it's with ease of my morning to take a walk right around the way. I can agree with you that waking up every time there is a major rainfall does suck, but Pistilli in my residence in the building has never left me stranded. There is always two sides of a story and as we know the good and bad of something, so I thought I would also share my story.

I was lucky to be one of those apartments that didn't have a water leak continuous, but since they fixed the side where I reside 23rd terrace, I have been leak free..... so far. I am happy along with that and not major rent hikes for the space, quality, and terrace... I am happy. I can truly say the management does lack in certain aspects, but nothing that is getting blown out of proportion. I have never been left with a problem longer then a manageable time, of course we are all human beings and surely we all can get frustrated.

The building overall is great, maintained truly well by our porters, of course Robert our super works very hard, can you say you have a super that will come to your apartment at 3AM to help with a leak?? On the other hand Belkis the office manager is very hard to get in touch with and I do agree with you that if you place a call for her, you will never get a call back or you will be pushed to one of her many office assistants. The dollar for the square footage is definitely holding me in, because with all the furniture I was able to get back when I first got the place not sure if I can find a place for the money I pay now and that will allow me to have a pet.

I understand your point fully and do read your postings and I do hope you get the help you deserve, I wouldn't want to be in a problem like that either...... The building is definitely not a slum, I would encourage anyone that would like to view a place in here to contact me to view my place. I hope you have a good night. Thanks for reading.
