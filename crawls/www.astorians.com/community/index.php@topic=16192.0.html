Maybe Studio Square Beer Garden?  They also have event spaces you can rent out.  They may let you bring food in to the outdoor space.  Not sure but it's worth a shot.

DaGianni on Vernon Blve in LIC has an outdoor garden.  They have a party room upstairs from the main dining room but not sure if they rent the outdoor space as well.

Gantry Plaza State Park is a great outdoor space on the LIC waterfront.  Not sure if alcohol is allowed.

Water Taxi Beach in LIC also comes to mind. 

Not sure if any of that fits your needs.  Good luck.
