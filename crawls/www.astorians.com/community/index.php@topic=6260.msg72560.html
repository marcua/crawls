I hope you guys will go back and read my posts about what we are looking to do, "Serve God through loving people."

I am sorry for their actions, I am sorry for those of you who have been hurt and are hurting now and that you harbour ill feelings towards my family because of association. I'm a 27 year old recovering newsroom junkie (reporter and editor) turned church planter and my wife is a 26 year old 8th grade algebra teacher, who wants to be a professional chef, and we happen to be Christ followers that want to see others connect with the same teacher, Jesus.

I think what I am saying and the message that we are living out is substantially different than what most have heard from Christians over the last 50-60 years, and I would argue that Jesus made it aware to the world his thoughts of religious people being condesending on humanity. The great thing is that we are all one in voice regarding the degradation of various people by the church. I am not the value giver, I only know the value that was placed in all of humanity when Jesus came and walked among us showing the world that trust, love and respect are to be freely given. Moreover, that he loves us more than we can ever imagine. I'm sorry if that makes a lot if his wannabe followers jerks because they have to cast down verdicts on others to make themselves feel more worthy of his grace. This is not who I want to be, nor who I am right now.

We hope the residents of Astoria will embrace us as much as we will live to love and serve them in hopes that they too will desire to become students of Jesus as well.
