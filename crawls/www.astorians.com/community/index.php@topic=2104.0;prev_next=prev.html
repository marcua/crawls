Indian shops in Jackson Heights
« on: May 19, 2007, 02:37:20 PM »
I had a great afternoon today.  I needed some fabric and the best places to get nice cotton or chiffon are in Jackson Heights, in the Indian neighboorhood.  I walked into all the shops, looked around, admired all the glitter and gold, went to a local supermarket where they sell items I have never seen.  I then had a delicious lunch for $9.99 - the buffet at the Jackson Diner.  It is outstanding and a great value, especially if you have it with a Kingfisher Beer.  I recommend this outing.  It is fun, interesting and delicious!
