Title: Re: chinese food in Astoria
Post by: FasterThanAShark on January 21, 2007, 01:21:08 AM
i'm a fan of Golden Dragon on 31st Ave; it's definitely a takeout-style place, so nothing super-fancy --- but they have a pretty surprising number of tofu dishes, and they'll even substitute tofu into any of their meat dishes to satisfy vegans and vegetarians. they're pretty accomodating -- one night i was taking the N train home and had an insane hankering for tofu with peanut sauce.  i called them and, despite the fact that the dish is not on their menu - they happily cooked up some tofu w. peanut sauce and broccoli for me.

prices are totally reasonable, and i always find the food tasty and filling.  this is definitely a *take-out* place, so i'm judging by those standards -- but as far as takeout/delivery goes, i have yet to be disappointed. they also just added Chinese Buns to their menu - which are the equivalent of hot donut holes/munchkins. insanely good.
