Title: Re: Help me find a cool bar!
Post by: Handyman on November 19, 2006, 06:45:48 PM
I would agree with GFBoy...  the rover is a good place.  Be fore-warned tho, when they get to know you a little, the buy-back ratio is 2:1.   Very dangerous if you hate leaving with coasters still on the bar.     McCanns on Ditmars and 36th-ish is good, tho I've heard it described on here as having a frat atmosphere.     I don't agree.   The food there is really good and the staff is top notch.   And you can take your buy-backs home with you to spend there another night.  (not the 2:1 the Rover has, but still a nice option)

Sunswick on 35th and 35th has a nice selection too.   24 taps.

I still think the Pit Stop on McGuinness still has the best juke box in Queens tho and the Turkey's Nest has the most lopsided floor in Queens.  It's rivaled only by the Subway Inn in Manh.

Happy Drinking.

(nothing wrong with meeting us cool old farts either)
