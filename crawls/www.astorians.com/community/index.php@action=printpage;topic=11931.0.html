There's already a Queens division of Transportation Alternatives; I think an Astorian bike group would be terrific. I'd be happy to get some beginners out there. The biggest problem I find is that people just aren't motivated to practice on their own and then have no confidence to join fun group rides, even the easy ones.

I'm already involved with a Long Island-based group of mountain bikers. This was just an unexpected day of biking when and the BF were going to do just a couple of miles, but kept on going!

Next Saturday, Park Avenue is closed to car traffic from 7am to 1pm, between 72nd Street and the Brooklyn Bridge. Not a bad way to get acclimated. That's all we were going to do originally.
