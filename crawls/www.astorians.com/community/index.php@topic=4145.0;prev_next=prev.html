Not much, unfortunately.  I wish there was more.  Here is what I have found in the humane/free range/organic category:

Fresh Start - health food store on 23rd Ave. between 31st and 29th has unfrozen organic chicken (Eberly?), and a few frozen meat products, I think.

Butcher on 31st under the train (near Zygos Taverna) on 31st between Ditmars and 23rd Ave. has Bell & Evans (or Murray's?  Only been there a couple of times).

Key Food on 31st between Ditmars and 23rd has Murray's Chicken (whole and some parts), Smart Chicken certified organic (whole and some parts), and Creekstone Farms Black Angus Beef.  They also carry some Applegate Farms deli meats, sausage and bacon.

Butcher on Ditmars between 33rd and 35th has whole Bell & Evans chickens (but their parts are Perdue).

Trade Fair on Ditmars betweeen 38th and 38th has Murray's Chicken.

Berry Fresh Farms in the little shopping center on Ditmars and ~43rd St. has one or two things, I think (probably Murray's), but I've only been in their meat department once, so I don't really remember.  In any event, it's not a big enough selection to interest me.
