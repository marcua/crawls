Title: Astoria Symphonic Choir Auditions
Post by: adamegg on August 23, 2012, 10:58:59 AM
JOIN THE CHOIR!

AUDITIONS: August 27, 28, & September 4, 5

Sing with the ASC and the Astoria Symphony this season...

Works by Brahms, Beethoven, Britten, Bernstein, Lauridsen, Persichetti, and more!

See the full season and learn more here: www.astoriachoir.org (http://www.astoriachoir.org/)

Singers with all levels of choral experience are welcome to audition for our 2012-13 season. Auditions will be held on the evenings of August 27-28, & September 4-5. For an appointment, please send an email to: info@astoriachoir.org

Don't miss out on an exciting season![/size][/font]
