YUM!  I've been pretty excited about the Twirls Frozen Yogurt since they put up signage on Astoria Blvd and (I believe) 27th St.  Me & the gf were passing by on Saturday and they were finally open and giving away samples: O.M.G.  They have a fat free peanut butter frozen yogurt that is AMAZING!  It looks like a mother-daughter shop situation, but I could be wrong.  They were totally nice and personable.  There was seating inside and outside.  The other flavors they had were chocolate, strawberry, and vanilla.... but seriously, the PB was off the hook. AMAZING.
