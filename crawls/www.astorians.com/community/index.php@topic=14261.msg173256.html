Everything is always negotiable.  I told me landlord I might be leaving at the end of my lease in August because I wanted a larger place and now seemed liked a good time to find one.  He called me back and offered me a 2 year extension with no increase.  I pay below market rent in Upper Ditmars right now at $1,150 month for a nice sized one bedroom with a storage room in the basement so I negotiated an even better deal.  I got a one year lease with an option to renew for an additional year with no increase in rent for the additional year. This gives me flexibility if I want to leave after one more year.  I also got him to refund my security since I have paid my rent on time (usually a week early for the past  3 years).  He told me that tenants who pay on time are a blessing.  He owns a 6 family home near 23rd avenue and two condos in upper Ditmars and he said many people are late with the rent every month.  He said the cost of housing court and trying to collect on a judgement aren't worth the hassle so he always negotiates with his good tenants.
