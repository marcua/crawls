Über is a German preposition that basically means over, although it has a number of meanings when you translate it into English.  It has come to mean super- in a lot of English contexts -- übermensch, for example, means superman.  Ultra. Uberhipster would be an ultrahipster, for example, the kind that would fall over dead if they ventured into Astoria.
