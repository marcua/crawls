Info on Kayaks

Okay so we went yesterday to 56th street on the Hudson (pier 96 I think)
There were Kayaks and they were free to use - but
The area you are allowed to paddle in is a relatively small unattractive square of water between two piers.  If you have never paddled and want to give it a try - or if you are looking for an activity to do with kids... I think this would be fun - we opted out. 
Also - you are allowed to paddle for 20 min and then you are supposed to come back in.  I didn't ask if you can stay out longer if there is no line.
There are however day long paddle trips that go out of this spot (and maybe the other boathouse locations as well).  I'm not sure if there are details on the website - but I think they go out on sat and Sun - you have to be there by 8am and if there are more people than boats (like yesterday) they hold a lottery to see who gets to go.  I would hate to schlep all the way over there and then not get to go b/c of the lottery. 

It may be time to start looking into un-free places to rent.
