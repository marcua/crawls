Was going through the same thing a couple of months ago.  Called the Salvation Army, and they were only available to pick up days after I was scheduled to move out of my apartment, but I called only two weeks prior to move, so maybe if you do it way in advance it will work out. 

Just a warning about one company.  I searched for other companies to do the same.  One that I contacted was called the Institute of Jewish Humanities (www.donate-furniture.com).  They set up a time and date to pick up the furniture.  They were super unorganized, had to call back several times for other information, were VERY rude (should have realized then they weren't reputable), and during one of the calls yelled at me for not understanding his broken English (I just asked him to repeat what he said). 

I called the day before they were scheduled to come to confirm the pick up.  And they did not have any records of my calling.  Insisted I was 'making stuff up.'  I ended up hanging up on them, had to move all the furniture out of my place.
