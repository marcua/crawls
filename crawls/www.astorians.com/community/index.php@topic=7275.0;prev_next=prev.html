That depends on which areas you are speaking of. We have alternate side of the street parking here; that means you cannot park on one side of a street between let's say 8:30am-10:00am so that the street can be cleaned. Then we have the No Parking zones, No Standing zones, No Standing except trucks loading and unloading etc etc.. Also, where are you from? For example, in certain European cities you have such a system like Red zones which mean no parking, Yellow zones which mean parking only between certain etc etc.
