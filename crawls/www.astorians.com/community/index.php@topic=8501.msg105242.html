I've been buying it at C-Town all year. If not longer. When did the Bravo open? I remember hoping that since they're related to C-Town they'd have it and save me the much longer trip (this was when I lived 2 short blocks from the Bravo). The funny thing was that I checked just about every market near me and never saw it except at C-Town. Plenty of Fage yogurt but no tzatziki.

I wouldn't be surprised if it had been there and it never registered with anyone because it wasn't something you were looking for. Then you see the thread and remember you saw it; activated that brain cell so to speak.

Oh and that other brand I got that's still sitting in my office fridge (tzatziki sauce never lasts that long with me-one of Sunday's containers is almost gone) I still can't get the name to stick, but I took another look at the nutritional label and the calorie content is actually 200+ calories for 2 tablespoons as opposed to 30 calories for the same serving of Fage, and it tastes waaaay better (and I don't feel bad about having eaten most of a container in a few days).

Thanks to this board I will always have places in Astoria to get my fix.
And for those of you who haven't tried it; in case you can't tell I think it's super yummy and you should try it!
