I swear this helps:  you can buy steel wool and shred it and stuff it into every opening around every pipe under your kitchen and bathroom sink.  I also stuffed it into every door (closet etc.) where the knobby part goes into the door frame.  That opening is part of a hollow place and I decided to stuff the steel wool there, too.  And last place: open your radiator covers, if you can, and stuff steel wool around those pipes.   Every pipe coming out of the wall has an opening around it.  I filled them all. Bugs come thru openings in the wall around pipes.  Mice do too.

Screens do help but only if you seal them at the top.  Unless you pull your window down to meet them, there's an open space between the screen and window.  Since I did the above I never see a roach or water bug any more.  It's been years.
