Title: Re: 33rd st and 34th avenue
Post by: geowrld on December 11, 2012, 12:41:36 PM
the 114th encompasses a large area.  Some parts of Astoria are nicer (and safer) than others, at least that's the way it appears.  I've lived here for 8 years and frequented it for over 20 - I've never felt threatened here.  That being said, I am always cautious at night no matter where I am.  When walking home from the subway after dark - even if it's 5:30 pm - I take the best lit route home and I usually carry keys in my knuckles and remove my headphones, but that's just me. 


Also, I have a friend in the precinct who has said that alot of the violent crime is gang related either with the projects or from the Albanian gangs coming down from the Bronx to antagonize the Greeks.  And there's a fair amount of burglary I've been told.


Get insurance for your belongings - something apartment dwellers frequently overlook, it's really not that expensive.  And, if you can, get an alarm system.  Even though the cops won't respond in time to save your life or stuff [based on my own experience of 2 alarm issues in the last few years in which the cops took 70 minutes to respond, I could have walked to the precinct in 10), chances are random criminals will skip your place if you have an alarm in place, they go for the easy stuff.
