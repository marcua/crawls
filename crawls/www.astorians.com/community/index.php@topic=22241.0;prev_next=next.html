If you are a voice professional (singer, actor, teacher, lawyer), I would investigate the Head and Neck Surgical Group at Roosevelt Hospital. They take almost all insurances, and are VERY sensitive to the needs of voice professionals. They are very conservative in their diagnostic therapies, and extremely knowledgeable.  I am a professional singer and voice teacher. From a professional perspective, I am most  concerned with vocal health as primary to long term vocal preservation. Many ENT's in the city are quick to recommend surgery or steroid therapies, which are dangerous to vocal longevity if overused.   http://www.entsurg.com/specialists/index.html
