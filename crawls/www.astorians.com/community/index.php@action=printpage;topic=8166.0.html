Title: Starting a Western Queens Parents Board.
Post by: redfragglemom1 on October 03, 2007, 11:04:23 AM
A close friend who lives in Washington Heights belongs to a parents board there that is quite successful. Take a look:

http://groups.yahoo.com/group/parentandme1/


This group enabled me to meet another mom in Sunnyside (her sister is in Washington Heights as well) and I'm helping her start a Western Queens version of this board. The board in Washington Heights serves as AN AMAZING resource for parents in that neighborhood to:

- find/exchange discounted and free baby gear
- arrange playdates
- find childcare (such as nannyshares and getting recommendations on daycare, nannies, etc.)
- vent, advise and share about such things as parks, organic shopping, and other neighborhood topics that might pertain to parenting

If you are a parent in Astoria, Woodside and Sunnyside, please join and contribute topics and discussions at:

http://groups.google.com/group/western-queens-parents-group

Thanks and hope to see you there!
