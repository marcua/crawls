Title: Re: dumbek classes in astoria
Post by: yuval on August 25, 2006, 12:15:24 PM
dumbek is the famous middle eastern drum, also called Darbooka / darbuka / Tabla and some more names. it is used also in Balkan countries and greece. it's a hand percussion that accompanies live music played by Oud (middle eastern "guitar") lute, harp, klarinet and more and also can be played solo for a belly dancer.
