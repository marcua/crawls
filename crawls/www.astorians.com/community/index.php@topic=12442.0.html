All are welcome this Saturday (and technically every saturday. there's a reason it says "community" in the name, despite what some gardeners might think... cough cough...)

Anyway, it's the little triangle of land where 30th Ave and Astoria Blvd intersect, round about 8th street. Head West on either road and eventually you'll get there. :)

They ask that all children be supervised by an adult, and to respect others' plots. (ie, don't let the kids run through the gardened areas or pick plants there). However there's a big green lawn and a big dirt pile that are very kid-friendly. This Saturday there will be some kids activities, as well. (i believe a costume parade might happen?) And yummy food to share.
