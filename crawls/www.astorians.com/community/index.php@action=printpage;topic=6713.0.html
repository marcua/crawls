Title: Get in Shape Tuesday's @ McLoughlins
Post by: Richard Starlite Karaoke on June 18, 2007, 07:37:06 PM
Every Tuesday Night at McLoughlins starting at 8:00 PM sharp we are now starting Ballroom Dancing with Starlite Karaoke's own Tara Martinez. For the next 5 weeks starting tomorrow Tara will be teaching East Coast Swing in Triple Time. I know it sounds complicated but it is really going to be a BLAST! Tara is a classically trained dancer and has many years of experience as well as numerous competitions awards under her belt. I promise this will be the most fun you will have and you will get in great shape doing it and not to mention you will be able to kick ass on the dance floor at your  next wedding or engagement...
Classes are only $5.00 a person or even better yet you can pay $20.00 for the 5 classes.  After the 5 classes Tara will go on to teach another series of dance based on Ballroom Dancing.
For more info contact Tara at her Myspace (She is in our top favorites) account or just show up at McLoughlins around 7:45 PM...
Afterwards we will start Karaoke at 9:00 PM which I will now be doing 2 for Tuesday Karaoke which will mean everyone will get to sing 2 songs in a row all night long... Also I will be playing the same dance music that we just learned how to dance to in between singers to let our dancers practice and strut their stuff... It's a great way to get in shape have fun meet new friends and just get out of your house for some excitement!!!
Hope to see you all there!!!!


McLoughins is located on Broadway and 31 Street just off of the N line train station in Astoria

No Cover just the same old cheap drink prices!!!
