Community Board #1 will be having a hearing on the Rezoning Proposal for Astoria
Feb 16, 2010; at 7:00 PM at
36-01 35th Ave
Astoria, NY 11106
718 786-3335
The proposed zoning map, appears that most of Astoria will be down zoned, yet there are several areas that will be up zoned. See the link below on how these changes may impact you and your immediate area.

http://www.nyc.gov/html/dcp/html/astoria/index.shtml

Anyone with interest should attend this meeting.
