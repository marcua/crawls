ok, so this week- Asparagus! i know that one!!!!
I'm making one of my favs. A grilled thing salad.
here's my makeshift recipe:

Asparagus - as much as you want i guess? Break them off where it's dry and hard. Blanch them. Toss with olive oil and sliced mushrooms, season with garlic salt and pepper. Grill them for a few minutes til everything is tender.
Meanwhile prep your lettuce. Cut up one onion, a few tomatoes and a cucumber.
When the grilling is done, add your salad dressing of choice to the salad mix then throw in all the grillins.
and for the meat eaters- sometimes i grill shrimp with it but tonight i'm grilling some chicken chunks.

this is really really refreshing for the summer. I think last year, we lived on different variations of this salad all summer long! Its easy and yummy. Might i suggest Ken's Steakhouse Light Caesar Dressing- it doesn't taste like ceasar and its not creamy, weird and good and a little goes a long way.

I'm not part of the CSA, but here's a great quick recipe for fresh asparagus...

Snap woody stems
Heat butter & olive oil in pan with a few cloves of thinly sliced garlic (don't be skimpy with the fats, be sure the bottom of the saute pan is covered)
Saute garlic until just starting to turn golden brown
Toss in asparagus and squeeze in 1/2 fresh lemon, pinch of salt and fresh pepper
Continue tossing until asparagus is cooked to your liking
Remove pan from heat
Generously sprinkle a layer of grated parmesan
Cover and leave off heat for a few minutes to allow parmesan to melt

Makes a great side dish or you could cut asparagus into 1" lengths and then toss the mixture with pasta - enjoy!
