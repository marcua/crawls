Title: Re: Who Serves the Best Espresso in Astoria
Post by: QuentinC on October 01, 2008, 05:36:21 PM
My hands down faves:

Terrizzi Bakery on 30th ave in the mid 30's south side-- you can go up to the counter have it in a REAL not paper cup and it is usually very very good- and they have a sugar dispenser just like you'd see at any caffe bar in italy. 35-14 30th ave.

I also like Gian Piero's but it is sometimes a bit bitter- I always get a Macchiatto there with just a touch of sugar- but it is always served in a paper cup.  they are 44th street and 30th avenue.


Enjoy
