Title: Re: Trying to get a sense of Infant Daycare Costs in Astoria
Post by: litza on March 06, 2013, 09:44:50 PM
I'm not sure about infant daycares, but in my recent experience of hiring in-home care for my kids, the rates tended to be $13-15 (if paying by the hour) and $500-600 (if paying by the week, assuming full-time).


I've used SitterCity.com several times to find babysitters/nannies, and had great success - there are plenty of qualified people with great references looking for jobs.


Good luck, and welcome to the neighborhood! If you haven't checked out the Astoria Moms meetup group, you should! http://www.meetup.com/asahmgroup/. They offer great events and a very helpful message board.
