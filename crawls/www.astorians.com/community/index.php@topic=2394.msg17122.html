I go to the Queens/Long Island Medical Center on Broadway and 23rd Street.  Its one of those large centers with all sorts of doctors, cardiologists on one day, prostate specialists on another, but general practioners all the time.  I see Dr. Batra and he is great!  One day the nurses there said he had an emergency and he was leaving, but then when he saw me he let me come in for a quick check up.  Although the wait is long sometimes (up to an hour) he listens and doesn't just prescribe antibiotics for everything.

My GYN is there too - he's a male but extremely sensitive.  He told me about the problems with the birth control patch b/f all the problems started being reported and made sure I started on something else.  I know you prefer women, but men can be just as good if you get the right one.  His name is Dr. Hleap.

I believe they take GHI - but here is their website:  http://www.qlimg.com/allcenters.html.

There are also labs and x-ray technicians in the same building which means you can just pop on over and don't have to make a seperate appt.
