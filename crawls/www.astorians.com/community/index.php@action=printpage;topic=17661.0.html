Title: 3-Bridges Photowalk
Post by: newell the jewell on March 15, 2010, 03:41:04 PM
A photowalk is being planned for Mid-April 2010:

What's a photowalk?  It's taking a walk/hike with fellow photographers.
Note: You need not be a photographer to attend and any level of photographer may participate

Dates under consideration:

Saturday, April 17th at 11:00 a.m
or
Saturday, April 24th, 11:00 a.m.

Please indicate your date of preference and we’ll take it from there.

Meeting location:
Hoyt Avenue & 27th Street, Southeast corner, next to the staircase to the TriBoro (RFK) Bridge Trail

Route
We will begin in Astoria, at Hoyt Ave & 27th St & walk across the Triboro Bridge to Randall’s Island, eventually making our way to the Harlem River Walk, and south to the 103rd Street Pedestrian/Wards Island Bridge to Manhattan.  Continuing to walk south, we’ll walk the East River Esplanade to 63rd Street, walking west 2 blocks to the walkway entrance to the Queensboro Bridge, east across the bridge to Long Island City and Astoria. This will all be accomplished in a leisurely manner. This isn't a marathon.

Some Points of Interest
  • Triboro (RFK) Bridge (East River Suspension Bridge) - Crosses the East River
  • Southern views of Manhattan Skyline from TriBoro (RFK) Bridge
  • Northern views of the Hell Gate Bridge (Through Arch Bridge) which spans the East River tidal straight called Hell Gate
  • 103rd Street Pedestrian/Wards Island Bridge (Vertical-Lift Bridge) - Crosses the Harlem River
  • East River Esplanade
  • Gracie Mansion
  • Roosevelt Island
  • Queensboro Bridge (Double Cantilever Bridge) - Crosses the East River
