I would talk to a lawyer and find out what exactly the law says first, but I am pretty sure that you are allowed to trim whatever parts of the foliage are growing on your property as long as you don't damage the root system.

This is what I would do.

1) Find out what exactly the law says regarding this.
2) Find a neighbor who speaks their language and can act as a translator. Explain to them that you want them to remove the parts of the plants that are growing on your house/property because you are worried about damage or whatever.
3) If they refuse, explain the law and that you are going to have it done yourself.

Obviously, once you proceed with #3 you will likely ruin whatever semblance of a civil relationship you have with your neighbors, so make sure it's worth it first.
