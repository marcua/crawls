Someone on my block cut down a tree in their yard on saturday an on Sunday after dark, I saw the neighborhood mope and two Spanish guys (i'm going to assume the prop owner paid them) distributing the bagged-up branches throughout the block and putting most of it on Hoyt South by the playground.  We're talking 25ish large bags of branches and debris. 

I did four things...

That night...
1) reported it to 311
2) went around with chalk and wrote the address where the stuff came from on the sidewalk by all the bags that were distributed
The next morning...
3) went to the Astoria Park field-house to report the dumping
4) flagged down a sanitation cop and told him as well

Not sure which one worked, but as I was headed out later that morning, I saw the homeowners carying back the bags that were left by the park.

I go out of my way to learn and follow all the sanitation rules, so it irks me double when I see bullshit like that go on.   Not to mention their lack of courtesy and respect for their neighbors and neighborhood that they showed.
