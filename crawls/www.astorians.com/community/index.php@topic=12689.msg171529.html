Went to Vesta for the first time in awhile this past weekend and it was great as always, but I had to mention the incredible fried polenta. It must have been created by god himself. So frieking good. They are these little fried pieces of polenta that are incredibly moist on the inside and flavorful- very sweet, almost like a dessert item. They were so rich that I couldn't eat them all...I left one on my plate and haven't forgiven myself.

The thing that I love about Vesta is that it's always very welcoming, the staff has always made me feel like a regular even though I don't go there that often. It doesn't appear that they've let the success of it go to their head which is incredibly refreshing in this day and age.
