We want to ensure that every pet will be safe, clean and happy in our facility, and that he or she can have a positive and fun experience. Our utmost concern is to ensure the safety and comfort of every dog that is entrusted to us.
The Jumping Bulldog's brand-new facility is designed and built with highest hygiene and safety standards in mind.  It features wide-open space that is well lit and well ventilated (ceiling fans, AC, window). 
•   Pet-CPR trained staff on premises
•   Individual bunk beds
•   Off-leash daycare
•   Cage-free, constant supervised care
•   Supervised socialization
•   Separate area for dogs with special needs (e.g. sleepy puppies, or elderly dogs, etc.) & a time-out area for the overly rambunctious

Please contact us at info@jumpingbulldog.com to inquire about availability & and pricing.
