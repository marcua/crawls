Title: Queens Pride House 16th Anniversary Sweet Sixteen on Friday Dec. 6th from 6-9pm
Post by: pridecenter on November 29, 2013, 09:54:52 PM
Celebrate Queens Pride House Sweet Sixteen on Friday December 6th from 6-9 pm at Queens Pride House
76-11 37 Ave Suite 206
Jackson Heights NY, 11372
Requested Donation of $20 and up.

Check out our RSVP page online for more details at: https://queenspridehouse.org/rsvp/ (https://queenspridehouse.org/rsvp/)
Also see page for details on taking out an ad on our ad journal.
 
Queens Pride House Sweet Sixteen will feature entertainment by Mary Mandolin Mandolin Player and Red Durkin.
 
The celebration will recognize the efforts of Richard Saenz, HIV/LGBT Advocacy Project staff attorney at Queens Legal Services, Angeline Acain publisher of Gay Parent magazine and Caaav: Organizing Asian Communities.
 
Any questions please email event coordinator Jonathan Acevedo at jacevedo@queenspridehouse.org
