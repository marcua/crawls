A quick question to ask:  what's the protocol on painting your apartment?  I've been living in mine for the past six years and have kept on with the same plain old white but I'm starting to think it would be nice to have some color.  A little skittish about asking my landlords, though, and probably no good reason on that front.  We get along well and I'm the longest-standing tenant in the building.  How have you all approached it?

Actually, they should repaint every year, and if you paint instead you should subtract the cost of the materials from your rent. Or at least, I think I read that somewhere.

You've been there 8 years and they haven't painted once ... they ought to let you repaint.

But if you are on good terms, I expect that if you just ask they'll be receptive. I've never had a problem painting any of  my apartments. Though I generally prefer to beg forgiveness than ask permission :D


ETA: Looks like I was wrong. According to this guy, landlords have to paint rent-controlled apartments every 3 years. they don't have to paint other apartments.

All the more reason they should let you paint, I say.
