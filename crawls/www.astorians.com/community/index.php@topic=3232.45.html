Okay back to the food.....

This is what makes it a little of a pain, and really only worth doing in large batches. It's a bit of a process at first getting the consistency right, but I could make them in my sleep, I've just never measured it.
In a BIG bowl you just squeeze the sausage out of the wrapper, add cheese, chilies. Mix this well before you add the 'Quick, mix again thoroughly. You have to get your hands in it. Don't worry,I always use the plastic "cafeteria gloves" for stuff like this. I'm sure a high-dollar mixer could work it, but it's really thick and cold and too much for my equipment.
