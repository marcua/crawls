H-Mark!!!!

The Homemark 99-cent store on the corner of 31st St and 35th Ave (easily viewable from the train).  It is the veritable mammoth of 99-cent stores.

My friends and I call it the "Store of Requirement" (a la Harry Potter) since whenever we seem to need something, it happens to be there.  Not always 99-cents, but usually.  And I have found some wonderfully odd items there as well.

And while it's not a 99-cent store, I have also found some wonderfully odd houseware items at the Bargain Stop at 30th Ave and 33rd St...
