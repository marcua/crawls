Milkflower
« on: June 15, 2013, 10:17:56 PM »
Location:  34-12 31st Ave
 
Opened June 12 and I decided to give it a try Friday night with a friend.
 
Menu Items we tried:
3 different types of beer (A Ginger lemon, Mama's Little Yellow Pills, and Allagash White) $4-$7
Beet and Carrot salad - amazing and really nice blend of herbed cheese with it
Mushroom Pizza - it was a white one which is not my preference but was willing to try (next time I'd order a cup of tomato sauce on the side), crust is delicious and soft, hint of rosemary was nice but this pizza was very bland...we had to help it a lot with red pepper and salt
They had a trio of gelato but we passed on dessert...grapefruit, chocolate hazelnut, and basil (sounded intriguing)
 
Positives:
Service - great and food came out in a good time frame (Steven answered all questions and was attentive and friendly)
Atmosphere  - laid back and casual
Decor - really cool, rustic and eclectic
 
Negatives:
Food was hit & miss - appetizer was great but pizza was lacking flavor
Outdoor patio - not open yet
 
Overall:
I'd definitely go back and would try some of the other menu items because the potential is there!
Welcome to the neighborhood!!
