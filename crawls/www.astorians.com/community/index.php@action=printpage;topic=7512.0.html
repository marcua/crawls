Title: 20th Rd. now a one-way street!
Post by: Alison on August 14, 2007, 02:04:27 PM
Hey guys, just a heads up for anyone with a car who parks on 20th Road (that little curved road the runs from 31-46 St between 20th & 21st Aves) - part of it is now a one-way street! They just did this today, according to one of my neighbors, so if you're reading this at work, you're probably fine, I can't imagine they'd give tickets for being parked in the wrong direction immediately after they put up the signs and painted the lines.

I'm parked just east of Steinway, and I had to turn my car around to make sure it was pointing in the right direction. I could see that it is one way through at least 42nd St, but it's still two way (from what I could see) west of Steinway. Not sure how far it extends in either direction.

Either way, if you're parked anywhere on 20th Road, go check on it and make sure you're pointed in the right direction, or else you might get a ticket tomorrow morning!

Also, alt side parking is suspended tomorrow (http://www.nyc.gov/html/dot/html/motorist/scrintro.html), so if you're on the Wednesday side (wherever you are), you're fine for tomorrow!
