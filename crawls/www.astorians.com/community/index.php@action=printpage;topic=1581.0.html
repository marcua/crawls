Title: Re: Wings and things March Date
Post by: Jonathan on February 13, 2006, 02:23:27 PM
Ive been there off and on for years now. 
Atmosphere:
you would think on that block it would be high end soho snob but its not that bad.
always a mixed crowd with some local fashion/art types, lots of japanese tourists?/ and regular crowd. 
never felt like mercer kitchen or merc bar at all.  looks hip but how can they be when they feature burgers and fries in baskets and wings?
the cool factor is more about the location, the bathrooms and the custom made  14oz martini glasses.
sllek and cool look.

Bar:
I think my last manhattan there was 12 or 14 bucks but so what its 14oz.  thats like 2 drinks anywhere else.
excellent and friendly bartenders that will make anything and make it look very good.  Ask for Justin
extensive and interesting martini menu
good bourbon and scotch selection

Food:
 is good and always surprised at the casual menu.  try the bacon wrapped water chestnuts with honey for an app. not expensive.

Why:
good date place either at the bar or a table.
good to meet friends there for the first round of the night.
