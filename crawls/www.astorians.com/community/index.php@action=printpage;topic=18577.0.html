Title: Re: Astoria Park Fireworks Concert is Early This Year: Wednesday June 30 2010
Post by: Starwind51 on June 19, 2010, 05:06:08 PM
Quote from: Kelli on June 17, 2010, 06:35:37 PM
I didn't know Astoria had fireworks! Are they good? Would you recommend?

Oh my god, newbie, you have no idea how good this event is!  It's in Astoria Park, between the Hellgate and Triboro bridges, so not only do you get the "boom" of the fireworks, you get the "boom, boom, boom" as the sound ricochets off the bridges! 

You do have to make a choice, though: stay in front of the bandshell to hear the music the best, or stake a claim along the railing by the water to see the fireworks the best (from take off to explosion).  You'll see the explosions just fine if do stay in front of the stage, and you'll still be able to hear the music if you stay by the railings. 

To all events you'll need to bring your own folding chair (available at CVS, RiteAid, Walgreens, etc.) or something damp-proof to put on the grass to sit on.  In my opinion it's best to bring your own stuff to eat, otherwise you have to wander away from the event to stand on line at the vendors.

The fireworks go off after the sky gets dark.  They're set off on Randall's Island.  Grucci is one of the biggest fireworks companies in the area.
