Looking for Web Programmers & Creative People to collaborate with
« on: September 11, 2012, 08:53:26 PM »
We are a creative design team that is looking for programmers, writers, and other creative individuals to collaborate with on multiple projects.
 
We are currently setting up office space in Astoria that we can use to work in, meet with clients, and discuss projects, etc. You can work remotely, or if you want, you can rent a portion of our office. Even though it is not very large, I'm sure we can accommodate you. We are also located in one of the coolest spots... right above Astoria Sound Works within that old factory building on Steinway and 23rd ave. Also, our office is accessible 24hours per day, which means, night owls would love this.
 
We are not hiring anyone, we are looking for a collaborative partnership where we can expand our services and share the profits.
 
A scenario would be:
I get a client that needs design and programming work, you quote me on your programming, and I will combine it with ours to make a full complete solution for the client. This way, we both get paid what we deserve, rather than work for some douchebag that undervalues your work by paying you crumbs per hour, 9-5 (ugh, we hate that).
 
We are doing it like this because #1, it's a place where our freelance/entrepreneurship can grow, and #2, working at our office together we can just chill out have fun pretty much all day while making money. If you feel more comfortable working in your own environment, no problem, we can still use the space to have proper client meetings. Personally we are tired of meeting up at random Starbucks and trekking all over the city looking for a wifi spot. Let us partner our creativity with a more professional workplace environment which will give increased value to our services. We have lots of ideas where both our talents can give us a profitable future.
 
P.S. Even if you don't have any of the skills, we are willing to pay commission to those that bring us clients. Sounds good? :)
 
Please PM us your contact information, and we will get back to you asap.
 
 
Any one of the following types of communication is fine:
Skype
MSN
Email
Phone
