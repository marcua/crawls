I wrote them a love letter a few years ago - begging them to open a store in Astoria.  I think they're awesome and they have such interesting off-beat things (have any of you tried the dried hibiscus?  or the spicy chocolate covered dried mango which i fondly refer to as smack?).

Anyway, I'm stoked if this rumor is true!
