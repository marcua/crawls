After the fireworks last night my friends and I went up to Igloo because, despite the bad treatment I and many other people have posted about, the food is good.


When we got settled in the back garden area the waitress warned us that the restaurant would be closing in about 1/2 an hour.  I ordered a Portobello-Swiss burger, but the waitress came back and said there were no more Portobello mushrooms and asked if I would like the regular mushrooms and Swiss.  I said no (I wasn't going to pay the Portobello price for regular mushrooms).  I specifically said I would order the other mushroom burger, which is $1.45 less.  It wasn't until after I got home that I realized I was charged for the original order.  Since I changed my order after discussing the situation with the waitress, she should have changed the written order on her note pad right then.  I don't live near the restaurant, never just pass by it, and it would cost a lot more than $1.45 to try to collect my due.  But once again, Igloo is on my s___ list.
