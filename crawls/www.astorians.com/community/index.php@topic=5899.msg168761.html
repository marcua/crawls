Ok, I gotta get my pet peeve out of the way first - Astoria is a neighborhood in Queens which is a borough of New York City. Kind of like how Chelsea or Murray Hill or Hells Kitchen are neighborhoods in Manhattan which is a borough of New York City. I understand that people on the island tend to forget about the other boroughs. That's fine, more awesome for the rest of us.

ANYWAY. I've lived here for only about 5 years, but I've always felt safe. Being that it's a city, you have to be aware of your surroundings, and if you're walking home late at night, make sure you're not alone. But as long as you're street savvy, you'll be fine anywhere. Whatever avenue/blvd you choose, I would stay between 21st Street and Steinway, mostly for ease of commuting...you can go east of Steinway around Broadway/34 Av though since there's an R/V stop on Steinway there.
