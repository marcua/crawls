The area use to be much better in the 1970's. There was an amazing Italian feast down there rivaling San Gennaro's downtown at the end of the Summer. I have fond memories of it when I was a child. It was a real neighborhood. Now, it is not safe at night. Hasn't been in 25 years. No matter how many "luxury" condos that may devour that area in the next few years, it will never be safe. There are no stores and it is completely desolate late at night, hence the plethora of illegal night clubs that are hidden throughout. I would never walk from the N train towards the river at 2am. No one does.
Title: Re: 14th and 30th? Thoughts?

I have a friend that lived right around there-- in one of those amazing houses actually, peach tree in the backyard, huge garage... Not sure what your specific address is or what the building is, but my friend's house was awesome-- Close to the water and park. Down side is that outside the cool houses/ newer constructions, the area ain't so good. I've walked around late at night and it can be a little sketchy. The best area for access to transportation (in my opinion) is the rectangle b/w 31st street and Steinway (basically b/w N/W on one side and R/G/V other side)-- also priciest. For other cheap rents try east of Steinway around B'way or 30th ave.
