Title: Astoria Community Joins in The Fight Against Cancer
Post by: Relay For Life of Astoria on January 27, 2009, 12:36:52 PM
Cancer affects more than 8.9 million people in the United States and over 9,000 people will be newly diagnosed in Queens this year.  We invite you to make a difference and help us provide hope to patients, families and friends coping with cancer.

Please join us for a complimentary informational kick-off party on Wednesday, February 11th to learn more about how you can form a team and volunteer at the American Cancer Society’s 4th Annual Relay For Life of Astoria 

“Relay For Life” is an overnight event that brings our community together to remember those who have lost their battle with cancer, honor those who have won their battle and encourage those still in the battle.  It raises awareness of cancer and raises money to support the programs and services of the American Cancer Society.
