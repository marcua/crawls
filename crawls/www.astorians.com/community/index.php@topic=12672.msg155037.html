'Help yourself' douche
« on: October 16, 2008, 10:46:41 AM »
Sometime late Tuesday night or Wednesday morning someone decided to clean out his closet and give a few of his clothes away.

While there's nothing wrong with giving away your unwanted clothes, putting them in a cardboard box with a piece of paper strapped to one of the flaps saying, "Help Yourself" and placing it on the sidewalk underneath the trestle on 33rd St and 23rd Ave is the most asinine thing they could have done with them.

Why, you idiot, did you think placing the box there, instead of in front of where you live would have been a better idea? It's not. It's just not your problem to have to deal with anymore by tossing it out of sight. What annoys me is that you might sleep at night and think that you've done your community a service. You haven't.

There are clothes drop boxes all over, a thrift store a block away, churches you could have donated them to... but no, you chose dump them in a cardboard box and leave it outside on the street.

You're a douche.
