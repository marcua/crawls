Title: Summer Theatre Camp
Post by: jmoody8712 on May 27, 2014, 07:57:35 PM
Imagine Works is a local children's theatre program. Our amazing summer theatre camp runs from July 14th-August 3rd at The Players Theatre, a professional, Off-Broadway theatre in the West Village. Our 3-week summer program is great for kids & teens ages 6-18. Our camp size is small to encourage more one-on-one attention. With such a small number of kids, it is important to register early!
We are so excited to present the silly, challenging, and fantastic musical "Seussical Jr." that takes audiences on a journey from the Jungle of Nool, to Who and Solla Sollew!
Students attend intensive morning classes on voice, drama and dance. After lunch, they rehearse intensively for their final musical performance. We invite agents, managers and casting directors to our performance to meet our talented students. This is one of the only programs to offer such industry exposure for kids!
To register, please visit our website at www.imagineworksonline.com (http://www.imagineworksonline.com/)
