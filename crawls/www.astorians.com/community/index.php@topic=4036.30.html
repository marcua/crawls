Hi all. First, thanks for all the feedback on this.

I don't think the nods are like a popularity contest at all, well, any more than agreeing with someone in a discussion is. In the last version (although I didn't take it personally) I do think it wasn't as useful because the last time we tried anything like this you got only one "vote" per day, and you were voting for the "poster". I see this as entirely different because you are expressing agreement or disagreement with what was said in each post. I don't see this as a popularity contest at all, and I think if you look at many of the nods on the board you'll see that people (including me) are getting both positive and negative nods depending on what content they are posting.

I also do think that it is useful, but not in a significant way. Maybe about as "useful" as the "ranks" that we have on the board. But not as "useful" as the spell checker for example. The ranks for example don't really serve a significant utility and could easily be thought of as as popularity contest, but I see it as just a fun way to show who has been around on the board longer. Likewise I think the nods can provide feedback to both the poster and the reader as to how people are immediately reacting to the content of that post. It serves a small utility, but is mainly just a different way to engage the content. I personally find it interesting as I read down a thread to see the reaction people are having as they also read the entire thread. I don't have any information to suggest that people are just negative-nodding people they don't like, based on what I'm seeing it looks like people are getting a variety of nods - positive and negative. I've found myself nodding with some folks that I usually find myself disagreeing with. It's kindof a mixed bag of nods so far for me.

As for eliminating negative nods, or changing the way the number is displayed I think each of these are interesting in their own way and am interested in more feedback as we continue to see how people are using it.

Just a note on my personal new nods policy...

I positive-nod postings that I think are interesting, or funny, or informative.
I negative-nod postings that I think are over-rated and have too many positive nods.
I positive-nod postings that have negative overall nods, 'cause I don't think that negative ratings are good for the board.
I otherwise ignore postings that are inane.

Feel free to adopt this policy, or for that matter, to adopt the inverse of this policy if you feel like canceling out my votes. :)

Personally, I now feel that it would be best if nods were thresholded at zero, so the worst nod rating a post could have would be zero.
