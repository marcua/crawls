I think you're missing my point- I completely agree that feeding your child is the right thing to be doing. There are a lot of "right things to be doing" that people do, and  they get done without disturbing other people, and I feel like for the most part that should be the general rule.

For example: I own a dog- and she has to go out and use the bathroom. The right thing to do is take her out and let her poop. Out of consideration to other people, I make sure that she is not pooping in front of a restaurant while people have to look at it while trying to eat. I walk her on side streets and pick up after her.

I realize it is not the same thing as feeding your child- but my point is, yes, you have to feed your child, I have to walk my dog, other people have to do a lot of other things- but we should be doing them in a considerate fashion so as not to disturb those around us.

Asking a mother to cover up when breastfeeding is not an unreasonable request. Each of have our respective responsibilities, and part of those responsibilities is being considerate of those around us.
