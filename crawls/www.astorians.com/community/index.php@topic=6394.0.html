"AECOM"?? (Google, Google) Oh, OK, it's a school of medicine. And... it's here:

http://www.onnyturf.com/subway/?address=1300+morris+park+ave,+bronx,+NY

Wow, that is not very close to any subways. From Astoria, you'd take the M60 to Harlem (20 minutes), take I guess the 5 train to the Pelham Parkway (30 minutes, probably), and then either walk for 20 minutes or take another bus. I don't think you want to live here, unless you have a car and drive to work every day ($4.50 in tolls each way). And if you have a car, I think you'd have a much better commute living in the Bronx or maybe in Westchester. Good luck.
