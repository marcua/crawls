Title: I'm Sponsoring a Comedy Special at the Beer Garden Thursday Night
Post by: JeremiahFP on July 06, 2010, 10:58:14 AM
Hi all,

One of the things I love about Astoria is that there are so many amazing artists who live in our community.  Last month we sponsored a concert in Astoria Park featuring lots of local talent - ranging from Broadway singers to local high school students who have the potential to be on Broadway.

My campaign is sponsoring a comedy special at the Beer Garden from 7-9 on Thursday night.  The comedy hour is hosted by Marion Grodin (StandUp NY and Gothem Comedy Clubs) and will feature Jamie Kilstein (Citizen Radio), Malcolm Barrett (Better Off Ted, Hurt Locker), Darian Dauchan (Solo Performer and Slam Poetry Champ), Scott Blakeman, Harry Terjanian and more...

This event is a fundraiser with suggested donations of $25 (Friend), $50 (All Star), $100 (Rock Star), $250 (Superstar) (sorry, fundraising is a necessary evil and, to the best of my knowledge, I'm the only candidate in my race who isn't taking corporate money), but it is an open event and no one will be turned away.

Details are below: 

Bohemian Hall & Beer Garden
29-19 24th Avenue
Astoria, NY 11102
7-9p Sharp!!
(Doors open at 6:30p)
For more information or to RSVP please visit http://www.actblue.com/page/jfpjuly8 or Call Ben Kalish at 646.797.3290

I'm hoping to see a lot of you on Thursday!
