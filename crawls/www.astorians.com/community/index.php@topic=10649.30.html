What about a story on some of the various subway performers we see on the N/W all the time?  The basketball coach, the blind accordion man who no longer plays the accordion, etc.  That would be an interesting story. 

I got the idea because I was standing on the N/W/R platform at 59th street last night, and I saw this hippie looking woman I used to see on the R.  She plays the guitar and takes donations in a bag that says "Mean People Suck."  She was standing with a friend, another hippie looking guitar woman, and they were having the most fascinating conversation.  From what I could gather, they were disparaging how a fellow underground musician only plays in the subway once in awhile for extra money, and does not view it as a fulltime job like they do.  The one woman was discussing how she maximizes her potential by keeping to certain trains and schedules.  Fascinating stuff, and would make a good "This American Life" or NYT type of piece.
