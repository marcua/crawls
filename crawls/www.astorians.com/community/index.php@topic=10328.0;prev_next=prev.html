Hello my fellow Astorians. We have been here now for 4 months and we love it. We just took an evening stroll through the park and love the community feel of this neighborhood. We have an 11 year old son and he'll be attending I.S. 141 come September. He loved the park where his dad and himself played catch while I sat with the two dogs just taking it all in. It's an amazing view at the foot of Hell's Gate bridge. There seems to be plenty of activities going on in the neighborhood for adults but I'm hoping to meet other parents with children of this age range. Let me know if there are any fun things going on that our children can interact and meet each other. We'll be going to the batting cages this weekend, so hopefully he can meet other boys his age there.

Hope to hear from anyone.
