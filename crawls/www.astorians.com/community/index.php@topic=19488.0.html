Greek House Foods 32-20 30th Ave
« Reply #5 on: October 09, 2010, 05:48:22 PM »
I too was shocked and dismayed to find Greek House Foods shut down. It is a terrible loss of a true treasure. The prepared foods were of exceptional quality, and Nick introduced me to many wonderful products over the years.

I also spoke with someone in a nearby shop, and they indicated that the Greek House was pushed out of business by a rent increase motivated by the building owner's desire to expand the Fruit Brothers. The Greek House has not made any moves to open in another location, although, according to the woman I spoke with, they are still living in the neighborhood and have not completely ruled out trying to find a new location. I sincerely hope that is true, and wish them the best of luck in whatever they decide to pursue.

It is so very sad to see old-line businesses like these, where the family owner-operators work tirelessly to provide something of quality and substance to the neighborhood, priced out of existence.

If anyone knows, or sees, Nick or his wife, please let them know that they are loved and missed.
