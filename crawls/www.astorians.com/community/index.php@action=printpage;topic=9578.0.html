Hello all! 

I have been stage managing a fun little one act called An Acute Triangle.   We have made it into the FINALS of the Strawberry One-act Festival.   If our play is voted as one of the 4 best shows (out of six)  our playwright will have her show published as well as be given the opportunity to create and develop a longer show with The Riant Theatre.

The 6 shows range from comedy to tragedy and from small casts to large ensembles.    We hope you come out to support us!    (You can even watch ONLINE!)

Our show is called
An Acute Triangle, written by Rosemary Martino, Directed by Matthew Hamel Simms
Starring Joe Danbusky, Scott Lilly and Laura Carbonell


The Strawberry One-Acts Festival Finals Winter 2008
Friday, February 22nd at 7:30 p.m. OR Saturday, February 23rd at 2pm
The best plays from the Semi-Finals & The Wild Night
Tickets:  $25.00.  Price includes a reception during intermission on each day.

At The American Theatre of Actors, 314 West 54th Street, 2nd Floor (Between 8th & 9th Avenue)

http://therianttheatre.com/strawberry.html

Call the box office 646-623-3488 RESERVATIONS ARE A MUST!!!!
