thanks for the feedback everyone.  So just to add a little to the story for Empire Today here is a little recap of my dealings with them.

I work in advertising and I used to sell commercial time to Empire Today...so I emailed my contact there and she put me on some sort of VIP service list.  This was after reading Jennifer's account above.  I was told that I would be getting a good rep (whatever that means) and that I would receive the corporate discount.

Please keep in mind that I was only looking to carpet one room that is 12x14. 

I had already stopped by the Carpet Place as mentioned above and another place right around the corner from them.  Both places quoted me around $4-500 depending on the carpet that I chose.  Also, both places said they could install it on a Sunday or after work hours during the week without a problem. 

The Empire Today rep showed up early to his credit.  He gave me a HEAVY sales pitch asking who I knew there and that I was a VIP...whatever.  After the whole song and dance he quotes me $688 for carpeting that I thought was of lesser quality than the local places in Astoria.  The $688 was with the "Corporate discount" and a 60% off sale on the carpet.  The rep told me that he was not allowed to take commission on the deal either (yeah, he is doing it for free). 

What a joke.  I ended up going with Kahn Carpets on Steinway St for $400 and the carpet looks great.
