LIC CSA 2018 Summer Share Sign-up!
« on: April 12, 2018, 11:10:49 AM »
Winter seems to finally exit left. April showers and fresh spring weather are in the forecast. Our local farms are preparing for an amazing summer share season for us!
 
We are proud to offer fresh, locally grown organic produce, beef, honey, coffee, and eggs from our partner farms: Farm at Miller's Crossing, Hepworth Farms, McMurray Farms, and Red House Roasters.
 
Finally, you can sign-up for a share or two or three with Long Island City CSA!
 
Wednesday distributions begin June 6th and will run for 24 weeks.
 
 Prices and details are available on the website now.
 
Please tell your friends and family! Forward our email to anyone you know looking to join an awesome CSA! The more the merrier!!
 
 Early sign-up incentive for all veggie share participants.
SIGN-UP TODAY!

 For questions about subsidized shares or payment plans, please email us!
