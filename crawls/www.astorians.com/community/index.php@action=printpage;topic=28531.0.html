Title: The Strand Storytellers!
Post by: The Strand Storytellers on October 25, 2014, 11:02:37 AM
Coming Monday Nights this Fall, The Strand Smokehouse presents an original series, The Strand Storytellers!

This Monday's Topic is : Ghost Stories

Participants who tell a story 3-10 minutes long pertaining to the week’s topic will receive a Strand Storyteller gift card with an $10 food/beverage credit, just for participating! Redeemable on their next visit. 

Stories can be true, partially true or fiction we don’t care as long as it’s good!

The final round of the evening is a competition called Power Improv! In which each week contestants will compete in an original Improv game where the audience clap-o-meter determines the champion. Each weeks champion will be entered into our Power Improv Championship Match at the end of the season.

Prizes for top 3 winners are:
1st Place Prize $3000.00
2nd Place Prize $2000.00
3rd Place Prize $1000.00

The evening will also feature special guest story tellers including top writers, directors and actors in their field.

Each week’s signup begins every Monday at 7pm and the show starts at 8pm!

Let the story telling begin!

*Free to participate*
