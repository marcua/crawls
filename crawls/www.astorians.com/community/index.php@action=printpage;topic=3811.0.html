Title: Puppet show & park clean up @ Rainey Park, Sun 10/22
Post by: SpokesmanCycles on October 15, 2006, 12:50:33 PM
Sunday October 22nd is It’s My Park! Day in Long Island City’s Rainey Park  

A puppet show, art activities,  and a chance to beautify the park are part of this free event.  Plans for a new playground will be on display.

Puppeteers from City Parks Foundation’s CityParks PuppetMobile will perform “The Princess, the Emperor and the Duck” and conduct a puppet-making workshop in Rainey Park on Sunday, October 22nd. That’s just part  of It’s My Park! Day, a free celebration running  from 11:00 AM to 4:00 PM.  Some of the day’s other  planned events include  art activities led by  the nearby Noguchi Museum, face-painting, and bulb-planting. Park-goers will also get a “sneak peak” at plans for a new children’s playground slated for the south end of the Park. 

WHO:      Sponsored by The Rainey Park Group, Partnership for Parks and CityParks Foundation. Puppeteers from City Parks Foundation’s CityParks PuppetMobile

WHEN:    Sunday October 22, 2006. 
11:00 AM – 1:00 PM  Park clean-up, leaf raking and bulb planting.
1:00 PM- 2:00 PM  “The Princess, the Emperor and the Duck” by CityParks Puppet Mobile
2:00 PM – 4:00 PM  Puppet-making workshop, teen art activities and face painting
All Day:  Plans of new playground on display.
 
WHERE:    Rainey Park is located along the Long Island City Waterfront, at the intersection of Vernon Boulevard and 34th Avenue. By subway: Take the N train to Broadway. Walk west to Vernon Boulevard. Turn left and walk South to Rainey Park.
