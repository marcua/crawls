I did some research on this a few weeks ago, and that whole block of 31st from ABC Super Stores to the public library is being bulldozed for new construction, with commercial space on the ground floor and 42 residential units on floors 2-6: https://www.yimbynews.com/2017/03/six-story-42-unit-mixed-use-project-coming-to-21-21-31st-street-astoria.html (https://www.yimbynews.com/2017/03/six-story-42-unit-mixed-use-project-coming-to-21-21-31st-street-astoria.html)

That's an improvement over what I had read before. Apparently, initial plans called for converting the whole block from the library to 21st Ave into 78 units. That would have included Elephant Thai, the 99¢ store, El Olivo, a salon, and Verdi Fresh. The old Sunbright laundromat was part of that, and it has closed already. The same company (RockFarmer) bought all of these spaces, so maybe they're just building in two phases now?
