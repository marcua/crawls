Not too sure if this is the right thread to post under, but I just wanted to note that we finally checked out Samba grill tonight (my boyfriend and I) and we loved it. We had never been before and the service style was explained in a cheerful manner, food was very plentiful and I liked most of the food I took from the buffet - the one caveat were the meatballs I picked up- they were perhaps out too long? They were somewhat dry. As for the rest of the food-which was delicious-I'm sorry to say that I don't know the names of what I ate - something with bacon, beans and collards was pretty fantastic- and I had a brahma beer with my dinner. My favorite meats were the pork sausage and the (I believe) rib eye. Although the decor is a bit "eh", it's so comfortable, who cares? We will definitely be going back.

If Samba still reads this, thank you so much for a nice night. My only suggestion would be to add perhaps a bowl of fruit salad to the buffet (like pineapple, grapes, watermelon) to give some of us more casual meat eaters (I eat red meat pretty rarely -har har) something to cleanse our taste buds.

See you soon!
