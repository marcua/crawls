My name's Preston, and I'm fresh meat here. Though I've frequented the cafe Freeze Peach for a little while now (really not all that long), I've rarely used the internet there, so ne'er have I stumbled upon the boards here. Until now, of course. I'm a musician by trade, moving here about a year and a half ago to pursue a more serious career. Between work and a social life, though, I haven't found much time to buckle down until the last couple of months. My mom always told me there's your vocation to bring sustenance to your life, and your advocation to bring sanity. Unfortunately, I'm not so simple, and lately I've been frustrated with how much I'm not focusing on what I truly love to do.

So I've played around, at the open mic at Waltz, and at Freeze Peach just this last Saturday. Astoria seems like a very creatively inclined community, and I feel that much more close to my goal (being able to sustain myself on my music alone) by just being here.

So with that long-winded introduction, I say hello.
