Title: 7th Annual “After the Holidays” Electronic Waste Recycling Events
Post by: megc on December 21, 2009, 01:41:28 PM
Since the question of where to dispose of your electronics comes up from time to time, I thought I'd share this with you.  It's not in Astoria, but could be of use to some of you, regardless:

7th Annual “After the Holidays” Electronic Waste Recycling Events

New Yorkers can realize their New Year’s resolutions to have less negative impact on the environment by recycling their working or non-working computers, monitors, cell phones, TVs, IPODs, DVD players, printers, modems, etc. at the Lower East Side Ecology Center’s 7th  annual After the Holidays Electronic Waste Recycling Drive.

This year the Ecology Center is offering five recycling days. All events will be held from 10 am – 4 pm , rain or shine:

  • Sunday, January 3rd - Union Square Park – North Plaza
  • Saturday, January 9th - Ring Garden at Riverside Drive between Seaman & Broadway
  • Sunday, Janaury 10th - Lennox Hill Neighborhood House, 331 E 70th Street between 1st & 2nd Avenues
  • Saturday, January 16th - Tekserve, 119 W 23rd Street between 6th & 7th Avenues
  • Sunday, January 17th - Prospect Park at 3rd Street & Prospect Park West

The Lower East Side Ecology Center (http://lesecologycenter.org) has details on hours, what to bring.
