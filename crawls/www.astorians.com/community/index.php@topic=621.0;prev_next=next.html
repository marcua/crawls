HAPPY FATHERS DAY!  Here are some of our favorite ways to celebrate Dad and enjoy the longest day of the year this Sunday: Go for a bike ride together along the Vernon Blvd/Queens East River Greenway or paddle around Hallets Cove in a kayak. Fire up the grill at Queensbridge Park, the only nearby park with designated BBQ areas.  Challenge Dad to a game of tennis in Astoria Park. Check out the innovative playground at Rainey Park. Relax and enjoy the view!
