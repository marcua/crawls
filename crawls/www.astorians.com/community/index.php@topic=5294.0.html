I like the suggestions above-Rosarios, Best Yet and Euromarket...here's a few other places that I know of that have good selections:

Titan Foods on 31st between 28th Ave and Astoria Blvd. has a  large selection of Greek cheeses, as does Mediterranean (31st St. just south of 23rd Ave in Agora Plaza and also on 34th Street just south of 30th Ave), and Christos (23rd Ave between 29th and 31st St.)

Berry Fresh Farms in the Ditmars Shopping Center (Ditmars and 44th) seems to have a pretty good selection as well including some interesting cheeses in their regular dairy section that aren't found in many other supermarkets.
