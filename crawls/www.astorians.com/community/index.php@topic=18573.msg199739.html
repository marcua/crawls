Hello to All!

If I am responding late to some of your questions, I apologize, I am new to this blog and only noticed the questions today.

For those searching for an actual bike store, you will not find us.
Because we are a start-up, we are operating Ride & Roll out of my apartment for the time being.
Hopefully by next year we will be renting a space in the area.

We are located right next to the Vet, I have put out Ride & Roll flyers on one of the windows, if you look up you will see them.

To be able to rent all you need is a valid ID and to sign our waiver.
