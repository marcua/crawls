Title: Purim Vintage Costume Party: Sun, March 12
Post by: abopevents on March 10, 2006, 10:58:54 PM
In honor of the 80th year of our synagogue, we encourage people to wear costumes from the 1920s onward that are symbolic of a certain decade. Break out your poodle skirt or bell-bottoms!  Typical Purim refresments will be provided and prizes for the best costumes will be given. Price of admission is $5 per adult and children under 12 are free. Begins at 4pm. All are welcome.

Astoria Center of Israel Synagogue
27-35 Crescent Street, Astoria

We're less than 20 minutes from 59th and Lex in Manhattan! Take the N or W subway towards Queens to the 30th Avenue stop. Walk west on 30th Avenue (towards Athens Square Park) for about 4 short blocks; turn right on Crescent Street and walk a half block down on the right. For more information, email: info@astoriacenterofisrael.org or visit http://www.astoriacenter.org .

Note we are also having a Megillah reading the following evening (Monday) at 7pm.
