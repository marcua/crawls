Title: Rally for Better Transit in Astoria- Tuesday July 24
Post by: costa01 on July 23, 2012, 08:07:51 AM
In 2010, the MTA cut transit service in Astoria eliminating the W and V trains and the QM22 express bus as part of citywide cuts despite the community fighting to keep them.


Last week, the MTA has announced that they would restore much of the service cut in 2010, but NOTHING IN ASTORIA!!


Come rally with us to tell the MTA that Astoria is a growing, thriving community that requires more transit options to Manhattan, not less!


TUESDAY JULY 24
7:30AM
Ditmars Blvd N/Q train stop.
(31 street between ditmars blvd and 23rd Avenue)


Here is an article about the MTA's proposed planto leave Astoria out of the restorations : [size=78%]http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/mta-announces-partial-restoration-q79-bus-article-1.1118005 (http://www.nydailynews.com/new-york/queens/mta-announces-partial-restoration-q79-bus-article-1.1118005)[/size]


Join our local elected officials, advocacy groups and community members in calling on the MTA to better serve our community!
