My criticisms of the political process notwithstanding, I am still undecided on both candidates.  Even though I feel Aravella Simotas' candidacy is for all the wrong reasons, she might very well do a good job in office.  Jeremiah Frei-Pearson has not yet distinguished himself in the race so far, but he is out there fighting a tough fight.

I know that there are two camps here with leanings for one candidate and the other, but I don't think anyone can honestly say how these two really stack up until we see them together in a debate.

On the basis of name recognition, financials, endorsements and strategy, Aravella Simotas seems to be way ahead.  On the basis of experience, capability, education, political views, they are similar.

As for John Ciafone, I know little about him but I don't think he is a wildcard in this election.
