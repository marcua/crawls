Hello all,

I just joined the site and find all your posts very helpful already. Currently I reside in NJ but I'm looking to move to astoria. I've been looking for places since I started my new job in manhattan. I originally wanted to move to Morningside Heights but then a friend of mine pointed out the beauty of living in Queens. Close to Manhattan, great cultural neighborhoods and the relativley affordable rent. Not to mention the MOMA and Shea stadium being nearby!

I am 28yrs old, currently residing in NJ, seeking for a 1brm apt for me and my soon to be wife. Our budget is in the $1100 range. I have seen postings on craigslist.org and other sites for this price but I am unfamiliar with the neighborhood and wonder if the estimate is a realistic figure. I work in mid-town so I am looking for a place with nearby subways and in a safe neighborhood. If anyone has any advice on how I should begin my search and if my expectations on price are accurate or inaccurate please let me know.

Hopefully soon I can be an Astorian just like you.

Thanks for reading!
