Just came across this thread so apologize in advance for the late posting.

My partner and I banned Igloo about a year ago.  Even though we ate there a couple of times a week the following was inexcusable so I can't justify supporting this establishment  The final straw was that we each ordered a chicken salad and the chicken came out grossly undercooked.  He had already poured ranch dressing on his part but we still sent them back.  They brought us "brand new salads" yet there was a huge white stain on the chicken from where they threw it (dressing and all) back in the deep fryer.  When we asked the waiter about this he insisted that they had made completely new salads and he had personally witnessed this.  We then called the manager over and he insisted that this was the case as well.  The ironic thing is that the chicken still wasn't fully cooked.  We simply asked for our check for the smoothies and they tried to make us pay for the salads that we hadn't even taken a bite of. After a heated argument (and those who know me that I'm calm 99.9% of the time until you push me to the limit) we gave them the money for the smoothies and left.  To this day when I walk by if the manager is outside smoking he gives me dirty looks.

I'm glad to see that other people are starting to ban this place as well.  It's a shame though because I really enjoyed going there but refuse to be treated this way when there are so many better dining options around Ditmars.

I have to say I'm really surprised to hear of the extent of some of these negative experiences at Igloo. I don't doubt anyone's account, as I wasn't there.

But I'll say I have literally been to Igloo hundreds of times in the last four years. The first month we moved here, we went almost every day because our kitchen was too full with boxes. The people there have always been really nice and professional, and the food is great. Sometimes there is a big waiter/waitress turnover, but I've never had a bad experience with any of them. I almost always go in with one other person (my husband) at all various hours, and probably sit at a booth half the time just the two of us. No one has ever said that was a problem. I have talked w/the owner/wife many times, but never the owner/husband (he does seem kind of stand-offish to me). I have never witnessed any inappropriate incidents between the staff and patrons.

Just wanted to give another point of view for those who haven't been there before.
