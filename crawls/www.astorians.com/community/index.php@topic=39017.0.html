Bright big bedroom 15 x 15' with three tall windows. This spacious bedroom is large enough to fit your bedroom, a home office and make your own entertainment area. The room is unfurnished, clean and ready now to move in.

This large two-level apartment is very spread out and features a small dining area, a large kitchen and full bathroom/shower on the first floor. Downstairs has a large group space / living room area and a half-bath. We have an in-house laundry with washer and dryer.
There's lots of space in this house to hang out, be creative and have fun!

The side walkway leads to a gorgeous back yard with a cement patio, grass and garden area which we maintain and share. We have picnic tables and a BBQ grill for summer parties and enjoy being outside.

There is an off-street parking space in front for your car and there's ample street parking as well.

This is a safe and quiet neighborhood. Lots of great restaurants and grocery stores are conveniently close. City Buses are right on the corner. Two subways are near. Easily get to mid-town Manhattan in 30-45 minutes.

Astoria Park is nearby. Enjoy summer swimming at the Astoria Park Pool, as well as tennis, running and public events.
Socrates Park and the Noguchi Museum are also right around the corner, offering additional social activities in the summer.

The room is ready to move in now.
$1200 + One month security deposit
We split ConEd and Internet three-ways

We are looking for a roommate who will come with a "Leave the place better than you found it" attitude. We keep a clean environment, especially in the shared kitchen and bath. You clean up after yourself.
No Smoking. No Drugs.

Men, women, gay or straight. Creative people - Great! Professionals are welcome!
