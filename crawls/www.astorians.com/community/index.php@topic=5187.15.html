So has anyone used The Magical Express, where Disney transports you and your luggage to the hotel?  Does your luggage really arrive 3 hours after you do, or do they just have to say that, just in case?

We used them and it was heaven. I wouldn't go any other way now. We just went down to the counter after we arrived at Orlando Int., got on a bus and never had to lay a finger on any luggage. Of course I was a bit worried the luggage would be lost (I'm a worrier), but it arrived at our room about 45 minutes after we checked in. I think the 3 hour thing is just a disclaimer. Just carry on anything that you'll really need and you'll have no problems. On the way back, it was the same. Just gave our luggage to the front desk and didn't see it until back at LaGuardia.

Have a great time and please tell us all about the trip when you get back!! :mrgreen:
