It's actually the location where Bulgara was, although there's no indication on the Bulgara website that they're closed.  It was a Bulgarian restaurant, not Romanian.  There is a Romanian restaurant called Ion's Corner Cafe a few blocks away, which as far as I know is still open.  It's at the corner of 36th Ave. and Vernon, across the street from the Roosevelt Island bridge.

I ate at Bulgara once with a group from Astorians -- the "mixed grill" demonstrated an impressive variety of pork products, and the Bulgarian music videos on the largest TV I've ever seen provided quite the entertainment.  I'll be interested to see what beers WunderBar (groans on the name) carries.
