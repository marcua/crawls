Title: It's My Park Day at Queensbridge Park
Post by: kovert on October 05, 2007, 05:09:51 PM
It's My Park Day will be held on Saturday, October 20, 2007 from 10am-3pm.  It is an opportunity for community members to celebrate their local park by helping to maintain and beautify Queensbridge Park (Vernon Boulevard and 41st Avenue). Volunteer projects include planting 2,000 daffodil bulbs, raking leaves, and painting park benches. This effort will be followed by lunch, free nature walk led by the Urban Rangers, an award ceremony and a puppet show, Cinderella Samba.  It's MY Park Day is sponsored by Partnerships for Parks in collaboration with Friends of Queensbridge Park Committee, Green Shores NYC: Astoria-LIC Waterfront Parks Alliance,  CityParks Foundation, and NYC Department of Parks & Recreation. 

Please RSVP: Karen.Overton@parks.nyc.gov
