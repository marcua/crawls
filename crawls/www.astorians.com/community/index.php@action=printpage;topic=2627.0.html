Title: Re: Power: Who has it, who doesn't?
Post by: merm on July 22, 2006, 12:29:00 PM
These kinds of problems indicate an inside wiring problem. ConEd supplies your building with one line, then that line is split off. If you have one outlet working, and others not working it's the result of damage or problems with the breakers inside your building.

There's a slight possibility (as is the case in my building) that some of the outlets already have low voltage (even when the power is on full) and so they operate at a lower voltage than others. With a low voltage coming in from ConEd it's possible that these outlets are indeed carrying power, it's just too low for any appliance to use.

To cycle your breakers just find the breaker box and turn off each switch one by one and then turn them back on.
