I live around the corner from that place and we call it the "Fakery" because they don't actually bake anything there.  Baked goods are brought in as they run out.  So if there are a lot of the items then they are  generally fresh, if the trays are low then stay away!  We have given up on that place except for the occasional weekend when we "need" a donut, or when guests are in town who drink coffee we send them their too, since we don't have a coffee maker.  Their coffee is supposed to be the "Best in Astoria"
