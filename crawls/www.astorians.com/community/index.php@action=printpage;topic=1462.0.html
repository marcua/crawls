Title: Re: Good place for lunch
Post by: thebigern on January 19, 2006, 06:32:01 PM
I guess it depends where in Astoria you are, how far you're willing to travel and what you're in the mood for, atmosphere, etc. There's so many great places to eat here.

Here's a few of my faves:
Yajai -- 33-16 30th Ave. Thai food, tasty & cheap, not much on ambiance, but you can bring it your own beer & wine
J.J.'s -- 37-05 31st Ave. Best Japanese food in Astoria, hands down. Nice ambiance, very friendly, melt in your mouth sushi, very reasonable
Soho -- 22-15 31st St. Cheap & tasty all day breakfast, plus sandwiches, salads, healthy food and kick ass pancakes

The Beer Garden's a good place for warm weather food. But in the winter months, you can still drink beer & eat great Czech food inside.

Hope that helps...but if you end up finding another great place, post it here...
