At some point in the '90s I used the Shurgard on Northern Blvd -- you can see it from the N train between 39th Ave. and QBP, just before you get to 39th. Ave.  A long time before that I used Shurgard in Brooklyn, and I didn't have any complaints about the company either time.  I think they've just merged with another storage company, though, so I don't know what's changed.

I do remember the loading/unloading area to be kind of cramped, and since the access hours were limited, it was sometimes a bit of a pain jockeying for a good loading position.  But that might be true in a lot of places.
