Rumors began circulating at New York Radio Message Board and other places that Jack FM was toast, and CBS Radio would soon be returning Oldies to 101.1 fm.  Whether that means any or all of the jocks from "CBS-FM" would be returning was unclear.  And whether you'd be getting hits from 50's 60's and 70's, or a new audio pattern, was also unclear.  Maybe you'll be getting 1964 (Beatles) thru some point in the early 80's (as the definition of what an "oldie" is may be changing) or even the terminology would now be "Classic Hits" as in other markets.
