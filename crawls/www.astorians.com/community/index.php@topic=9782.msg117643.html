The violation of citizen rights is the provisions allowing roving wiretaps without court approval, and other such wiretaps that do not require search warrants. I believe it is a violation of citizen rights to circumvent warrants, because they exist so that the judicial system can protect citizens by judging whether or not reasonable cause for search and surveillance exists.
There is also the immigration provision that allows detainment of any alien, legal or illegal, without due process or trial. Granted, they are not American citizens, but if they are subject to our laws, they should be subject to our trial process as well.
