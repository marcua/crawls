In addition to the great work that OutAstoria is doing for the social fabric of our community, other things seem to be taking shape like the weekly Monday Happy Hour, etc. 

Good news, in cooperation with hellgatesocial, we will be starting a new Saturday event called OPEN THE DOOR this Saturday Oct. 21.
