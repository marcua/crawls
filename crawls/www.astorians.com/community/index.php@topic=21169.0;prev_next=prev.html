Thanks for the recs - I really appreciated it.
My bad experience at GiGi's was more than just a bad cut. I went in for a bangs trim with my stylist. He's been cutting my bangs quicker and quicker over the last few months. As soon as he's done, he exclaims that I look fabulous - and sends me on my way. He's actually stopped checking the cut for evenness (sp?). Anyway, I go home to find that they are completely wonky.
I was going to my sister's wedding in a few days, and I needed to have it fixed. I called ahead of time to explain my situation. The receptionist said that my stylist needed to correct the cut. I explained my situation and told her that I couldn't wait until he was in again b/c I had to go to the wedding. After putting me on hold for a few moments, she came back on the line and said that they could fix it, that I could come in now.

When I got there, she told me that nobody was available, and that I might have to wait about 30-40 minutes. I told her that I had just called. She then added that I would be charge for the bangs correction b/c it wouldn 't be with my stylist.
I asked why she didn't inform me of that when I had called. I told her that someone needs to correct this, that it was not my fault, and that I didn't think I should be charged. She left the desk and within seconds - I mean seconds, she came back with a stylist who then did a great job of correcting the bangs. I think I'm lucky that she didn't make things worse out of spite. But seriously, she was very professional.

I'm not going to lie - I was pretty pissed, and I made it known in the tone of my voice. I did not yell. That's not my style.
Anyway, I'm so embarrassed and angry over the whole thing that I will never go back there to get my hair cut.

I'm not trashing GiGi's. They are a nice bunch. I'm just sorry that the stylist behind the reception desk has no understanding of customer service in regards to correcting a mistake that their salon made. Very disappointing.
