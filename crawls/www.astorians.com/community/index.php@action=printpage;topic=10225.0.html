Title: Whiffleball in Astoria On Sale Today!
Post by: ZogSports on April 21, 2008, 10:57:18 AM
Registration has finally started for our first ever co-ed Astoria WHIFFLEBALL league!

Kick it old school this summer with this 5-on-5 league (3 men - maximum; 2 women - minimum, in the field at all times) with everyone running bases with force outs and pegging.  Games will run about 50 minutes with no inning limit.

The league starts in early June.  Games will be on Monday-Thursday evenings between 6:30 and 8:30pm (Most games will start at 7:00pm).   

As always, a portion of all league fees, as well as 15% of what participants spend at the post-game happy hour partner bar, will go towards charities chosen by our teams. 

We can't think of a better way to get outside and meet new Astorians this summer! 

Registration is on NOW!   Details and how to register can be found here: http://www.zogsports.org/activity_details/162
