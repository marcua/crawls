Astoria's Music

Waltz in the City
 
Saturday, January 20, 2007

8:00PM
 
Location:
 
Merkin Concert Hall
@ Kaufman Center
Goodman House
129 West 67th St.
NYC, NY
 


Waltz in the City
Astoria's Music

Waltz-Astoria is hosting a very special event . Number one in a series of concerts that will be showcasing artist from
the outer boros of NYC.

Our first concert is of course , our very own Astoria based artists & we are happy to announce that tickets will be on sale at Waltz-Astoria in limited amounts for both the show and the post concert reception for our Astoria "Freinds of Waltz"

The show includes

Opening act : The Day Manolete was Killed
( a fantastic new band out of Astoria )
They are a driving mid-west flavored mix of heartfelt Americana and pure fun.

Warren D. Kimmel's Zen Roses
Astoria's own singer-songwriter returns with a collection of musicians that will make you a believer, Zen Roses reaches out and takes the audience into the heart and soul of this band.

The Veltz Family
This family band , lives and works very close to home for Waltz Astoria, they hit the high harmony's and depth of the human condition, songs from those places only a ture Rock and Roll family could pen.

Hosted By Our Own
Matt Taylor
Matt is the host of the best comedy night in NY, Waltz-Astoria's " Agents don't come to Astoria " , where Matt manages to bring the best comics working in NY today .
Many if not all of them hail from Astoria and have made Waltz the palce to be on Tuesdays

Tickets w/ reception & VIP seating
$50.00 in advance at Waltz-Astoria

General Admission Tickets available @ $30.00 at the box office , or @ Waltz-Astoria

www.Waltz-Astoria.com
 
We also have sponsor packages available for any business owners in the Astoria/LIC area
e-mail Music@waltz-astoria.com for details.
