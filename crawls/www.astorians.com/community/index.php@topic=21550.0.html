Assume no power and no potable water for at least a week.  The bathtub water is for flushing and washing.  If you are good little recycler, and happen to have a butt-ton of empty 2-liter bottles like I do, wash them out and fill them.  It's probably a good idea to freeze as much water as practicable, as that will help extend the useful life of your freezer or picnic chest, and you can always use that water later after it melts.

If this thing fizzles out and we get nothing worse than big rain storm, just consider it a practice run.
