Title: Re: Create a blog
Post by: megc on October 05, 2006, 11:54:42 AM
I use Blogger and really like it.  There are more user-friendly permissions you can set up to let only certain users read your output.

Wordpress is nice but sometimes seems a bit bloated and slow to load.

Movable Type is great but not free.  Even Typepad costs a bit.

There are older things like Diaryland and LiveJournal, which are more like, well, diaries.  I have one friend who has used both and continues to use Diaryland and likes it.  They look very no frills.  I don't really know what it's like to use LiveJournal.  I haven't really read too many of them to know what they have to offer that is unique from the others.  It's funny - the two LiveJournal users I know are both from Kansas!
