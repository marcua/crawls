I'm sorry for your loss.  We recently went through something similar and it wasn't much fun - especially because she left her littermate/best friend behind.  We got them through KittyKind at the Petco in Union Square and had a good experience with that organization

A couple of suggestions for a new furry friend:  Jumping Bulldog just did an adopt-a-thon (I believe yesterday) in partnership with the Mayor's Alliance.  You might check in with them for local recommendations.

Also, we just got our little guy a playmate (a 15 month old ball of energy) and actually got her through our Vet, Dr. Hatzi at All Pets.  One of the vet techs there helps adopt out cats and this one was a perfect match for us on the first try (I know, a rarity).  If you have a local vet it might not hurt to give them a call as I'm sure they would know if someone is trying to home some cats/kittens.
Lastly, if you don't mind taking the train I've heard that the North Shore Animal League is really great for adoptions and is a short walk from the LIRR.

Good luck!
