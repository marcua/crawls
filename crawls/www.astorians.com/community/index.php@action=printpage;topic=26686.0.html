Title: St. Pat's for All Parade: Invitation to march with Make Queens Safer
Post by: tgoldman on February 25, 2014, 01:40:09 AM

(http://makequeenssafer.org/home/wp-content/uploads/2014/01/cropped-Sign-With-Sky41.jpg)


An invitation for the schoolchildren of Queens, their families, and their neighbors:


Please join Make Queens Safer (http://makequeenssafer.org/home/) as we march at the St. Pat's for All Parade (http://www.stpatsforall.com/) on Sunday, March 2nd!


We will be celebrating St. Patrick's Day, the magnificent diversity of Queens, and the rare joy of strolling city streets without having to dodge traffic! And we will be marching to show support for urgently needed traffic and pedestrian safety initiatives throughout our borough.


We'll be gathering at 47th & Skillman in Sunnyside at 1pm on Sunday, March 2nd. Look for the Make Queens Safer banner, and don't forget to wear green!
