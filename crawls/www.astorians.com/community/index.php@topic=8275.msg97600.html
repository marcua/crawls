Just because other countries have problems with their national health care, doesn't mean the US is bound to repeat those problems.  We have the benefit of all the examples (EVERY other westernized nation in the world) that have socialized medicine to design one without the pitfalls they have.

Here's my point...
We live in a SOCIETY.  A society consists of lots of people.  Part of living in a society is contributing to society so we ALL have certain benefits.  Part of that contribution is paying taxes.  Roads, schools, defense, free network television, fuel, medicaid, social security, etc...
