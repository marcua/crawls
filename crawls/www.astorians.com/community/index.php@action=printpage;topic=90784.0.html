Title: Hebrew School and High Holy Days at the Free Synagogue of Flushing
Post by: tgoldman on September 03, 2018, 12:41:22 PM

Come welcome 5779 with the Free Synagogue of Flushing!


The Free Synagogue of Flushing (https://freesynagogueflushing.org/) stands proudly in the midst of one of the most ethnically diverse neighborhoods in the world, an enduring center of Jewish culture. Founded in 1917, it is the oldest Reform Synagogue in Queens. True to the ideals of its founders, FSF is an all inclusive, vibrant community dedicated to equality, freedom, social action and commitment to Torah.


Join us in worship in our beautiful, historic sanctuary surrounded by the beauty of stained glass windows. Be welcomed by our open congregation.


Join us in our holiday celebrations and take part in the many educational, cultural and social events The Free Synagogue of Flushing has to offer.


- No tickets needed for High Holiday services... just call (718-961-0030) or email the office (freesynagogue@freesynagogueflushing.org) to reserve your seats. Here's the full schedule for High Holy Days services (http://freesynagogueflushing.org/wp-content/uploads/sites/78/2018/08/FSF-High-Holy-Day-Schedule-5779.pdf). Children are welcome!  And in the coming weeks, we'll be celebrating Sukkot and Simchat Torah as well.


- Please join us on Sunday mornings for our Hebrew School and Family Education programs, beginning on September 16th. Please call the office (718-961-0030) for more information.


- Just three blocks from the #7 train at Main Street.
