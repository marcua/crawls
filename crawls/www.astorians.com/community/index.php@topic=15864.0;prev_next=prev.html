Does anyone recall the name of two bygone Pizzerias in the Ditmars area.

One was on the SE corner of 31 St. and Ditmars Blvd.  It was under the old Famous Fashion.  You could order your pizza directly from a window on 31 St. and I believe one could walk into the shop from the corner of the two streets (but I'm not sure about being able to walk in).  "Leonard's" comes to mind as the name but I'm not sure.

The other pizzeria was on the SE corner of 31 St. and 23 Ave.  This was a walk-in pizzeria with tables.  "John's" of "Johnnies" comes to mind for this one but again I'm not sure.

If anyone recalls, would love to know.

Thanks.

Nick

Half Moon Pizza was below Famous Fashion. Lisa's Pizza World was at 31 St and 23 Ave and can be seen in the opening credits of Archie Bunker's Place along with many other bygone businesses. I remember Champion Pizza and Donuts next to Trade Fair on Ditmars (which was Met Food) where Golden Chopstick is. GC was a liquor store. Pizza Palace did occupy L'Incontro's space but before that it was a paint store and a pet store next to it. DiDio's pizza on Steinway and Ditmars was a great spot too.
