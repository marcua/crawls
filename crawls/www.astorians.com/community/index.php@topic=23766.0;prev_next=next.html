Lots of places will pick up furniture to be donated.  Housing Works, The Salvation Army and The VVA all do free pick-ups.

Housing Works:
http://www.housingworks.org/donate/free-furniture-pickup/

The Salvation Army:
http://satruck.org/search

VVA:
http://www.pickupplease.org/donation-pickup?gclid=CPXj5ar34LMCFU-d4AodYCwA1Q

Also, www.taskrabbit.com will pick it up and drop it off anywhere you tell them to the same day.  (But they're not free.) 

Hope this helps.
