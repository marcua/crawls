Title: Kabab Cafe
Post by: Yaxpac on June 23, 2009, 03:21:31 PM
I had been wanting to go back to Kabab Cafe since Ali had returned from Spain.  I have never had a bad meal there but Ali did seem to be a little tired prior to his vacation and the food seemed to suffer. 

Well, I am very glad that he took a break because his food is back to the excellent level.  He has new items on the "menu" and his spark seemed to be back. 

Last weekend we had an exceptional meal at Kabab Cafe.  Ali always treats us like old friends and it is nice to be welcomed like that to any restaurant.  He seems to have many loyal followers.

Anyway, he had a new appetizer that was fantastic.  It was a duck ragu over a rice dumpling.  The rice dumpling had a date mashed in the middle of it.  This dish was very very tasty.  Other standouts was an entree of shredded lamb over rice and roasted vegetables but that description doesn't really do it justice. 

I have not seen Kabab Cafe mentioned here recently and I wanted to keep Ali top of mind as a true Astoria treasure.  He also said that he will be introducing several new duck dishes as he is shooting a new show with Jamie Oliver...
