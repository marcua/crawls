That's so killin.  I wish I could go.  I actually grew up listening and playing Barry Manilow(because of my mom, of course).  He is probably one of my earliest musical influences.  What a lot of people don't realize about him is that he is a world class musician.  He writes his songs(or should I say THE songs), sings them, plays the piano, and arranges!  It's pretty rare to find musicians who play all of those roles, and play them well.  A side note, I actually passed him going into work the other day......he was coming out the door as I was going in.  There is a post-production studio in our building, so maybe he was doing something for a new movie.  Enjoy the show!
