Just needed to vent.  Took me over an hour to get to the office this morning (my normal commute is about 25 minutes) due to our friends at the MTA and a broken down W at Astoria Blvd then again at 30th Ave.  The conductor told everyone to get off at 30th Avenue and take the train back to Astoria Blvd and to cross the platform for the express train.  The sad thing is that he told everyone to NOT go downstairs as the train would open its doors on both sides.  Something made me not believe him so I stayed on the train to Ditmars and just started all over but it really irked me to see 100's of people (literally) cross the platform as they had been told to do only for the train doors to only open on the TriBoro side of the train.  You'd think the MTA would actually say something follow through rather than making tons of people late for work.  Fortunately I have some flexibility with my arrival time at work but heard tons of people having to call their bosses frantically explaining the train situation.

For the record, the MTA didn't put any sort of "Sevice Advisory" on their website about this.

Anyhow, I just felt like venting and hope that everyone on the N line was able to make it to work okay.
