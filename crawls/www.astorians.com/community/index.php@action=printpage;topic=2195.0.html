Title: Bike trails in Queens!
Post by: Christine on May 23, 2006, 12:12:41 PM
Made out of (get this) real dirt!  :lol:

Help build the first NYC Parks bike trails in Cunningham and Highbridge. Meet other trail bikers and get some free stuff (like t-shirts and snacks  8-) )

Bring gardening gloves and sturdy shoes. Learn about the flora and fauna that thrives in the midst of urban sprawl, and get a relaxing nature fix while working the upper body.

Work parties generally last 9am-1pm, but if you come earlier/later, that's no big deal. We're just thrilled to have extra help. Groups can range from three to over a dozen people.

More info about upcoming work parties: www.climbonline.org and www.nycmtb.com
