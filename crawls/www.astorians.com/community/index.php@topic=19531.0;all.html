If you're going to leave it outside, you could get a wide roll of shrink wrap plastic from Staples or a moving store, and wrap the unit super tight with multiple layers. Wrap enough layers of that stuff around it and it will be water proof. You could then get a bbq grill cover (or something else) and put that over the wrapped unit. My grill cover is thick canvas on the inside and waterproof rubber on the outside, which gives it a sort of padded effect. That could help further protect the unit from potential debris damage. Good luck!
