Post by: globewalker on December 09, 2007, 09:29:26 AM
Call the police station to find out when it can be done - explain its for the Peace Corps and they are much more willing to help you out and do it.  It should only take a few minutes but be sure they do a good job (smudges will get you back to square 1!).  Oh and just in case you have prints on file for another reason (ie, your job, your parents did it when you were a kid, etc....they don't care and you have to have it done again!).  I don't believe there was a charge when I did it, then again that was in 2000. FYI - I was a PC Volunteer and had a great experience, you can PM me if you want more info.  Good luck!
