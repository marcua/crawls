Reveries of Rosanna and the River Keeper

(1/1)

Photographs by Astorian poet Paul Takis will be on display at the Freeze Peach cafe beginning Saturday, September 18. The show, titled 'Reveries of Rosanna and the River Keeper' will run two weeks, through October 2.



The Freeze Peach is located at 22-00 29th St. near the corner of Ditmars/29th just beyond the Tara Nail Salon.

Hours: Noon til Midnight daily.
