For over 12 years I have admired Daisy, the Proprietor of the former Newton Cleaners which was located on Ditmars Blvd near 31 Street.   Daisy always had a friendly smile.  She listened to whatever you said.  I would bring tons of dry cleaning and it was never a problem. She always had a rack of really nice clothing that no one picked up for a long period of time.  A good quality blouse for $2.00 was the best bargain ever. I was really sad when the store closed.  I was surprised and glad to see her again at the Dry Cleaners across from ABC Dept store on 31 Street.
