Lucid dreaming is just being aware that you are dreaming. So you can choose to either go with the flow of the dream (which a lot of people do) or you can try and control the dream. People who master it say that it's a good way to stop nightmares or to do fun things they can't do in real life. That site I linked at the top of the thread has lots of really good information on getting started. I've been journaling my dreams for 4 nights now, but still haven't gone lucid yet. Lucid dreaming just means you're aware enough to make a choice as to whether you want to follow or lead the dream.
