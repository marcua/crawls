Hi all,

I've written two full manuscripts for potential novels.  The first has been edited and is in the rewrite stage, the second needs some major repairs, such as a coherent ending (those help).  I have ideas for a third novel (ideas presently in the form of 2,100 scattershot words), and I plan to participate in National Novel Writing Month (all of November): http://www.nanowrimo.org

Let's see... I intermittently write freelance for a magazine group located in L.A. (paid, thankfully), and I'm an editor with an indie press based out of Portland, OR.  I'm compiling and editing a short story anthology for them right now, actually, titled "Falling From the Sky"... give us a visit at http://www.anothersky.org.  It's a highly worthwhile venture - a different kind of publishing paradigm - and I hope to have the aforementioned first novel out with them next year...

And I would be happy to exchange works of fiction, be it short story or novel chapters/excerpts.
