I decided that I really liked the place after my fourth or fifth time going there. A guy was pretty drunk and trying to pick a fight with just about anybody. Title: Re: McCaffrey and Burke

    I heard a few people refer to it as "Mcaffrey and Jerks", so I guess it does have a bit of a negative reputation in the neighborhood.  However,  I have gone there every now and again over the years, and I never had a problem.  Some people seem friendlier than others, and the bartenders always seemed friendly and quick with the drinks.  There does seem to be a bit of a clique-ish crew that goes there every now and again, they tend to stay near the back of the bar, near the pool table, but I never saw them start any problems with strangers, especially not just for the sake of causing trouble.  I would say if a stranger goes in and doesn't act up, they shouldn't have a problem.
