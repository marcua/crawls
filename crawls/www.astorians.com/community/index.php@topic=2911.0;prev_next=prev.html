Garbage... this is my favorite complaint

i think there should be an ad campaign that informs people how much the city spends on cleaning up garbage and how just saving a small % of that money could then be spent on other things like improving parks, more trees on streets, improving libraries/schools/playgrounds... whatever...
I just can't stand the attitude "it's someone else's job to clean up after me."

Next - garbage cans... the thing is ... they don't always help - in fact, sometimes they make the situation worse.  My parents have a live across the street to the entrance of a state beach.  There's a bus stop and a bench right be the entrance.   A few years ago the town added a garbage can.  Unfortunately that can didn't get emptied enough and soon that corner became a magnet for garbage.  It didn't matter how full the can was, people would continue to dump their refuse.  After a couple of seasons of complaints by the locals - either the town took away the can - or more likely a neighbor who was fed up with the garbage stole the can.  Problem solved.  People don't dump their garbage there any more. 

I wonder if the disappearing cans are due to a) pranks or b) people sick of the garbage that piles up and overflows from the cans. 

Another question - who thought it was a good idea to design a can that is made up of holes??? Those metal... I don't know how to describe it...   you know... it's just like... chain link sort of... just holes... they don't put bags in them and then the small garbage goes in and then falls out. 

And... have the "Big Bellies" (I think that's the name), the solar powered compactors helped the situation?? I'm sure most people don't even know what they are so they don't even use them. 

i think i'm done
