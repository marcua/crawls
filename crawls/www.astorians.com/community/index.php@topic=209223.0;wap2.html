Newtown Literary, a semi-annual print and electronic journal based in Queens and publishing poetry, fiction, and creative non-fiction from Queens writers is seeking submissions for Issue 16 through January 10th, 2020. Submitting is free (there is also an additional option to pay for written feedback from an editor) and can be done through the website at the following link: https://www.newtownliterary.org/submit.


Please feel free to forward this information to anyone who might be interested in submitting work to us.


Thank you for your time and consideration.


Sincerely,


Allison, Associate Editor
