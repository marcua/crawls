Thanks for the birthday wish Meg...I had a great day!

Yes I did live in Northern California for a little over a year while in the U. S. Coast Guard. I was stationed in Petaluma, about 40 miles north of San Francisco. It is one of the most beautiful places I've ever had the pleasure of living. (thank you U.S. Govt) The base was actually on a National Wildlife Preserve. Sometimes marching from the barracks down to the school we would have to wait for the deer to cross. They weren't bothered by us at all and would just take their time...it was awesome! Everything was so green and lush. I've got so many great memories from there....a bunch of us too broke to fly home for Thanksgiving eating at a tiny  Italian restaurant off Hwy 101 (there are few things more depressing than eating a holiday dinner at a military messhall), Christmas day having a picnic and bonfire at the beach - so much fun! And don't get me started on the wine-tasting trips...Johannesberg Reisling from the Robert Mondavi vineyard will always have a special place in my heart  :-)
