Yeah, you can't go wrong with LL Bean's, but I wear Lowa waterproof hikers. I know you said you don't want hiking boots, but in this weather you need something that grips well on slippery surfaces, is waterproof and will keep your little toesies warm. Timbies I only wear when I'm stomping around in the dirt in my yard. They're not that great-not the regular ones that you see construction guys wearing. They're too stiff and the soles are slippery.
