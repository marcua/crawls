What is Laban Movement  Analysis?
Movement  Analysis is a method for observing, describing, analysing and understanding movement.
Through LMA you can understand your body preferences in different componentes: BODY, EFFORT, SHAPE AND SPACE (BESS) and explore your Body Mind connections.
When you do Movement Analysis you have two methods at one time. You get Laban Movement Analysis and the Bartenieff Fundamentals exercises designed to facilitate integration and expression in movement.
The fundamentals are based on anatomical principles that work with alignment,  coordination,strength, flexibility, mobility and kinesthetic awareness. They will also reduce physical stress and injury.
When you combine Laban Movement Analysis and Bartenieff Fundamentals you will get greater understanding of yourself and others as well as be able to enhance the quality of all kinds of movement and performances.

This class will give the opportunity to explore your own ways to move.
You will  learn; create movement;  and understand how to relax and reduce stress through everyday  movement.
This class is appropriate to everyone, because this is a new language.
From theater, dance, phisycal education, music, therapy,athletics,non-verbal communication, cross-cultural or  just to understand  movement  you will have a great benefit of this class, come and see it.
