So we checked this place out today!

We were super excited, because though we love Brooklyn Bagel and Astoria Bagels, we really did need another bagel place in this area, in my opinion. Brooklyn Bagel is delicious, but it always has a line out the door and it's hard to find a seat. I like Astoria Bagels because it's right next to my house and cheap, but they don't cook eggs. Neither place is open late though, so you can't just go hang out with a coffee in the evening (which you can't at our Starbucks either). This new place is open till 10pm! yay!

First thought upon walking in - terrible music (which Brooklyn Bagel also suffers from), TVs (which we don't like because you end up just staring at the TV... also when we were there they were showing Dr. Oz and something about intestinal parasites, not good to watch while eating, AND it was on Fox... BUT I can understand the really early morning crowd probably wants to watch the news there... but seriously, Fox?)
Second thought - hats. I felt like I was in Brooklyn... I guess its kind of cute, but I can't help thinking everyone wearing this cute matching fedora hats... is dumb. We're in Astoria. Just got a super hipster impression. Lots of seats though!! And the interior is really nice.

Food and coffee though - delicious! Pricier than the same order would be at Brooklyn Bagel, and we ordered mini bagels. Note - the mini bagels are really small. These aren't Brooklyn Bagel mini sizes. They are small. Next time we'll be ordering the regular size. They were super tasty though. We got two everything bagels, one with veggie cream cheese, and one with ham egg and cheese. Yum! The iced coffee was great too, and we liked that you can put your own sugar and cream in, so you don't have to worry about it being too sweet. Pretty sure there's a bathroom in the back too, always good. And the pastries looked awesome.

Anyway, there is my first impression. I will definitely be back!!!

The bagels don't have the weird hint of sweetness that other shops' bagels have, and the nova is sliced thinner and more tender. They're no cheaper than the other stores - maybe even a little more expensive.
