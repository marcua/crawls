I would say that much of it will depend on what you consider 'high end designer' and what you think the store's overall style will lean towards.  In other words, I would imagine that you as the store owner have your own look that you tend to prefer, and that in turn will probably dictate the type of 'designer' clothing you will want to carry.

Will it be minimalist, asymmetrical Japanese designer stuff or one of a kind stuff found at thrift stores like Tokio 7 in the EV?  If so, I don't know that folks who'd dress that way would be interested in 'renting' clothing as they tend to be very original in how they compose their own outfits.  Such people usually can make 'new outfits' simply by creatively using what's already in their closets.

I would think that perhaps the Latinas in the nabe (i.e., the ones who go to that fancy Puerto Rican club/restaurant on 31st Street near Newtown?) and/or the particular type of Greek women who are sort of into the Euro club/look thing...in other words women who are really into their appearance, high heels, designer bags, etc., ...that they might be your best bet...and featuring sort of slinky or flashy designer type clothes.

Then the question would be where would you want to have such a shop?  Probably not on Steinway...;-) ...you want a location that feels a bit more elegant for such special ladies.  ;-)
