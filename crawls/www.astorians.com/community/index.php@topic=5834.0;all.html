21st St. is getting very residential in the area between B'way and Astoria Park, but I doubt that the section between B'way and the bridge (with the exception of Ravenswood) will ever get residential, although now that Queens Plaza is getting residential, you never know.  It's a very gritty industrial/commercial area with few amenities (aside from the car washes!).

On the bright side for that area, a brand new public library just opened up, replacing the two smaller branches in Ravenswood and Queensbridge.  And the KFC that closed became a bank (Amalgamated), one of the few banks in the area.  There's also a new storage facility, which opened up within the last year.
