acrylic tips - bad for your nails. and kinda old school, thick and clunky and smelly
UV gel nail tips - i have this right now and i LOVE them. its the closest thing to natural you can get if your going to get anything over your nails. its a bit expensive and a bit time consuming but the results last and you don't have to apply polish and they stay shiny the whole time.
silk wrap -  not bad, this along with fiberglass is pretty much the same i think. i had these for a few years. they are pretty durable, but dont let your nails do much breathing.

you can ask them to just to a regular gel manicure. thats applying the gel that that they would use for a silk/powder job but not add anything else. its relatively thin and natural and may be a good place good place to start. make sure they dont do the uv, just a gel mani.

i've been doing these things to my nails for about 10 years, my nails are paper. they pretty much lose all strength when you do these things too. mine were hopeless. when i do take everything off for a rest time ( a month or two every year) mine wont pass my finger level just cause they bend and break.

anything else? ask away!
