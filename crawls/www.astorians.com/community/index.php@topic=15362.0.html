I don't know if it's still there, but there was a Curves on Ditmars Blvd. I think it was between 32nd -33 Streets.
I'm a member of the Rock and I love it. It's well run, with great classes and equipment.

They do participate in one of the insurance programs - I think it's Global Fit. Also, if you can pay upfront in cash, they will give you a cash discount. I do this and it works for me.

They also offer discounts when you belong to certain unions - I think SAG, firemen, police, and  maybe teachers, but I'm not sure about whether they still offer this.

If you have more questions about, feel free to PM  me.

There's also the new yoga studio on 31 St., The Giving Tree. It's run by a former Rock yoga instructor and her husband. You could opt for a series of their classes and get some home equipment to supplement.

There's planet fitness on steinway if you're willing to go that far, they're only $20 per month, plus you can bring a guest anytime you go no charge, free tanning, and a bunch of other stuff. 

Potential let downs are the free weights only go up to 60 and they don't have barbells and such.  So there's only so much you can do. 

I run a work out program in Astoria Park if you're interested.  We do cardio, endurance training, strength training, and flexibility, and we like to vary it up, so there are new challenges every day.  Doing it with a group and instructor helps keep motivation high, as opposed to the all too common unused gym membership.
