Halloween Fun at Creators' Co-Op: Oct 30th
« on: October 25, 2011, 10:04:15 AM »
Halloween Fun At Creators’ Co-Op! 26-16 23rd Avenue, Astoria   Sunday, October 30th 1-4pm

Kids of all ages!  Come join us for the afternoon of Halloween fun!  There will be costume contest, complimentary drinks and snacks, and super cool activity books by Lazoo will be given out while supply lasts.  You can make your own princess wand or mask ($10pp) while waiting for your turn on the free face painting!
For the craft, please RSVP by emailing creatorscoop@gmail.com to ensure we will have enough material for you.

- kids’ costume contest at 2:30pm
- free face painting
- craft table ($10pp): princess wand/mask making - register now by email
- (non-alcoholic) refreshment: Dead Elvis
- complimentary activity books by Lazoo
- complimentary little snacks by Foodoni
