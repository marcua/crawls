I don't have any leads for you, but you should know that the E only stops at Steinway late nights (12am-5am). You could take the local (R or M on weekdays) to either Queens Plaza or Roosevelt and get the E there (you didn't mention which direction you were traveling). If you're heading toward the city during rush hour, getting the E at Queens Plaza is...challenging. It gets really, REALLY packed.
