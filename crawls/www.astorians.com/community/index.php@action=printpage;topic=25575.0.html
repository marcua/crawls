Title: QCA Presents An Evening of Performances by Judith Sloan & Warren Lehrer
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on November 22, 2013, 01:28:15 PM
Artists in Queens now have a unique independent creative space where the artistic and cultural communities in the borough can come together to talk shop, attend workshops, network and share their works in progress in the 3rd Space (http://queenscouncilarts.org/3rd-space/) at the Queens Council on the Arts. 

The next 3rd Space event features Judith Sloan and Warren Lehrer performing excerpts from their work. The performance fuses the art of theatre, radio, and poetry. A Q&A between the artists and audience will follow.

3rd Space is a series of monthly events that invites local artists to host events to build strong and vibrant artist networks and to creatively lead the continuing transformation of the borough's cultural economy.

For more details visit: http://queenscouncilarts.org/performance-and-proccess-with-warren-lehrer-and-judith-sloan/ (http://queenscouncilarts.org/performance-and-proccess-with-warren-lehrer-and-judith-sloan/)

Friday, December 6th from 7pm - 9pm
Queens Council on the Arts
37-11 35th Ave, Entrance on 37th Street, Astoria, NY 11101
