In no particular order, here are some of my kids' faves:

Central Park (the Heckscher playground, followed by a ride on the carousel, and sometimes the CP zoo)

Staten Island Ferry (best free entertainment in the city)

NY Hall of Science (but bring your own food!)

Queens Zoo petting zoo and the Playground for All Children which is in the same corner of the park

Museum of Natural History (don't miss the whale room and the dinosaurs)

If you have a car and don't mind the drive, the Brooklyn Children's Museum is a pretty great place for kids too

I would strongly recommend against the Children's Museum of Manhattan - that place is a nightmare!!
