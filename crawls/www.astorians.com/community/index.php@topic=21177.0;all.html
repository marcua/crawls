Went last night.  No complaints at all.  The service was perfect and very friendly and attentive.  There are menus but frankly, I didn't ask if delivery was up and running yet - or their delivery territory.  I had the Ostrich burger and it was cooked perfectly.  I like that they offer the burgers in a whole wheat wrap.  This place is a huge hit already.  People waiting outside and every seat taken.  It's so nice to see this much activity on that corner for a change.  I can only hope it will have a ripple affect.  I bet it's no coincidence that Los Amigos - nearby Mexican - gave themselves a nice little facelift.  Maybe something cool will open in the old OTB.  The only thing Bareburger might have done differently would be to have nothing but tables for two that could be either joined or separated.  There were a lot of couples (us too) that were taking up tables for four - keeping other people waiting.  Otherwise I'm really glad this opened in that location.
