I had a post about the Nutella hot chocolate that must have been lost in the server outage - I tried it a couple of weeks ago and really liked it.  It's not in your face, but I found it did have a really nice level of subtle Nutella flavour that was just right for me.  Then again, I don't like things like flavoured coffee or so-called "coffee drinks" like "pumpkin spice latte" or what have you, so I appreciated the understatement.  It was definitely noticeable, though.

Just wanted to let everyone know that Il Bambino now has opened their outoor patio/garden in the back.  I was there this afternoon and thought it was lovely.  They do plan to install awnings for shade, because it can get warm back there mid-day.  Anyway, it's great!   Worth a visit for sure.

They added a bunch of new things to the menu, too.  I would recommend especially the spicy avocado and goat cheese crostini, as well as the "salsa formaggi" crostini.  The carrot and feta salad is also delicious.  I'm a big fan of the "Rain" white wine - it's delightful.

This is a great addition to Astoria's summer outdoor restaurant spaces!
