A 27-year-old woman said she was held at knifepoint and then sodomized Sunday night.

The alleged victim was walking on 26th Street around midnight when, she said, a man in dark clothing approached her. The woman said the man demanded money, which she handed over.

Anyone with information is encouraged to call Crime Stoppers at 1-800-577-TIPS.
