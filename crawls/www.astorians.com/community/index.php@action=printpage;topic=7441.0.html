Title: Re: Does anyone know where can I get an inexpensive mattress in Astoria?
Post by: Pawgny on August 11, 2007, 03:54:42 PM
Call 1800-mattres(s), know the make/brand of what your looking for.  Tell them you were quoted a price(from Uncle Vinny's Bed Barn) & ask if they can beat it.  They'll put you on hold & then come back with a reduced price.  Ask if you can get their extension & to hold the price (To call back later, which you won't) .  Call Again (a different Op. will pickup) give the same scenario & give them a lower quoted  price & WORK IT!

I got a $1400 set for $503 this way.  :mrgreen:
