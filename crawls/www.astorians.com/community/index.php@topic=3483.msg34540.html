From what Ive gathered over the years the problem stems from a few issues.

1. Very often scaffolding is left up after work is done because advertising makes the building money and scaffolding rentals are cheap.
2. Permits expire and no one is there to check / enforce it.
3. The amount of inspectors for site inspections has been slashed over the years.
4. And once again there is tons of money to be had from advertising, in the city at least.

5. And I guess you could argue that the building owner spends money out of his/her pocket so that neighbors can get out of the rain and have place to park their bikes.  That and Republicans are really benevolent caretakers of planet Earth.
