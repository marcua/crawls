Title: Re: 24th ave and 26th st
Post by: JAC on July 23, 2012, 12:31:18 PM
It's a generally safe area. Obviously crime can hit anywhere so I'd never say anywhere is 100% safe.
There is a wine bar on the corner of 26th street and 24th ave. There a many laundromats, an ice cream shop and a deli. Headed down 24th ave toward the park you will find a Thai and an Italian restaurant and small pharmacy. You're around 1/2 mile from grocery shopping an the main Ditmars shopping area and about 1/2 mile walk from the 30th ave area where there are many restaurants.
I have lived in the area over 4 years and like it very much but if you're looking for excitement and a young vibe, this is not that area. It's more families and elderly with some younger folks mixed in. A lot of people live around this area to take advantage of the elementary school.
