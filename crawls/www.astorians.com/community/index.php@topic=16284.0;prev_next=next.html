I would second Mojave and Vesta. Both have menus with a little something for all, reasonable and nice atmosphere.

Brick is good too. The lounge in back serves food - I think you can order off the dinner menu. It's casual, relaxed and
a good crowd. I personally don't care for the food, but I'm a regular at the bar on Fridays and everyone's eating and enjoying,
so I'd still check it out. The lounge is more relaxed than the restaurant is.

L'Incontro is wonderful - just took my boyfriend there for  his b-day dinner. Pricey but worth every delicious penny!
