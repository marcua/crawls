Title: Josh Irving Quintet at Beer Garden!
Post by: Jtrane on January 21, 2007, 01:19:16 PM
Live Jazz has made it to Astoria!  And what better place than the Beer Garden!

You all know where it is.  The music happens downstairs in the restaurant area.
Thursday, March 22.
8:30
This is a FREE show.  There's not even a drink minimum.  But you are encouraged to drink and eat.  That's how money is made to pay musicians and keep this wonderful series going.

With:
Danny Zanker — Bass
George Dulin — Keyboards
Alan Ferber — Trombone
Bryson Kern — Drums

It doesn't get anymore convenient that this to see good jazz for all you Astoria dwellers!  Please come out, not just this Thursday, but as many Thursdays as possible.  It's free, and it's great music!  Please support live music!
