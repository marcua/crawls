Title: Spring Softball in Astoria
Post by: AstoriaPlaysBall on February 26, 2014, 04:42:05 PM
Astoria Plays Ball will open spring sign-ups this Saturday, March 1st!



"Sign-ups will be open for almost 6 full weeks! In order to encourage early sign-ups, season dues  will be $15 if you sign-up March 1st through March 14th (This includes when you sign up in person at the Mixer) and increase to $20 from March 14th through April 11th."

You can go to www.astoriaplaysball.com for more information and also check out Facebook.com/astoriaplaysball
