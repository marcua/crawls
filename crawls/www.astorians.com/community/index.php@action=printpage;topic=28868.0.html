Title: Kidical Mass Family Biking Forum 1-31
Post by: Missmarty on January 25, 2015, 11:36:17 AM
Hi. If you've ever considered riding your kid around on a bike, or if your older kid is looking for some independence and new riding experiences, please attend our Forum next week in Jackson Heights. In collaboration with groups in Manhattan and Brooklyn, we will present a conversation and information about biking with Kids. Kidical Mass is a national movement for Family Biking. In better weather, we also host monthly rides, where a whole family can enjoy new scenery on two wheels, either riding your kid or doing something great together.
If you have experience you want to share, or have other questions, please get in touch with me, or check our Facebook page. https://www.facebook.com/kidicalmassnyc


There is also a link to the January event there. We will have some giveaways and information available for all ages.
