We went on Saturday night.  Really interesting interior - I thought the doorman was over the top but that might just be me. 


We ate the dragon chicken tempura app - kind of like fancy chicken fingers but that would be a gross misrepresentation.  They were DELICIOUS!  Light and crispy and tasty and the wasabi aioli was stupid good.  Hubby had the roast chicken main and I had the mussels.  The chicken was fine - nothing extraordinary - and the mussels were good.  I was surprised the mussels weren't served with anything like fries or bread.  And, I thought the portion was rather small for a main.  We didn't have dessert but it looked amazing.


The beer list was good, well curated.  The cocktails looked interesting. 


I still don't understand why one would name a restaurant after a landmine but that aside, it's worth a try, we will definitely be back.
