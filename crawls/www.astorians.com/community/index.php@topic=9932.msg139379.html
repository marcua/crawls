I live a few blocks away, and would eat there more regularly if it wasn't so damn expensive.  The prices are NOT right for the 'hood, and the rolls weren't anything extraordinary.  I didn't try the other menu items.  The place is perpetually empty-looking.  The food was marginally better than Go Wasabi, but that place had gone so far downhill in the past few years I'm shocked that it held on that long.  Bottom line:  WAY overpriced.  I could just as soon eat in Manhattan for that money and get better food (which wouldn't be hard to do).

I miss Dragon Gate--they were the only OK Chinese takeout in the area.  They had AMAZING spare ribs, but I digress.

OTOH there's a new cheapie Brazillian riodizio place next to them, next door to Omega Wines and Spirits, that looks amazing.  It's also just ramping up crowdwise but I am friendly with the family that owns Omega and they all say it's delicious.
