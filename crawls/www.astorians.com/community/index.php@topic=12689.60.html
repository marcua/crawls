Last Tuesday night a group of five of us went for dinner to Vesta - a couple of friends made the trek from Long Island City - it was all of our first time.  We were all so pleased with the meal and overall experience and we'll definitely be back.

We ordered a margherita pizza, cheese plate and fennel and orange salad to start.  The pizza was cooked perfectly and we should have ordered another one for our table of five!  The manchego was great with the fig spread and the fennel (my favorite thing to put in salad) was light and fresh and dressed perfectly.  We had a bunch of different things for dinner and passed our plates around to taste - the favorites were the steak, salmon (the prune reduction with lentils was awesome!), and chicken with polenta.  Polenta is out of control good.   The only critique we had was for the linguini with breadcrumbs which was a little dry, but we asked for more olive oil an all was good. 

We were so happy with our wine selection that we ended up ordering four bottles - Aglianco - and didn't get to try anything else.  But we'll definitely be back - especially since I just moved into the neighborhood.  Looking forward to sitting at the bar for dinner, which seemed like the place to be the other night.
