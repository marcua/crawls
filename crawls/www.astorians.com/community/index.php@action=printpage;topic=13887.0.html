Title: Re: A good, reliable (inexpensive) printer
Post by: Harlan on February 04, 2009, 09:57:59 AM
Laser's are much cheaper over the long run. Toner is much less expensive than ink. You can buy a good desktop laser from Samsung for $100. Text quality is better than ink jet too. And on those rare occasions you need color, you can take your document to Kinko's and print it there, and it'll still be cheaper over the long run.
