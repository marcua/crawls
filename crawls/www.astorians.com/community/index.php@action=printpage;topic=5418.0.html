Title: Re: Tipping for an appliance delivery
Post by: NYCMacUser on March 03, 2007, 08:14:45 PM
If everything goes as expected, I would tip $20. Assuming there will be 2 people making the delivery, I would tip them each $10, separately. If they are also supposed to change the way the door(s) opens, I would add an extra $5. I believe even if you are paying a delivery charge, this is tough work. Check the refrigerator very carefully before they leave; make sure it is running, that there are no chips on the surface and that all the shelves and drawers are in place.
