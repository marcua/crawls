please come to this very important meeting! 

If you have a four year old, consider having your child tested for the program. here is the link to download an application to have your child tested, free of charge, for eligibility to the program.  http://schools.nyc.gov/Academics/GiftedandTalented/default.htm.  The deadline to apply is NOVEMBER 9

FYI:  there are big changes this year. First, the DOE is no longer guaranteeing seats to qualified students. Second, the DOE is no longer using the Bracken test and has replaced it instead with the Naglieri Nonverbal Reasoning Test. Third, there is basically no sibling preference anymore.
