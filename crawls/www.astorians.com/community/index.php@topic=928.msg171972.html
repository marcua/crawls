That's very interesting... I had the same addiction (to sleep) awhile back, and I'm not sure exactly why. Most people told me it was depression, which I guess at times it was. But at the same time my body seemed to need the long hours of complete Delta. I was also fascinated with the REM state and dreams in general... particularly the phenomenon labeled 'astral projection' which I nightly excercised at will... exploring the subconscious Realm consciously; Lucid dreaming, etc.

After awhile, I stopped doing this though because I was getting too fascinated by some of the powerful and all-consuming Archetypes of the Unconscious and felt I was being engulfed in that Sea. Also, some rogue complexes (splintered or supressed aspects of my personality) wandering around in my psyche were also quite disturbing and scary to look upon so I made a conscious decision to "close the door" after I had dealt with the more troubling of them. Unfortunately my shut off valve to that World (a known & proven inhibitor) was alcohol. So here I am... in Occlusion, struggling with demons of which I am only dimly aware. I feel that there is some form of synthesis available now, however... as time and some experience have enabled me to deal with some of these enigmas which have plagued humanity since the beginning of time.
