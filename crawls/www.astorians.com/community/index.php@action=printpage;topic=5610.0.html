Title: Re: Where to take my brother for dinner?
Post by: Jonathan on March 20, 2007, 10:50:59 AM
We went to Island, the new place by Cup. Full review with pics by Wednesday morning.
For now: 
Great bartender Brendan. Usually there fri and sat nights.  Good Manhattan and cocktails.
Beautiful space, two levels and nice lighting.
Good people run the place and staffing seemed very good.
Food is off to a so so start but it seems very promising. It is the first week after all.
Lots of small plates and half as many mains.
Lots of Med. influences and perhaps some Portuguese.  I say that because there were some dishes with chorizo or lardons with shellfish or octopus.
Prices were moderate to high.
