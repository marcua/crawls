I was shopping at the new party supply store across from Sandro's yesterday and we decided to give it a try.  I'm really glad we did.  This place has come a ways in year.  No more menu filled with awful looking photos.  Well, there are still photos but it's much better.  The place itself was remodeled a bit and is clean and comfortable.  Service is very friendly.  Easier if you have even a slight comfort level with Spanish.  The tamales are really delicious.  The inside is rich like a custard.  Really excellent.  The plantains with cheese are unbelievable.  Caramelized in a hot buttery pan or grill to perfection.  I had a mixed grill platter that was delicious.  Not fatty or greasy at all.  Not exactly prime cuts of meat.  But very satisfying and very tasty.  In short, I'm really glad I finally ate here and I will be back many times.
