I was on the W train at QBP that had door problems.  Totally rediculous.  I called the N train superintendant.  He was very receptive and understanding, actually.  He rides the trains himself, he told me.

He encouraged me to ask everyone who experiences trouble on the trains to call him and let him know what's going on!

http://www.straphangers.org/linesupers.html

The above link has phone numbers for all train superintendants.  I suggest calling the N train super for both N and W issues.

The only way they know people arent happy with service is by us, the riders, contacting them!

It only takes a few moments, and he actually took the time to listen.  He asked for my contact info so he could keep me up to date on improvements, and also gave me alternate contact info for him if I had other serious issues to notify him about.
