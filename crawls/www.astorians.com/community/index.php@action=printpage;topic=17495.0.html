So I decided to walk to my appointment up on Ditmars, from Broadway to check out the sights.  My route there was along Steinway and I took 31st back.  I made a few observations...

1.  Ladies - there are boots and then there are WINTER boots.  Today is the day you wear the winter ones. How do you know?  Well, crossing the street and trying to get across the huge puddle of slush will confirm if you've got the right pair on.  FYI I'm the one laughing behind you when you shriek "my foot got wet".  No *%&! there is a foot of snow out there!

2.  Rock salt helps - but only AFTER you shovel the snow and just have little bits of icy patches left.  I got quite a chuckle out of the Vitamin's Shop's attempt at clearing their snow away with a creative approach. 

3.  The snow/ice sliding off the awnings can't get you if you are under it.  Watch out people I had several close calls and I'm sure someone else would have appreciated the show had I gotten pelted.

All in all, great walk  and yes, most people have gotten their sidewalks cleared so no need to stay inside.  Just don't forget your WINTER boots :)
