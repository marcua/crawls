One of my favorite restaurants in Astoria...I have never been disappointed.  The atmosphere is not always cozy, the service is not always great, and the beverage choice is limited (I find that choosing the fresh squeezed lemon juice is usually the best option), but everything from the red mullet to the octopus is delicious, and can serve as at least two meals.

And after dinner across the street is a great bakery, Laziza, where they are happy to give out free samples of any one of the tasty items in the showcase to help you decide which ones to take home.
