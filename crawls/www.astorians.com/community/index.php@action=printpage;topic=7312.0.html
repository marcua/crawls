I like to get children's books that might have the child's name in the title. If it is a unique name, it might take a little longer, but my friend had a daughter ella, and we were able to find about 10 books that had her name. As a kid, having a book that has your name feels pretty cool. In each book, I dated it and put a little message for her. It is personal, and books are something that can be held onto for a while.
