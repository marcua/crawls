Given the number of folks around here who go out to eat and breadth of the places they visit, maybe we ought to have a thread for folks to post their particular, recent restaurant experience.  I thought it would be a good idea as I went out Crescent & Vine on Saturday, wanted to give it a hat tip, but I didn't feel it warranted a specific thread.

I'd propose the following thread guidelines:
1.  Personal recent experiences only.  Post up within a week.
2.  "Philosophical" discussions about the establishment be kept to a minumum (e.g. Applebees).
3.  Replies related to the review are encouraged, but seperate discussions are not.  Want to make it so folks can browse the thread and get reviews without too much clutter.
4.  Keep any criticisms within the forum's guidelines.

If anyone has anything to add, then that would be great.
