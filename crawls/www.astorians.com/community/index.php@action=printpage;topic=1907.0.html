Title: Re: Things to do in vegas
Post by: megc on April 07, 2006, 10:18:18 AM
I highly recommend taking some time to visit Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area (http://www.americansouthwest.net/nevada/red_rock_canyon/nca.html).  It's an easy drive from Las Vegas.  I have very fond memories of visiting this beautiful place.

The Old Spanish Trail (http://www.sunset.com/sunset/travel/article/0,20633,687507,00.html)
Sunset magazine (http://www.sunset.com) is an excellent resource for travel, food, etc. in the West.

If you can take the time, venture over into CA and visit Mitchell  (http://www.birdandhike.com/Hike/MOJA/Mitchell_Cav/Mitchell.htm)Caverns (http://www.desertusa.com/mnp/mnp_mc.html).  The Eastern Scenic Area of the Mojave Desert is absolutely beautiful.  The caverns are really fun!

Try checking out the Southwest and Western States (http://forums.egullet.org/index.php?showforum=131) forum on eGullet for food recommendations.

Then there's lasvegas.about.com (http://lasvegas.about.com/) for more tips and articles on Vegas.

Maybe watch Oceans 11? :lol:

Have fun!
