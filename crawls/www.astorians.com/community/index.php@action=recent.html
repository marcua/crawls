Just logging on to say how much this forum meant to me while I lived in Astoria, a place I still feel connected to even though I live all the way across the country in Portland, OR. I met some great people though these forums, attended some awesome Astorians events, learned about Freeze Peach here, and overall it gave me a sense of community. Big thanks to merm for running this forum and also to the merm-Meesh team who ran Freeze Peach. Rose Garden will forever be one of my favorite teas, too.


I know we have Facebook, Nextdoor, and other social media these days, but I maintain that hyperlocal forums and - for the olds among us - local BBSes are superior when it comes to community-building and connection. I still maintain friendships I built through those pre-social media online communities I frequented in the 90s and 2000s. But I also understand that merm needs to shut things down here, and I accept that.


So, adios everyone. Be well, stay safe, and love Astoria!
