I finally tried the Il Bambino brunch.  It is out of this world.  They stick with what they do best: Paninis.  They are creative and delicious.  I'm giving it my official brunch expert's thumbs up.   :mrgreen:  And the coffee was really good, too.  (And yes, they had the full menu also, including the soups.)

EDIT: Yes, they had mimosas but not Bloody Marys.  I think it's the whole liquor-vs.-wine-license thing.

I can't wait til the weather gets warm and I can sit outside in the back for brunch.
