I signed up to voluteer at the pit stop at Astoria park actually.
 
I rode the 5 borough last year and although i started with the "wow this is so insanely cool riding down the middle of these avenues" thought.  I ended up thinking "most of these people shouldn't even be near a bicycle - let alone riding in the middle of a crowd."  The overall experience was - yeah it was great once - but I didn't really have to do it again.  

But I highly recommend that those of you who like to ride - go for it - have a great time.  But if you don't feel so up to riding with 100s of others around sometimes tight corners - come join me as a volunteer.
