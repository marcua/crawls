Title: Re: Fire at 41st St/Newtown Road
Post by: Pulled pork on September 21, 2008, 07:56:15 PM
We live across the street from the fire location. We got home around 5:30pm to glass on the street, part of a tree cut down and lots of boards stacked up for covering windows.  Apparently a woman was at home on the third floor taking a nap. A fire started somewhere in the apartment and she was trapped. Her husband was downstairs (street level) and never smelled or heard a thing. Neighbors say the same.  She wanted to jump out the window to safety but fire department not there yet. Some neighbors came out with a big drop cloth and some other neighbors grabbed a mattress.  They held the drop cloth tight over the mattress and she jumped. Totally fine from the jump but went to the hospital for smoke and shock(?). Same for her husband.  This is all second hand but it's from two neighbors who were there and one of them got the drop cloth.  Sounds like a bunch of Heroes living on our block.  Anyway, looks like they will be boarding up the third floor for a demo job. No one said anything about the 1st or 2nd floors so not sure of their status.
