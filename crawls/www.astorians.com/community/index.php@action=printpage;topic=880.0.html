Title: Yay pilates!

It's taken me a while to finally get to the pilates place but I made it -- and it's pretty cool.  I took a session with Mary and have nothing but good things to say.

Having increased running miles lately, my muscles have been a little unpleasantly tight lately -- the cure to this seems to have been an hour or so with the reformer.  Scary name but pleasantly relaxing in the end.  It was my first pilates experience -- but it seems a little bit like yoga that's more concentrated on specific muscles.  Yoga with resistance.

Anyhow, if you haven't checked it out yet, you should.  It's lovely.  Prices and class offerings vary.  The Thursday evening yoga class is also highly recommended.
