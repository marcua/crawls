There's a baby/young children's wear shop on 36th St. just off of Ditmars called Coo-key Boo-key, or something like that.  Could that be the kid's shop you're referring to?  I've never been inside and don't know there price range, but it looks like they have some interesting things.

I do like Blooming Company, on 31st St. between Ditmars and 23rd, underneath the Ditmars N/W stop.  It's across the street from the post office.  They've got some interesting clothes, handbags (which I think are designed by the owner), jewelry, and nicknacks/gifty things.  I've found some good stuff in there, and the prices are pretty reasonable.

Post by: abichuela on July 18, 2006, 01:34:11 AM
A new addition to the neighborhood (on 23rd Ave, between 31st & 29th Streets - in the shops where the old Paradise/Metro Cafe used to be) is Sweet Boutique. It looks to have some nice clothes from the window; I haven't gotten a chance to stop in yet, but was planning to this week.

Also relatively new is Brazilianaki on Ditmars and 32nd (I think), which has shoes, clothes, bathing suits, jewelry, and bags (and free little candies at the cash register, and I'm talking good candies - mini Snickers, Milky Ways, 3 Musketeers & Tootsie Pops!).
