Title: Warning: Possible Scammers
Post by: redtrainrider on October 31, 2007, 03:56:46 PM
I read the boards all the time but have never posted here before - however something just happened to me that I thought people should look out for.

Right after I got home from work today my doorbell rang. I answered it and there was a man with a woman that looked like it could be his daughter. He said:

"Hello, we are from the New York State Gas and Electric Corporation. We want to make sure that you will not be affected by Con Edison's 34 percent rate hike. I need to see your Con Edison bill."

I told the guy no thank you and he asked me why. I told him that I wouldn't show it to them and he said ok and left. He never showed or offered to show ID the entire time.

This totally seemed liked a scam. I'm concerned because there are a lot of elderly people on my block (42nd between Bway & 31st) that could fall for this.

I called the 114th to report it and they weren't very helpful at first. The woman who answered the phone asked me if I could call back in 20 minutes! I said I really wanted to report this - she put me on hold for awhile, then finally I got to speak to an officer, who said they'd check it out (whatever that means).

I suppose it's possible that these people are legit, but my gut was screaming fraud the whole time. Has anyone else encountered something like this in the neighborhood recently?
