I've also made sago (the tapioca balls) before.  It does take about an hour for these starch balls to cook up. Then you have to add simple syrup, sweetening to taste. It's not hard.  All you need once those are cooked is iced black tea and some condensed milk.  I substitute the condensed milk with regular or sometimes even skim milk.  You can also make a green tea sago drink or juice as the recipe says.  Best of luck.
