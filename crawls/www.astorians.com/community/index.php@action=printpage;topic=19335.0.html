Title: Children's Theater Class starting this Saturday
Post by: arthouse on September 12, 2010, 05:13:44 PM
Childrens Theater at Art House Astoria
(Ages 6-8) Saturdays: 1:15-2:15
This class will be an exploration into the world of theater for youngsters aged 6 to 8. Focusing on music, games, puppets, and short plays, the class will help your child to gain self-confidence and explore creativity in a fun environment with other kids. Students will have the chance to perform in a showcase at the end of the semester and feel the thrill of performing before a live audience.

FEE: $255.00 for 14 weeks (Class run to January 15th)
Contact Art House Astoria for more information: 347-738-4148 or email: info@arthouseastoria.org or
Register online: https://www.securedata-trans13.com/ap/arthouseastoria/index.php?page=10

Laura Ware, instructor
B.F.A. in Acting, UC Santa Barbara
M.F.A. in Musical Theatre, San Diego State Univ
Laura Ware's credits include Rosie in the National Tour of Mamma Mia!, As the World Turns and The Naked Brothers Band. Some of her favorite stage roles include Miss Hannigan in Annie, Maggie Jones in 42nd Street, and Reverend Mother in Nunsense.
