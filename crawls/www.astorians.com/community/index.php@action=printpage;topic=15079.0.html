Post by: YaFace81 on June 10, 2009, 10:51:41 AM
I've posted about Steinway Ct. before and can say I won't be going back.  Dr. Glasser and his staff are very professional and kind, for the most part (his "bedside manner" can use a little work - using large words and "doctor-speak" a lot), but they will rip you off. 

Our cat has genetically bad teeth and had some other maladies when we first moved here (he was a rescue) and for removal of 2 teeth, Steinway Court charged us $750.  They cleaned the rest of his teeth while he was under general anaesthesia, which I can understand, but we weren't notified ahead of time so the charge for that came as a surprise.  A general exam fee is $75, which, to me, is pricey considering our new vet's fee is $40.

Add to that lots of weird tests, fees, unnecessary scans/bloodwork, and the bill can really climb up there.  For example, he charged us for putting a scope down our cat's ears to check for ear infections/mites, something he did during the check-up but didn't tell us we'd be charged for.  He also insisted on multiple rounds of bloodwork, claiming our cat tested "inconclusive" for Bartonella (aka cat scratch disease), and not knowing any better at the time, we let him run more tests.  Lo and behold, after waiting for the test results to come back from his off-site lab (in Florida...??), our cat tested negative.  Seemed fishy.

Plus, we were told that our cat was overweight (by all of one pound) and needed to go on Science Diet dry food, which is only available in his office (how convenient) and costs $20 for a bag that only lasts about 2 weeks. 

All in all, a ripoff.  The kind of thing where you check out with the receptionist and your jaw hits the floor when he/she tells you how much your bill is.

 We now use All Pets Veterinarians on Ditmars and 35th Street and couldn't be happier.
