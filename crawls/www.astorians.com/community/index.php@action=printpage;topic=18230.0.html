Title: Fund Raiser for Cooley's Anemia
Post by: Thal Gal on May 14, 2010, 09:06:23 AM
You are invited to participate in the first annual Summer Lovin’ celebration - a fund raiser that honors all those with thalassemia and raises funds for patient incentive awards and other patient programs offered by Cooley's Anemia Foundation (CAF) and THalassemia Action Group (TAG) patient support group.

EVENT DETAILS:
June 9th 6pm to 9pm
At CAVO Cafe 42-18  31st Avenue Astoria NY

Tickets are $20, which includes a glass of wine or draft beer and hors d'oeuvres
Journal Ad: Full Page Ad: $50 (8"H x 6"L)
Half Page Ad: $35 (4"H x 6"L)

We hope that many members of the CAF family - patients, family members, friends, loved ones, colleagues - will join us at this special celebration. Please help us spread the word!

Even those of you that are not able to attend that evening can be part of this important event by placing an ad in the event journal.

Like other non-profits, we are working hard to keep our programs running, so we hope you can help us make this event a big success and help ensure we can continue to offer amazing programs and services.

We hope we see you there, and if not, we hope to see you included in the journal.

Thank you.

Gina Cioffi
National Executive Director
http://www.cooleysanemia.org/

P.S. Can’t be at Summer Lovin’ but still want to make a donation? You can donate online at www.firstgiving.com/summerlovin
