Title: Beer Garden 75% non-smoking
Post by: okkichan on August 14, 2010, 11:39:20 AM
I went to Bohemian Beer Garden last night, and most of the tables and the walls have "no smoking" signs on them. They now only allow smoking in a small section of the yard. (When you walk in, facing the stage, it would be the upper left hand corner of the yard.)

I'm not surprised by this, after reading this article on Gothamist the other day about crackdowns on backyard smoking. (http://gothamist.com/2010/08/12/smoking_crackdown_outside_bars.php) I'm left wondering if the beer garden took it upon themselves to implement this rule before the health dept. came around and slapped them with hefty fines. I don't know though.

I'm torn on this new policy. I mean, it's outside, who cares? However, as a person who is trying, and occasionally failing, at dropping the smoking/drinking habit, I was grateful for this last night. My smoker friends on the other hand were put out. I imagine seats in that small quadrant are going to be hard to get.
