Pineapple - if you work near a bookstore, or find yourself having to kill some time in a B&N or whatever, stop in and look at one of the 4 (!) Postsecret books that exist. (It's been around since '05, and wildly popular.) So yeah, go to an actual store and poke through the books. It's a great way to kill some time, and people have this habit of leaving secrets in Postsecret books too - like, going to Barnes & Noble and sticking a note in one of the books for sale. I found one once, and I put it back, since I wasn't buying the book :)
