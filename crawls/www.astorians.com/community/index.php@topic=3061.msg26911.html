I used to live on 38th and Ditmars (bed bug central), above the Bat Cave Men's Club.   Only men were allowed inside except for waitresses.  Their back window used to open up into the center courtyard of the apartment building.  As residents of this building, we were treated to loud obnoxious music at all hours no matter what day or time of day it was, shouting above that music, and the smoke that would waft into our homes.  Every night we complained, called 311, and put up with the assholes.  For 3 years I suffered thru this.  I guess the landlord didn't renew their lease because they are not back.  But the shoe guy is and some of the other places.  I'm happy the tenants are finally getting a good nights sleep, that is if the bed bugs have been totally eradicated  :?

Oh, and the place across from 718 always struck me as a front.  It's always the same guy sitting in there alone who owns it, yet there's always a sign looking for new management.  I have had food there when it was a Brazilian steakhouse, it was very good.  But it's either a front or is cursed because it's always going under, and there's no reasonable explanation for it besides mafia or a ghost.
