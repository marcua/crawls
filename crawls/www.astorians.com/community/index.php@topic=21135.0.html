We've been going to a shop recommended on this board by NYCMacUser for 4-5 years now.  They're on 30th Ave. almost directly across from the hospital, and next to the funeral home... around 27th St.  We actually brought in our car this past week for a similar exhaust system repair - a flange holding two parts of it together had rusted away.  They went to the Iron Triangle and got a piece of whatever they needed, cut the rusted part out and soldered in a new piece, which ended up saving us hundreds of dollars because they didn't replace the whole exhaust system.
 
 We also get the car inspected there, and bring it there for other repairs.  Not that I've done a lot of comparison shopping on car repair prices lately, but we're satisfied with their work and don't feel that they overcharge us (and they'll work to repair a part like they did above, instead of just automatically slapping in a whole new whatever it is and charging you double).
