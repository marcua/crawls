Ok I'll do it this time...

Tuesday December 4th @ the Irish Rover, 7pm til the last one of us is left standing. (I think I left the last one around 10:30). If you are an early bird, just chirp up and I'm sure there will be a few folks who would prefer to start earlier and you all can score a spot in the back. For those that haven't been, the Irish Rover is a fun laid back Irish bar on 38th street and 28th avenue. They don't serve food, but they don't seem to mind when you bring it (I've had Subway there at midnight). The back of the bar has tables and lots of space for us to congregate and mingle together. And the best part? This place rocks at buy backs. Like, every third or fourth is on them (usually the third though).
Ok, let's get the ball rolling. if you're in copy and paste the list into a new post and add your name at the bottom. If you're going to be early or late, note that next to your name. And if I erroneously included you, sorry and feel free to adjust or admonish me. I just went through the thread and included just about anyone amenable to the idea.
We'll see y'all there!
