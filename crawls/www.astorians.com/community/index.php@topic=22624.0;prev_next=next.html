I actually live on 47th and Ditmars so some might not consider it the upper Ditmars area. To answer your question, the planes aren't a big issue. The noise varies according to what runway is being used. Although at times it can be quite loud when they take off. One caveat, I lived next to an airport for more than ten years prior to moving here.


In regards to the "fighting for everything" comment. It is so different here, the pace of things and our interaction with masses of people can make mundane things become far more complex as you know. I was only trying to give a perspective in coming from Ca. to here. It can be a bit of a culture shock, especially for those coming from Central and Northern Ca. as people are much more laid back.

Hardly any plane noise in Upper Ditmars area or any noise at all  since its mostly families here . People tend to go to bed earlier and no late night bars or cafes pumping music and drunks at 3am .   A couple of new wine bars and pizza places have just opened for the spring so its actually pretty nice . Only thing missing is a local bank .  You would have to trek to lower Ditmars for that .    If you do drive , upper Ditmars has alot more parking than lower Ditmars and the apts are much larger and newer .
