Greetings all,
 
Join us this week for...
 
Wednesday, September 19th
This is your last chance to catch the beautiful work of Peter Kane while catching a buzz here at Hell Gate Social.
You can also see his work if you visit http://www.peterkaneartworks.com/ or email kanep1111@yahoo.com to make a studio appointment.
 
 
Thursday, September 20th
Opening reception for artist Demetrius Manouselis
7pm reception for Creten born artist/painter Demetrius Manouselis with an exhibition of 8 works (artworks & critical writings can be found on his website: www.demetriusmanouselis.com ) as well as a
9pm & 10pm performance by the Far East Blues Band.
 
Friday, September 21st
Special guest Dj will be spinning classic punk, ska and classic angst music for all the kiddies.
 
Saturday, September 22nd
Sven is coming by with some house, hip-hop and galactic grooves for Saturday night.
 
Sunday, September 23rd
Sunday Night Cinema
Camp ClassiQue presents Hairspray
Pleasantly plump teenager Tracy Turnblad and her best friend Penny Pingleton audition to be on The Corny Collins Show and Tracy wins.
But when scheming Amber Von Tussle and her mother plot to destroy Tracy, it turns to chaos.
Movie starts as soon as the sun goes down, in the back patio (weather permitting).
Happy Hour 7-9pm
 
Hell Gate Social
12-21 Astoria Blvd
(between 14th & 12th Street)
718.204.8313
