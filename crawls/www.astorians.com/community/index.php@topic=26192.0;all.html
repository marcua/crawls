I agree that really good Chinese food is lacking and that someone could make a killing if they opened a great Chinese place here. I also agree that it's easy to live with the lack of terrific Chinese when we have so much good food around here.

I was surprised to find, quite by accident, that Dragon Gate across from Singlecut is very, very good. Unfortunately I don't live near Singlecut so I don't go there too often, but if that place was local to me it's be my pick. Over by 30th Avenue, I like Sampan. Still, it's in the category of "Good, perfectly decent, but nothing to write home about" as are most of the Chinese takeout spots I've tried here over the years.
