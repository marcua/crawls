Christos is especially flagrant about their "valet" parking. On busy nights(especially around the holidays) they will take two spaces to save one. then when the next car comes they move the other so they can get the next one in. So a local is forced to drive around to find a spot a few blocks away. Of course, if you check at 11:30pm all you see are double spots all along the block. At one time, they would use the schoolyard on 42nd which wasn't so bad. Probably until they got caught or something.

Oh, and after watching them park some cars on Astoria Blvd, I'm sure that the car owners probably weren't too happy about them parking there.
