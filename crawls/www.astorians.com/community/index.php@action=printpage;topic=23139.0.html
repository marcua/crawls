Title: Free Baby Sign Language Workshop
Post by: My Smart Hands on July 10, 2012, 02:11:45 PM
My Smart Hands is hosting a free baby sign language workshop at Raising Astoria this Thursday, July 12th @ 11:00 a.m. We'll talk about the benefits of baby sign language, learn some common first signs, and sing and sign a couple songs. You'll also receive a handout with the signs and songs we learn that you can take home and refer back to, so that you can start signing with your baby right away!

This workshop is recommended for parents of babies up to 18 months old.
