Bistro 33 is back

"Word on the street" as mentioned in other posts is correct... The official opening is June 11, but for people in the 'hood, old friends from Lil' Bistro 33 on 36th Ave and those who bore witness to the seemingly endless construction on the Ditmars spot -- doors will open on Thursday, June 7th! :roll:

It's dinner only for now (with brunch coming soon), and unfortunately no liquor license yet...but there will be a full menu including sushi and some old and new favorites.

P.S. - just for now, it's cash only

Tried it finally last night before the baseball game (was attending w/dad, sis & her husband.) Odd choice before baseball but it's otherwise impossible to get them to eat in the 'hood with me!

Had the spinach salad (would've preferred w/o bacon bits as I try to eat vegetarian) which was great; the crawfish was delicious as everybody said; vegetable tempura nice; Godzilla roll satisfyingly huge.

We even had fun people-and-dog watching, good location for that  :mrgreen: All in all a pleasant experience, hope to return for the main entrees!
