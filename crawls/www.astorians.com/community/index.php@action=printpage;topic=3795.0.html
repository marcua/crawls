Post by: NYCMacUser on June 15, 2009, 07:54:58 PM
As I understand it, on a $25 GC you need to spend at least $35 for the GC to be used. If you have a $50 GC you need to spend $65 in order to use the GC.

As far as the gratuity goes, a restaurant may add it to the check, but you should be told in advance. This is how this was handled at one of my restaurants:

FOOD:               39.00
BAR:                  14.50
TAX:                    4.48
GRATUITY:           9.83
Gift Certificate: (25.00)

TOTAL:              42.81

I spent in excess of the $35 that was required, so my dinner companion and I split the expense at $21.40 each!
