Yep, Fat Cat has been around for a while.  This is probably their 3rd location since they first opened.  I got my first tattoo at Gallo's shop (not by Gallo) when it was down on 14th Street.  That was way back in the days when all work was done in an underground (ie: unmarked) shop.  I think some of the younger folks from Gallo's shop branched out and formed Fat Cat and started their first studio on Steinway St. heading towards 21st Ave....I got 2 tattoos from them....also when the shops weren't bright store fronts with neon signs.  Gallo went on to open Painless Steel on Steinway after a short stint out of Astoria.  Fat Cat moved again to Ditmars in the 20 somethings (a block or two down from La Guli's) but I never went to that shop since I already had enough tattoos.  :roll:
