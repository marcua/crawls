I loved the first season.  I don't like this season very much.  I thought the episode on Monday the 26th, was terrible to the point where I could hardly watch it.

I think there are too many stories going on at once.  It's gone beyond tantalizing and become pesky. 

I don't think there was any reason to bring Sylar back.  The Mexican siblings have been irrelevant and I think their power is stupid.  The situation with the brother on Monday was absurd. 

The character I like least is Wes/West, the emo kid who has spent most of the season complaining about things and being emo to the point where I kept expecting him to write Claire a poem.  I didn't like Caitlin, the Irish girl, because the actress' attempt at an Irish accent was so bad that I couldn't focus on anything else in her storyline.  For God's sake, stop calling him "Paytir!"  (Side note: did you know that none of the actors for the scenes set in Ireland are actually Irish?) 

The new addition to the show that I've liked was Matt Parkman's dad, who I thought was exceptionally creepy and had a frightening power.  Contrary to others here, I kind of like Kristen Bell the "electro girl."  I think that her character is interesting because of how her father basically kept her in a Skinner box and created a killer out of her.  I'm interested in seeing whether she rejects him in the end and becomes "good."

The more I think about it, the more I realize that there's not much about this season that I like.  I think I'm only watching it because I'm very lazy on Monday night and I want to find out what happens, but don't want to read the spoilers.

What's going on after the "last episode" on Monday.  Are they having a recess and then bringing it back?
