Hey Rachel,

I'm an avid Astorian vermicomposter also!  I started my first bin about a year ago, and am just beginning to harvest the first of the compost now.  I don't think there's any harm in leaving it in the bin as long as your bin doesn't overflow.  I believe that it gets richer if you leave it in there a little longer also... also better for the conditions of your bin to have extra microorganisms.  Making (non edible) compost cookies with mine... I often equate compost cookies to miracle grow spikes for houseplants when explaining.  Let me know if you want to how to do that or else I might be able to find a home for your finished compost too.
