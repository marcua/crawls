I'm digging this thread ... it's totally a California love-fest.   :-)

The Getty is wonderful!  I agree with anzubird.  Though, to be perfectly honest, I didn't really care too much about the art inside.  I love the building itself and all the beautiful travertine marble, the gardens, and especially the view! 

California is so unique in that you get the beach, desert, and mountains pretty much in one place.  They're only very short drives away from each other.  Everybody needs to check out the central coast, too ... Santa Barbara, Monterrey, San Luis Obispo ... Beachy like San Diego (which I adore), but there's a different vibe.  Amazing what a four-hour drive can do.  Just get on that 101 and head north and prepare to be dazzled.

Among architectural icons, you should see the new Gehry-designed Disney Concert Hall downtown. There's one spot where, walking by on the sidewalk, the sun is reflected toward you because of the parabolic shape of the shiny metal. If you find the exact spot, the focused sunlight makes it intensely hot. You might even be able to cook some hotdogs there!
