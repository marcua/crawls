Tried a new venue tonight - Zebulon in Williamsburg.  I wanted to like it, but it's really got bad air circulation and is hot as hades.  Great show (Francis and the Lights - they were amazing - as always - http://francisandthelights.com/ ), but it was just not a very enjoyable show overall.  I had the misfortune of being next to a pack of early 20somethings with no sense as to personal space (I mean, if you have three feet open behind you, shoving folks into walls and immovable poles really isn't a kind thing - in fact, it's downright rude).  After I gave up my space for them, they lost interest in the space, then wandered back 20 minutes later and made three other people move around for them.  I mean, after the first person says, I'm sorry, do you want my space?  Are you trying to merge with me?  and everyone around you keeps glaring at you, wouldn't a person get the message?  So very weird - in 20 years of catching shows, I don't think I've ever seen anything that obnoxious (except for maybe folks looking to start fights).
