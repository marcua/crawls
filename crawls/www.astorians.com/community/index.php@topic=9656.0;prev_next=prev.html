I think Laminall is a place I used about 10 years ago. I had them laminate a certificate that I got
from the Ellis Island foundation for when my maternal grandmother came here from Sweden - they
used to do that when you gave a donation for the memorial wall.

Anyway, I had them laminate it on a wood plaque and they did a beautiful job. I'm pretty sure it was them
because I remember bringing it to a place at the Courthouse Square (46th Rd. stop on #7 train).

We still have it hanging  up and it looks as good now as the day it was done.
Very high quality work. Definitely worth the trip over there.
