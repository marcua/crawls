Rent-Direct sucks, I dont know but I dont buy into those sites where you have to pay to see listings.  I got a 2 bedroom in ditmars for 1100.  It CAN happen, but what works best is working with the agents, calling them, hounding them even to find you a spot and being persistent about it.  Checking craigslist of course, but you will find that it is the brokers posting on there anyways, RARELY would there be a by owner posting.  Also be mindful of the pictures you see them post too because sometimes they use pictures from different apartments on listings to get you to call.

Ditmars try:

Metropolis - Nick, he is always all over the place but will work with you and find you sometimes 1000 1br deals
Century 21 - George, he is very patient and a straight shooter, will find you places but u gotta call him
Metroline - Tanya, she is aiite but does have some good listings
Buy Sell RE - Eva, very cool agent and follows up with u
