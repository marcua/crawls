HUMP Wednesdays at Bungalo in Astoria :)

32-03 Broadway, (btw 32 and 33rd street, just 1 block from the Broadway N train)
9pm- closing

Trendy Neighborhood Bar & Lounge
Gay, lesbian, and anything inbetween with a house DJ spinning favorites all night long. Minutes from midtown, in the heart of Astoria.

Great drink specials:  $2 specialty shots, $3 Bud Light Bottles, $4 Chardonnay, and $5 Vodka Mixed Drinks
