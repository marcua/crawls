« Reply #4 on: March 03, 2008, 08:04:06 AM »
Fatty's on Crescent and Ditmars has a brunch that's a little out there and pretty good. The price is right, a drink is included and the polenta cakes are awesome.

For dinner style fair in the Ditmars area, I like Igloo. They make great juice.

If you want to go cheap, gritty and quick, there's the Family Corner on 31st St and 21st Ave. For $5 you can get a pretty decent omlette with all the fixin's.

I don't know the other parts of Astoria's brunch as well as the Ditmars scene, but those are my favorites there. I will also make the trek down to Michael's on Broadway because the food is good, but for $12 you get two drinks too, which is a big part of what I love about brunch.  :-D (Or any meal, really, I guess...)
