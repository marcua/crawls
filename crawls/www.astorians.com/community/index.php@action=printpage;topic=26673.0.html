Title: Queens Council on the Arts Presents Senses Transport
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on February 20, 2014, 12:58:32 PM
Queens Council on the Arts mixes up dance and food in the second Moveable Feast Artist Dinner Series which will raise money for a stop gap scholarship fund.
 
The Moveable Feast Artist Dinner (http://queenscouncilarts.org/support/) features Senses Transport, a performed participatory meal that transports those in attendance to a world of heightened senses with Performing Artist Emily Berry and Chef Gianna Cerbone-Teoli. Participants experience all five senses as they are moved through a multi-course meal that places them in the performance space with dancers, a violinist, and a chef.  This is a rare opportunity to get up close and personal interactions with artists as they take you on a journey of the senses!
 
The funds will support scholarships for our students participating in the High School to Art School Portfolio Development (http://queenscouncilarts.org/student/) Program. You are giving underserved high school juniors and seniors the ability to attend nationwide art colleges by literally closing the gap of their financial assistance for their 1st year of school!
