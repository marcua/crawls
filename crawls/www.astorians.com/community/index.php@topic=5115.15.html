went into Sparrow for the first time on Saturday as we were coming home for a walk and wanted to keep Lyla awake for a little while.  I didn't realize how rundown and shady looking the place was inside, though I should have figured that by the outside.  We loved it.  We checked out the brunch menu for future reference and although it starts a little late for our taste, it definitely is pretty fancy for a divey joint. 

The people in there were great and loved Lyla.  A little while later a guy came in with a sleeping 11 month old in a stroller.  He said on Sundays the place is full of families and it's like the playground in a McDonald's in there.   Definitely something we'll check out, though I don't know if that would appeal to many others. 

Great to have a nearby location to duck into for a quick beer or two, too.
