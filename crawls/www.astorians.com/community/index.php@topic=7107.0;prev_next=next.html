Hi, I'm new to the neighborhood and have been checking out a few places by myself (some thanks to this post).  My favorite so far is Bistro 33. I've been there 2x alone  and once with friends.  Every time the food was great and the service really friendly.  The sushi is great (I had the Bistro roll and the spicy blue crab), but it's not a "regular sushi place" at all.  There is a full menu of non-sushi.  The lamb chop appetizer and seabass are also really good!
