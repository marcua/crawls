Fatty's Cafe - MOVED
« on: March 08, 2014, 01:47:07 AM »
Fatty's Cafe has been the brunch innovator in the Ditmars area.  Where else would you grab a drink and brunch in the area 10 years ago?  Unfortunately, the owners decided they want to open yet another pharmacy in the area (aren't 2 CVS' enough?) and forced Fatty's to leave their location.  I saw on their FB page that they're moving to 28th Avenue and 45th - 46th Street  - another area that has no dining establishments besides a pizzeria.  I hope they do well!
