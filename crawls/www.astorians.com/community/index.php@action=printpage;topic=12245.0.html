Title: Mughal Diner
Post by: Christine on September 13, 2008, 09:28:06 PM
Why is this place almost always empty everytime we go? Could be that we tend to go earlier than usual. It's on Broadway b/w Crescent and 29th, next to Los Portales and Dunkin Donuts/Baskin Robbins; across from Bakeway.

Never mind the mouse I saw last time we were there- food is good and cheap enough that we're not fazed by that (besides, the door was open the whole time, probably just stopped in to check things out.) John got the biriyany lamb (basmati rice w/lamb chunks, almonds, peas, etc.) and I got the balti seafood (fish in some kind of sauce w/white rice and yogurt sauce on the side.)

They start you off with a crisp, spicy flatbread and three sauces. We also ordered garlic nan to go with dinner. Just water to drink and for both of us, less than $25 ($30 with tip) and enough to take home (we just mix our leftovers together with the extra rice).

Anyway, it's flavorful comfort food. Wish they'd mix up the CD selection a little more, seems like it's always the same sitar music.
