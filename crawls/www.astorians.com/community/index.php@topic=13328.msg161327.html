  This Contest starts tonight. Please tell anyone you think has a chance of winning. There are going to be added prizes real soon, that is on top of the 10 Grand. This contest is open to everyone.

DOYLE’S CORNER

ANNUAL KARAOKE CONTEST  –  30 WEEKS
EVERY SUNDAY
DECEMBER 7, 2008 TO JULY 12, 2009
________________________________________________________________

SCHEDULE

Dec    7th     2008      Preliminaries begin
May  24th   2009    Preliminaries end

May  31st   2009      Semi-finals begin
June 28th   2009       Semi-finals end

July    5th   2009      Suspended

July  12th   2009      Finals
________________________________________________________________

FEES

Registration fee    $10 per contestant*

The $10 registration fee will be used for 2nd, 3rd, and 4th place prizes (TBA).
Repeat contestants must pay $5 in addition to the initial registration fee of $10.
*Finals night:  All patrons pay the $10 admission fee.  Final contestants will receive a $10 refund.
________________________________________________________________

RULES

Contestants must appear each week on Sunday at 8 PM for registration and song selection. 
The contest will start at 9 PM . . . no exceptions.

Each week the top 3 performances will be chosen and the performers will be given an automatic bid in the semi-finals.  If you do not make it into the top 3 performances, you may try again during the remaining weeks.     Hint:  Invite many friends to applaud you!

Once you are in the top 3, you are in the semi-finals.  At this point, you will be given a designated night to appear in the semi-finals, and thus, you will no longer be required to appear every Sunday.


If a contestant will be absent from a semi-final contest, the contestant must reschedule with another semi-finalist.  If not, the absent contestant cannot compete any further.
As there is ample time to prepare, attendance should not be a problem.

After 25 weeks there will be 75 top contestants and the semi-finals will begin.

During the 5 weeks of the semi-finals, 75 semi-finalists will be placed into 5 groups.  Thus, each group will be comprised of 15 contestants.  Names of contestants will be chosen randomly (handpicked from a hat) in the presence of Saturday night karaoke performers.

The judges of the preliminary and semi-final contests will be Bartenders Paul and Brendan of Doyle’s Corner, Richard of Starlite Karaoke, a guest judge and the audience.  The voting process by the judges and the audience shall be 50/50.

Ultimately, there will be 15 contestants who will compete in the finals.

Each of the final 15 contestants will sing one song.  Then the top 10 singers will be chosen from this group and each will sing again. Then the top 5 will be chosen and each will sing again. The lineup will again be determined randomly.

After the 5 finalists sing, each will again be chosen randomly.  Each of the lucky 5 finalists will sing one last time.  And then the judges will pick the winners!
 
Ruling for the finals will be determined by the judges (4 of whom will be from the music industry) and by the audience.  The voting process by the judges and the audience shall be 75/25, respectively. 
________________________________________________________________

Starlite Karaoke offers over 200,000 songs from which to choose but you may bring your own music, on a CD.  Please be aware that if you need to rip a CD for the contest, you will have to know all of the lyrics, verbatim, as there will be no lyrics to view on the monitor. 

But, with or without your own CDs, we are looking forward to your participation in the annual Doyle’s Corner Karaoke Contest 2008-2009.  And for your additional pleasure a 50/50 raffle,
prizes, drink specials, and giveaways, will also be offered.

 
All of the semi-final contests as well as the finals will be filmed for QPTV.

Richard Buccola
A contestant may choose any song but the song you choose may win or lose . . . $10,000.
