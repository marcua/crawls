I recently attempted to cut out my 5 cups/day - BIG mistake. I had headaches, was lethargic and pretty bitchy too. I tried the green tea, then black tea (because I was told it has more caffeine), then even just drinking one cup of black (yuck!).

Prior to quitting I had really begun to resent my addiction. I hated the idea that something had such a hold of me, but I was physically feeling so awful and suffering from withdrawal symptoms that I had to compromise -

I am now a 3 cup/day-er, but I did cut down to 1% milk and cut down my sugar. I feel much better all around.

I just realized that I am not answering your question, sorry, I guess I am trying to say, "I relate."

Good luck, try the stronger teas (go to Freeze Peach - they'll tell you all about tea) and munch on chocolate covered coffee beans (soooooo great) late in the afternoon.

If you can make it, my hat is off to you.
