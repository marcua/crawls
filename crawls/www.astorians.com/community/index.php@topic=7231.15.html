Never happen..............I love hearing other peoples memories of the "neighborhood"...........I went to PS 5...just threw that in. For some reason we walked up and down Steinway St. a lot. And rode our bikes all over. We used to aggravate a man named W.E.Bowen, who owned and ran the liquor store just around the corner from me towards Steinway St. There was an empty lot across the street from me to the right, where I took my dog every day to play and run around. And go potty. I think that's where the big Market is now. It had been a mansion that was torn down, or burned down. Nothing was left but the big gate and chimney. I thought it was the greatest thing, to play with my dog in all that open space. The bar under my apt. played Eddie Fisher's "Anytime" which was a big hit then............they played it constantly and I liked it. Yes, I'm trying to get up there.
