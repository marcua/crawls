Slow service is often par the course in Astoria, even been to CafeBar, or any other cafe where you have to wait forever for the check or more water?  I've been in Fatty's with just two tables occupied, and the waitress and food order were still slow as snails.  That being said, the food's always been awesome, and the staff very friendly, even if slow and unattentive.  I don't think there needs to be a debate on it, just don't go there if you have somewhere you need to be on time.  Some people are going to love the place, and some people aren't.  And some people apparently get attitude and some don't.  I don't know that I have any restaurants where I would say I see the "beauty" in them, that's a bit much.  But I do like Fatty's.
