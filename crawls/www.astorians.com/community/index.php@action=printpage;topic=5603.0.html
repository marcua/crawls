Title: 3/25 Hell Gate Social Movie Night-Contested Streets
Post by: heff on March 19, 2007, 10:36:52 AM
The Astoria/LIC Transportation Alternatives Committee is screening the documentary Contested Streets at Hell Gate Social Club this Sunday.  Here's the info:

Hell Gate Social
12-21 Astoria Blvd
(Cross Street: Between 12th Street and 14th Street)
Sunday 3/25  Screening at 8p

CONTESTED STREETS explores the history and culture of New York City streets from pre-automobile times to the present. This examination allows for an understanding of how the city - though the most well served by mass transit in the United States - has slowly relinquished what was a rich, multi-dimensional conception of the street as public space to a mindset that prioritizes the rapid movement of cars and trucks over all other functions.

Should be a lot of fun, and a great chance to meet up with other people who also want to see some kind of change in the way traffic works in out great city.
