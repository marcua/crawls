Let Last Poster Wins Thread Die
« on: May 06, 2007, 12:33:22 PM »
it's been going since Dec 30th and since that time it's done little to enhance this board.  In fact I think it's probably done a lot more harm than good - maybe I'm nuts - so please let me know what you think. 

The thing is, I used to keep up with what was going on here and in my neighborhood by signing on and checking out MOST RECENT POSTS... and within a page or so I could catch up.  Once we hit 1000 members it may have taken a bit more time - but I could more or less catch the drift of most of what was going on here at Astorians by checking out the most recent threads. 

Since the beginning of 2007 the last poster wins thread has cluttered up the most recent threads... and sifting through to find relevant posts became annoying and tedious.  I personally stopped checking regularly and lost a lot of interest in this board. 

Now I'm sure that the thread with two hundred something pages has brought a lot of good to the universe.  People who didn't know each other before may now know of lots of people that they still don't know... except through LPW. 

I don't mean to offend anyone with this post.  And I'd like to add that when I first read the post over 4 months ago i thought it was cute and clever. 

I would like to officially name - no winner.  And stop the thread from going on any longer.  I think it's time to get back to more interesting matters.  Like - where/when is dinner?  And which bars have a decent happy hour.  And where is the next Astorians for Obama meeting?

people please.  it's time. 

let it go!!!!


i'm sorry actually that it's taken me so long to write this post... i simply couldnt' figure out why LPW was bugging me so much.... maybe i'm now beating a dead horse.

I have no problem with letting that post die.  While I did participate in it, at this point, it's gone on forever, and I could care less if it lives or dies.  But I'm really not sure if that's why many people have lost interest in this board.  LPW is just one of many posts, and is easily skipped over by not clicking on the link to read it.  I often do that with many posts I have no interest in reading. 

But I have been wondering what has made people lose interest in this board.  How could it really be just LPW?  Just playing interested observer here.  Will anyone ever take over Astoria Eats?  Taco crawl?  Post randomly to meet for happy hour?  Dinner?  Or are we all just too busy with our own lives?  That happens a lot in nyc and is completely ok.
