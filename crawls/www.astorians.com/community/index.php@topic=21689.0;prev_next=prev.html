Woks and Lox, the first Asian and Jewish Christmas
« on: December 08, 2011, 02:32:49 PM »

Ever since the first lonely Jew ordered Chinese take-out on Christmas, there has been an undeniable bond between Jews and Asians. This Christmas Eve, we want to wipe away those lonely tears and celebrate our combined heritage atWoks and Lox, the very first Jewish and Asian Christmas.

Saturday, December 24th, at [/color]The Queens Kickshaw[/size][/url]in Astoria, Queens, Woks and Lox is throwing a dinner and nosh party prepared by our spectacular Chinese chef Chichi Wang. But it doesn’t stop at food - while we kick it at the Kickshaw, we’ll have a Chinese auction, Mah Jong, a Bar Mitzvah-esque sign-in board, and lots more Jewish and Asian-inspired fun while throwing down some He’Brew and Fresh Ginger Ale by Bruce Cost.

And even if you can’t make it, we want to be with you in spirit. Through the medium of [/color]IndieGogo[/size][/url], we are offeringCh’aliday grams that include ChiChi’s recipe for wonton & matzoh ball soup (discount if you send to a friend), Chopsticks & Tchotchkes to-go boxes filled with everything you’ll need to have your party at home, limited edition Woks & Lox money holders created by [/color]Hail the Right Brain[/size][/url], and other ways to warm up your tree-less home.

This December, no more lonely Christmas.

Thanks to our sponsors for their giveaways at this event:
Shmaltz Brewing Company, Fresh Ginger Ale by Bruce Cost, Scott’s Pizza Tours, Gabrielle Glaser’s book Strangers to the Tribe: Portraits of Interfaith Marriage, Louie’s Pizzeria of Elmhurst, Roebling Pizza, Real Pizza of New York iPhone app, The Roosevelt Avenue Midnight Street Food Crawl, Hail the Right Brain, Jack Eichenbaum's 7-train Tour, and more.

Date: Saturday, Dec 24th, Christmas Eve
Location: [/color]The Queens Kickshaw[/size][/url] in Astoria, Queens

Website: [/color]WoksAndLox.com[/size][/url]
For tickets and other ways to participate, see our [/color]Woks & Lox IndieGoGo[/size][/url] page
