I've been a member for a little over two years. The manager told me that the towel thing
is because the regular workout-size towels have been disappearing and the cost to replace them
has gone up 70% - that's what I was told.

As for the fans, if you want them on all you have to do is ask. I haven't lately because I've been
feeling cold there but when it was warmer in Sept. & Oct., I just asked and they were turned on.

Things have improved since this thread was at its peak a couple of months ago with all the complaints.
I printed out a copy and sent it anonomously to the owners. Don't know if that did it or something else,
but even though the price is high, you do get good value.

The equipment is great with lots of variety. Classes are excellent. I used to take pilates at a studio 2x
week near my office and now I take 3 classes per week at the Rock and the quality is much better.

It's really a matter of how much you train, the priority you put on it  and, of course, economics!
