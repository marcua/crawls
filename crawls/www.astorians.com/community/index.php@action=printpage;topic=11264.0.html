Post by: elmaestro on July 14, 2009, 01:02:48 PM
Waking up this sleepy thread to chime in.

I just moved here not too long ago. I had lived on the border between Bed-Stuy and Bushwick for four years prior. Before that I lived in the Upper West Side. I moved here originally to take the sound/light/recording tech position at Birdland jazz club. I eventually left to continue my aspirations as a musician and photographer. Most of my gigs are on electric and double bass but much of my time at home is spent playing or teaching the classical guitar. I play plenty of classical repertoire but I also love música Brasileira and flamenco.

I started out on trumpet in third grade though I have no chops to speak of anymore. I played drumset, mallet, symphonic and marching percussion for many years throughout middle school, high school and college. I took private classical guitar lessons in middle school and double bass lessons in high school. Eventually I had to choose between drums and bass as a focus in college and I chose bass but I did end up getting my major in theory/composition and minors in double bass and percussion. I also sing and play some saxophone.

My main groups right now are the French funk/rock/jazz group Amie Amis and the exploratory jazz outfit 5 Point Perspective. Amie was a long-time Astoria resident. Ironically, just as she moved near my old area I moved to hers! I'm becoming less active with her but I still do lots of gigs with 5 Point and lots of subbing and sit-ins with several other groups. I have been working on writing and arranging music for my own group for several years now. I hope to have it done by the end of 2010. I plan to play all the instruments on some of the tracks. Most of my time these days is spent with a camera in my hand or, as mentioned before, a classical guitar in my lap. I hope to see some of you guys around!
