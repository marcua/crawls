Title: First time rentals in a rent stabilized apt: Advice!
Post by: deefunk78 on March 28, 2007, 11:07:13 AM
Hey!

I wanted to share some advice with anyone going to rent for the first time in a rent stabilized apt:

YOU SHOULD be given the option for a one or two year lease off the bat, during your original signing with the building/agency.

We were only given a one year option at first, and now at renewal time, we found out that it was going up either 4.50% or 7.25% with a one or two year choice, consecutively.

Thank GOD i work in real estate and i just found out from a Real Estate Attorney friend that we should of been given the two year option at first. That is the LAW according to the Rent Stabilization Board or something like that.

So after speaking to the agency of my building, we got them caught and got them to tell my landlord that we would like another year of the same rent, THEN we'll get the increase if we renew next year!

Just a tip for you all.........
