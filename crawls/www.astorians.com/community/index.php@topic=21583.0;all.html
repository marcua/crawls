John Brown Smoke House WOW!
« on: September 02, 2011, 10:41:22 AM »
We had lunch here this week.  A hoe in the wall location with out of this world authentic Kansas BBQ. 

I had the two meat and two side platter.  Burnt ends (brisket) and pork ribs.  Both perfection but the burnt ends we just a bit better than that. Super moist and tender with a smokey finish. Really good stuff folks.  My sides were coleslaw and baked beans.  The slaw is vinegar based and finely shredded.  A nice balance to the meats.  The beans were a deep rich flavor, studded with bits of meat and surprisingly the whole thing was not overpowering in any way.  A perfect side dish.  The platter came with two slices of Texas toast (double think) white bread.  The table was set with a roll of paper towel and a squeeze bottle of house bbq sauce, mmm mmm goodness.  The Guys behind the place are alumni of Hill country and Dinosaur BBQ. 

People have complained about the so so fries and desserts.  Personally I don't see a need for french fries at a smokehouse.  The sodas were from a self serve fountain and there was no sweet or any house made iced tea.  On the other had the place is just a month old so they get some slack here.  The fact that the bbq and sides may be the best in NYC is good enough for me.
