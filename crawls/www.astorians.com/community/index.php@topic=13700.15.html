Hey Guys,

If you're missing out on Iji - you're missing what in my humble opinion is the best Japanese food in Astoria.  Apologies to Linn and JJ who all have their good points - but for me IJI comes out on top.  (Osaka was pretty terrible in our opinion - sry guys)

My advice for those who may not have tried IJI is to check out their lunch bento (which I believe are down to $9) one day.  It's a simple yet elegantly done shumai, very decent california roll, and their teriyaki, katsu, and sushi have all been of excellent quality. 

My wife and I (we're both Japanese if that counts for anything) are both extremely happy and surprised with IJI's quality.  While it ain't exactly 'authentic' or Manhattan-quality Japanese - for Astoria it's as good as it gets - which is surprisingly... quite good.
