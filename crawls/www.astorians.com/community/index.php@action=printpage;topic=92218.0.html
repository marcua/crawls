Title: Queens Stories! Stories! Stories! Sunday, 10/28 at 6:30pm
Post by: nancygoldman on October 23, 2018, 10:06:19 PM



On Sunday, 10/28 at 6:30pm, I'm hosting a storytelling home concert in Jackson Heights, Queens and hope you'll come. If you've never been to a storytelling home concert, I invite 4 master storytellers into my cozy living room and invite neighbors to listen to them tell true stories from their personal lives. We have a sofa, chairs, meditation cushions, and pillows and some beverages and refreshments. I created this event so that neighbors could meet. As you plan your weekend and are thinking about ways to keep warm, this will be a friendly and toasty evening. I'm asking for $15 donations to pay the performers but it's just a suggestion. Please let me know if you plan on coming since seating is limited.  Email me at heightshub@gmail.com for reservations and our address.

Also, thanks for any help you can provide spreading the word.

Nancy
