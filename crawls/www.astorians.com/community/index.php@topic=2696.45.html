I live south of GCP so I don't know the Ditmars area, but my four "best of the worst" Chinese places are these:

Golden Dragon on 31st Ave. is the best flavor-wise, but they don't serve the food with brown rice--at least they don't say so on the menu.

Sunrise--sorry I haven't had any bug experiences with them.

No. 1 Kitchen on 30th Ave.--it serves the dishes I like in small portions with brown rice, but their regular cook must be on vacation.  The last Szechuan shrimp I had from them was not up to snuff.

Lilly's on 30th Ave.--their veggies are the freshest, but you'd better make sure they get your order right.
