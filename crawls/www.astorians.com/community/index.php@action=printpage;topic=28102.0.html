Title: FREE SUMMER YOGA in ASTORIA PARK
Post by: cityviewpharmacy on June 02, 2014, 01:49:44 PM
City View Pharmacy's Wellness Program presents
FREE SUMMER YOGA in ASTORIA PARK every Wednesday evening at 7pm, weather permitting, from June 4th through August 27th.

Wear comfortable clothes and bring a large towel or yoga mat*. The group meets at Astoria Park. Enter at the parking lot and proceed toward the river. The group meets in the grassy area to your right, between the RFK bridge and the pool. Class will cancel due to poor weather, so LIKE US on facebook to easily check for updates!

For more information, or to sign up, visit http://www.cityviewpharmacy.com/yoga.html (http://www.cityviewpharmacy.com/yoga.html)
Tell your friends, family and neighbors! All are welcome.

(*Yoga mats are available for purchase at City View Pharmacy for $16.99, while supplies last.)
