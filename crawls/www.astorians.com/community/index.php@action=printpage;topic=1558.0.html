My roommate is vegetarian, lemme check the take-out menus in the drawer......

Tacos Mexico 32-08 38th St.

This is hands-down highly recommended by everybody who's eaten there. What it lacks in decor it makes up for in quality. Authentic and inexpensive. There's even a TV playing Spanish soaps, plus adjacent pool room with a bar. Open everyday 'til 2am.

Gandhi 34-14 30th Ave.

Indian as you can guess, haven't tried it yet (maybe somebody else can offer a review.) "No additives or fat used in cooking. We only use vegetable/olive oil." Open everyday 'til 11pm.

gowasabi 34-02 30th Ave. and 29-11 Ditmars Blvd.

Chain restaurant but nice ambiance. The one on Ditmars is friendly, haven't tried the other one. Live jazz Sundays 6:30-9:30. Open every day 'til 10pm; Fri-Sat 'til midnight.

Thai Angel Kitchen 34-11 30th Ave. www.thaiangelnewyork.com

Another one I haven't tried yet, they don't use MSG according to the menu. A whole list of vegetarian dishes and pad thai under $10. Open everyday 'til 10:30pm.

Of course you can't swing a granny cart w/o hitting a nice local place, Greek or otherwise.
