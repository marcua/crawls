Title: Framing
Post by: AstoriaK on September 12, 2005, 08:59:56 AM
I'm sorry that I don't know the name of it (and I'm don't know where in Astoria you are) but I have taken several posters/pictures/diplomas in to get framed at the photo lab shop next to Blockbuster on Broadway (just east of Cresent).  They have a nice selection of frames to choose from and they are about half the price of most Manhattan framers I've used.  We've always had very good service with them, even with our picture developments and enlargements.

There is a great little place on 30th Ave between 36th and 37th Street.   They recently moved locations (across the street) and I believe its called Astoria Framers.  They have a ton of selections and the guy who runs the office (I think his name is Pablo) was very careful with some of our sensitive/valuable prints.    The rates were very reasonable and he gave us a discount because we had a lot done at once.
