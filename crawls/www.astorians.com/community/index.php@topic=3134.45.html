My name is Abby, and when I lived in Ecuador, abichuela was one of many word plays my friends there tried to make with my name, and this one stuck. I like it and have used it as a username ever since.

It means bean (as in kidney beans or black beans, like the can of Goya pictured), though the real word is actually spelled habichuela. Also, adding -chuela/o to the end of a word in Spanish is sometimes used as a diminutive (though less common than -ito), so it's sort of like a term of endearment, little Abby.
