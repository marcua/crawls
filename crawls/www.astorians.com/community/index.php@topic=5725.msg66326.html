For anyone that is interested here is the link for the Transportation Alternatives LIC/Astoria Committee http://www.transalt.org/campaigns/astoria-lic/index.html#events  We should have some more biking events coming up for Bike Month in May, I know there are some historical bike tours and an cuisine bike tour in the works.  Anyone that wants to join the committee should email Wiley at the address on the site, anyone that wants to receive email updates about T.A. sponsored bike events in Astoria/LIC can send me a PM with your name and email and I can put you on the Upcoming Events email list.
