Title: Re: 1st Birthday Party Places in Astoria?
Post by: BronwynBurke on September 12, 2007, 03:20:30 PM
Astoria Sports Complex (1 block north of Kaufman Astoria 6 movie theater) has a gigantic party room that comes with blow up slide and choice of sports for kids...they have a website with pics of the room and rate chart. Very reasonable.  I'm having my daughter's party there next month. Also, the China King Buffet (think it's called Home Select Buffet now) has two nice party rooms that are free.....with purchase of food. I had my kid's party there last year and it was fine and not very expensive.  The larger room has a stage, music capabilities and holds about 100 people. The smaller room holds about 35. The restaurant is located in LaGuardia Shopping Center (43rd and Ditmars). Hope this helps.

Title: Re: 1st Birthday Party Places in Astoria?
Post by: danigirl on September 13, 2007, 09:51:13 AM
I had my son's birthday party (when he turned 5) at the Astoria Sports Complex.  It was nice but expensive.  I had about 15 kids and we had swimming and the super slide.  (you can choose 2 activities like soccer, basketball, swimming)  If I remember correctly it was like $20 a kid plus the pizza & drinks for the adults plus tax plus they also charged gratuity. One of the nice things they do is take pictures for you of the kids having fun and then put it on a cd for you.  (you wind up paying for it but it is nice) It was more than I expected. 
They next year I had has party at the bowling ally (Maric lanes).  That was fun!!  The kids had a blast.  They put up the bumpers for the kids.  They also have a room upstairs - depending how many people you are having. 
Good luck in finding a place.
