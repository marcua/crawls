Title: Taste of Subway
Post by: lanseaux on June 11, 2006, 12:02:57 AM
An intriguing, if perhaps somewhat unfortunately named project, Taste of Subway (http://idlewords.com/taste_of_subway/) -- Taste of N Train (http://idlewords.com/taste_of_subway/taste_of_n_train.htm), that purports to list all the restaurants within 200 meters of each subway stop, organised by line.  Of course, in Astoria, that leaves out a tremendous number beyond a few blocks east or west of 31st St., but at least it's a start.

It's not entirely up to date or complete yet.  Here's (http://idlewords.com/) the home page of the project, where its organiser says that it's "part of an upcoming New York City restaurant bookmarking service called MUBOT."
