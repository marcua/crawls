A Reading of True Stories of Social Change

(1/1)

Nevart:
Heightening Stories

presents

"True Stories of Social Change"

a reading of recent work

by members of the Creative Nonfiction/Autobiographical Fiction Workshop


Come hear captivating, heartfelt stories of loss and transition set against backdrops of resistance during WWI in Europe & the Middle East, apartheid-era South Africa, 1960s race relations in Queens, and the politics of aging in Florida.  With writers Malcolm Chang, Jackie Giurato, Harry Koumrouyan and Generose Lambert. Hosted by local author and Heightening Stories founder/instructor Nancy Agabian.

Wednesday, October 21, 2015
7:00pm
Free!
