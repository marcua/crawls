Title: Christian Finnegan @ Comix
Post by: kambric on February 27, 2009, 03:37:17 PM
Christian Finnegan, long time resident of Astoria, will be headlining Comix on March 13th & 14th.

Christian has a discount code "CFEB" good for $5 off each ticket. 

FRI, MAR 13 @ 8:00 & 10:30 PM
SAT, MAR 14 @ 8:00 & 10:30 PM
Comix
353 West 14th Street

Tickets are $22 in advance and $27 day-of-show plus a two item (food &/or drink) minimum in the show room and can be purchased online at www.comixny.com or by calling 212.524.2500.

DINNER PACKAGES are available for $77.75 per person and includes Inner Circle seating for the show, a three-course Prix Fixe dinner (See website for details) and includes 18% gratuity.

Here's the release from the Comix site:

Finnegan is a stand-up comedian, writer and actor based in New York City. A long time panelist on VH1's hit series "Best Week Ever", he is a regular contributor to MSNBC's "Countdown with Keith Olbermann". Other credits include his own half-hour special, "Comedy Central Presents: Christian Finnegan," "The Late Late Show with Craig Ferguson" and numerous appearances on "The Today Show". You might also have caught him on "Chappelle's Show" playing Chad, the only white roommate in the infamous "Mad Real World" sketch.

Finnegan recently filmed a new one hour comedy special titled "Au Contraire" to air on Comedy Central in Spring 2009 and will be distributed by Warner Brothers. His debut stand-up CD "Two for Flinching" was released by Comedy Central Records and is available in stores everywhere, on iTunes and at ComedyCentral.com.

Additionally, Finnegan co-starred in the film Eden Court with Kimberly Williams-Paisley & Reno 911's Tom Lennon premiering April 2007 and served as a staff writer for Comedy Central’s Tough Crowd with Colin Quinn.

For Finnegan’s complete resume, photos, video clips, calendar of events and more, visit www.christianfinnegan.com. “Two for Flinching” is available in stores everywhere, on iTunes and at ComedyCentral.com.

P.S. Christian Finnegan thinks you're a wonderful and talented human being.
