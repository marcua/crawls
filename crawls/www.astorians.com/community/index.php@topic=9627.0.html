Dodgeball in Astoria & Touch Football in LIC - 1 Week Left to Register!
« on: February 27, 2008, 12:12:04 PM »
Looking for some fun, social people for co-ed, social, charity-focused DODGEBALL league in Astoria and Touch Football in Long Island City.

We have a blast every Tuesday night at a school gym reliving those carefree elementary school days by throwing red foam balls at opposing teams! Then, unlike grade school, we got to a nearby bar and drink after!

Not into Dodgeball? Want to get outside this spring? On Saturdays or Sundays we play awesome games of co-ed Touch Football in Long Island City. Never played before? No problem - we've got a division for that! Ditto for competitive players! Then we party after the games!

The best part? It's all for charity since each team gets to pick a charity to play for. Plus the league has different divisions of play so if you think you're an expert player you can join the Players Division or if you want to keep it low-key you can join the Extremely Casual Division or any in between!

You can join as a full team or as an individual or small group and we place you on a team. It's a great way to meet new people!

All the info is at www.zogsports.org but registration ends next week, so sign up soon!
