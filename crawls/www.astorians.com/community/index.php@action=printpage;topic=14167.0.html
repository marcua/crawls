Title: Purim Party Raffle Tickets to Support Kehillat Tikvah!
Post by: KehillatTikvah on March 04, 2009, 01:31:07 AM
Try Your Luck for Purim, The Jewish Holiday of Chance (Even If You're Not Jewish)!

With a $2 raffle ticket you support an inclusive and welcoming new Jackson Heights congregation and could win one of the following:
 
* Paella Gift Set consisting of 14-inch carbon steel pan, 1-gram jar pure Spanish saffron, one tin pimenton and ½ kilo bag of bomba rice
 
* $25 gift certificate to Espresso 77
 
* Gourmet basket with assorted Middle Eastern spices, nuts and recipes
 
* Autographed copy of “Knives Cooks Love” by Jackson Heights author Sarah Jay and Sur La Table (2008, Andrews McMeel)
 
* Autographed copy of “All of Baby, Nose to Toes,” a picture book by Jackson Heights author Victoria Adler (June 2009, Dial)
 
Tickets available at Espresso 77 now through 3:30 p.m. Sunday and at our Purim Party (Sunday, March 8 from 4-7 p.m. at Community Methodist Church, 35th Ave between 81st and 82nd.) Five winning tickets will be drawn at the party; winners not present will be notified the following day by email.
 
All proceeds benefit Kehillat Tikvah: A Jewish Community of Hope in Jackson Heights. To learn more about Kehillat Tikvah and the Purim party, please go to http://jhtikvah.wordpress.com, join our Facebook group or call 718-569-1887.
 
Special thanks to Espresso 77, Sarah Jay/PaellaPans.com, Karyn Slutsky and Victoria Adler for their generous donations.
