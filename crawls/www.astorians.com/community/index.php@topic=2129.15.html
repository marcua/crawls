Yeah, Barb can be a little heavy-handed and self-righteous, but I see that as her responding to being WAY over her head and unable to come to grips with how little real power she had. This is the second time in the last two weeks that she has tried to rally the other wives against Bill - the first was successful, this one wasn't.

I don't like the way Bill mislead her this time and I really felt for her. It wouldn't have been so bad that he just did it without asking, but the pretending - that the wives actually had a real choice, that their opinion really counted for something - and also that he had told Barb first - was disgusting.

I think the gaming business is going to suck them deeper and deeper into the shadowy Juniper Creek world ... I don't think it will really provide the kind of protection that Bill said it would. You could argue that Margene made the decision for herself, but don't forget that she also really, really wants to please Bill and he had already approached her about it and she just said "Tell me what you want me to think". If she had said no, she would have been in a standoff with Barb against Nicki and Bill, and I think it's not in her nature to be non-devisive - and on some level she is also aware that it serves her well to stand by Bill and that he would get his own way in the end anyway.
