Title: Re: Cinema Paradiso Resturant
Post by: goRaina on February 28, 2008, 08:35:53 PM
My husband and I had dinner there before seeing a movie, and since it wasn't too crowded we thought an hour would be enough time to eat before our show.

We were wrong. The service was slow, we had to ask/remind the waiter to bring us water, our bread, and our drinks. The salad I ordered mentioned olives on the menu; when my salad showed up without olives and I asked for some on the side, they tried to charge me extra; overall the food was mediocre at best. And expensive!

The manager apologized to us on several occasions and chewed out the waiter in our presence, but the whole experience left a bad taste in my mouth.
