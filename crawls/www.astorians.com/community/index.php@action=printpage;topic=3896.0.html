Title: Beginners Course in Yiddish: Mondays, Oct 23-Dec 11 (8 weeks)
Post by: abopevents on October 22, 2006, 09:49:37 PM
If you are curious about the heritage of Ashkenazic culture, come and learn to speak, read, and write Yiddish! As a beginner you will enjoy discovering this wonderful legacy: the key to Yiddishkayt is access to Yiddish language. The world of Yiddish is not lost, itâ€™s all out there if you only scrape the surface. Through an interactive method of learning songs and reading simple texts with an experienced, native speaker, you will start putting together the pieces of the puzzle. The instructor, Isabelle Rozenbaumas, was trained as a historian and for the last 10 years has worked as a translator, editor, librarian, and filmmaker. Before leaving France in 2005, she was a Librarian at the Museum of Art and History of Judaism in Paris. The fee is $12 per session per person and you must commit to the entire cost of the eight week class ($96). Classes will be Monday nights at 7-8:30pm. Please register at info@astoriacenterofisrael.org  to help us better gauge class size.
