There has been some interest in putting together another CSA for next year -- with a pick up/distribution more convenient to folks in central/southern Astoria.

I'm calling for an informal organizational/brainstorming meeting on this potential new CSA development.

Details:  Monday 9/11
Time: 8:30-9:30
Place: Cafe Bar

You do NOT need to be a current CSA member to attend.  Meeting is open to anyone and discussion will revolve around the "how to" of setting up a new CSA.
