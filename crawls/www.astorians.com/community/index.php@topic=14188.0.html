I just went with my boyfriend yesterday to Da Franco's. we heard it was an italian restaurant associated with forno italia.. since we've been going to forno's for a while we thought we'd give it a try and the food was amazing!!! we spoke to the very italian, accent & all, owner and it turns out that he's one of the owners from forno italia and decided to go solo this time around. The staff was friendly and helpful, you can tell though that they seemed a little nervous. We started out with the pizza margherita. It was incredible how much it looked and tasted like the pizza we've had in italy. We then had the penne del sindaco and ravioli tartufo.. both delicious. Overall everything on the menu was very traditional and surpising to see such a wide variety. it's about time we got a good authentic italian restaurant in the area.
It is also worth mentioning that they have gelato imported from italy.. soo good!!!
Yeah there was a few glitches but its understandable considering it was the first day open.  cant wait to go back. i'll deff be a regular!
