"To fight mildew, mix 2/3 cup of baking soda with 1/3 cup of borax.  Add enough water to form a paste (just a few drops), mix well, and scrub the misture into mildewed areas.  Let it sit for a few hours, and then rinse the area well with warm water.  Fod extra cleaning power, add 10 drops of lemon essential oil."
