In defense of Telly's Taverna
« Reply #10 on: December 22, 2006, 05:22:52 PM »
We went to Telly's Taverna for the first time last night; we had put off trying it because many people (especially on Chowhounds, but also on this board) seemed to prefer Agnanti and Kyclades.  Of Greek restaurants in other parts of Astoria (Ploes, Oveilia, S'agapo, etc.), I cannot speak, for I have yet to try them.  But we had the grilled (whole) striped bass at Telly's - I picked it out at the counter and everything - , and it was as good if not better than the fish we had at Kyclades; absolutely fresh and delicious.  And the dips at Telly's (Tzatziki, taramasalata, skordalia, and whatever the spicy feta one is called) were much tastier (not to mention more garlicky) than the ones we had at Agnanti and Kyclades (Agnanti's Tzatziki may win, but as for the other ones, Telly's seemed superior).  And the loukomades on the house is a nice touch.

This is not to argue that one is better or worse than another - just that we had the fish at Telly's, and it was quite good.  As for meat dishes, we didn't try it, so I couldn't tell you.
