Congratulations for finally moving to the best neighborhood in Queens!

You should not miss an opportunity to check out Cafe Soleil, on 37th St. & Astoria Blvd.  They recently re-opened, and I'm soo thankful!   

Soleil has top-notch teas and coffee, very cozy ambiance and free wi-fi that will be coming in a week or so.  The owners are really friendly and take good care of you. 

Why bother with Starbucks when local is better!  :mrgreen:

Two of my favorite spots in Astoria are Martha's, on Ditmars and 37th Street, which recently added outdoor seating but does not have the same "vibe" as the 30th Avenue cafes, and Cafe Bar, on 34th Avenue and 36th Street.  Martha's is a good choice for coffee and a pastry...not much in the way of snack foods, whereas Cafe Bar offers lots of sandwiches and snacks, as well as a pretty large coffee and tea selection but less in the way of desserts or pastries.

The wine bar you are speaking of, Crescent & Vine, does not have coffee nor wifi. They also do not typically open until 5, which might be a little late for the cafe type.

In the Ditmars area, Freeze Peach and Martha's are both great suggestions. Oleput, when it is not remodeling (I think they are open now) is another good place to kick back with wifi and a beverage.

I'm also a big fan of Cafe Soliel. They are very relaxed with super comfy seating. From the Ditmars area, it is a tad of a trek, but when I lived on Crescent and Ditmars, the quality and quantity of time I spent at the Astoria Blvd and 37th st cafe was worth the walk.
