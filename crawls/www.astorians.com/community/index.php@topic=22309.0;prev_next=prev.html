I dunno if gochujang comes in a bottle, it usually comes in a box, but it is mighty tasty and spicy. There's also an H Mart in Woodside, which carries like 90% Korean foods, sauces, etc. If you take the 7 train towards Flushing and get off at 61st st. you head towards the Duane Reade and you'll see it off to the side. There's also a liquor store next to the H Mart that sells cheap soju too. (There's H Marts in Manhattan and Flushing too, but I think the Woodside one is closer, plus there are probably some buses in Astoria that can get from Astoria to Woodside faster than the train can too.)
