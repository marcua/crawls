2nd Best Thrift Shop
« on: February 20, 2013, 01:35:34 PM »
About a week ago, I was cleaning out my garage and threw out some old furniture in the hopes that someone could use everything. The next morning things were gone and I was happy.

A few days later, I am walking by the 2nd best thrift shop, and there was one of the pieces I threw out. It was actually being sold. I asked the person working how much, and they told me $45. I laughed and told her that it was actually a piece that I put on the curb a few nights before.

Just so you are aware that they are selling people's garbage instead of allowing people from the neighborhood to use it.
