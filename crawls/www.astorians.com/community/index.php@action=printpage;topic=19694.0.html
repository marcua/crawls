Title: Jackson Heights Halloween Parade -- Calling Harry Potter fans
Post by: tgoldman on October 27, 2010, 07:55:02 PM
Calling all Harry Potter fans!

Please join us at this year's Halloween Parade in Jackson Heights, where we'll be marching to celebrate the 4th Annual Quidditch World Cup!

Yes, believe it or not, Quidditch has crossed over into the muggle world, and the World Cup will be coming to New York City this November (see http://www.internationalquidditch.org/worldcup/ for details).

We're especially looking for wizards, witches, and monsters from the Harry Potter novels, but fans of all ages are welcome to join us, regardless of costume. 

The Jackson Heights Halloween Parade is the second-largest Halloween parade in New York City, and one of the city's largest children's parades held all year.  We'll be gathering on 89th Street, between 35th and 37th Avenues, on Sunday, October 31st, at 11:30 am.   The parade starts at Noon.  (To get there by transit, take the 7 train to 90th Street and walk north).   Just look for the Quidditch World Cup banner!
