I was accosted by one helper walking in the door yesterday who wanted to know if I needed help. I said, no, I'm ordering a beer and then heading out back. Well then, I was told, could she get me someone to take my order for me at the table? I said, no, I'd prefer to order here then join my friend already at a table. She said, well then he probably has a server already, you should go out there. I did, and there were about 10 of these "servers" lined up like an army just outside the door.
We did get that table service (it seemed mandatory, which was quite off-putting), and we helped her through ordering (seems they are all "new" and none of them know the menu yet). I ordered a Becherovka and was met with an inquisitive glance, one trip to the bar, a return question and finally my friend pointed to the GIANT banner in the yard advertising it and said, "he wants that."
It was really awkward.
It was a nightmare. That place is gone.
What a damn shame.
