Queens World Film Festival Call for Volunteers on March 3, 4, 5 & 6th

Thank you for joining us as we prepare to provide an engaging film going experience to filmmakers and their audiences from around the world and around the corner. We plan to screen over 100 films in two locations, with 2 industry panels and several Q and A’s. View the schedule at www.queensworldfilmfestival.com.

We anticipate around 3,000 people at our events and we want every customer to have the best possible experience and we want to give the filmmakers the best possible support. To that end, QWFF call for volunteers to greet, hospitality, box office, host, production, projectionist and outreach.  Contact me at lucygrad@yahoo.com to attend the QWFF Volunteer Kick-off event this Thursday, Feb 24th in Jackson Heights.
