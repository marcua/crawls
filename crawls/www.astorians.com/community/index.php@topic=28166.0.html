Hi I work for RCN and I couldn't help but notice this post. That is correct, if your cable box broke, we would replace it for free. I would not recommend going to Direct TV. Your signal is susceptible to the weather, their channel lineups get exponentially more costly for the better channels, you are responsible for the equipment, and they have no "boots on the ground". You would have to call into a call center based out of "god knows where", meanwhile RCN is based out of Queens (LIC).
