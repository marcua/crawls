The white cat looks good, but some of the others...well, I dunno - the shave is too close.
I have 5 cats . One has a very thick undercoat that gets matted and sheds all the time, even with constant combing. IMO the only drawback is the fact that the vet has to tranquilize the cat in order to do a nice shave.
