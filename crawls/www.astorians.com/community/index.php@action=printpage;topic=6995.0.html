Post by: ladydi on July 16, 2007, 12:01:36 PM
I belong to The Rock, over at Ditmars. They're offering private pilates and yoga sessions for members,
but I would think that non-members could probably also schedule sessions since the trainers there have clients
who are members and non-members. They would probably just have to find a space to work with you
since Synergy probably wouldn't allow a trainer from another gym on the premises.

Your options are limited in Astoria. If you really want excellent pilates private instruction, I recommend
Power Pilates on West 23rd between 5th & 6th Aves. Website is www.powerpilates.com.

They have a great intro package where you get a private class, a mat class and a massage for, I think, around
$130  -- check the site for that.

They also do private and semi-private classes where you bring a couple of people for a small, intimate group and
it saves you $$.

PP is great - I go there in addition to my classes at the Rock.

You also mentioned the Yoga Room. I think that one of the instructors at the Rock may teach there too and if so,
she's wonderful.

Good luck.
