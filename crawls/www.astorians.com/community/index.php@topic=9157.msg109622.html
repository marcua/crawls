What a great game the Giants had last night. They're one of the few teams in NFL history to beat two teams on the road in a row during the playoffs. If they can pull it together and send old man Favre packing, they'll be (I'm pretty sure) the first team in NFL history to win three road games in the post season.

Also, I could not believe the holding call on the Chargers during the second quarter. I watched the play over and over again (thank god for DVR) and for the life of me could not find the hold. None of the sports casters could find it during the half time shows either. What a load. It was arguably the worst referee call that I have ever seen. I'm sure the Monday "sorry for the penalty" letter will be nice, but that almost cost them the game. Great to see the Chargers rally against Indy.

Congrats, Eli, you're finally on your way out of your brother's shadow. And Peyton, you better buy some bigger shirts.
