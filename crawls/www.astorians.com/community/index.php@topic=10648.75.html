The fiance was getting his hair cut at SPAce and we decided to go ahead and get iced chai teas at Freeze Peach afterward, you know, see what all the fuss was about.  They were good.  Not the most delicious I've ever had, but solid and relatively inexpensive, and served in an eco-friendly cup which I appreciated.  People seemed nice enough (no one had the glazed over, slightly fevered look I'd associate with cult members).  I'd stop in again if I were in the neighborhood, but I don't know that I'd go out of my way to get a beverage from them.  And that's my Freeze Peach review, for better or worse.
