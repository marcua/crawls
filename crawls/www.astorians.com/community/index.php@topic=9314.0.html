Not another Rite Aid....Enough Already
« on: January 26, 2008, 11:44:57 AM »
So I was going home and walked by the old Blockbuster on Steinway and 34th.  I noticed that now Theo and Theo next door has gone out.  There are now 3-4 spaces next to each other that have all gone out.  I thought great, something big is probably buying all the space and will open something great here.  Nope.
I looked on the paperwork on the Blockbuster windows and low and behold another Rite Aid is coming in.  Do we really need another one.  There is one on Steinway and 31st (Where Eckerds was), Broadway and Steinway and now it will be Steinway and 34th.  3 in a 3 block radius.  I am starting to think they are the new Starbucks.  Sad thing is, they aren't that good.  I would prefer a Duane Reade or Walgreens. 
Come on...do we need yet another Rite Aid here.  Who approved this for the neighborhood and how can we stop it.  Enough of the inanity. 
Sorry....felt like I needed to vent and rant.  Curious what everyone thinks though.
