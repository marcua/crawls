Re: Bareburger on 31st Ave
« Reply #184 on: September 20, 2011, 11:30:49 AM »
Went there for the first time Sunday night with my Boyfriend.
My Burger was tasty, but not the best thing ever.
The fries were really good and the dipping sauces were awesome. The beer was good and cheap. The service was sort of slow but I didn't have any real complaints. All of the servers were really nice and they got our order right.
I will say that for the Vegans out there this place is awesome. My boyfriend had the veggie burger and he said it's probably the best veggie burger he's ever had.
And sitting outside was nice.
 
So overall I think this place is a great addition to the neighborhood and fine for a nice lunch or dinner. It definitely isn't gourmet or the best burger you will ever have and it isn't fast food by any means, but for a nice afternoon sitting outside and chilling with some burgers and beers I think it's a good option.
 
Plus the milk shakes looked awesome..I didn't have one but I need to go back for one. The girls next to us seemed to be really enjoying theirs...they were making me jealous!
