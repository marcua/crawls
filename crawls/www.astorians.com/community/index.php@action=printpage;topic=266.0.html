Title: Also check out Habitat for Humanity Restore - Build It Green
Post by: Justin on October 04, 2005, 10:53:31 AM
Also check out our new non-profit store for salvaged and surplus building materials called rather awkwardly Habitat for Humanity Restore - Build It Green. Right in Astoria at 3-17 26th Ave at 4th Street which is tucked in just north of Astoria Houses on the waterfront.  Our website www.bignyc.org has more info.

We recycle unwanted building materials to raise money for environmental education and Habitat for Humanity.

We have doors, cabinets, windows, paint, wallpaper, picture frame trim, other lumber, sinks, toilets, appliances, lights, hardware, flooring and other odds and ends.  Come check us out and spread the word.

Title: Q66 bus
Post by: claire_wil on October 19, 2005, 05:53:06 PM
I know this is quite awhile since the original question, but the Q66 bus stops right on Northern Blvd, right in front of Home Depot. It runs along 35th avenue from 21st street to just past Steinway Street, so you can connect to it from the N (36th ave stop) R (steinway) or several buses which run along 21st street, 31st street or steinway street.
