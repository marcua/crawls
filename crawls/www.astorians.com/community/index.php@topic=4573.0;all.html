NY Hosp of Queens (formerly Booth) in Flushing has truly raised it's standards.  The old Booth was not very good at all and most referred to it as a 'chop shop', but the with the new affiliation, the NY Hosp of Queens now has a Trauma 1 rating, heliport and very highly rated physician staff.  Many of their leading physicians (heads of depts, etc) are on staff and teaching professors and researchers at the top NYC medical schools.  I would not hesitate to recommend this facility.  I've had recent experience with trauma, cardiac care and neurology staff - all very good.
