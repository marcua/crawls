Hi Everyone,

Over the past five years I have made it my mission to patronize many of the shops, restaurants and specialty stores in Astoria.  I have also taken advantage of services provided by individuals who live in the area.  We each have our criteria for what we consider to be establishments worth patronizing on a regular basis.  For me, at the top of my list, great customer service is key.

This posting is the first in a series of what I call "My Favorites in Astoria".  My focus this time is "Cleaning Services".

For the past year I have been using a woman who is one of the most versatile individuals I know.  She does not just clean.  She does whatever it takes to keep your home clean and organized.  She uses her time efficiently and works at a steady pace that allows her to not only be efficient with her time, but thorough as well.  She charges by the hour and is very reasonable.

So, if you are looking for a great cleaning person I would like to recommend mine.  She lives in Astoria, just blocks away from Ditmars.  She has given me permission to give her phone number to anyone interested in her services.

For additional information, please send me an email.

Bye for now!
