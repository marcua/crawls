Just be aware of excess fees and interest -- the money may be coming from the same place, but your out-of-pocket amount may be different.  Be very careful what excess fees and interest rates you may be triggering by obtaining a cash advance from a credit card  or using a credit card check.  Either of these options usually has fees attached, and they also often have higher interest rates than a normal credit card payment if you end up carrying any of the balance.  If you pay the balance off immediately you shouldn't be charged any interest (although individual card agreements vary), but you will still likely get hit with a fee.
