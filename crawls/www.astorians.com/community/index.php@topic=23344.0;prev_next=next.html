^ What she said.


I'm actually sick of complimenting this place; I have tried about 10 different dishes, and every one was very good or great; there's not one that I wouldn't happily order again.


The one small drawback, and it is a very small one, is that this tiny place is sometimes full of family members uncle-ing hither and yon.


Now, mind you, they're very nice, and very attentive, and they're not sitting there eating and taking up the tables (they really only did that during their little opening-week celebration). In fact, these days they're often helping out with table service and take-out orders, holding the door open for customers, and so forth. It's just that, in such a small place, it gets a little crowded with all of them underfoot.


I generally just choose to find them amusing; as I say, it's a very small drawback in a wonderful little place like this.
