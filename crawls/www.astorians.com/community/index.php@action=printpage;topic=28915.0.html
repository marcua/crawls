Title: Mardi Gras at Bourbon and Vine
Post by: roccodelgreco on February 11, 2015, 05:29:05 PM
Bourbon and Vine, Astoria’s premiere whiskey and wine bar, is hosting a five day festival of food and drink celebrating Mardi Gras from Friday, February 13, 2015 through “Fat Tuesday,” February 17, 2015. Bourbon and Vine is open daily at 3pm and remains open until the early A.M. hours.

[/size]In true speak-easy fashion, Bourbon and Vine is celebrating the joy of Mardi Gras in our own way, right on Astoria’s famous 30th Avenue (formerly known as Grand Avenue). Bourbon and Vine boasts one of the most eclectic whiskey collections in New York City, offering something for everyone, from bourbon virgins to whiskey aficionados. Our Prohibition-era cocktails are a throwback to classic style, hand crafted cocktails, where the skill of a professionally trained bartender makes all the difference.

Not to be outdone, Bourbon and Vine is proud to relaunch its kitchen under the direction of Chef Thomas Pepe, a native Astorian with over 18 years of experience with a range of worldly cuisine. “For Mardi Gras we are bringing out the classics, the familiar New Orleans home-cookin’ that turns eating into a party of bold flavors. Come and sample the food, it will keep the hand without a drink very happy,”said Thomas Pepe, Executive Chef. Featured menu selections for Mardi Gras will be: Chicken or Crawfish and Andouille Gumbo, “Dirty Dirty” Rice, Zucchini Hush Puppies, Cajun Style Chicken Fries and Walnut and Cherry Bread Pudding with Whiskey Sauce. Friday through Fat Tuesday, guests can enjoy one free dish from our Mardi Gras menu as we welcome them back to enjoy food at Bourbon and Vine.
