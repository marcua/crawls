After having a surprisingly good experience at Just Burgers on Ditmars, I made it a point to try this place over the weekend.  I really liked it.  I think the issues they had at the beginning may be solved.  I saw no growing pains or slip ups at all.  Service was prompt and very friendly.  The short rib quesadilla is delicious.  It's on a cheddar/jalapeno soft tortilla.  Really good combination of flavors and a generous portion of short rib meat inside.  I had the Frenchy Burger.  The foie gras was seared nicely - as good as any French bistro.  The burger was a perfect medium (how they cook all their burgers) and the sauteed onions and mushrooms were very tasty and just the right temperature and texture.  I'm not a fan of the brioche bun.  But that doesn't concern me as I'm not there for the bread.  We had the sweet potato waffle fries.  Excellent.  No greasiness at all and not over seasoned.  If you ask me, each of these burger places has done a nice job filling a unique niche.  Five Napkin, Burger Club, Bareburger, and Just Burgers all have very different menus and things they do well.  I like all of them almost equally depending on what I'm in the mood for.
