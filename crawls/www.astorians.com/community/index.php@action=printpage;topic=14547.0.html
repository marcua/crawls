Man, I used to love my Breakfast Treats dunked into hot morning coffee.  But I haven't had 'em for a long while  :cry:.  And I won't!  Whoa, you say, I see them in the stores all the time!  Yeah, those are scab cookies!  The firm that bought them out from Kraft in 2006, some entity called Brynwood Partners, locked out the BCTGM Local 50 striking workers and replaced them all with non-union bakers.

Just the other day I saw a Stella D'Oro product on the shelf at Jack's 99 cents.  Proof fewer people are buying them in the full-price stores.
