Title: Re: Fried Turkey
Post by: Christine on November 21, 2008, 08:48:11 PM
We had some Jive Turkey for lunch today, in fact. My co-worker collected $20 from each of us (about 10 people) and picked it up in Brooklyn (they don't deliver this time of year.)

I was expecting something like KFC, but instead it was a flavorful outer crust and very succulent meat throughout- not bad at all! The "Yams brulee" were mashed sweet potatoes w/mini marshmallows and pineapple bits- not a crowd pleaser with the pineapple. Also had regular mashed, green beans with onions, and stuffing- pretty standard.

This is great if you have to entertain, but don't want to go nuts cooking and don't want to spend a fortune elsewhere. BUT, it's a long way to go for take-out when you'd do just fine for the same price (actually, less!) eating out here in town. Normally, I'd refuse to pay $20 for a basic lunch (it's not like I piled it all on) but I didn't want to be a wet blanket. Besides, I did want to try fried turkey at least once!
