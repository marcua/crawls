This is what it looks like:

http://www.fitnessinfomercialreview.com/reviews/torsotrack2

It folds pretty much in half and I simply store mine standing up against the wall in a spare room.

Some people get lower back pain when they use this incorrectly with bad posture. You have to keep your back a alittle stiff (a tiny bit convex rather than concave) like you're doing push-ups. Keep that in mind and no back pain will ensue. After a time off due to laziness I start off with 60 reps if I'm in decent shape. I usually do anywhere from 80-100 reps on it set at high tension after a couple of weeks of continuous use.
