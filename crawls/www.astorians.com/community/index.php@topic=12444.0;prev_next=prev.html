There are some restaurants and a Hair Salon in Astoria that are fabulous:

Soho Cafe & Grille on 31st off Ditmars Blvd. (next door to Rock Health & Fitness)

East Moon Fusion Restaurant - serves the best Sushi I have ever tasted.  They offer, Chinese, Japanese, Thai & Vietnamese food.  They opened almosst two months ago and already have a steady following.  Most recently I had their Sweet 'n Sour Steamed Sesame Scallops with Brown Rice & Vegees - Fabulous.

New Image Hair Salon - On Ditmars Blvd. a couple of doors down from McCann's Pub - They are all great, but ask for Jen.  I use her and she gave the best hair cut I have ever had.  They also do extensions and great hair coloring.

1. Rim's Laundromat on 30th/37th - Men's shirts washed and pressed $1.65/each - and they are immaculately done!
2. Titan Greek Foods - great selection of greek stuff and friendly staff
3.  Paul Gans, MD...28th Avenue and 33rd Street - going into his office is like stepping back into the 50s but   he is a truly excellent and compassionate dr.
4.  VideoExpress - 30th Ave b/w 34th and 35th Streets - great family owned business, friendly service and great selection of movies - including esoteric stuff.
5.  Taco Truck - 30th and 33rd - El Rey is the Mack!
6.  Pinocchio on 30th and 32nd. Nice food and atmosphere.  Highly recommend the pasta with bolognese sauce - I order the family size when I have people over for dinner - It feeds about 8 people and it's cheap and delicious - @$22!!!
