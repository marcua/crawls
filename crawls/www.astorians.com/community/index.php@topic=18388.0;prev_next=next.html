It's really irritating to drive on.
Semi-related story:

My car was legally parked on Ditmars and 27th last week. I come by that Thursday, and it's gone. I see a bunch of paper signs indicating that the cars there were re-located for road construction- OK, usually no big deal. I walk around a few blocks to see if they moved it closeby, but see nothing. I call the 114th precinct, and after 45 minutes of trying to get through, I finally get to speak to a real, live person! I give them the license plate number and after 20 minutes on hold, the person at the precinct tells me there is no record of my car being moved. I ask her what to do, and she tells me to call 911 and report it stolen. Finally, I get frustrated, and hang up. I think to myself that it is entirely too coincidental for my car to disappear, and assume some idiot has screwed up the log book.
A friend was kind enough to come by, drive me around and, ten blocks away- there it is. My car, with a note on the windshield from the DOT stating that the car was moved for road construction.

One more point for the 114th- thanks again for nothing, you idiots.  :mrgreen:
