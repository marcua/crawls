On a serious note, and I write this here because I know that everyone takes a peek in here (yes you do!!!)  I just want to say that it's sadden me to see what's been going on for the past few days and I hope that when the changes are done that all these past events and opinions are taken into consideration.  This isn't just a board, this is our neighborhood and the people the frequent this board are our neighbors that we have met,  perhaps not everyone but a good amount so that takes us to a different level than other boards, we actually do know each other (at least some of us) and I truly believe that a lot of new friendships have been made and that also needs to be taken into consideration, we are not strangers here, we are a living thriving community, we a real, we have seen faces person to person.  When I moved back to Astoria after being gone so many years it was hard, I did find "some" of my childhood friends but making new friends is a whole different ballgame given that most of us work, go to school, or both and have very busy lives.  This board has made it possible for the us to really be neighbors.  The fact that there's another board is just an indication of how diverse Astoria is and how good we Astorians are at that, at being able to live with the diversity and differences most of the time in a harmonious way, sometimes not but that's OK too, we are different so there will sometimes be some controversy but in the end...we stick up for each other and we care for each other ... and if we want to know what happened to someone it's probably because we miss them.

On that note.......I STILL WIN
