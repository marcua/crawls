Title: Knife Attack at No Partners on 21st ave (Between 29th/31st street)
Post by: Pawgny on May 06, 2012, 07:17:22 PM
Just was told by the bartender that works at No Partners Bar (29-11 21st Avenue) that there was a stabbing/knife attack at the bar.  This occurred during the early hours (3am - 4am) of May 6th.
According to the bartender, six hispanic men came into the bar for a few.  Everyone was having a good time until the bar was about to close.  One of the six men became irate over the closing of the bar and demanded to be served further.  The unknown suspect(s) attacked the owner and slashed his face/neck.  A patron came to the owner's defense and attacked the assailant(s) with a poolstick.  This patron then became the next target and was slashed in the arm.
The owner is still being treated as we speak and the patron has been released.
The group of men in question have yet to be found and the incident is currently being investigated by the NYPD.  **Hopefully the security cameras will provide results**
If you know something, say something - contact the local 114th PCT.
