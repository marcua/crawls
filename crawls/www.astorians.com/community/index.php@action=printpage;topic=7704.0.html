Title: NY Calling (Book I wrote for...)
Post by: DogBert on August 28, 2007, 09:57:28 PM
Anyone that reads a lot and is interested in NYC in general will find this book to be pretty cool I think:
NY Calling: Blackout to Bloomberg. It is a collection of essays that create a recent history of NYC. Many many good writers in this thing. I'm honored to be amongst them. The chapter I wrote is about graffiti, and has many many Astoria references... hell, it's framed around my commute to work on the N and 4/5 trains...

Anyway, if ya want, give it a look:
http://www.amazon.com/New-York-Calling-Blackout-Bloomberg/dp/1861893388/ref=pd_bbs_sr_1/102-9423031-8458562?ie=UTF8&s=books&qid=1188352341&sr=8-1

At almost 400 pages this thing is quite the read... and for $16.50, a good deal me thinks.
