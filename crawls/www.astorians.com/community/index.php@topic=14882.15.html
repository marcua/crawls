Ramadan is a good guy. He used to by my dentist.
He's quick and painless with his work.

That brings me to why he used to be my dentist. While he can get in there and fix things, he didn't offer me much in the way of advice to prevent cavities. I know there's not much you can tell an adult beyond what they should already know. Brush 2-3 times and floss. But I feel like he could offer some kind of advice beyond "People get cavities."

My new dentist is Dr. Herzlinger on Steinway. An awesome woman. She gives me that motherly vibe I hope a medical person would/should be like in caring for their patients.

If you're good with your dental hygiene and are just looking for someone in your network to do work or just see for cleanups, out of the last I know I can recommend Ezzat Ramadan. (If you're cash strapped, he's pretty good about working out a plan of payment with you. Very good about the whole money issue.)
