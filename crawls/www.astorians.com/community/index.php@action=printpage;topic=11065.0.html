Title: Re: Marvelous flowering street trees that smell like honeysuckle?
Post by: lanseaux on June 25, 2008, 12:55:14 PM
It might be an American Linden (aka Basswood) tree.  They are very popular street trees in NYC.  We've got one out our living room window that's flowering right now, and the scent is just amazing.  Very fragrant - I'd say lighter and more perfumey than honeysuckle -- less honeylike.

I've looked for a picture online, but I can't find a really good one.  In any event ours is a young one -- the leaves are rather large, and almost rounded, and grow linearly off branches.  The flowers are smaller, and yellow, and are kind of under the leaves.  Very dense foliage.
