There's a subtle problem with that you want to do. The contacts application once started and used (either directly from the contacts icon or from the contacts tab once you're in the phone app) remembers where you last were. Hence, if someone were to open your contacts list, they'd see what you were last browsing.

If they're smart enough they'll figure out how to get to contacts beginning with ICE. Or hit the phone icon and see favorites and recents. I think the hardest problem for non-iPhone users would be unlocking the phone ;)
