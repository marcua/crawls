When there were many conflicting threads, with practically the same title, there may have been some confusion. If people didn't turn off their notification emails, well there *was* a lot of that generated by this thread.

If you want to enjoy your esoteric conversations, go ahead. Knock yourselves out.

If you find the intricate details of the taco brilliant conversation, enjoy.

If you can afford to eat out, or take out food as frequently as it seems you do, please converse on this subject to your heart's content.

If you prefer to maintain a vegan lifestyle, I applaud your commitment to your personal health, and I wish you success.

If you want to chat about movies, or musical groups, or pets (yours and mine)—great. They help inspire others to become more vocal on our board.

These and all the other myriad of threads make this board what it is to all of us: interesting, informative and somewhat addictive. This thread is simply for fun.

But when you blatantly denigrate both the thread, and thereby the people, who are just having a little fun in Last Poster Wins, you step over the bounds of graciousness. Because a group of very nice folks want to have a little fun, and do something that they will always be remembered by, and that they will always remember, no-one has the right to denigrate either the thread or those who post to it.

TO ALL THOSE WHO HAVE NEVER POSTED HERE: We know you *do* look to see what is happening. We know simply based on the number of views this thread has received. Yes you. You all know who you are. You have some curiosity or you wouldn't be looking.

We don't knock what you are doing on this board. Please be kind enough to leave us out of your *other* conversations as we respectfully do of your choices.
