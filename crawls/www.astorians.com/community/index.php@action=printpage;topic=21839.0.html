Title: Risorgimento! Unification of Italy Saturday, October 22
Post by: greentiger on October 20, 2011, 07:46:14 AM
Greater Astoria Historical Society event  Risorgimento! Unification of Italy Saturday, October 22, 2011 1:00 pm $5.00 (GAHS members: FREE)

 Join us as we commemorate 150 years of Italian Unification. Joseph DiPietro, President of Federation of Italian American Organizations of Queens, Inc. will speak on Italian history. A discussion will follow with Italian fun facts, a short film and a power point presentation focused on Italian American celebrities and those whose roots are from Astoria/LIC. Stories of Italian American food, culture, and music are welcome. We will end with a question and answer period and guests are invited to share their Italian stories
