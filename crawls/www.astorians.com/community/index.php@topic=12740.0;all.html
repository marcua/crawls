I'm a recent fan of this place:

Hair Shapers
33-12 Broadway
Astoria NY 11106
718-545-3846 / 718-777-3735
Tues-Weds 9:30-7:00 Thurs-Fri 9:30-8:00 Sat 9:00-7:00 Sun 9:00-3:00 (closed Mondays)

Hair washed and cut, no blow dry - $25 bucks + tip, in and out the door in 25 minutes.
They hang your coat up for you. They bring you coffee. Maria and Billy are both really nice.
Both are content to just do their thing if you aren't the talky type, but can chat while they work if you want them to.


I've got your basic shoulder length "Johnny Ramone" haircut.. not fussy or anything but I do like the bangs cut just so, and lately I'm getting those little angle shapes framing my cheekbones. I never blow dry it, except in the dead of winter... They don't push for the blow dry anyway so you won't feel cheap if you say you don't want it. That's always been a selling point for me at salons.
 
I noticed this topic yesterday, so I went by there and picked up their menu just to post it here:
  • mens cut 20
  • womens cut 20 - up
  • blow dry/style (flat iron add $3) - 20-40
  • updos & curls 60-80

They carry L'oreal and Paul Mitchell products (which I use), also Rusk(?) and Nioxin(?)
Also waxing, hair spa treatments perms, color, highlights, bridal hair and the whole 9 yards. ^_^
