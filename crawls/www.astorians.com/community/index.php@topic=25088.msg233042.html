I have a ton of great memories at Uncle George's.  Great, simple food and wine at dirt cheap prices.  Open at all hours.  A surly yet consistent wait staff.  You always knew what to expect there, and you were never disappointed.  I always wondered if the old woman who usually operated the cash register was related to Uncle George.  I do agree that in the last few years, the quality of the food declined and the prices went up.  I unfortunately went there less and less in the last few years.  Many other places in the neighborhood have better food (and definitely better ambiance), but don't feel as cozy as Uncle George's did.  Thanks for the memories!
