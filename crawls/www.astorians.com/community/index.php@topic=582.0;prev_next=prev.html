Quote from: Astoria Luv on June 16, 2007, 03:56:34 PM
I stopped going to Neptune's after a night out at the bars because most of the patrons were drunk and it was too crowded.  However, I really liked going for breakfast or a quick dinner.  I took anyone who was visiting from out of town and none of my guests had any complaints!

Yes!  I mentioned this a while back in one of the other sections of the board. 

The place gets absolutely chaotic at about 4 a.m.  They actually have a bouncer who stands in front of the door in the vestibule and admits people one at a time.  But there's still no order.  People try to shove their way in, often succeeding, and bitch and moan when someone who was there before them is admitted.  It's a lot like trying to get on a train at rush hour on a 100 degree day - only everyone is drunk, too.  That said, the service is solid throughout the rest of the day.

I don't think the food is bad, I just don't think it's really that great, either.  The diner I used to frequent on the Upper West Side was much, much better.

I believe it was the Daily News that rated them the best diner in NYC and that this was in 1998, which, for those playing at home, was almost a decade ago and a time when Hanson was one of the biggest acts in the country, Bill Clinton was president and I was growing into the handsome young man I am today.
