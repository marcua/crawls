Title: Re: hard core tub and tile cleaner

Hey Meg. After years of cleaning that sort of thing with 409, I/we recently discovered that Chlorox Clean-Up with Bleach (tm) is amazingly effective at getting rid of stains and such! 409 seems to be better with grease, though.

(PSA: Don't mix ammonia-containing cleaners like 409 with bleach-containing cleaners! Dangerous!)
