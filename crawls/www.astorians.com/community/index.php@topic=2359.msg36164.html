I rarely if ever give to panhandlers. Usually the only time I ever give to people is if they're playing classical music. Like a violinist or something. It's not intentional. I'm just thinking back over the few times I've given and I'm pretty sure they were all playing classical music - it's soothing, which I really appreciate when I'm commuting! One time out in Park Slope this guy was playing the violin and it was SO lovely. And he was cute. So I gave him a buck. 

And I don't like giving to people ON the train. Only if they're in stations, on platforms and such. I've got issues with people invading my space and MAKING me listen to them, you know? But if you've just picked a spot somewhere and are playing, that's cool.
