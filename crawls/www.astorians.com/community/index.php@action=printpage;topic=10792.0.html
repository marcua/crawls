I'd leave it as it is.  Go ahead, call me a party pooper, but I'm tired of Acronyms for neighborhoods.

For history buffs, way back in the 70's and 80's,  'L.A"  was not Los Angelos, it was Lower Astoria.  Refered to  all streets lower in number than 21st.   Usually used by people from 14 the street and Broadway, down to 14 and Astoria Park.
