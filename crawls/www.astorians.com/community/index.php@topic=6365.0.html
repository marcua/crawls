I was wondering if anyone here is participating in the American Cancer Society Relay For Life @ Astoria Park on June 2nd and 3rd?  I thought it would be a great way for you all to meet up and hang out, raise money for a great cause. 

Here's a blurb about the event:
Relay For LifeÂ® is a fun-filled overnight event designed to bring together those who have been touched by cancer in our community. At the event, we celebrate survivorship and raise money to help the American Cancer Society in its mission to save lives, help those who have been touched by cancer, and empower individuals to fight back. During the event, teams of people gather at schools, fairgrounds, or parks and take turns walking or running laps. Each team tries to keep at least one team member on the track at all times.

But, Relay is much more than a walk around a track. It is a time to remember those lost to cancer and celebrate those who have survived. It is a night for people who have shared the same experience to comfort and console one another. During this event, we honor survivors during the Survivors Lap and we remember those we lost to cancer during the Luminaria Ceremony. We also celebrate life, friendship, and a chance to work together toward a cancer-free future.


So, there are teams of 15. My sister actually has 3 teams (2 are full, 1 still has places left). The teams play games, raffle off prizes, and generally have fun while supporting the cause.   If enough of you here wanted to get involved, you can start your own Astorians team!!! Or, you can join an existing team.

If you have kids, bring them!!  Last year they had face painting, games for kids, Scooby Doo was there! I have all three of mine participating! There will be games going on, food, drinks, prizes will be raffled off ( Our team has secured some great raffles, like massages, manicure/pedicures, food and more!). 

It is an overnight event, but you don't have to stay all night if you can't.  Starbucks is providing coffee, Eckerd is supplying water, Commerce bank is going to be giving out "midgnight snacks"...    It really is going to be a fun event, and a great way to hang out with your neighbors!!!

If you can't join a team, that is fine. Come down to the event and hang out! June 2, 6:30 Astoria Park Track!
