Title: !!! ALL DAY ASTORIA MUSIC & ARTS FESTIVAL IN ASTORIA PARK THIS SATURDAY !!!
Post by: audreyLICmag on July 20, 2009, 10:00:52 PM
HI ALL:

Astoria Music & Arts (AM&A) is a local organization dedicated to promoting, supporting, and organizing artists of all types, especially in the Astoria/Long Island City, Queens area.

This Saturday, July 25th, AM&A is holding its second annual **FREE!!** Astoria Music Now! Festival in Astoria Park, an all-day event from 12pm to 9pm featuring over 50 acts on four stages comprised of local musicians and performers of all genres, joining together with visual artists and vendors for an extraordinary day of song, dance, and entertainment.

AM&A would like to invite you all (children welcome, too!) to Astoria Music Now! this Saturday. For more information about the performers & line-ups, the festival, and AM&A itself, please visit:

http://astoriamusicarts.wordpress.com

Thanks & see you in Astoria on Saturday!
