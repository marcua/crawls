Title: Re: Looking for CSA Share
Post by: lanseaux on May 08, 2011, 07:22:36 PM
Arrow Community Center is on 35th St. between 35th and 36th Aves.  The CSA that distributes out of Arrow is Astoria CSA.  You can find more information on their website:  http://www.astoriacsa.com/

Unfortunately, Astoria CSA is also full for the 2011 season.  Information about signing up for their wait list is on their website.

Both Hellgate CSA and Astoria CSA allow members to split shares - to find a Hellgate share partner, start a thread in this section of Astorians.

The last I heard, the only CSA in the area that still had room was the one in Woodside.  On Hellgate CSA's home page you can find links to all of the other CSAs in Western Queens.
