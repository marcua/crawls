The PFLAG Queens Chapter Named Their 2006 Awardees

Forest Hills, NY – October 3rd 2006 -- The Queens Chapter of PFLAG (Parents, Families and Friends of Lesbians and Gays) announced their 2006 awardees. The Honorees will be Dirk McCall, President, Stonewall Democratic Club of NYC who will be awarded the “Morty Manford Award” and Tom Limoncelli, Long Time Bisexual community Activist, as the recipient of the “Brenda Howard Memorial Award”. Currently, the recipient of their “Carmel Tavadia Award” has not yet been confirmed.

The Awards will be presented when The Chapter holds its Annual Fund Raiser Awards Luncheon on Sunday November 5, 2006 at Riccardo's, 21-01 24th Avenue. Astoria NY.

Tickets for the fund Raiser are $50 and a table of ten is $450.

For more information see http://www.pflag-queens.org/lunchon.html or call (718) 271-6663
