Went here this weekend for the first time.  The atmosphere is great - and the band was wonderful.  The food was ... OK.  I say this because the sides were SO salty my tongue was sore by the end of dinner.  I had the brisket which was great.  My BF had a pulled pork sandwich that he didn't love - he liked Dinosaur BBQ's better.  We also had the Catfish Fingers which were not so great.  We will be going back to try other stuff. 

The staff was really nice and accommodating - they turned off the music and the TV's on for the 4 x 100 relay where Phelps broke the record - it was awesome!  (this was at the request of the customers). 

I wish there was something like this in Astoria - not that LIC is SO far away - it's just not around the corner.
