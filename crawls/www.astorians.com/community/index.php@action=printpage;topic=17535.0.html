Title: Have a Car Accident? Did you know...........
Post by: JourneyIntoWellness on March 01, 2010, 10:11:10 PM
Many people are unaware that Licensed Massage Therapy & Therapeutic Exercise is covered by No-Fault Insurance. :-o

I have been Licensed and practicing in the medical field my entire career, and I accept No-fault as payment for your injury care.
Many people have suffered a MVA (motor vehicle accident) usually enter into ongoing treatment with a Chiropractor or Physical Therapist or both.
Go for a series of MRI's, CT scans, X-Rays etc.... in most instances, the main causes of dysfunction and pain are not addressed, Why? :?

The Chiropractor focuses on the bony  of the spine/nerves.
The PT focuses on muscles/joints, exercises, bikes, treadmill, ultrasound, electric stimulation etc...... you go three times per week, perhaps you need medication, or even surgery.  :cry:

The Licensed Medical Massage Therapist focuses on the Body, Mind, Spirit connection, as well as soft tissue dysfunctions and myofascia.
All of the aspects that are causing you discomfort, and others that were there even prior to your accident.
Massage is the least invasive modality of treatment, with the most effective and lasting results. 

The FACT is that you can actually surpass your normal state of health to achieve an OPTIMAL state of health and well-being, on multiple levels.
Free of Drugs if you chose, minimize unnecessary surgery, minimize unnecessary visits to practitioners that don't help much.

Medical Massage/PT/Chiropractic have overlapping modalities:

Chiropractors adjust the spine and limbs-But no not address the musculature-the muscles pull the bones out of alignment.
Massage therapist can not adjust bones; however, during natural movement of joint structures -often this happens naturally and non abruptly.
Both PT's & MT's perform massage but PT's are not Massage Therapists.
Both perform therapeutic exercise with patients, Massage Therapists are more hands on (no treadmills or Bikes)
Both are educated in Anatomy & Physiology, Biology, pathology, Kinesiology,  gait training, trauma, etc.......
Chiro & PT utilize electrical muscle stimulation - Massage therapist use our hands and heat packs.
Chiro & PT utilize Ultrasound - Massage therapists suggest other more natural & less invasive ways to induce new tissue formation, release adhesion, prevent scarring, & increase circulation. 
Massage Therapist can treat post surgery wounds.
Medical Massage therapists are able to understand medical and radiological reports and films.
Massage Therapy does not require a doctors referral, however; when dealing with injuries I consult with other health care practitioners involved in my patients care.

Call today for your consultation, or refer someone you know that has suffered a Car Accident.
