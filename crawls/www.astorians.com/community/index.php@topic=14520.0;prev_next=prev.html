2 BDR Apartment Needed for Shoot - Astoria
Hey guys, I have a new startup with scripted web show about video game culture seeking an apartment to shoot in.  The apartment is preferably occupied by twenty-something bachelors who have television and game consoles.

Requirements:

Frequency
2 Saturdays per month
12 months
5 hours per Saturday

Size
2 or 3 bedrooms
Largish living room (primary set)
Moderate to spacious kitchen

Location
Anywhere in Astoria is highly preferable although we will consider certain Manhattan neighborhoods as well

Condition
Prior to each shoot, apartment must be in usable condition
After each shoot, we will leave your space in found condition or cleaner

Compensation
$50 per month

Other Compensation Options
If you are willing to consider non-money payment, we are willing to consider teaching film/production, and post-production skills.  This is a chance to get hands-on experience with a tight-knit, professional crew.

We're looking to build a long standing working relationship with you.  If interested, please contact goodgametv@gmail.com with precise description of your apartment, location, and availability.   Thanks.
