Went here last night for my husband's bday. 
After reading all the comments about the small portions I was pleasantly surprised that we left feeling very content, and we are good eaters.
We started with the pizza (with greens/apples/potatoes)...fantastic, very light, nice combination of tastes.
For entrees we ordered the steak (nice and tender, very very good) and the linguine- which was also good.  Slightly disappointed that they did not have any specials and no seafood on the menu last night, but it was all good in the end since our food was excellent.
We don't usually order pasta when we go out but honestly we didn't know what else to get (we don't eat pork and we didn't want chicken).
I skipped dessert (gave up sweets for Lent) but my husband had the baby Jesus cake and liked it.  He thought it tasted better as he went along...said it got better as it cooled down a bit.
We also both had a glass of wine, not sure which one but it was very good.
I will definitely be going back.

I went to Vesta for brunch for my birthday two weeks ago and it was divine!  The Warm Bankie was amazing and personally I found it to be a better hangover cure than the hangover pizza (we went to Sunswick's the night before and that place is just dangerous).  My dad loved the mushroom and chickpea soup so much that he ordered another one to take home with him for later!
I don't agree with the portion size complaints, though.  My husband and brother-in-law both ordered the hangover pizza and those things were massive for one person!  My nephew and mom both got pancakes and those were pretty huge as well.  The bankie looked small but with all of the butter and cheese and truffle oil in the polenta it was super-rich--I wouldn't have been able to finish a bigger portion!  I can't wait to go back for dinner.
