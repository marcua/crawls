I'll just quickly add here that for anyone who is interested that Wasabisam and I are working on an "official" astorians.com store that will have a variety of items with an "astorians" theme, and Astoria in general. We thought this can be one fun way that people who are interested can help support this site. We'll also be adding an unobtrusive donate button to the page sometime soon as well.

Wasabisam also has a button design, but we haven't found a place yet that has a good price on buttons while at the same time giving us a good way to distribute them (centralized shipping of some kind). We're not sure yet exactly on how we'd do buttons if we ended up putting out one ourselves, but we're keeping an eye out for an inexpensive, yet good quality option that's easy for everyone to access.

Nonetheless, it's great to see so much talk related to astoria "accessories" and I hope that we're able to see some of these ideas actualized soon!
