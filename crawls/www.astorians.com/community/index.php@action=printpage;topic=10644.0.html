Title: FREE Night of Co-ed Whiffleball on June 3! Happy Hour Immediately After!
Post by: ZogSports on May 28, 2008, 05:30:14 PM
FREE night of Whiffleball games in Astoria on Tuesday, June 3!

Heard about the brand new ZogSports Whiffleball league in Astoria but not sure it's for you?  We’d like to give you a chance to see what all the fuss is about and try out the sport yourself – for FREE!

On June 3, ZogSports Queens invites Astorians and their friends to join us for an evening of casual Whiffleball games at our new court, Hoyt Playground (Hoyt Ave between 29th and 31st Street)!  Happy hour will of course follow at a local bar.

Don’t worry if you’ve never played or don’t know the rules – this is just a casual evening of games to give everyone a chance to hang out and find out what this new activity is all about.  The good news is if you enjoy yourself there’s still a chance to sign up for the summer league which will be played at the same time in the same location!

Have an already-established team or group of friends that want to play together?  Come out as a team!  Joining on your own or with a friend or two?  We’ll put together teams at the field.  Already signed up for this season?  Get in a practice game before the season starts.

•When: Tuesday, June 3. Games from 7:00-8:00pm.  6:45pm if you want us to put you on a team for the evening!  Happy hour(s) 8:00pm-???
•Where:   Hoyt Playground - Hoyt Avenue between 29th and 31st Street, just off the Astoria Blvd N/W stop
•Who: All ZogSports Queens members and any interested friends!
•How: Sign-ups are not required, but if you think you might come out, email league manager Brandon Bitterman at brandon@zogsports.org so we have an idea of how many people will come out.

Get some details on the league and how the game is played here: http://www.zogsports.org/activity_details/162

Hope to see you on June 3!
