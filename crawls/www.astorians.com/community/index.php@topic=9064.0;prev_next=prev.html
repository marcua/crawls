They're not right in Astoria/LIC, but if you have a car or a buddy with one- this is the place to go:
http://www.nsalamerica.org/
The North Shore Animal League is the largest no-kill animal shelter in the area (actually the country I think). They're right over the Queens border- in long island. You can take Northern Blvd the whole way there.....

North Shore Animal League America
25 Davis Avenue
Port Washington, NY 11050
516-883-7575

We adopted one of our kitties there and loved the staff/volunteers and the whole place in general. They're a great organization and have a TON of dogs and cats for adoption always.

Hope this helps!
