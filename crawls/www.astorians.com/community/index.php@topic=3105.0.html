Perils of the office refrigerator rant
« on: August 23, 2006, 02:26:47 PM »
Just went to pull my pizza out of the fridge for my lunch and some butthead had gnawed on it.

Details: I made a pizza this weekend and put the last two slices in tupperware for my lunch -- this was good pizza with green peppers from the CSA on it. And half of one of the slices was gone -- like someone tore it off to try it or something. I mean, I know the risks of a community fridge. Tupperware is usually safe. A cup of yogurt (unless it's wrapped up in a bag or has your name etched on it) is usually not. But who eats food that's in tupperware that's not theirs?? Even if you were really desperate -- there's a place in the lobby that sells pizza! And why not eat the whole thing anyway? Now I have to toss this half-gnawed on piece.

I mean, we're living in a society here!!! (Okay, that's a George Costanza tribute, but it works with the situation.) I wrote up a big fat dirty note and taped it the fridge.

I'm peeved...  :x  Worse yet, now I have to buy some lunch.
