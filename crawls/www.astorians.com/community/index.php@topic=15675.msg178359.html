Re: Best Italian Restaurant in Astoria?
« Reply #2 on: July 27, 2009, 09:03:43 AM »
Depending on the type you want, either Vesta or Michael Angelo's. Michael Angelo's has backyard seating that can definitely accommodate your group and it is excellent traditional Italian American food (try the Sophia Loren pizza, gnocchi, eggplant parm, veal parm, and smoked salmon in a pink cream sauce). Vesta you will definitely need to make reservations for since it is not very big, but they have the best service is Astoria and the food is amazing. Their menu rotates, but features local greenmarket produce. I just had the new eggplant pizza there yesterday and almost wept for joy it was so good (only slight hyperbole  :mrgreen:). Vesta is also a wine bar where you can order wine by the shot, glass, or bottle - their motto is you should never pay for a full glass of wine without knowing what you are getting. At the end of it, at either place you will get amazing food, be treated well, and have a great time.
