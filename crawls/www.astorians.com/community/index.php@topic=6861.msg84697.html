I think I can answer that one for you. People who charge by the hour can take their sweet time to get the job done. Let's say you know how long it takes you to rip through your home and get it all done. If you are paying by the hour you probably will find that your cleaning person might take twice as long. I used to pay that way. Never again. I don't care if it takes 3 hours or 7 hours. Just get it done correctly each time and I prefer to pay a flat rate. I love to see the smile on the person who cleans for me when I tell her to be out of here as fast as she can—and she does. That allows her to do 3 places a day instead of only 1 or 2. I also know that each time she comes here it will cost me the same amount, even if there is a little extra to be done every now and then.
