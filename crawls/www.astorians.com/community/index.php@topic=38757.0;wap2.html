Free Saturday Art Workshops for Kids!

(1/1)

GrowNYCgreen:
Located in Socrates Sculpture Park in Long Island City, NY 11106. 12pm - 3pm.

Saturday Art Workshops are offered to kids, ages 5-13, and their families. Workshops take place every Saturday from May 9 – September 26; they are free and offered in the park’s education area on a drop-in basis. Participants work with a different artist each Saturday, exploring innovative art mediums and a variety of subject matter.

For further information visit: http://socratessculpturepark.org/education/kids/saturdaysculpture
