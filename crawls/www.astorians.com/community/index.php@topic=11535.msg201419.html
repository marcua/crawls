I deal with animal issues all the time. Dying animals is part of the cycle of stores receiving them, caring for them, and reselling them. A few dead hamsters or gerbils or a dirty kitten or dog is not going to warrant the attention of ANY animal group. The bigger issues for them are shutting down puppy and kitten mills and people fighting their dogs and roosters.

ANY fish store you go into has dead fish floating in tanks. Any pet store will have the occasional dead small pet in a tank. And, having worked at many different shelters(including the ASPCA) puppies and kittens tend to get dirty. VERY dirty. I am not saying that cannibal hamsters is normal, but I have been around enough to have seen a lot worse. If you shop around and spend time in ANY of these stores, you will find that all of them are pretty much the same. Use your resources wisely and get after the puppy and kitten mills!!!
