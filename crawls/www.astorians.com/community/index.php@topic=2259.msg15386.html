Hey, how 'bout that! Just as I was still basking in the glow of the cone of Blue Bell I had on Sunday, the Times was getting that story ready to run. I HAVE been to the annual ice cream festival in Brenham though, when I dared my roommate and best friend to enter into the pint-eating contest.
