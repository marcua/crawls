 We will be showing “LES AVENTURES FOLLES DE RABBI JACOB” (“THE MAD ADVENTURES OF RABBI JACOB”), this comedy concentrates on the mishaps befalling bigoted Frenchman Louis DeFunes. While en route to his daughter's wedding, the outspoken DeFunes accidentally gets mixed up in an Arab nationalist uprising. Through a bizarre and nearly indescribable combination of events, the fiercely anti-Semitic DeFunes is forced to disguise himself as a Rabbi. The sociological statements in Mad Adventures of "Rabbi" Jacob, coscripted by director Gerard Oury, are pepped up with heavy slapstick… It will be shown in French with English subtitles.

mustard sauce or with tomatoes, Provencal herbs, spices or with chick peas & spicy lamb sausages
