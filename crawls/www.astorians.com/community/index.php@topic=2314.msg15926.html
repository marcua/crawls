Setting: standing at podium in front of people, coffee and doughnuts on back table. coughing and chair squeeks from crowded room.

"Hi, um, my name is Jonathan"

Room "Hi, Jonathan"

"Ive been a chef for many years now and um, uhhhhhh, hmmmmm,  well you see.....  Im uhhh  intimidated by baking"

Room "speak up please, what was that?"
"dont be afriad, say it again"

"Im intimidated by baking and dessert making"
"Its all those exact recipes, its kinda of like chemistry class, I failed that one."

Room "go on, its ok, no no dont leave, someone stop him"  Scuffle scuffle scuffle.....

" OK so im the kind of chef who likes to throw things together, live in the foodie moment so to speak.  Im good at it, well darn it, im great at it."  " Dont want to be precise, it stifles me." "or so I thought."  " Im envious of those people who make such great desserts."

Room " so what are ya gonna do about it" "just mope around enjoying other peoples ice creams, cakes and trifles?"

"No damnit, Im going to do it,"

Room " Say it or do it???"

"Do it!"  "Im going to make walnut muffins with rhubarb strawberry jam!"

Room " yeah what ever, tell us tomorrow how it turns out."
