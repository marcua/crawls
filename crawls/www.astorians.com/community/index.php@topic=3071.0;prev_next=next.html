Hello~
I was curious to see if there were other Astorians out there who are interested in, studying, or already practicing art therapy or social work. I also noticed a few members who are getting their MSW's and would love to hear more about some of your experiences with the program and/or work in the field.

I have a BFA in Painting from Rhode Island School of Design, graduated in 2002.
I live in a 2 bedroom here in Astoria with my boyfriend- and paint in the 2nd bedroom. I'm working full time and in a certificate program at New School for art therapy. Obviously- it's not a masters program- but it's a very nice intro to the field and I'm able to keep working while taking evening classes a few nights a week.

I'm enjoying the program for the most part- but since it's not a graduate program, it's not as in depth and after finishing this program (in about another year) I will most likely look into grad schools. I'm checking into both Art therapy and Social Work grad programs- very different approaches of course, but I'm very interested in both. I go back and forth a lot since both educations would have different things to offer.

Anyway- I would just love to hear from others in those fields, or interested in them. Any tips or stories are greatly appreciated too!

Thanks guys- Sara
