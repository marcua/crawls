Title: Donation based community yoga starts this Monday April 19th
Post by: arthouse on April 16, 2010, 09:39:49 AM
Bring your own mat to this exciting new yoga class taught by Desiree Duarte at Art House Astoria.

Desiree started her yoga practice over a decade ago while working as a dancer on Broadway. Yoga started as a way for Desiree to warm up for a show and to prevent injury but became so much more. She went on to study and become certified in Vinyasa flow through Sonic yoga NYC. As a professional dancer and certified Gyrotonic instructor, Desiree has a wide range of movement modalities to draw from and inspire her creative class.

The class is one hour and will begin at 6pm.  Art House Astoria is located at 23-35 Broadway (between Crescent and 23rd st.)

The fee is a suggested donation of $5-$10.

Call 347-738-4148 or email info@arthouseastoria.org for more information.

You can also visit us on the web at www.arthouseastoria.org.
