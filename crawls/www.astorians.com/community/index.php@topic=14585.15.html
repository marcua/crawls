I stopped in this evening with my girlfriend and her mom/sister... it is definitely large with a lot of potential, but even with the bar area roughly half full, it felt kind of cramped.  I'm hoping they add more seating in the empty areas.  The menu looked good, though I'm wondering if it will be expanded (it was only one large menu page).  I would not be surprised if this were not the final version of the menu, mostly due to the number of typos in it (mashed potatos, french fires, masculine salad, wings 6/6.95 4/12.95 <- I'm not even sure what this means).  I'm also hoping that they'll put up a beer menu somewhere, because on a crowded night it's going to be tough to get close enough to read them off the taps.

Overall, as we all know it's very nice and large.. and it's been pretty busy lately.  I hope the operators continue to be as serious about this place as those of us in the neighborhood!  I wasn't too impressed (though the interior is VERY well done), but hopefully they're still working on getting things finalized.
