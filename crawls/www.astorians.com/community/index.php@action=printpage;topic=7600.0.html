Quote from: KatieP311 on August 20, 2007, 05:28:54 PM
*not sure if there's some kind of copyright risk here...?*

There actually is. From previous experience, if you post materials on the internet that it does not require a pass word to view or is not sent individually to people, you cannot sell someone first publication rights, which is what magazines and such want.

It's a shame, but it makes sense. Magazines don't want to pay you for what everyone's already seen for free. That's why the editing forums I used to belong to were members only, so that you could still sell the work you had edited there.
