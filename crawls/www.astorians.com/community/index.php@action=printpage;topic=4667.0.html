WINTER PRIDE

Every June, Queens hosts the second-largest Pride Parade in NYC.  This year the parade will be on Sunday, June 3.  The organization which runs the parade, the Queens Lesbian and Gay Pride Committee holds an annual fundraiser to raise the money to stage the parade - this event is Winter Pride, and it's held here in Astoria at the Astoria World Manor.  Winter Pride will be on Saturday, January 20, and one of the two honorees is Dirk McCall, one of the founders of Out Astoria.  Details are below - we're putting together an Out Astoria table - please email Joe Moran to reserve a seat - josephcmoran@gmail.com

Dear Friend,

I am writing to invite you to Winter Pride, the annual fundraiser for
the Queens Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual and Transgender Pride Parade and
Festival (which is always held on the first Sunday in June in Jackson
Heights. This year our 15th annual Parade and Festival will be held on
June 3, 2007.)

Winter Pride, which will be held on January 20, 2007 from 7 PM - 12 AM
at the Astoria World Manor , is the major fundraiser for the Parade
and Festival which is now a tradition in Jackson Heights.  The Parade
and Festival contribute significantly to the neighborhood's colorful
diversity.

This year we are proud to honor two really outstanding individuals at
Winter Pride:  Congressman Barney Frank from Massachusetts and Dirk
McCall, the president of the Stonewall Democratic Club, the Director
for External Affairs at Quality Services for the Autistic Community
(QSAC) and one of the most beloved activists in the LGBT communities.
