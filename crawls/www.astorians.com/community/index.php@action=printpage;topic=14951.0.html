Title: Bicycle Scavenger Hunt! This Saturday May 16th @ 10am
Post by: lawoods1 on May 12, 2009, 11:43:41 PM
  Put air in your tires and get ready to ride!

This Saturday the TA Queens Committee is hosting a free Bicycle Scavenger Hunt along the waterfront of western Queens.

Starts @ 10 am at Queensbridge Park (Vernon Blvd & 41st Avenue)

Teams of up to 4 people will find clues from LIC to Astoria and compete for prizes of Crumpler Messenger bags filled with bike goodies. 

Event will end at 1 pm at  Astoria Park w/ the awards ceremony.

MUST BRING:  working bike, helmet, writing utensil, digital camera or Ceil phone with a camera
RECOMMENDED to BRING:  bike lock, water, sunscreen, Ceil phone

RSVP to:  Taryn.Cunha@Mattel.com
