I would say from my experience, the butcher shops I prefer in Astoria are the International Meat Market, on 30th Avenue between 35th and 36th Streets; Akropolis, which is right off the corner on 30th Avenue and 31st Street; as well as the meat market within Berry Fresh Farms Supermarket in the Ditmars Shopping Center, Ditmars Blvd. and 44th Street. 

Both International and Akropolis are very clean, especially International, and I've never had an issue with meats I've purchased from there.  International seems to be especially busy around the holidays...Christmas, Thanksgiving, Easter, etc., which probably bodes well as to its reputation in the community.

The meat market inside Berry Fresh Farms was a bit of a surprise, but it has a great selection of meats, and Joe, the friendly Italian butcher, is especially helpful.  They have a larger meat selection than any supermarket I've ever seen, more diverse than Western Beef, and cleaner too.
