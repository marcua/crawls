There's another new taco truck, different from the ones mentioned above, that sets up on 30th Avenue between 29th street and Crescent. Today was the first time I'd tried it, even though I live literally half a block away. The taco de lengua I had was great--tender, fatty, great flavor. And no skimping on the meat, either. If their other offerings are this good, this truck is going to beat out El Rey del Taco as my "best taco truck in Astoria".

Meanwhile, the truck on 31st street seems to have disappeared (although I confess I wasn't a fan of it anyway). Anyone seen it lately?
