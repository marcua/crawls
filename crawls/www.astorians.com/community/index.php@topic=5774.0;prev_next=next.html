You already know you can get a free credit report once a year, but finding out your all-important FICO score usually costs a bit of cash. If you're curious - but not 15 bucks curious - Bankrate has a free FICO score estimator.

The estimator boils down to a series of 10 questions about your financial history, mostly regarding loans and credit card balances. The result is a range that seems fairly accurate, at least by one account. If you're concerned about that number - or even wondering what financial factors play into it - this estimator's the place to start.
