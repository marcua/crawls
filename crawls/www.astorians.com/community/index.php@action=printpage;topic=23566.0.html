 THE HAUNTED WATERS OF THE EAST RIVER-
Greater Astoria Historical Society Walking Tour
SATURDAY, October 27, 2012, at 11:00 AM
Meet under the Hell Gate Bridge, on Shore Blvd. just south of Ditmars Blvd., Astoria.  Take a haunted waters Halloween walk with GAHS along the East River and through Astoria Village to hear tales of fright and woe which took place along our route.  Shipwrecks, murder, cemeteries, war ships, the treacherous Hell Gate, typhoid fever, shootings, and other macabre events grace our fair village and its mighty river.  Bring a friendâ€¦to hold on to!
 
Come in Halloween costume and pay half price.  Wear sturdy shoes and bring water.  There are hills and some rough terrain.  There are bathroom stops at beginning, near the end and at the end of the tour.  Tour ends at Socrates Sculpture Park.  If you wear a costume, please consider your vision requirements through masks and tripping hazards with a long costume.  Safety first!  Fee: $15.00 to GAHS members, $20.00 non-members
