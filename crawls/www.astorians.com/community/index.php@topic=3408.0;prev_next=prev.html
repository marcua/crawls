I've been to a few community meetings. I went to an old CSB 30 School Board meeting. AT the time they seemed very intent on not letting any of a vocal group of parents speak. They went through the minutes, old business, then when people tried to speak, they were ruled out of order. Everyone was told they had to only speak to the agenda topic. The topic was to vote as to whether or not to set all school clocks in the district to the exact same time.

I was amazed that with all the problems in the public schools , crimes, order, reading levels, programs etc. This was the only subject deemed worthy of discussion. After much debate the Board could not make a decision one way or the other. What a waste.

Now Rosemary Promovera's group Astoria Civic Association is usually entertaining . She is a great witty speaker IMHO and I think she should run for something, but she prefers to be a gadfly. If you want to go to a local meeting this one is most informative and sometimes entertaining and at least everyone gets a chance to speak their mind.
