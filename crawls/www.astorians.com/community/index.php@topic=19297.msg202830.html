This morning on my way to work at 7 a.m. film crews/trucks were setting up for shooting along
30th Avenue between 31st & 32nd Streets. That's as far as I saw - they may be further up the avenue
as well.

Anyway, I checked the sign and they're doing the remake of 'Arthur'. This one is with Russell Brandt from
Get Him to the Greek and Helen Mirren who, if I'm remembering what I read in People correctly, is taking
on the John Gielgud role. I know they've been filming around Manhattan, but it's great that they're in Astoria.

If you're around there today, maybe you'll see them.
