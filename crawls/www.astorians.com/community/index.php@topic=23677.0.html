ConEd Scammers Going Door to Door
« on: November 02, 2012, 04:49:41 PM »
Greetings Astorians!


I may be a bit crazy here, but I suspect that I was attempted to be scammed by two men posing as ConEd workers. They knocked on the door and told me that ConEdison has been attempting to get in touch with me about something on my bill for the past 6-8 months. They said that they were there to let me know that I needed to respond to something on the back of my bill and asked to see my last month's bill. I lied to them and said that I've only been living at that location for the past 3 weeks and have not received any bill yet and they didn't question that at all. They then asked me if I had a bill from a previous address, and I told them that I moved from out of state. If they knew that ConEdison has been trying to contact me for months, wouldn't they have any proof of it or know that I was lying about not living there for very long? I never received any emails or anything from ConEdison about what those two men were talking about.


Has something like this been happening to you guys? They targeted my co-op building near Crescent St. and Astoria Blvd. I'm not even sure how they were able to get inside the building unless perhaps a delivery guy inadvertently opened the door for them. They weren't wearing anything that would resemble a typical ConEd worker. One of them simply just had a big black coat holding a black bag.
