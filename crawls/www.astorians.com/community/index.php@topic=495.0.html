Happy Fourth of July!!!

We have been celebrating a lot of borndays at the Freeze Peach lately... and since the name of the cafe was born because of it.... let's celebrate the bornday of our country!

Let's use this post to reflect on the reasons why we love our country. And for those who are not from this country, if you like, feel free to jump in and share how you got to be here and are still here.

I'd like to share a little bit. I tell this story to everybody. Sorry if you've heard it before. My parents are from South America. They both came to the United States in the 1970's, for a better opportunity for themselves, even though New York City and the rest of the country was going through a rough time with crime and race relations. My parents experienced much prejudice during their first years here.  Regardless of the hard times, they strove to become naturalized citizens anyway. On the day that they became naturalized, someone asked them if they wanted to register to vote. My parents have never missed an election since. My parents know how important it is to be part of the political process and that compared to other countries, their voice does make a difference.

I feel very lucky that I, a female, a person of color, am able to live in this country, be able to vote, get an education and be able to use it and actually have a job and make a living off it. There are many young women across the globe who do not have these same rights or opportunties. Even though our country is involved in a terrible war right now, and current administration is doing some very frightening things, I feel hopeful that myself and other proud Americans will get together to work for what is best for our country.

HAPPY BORNDAY USA!!
