Post by: Jonathan on November 11, 2005, 06:00:11 PM
Folks, I have to put in my two cents here.

I've been a few times now for fun and professionally; here is the deal:

if you dont have tickets you stand on line with tons of upper east and westsiders thinking this is the best thing ever.  when you do get in (after an hr or two wait) you get to pay money, stand in long lines at preferred booths either tasting samples of chocolate disks or small small poor samples of the companies choc offerings OR spend money buying product.  it gets hot, cramped and boring fast.  It can be cool if you get in free, know some of the chefs and get to hang with them tasting all you like but thats rare.  

why not get a group together, go out and buy some of the best chocolate around and have a tasting pig out party in someones home or better yet get a group and go for a chocolate eveing at payard, chocolate bar or la maison  du chocolate, just to name a few.
