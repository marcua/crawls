Had a great dinner at Sabor Tropical last night. We did the riodizio option for $27 per person.  It was really great, the meat is very tasty and comes out often.  I felt bad because I accumulated a little too much meat on my plate and didn't finish it all, although I tried.  They didn't allow me to take any of it home, not even the rice and beans and fried bananas, and it's too bad, it was such a waste, when you know they're just going to throw it out.  But it was definitely a great meal and the caipirinhas rock!
