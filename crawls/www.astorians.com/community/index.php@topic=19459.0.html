If/when utilities are included this is AN ILLEGAL (BASEMENT) APARTMENT. I am really surprised that any honest broker would be pimping this kind of fire trap. In order for you to have a separate meter the electric company would have had to install it. If the electric company is called to do that, they require a Certificate of Occupancy for the premises to do the work. Without that, there can be no direct utilities billing. This place would be sharing a meter.

The City of New York is finally making forced inspections/visitations of these dumps, and if they find that it is illegal, you will be evicted with or without your belongings within 24 hours.

Astoria is a prime target for this investigation. AS IT SHOULD BE!

Before you agree to/sign anything ask the following questions to both the broker and the landlord:

1) Can I get a lease? (It compounds the legal situation if a lease is given for an illegal apartment)
2) Verify there is a Certificate of Occupancy for this rental unit.
3) DO NOT PAY CASH FOR RENT, or the broker fee. If you use a money order, GET A RECEIPT.

Use caution. Getting caught in a fire trap during an emergency situation can cost you your life . . .and the life of a firefighter.
