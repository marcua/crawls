I just got back from this place..and it was the best $50 I've spent for as long as I can remember.  The man @ the desk was super nice, gave me water & brought me into the room where he told me to relax for the 10 minutes I had to wait.

I've gotten lots of massages, around the country for various prices @ spas.  For the most part they have focused more on the relaxation end rather then the restorative and healing side of massage.

This place isn't a spa.  It was clean, cool & quiet, save for the low talking of the other two massages happening next to me.  My therapist really dug into me with long strokes and found knots I didn't even know I had.  She really paid attention and worked them out rather than work on them for a few and then continue on like most traditional spa massages do.  I also liked the fact that when she worked on my legs and feet she went all the way up the muscle to the top of my butt.  Most places don't for a few reasons.  I didn't feel awkward or uncomfortable.  She was just working the muscle from the bottom of my foot all the way up.

I am super sore & drinking a lot of water...and thinking of when I can go back to have the rest of the knots rubbed away.
