Okay folks....her we go...

We were thinking of starting an organized get together to kick of the beginning of summer.  This event should be in Astoria, fun, cheap and have the potential for being a little crazy.

What do you think about when you think of Astoria? Me, besides an overwhelming Greek population, those personal shopping carts that EVERYONE here seems to own. Shopping, laundry......a cheap walker to prop up your mortal remains.......why not make a rally using these carts! How about giving awards for best team rally time......best dressed cart ( this is where you really can get creative!)....best costumes ( this is where you can really get CRAZY!)...get the idea?

Okay now, what should this rally be doing? SHOPPING of course! Now, this is where you all come in. WHAT do you shop for? We were thinking of giving teams identical shopping lists of items that could then be pressed into service at a picnic in the Park...Astoria Park of course.
Can you picture the scene of this motley crew of contestants rolling into Astoria Park afterwards, ready to party? ......can anyone say TOGA?.............
But I digress......this whole thing probably should take place sometime around the summer solstice...on a weekend. YOU FOLKS would need to submit ideas....hey, spring has just started, you've got plenty of time, as to how to get this thing going, what we should be shopping for, perhaps even where to shop.
GO FOR IT!
