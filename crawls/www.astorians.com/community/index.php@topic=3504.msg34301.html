I had a nightmare boss once. I'd put it in this category:

Quote from: SpecialK on September 21, 2006, 02:25:32 PM
On the one hand, I can totally sympathize with bad boss stories.  I have the mother of all stories, though it's hard to sum it up in a paragraph or two.  Ask me over drinks.  I promise not to cry this time.

Anyone ever been at a place where they didn't fit the "culture"? This one company I worked for (which was and probably still is one of the Fortune Top 100 companies to work for), had this very distinctive culture. They made us read this business book - I can't even remember the name - and we had these "book club" meetings about it to discuss how it exemplified the culture of our company, then pointed out (as the book plainly did) that people in the company who didn't fit the culture were sort of naturally expelled. About six months later, I too, was "expelled", although of my own free will. But only because things had become so unbearable that there was nothing left for me to do but leave. Guess I didn't fit the culture! Man was I miserable working there.

It ended up being a good thing though - when I left that job, I moved to New York!

That sick feeling on Sunday night before you go to work on Monday morning is a benchmark for me. I never want to have a job again where I feel sick and depressed on Sunday night at the thought of another week at work.
