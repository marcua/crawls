What is a food co-op?

It's a non-profit grocery store run by the people who shop there. Most focus on organic and natural foods. Typically, you buy a share to become a member, and usually work a certain number of hours (or pay extra), and you usually get the food at quite a bit cheaper prices than Whole Foods.
