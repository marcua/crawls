On 30th Ave there are a couple of places - Gandhi is so-so.. I've had their lunch special and it's alright.  Meghna is awesome though.. it's on 34th street (I think) and 30th ave.. it's actually around the corner from 30th ave.  It's great Indian Food.  Takes them a really long time to cook it though - but well worth the wait.

And also - it's not Indian food, but there's a guy with a cart on Steinway and 34th ave that makes the best Chicken and rice plate ever!
