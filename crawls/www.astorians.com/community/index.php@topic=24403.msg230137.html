Hey Julie , i can see you are new to Astoria  , and just to let you know while many apts might have some of your requirements , to get all of them  is almost impossible . You have a better chance if you head to a newer or new building to get all that but its gonna cost . No one includes a parking space that i have ever heard of and i grew up in Astoria my entire life .   If you want a garage you would have to rent that out at additional cost .  Very few buildings include WIFI for free but you might be able to get it added to your rent at a lesser price then you would pay if you got it alone by yourself .  Sorry but  just being honest .   Dishwasher , and washing machines r usually new or newer buildings  at higher end apts but they arent cheap . The rents here seem to being going up and up , so you might find something in your range a little farther from the train but def walking distance . If you wanna be very close get ready to overpay .

Hey Julie,


I'm 24 and I just moved to Astoria about 7-8 months ago so I know what it's like to try to apartment hunt from afar.


I think it's possible for you to get most of those things, except the parking spots. That's definitely going to be extra, unless you're willing to either park your car in an inconvenient location where there's not alternate side parking or have to drive around endlessly looking for parking at night. If you're willing to live either farther east (Steinway and beyond) or farther north (Ditmars area) you can find things for cheaper. You probably won't be able to find your own place for < $1,000 but you could probably find a place that fits most of those requirements.


Feel free to message me if you have any questions :)
