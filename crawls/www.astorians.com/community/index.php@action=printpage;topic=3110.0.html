Title: 2006 Shoreline Cleanup
Post by: megc on August 23, 2006, 04:06:18 PM
Last year I participated in this and found it really rewarding.  When we started, the shore looked SUPER NASTY, littered with, well, litter.  We cleaned it up and it looked AMAZING!  What a difference.

Well, the shoreline cleanup is happening again this year.  I think it would be great to have an astorians.com contingent out there helping out.  I'll be there.  Anyone interested in joining me?  Here are the details:

Shoreline Cleanup and Celebration!

What: Doing good in the neighborhood -- Join the Astoria/LIC Waterfront Parks Alliance as we enhance our beautiful shoreline! Sites include Astoria Park, Hallets Cove, and Socrates Sculpture Park. Come prepared to get a bit wet and dirty. Tools, gloves, and good company provided! Cleanup held rain or shine- thunderstorm cancels.

When: Saturday, September 16, 2006. Time TBA.

Where: Astoria Park, Hallets Cove, and Socrates Sculpture Park
 
More info:  For more info, or to get involved, please call 718-706-8044, or email Emily.Maxwell@parks.nyc.gov. For info on the day of the event, please call 917-902-9165.

Last year there was a party afterwards, and I know they've been talking about having one this year, too.  It might get merged with an even bigger community celebration, which is cool.  I'll post more details as I learn them.
