I agree that this is misguided and wasteful.  I'm okay with naming government office buildings and courthouses after people such as Koch or RFK.  Even libraries and other municipalities.  But bridges, tunnels and highways are best named after where they are located or where they take you.  59th Street Bridge and/or Queensborough Bridge are not sexy or nostalgic names, but they are very descriptive and helpful to anyone using the bridge.  As are the former names Triborough Bridge and the former Interboro Parkway.
