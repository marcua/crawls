Tough, yes, but not entirely impossible.

That's what we have Community Boards for. Things change—some for the worse, some for the better.

One other thing, when this community allowed the building of 6+ floors it was because they were designed with lower ceilings. The original pre-war, 6-story/floor buildings had 12' ceilings. The post-war buildings had 10–11 foot ceilings so they were allowed to go up to 7–8 floors.
