The Key Food on 30th Avenue started going downhill about 3 years ago when it changed management. Since then they had poor quality produce at high quality prices. Recently however they added new refrigerators for produce and increased both the quality and selection. Keep an eye out for their dairy products, they are notorious for keeping yogurt, cheeses, and even some milk products past their stale date. I have even pointed out the past due date to the stockboy, and when I return days later the same container is still there, which means they are told not to throw anything out.

Most of the cashiers are still unprofessional though, but that's more due to poor training and oversight than the cashiers themselves. Employees will not perform above the level that's expected of them.
