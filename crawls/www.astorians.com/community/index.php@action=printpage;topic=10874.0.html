I am inviting all Astorians to visit my Delphi forum, Wake Up Little Sushi.  

http://forums.delphiforums.com/wakeupsushi/start (http://forums.delphiforums.com/wakeupsushi/start)

Wake Up Little Sushi is a forum that poses the question "Why is it so hard to change?".  It is a place where people can talk about their on-going struggle to change some aspect of their lives that they don't like.

I used to wonder why nothing in my life was going right.  I thought about it for years and noticed that lack of sleep was a major component in every other aspect of my life.  At first I thought I was simply staying up too late and that if I simply went to bed earlier, that would take care of that.  Then I found out it wasn't so simple.

That's my on-going struggle.  You might have something else you'd like to change - your weight, your job, your relationships - whatever is important to you, you're welcome to discuss it in the forum.

I don't think you need to be a Delphi forum member to read forum posts, but you do need to be a member to reply or post new messages.  Membership is free, and the Delphi website will tell you about advantages of paid membership.  To join at any level you give them your e-mail address and you'll receive notification there of any new replies to your messages.

On my forum I have set up a folder welcoming any Astorians who come by, even if it's just to say hello.  You may post messages there and in any other forum folder.  Let me know if you would like me to open my chat room at regular times.  My current name at Delphi is Little Sushi, and that's the name you'll see on my messages and in the chat room.
