Title: Performing & Workshop
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on February 28, 2014, 12:28:23 PM
Calling all writers! Queens Council on the Arts’ 3rd Space is proud to present In Between the Lines, an inaugural writing workshop hosted by Queens-based performance collective The In Between People.
 
Join us for an evening in experimental performance exercises that you can take with you to influence your writing practice! This workshop encourages lovers of the pen to wean themselves off their computer screens and try writing from a new perspective. Convention tells us that writers put their words on the page, while actors put their bodies on stage. But we want to know:  what happens if before writers put their words on the page, they put their bodies on stage?
 
3rd Space – “In-Between the Lines”
Friday, March 21st from 7pm-9pm
