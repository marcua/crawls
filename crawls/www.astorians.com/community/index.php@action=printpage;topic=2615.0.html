Title: Early pick up July 18th
Post by: jayme on July 18, 2006, 11:19:31 AM
Beat the heat with an earlier veggie pick up this week!

In honor of this week's NYC heatwave, Hellgate CSA will be offering early pick up.  For this week only we'd like to extend distribution hours.  Beginning at 2pm, the tables will be set up with veggies ready to go.  We'll be keeping them under cover in an attempt to keep them cool so you will need to peek inside the bins to claim your share.  Jayme (our site coordinator) will be on site -- inside (air conditioning!) if you have any questions.

This week we are also expecting a flower share and to minimize heat stress on flowers (wilt), flowers will be kept in the back.  Find Jayme to pick up your flowers!

Today only, distribution runs 2:00pm-7:30pm.  Don't forget to sign in!

thanks,
Jayme & Hellgate CSA
