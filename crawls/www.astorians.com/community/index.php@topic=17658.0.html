New development plans for the 31st Avenue/Crescent Street vicinity
« on: March 15, 2010, 01:43:58 PM »
As if it wasn't bad enough that a huge building is being constructed on 30th Drive, there are plans to build a 5-story building on the parking lot space between the Concord and the Asti on 31st Avenue.  This space is first of all too small to contain such a large building--there would have to be a very narrow entrance leading to an almost-as-narrow main building--but the proposed structure would also cut off light and air to many of the surrounding buildings on 31st Avenue and Crescent Street.

I always feel pessimistic that developers' plans can be stopped, especially given our Community Board, but I hold out a little hope that at least a few of the nuttier ones can be killed or reworked.  If anyone's interested in learning more, PM me or come to the next Long Island City Alliance meeting on Tuesday, April 13 at 6:30 PM at the Quinn Building (35-20 Broadway, 4th Floor).
