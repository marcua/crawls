Title: The Wild kingdom Comes to Sunnyside!
Post by: Dance Entropy on June 08, 2012, 09:05:50 PM

Sunnyside Community Services invites you
to step into the
WILD KINGDOM
SUNDAY, JUNE 24 @ 3 pm
FREE
Valerie Green/Dance Entropy performs a vibrant
fun filled dance for all ages.
The program includes
Chiquita Chiquita, a humorous, absurd performance
full of surprises, Wild Kingdom, a playful rite of
animals, and Dandia, based on the Indian stick dance.
As a special feature, the program will include a dance
choreographed with and performed by members of
Sunnyside Community Services’ Center for Active
Older Adults.


This performance is made possible by
Seniors Partnering with Artists Citywide (SPARC).
SPARC is a collaboration among the New York City
Department of Cultural Affairs, The Department
for the Aging and the City’s five local arts councils.


This program is supported, in part, by the National
Endowment for the Arts.





Sunnyside Community Services presents Wild Kingdom, a free performance by Valerie Green/Dance Entropy
and members of the SCS Center for Active Older Adults, 43-31 39th Street, Sunnyside, NY 11104
