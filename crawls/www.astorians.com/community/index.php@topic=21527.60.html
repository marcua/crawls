I finally actually had a meal at Butcher Bar. And WE LOVE IT!!!! I felt the prices are very reasonable.  I had a half rack of ribs - the pricing is about average for average ingredients. But these ingredients are much more expensive than what most restaurants are sourcing. For the quality I feel the prices are low.
Our food wasn't fatty at all - and well - i'm not really alarmed by fat. If you want lean - pork belly is not the way to go - pork belly IS mostly fat - you should consider trying a cut of beef as grass fed beef is very lean. Pastured pork is generally fattier than it's commercial counterpart because the farmers who raise it generally raise fattier heritage breeds.
We also had the "Bob's burger" which is 80/20 beef/bacon - and the  bacon just melted into the  burger. This burger doesn't need any adornment. Just great meat flavor. 
We also had a couple of sides and all were quite tasty - the cole slaw was a little heavy on the mayo for me but that is personal. The mac and cheese was surprisingly good - i am always dispointed with this category. We also tried the yams and green beans. Full disclosure the owner sent those two items to our table for us to try and i would absolutely order them next time.
I can't wait to be back and expect to be a regular.
