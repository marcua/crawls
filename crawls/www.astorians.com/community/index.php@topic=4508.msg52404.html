Unfortunately, I let my notary license lapse.  As firelight79 said, notarial fees are $2 in New York State and are set by law:

Quote
ยง136. Notarial fees.
     A notary public shall be entitled to the following fees:
     1. For administering an oath or affirmation, and certifying the same when required, except where another fee is specifically prescribed by statute, $2.
     2. For taking and certifying the acknowledgment or proof of execution of a written instrument, by one person, $2, and by each additional person, $2, for swearing such witness thereto, $2.

Also, the fee to become a notary is $60, not $75.  Here's a general FAQ page on notaries in New York from the NYS Department of State.

There is a notary in the little office on 35th St. just behind that newish pharmacy I can never remember the name of, just south of Ditmars (across 35th street from the ill-fated Les Moules location).  He charges $2.
