As someone who has been accused of being "the loud neighbor", I think there is something to be said about people who are irrationallly intolerant of any noises in apartment buildings.   And the problem is that once these people work themselves up into a hissy fit about one noise, they become hyper-sensitive to every single noise that you are making - even the noises that would otherwise pass. 

I lived uptairs from a couple who actually called the police on me when I started vacuuming at 11:00am on a Saturday!   They were totally nuts.  I did not listen to loud music at all hours of the night. I did not have raucous parties.  I just did "normal" stuff around the house at "normal" hours and they were constantly banging their broom sticks on my floor.   After months of screaming matches from our respective windows and several visits from the police, I actually decided to move because it was impossible to deal with them.    I think they were just miserable people.   The funny thing is - I would constantly hear them argue with each other at the top of their lungs.  They were awful to each other.   I guess they just wanted to make me miserable too.
