My husband and I ate there a short while ago.  It is simple, both in atmosphere and preparation of the fish.  IMO the fish was so good & fresh, it didn't need a whole lot of seasonings or dressing up.  I believe the lunch special came with salad served family style, garlic bread & either rice or potato.  We really enjoyed it and would definitely go back.  Prices were very reasonable for portion size and quality of the fish.  We had fried calamari, broiled grouper, fried shrimps which were all fantastic.  We also ordered baked clams which were very fresh but I wasn't nuts about that dish.  Add in a few sodas and I think the bill was less than $50.
