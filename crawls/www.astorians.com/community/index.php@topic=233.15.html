I am sorry that you guys haven't found a space yet that you can have your garden. However, I just wanted to share my excitement. The weather is getting warmer and for the first time in 7 years I finally have a little bit of space to start my own small garden. I have a front yard and also thinking of doing some potted garden and flower boxes in the back.

My sweet basil, sweet marjoram, and dill are doing fine. Just need some egg cartons because they are getting too big and there is too many for their container. I am the type of person that does not like to throw away perfectly good plants just for the sake of weeding them out. I would rather grow them a little more and then share them. I am also going to try growing parsley, cilantro, and thyme.  

Flower wise I bought 3 rose bushes in the colors of red, light purple, and a pink/yellow mix. I love roses. I also have some wildflowers, Marigold, Clarkia, Impatiens, Pink Dianthus, Bachelor Buttons, Alyssum, and Forget Me Not. One of these days when I have everything going you will have to come over and take a look.

I will probably have extra plants as well, especially when I start the weeding process. If I do I will let you guys know so you can have some of them. Even if you don't have space for a garden where you are you can get a pot and have an inside herb garden all grown inside one pot. :) Nothing like freash herbs.

Well, I need to go run to Eckerds, buying some cheap plastic tables to put my plants on so they can get some light and grow and strong.

Colleen
