Yipeeee sounds a bit mistaken.   I actually almost moved into the luxury building he is talking about (Yippeeee if you remember we discussed in the thread).

Oddly enough, my gf and I were both concerned about the no man's land over THERE.   While nothing that would stop me from moving, 21st street has very little charm in that area.  Yes 30th avenue and broadway are close by but as someone who has lived on 21st Avenue and Steinway area for 7 years now, its at an all time high.     Just to inform:


On 20th avenue you have Best Yet supermarket which is a Suburban sized supermarket in a shopping plaza with a deli, pizza place, liquor store and SingleCut Brewery right across the street.


An avenue the other way you have Ditmars Blvd which has a ton of restaurants along with TWO CVS's and on 31st and ditmars is a common area for some food trucks.   Additionally you have bars  such as:


McCanns sports bar
Sweet Spot
Ditmars Station
Astoria Bier and Cheese


all on or just off of Ditmars.   The Beer Garden is a short walk away as well.     Steinway street also has the very convenient Q101 which I generally take to hit the higher Avenues.  Stops right near my apartment.


Again, lived here for 7 years and love this area.  If you HAVE to be right next to as many bars as possible, then sure its not the best spot for you....but otherwise i find it hard to complain about this area at all.
