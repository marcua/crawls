Finally had a chance to try Il Bambino tonight when BF* suggested "a hole-in-the-wall Italian place." He started with the tomato lentil parsnip soup** w/pesto crustini, and he was impressed.

We then moved on to crustini sandwiches: Me, the mushroom/tomato pesto/asiago cheese; he the basil/tomato/mozzarella. He saved half for lunch tomorrow.

Had decaf cappuccinos for dessert.

Music was also excellent: Vintage Who; mellow Clash; a David Bowie song I never heard before; John Lee Hooker.....  :mrgreen: :mrgreen:

* His favorite stupid joke: "Oh, look, they have Soup du Jour. I hear it's excellent." Told him that in this place, that's not a joke, the Soup du Jour is excellent.
