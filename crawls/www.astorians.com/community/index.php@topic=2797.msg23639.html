I've been dying for Mister Softee all day, and I seem to never be able to locate a truck when the time is right.  I see them around my neighborhood all the time, but when I am in the mood....no luck.  Anyway, I wrote to Mister Softee today with a brilliant plan to revolutionlize the ice cream truck industry.  I think my letter speaks for itself, so I will post it below.  Who's with me??

----------------------

Dear Mister Softee,

I had a brilliant idea for you.

It all started earlier today when I had an uncanny craving for the luscious creaminess and otherworldly delights of a vanilla cone with rainbow sprinkles.  I walked over to the local playground where I have often found one of your beautiful trucks.  Alas, no truck.  Dismayed, but not discouraged, I walked back to my apartment until an hour passed when I felt it safe to venture out again.  Once again, my silky goddess evaded me.  Thrice more did I attempt this voyage in vain.

As if to add salt to my wounds, I later heard the distinct toll of the ancient Softee hymn.  I ran outside my gate but found it impossible to locate the source of my calling.

Mr Softee, wouldn't it be grand if every child (and 26-year-old man, as in my case) could unlock the mystery of the location of a Softee truck at any given moment?  I plead with you to begin installing GPS locators into every truck and connecting them to the glorious home of Mister Softee on the world wide web, mistersoftee.com.

I hope you take my story to heart and follow your dreams and mine to a more perfect world...a world where Mister Softee ice cream is always just a click away.

God Bless,
Your friend Adam
