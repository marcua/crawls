In Praise of St. Honore's Coconut Custard Pie
« on: February 21, 2009, 11:30:21 AM »
Mostly I want to give praise to St. Honore Patisserie's amazing delicious coconut custard pies, but a little bit I want to damn them for being so good that I can't walk by the place when I spy one in the window.  Sucks me in right off the sidewalk like a supersized magnet.

The pies are so good because they have the perfect consistency, and the perfect balance of sweetness.  In other words, they're are subtly, and not too, sweet.  Plus an excellent crust.

I love St. Honore for many things (great quiches, amazing bread pudding, fab cookies, etc.), but right now, the coconut custard pie is kind in our house.

I think St. Honore is one of the gems of Astoria.  I seldom here folks talk about it, but almost everything that i have had there has been great.  It is as good as most (not all) french bakeries in Manhattan and half the price.  I think their baguettes are some of the best around.  The French guy who I believe is the baker/owner always asks how i like things and wants a sincere answer when asking.  I recently got a donut and the following weekend I was getting a baguette...he asked me how I liked the donut from the previous week.  I was impressed with that as I am only there about once every 10 days or so.  I enjoy both the pear and apple tarts as well as the fresh marshmallows that they have.   Once and a while he makes apple dumplings which are incredible...

Since Dolce Italia went under, St. Honore has the best cheese danish in the area. Nearly everything I've gotten from St. Honore is good-- and I'm quite picky about baked goods. As with anything else done by a human being, quality is not perfectly identical from one day to the next and I can recall one occasion when my pastry was quite dense, so maybe our forceful naysayers happened to get an off day. The baguettes aren't comparable to my ideal dreams of the baguettes I had in France, but only one baguette I've had in the U.S. is, so I hardly hold that against them. Overall, I think they have wonderful pastry and kind, helpful staff. Definitely my favorite bakery on Ditmars.
