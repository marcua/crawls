Title: It's your waterfront: Astoria Waterfront Design IDEAS
Post by: greenshoresnyc on October 01, 2010, 10:39:59 AM


WATERFRONT DESIGN DAY
 
 

Wednesday, October 6, 2010

Green Shores NYC is partnering with the Trust for Public Land to produce a community-driven vision for the Astoria-Long Island City waterfront.

After holding seven public listening sessions throughout Astoria & Long Island City this summer, we learned how community members are using the waterfront now, how they are getting to the waterfront and what is not working on the waterfront.  NOW we are asking community members to DESIGN THE FUTURE OF THE WATERFRONT by sharing their ideas for how to ensure a CONNECTED, GREENER, CLEANER, MORE ACCESSIBLE and USER-FRIENDLY waterfront from Newtown Creek to Bowery Bay.
