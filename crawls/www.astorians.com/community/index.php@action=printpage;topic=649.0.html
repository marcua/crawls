Post by: blueeyes on August 28, 2005, 02:10:34 PM
I don't know of anything in Astoria, so this might not be immensely helpful, but Porto Rico Coffee Company sells great whole-bean coffee.  They have a good selection of very tasty organic, fair-trade beans (which I also buy) at a decent price.  The selection is not huge, but the variety is good, and I have been extremely pleased thus far.  Also (given that I know you like espresso   8)  ) they have a great organic, fair trade Mexican espresso.

They have two stores that I know of: one in the East Village on St. Marks btw. 1st and 2nd; the other on Bleecker by 6th Ave.
