Post by: mcdirk on January 16, 2007, 03:07:49 PM
this would just be for Manhattan.  You'd have to call the Queens Borough President's Office to get applications for Community Board 1 - Astoria or Community Board 2 - Long Island City.
The Borough President makes all the appointments, but Councilmembers recommend half of the 50 members for each board and they're staggered so 25 members are up for reappointment each year for two year terms.  Vacancies are usually filled mid-term.
