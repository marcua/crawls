my sister loved the Artist's Way. she thought it was very helpful and inspiring.

one thing might help is to take an art class at a community college. that's how i got into watercolor. i learned what materials i needed to buy and how to work with the paint. if you take a class or two, you not only get some techniques down but being with other students can be very inspiring.

the other thing that really helped was when i bought a digital camera. i take it everywhere and always find subject matter for painting.
