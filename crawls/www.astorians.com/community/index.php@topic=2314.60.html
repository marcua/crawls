There was a little town right on I-35 between Dallas and Waco called West, and as it was right on my way between home and college, I was always stopping in to pick up a few. Everyone knows West and stops in. It's known as the Czech-stop of Texas! Ha!

Speaking of the wonderful joy that is kolaches, here are a few Texas-based kolache recipes:
