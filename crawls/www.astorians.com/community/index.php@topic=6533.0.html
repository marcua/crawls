From my personal experience, it's approximately in the 1250-1400 range for a one-bedroom. Sure, you can find something for less, but it'll probably be pretty small/5th floor walk-up type deal. I'd say realistically you should be able to get a decent-sized one-bedroom with a separate kitchen for around 1300 in the area. Unless it's a luxury building, which is rare, I doubt paying much more is worth it.
