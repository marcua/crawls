I went on this same search myself a year or so ago after blowing a lot of money on Thai tea from restaurants. I found a small Thai grocery in Woodside that sells sizable bags (about 30 servings worth) of loose Thai tea for $2.50. It works very well and I love making it myself. I use half & half and buckwheat honey. Take the 7 train to the 61st St/Woodside stop. I can't remember the name or exact address, but I've gone there twice and it's right around there and not a long walk from the train. Both times I think I found it by accident, but I believe it's in the 39th ave and 62nd st area. The store I'm describing has the tea sacks in a bucket on a bottom shelf towards the front of the store.
