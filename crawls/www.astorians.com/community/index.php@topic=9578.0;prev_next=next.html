HILLARY NEEDS YOU - in Astoria!!

Astoria 4 Hillary is a local Hillary supporters group. We've recently been active doing voter registeration, petitioning, fundraising and visibility work in Astoria in the run-up to the NY primary. Our next meeting, on Wednesday February 20th (7:30-9:30pm), will be at Omonia Cafe, 3220 Broadway, Astoria, NY 11106 (close to Broadway station on the N, W Line). It will be an opportunity to find out how to get involved in the NYC Hillary campaign as a Volunteer and help elect Hillary Madam President on Nov 4th. We will also be discussing plans for weekend campaign trips to the upcoming primary states of Ohio and Pennsylvania.
Please rsvp at: www.hillaryclinton.com/actioncenter/event/view/?id=9169

Any questions or for more info please email: astoria4hillary@hotmail.com

To volunteer in the meantime please contact the NYC Hillary campaign office on 212 213 3717.

Look forward to meeting you on Wednesday February 20th!

Michael Borio / Anne-Marie Vaduva
