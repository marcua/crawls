 Penthouse unit in the Ionian Condominium.  The apartment has 630 square feet inside and another 440 outside on a private terrace, where we've got bamboo, a Japanese red maple, a juniper, and a weeping cherry tree.
 
 The unit gets tons of natural light, with western and northern exposures. You get a full view of the Empire State Building, Citigroup Building, Bank of America Building, and the rest of the Manhattan skyline from the kitchen's bay windows, couch, or terrace. Craigslist only allows four pictures with an ad, so I couldn't squeeze in any pictures of the view. I'm happy to e-mail them to you, though.
 
 The building is only about three years old and is in great shape (I'm the Condo Board President).  There's an independently-operated parking garage, as well as an outdoor lot.  There's also a free bike rack for building residents. The building has a giant elevator and laundry in the basement.
 
 The building is on the same block as the Steinway R/M subway station, which is a 9-minute ride to Manhattan (59th & Lex) and about 20 minutes to midtown.
 
 It's also:
 
 1 block from a Zipcar lot, Queens Kickshaw, Shout Karaoke, Rite Aid, Duane Reade, and a boxing gym;
 2 blocks from the Kaufman Astoria Regal Theater, the Yoga Room, Panera Bread, Applebee's, Pizzaria Uno's, the Gap, Victoria Secret's, and two great playgrounds;
 3 blocks from 5 Napkin Burger, the Museum of the Moving Image, the incoming Five Guys Burger, Life Health Fitness, the Astor Room, and two Starbucks; and
 4 blocks from the Studio Square Beer Garden, Bravo Grocery, Gap, Victoria's Secret, and New York Sports Club.
 
 This has been our first home and we love everything about it: the neighborhood, our neighbors, the ability to grill & host parties outside, etc. Sadly, though, we're headed to Washington D.C. for a job that I couldn't turn down.
