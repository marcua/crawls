Although some of our regular LPW players have been on a long holiday weekend, Ellieworm's been away from the board for a different reason. 


For a couple of weeks her husband wasn't feeling well, but wouldn't go to the doctor.  When he finally did go, an MRI and CT Scan seemed to reveal the worst - he seemed to have a malignant growth on one side of his brain, and an operation to remove it would only buy him a few more months of life, but in what state, with much of his brain gone?.  Take a moment to consider how you would feel, to know the life of someone you love was about to be taken away, in such a horrible manner..


Fortunately, the surgeon found something entirely different going on.  An abscess from bad teeth had spread though his sinuses into his brain.  A serious situation, but not it's not cancer.  Still hospitalized, he's being given strong antibiotics, and they are working.  He's slowly gaining his appetite, and for some reason has quite a craving for milk!  Ellie's been spending long hours at the hospital, encouraging him to eat a variety of foods and bringing him milk.  He may be out in a week, but till then, Lenny,
