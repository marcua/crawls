It's worth calling 311 when this happens. It doesn't take long to set up an appointment with the T&LC. If you don't show up, the cabbie doesn't get fined, but they do have to take the time to go downtown and wait. If they have to do that every time they refuse service to an OB, they'll pretty soon stop refusing service.

Also, get in the cab before you tell them where you're going. They're not nearly as likely to kick you out of their cab once you're inside.
