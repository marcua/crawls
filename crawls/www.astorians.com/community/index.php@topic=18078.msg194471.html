I was assigned to the courthouse in Jamaica about a month ago.  You can bring a laptop and they do have wifi.  Be prepared to close it up on a moment's notice if they call your name.  I sat there for one full day and then the next day they called my name.   I served two days and then they let me go home.

There is an area where you are allowed to use your phone but it's not in the main area.  If you use your laptop while the cop is talking, he may yell at you.

One tip - pay attention to everything the cop says.   He does not like repeating himself and is quite the character.

There are plugs near the back of the room for the laptops but I preferred relying on battery power to sit near the big bright windows.  It put me away from the break room where everyone is really loud and annoying.  If you are near the windows, it's a lot quieter and nicer.
