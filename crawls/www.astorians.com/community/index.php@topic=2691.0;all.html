JustDesserts,

Because in today's job market there are alot more applicants and fewer available jobs, the simple consideration of giving feedback to an applicant is low on their list of things to do. When I was looking for a job, I must have given my resume to at least 30 companies or placement agencies, and I think I only got back 3 replies stating that the job was filled, or thanking me for my resume/application and that will notify me if they had something available that suited me. Hopefully when the job market changes and more jobs become available again, applicants will start getting back courtesy calls again.

Keep pursuing other positions, don't wait on just the one or two. The more options you have the better chance you have of landing something.
