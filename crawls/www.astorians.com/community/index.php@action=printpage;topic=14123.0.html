Title: Re: any good thai food on steinway ?
Post by: TER on February 28, 2009, 03:47:13 PM
At 35th(-ish) and Broadway there's Benjama's Taste of Thai.  I love the place.  It does not skimp on the fish sauce/heat end of things if you ask them to make it Thai spicy. 

For apps they make awesome garlic calimari; this BBQ pork tender with sticky rice that comes with a side of hot fish sauce; and good spring rolls.  On the entree side of things, their portions are generous and I haven't had anything I haven't liked.

They're also really good about respecting customers' requests; I recently dined here with some people who could not tolerate any sort of heat at all (pepper was too spicy) and they really did their best to accomodate them.

The restaurant itself is strangely relaxing, in my opinion.  Certainly worth a try if you like Thai.
