Title: World Wide Knit In Public Day -- Astoria Park -- June 14, 2008
Post by: cabanird on May 20, 2008, 07:36:03 AM
Astoria Crafty Knitty (ACK) & Steinway Astoria Craft Crochet & Knit (SACK) are hosting an Astoria KiP to celebrate World Wide Knitting in Public (WWKiP) Day event on Saturday, June 14, 2008 from 12pm to 5pm in Astoria Park. The group will be meeting on the lawn area near 23rd Ave and 19th St, and will be easily located after walking into the park at 23rd Ave.

Please join us to knit, crochet or craft in the park! We will gather to enjoy an afternoon of being creative together in the sun in a show of community. In 2007, almost 200 local groups took part in this event; this year the Astoria KiP will add our local crafty needles to it.

To make it an extra fun day outdoors, we’re planning some special events!

There will Knitting or Crochet instruction available throughout the day! Newcomers are welcomed to join and learn a new craft.


SHOW & TELL  12:30pm

Meet the event participants and see some of their favorite finished objects,or just check out other people’s work! Bring your own favorite objects to share!

Workshop: Working with Plastic  2:00pm

Rosemary & an another knitting instructor will teach you how to make yarn out of your plastic shopping bags and start you on a knit or crochet market bag! Think Green! Think DIY!

Astoria Knit and Crochet Quiz 4:00pm
Use your knowledge of Astoria, knitting, or crochet to win prizes!

For more info, please visit: http://astoriakip.blogspot.com/

Or contact Rosemary or Kim at Astoria.kip (at) gmail (dot) com.
