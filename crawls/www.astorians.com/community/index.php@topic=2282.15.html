I had an adventureous commute home too. I got on the N at 57th Street a little past 5 PM. When we got to Lexington they said it would be the last stop. My first thought was to take the 6 to Grand Central and catch the 7, but there were swarms and swarms of people so I decided to walk across the bridge instead. It was only drizzling at this point, but so disgusting and muggy.

Queensborough Plaza was completely insane when I got there. There was no N/W service in either direction, huge crowds and no one seemed to know what was going on. Patience is not one of my virtues, so I decided I'd already walked across the bridge, might as well walk home.

It started pouring around Broadway. By the time I got to my place (21st Avenue/24th Street) I was drenched. But I did walk about 5 miles, so it was a good excuse not to go to the gym last night.
