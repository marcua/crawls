Remember:
1) See or call your own personal physician prior to leaving for any ER
2) Be certain that your insurance covers the hospital
3) If time permits, call the ER to be sure that they are *not* backed up
4) You have the right to be taken by a 911 EMS ambulance to the hospital of your choice
5) Emergency Rooms work on a priority basis; they reprioritize every 5 minutes
