Got Empanadas?

I just stopped in to Got Empanadas? on 34th ave between 28th Street & Crescent.  Delicious & cheap!  Plenty of space to eat in, I had the chorizo & cheese (also has potatoes) and a rice & beans with beef.  I definitely recommend stopping in.  They do deliver, open til 10pm weeknights, 11pm Friday & Saturday I think.
http://www.gotempanadasny.com/
