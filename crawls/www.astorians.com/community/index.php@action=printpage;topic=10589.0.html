Title: Re: Getting an apartment as a recent grad?
Post by: purplecurlygirl on May 27, 2008, 09:56:14 AM
You'll probably have more luck going in as someone's roommate in an established rental situation or in a private home. I don't think you'll be able to find or afford your own place (even a studio) on that entry level salary.

I'd start building your credit (get one card and use it responsibly), look for roommate(s), and don't think about getting your own place for a couple of years. It's also a lot cheaper to initially move in with someone than finding your own place (no broker's fee, split security deposit, etc). Just put everything in writing between you and the roommate or you and the landlord and get receipts for any money you hand over. Good luck!
