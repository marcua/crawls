Club 21 is a chill Czech bar with a great selection of Czech and German beers on tap. Good selection of Czech food to snack on or have dinner. I had the grilled chicken with peaches and cheese last night. The ambiance is quite dark, candle lit, vintage furniture and lots of candles. One of my favorite things about the place is, though, a different happy hour every night from 5-9. yesterday was $2 well drinks!

Also, the back outdoor area is very cool.
