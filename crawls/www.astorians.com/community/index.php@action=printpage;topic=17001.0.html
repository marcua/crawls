Title: Young Adult Mass - Italian Dinner - Currents on "Journalism on Catholicism"
Post by: cfnyc on January 01, 2010, 11:15:56 AM
Join us, The Catholic Fellowship (http://www.cfnetwork.org) for the Young Adult Mass on Sunday January 17th at 5pm at Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church in Astoria. The church is located at 23-25 Newtown Avenue, Astoria, NY 11102. Info on the church can be found at http://www.mountcarmelastoria.org.

Directions to the church: Take the N train uptown to Astoria and get off 30th Avenue Station. Walk down 31st Street and turn left on Newtown Avenue. The church is on the corner of Newtown Avenue and Crescent Street.

Following the mass, please meet the group in front of the church to head to dinner to:

Dino's Restaurant & Bar
29-35 Newtown Avenue
Astoria, NY 11103
http://www.dinosny.com

Dino's is a good Italian restaurant, which introduces the taste/flavor of Montenegro. We negotiated a 4 course dinner for a great price $20 (tax/tip will be $25) and reserved a private space for our group. We also negotiated 1 hour of open bar (beer, wine, well liquor) for $15.

During dinner, Francesca Maxime, host of Currents will give a talk about "Journalism on Catholicism." To learn about Currents, the first daily Catholic TV news, please go to http://netny.net/currents/. For a bio on Francesca Maxime, please click this link http://netny.net/currents/anchors/.

Currents covered 6 of our group events, of which 4 videos have been archived. To view 3 events of The Catholic Fellowship of NYC filmed by Currents, please click this link http://netny.net/currents/?s=The+Catholic+Fellowship. To view 1 of the events of The Catholic Fellowship of Young Filipino Professionals filmed by Currents, please click this link http://netny.net/currents/video/stories/simbang-gabi-121409/.

Questions: Contact Joseph Mahon of The Catholic Fellowship at 973.418.2817 or josephmahon4@hotmail.com.
