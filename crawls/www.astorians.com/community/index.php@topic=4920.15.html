Chowhound has been having an ongoing discussion about Mina's for sometime now and for that reason we have yet to try it, however once the warmer weather approaches we shall walk up and back as I do want to try it.

On the Great Indian I would like to second and third the votes for Delhi Palace as well as second the vote for Indian Taj. Although Taj is buffet, I have never had any problems, the service and atmoshere are fine and it is all your can eat!!!

Jackson Diner on the other hand is for me a miss, have been there three times and was not impressed at all. I think they get more business due to thier name, rather than due to the heartiness of thier dishes.

I also heard that Dosa Diner was decent, however have not been yet, however is on the "get there" list.

What about the "cabbie diner" on 21st St. under the Queensboro Bridge? You can see it across the street when you come off the bridge at the light. I think it's called Five Star.....has a new catering hall right next to it (which cracks me up- probably the same kitchen!)

It's a Pakistani greasy spoon with a large-screen TV showing Bollywood films and the cheapest furnishings possible. Nothing healthy about it, but plenty for vegetarians and it's cheap and tasty. My cousins go there weekly, and even that one time we spotted a mouse doesn't phase them. I'd go more often if I weren't concerned about the calories.
