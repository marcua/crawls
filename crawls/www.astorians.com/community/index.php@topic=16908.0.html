I've made them both ways, and think that if you use banana leaves the flavor isn't as good. The flavor absorbed from the corn husks is definitely better. The Original Mexican Deli on 29th St and 30th Rd has leaves to buy, it's like $5 for a huge stack of them. They also make really good tamales for $1.25. I know it's not too close to Ditmars, but it's right off the 30th ave stop on the train if you stop on your way home assuming you take the N/W.
