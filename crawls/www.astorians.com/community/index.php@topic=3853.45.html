This weekend, both the N and 7 trains will stop at Queensboro Plaza--if you can get there! Only the N will go to Manhattan, the 7 will not.

The shuttle buses are in effect all weekend (sorry wasabisam). It's 12 AM Saturday through 5 AM Monday  (If it was late-night only it they usually say 12:01 AM - 5 AM Saturday and Sunday, etc).

Next Thursday night, from 1AM to 4 AM there will be no service at Queensboro Plaza at all.


N TRAIN
Late Night, 1AM-4AM Thursday November 2.

Queensboro Plaza Station Closed
Shuttle buses replace trains

To allow for NYC Department of Transportation work on the Queensboro Bridge, the following changes will be in effect:

N Service
Shuttle buses serve N stations between Ditmars Blvd and 39 Av, then connect to the Queens Plaza E station, where E and N service to Manhattan is available. Transfer between the shuttle bus and the N at Queens Plaza.

All N trains from Manhattan terminate at Queens Plaza.

7 Service

Shuttle buses serve 7 stations between Woodside-61 St and Hunters Point Av.
These buses stop at Queens Plaza for customers looking to transfer to the N.
