I don't think I can make this meeting (at least not until later) but I'm very interested in bringing a greenmarket to Athens Square.  As far as the loading and unloading issue goes, there is a greenmarket in front of St Marks Church in the East Village with similiar issues and they manage to make it work.  I think Athens Square would be great for the people already in the area, it would attract more shoppers to the existing businesses, it would be great for the farmers because there is already so much foot traffic there (the location on 31st and 14th is out of the way for anyone who doesn't live right around there, no public transportation etc.) and wonderful for all the urban kids who don't get access to farmers.  Perhaps I'm naive but isn't the community board supposed to represent the community, not just the businesses?  It seems to me that there is quite an interest in better produce in general in this community (see CSA threads) and one day a week of a farmers market is more likely to energize the area rather than threaten the existing vendors.
