Title: A few suggestions
Post by: nancy_in_dutch_kills on October 16, 2005, 11:34:31 PM
"Lil' Bistro 33" might prove a good option. Here's a link to their website:
http://www.lilbistro33.com/
They don't have a private party room, but they're so small that if you make a reservation for your party of fifteen, you'll have the place practically to yourselves.

"Arharn Thai" has a special table for banquets and could easily accommodate a group of fifteen or so. It's not a private room, but I have been going there often for a long time and there always seems to be a large banquet going on. So I guess they really have their banquet deal down! And they're very child-friendly. Info link:
http://dinesite.com/info/rstrnt-58996/

"Gallini Seafood Restaurant" has a smaller room off the large main room.
I couldn't find a link for them, but they're on the corner of 34th Street and 36th Avenue. You should be able to get their phone number in the Yellow Pages.

I've been to the above three restaurants multiple times and can say the food at all of them is excellent, and the prices most reasonable considering the high quality.
