If you've never been to MOMA, I would like to seriously suggest not going tomorrow.

The museum is really beautiful with lots of interesting stuff but right now there is a Dali exhibit there and the already packed to the brim free Friday last week was doubly obnoxious because of the extra influx of people coming because they know Dali.

Free Fridays aren't usually too bad, but when you get a big name like Dali as the special exhibit, it draws so so many people.
