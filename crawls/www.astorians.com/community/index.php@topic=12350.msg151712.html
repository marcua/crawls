They have it at Indigo (31st St, betw. 30th Ave & Newtown -- right by the 30th Ave subway stop). I've gotten the eggs benedict a couple of times and thought it was good. I recommend their bloody mary as well.

Indigo is great for brunch in general-- $13.95 for brunch entree + 2 drinks. And they serve brunch Sat & Sun til 4:00.

Ah ha, a question I am very qualified to answer!  I eat eggs benedict every week for brunch, it's required, so I know pretty much every restaurant in the 'hood that serves 'em!

Like said above, Ovelia and Indigo are both pretty good.  Last time I visited (which in some cases has been awhile, so YMMV!), you can also get 'em at:
*Flo (38th St and 30th Ave)
*718 (Ditmars and 35th St)
*Blackstone (38th and Broadway) - although, hmm, they were renovating last time I went a few months ago, anyone been recently?
*Michael's Restaurant (33rd and Broadway)
*Stove (45th St and 28th Ave)
