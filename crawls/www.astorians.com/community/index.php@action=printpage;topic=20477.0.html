Title: Astoria prices going way up?!
Post by: SplishSplash on January 31, 2011, 10:22:03 PM
I've been poking around on Craigslist just for fun to see what Astoria apartments are going for these days and I'm pretty shocked. It seems like in the 6 months that I've moved, prices have gone up dramatically. I am paying $1250 for a one bedroom and haven't seen anything decent under $1500..In fact, I've barely seen any apartments under that. Maybe I was just lucky, but when I was looking over the summer, I found a lot of apartments in the $1200-$1300 range. I wanted to move again in July, but I can just imagine how much a one bedroom will be by then. I love Astoria and I know it's appeal is growing every day, but part of the appeal is affordability...and it seems like that will soon be gone.
