Italian Cucumber & Tomato Salad

Peel and slice cucumbers.  sixth or eighth plum tomatos (depending on size).
Put it into a huge bowl.
Add good olive oil, red wine vinegar, salt, pepper and oregano.
Mix by hand.  Let it sit for an hour or so.

Its very cheap, very easy, can make loads of it, its very summery and refrigeration is never a problem

Mayo Free Potato Salad
(increase exponentially for up to 100! )

3 medium sized baking potatoes
4 scallions
2 large lemons
olive oil
1 red onion, finely chopped (can be omitted if you want)
grainy mustard- about 1/4 cup
cumin powder
salt and pepper

bake potatoes in microwave and allow to cool (slice them in 1/2)
in a large bowl combine the juice from teh 2 lemons & the mustard using a whisk, then slowly drizzle olive oil into the mixture until its mixed together; you will need abt 1/4 cup of olive oil when its said and done.

once the potatoes have cooled crumble them into the bowl.  add scallions, red onion, and cumin, salt and pepper to taste!

Here's a favorite of mine, great for the summer, and part of the hood: 

Greek Pasta Salad
1 Box of Penne Pasta
2 vine-ripe tomatoes, chopped
1 red onion, chopped
1 green pepper, cut into thin strips
3/4 cup of black greek olives
4 oz feta cheese, crumbled
2 Tbsp. fresh dill, chopped
Juice of 1 lemon
1/4 cup of olive oil
Salt and Pepper to taste.

Boil Pasta to al dente and rinse with cold water, set aside. Chop up all the veggies, then toss with the pasta, cheese, oil, juice, and olives. Season with salt and pepper. Tastes better if it can sit in the fridge for a while.
