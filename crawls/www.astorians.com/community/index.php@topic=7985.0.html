PROJECT DIVERSITY CELEBRATES QUEENS' CUTTING-EDGE VISUAL ARTS SCENE


Please join Queens Council on the Arts (QCA) for Project Diversity Queens, an exciting multimedia art exhibition featuring 87 Queens Artists at 13 galleries throughout Queens.

Come and enjoy the revealing artist talks, lively receptions, inspiring exhibitions and events throughout the months of September, October and November.
 
For a list of participating Galleries and Artists, please visit the Project Diversity Queens gallery at the QCA website, www.queenscouncilarts.org.
