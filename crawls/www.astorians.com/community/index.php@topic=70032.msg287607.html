« on: April 14, 2018, 11:36:29 AM »
Available May 1 (somewhat flexible). No sublets/airbnb allowed. Ideal for young families and professionals looking for more room without moving far out to the suburbs and a quick commute to Manhattan/Brooklyn.
Looking for the right tenant (families ideal) who will respect our home. Please send details about yourself when responding.

Attached 3 level rowhouse with garage, private driveway, front porch, back deck and large fenced backyard with turf. 1500 sq ft of living space plus an additional 600 sq ft finished basement! Walkable to subways (M/R/7/E/F) and buses. Location is convenient to Astoria/Woodside/JacksonHeights/LGA/BQE and 10 min uber ride to Williamsburg/Brooklyn.

Layout: On the main floor, there is a half bath near the entry hallway and a deep closet, opening up to a large open floorplan for the Living and Dining space. The marbled kitchen includes high end appliances with plenty of cabinet space. Large windows throughout. The kitchen opens out to a large deck and fenced backyard with turf grass ideal for kids.
On the second floor you will find a king size master bedroom with king sized california closets, and a full bath with a gorgeous European shower. Also the generous second bedroom features wall length closets. The third bedroom includes a deep closet as well.
In the basement there is another full bath, laundry (washer/dryer included), and lots more storage with access to the garage and the backyard. The back yard is a grill master's dream, with a great combination of sun and shade!
One car garage with pass through entry to the basement.

Deposit terms: 2 months rent deposit.
