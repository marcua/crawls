I'm surprised no one has mentioned Dolphin.  It's on 31st Street between 31st Ave & Broadway.  I'm paying $39 a month, I think it was a $50 down payment, but some of my friends have had that waived.  Its for a year contract.  Its a pretty small gym, but they have a decent selection of cardio machines, which includes a whole bunch of treadmills.  Their weight room seems good, but I wouldn't know since I only use free weights.  They also offer classes like yoga and kickboxing. 

The staff is friendly, the place is really clean, and its never ever crowded.  I go at all different times (6am, 2pm, 9pm) and I've never waited for a machine.
