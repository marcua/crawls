Last night I was on my way into the city for an 8.15 appointment in Times Square.

At QBP I looked across the platform at the 7 train and considered taking it instead, but decided I'd like to avoid running the extra block in the rain.

I almost immediately regretted that decision. We stopped and waited for several minutes on a signal problem. Then the train crept forward about 4 feet and stopped again. This time because the emergency brakes were on the in R train ahead of us. I assume this meant someone pulled the emergency cord.

We sat for about 20 minutes, with occassional brief lurches forward which only served to make the tension even worse.

I'm still wondering why the emergency cord was pulled....
