Good places for used books in/near Astoria:
 
-Salvation Army, corner of Steinway and 34th Ave
-Thrift Store on Broadway, between 42nd/43rd Street--always tables of used books outside on sidewalk
-Salvation Army in Woodside (61st St. stop on 7 train, on 61st St. b/w Roosevelt Ave and 39th Ave.)-bigger and better book selection than the SA in Astoria
-www.bookmooch.com - you can swap your used books with others
