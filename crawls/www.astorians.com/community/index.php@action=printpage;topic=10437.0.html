Title: anyone ever been to the RUDE hipster Cafe Lalo in UWS?
Post by: moodle on May 11, 2008, 10:53:11 PM
So i was in UWS yesterday wandering around and had hankering for some coffee, i wander into Cafe Lalo and notice they had many many treats as well! So I wait for and get a table. I'm all excited, and I wait for a waitress.
One comes after five minutes to bring food to the girl at the table next to me, ignores me. I figure, well, who knows, maybe it's not her section.
I wait five more minutes. A guy sits down at the table to the left of me.
I wait another five minutes.
A DIFFERENT waitress comes to HIS table, and expressionlessly takes his order, and again, ignores me!!!
WTF? I sit for a few minutes dumbfounded, and finally decide f___ THIS, and go to leave. I grab the host dude who seated me, a fey italian-ish chap. I tell him, that I sat there for fifteen minutes while two different waitresses served the people around me and I never got waited on.
He kind of puts his finger on his chin and stares at me blankly and says, "yah?"

I would have tripped a waitress on my way out but they were busy ignoring other customers in the corner at the time.
YUCH!!
