Jim and I recently ended up in Beacon for the weekend.  Great town, a lot of hiking, shopping and eating in the area.  It's just north of Cold Spring, in fact.  A lot of the main street in Beacon is brick row houses that have been renovated and have shops, galleries, coffee shops and restaurants.  The second Saturday of every month a lot of the galleries stay open late, and have free wine and cheese while you browse, and several of the restaurants also have little discounts or a free glass of wine.  There is also Dia:Beacon, a renovated Nabisco factory that houses the art collection of the Dia Art Foundation from the 1960s to the present.
