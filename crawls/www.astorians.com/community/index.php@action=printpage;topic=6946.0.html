get a letter on the company letterhead from your new employer stating your new salary and your start date at the company.

have your tax returns from 2005 and 2006

your drivers license, and social security card

bring at least a month of paystubs as well (even if its less money)

have a recent copy of your credit report.

be able to pay with certified checks (if your bank has a branch in nyc). (and make sure when you do look, you are able to pay at least 3 months of the rent (1st, last and broker). even with management companies and private owners they sometimes ask for 3 months as well (1st, last, deposit).

that should be enough. good luck!
