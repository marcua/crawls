I was just sending an email and up at the top, above my mailbox bar, was a line asking for people to help petition for an "independent" candidate for NYS Attorney General, someone who doesn't appear to be on the ballot yet.  While I thought the deadline (and still think) has passed to petition onto the ballot, this seemed really unusual and odd.  I clicked the link and this is what I got.  It makes me wonder what is going on - what motivates people to do these Don Quixote bids for public office?  Someone with no money, coming into a race as October starts, for a November election, with no name recognition, and who doesn't even say they are an attorney in their material?  This is weird.

-----

With Only 5 Minutes of Your Valuable Time, You and I Can Beat the Political Bosses in New York

Only 43 Days - Please Help Me
Here is the situation.

I'm running as an independent candidate for Attorney General of New York and as such I have to obtain 15,000 signatures on what is called "Nominating Petitions". I only have 43 days in which to do this, a 6-week period starting on July 11, 2006 and ending on August 22, 2006.

During this period, to have my name placed on the ballot as a candidate for New York Attorney General, I have to obtain at least 15,000 signatures from registered voters in New York (they can be from any party or have no party affiliation at all). Out of these 15,000 signatures I must show that at least 100 come from residents of each of 15 different "Congressional Districts" in New York State (out of the total of 29 Congressional Districts in the entire state).


Help Me By Downloading, Signing and Mailing a Petition
You can help me by downloading one of two forms of Nominating Petition, executing it as instructed (on the 2nd page of the form), obtaining verification of your signing of the petition from another NY registered voter or a New York notary, then mailing the executed petition back to me, as soon as possible.


NY Attorney General - 2nd Most Powerful Office in the US
If you and thousands of other New Yorkers can do this for you, me and most other New Yorkers, we will be able to achieve far more for the citizens of New York than you could imagine. The key is that the Office of the Attorney General of New York is the 2nd most powerful office in the United States, and its real potential has hardly been explored by its present occupant, Eliot Spitzer. You should know that to the extent he did use his powers, he scared the hell out of, and angered, some of the major corporations and party contributors - to the extent that they are now kicking him upstairs and making him become Governor of New York, to stop his lawsuits against errant corporations.


Here Is What I Plan to Do for You and the Other New Yorkers
If elected, I plan to do a lot of things for New Yorkers that need to be done. My campaign issues are available at different places in this website for my campaign, starting off with the home page, at Home Page. some of my main issues are to sue major corporations for moving jobs out of New York State to other countries or states; to investigate (by grand jury and subpoena) how 7 WTC was demolished on 9/11 without any plane having hit the building; the healthcare and causation issues relating to the 9/11 toxic air and the various victims: First Responders, other emergency personnel; local GZ residents; local employees; and people living and working in the surrounding areas (with the extent of the ulimate injury not being known at this time);

... and to audit county prosecutors to uncover any illegal practices that have resulted in 98% of the persons indicted of major crimes agreeing to be convicted (through plea bargaining, without any trial), and about 80% of the 2% (who have the money to defend themselves) also being found guilty after trial, with the resulting meteoric growth of a for-profit penal system, unjustified loss of too many breadwinners and too many persons who would be inclined to vote for Democratic or independent candidates, and heavy costs upon the local communities and the families involved; forcing Wal-Mart and other major retailers to deal honestly with the communities in which they building new stores and the competitors who they seek to put out of business by requiring full disclosure (e.g., of the healthcare costs they are going to impose on the local healthcare providers).

There are many more issues, such as the oil issue - I would scale gas prices at the pump, using a scannable card that identifies the fuel efficiency of the vehicle, and gives the lowest price (say, $1/gallon) to the most efficient cars and $15 or $20 to the least efficient (such as a Hummer), with the excess money raised through this pricing system used to encourage purchase of fuel efficient cars and the destruction of the non-efficient motors.


Speedy Action Needed
I have many more things I can do for New Yorkers but time is passsing by and I desperately need you (and one or two other members of your family or your friends or associates) to join in the signing of the petition (before another voter or a Notary Public), and then have you mail the executed petition to me.


Just Dowload, Sign and Mail the Petition
Just download the "voter-witnessed" petition by clicking on 3-Signature Form of Nominating Petition for Use by 1 to 3 Voters, at the Same Time before another Registered Voter or the "notary-witnessed" petition by clicking on 3-Signature Form of Nominating Petition for Use by 1 to 3 Voters, at the Same Time before a Notary Public in New York and help me make the 15,000 requirement. If I do not get the 15,000 signatures within the 43-day period, my name will not be on the ballot and the party bosses will continue to rejoice in having set up a system that few people can overcome. Please, let us show them that their system is capable of being overcome and that highly qualified candidates can present themselves to the electorate to challenge the candidates of the party bosses.


Actually, I Need 30,000 Signed Petitions, coming from 50% of the CD's in NYS
Incidentally, even though 15,000 valid signatures are required, candidates traditionally try to get twice the required number (i.e., 30,000 signatures) to ensure that any invalid signatures after being deleted will not take the number of the remaining (valid) signatures below the required number of 15,000.


I Am by Far the Best Qualified Candidate for NY Attorney General
You should be aware that I have 40 years' experience doing what I would be doing as New York Attorney General, suing to enforce the state's and nation's antitrust and civil rights laws. Neither Andrew Cuomo (Democratic nominee and the person most likely to win if I do not beat him) nor Jeanine Pirro (Republican nominee who is currently District Attorney of Westchester County) have any experience with antitrust or civil rights litigation, according to the research I did with existing published decisions.


Please Help - and Thanks
Please help me to help you, and when you are ready to mail the executed petition to me, let me know by email: click on Email to Candidate Carl Person

I thank you for your time and I would be very pleased if you would call to talk about my issues and candidacy and anything I can do to be of help to you and other New Yorkers.

Carl E. Person
Independent Candidate for New York Attorney General 2006



In case you have any questions about use of any of the forms or how you can get others to sign one of the forms, you should call either:

Carl E. Person at my office: 212-307-4444; or my cell phone: 917-453-9376; or my home number: 212-974-1030;

or you should call:

Your effort is needed and would be a most valuable contribution to cleaning up the political mess that you, I and the country find ourselves in.
