Because I am a perfect yutz, I managed to lose my IDpass, again.  Since it has transit priviledges attached to it, its quite a hit in the pocketbook if I report it lost.  Fortunately, my ineptitude at remembering to keep an eye on my belongings is matched by my karmic standing in the universe, as once again, a city of this fair citizen, Anthony of Astoria, has returned my valubles to me.  Folks like this guy just keep reaffirming my faith in my fellow man. 

When he returned it, he mailed it in to my job.  So, I have his name and address.  Naturally, he didn't ask for a reward, but I wanted to see if any folks had any other ideas other than just dropping a thank you note (and of course, paying it forward).
