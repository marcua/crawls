Ever think about doing a Half Marathon, or a full one while doing something for charity? Team in Training works with the Leukemia & Lymphoma Society to raise money for research and new therapies for cancer patients.  The Queens Team meets 2x a week in Astoria Park and the coaches and mentors 100% support you as you prepare for your event.  ALL fitness levels are welcome! Fall season kicks off June 11th!
