Co-ed, Social Dodgeball and Touch Football Leagues!
« on: October 23, 2008, 11:32:56 AM »
Looking for some fun, social people to get active and party with this fall in Queens?  Play Dodgeball or Touch Football with your neighbors!  Then celebrate wins and drown the sorrows of losses at organized happy hours after all games.  Help raise money for your favorite cause while doing it!

Join as an individual or with a small group and we’ll place you on a team.  Or sign up a full squad!

Registration opens Monday, October 27 for these leagues:
•   Touch Football in Long Island City (more details are here: http://www.zogsports.org/activity_details/224)
•   Dodgeball in Astoria (more details are here: http://www.zogsports.org/activity_details/212)

Whether you haven’t played in years or you’re an ex-college athlete, we welcome everyone to join our leagues.

The Winter season begins in January and runs into April.  Dodgeball games are played on weeknights and Touch Football on the weekends.

Details and how to register can be found at www.zogsports.org

Questions? Email info@zogsports.org

About ZogSports
ZogSports, the charity-focused, social, co-ed sports league, donates a portion of all proceeds to charities chosen by our teams. Join our leagues and play for a charity of your choosing!  Then head out to our organized post-game happy hour to socialize and feel good knowing that the bar is donating a portion of what you spend to yours and your fellow participants’ charities!
