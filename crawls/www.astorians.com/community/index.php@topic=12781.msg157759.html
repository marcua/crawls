I used to take my guitars for repair at 30th Street Guitars in Manhattan - they were pretty decent.  I haven't been there in quite a while, but I see by the Internet that they're still there.  I don't know if they paint there, but you can give them a call or e-mail them from their home page.  The only problem with the place is that they have such a great selection of vintage axes, it's hard to leave without buying one!  The address is:

236 West 30th Street
between 7th & 8th Aves.
212-868-2660

www.30thstreetguitars.com
