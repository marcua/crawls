Title: Bookswap: FreezePeach Cafe, Sunday, November 19th, 2 pm
Post by: BookMinstrel on November 15, 2006, 10:51:01 AM
A pre-turkey swap:

When
Sunday, November 19th, 2-4 pm

Where
FreezePeach Cafe
22-00 29th Street
just off Ditmars Boulevard
map (http://www.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=22-00+29th+Street.+astoria,+ny&ie=UTF8&om=1)

Who, What, Why, How
1. Bring books to lend or give away.
    a. If you want a book back, put your contact information inside (name/phone/email).
2. Take books to borrow or keep.
    a. If you take a book that someone wants back, bring it back.
3. All books must be removed from the cafe at the end of the swap. A thrift shop nearby happily accepts our donations.
4. (Most important) Have fun and meet fellow bibliophiles!
