Title: Afterschool programs in LIC?
Post by: mamidesamuel on August 13, 2010, 08:59:37 AM
I received this email from Queens Padeia School about new afterschool programs in LIC: 

Two great after-school classes will be happening at the Queens Paideia School this fall. They were designed by educators and parents, with an eye on the quality of the learning experience.

•   Classes start week of Sept. 20
•   Location: QPS at the LIC Arts Center, 44-02 23rd Street (near the Citicorp Bldg).
•   To register, contact tqps@earthlink.net
•   Visit QPS online at www.queenspaideiaschool.org

Repurposed Inspirations  Fiber, plastic, glass, and art media morph into works of fine art (sculpture, collages, vases) and now-usable items as students gain an understanding of where things come from and where they can still go. It is through this connection that our next generation will see the potential for purpose and beauty all around them. An exhibition of completed works will be held in December.
 
With Alyssa Ratkewitch, master’s in art & education from Pratt Institute and QPS art instructor. She also teaches at the Dalton School.

Ages 5–7, Thurs 3:30–4:30 PM in Studio 214A
Ages 8+, Thurs 4:30–5:30 PM in Studio 214A
Cost: $240 for 12 weeks

Writers Workshop   This workshop engages participants in writing as a process and a way of exploring the world. Students will write poetry, fiction, and essays, and learn to self-assess and peer edit as they draft and re-draft their work. By workshop’s end, students will view writing as an extension of their thinking and playing rather than as an academic exercise.

With Martha Witt, MFA in fiction writing (NYU) and PhD in English education (Teachers College, Columbia); professor of Creative Writing at William Paterson Univ., author of the novel Broken as Things Are; recipient of numerous fellowships.

Ages 8–12, Thurs 3:30–4:30 PM in Studio 214
Cost: $200 for 10 weeks (no class 10/7, 12/2)

Title: Re: Afterschool programs in LIC?
Post by: ssewell on September 09, 2010, 11:13:16 AM

Beginning on Monday, October 4, 2010, Serious Fun After School, Inc., in partnership with PS 85 The Charles Vallone School, will offer a full after school program for children ages 5 to 11 in the community. Serious Fun will offer care at PS 85 from dismissal until 5:45 every evening, and will provide homework help, specialized arts and music classes, as well as light snacks and active play. The cost is $15 per day for one day per week to $60 per week for 5 days a week.

REGISTRATION AND ORIENTATION
WEDNESDAY, SEPTEMBER 22, 2010
6:30 – 7:30 PM
PS 85 CAFETERIA
23-70 31ST STREET
ASTORIA, NY 11105

Families can enroll their children for as many days per week that they need care, even if it’s just one day a week. Children can be picked up from the school any time before 5:45 pm. Please note that all children must be picked up from PS 85, there is no bus service from PS 85 after the conclusion of the Serious Fun after school program.

For Fall 2010 Tuition and Fee information: http://seriousfunnyc.com/?page_id=23
For Registration Information: http://seriousfunnyc.com/?page_id=518

For children attending PS 122 or PS 84, one way transportation to PS 85 is available for $5 per child per day. Transportation from other schools can be arranged for an additional fee by calling 646-415-8901.

The Registration Fee of $50 per family and ½ of Fall tuition is due upon registration. Any remaining balance will be due November 30, 2010.

The Fall 2010 program BEGINS OCTOBER 4, 2010 and runs through DECEMBER 23, 2010 on all public school days. Serious Fun WILL NOT BE OPEN on the following school holidays or half days: Oct 11, Nov 2, Nov 9, Nov 11, Nov 25, Nov 26, Dec 24-31.

For more information on Serious Fun, please visit our website at www.seriousfunnyc.com.
