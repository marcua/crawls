Post by: AllisonTartalia on October 16, 2009, 03:28:04 AM
Hello everyone

I'm writing invite you to a free Music for Aardvarks trial class this coming Monday, October 19, specifically geared towards older children, ages 3-5.

Music for Aardvarks is an early music program that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, rhythm training and instrumental jam sessions. The original material used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.

The Super Aardvarks class will be held at Queensview Coops, 21-50 33rd Rd between crescent and 21st St) on Mondays at 3:30pm. 

I'm offering a free trial class this coming Monday, October 19 at 3:30pm.  If you'd like to join us, please rsvp by phone (212-769-6812) or email (info@musicforqueens.com)
