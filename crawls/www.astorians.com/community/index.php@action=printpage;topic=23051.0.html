Title: Re: Injured baby bird, what should I do?
Post by: masonicboom on June 27, 2012, 01:44:26 PM
Only seeing this thread now, unfortunately.

Next time, call 646-306-2862.  It's New York's new Wild Bird Fund ( http://wildbirdfund.com/ (http://wildbirdfund.com/) ) and they'll be able to give you advice and, if warranted, send somebody to pick up the critter or tell you where to drop it off (Columbus and 88th Ave).  These volunteers rescue all sorts of birds, including pigeons, plus squirrels and other critters.

Additionally, their facebook page, http://www.facebook.com/wildbirdfund (http://www.facebook.com/wildbirdfund) is fun to follow as they post photos of new patients as well as update you on the progress of others.   Not every story ends happily, but a whole lot of them do.
