I've done the Landmark forum, and can attest to what it has done for me. It works better for some folks than others, but that is really true for anything from education to therapy to medicine.
First off, I want to clarify one thing: They do not try to hide the fact that they are a business. Though their business is based on trying to help people, they make no qualms about the fact that first an foremost it exists to make money. They are not a cult, but they are built on the recommendations of their attendees and graduates.
As far as the forum, it worked for me in a few different ways. It really is hard to explain what the forum is, because from my experience, and the experience of those I've recommended to take Landmark, everyone takes something different from it. They call it a "transformation", which really does sound like a creepy cult word, I personally like to think of it as a change in perception. I analyze and look at things differently, and kind of think about how I handle important choices in my life. To me it was like a really engaging philosophy course. Yes, it did change my life for the better: I am able to handle my own shortcomings better, hurdles in my career have become incentives rather than challenges and I even noticed a stronger relationship between my close friends and I.
Like I said, It has been different for everyone I've talked to, so take what you can from it should you decide to do it. I definitely recommend it, and PM me if you have any questions about it.  :mrgreen:
