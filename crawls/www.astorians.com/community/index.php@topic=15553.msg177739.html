long story short, doctors are going to most likely give you muscle relaxers to treat the immediate pain issues and then check for serious damage - herniated discs, etc. if it's that bad you need to get to a doc asap. they can recommend you an ortho guy from there out if it's something really bad. if you trust your regular doctor it might be worth the terrible cab ride, presuming you're close to someplace you can get xrays/mri done.

as a side note, i've found the right chiropractor is great for keeping one's back from being a terrible mess or backsliding into shittiness. but for acute care you want to hit a standard/ortho doc first.
