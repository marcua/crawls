Title: Re: "Captain's Table"?
Post by: belle on June 09, 2009, 04:02:26 PM
I also really enjoyed Greek Captain last time I was there (ok, so it was maybe 3 years ago). I usually don't order fried fish, so can't say it was too greasy-- my grilled fish was always decent. As said, it's no frills, fairly basic. Most dishes were about $15 for the whole fish. You can order it grilled, fried or broiled and you receive garlic bread, a salad and a carb choice (potatoes, rice or fries). For about $15 I always thought it was an amazing deal and good, fresh fish. And sure, they prepare all the fish the same (with olive oil, garlic and red peppers) but for the price, who cares. From what I can remember there aren't really any apps, no dessert, and fairly horrible house wine, but it's the fish!

Not sure if prices have gone up and since I haven't been in 3 years and cannot say anything about the new ownership but I would recommend it (at least once to try). :mrgreen:
