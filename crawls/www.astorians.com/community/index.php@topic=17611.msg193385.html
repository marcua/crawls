I think there's a lot of potential for a Trader Joe's store on 20th Avenue from about 38th to 45th streets. There are a few large warehouses for sale over there that would make good sites. The corner of 38th street was being developed for something until it apparently stalled--not sure what's happening there, but it's depressing. The upside would be plenty of parking for those who want to drive, as well as walkability for folks living in the area. It's also further enough away not to directly compete with the smaller stores on Ditmars. The downside is more car traffic, which would be significant. Still, I'd much rather have a Trader Joe's than something else.
