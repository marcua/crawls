Title: seminar on using the internet to get published
Post by: Christine Hamm on September 27, 2005, 10:50:51 AM
use the internet to get published


Christine Hamm, who has used the internet to get published in over 70 print and online publications, is giving a seminar on publishing your poetry, fiction and articles using only online media.  She will also discuss the benefits of online networking in furthering your writing career.

Free handouts with lists of important and useful websites will be given out at the meeting.

Seminar is this Friday, September 30th, at 7PM at the Women's Studio Center in LIC, Queens.  The address is 21-25 44 Avenue, Long Island City.

For directions and more information, go to www.womenstudiocenter.org, or call (718)361-5649 or write wsc586@aol.com

The fee is 10 for women's studio members and 15 for non-members.  I know it's a little steep, but there are free handouts and snacks!
