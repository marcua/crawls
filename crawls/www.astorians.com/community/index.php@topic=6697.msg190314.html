Quote from: rg on February 26, 2010, 10:56:15 PM
The ivy room was still open into the 80's because I remember it and wasn't born until '77. Maybe some new owners took it over at some point, but it was definitely still called the Ivy Room until much later than the years you indicate.
That's true. Jack Rogers, the original owner and manager, did sell the business and move to Florida sometime around '72 or '73, though. The Ivy Room was then run by a succession of new managers, none of which lasted any more than two or three years. The former regular crowd soon moved on, and the place got seedier and seedier as it successively changed hands. Sometime in the eighties, the bar was bought by someone who changed the name, although I can't recall what it was. I moved away from that area to where I am now in 1995, and have passed by the bar only a few times since. Last time I looked, a few years ago, there was still a bar at that location. Whether the location is still a bar or not, I have no idea.
