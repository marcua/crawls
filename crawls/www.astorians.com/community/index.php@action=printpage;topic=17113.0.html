"A CUCKOO FAMILY" "MIA TRELH TRELH OIKOGENEIA"

ETHOS Productions presents the theatrical performance “A Cuckoo Family”, adapted and Directed by Larisa Antypa.
The story takes place in 60’s Athens. Andreas and Mika meet during Carnival in Venice; they fell in love and get married that same day. With their return to Mika’s house in Athens, Andreas finds out his wife is not only very spoiled but also engaged to someone else. Their home is a place with no rules, where everybody does whatever they like. Andreas will attempt to win Mika’s heart and put some order in this Cuckoo Family.

FEATURING:

Larisa Antypa, Antonis Armeftis, Ioanna Chasta, Athina Deliadi, Joanna Kyriacou,
Alexandros Malaos, Diodoros Pagoudis, Natalia Papaconstandinou, Dimitris Stivaros

and the Dancers:
