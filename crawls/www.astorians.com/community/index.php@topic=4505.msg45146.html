2007 Astoria Street Fair Schedule
« on: December 27, 2006, 12:54:25 AM »
April 29, Central Astoria LDC, Steinway St. between 28th and 34th Avenues
(1 day, 4 blocks).

May 16–21, St. Demetrios Cathedral, 30th Drive between 30th and 31st Streets
(May 16 for set up, May 21 for dismantle, 4 days, 1 block).

May 28, Broadway Astoria Merchants & Professionals Association, Broadway between Crescent and 47th Streets
(1 day, 17 blocks)

June 20–24, St. Antonio Abate Society of Castrofilippo, Ditmars Boulevard between 35th & 38th Streets
(June 20 set up, June 24 for dismantle, 4 days, 3 blocks).

July 15. Friends of Community Board 1, 30th Avenue between 29th and 42nd Streets
(1 day, 13 blocks)

July 26–29, St. Irene Chrysovalantou Church, 23rd Avenue from 35th to mid 37th Streets and on 36th Street from 23rd Avenue to under the Amtrak overpass
(4 days, 2 blocks)

July 29, 114th Precinct Civilian Observation Patrol, Broadway between Steinway and 47th Streets
(1 day, 7 blocks)

August 12, Steinway Street B.I.D., Steinway between 28th and 34th Avenues
(1 day, 4 blocks)

August 16–19, Borgetto Cultural Association, Steinway St. between 25th and 28th Streets
(Aug. 15 for set up, 4 days, 1 block)

August 18, Dutch Kills Civic Association, 36th Ave. between 29th and 35th Streets
(1 day, 6 blocks).

September 3, 30th Avenue Business Association, 30th Avenue between 29th and 42nd Streets
(1 day, 13 blocks)

September 20–23, Hellenic Orthodox Traditionalist Church of America (St. Markella), 26th Street from 23rd Avenue to under the Amtrak trestles
(4 days, 1/2 block)

October 14, Broadway Astoria Merchants & Professionals Association, Broadway between Crescent and 47th Streets
(1 day, 17 blocks)
