I've got a copy of Agnanti's menu at home (I think I downloaded it, but you don't seem to be able to now), so I just checked it for prices.  I think it's about a year old but it should give some reference:

Appetizers:  range from $4.50 to $13.00; most are about $7. 
Cheese dishes (including saganaki) are mostly around $7. 
Soups are $4.50.
Salads are $5-$9 for a small, and $7-9 for a large.  I've had the large Greek salad ($9), and it was enough for three people to share.
"Tastes of Constantinople" are $5.50 to $10.50; again, most are around $7.

Meat/Chicken entrees - several are $10.50, lamb chops are $17.50 and a T-bone or rib-eye steak ("Brizola") grilled is $20.50.
Seafood entrees - most range from $10.50 to $15; the exception is two shrimp dishes which are around $20.

There are two desserts listed - one is $3 and one is $4.
