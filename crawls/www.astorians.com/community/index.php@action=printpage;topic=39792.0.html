2017 Queensboro Dance Festival VIP Happy Hour + Show

Date:  July 19-20

Time:   5:00 PM (Pre-show VIP Happy Hour)
Followed by 7:00 pm dance performance outdoors at the Hunter’s Point Parks waterfront

Where:  Crabhouse, 2-03 Borden Ave, Long Island City Queens

VIP Happy Hour + Show Tickets:  $20 online, $25 door

Tickets & Performance Line-up:  www.QueensboroDanceFestival.com (http://www.QueensboroDanceFestival.com)

Enjoy two cool summer evenings of drinks, food, prizes, and dance performances at the Queensboro Dance Festival!  All performances are by a vibrant mix of cultural and contemporary dance groups, only from the diverse borough of Queens.  This year's AMAZING line-up includes aerial, Columbian folk, house, Bollywood Bhangra, ballet, Chinese Lion dancing, modern, Japanese, Indian Kathak, belly dance, tap dance, and more-- all FROM QUEENS!

​VIP Happy Hour includes:
~Free drinks and hor d'oeuvres
​~Awesome gift bag stuffed with QUEENS swag
​~VIP extended happy hour specials
​~VIP access to the 7pm Queensboro Dance Festival performance on the waterfront,
Hunter's Point Park
​~Free chances to win amazing Queens prizes from local Queens favorites including the NY METS, Cliffs Climbing Gym, Pilates Works, The Yoga Room, Rockaway Brewing, LIC Beer Project, Singlecut Brewing, Museum of the Moving Image, Sacredwaters floatation spa, and so much more!


*Event raindate July 26-27
