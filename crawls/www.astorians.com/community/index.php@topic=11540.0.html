Since your mother seems to be a picky eater who prefers plainer food, I'd suggest Cup, on 35th Ave/35th St (or maybe it's 36th St--just walk down 35th Ave from the movie theater).

Cup is a diner, where you can get breakfast food any time of day, and typical diner fare such as burgers, sandwiches, etc.  I'm a vegetarian who prefers healthier options and I find that there is a good variety for me there (they even mark the healthy options on the menu), including greek food.  I can bring anyone there and they will find something.

I would have to disagree with the suggestion for Cup.  In my opinion, the place has really gone downhill in the past year or so.  The last few times I've been there, my observation was that the quality of the food had really taken a turn for the worse....greasy, obviously cooked in bad oil, and extremely slow service to boot.  Maybe I just happened to go on bad days, but it is true that Cup's management has been a revolving door and unless something has changed very recently, things there have really taken a nosedive.

The other suggestions, however, are all quite good (well, except maybe Applebees!  :lol: ) and I would also add Cafe Bar to the list, on the corner of 36th Street and 34th Avenue....offering sandwiches, burgers, salads, and some healthy dishes along with a large variety of coffees, teas and other drinks, along with some outdoor seating.
