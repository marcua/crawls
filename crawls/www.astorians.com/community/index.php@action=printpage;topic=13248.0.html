Title: Re: Tiny Squashes, Baby Squashes
Post by: SmazenySyr on December 01, 2008, 11:28:00 AM
I think the mini-pumpkins are really just decorative, but ask a grocer...

As for the baby squash, I'd cook them just like bigger squash, but for less time. I like roasted squash best: split them, put them cut side down on an oiled baking sheet, and bake at 375-400 F until tender. You can check by spearing them with a sharp knife; if they slide off the knife with no resistance, they're done. You can then cube them and toss in pasta, or you can stick the cubes back in the oven and bake a bit longer with some grated cheese on top. Bon appetit!
