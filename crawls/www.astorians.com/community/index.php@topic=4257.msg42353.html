Now three or four other houses on that block (24th ave. btw 24th and Crescent) are all decked out in full regalia.  It is quite a sight to behold at night - every square inch of house-front for a good two thirds of a block is occupied by a lit-up Christmas light or decoration of some sort.  It's sort of like if you took my whole childhood block in the burbs where I grew up, and crammed all the houses into a quarter of the space, and then plugged them in.  And if you're walking west, home from the subway, you see all of this with the Triboro bridge all lit up in the back.  And then you realize that only a few short blocks ago, you passed by the Beer Garden.  And then you love Astoria.
