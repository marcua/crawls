I've eaten there a few times now, and it's good. I can't say it's great, since I"m not that big a fan of Filipino food. (I don't eat pork or beef, so that may be my problem). The one time I ate in there, I ordered a shrimp with something sauce, and when I asked the waitress to explain it, she said..."it's shrimp with something sauce". Great, thanks. It turned out to be fried battered shrimp with a little bit of pink, vinegary dipping sauce, with a side of fries. Not the most exciting meal of my life. Mr.NJinNY, however, is Filipino, so the dishes in there remind him of Mom's cooking, especially the beef jerky with white rice. I suspect that the chicken adobo, and most other Filipino dishes, contain a lot of MSG, either added or in the sauce. So if you're sensitive to it, like me, you may want to avoid. All in all, I think you have to be a real pinoy to appreciate this place.
