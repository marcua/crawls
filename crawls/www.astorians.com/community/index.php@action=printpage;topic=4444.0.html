Title: 32nd St. Step Street Nearing Completion!!!
Post by: eblack on December 17, 2006, 10:47:02 PM
It's finally time after about 13 months of work and jackhammering and trucks backing up at 6:30am, the work on my street (32nd St. between Astoria Blvd. and 24th Ave.) is almost done.  The construction crew said it would be done by Christmas and they're all laid off at the end of the year.  It's actually looking pretty good! 

They laid in the new street this past week and have started bricking over the ramp/stair part in the middle of the street.  I'm very excited to see the finished product and how long we can keep it free of trash and graffiti.  I really hope the neighborhood will put an effort into keeping it looking nice.
