It's a great area!  Not only close to the subway between Broadway and 30th but also an easy walk to Socrates and the waterfront.  That immediate area has seen a lot of new construction recently.  A lot more people and action.  I like it.  You may also enjoy the proximity to Bear (restaurant) and The Barn on 21st (mini beer garden and really good food).
Title: Re: 23rd St and 31st Ave - Nice area?
Post by: StephanieL on May 22, 2014, 02:28:28 PM
I live 2 blocks away, and yes it's nice.  Primarily residential but still on the "good side" of 21st Street.  One block away on Broadway is a mini-mall with a supermarket, post office, Rite Aid, Chase, a very good Venezuelan restaurant, and a terrific diner.
Title: Re: 23rd St and 31st Ave - Nice area?
Post by: Ntrain on May 22, 2014, 02:37:27 PM
It is a nice, quiet area.  Good proximity to shops on Broadway and 30th Ave., as well as the shopping center on 21st St. mentioned by another poster (unfortunately the White Castle just moved out recently, though some may be happy about that; I personally like the pizza restaurant, Samaria, in that center).  It does get very quiet at night, so if one or both of you will be coming and going at night and don't like quiet streets, then I would certainly consider that.  Better yet, visit the area at night and see how you feel.
