I work for Diki Daycare who have three centers in Astoria.  One on 41 Street and 31 Ave for toddlers, one on 38th Street and 31 Street for 3-4 year olds and new one opening in Sept. 2009 on Steinway Street and 31 Ave for 4-5 year olds.  Our rates are some of the lowest in Astoria, weekly between $130 - $250 (depending on the age of your child).  These rates are for a full week, M-F 7am -7 pm and includes hot meals and snacks.  The Teachers are all certified by NYS and have First Aid and CPR training.  You can call (718) 278-1033 for more info and to take a tour.
