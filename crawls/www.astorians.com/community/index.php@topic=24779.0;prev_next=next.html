First, I'm not that upset by anyone or anything that falls into the hipster category. I would avoid such a place though, since it's not my thing.
 
But the definition of hipsters is vague. I see two kinds of people. There is the one kind that does a lot of hipstery things, and then there's the wannabe, "trying-too-hard" hipster. I like the former and their contributions to Astoria, and I am annoyed by the latter and avoid their presence when possible.
 
I could dive more into it all psychologically about why the wannabe hipster persoanltiy takes hold and why it all annoys me, but maybe another time. Otherwise, I love all of the cute spots that Astoria offers, the food, the bars, the shops and the creative young people. There's just a sect within it that annoys me. Not trying to hate on anyone as a whole.
