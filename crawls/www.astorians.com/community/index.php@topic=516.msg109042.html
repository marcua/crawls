I know that one board member in particular cultivated bunches of friends before she even moved here by making everyone go drink with her when she was up looking at apartments!

Freeze Peach is a great little cafe on 29th Street, almost on the corner of Ditmars. They make a mean latte and have lots of great teas. Internet access is free with purchase of a beverage, so it's a great place to hang if your service is having glitches or you're waiting to get connected. You'll find, also, that most of us on this board are really interested (for the most part) in the more Mom&Pop kind of place than a big chain, so we're always on the hunt for local coffee shops, restaurants and the like!
