I had a co-worker who started out as a page.  Eventually he made his way over to the Insomniac show, then back to NBC to be a writers apprentice on SNL, now he's on 30 Rock.  You can see him once in a while, he plays the bald very white guy who sometimes sits at the writers' table and gets no lines but an occasional goofy look.
