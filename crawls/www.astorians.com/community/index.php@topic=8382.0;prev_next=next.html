I don't really understand why you basically would need three securities. The last month is generally considered another form of security for various reasons, so two security deposits means that they either think you are going to back out of the lease or not pay rent. Since security deposits must go into an escrow account and accrue interest, perhaps they have found that by asking for two securities, they are able to swindle people out of even more cash?

I'm being sorta left fieldish here but still! So shady!

People have a hard enough time getting just one security deposit back, let alone two.
