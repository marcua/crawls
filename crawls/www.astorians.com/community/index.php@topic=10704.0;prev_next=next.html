Very nice.

Went on Saturday night, about 9 PM, and the place was little less than half full.  It was our bartender's first night on the job, so he was a little perplexed and slow to get the job done, but was honest about his unfamiliarity with the wines & menu which was much appreciated.  He happily asked the chef about a menu question, and it took a bit longer than we expected to get our order, but we found it all forgivable because the "kitchen" is about three feet square, and there's only one guy cooking.  Once they get the kinks worked out, I think the service will be more spot-on.  The food was wonderfully prepared, everything was fresh and nicely presented, plus there are some real deals to be had.  Decor is warm and welcoming, too.

A nice addition to the neighborhood.  Look forward to going back.

Welcome, Winegasm.

 Hey everyone!
     Thank you so much for the support this weekend, we hit a bit of a rough spot during our rush hour on both Friday and Saturday night, our electric kitchen lost power plus our restroom almost fell apart!  It seems that Mr. Murphy was hanging from the rafters laughing at us:)  Of those of you who were there, we appreciate everyone's patience and understanding, we are working out all the kinks to bring you an even better experience at Winegasm.  Oh and FYI, we are now open on Monday nights, we are calling it Sin night at Winegasm, the SIN stands for Service Industry Night, we know a lot of our friends in the service industry have Mondays off and most places are closed Mondays so we thought we would open up and accomadate you!  Anyone, and everyone, is of course welcome to come.  Hope to see you there!

Richard, Laris, and Dean
