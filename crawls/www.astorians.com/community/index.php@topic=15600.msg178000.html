I've suddenly decided that I'd like to learn another language.  I haven't yet on the language.  It's a toss-up between French, Arabic, Russian, Japanese and German at the moment, but French is leading the pack.  I'm curious as to whereabouts I can go to take classes in the language.  I'm bilingual and have studied two other languages so I'm familiar with grammatical tenses and the like and feel like I can teach myself a language after a certain point, but feel like I need a guide such as an introductory course to get the ball rolling, just to learn proper pronunciation and to get in-person answers to inevitable questions that arise.  Anyone have any idea on where I can go to take evening or weekend classes in a language and as far as roughly how much they will cost.  Of course, I'm trying to keep the cost down, but don't want to totally scrimp on quality to save a few bucks.
