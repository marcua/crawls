If you're not into paying a lot or drinking much but still want to go out and have fun: The NY Road Runner's Club has their midnight run in Central Park. I think it starts near Tavern on the Green.

3-mile run starts at midnight, and features Grucci fireworks, cups of champagne at the water tables, and costumes! I did it a few years ago by myself, met some other people along the way, and had a blast.

Forget the cost and all that stuff.
