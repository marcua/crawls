« on: February 07, 2008, 06:49:35 PM »
I'm always wondering what the soup of the day is at Il Bambino.  Their site is down and they don't have a regular soup schedule so I propose this thread.  I'll call everyday, find out what the soup is and post it here.  I think all the soups at Il Bambino are delicious.  They all come with crostinis and are a comforting and hearty meal.

Il Bambino :smitten:
34-08 31st Avenue (Between 33rd and 34th Street)
Astoria, NY 11106
Phone: (718) 626-0087

Hours:
Sun 8am-9pm
Mon-Sat 8am-10:30pm

Others can feel free to call and post it as well.  This can be the place people look before ordering.  I consider this my public service to the Astorian community.   :mrgreen:

Today's soup is:

Maple butternut squash with cinnamon. 

Oh, man, that sounds good.
