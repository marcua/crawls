Waiting for the Sun[/u]
 
I wake up in the morning.
and look at the clock.
I check the telephone,
for any missed calls.
There’s nothing to look forward to,
it’s just another day.
Nothing is the same,
since you went away.

The sun is out today,
but to me it’s always gray.
You took the sun with you,
to a far away land.
Before I get up,
while I'm still in bed,
a tear escapes my eyes,
as I pray to start the day.

and woo oh oh...

[Chorus]
No matter where you are, you’re always here with me.
I carry you in my mind, heart and soul,
You’re the sun that shines my days,
So I sit here, waiting for the sunrise.
So I sit here , waiting for the sunrise.

I brush my teeth,
Find something to wear.
I step out the door,
go wait for the bus.
I don’t see the faces,
and I don’t even care.
It’s just a another day,
in the big apple today.

Stop to get my coffee,
and continue to work.
Log on to my computer,
But it’s not the normal routine.
The difference now,
since you’ve gone away,
is that I can’t start the day.
without traveling to you.

and woo oh oh...

[Chorus]
No matter where you are, you’re always here with me.
I carry you in my mind, heart and soul,
You’re the sun that shines my days,
So I sit here, waiting for the sunrise.
So I sit here , waiting for the sunrise.

The work day is done,
I go home.
Cook dinner,
Wash the plates,
And think of you.
I say to myself
It’s 2:00 a.m. there,
and he’s probably asleep.

So I go to sleep as well,
and let my soul fly free.
I end up far away,
but right next to you,
I look at you,
let a teardrop kiss your face,
and whisper softly in your ear
I hope you realize,
That to me you are the Sun…

and woo oh oh...

[Chorus]
No matter where you are, you’re always here with me.
I carry you in my mind, heart and soul,
You’re the sun that shines my days,
So I sit here, waiting for the sunrise.
So I sit here  , waiting for the sunrise..

…and I sit here, waiting for the sunrise.
…and I sit here, waiting for you.
...and I sit here baby, waiting for you.......

   

ES
