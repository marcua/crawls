Congratulations!!!!

I had a baby in December and wanted initially to switch to a midwife. In the end I stuck with my OB/GYN, Dr. Jeffrey Mazlin, simply because I felt such a good rapport with him and just really didn't want to leave his practice.

I had a great birth at Lenox Hill (with his partner, in the end) -- they supported my desire to have few interventions, so I didn't have an induction, epidural, IV, etc. I did have to be pretty emphatic about these things ahead of time both with them and with the staff.

If your search for a midwife comes up dry (and I know it's much harder to connect with midwives now that St. Vincent's has closed, sadly), I would give my unequivocal recommendation for him:

Dr. Jeffrey Mazlin / Dr. Howard Shaw
212.517.9048

His office is at the corner of 67th and Park, so it's also really easy to get to from Astoria, even when you're 9 months pregnant ;) Oh, and he definitely takes Aetna.
