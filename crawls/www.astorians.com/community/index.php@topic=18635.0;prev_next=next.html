Hi all,

I am as disturbed as most of you regarding the recent crime wave in Astoria. My family and I are doing whatever we can as civilians to help ensure that our neighborhood stays safe.

If you are concerned, please contact the following:

The Guardian Angels:

http://www.guardianangels.org/

The 114 Cin-Op:

http://www.114thciv-op.org/

And Peter Vallone:

http://council.nyc.gov/d22/html/members/home.shtml

Below is a form letter you can use:

Dear ___________,

I am writing to express my concern over the alarming rise of crime in Astoria, particularly the Ditmars area. There have been sizeable number of incidents this summer that have turned an otherwise safe, family-oriented area into a very frightening place to be. I am hoping that your office can make it a priority to get some patrolling and other support in the area to help make sure that Astoria does not spiral into a place where criminals and thugs can operate unabated.

Some of the recent incidents include:

1) Mugging on 21st Ave between 27th and 28th Streets on June 19
2) Murder in Astoria Park in early July (was covered on NY1)
3) Mugging on 24th Street and 20th Ave on July 14
4) Shooting at Mike's Diner on 31st Street on July 18
5) Murder on 28th Street between Ditmars and 23rd Avenue on July 24

It is inconceivable that these crimes are being committed in Astoria, traditionally one of the safest parts of the city. There is growing concern in the community about the existence of seedy bars in the area that attract dubious patrons, many times underage. Another issue is the train stop at Ditmars and 31st Street, which has become a haven for homeless and questionable individuals that become intoxicated and create all kinds of problems to train passengers and passers-by.

I urge you to investigate this situation as soon as possible. It will be devastating to see this quiet, family-oriented community surrender to crime and lawlessness.

Thank you very much for your attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
