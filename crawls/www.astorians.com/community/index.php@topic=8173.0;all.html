I looked around and a replacement CD burner for your inspiron is $150, which isn't bad at all. However, since the external burner you tried was giving you the same problem, I'd be willing to bet that there isn't anything physically wrong with the computer, or at least not with the CD burner anyway. So the way I see it you have three options

1. Replace the CD burner. I'm not entirely sold on this solving the problem, and if it doesn't you are out the $150 or so for the part.
2. Trying to resurrect the CD burner you have. This is the avenue I'd probably go down if I was in your shoes as the only thing it might cost in the end is a few hours of time. BUT, if you have to pay someone at a shop XX/hr to do it, it won't save you any money in the end. I could probably check it out for you, if you'd like.
3. Buying a new computer.
