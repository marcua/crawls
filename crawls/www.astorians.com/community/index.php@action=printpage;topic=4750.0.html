Title: Junk Store
Post by: Newtownian on January 16, 2007, 03:58:10 PM
I was in brooklyn over the long weekend and found a store called Northside Junk. It's like a ginormous garage sale! I was in heaven being a garage sale junkie. The place is pretty disorganized, so I happened to wander to a section that, Unbeknownst to me, was off limits to customers.
Some guy working there, was totally rude to me, asked me "what do you think you're doing?" Thinking it was a joke I said " Uh, Shopping? What are you doing?" He comes back with " what makes you think you can be back here?" so I came back with" you're kidding right?" Then,  in a huff he says, "no man! All this stuff isn't priced yet".
I thought okay, you want to be an jackass that's fine with me but I want this 8-track. I ask "can you price this for me then?" He replies with a kurt "no, it's not priced yet"

"Okay, thanks alot you've been so helpful"
Now, I may be naive, but shouldn't he have been nicer? What should I have done. There was an alternate ending but I want to go back for the 8-track. And most importantly, Where is the best "junk" store in town?
