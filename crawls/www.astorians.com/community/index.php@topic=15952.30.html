I finally went last night...that mac and cheese is ASTOUNDING!!!  My friend and I introduced ourselves to the chef, who was walking around and chatting, and he very kindly told us what kind of bacon he uses in it because we liked it so much.  Our fries were amazing, not soggy at all, but we wanted more malt vinegar taste. Next time we may just ask to get a side of it for dipping.  The nut brown ale was great, although watching the bartender fix drinks made me crave one.  I didn't notice the music after a while (side effect of the beer, I bet), and Stuart the bartender is SO nice.  This could be my second favorite bar in Astoria (Sunswick's will always win).
