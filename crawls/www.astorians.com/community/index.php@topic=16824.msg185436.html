Pistilli Riverview East - Cigarette Smoke from Neighbors Apartment - Help!!
« on: December 06, 2009, 12:45:06 AM »
Hi everyone.  I've been living in the Pistilli Riverview East apartments for almost a year now.  A few months ago, I started to have a serious problem in my apartment -- my next door neighbor started smoking and the cigarette smoke comes into my apartment.  It blasts into my bedroom from the heating/AC unit (it seriously seems like there's someone in the room smoking), and I've woken up some mornings with lung fulls of cigarette smoke.  The smoke also comes into the apartment through the recessed flood lights in the kitchen.  And I think that it also may be coming in through the vents in my kitchen and bathroom.  So I'm pretty much trapped into inhaling and smelling the smoke.  It's really bothering me (physically and as an intrusion) and after more than two months of phone calls, the assistant super was here today to address the problem.  He took off the air conditioner cover in my bedroom to show me huge openings where the smoke was coming through, and sealed them with some kind of sealing foam.  But he insisted that the smoke wouldn't be coming in from the kitchen ceiling or the air vents on the first floor.

I think that his sealing the openings in my bedroom helped -- because tonight I smelled cigarette smoke in my bedroom, but it wasn't very strong.  However, on the first floor, it is still pretty bad.  And I'm certain that the smoke is coming through the recessed lighting in the kitchen ceiling.

Have any other Riverview East residents, or others, had this problem?  And if so, have you been able to get it fixed?

Any suggestions would be greatly appreciated.
