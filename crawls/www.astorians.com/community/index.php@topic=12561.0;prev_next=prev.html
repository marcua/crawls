NEW MEZZO COMEDY CLUB - ASTORIANS NIGHT
« on: October 06, 2008, 04:24:05 PM »
Hello everyone. My name is Andrew and I am the new manager at Mezzo Comedy Club. I wanted to invite everyone here to come down to a special night this Tuesday, October 7th at 9 p.m. If you say you saw the ad on Astorians, you'll get free admission and one free drink. There is a two item (food or drink) minimum in addition, however, but if you want to get dinner then we'll throw in the show for free.

I think the club and this board have had a few issues in the past, but I promise I will not spam or otherwise annoy you. I'm posting this in the activities section because it's a special event for you guys. Tickets usually range from $5-$10 otherwise. Most of the time i'll just let you know about any special acts we're having every once in a while, probably in the business section.

We're located on the corner of Ditmars Blvd. and 33rd St. We have a lot of very funny comics, most of whom live right here in Astoria. We look forward to seeing you at the show.

Andrew
