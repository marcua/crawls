My wife and I bought a house in Astoria a couple years ago and it was one of the best decisions I've ever made.  I would highly recommend not getting a broker.  We had one who was awful and decided we'd be better off looking ourselves.  When we looked at our house, we put in a low bid, not thinking that we'd get it, but the seller's agent was happy to take the full commission himself and pushed for us to get it.  It helped that our credit was good and we had a pre-approved loan and all that.

My wife's uncle works in real estate a lot and told us that whatever the market is doing, the best time to buy a house is when you're ready to own a house.  Don't worry about housing bubbles or anything else, especially in a community on the rise like this one.
