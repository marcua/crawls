Post by: sybil86 on August 13, 2011, 05:34:08 PM
The realtors that we've been working with have been saying the same thing.... so I don't think they're trying to scare you.  August seems like a tough month for apartments in general.  Any decent inventory that is available is quickly snatched up and landlords are jacking up the prices like crazy due to the demand.  Apparently there are small one bedrooms now going for 1900 which is ridiculous... and just because they've been renovated with marble countertops.

I would definitely try craigslist though... we lucked out on our apartment by going that route. The apartment in the ad wasn't available by the time we walked into the office, but the realtor had just gotten a fresh listing that was perfect for us in every way.

August seems a little early to search for an Oct 1st apartment.  Hopefully there will be a better inventory in September.  Good luck.
