I lived on 32nd Street near Broadway between '87 and '01 so that area is near and dear to my heart.   I don't get over there much anymore, but recently had a dentist's appointment at 33rd and Broadway and I did notice all those Broadway vacancies.

Between 32nd and 33rd it was sad to see that deli go under.   When I lived there it was a terrific deli run by a Greek family.   All the siblings worked in there and it was friendly and had a great selection on items.   Before I moved, the family sold the business and there were several owners after, but it was never the same after the family sold the business.

And I never shopped in Raleigh, but remember when that clothing store and another almost identical clothing store between 32nd and 31st were both open.   Both are now gone.

And there was that catering hall on between 32nd and 31st.  I never went into the place, but I thought it did well.   I remember several real big occasions in '88 when Greek American Michael Dukakis ran for President.   He made several visits to that hall.  It was a zoo when he visited.   The crowds spilled into the street.
