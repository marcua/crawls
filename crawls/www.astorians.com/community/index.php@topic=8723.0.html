While it's not as nice as having a temporary butler deliver wings directly into ones hands, couldn't you call McCann's or Dillinger's and ask for X wings to go, and then pick them up, and eat them on your friend's couch, sofa, or even love seat?
If that's not an option, and you're still out of options here is what I would reccommend:

Step 1: stop by your local grocer's and procure 1 large bottle of Frank's Red Hot Hot Sauce™ and one stick of Butter™.
Step 2: Go home.  Open a beer (or pour yourself a nice drink).
Step 3: Call a Chinese delivery place and order the number of chicken wings you'd be interested in consuming this evening.  Chinese places have frightfully delicious chicken wings because they fry them in the same vat (or fat) as all their other scrumptious food.
Step 4: You've probably finished your first drink, so have another while you're waiting.
Step 5: Melt the stick of butter in a sauce pan, making sure to not burn it, if you're drinking quickly, have another, you're almost done.
Step 6: Answer the door for the delivery guy and exchange currency for fried chicken flesh.
Step 7: In a large bowl mix melted butter, fried chicken flesh and Frank's Red Hot Hot Sauce™.  Tada, Tuffalo Wings, and you can make them as mild or spicy as you like by just adding more or less Frank's Red Hot Hot Sauce™.
Step 8: Return to couch, consume, don't forget your drink.
