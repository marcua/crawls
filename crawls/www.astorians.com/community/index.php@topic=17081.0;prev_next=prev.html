The Wall Street Journal's radio reporter on 880 said Walgreens doesn't know how it will ultimately absorb Duane Reade.  At one half  hour he said they'll keep their old name, the next it gets vaguer.  There's been a 20 year long mating ritual between these two chains.  "Duane Reade's goal was to be taken over" doesn't quite cover it.  DR was saddled with so much debt there was nothing else to do, there was no connected Medicaid pharmacy group tied to it as with Rite Aid.  It's that national shipment pharmacy that is keeping Rite Aid afloat at the moment.

They'll be site duplication just as when Rite Aid bought Eckerd;  that will mean the smaller non-performing stores will also go.  Remember, LOCALLY we'll have job loss in the chain's main office in LIC.
