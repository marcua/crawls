Title: Our very own Costa to be honored!!
Post by: mcdirk on March 25, 2009, 08:45:53 AM
Come out and join us at the Wood-Heights Democratic Club Dinner Dance, where our very own Costa Constantinides is being recognized!

The Wood-Heights Democratic Club's sixth annual dinner-dance will take place on Thursday, April 23 at Dante Caterers, 75- 07 31st Ave., Jackson Heights. The club is headed by Assemblymember Michael Den Dekker, who also shares the Democratic district leadership with Ellen Raffaele.

Honorees at the affair will be Joseph Mannion, president of SEIU Local 444, Sanitation Officers Association; James T. Callahan, president/business manager IUOE; Costa Constantinides, President, Queens County Young Democrats and Donna Raymond, North Queens Homeowners Civic Association president.

Tickets are $65.
