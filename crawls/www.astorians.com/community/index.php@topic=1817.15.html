I think when it comes to salons its very much in the individual experience and interpretation. I know people who RAVE about SPACE and once I went there and this girl did a fab job on my hair.. I went again, and she totally destroyed my hair and put in a color that pretty much didnt show. The ends were razored to the point where I had to chop off about 5 inches of my hair to get it to be healthy again.  But this same girl did an amazing job the time before so I cant say she sucks or anything, just not my cup of tea.

Hair stylists are artisits... And 90% of the time, its about interpretation..and communication. Im pretty open when it comes to my hair, I steer sort of on the funky side and like to let people who have the same vision as me, be creative.. within reason. If there is something specific I want, I bring LOTS of pictures.. The girl who did my hair at lift was totally my style.. infact when I saw HER hair I was like I LOVE IT and we just had excellent communication, so I walked out happy. lol

Asia was really really nice with me, we chatted for about an hour about kids, and catty hairstyleists (how its so unnecessary) and random stuff. But who is to say she wasnt having a bad day when she saw you .. who knows. I also work in an industry where I have to deal with people, and while you try your best to run your business with a smile on your face .. you do have those days. While I LOVE LOVE LOVE all of my customers, Im sure there is someone out there saying "ugh that Jessica at bejessie was so rude to me! Im NEVER going back to her again!". And Ive also been on the other side of peoples rudeness as well (I once had sales guy call me trash at a boutique in SOHO because he was hitting on my boyfriend), and yeah for that I will prolly never go back (unless its to show him how "trashy I can be" hisssss! lol). SO I totally hear you about lift.

If you find a spot/styleist that works for you go with it...
