Title: Ski Lessons for 6 year old
Post by: Sweeper on November 20, 2008, 11:05:22 AM
With the feel of Winter upon us, does any Astorian parent have any recommendations for a family friendly ski resort that offers lessons to children?
Title: Re: Ski Lessons for 6 year old
Post by: murph on November 20, 2008, 03:02:46 PM
i am neither a skiier nor a parent, but i can say that Mount Snow, VT is very family friendly. there are kids everywhere. there is a large bunny hill for lessons, with a "magic carpet" to get the little ones back to the top. the magic carpet is like those "people movers" in the airport where the floor moves forward...but on an incline up the hill obviously. it's a million times easier than trying to teach kids how to use the J or T bar lifts.

i've snowboarded at Mount Snow for the past 5-6 seasons. it's about 4 hours away in southern Vermont.

other places to check would be Hunter Mtn, NY and Mountain Creek, NJ. i'm sure they have lessons. what i don't know is how family/kid friendly they are. both mountains are about 2-2 1/2 hours away.
