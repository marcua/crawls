More than likely it's planes that are taking off and not coming in for a landing that we hear so loudly. When it's stormy (or when the wind blows in a certian direction) planes will take off heading out over the water. It's safer in low visibility to have a plane take off over water obviously so that if there's a problem they don't blindly land on houses.

The problem is though that when the take off in that direction we are directly behind the engines as they climb out after takeoff. You hear a loud rumbling for a minute or two then it quiets down as they make their turn. It can get really annoying when you're trying to sleep, that's for sure.
