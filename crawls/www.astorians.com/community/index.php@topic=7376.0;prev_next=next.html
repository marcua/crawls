Farminginton River Tubing! So much fun! (Especially if you have a group going up.) It's about 2 hours away in northwest CT, but its an easy ride - take 95 or the Hutch/Merritt to Route 8 in Bridgeport, then just go north all the way to the end of Rt. 8.

One suggestion: if you are flexible about your dates, try to go after a good rain - the water will be deeper and stronger from all the runoff from the mountains. There are only a couple of "rapids" (as they call them), but they're not too scary and they have staff there to help guide you through them. Have fun! :)

(Also, if you choose this place, I will let you know where to get the worlds best hot dogs about halfway between here and tubing - I'm from Bridgeport :) )
