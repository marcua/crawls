Title: Re: Anywhere I should *not* buy lamb?

I like Akropolis on 30th Avenue just east of 31st Street.  We bought a 6-pound leg of lamb from them on Saturday, and the guy behind the counter cut it so that it would fit better in our roasting pan.  Also, when I've asked for ground lamb there, they grind up shoulder cubes fresh.
Title: Re: Anywhere I should *not* buy lamb?
Post by: Yaxpac on April 07, 2009, 12:21:25 PM
I think that International Meat Market on 30th ave around 36st is the best in the area by far. 

International Meat Market
36-12 30th Avenue
Astoria, NY 11103
Tel: (718) 626-6656

It is one of the cleanest butchers I have ever seen.  They will cut to order, debone, roll & tie...whatever you want.  I think it is one of the better butchers in all of the boroughs actually.  They will order anything that you want too.  I ordered a rack of elk last year and it was amazing.
