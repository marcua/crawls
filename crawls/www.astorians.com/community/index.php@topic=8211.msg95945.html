el Taco Truck in front of Rite Aid on 30th avenue and 32nd street. Evenings from 730pm on.
Yes it's a grill on wheels, i was wary at first---but he's been there for at least a year and a half and is superfriendly.
He's helped by his family, seems like a true mom and pop operation.
I haven't been dissapointed yet. I've had the soft tacos, the rice plate and the huarache.
The soft tacos are unlike any i've ever had they have radishes, onions, lettuce, tomato, lime wedge, red and green sauce and choice of filling.
good stuff kids, check it out!
