one of the things i really like about NYC is that we don't have huge houses.  while i suppose all of us want "one more room and two extra closets" i have no interest in a 3500 sq foot home, or even a 2000 sq foot home.  i would have to fill it with furniture and heat/cool it.  i like having fewer possessions and a smaller ecological footprint.  i like not needing a car.
