It depends on what kind of event you want to go to. I'm not sure if your sister is older or younger and if she can drink or not. If she can drink, Karaoke shout is fun, you can rent a room and order drinks and sing it's located on 32-46 Steinway St., Astoria, NY 11103 it's good for a group

If you're looking for more family friendly activities, there's Astoria Bowling located 19-45 49th St. Queens, New York 11105 and there's a Groupon deal for that: here     (http://www.groupon.com/deals/astoria-bowl-3?pt=aUTYqKDAAAAAFs2RNyaUauCFqYX6C3Fbr)

I also saw something on living social about a bus that takes you paint balling to some place in PA and then you have a BBQ, but if you're going with a group, it might be a bit pricey I saw that: here (https://www.livingsocial.com/events/575448-paintball-bbq-day-trip)

and if you just want to have a nice dinner out there's Wave Thai, which has really good food, but it's kind of a quieter, smaller setting : 2137 31st St. Astoria, NY 11105
Also there's both beer gardens if you have a big group and also this place opened up not too long ago and I've wanted to check it out, but it looks kind of cool called The Strand: 25-27 Broadway Astoria, NY 11106 I've walked past a couple times and it seems like it would be good for a big crowd, again probably only if your sister is old enough to drink.
