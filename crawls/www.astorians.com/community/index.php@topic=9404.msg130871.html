I think it's more like a cake that you were all excited to eat, but then you put it in your mouth, and it tastes slightly off, and you realize some ingredients were missing. And you're really bummed because you were really excited about that cake. But somebody left the cake out in the rain...and it too so long to bake it...and I'll never have that recipe again...

***SPOILERS AHEAD*****
but seriously i'm disappointed. The episode before last was building up to something awesome (the Ben and Hurley candybar episode) and this just feels like they kitchen sinked a bunch of crap for next time.

I did enjoy it, though. It was fun guessing what was going to happen to the ones outside the "oceanic 6." Was nice to know that things worked out for Desmond and Penny. But I wanted to know if Penny knows what her dad is up to. Why her dad hates Desmond. 

Problems:
1. The reason for them lying is stupid. The plane at the bottom of the ocean story is already out and accepted. If it is now shown that there were survivors, aren't the survivors now going to be targeted by Widmore and his gang? I'd think now that the island has been moved, the ones left on the island are now pretty safe too.
2. What has Locke done that is so horrible that they all hate him?
3. Why would it have been so important for Jack to remain on the Island? Obviously the island has plans for Locke, and Ben (hd), but I never got the impression the island cared one whit for Jack. Unless it's woven into this whole Claire thing. Tht's really confusing.
4. Why would Kate be like, "i have to explain to (Aaron) every day why you're not here). It's not like Jack is his biological father! And she's not even his mother! Just seemed kinda weird.
5. Why didn't Desmond yell at all the deck hands to get off the boat? When he came running up top, there was a poor dude swabbing the deck - shouldn't he at least have said, "if I were you I'd get the hell off the boat!"

I do however liked how they repeated a flash forward, and how now we know how Ben ended up in the desert with that parka on. Ben has really grown on me the last few episodes.
