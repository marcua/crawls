rock on!

please join us tomorrow.

if you plan on crocheting or knitting start off by buying a skein (ball of yarn) that is not all bumpy and funky looking, use worsted weight yarn. it looks like a normal ball of yarn. :) red heart super savers are great for beginners. and be sure to get yourself a color that is not too dark. it will be easier to see you stitches in a lighter color. (grays, pastels)

if you are crocheting, be sure to purchase a J (6.5mm) hook.

if you are knitting, get size 10 needles.

get yerself a great cup of tea, and join us! :)
