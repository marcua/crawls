Going rate for a 2BR in Ditmars area?
« on: June 13, 2008, 02:35:10 PM »
Well, folks, our landlords are at it again.

After 1.5 years in a small 2 family home, the owners told us they're raising the rent by $100 per month in anticipation of "high cost of oil".

Can anyone weigh in on the current rate for a 2br apartment around Ditmars these days? I want to know if we're getting shafted or not.

Unfortunately, www.rentometer.com places $1750 as the median rent for our area. Also, a neighbor told us he's renting an apartment all the way up on 21st Ave & 80th Street for the same amount...seems like a lot for a remote location, which makes our new rent not seem so bad.

Some background: We moved there in April 2007 for $1550 per month. They upped it to $1600 in September, and in lieu of a lease, which they refused to give us, we asked them to sign a document promising not to raise the rent for a 1 year period. So now they tell us they're raising it to $1700 per month this September. It seems to me a 10% increase in less than 2 years is a little excessive. But I know, I know, that's the risk you take when you don't live in a stabilized building.
