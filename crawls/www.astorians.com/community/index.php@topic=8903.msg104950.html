Oh, I forgot to mention-Canton Gourmet is excellent, though it's not in Astoria. It's located in Flushing at 38-08, Prince Street near Main Street. Definitely worth the trip for their tasty seafood dishes. Their specialty is garlic chicken - whole roasted chicken smothered in garlic, and garlic crab, but they have a lot of other great dishes to choose from! Reasonably priced too. Small restaurant, usually crowded, but go on a week day or Saturday at an in-between time. Service is friendly too. The best Chinese food you will NOT find in Astoria. Anyways, it's nice to get away from Astoria sometimes! I like to go to China town or Flushing for Chinese food and to buy my fruits and veggies.
