From Costa Constantinides' site:

The Astoria community stands together with Muslim-Americans. All are invited to join me, Congress Member Carolyn Maloney, Congress Member Joe Crowley, State Senator Michael Gianaris, Assembly Member Aravella Simotas, Assembly Member Ron Kim, Muslim American Society of Queens, Ansob Center for Refugees, Council on American-Islamic Relations - NY Chapter, Muslim Democratic Club of New York, and others tomorrow evening for a rally to reaffirm our support of the Muslim community and our belief in justice.
Tomorrow, February 3rd
5pm-6pm
Gather on 25th Avenue (at Steinway St.)
Representatives from immigration and refugee assistance groups will be on-hand to provide information about resources available to families who may be affected by the executive order.  Muslim-Americans and Muslim immigrants are a core part of Astoria's history and community. Our nation was founded by immigrants and immigration continues to be an integral part of achieving the American Dream.
