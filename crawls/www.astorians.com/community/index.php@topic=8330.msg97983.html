I tend to be a bit blase about this sort of thing and I think it is due to living in NYC in the 70's and 80's.  There were homeless absolutely everywhere, yelling in the streets, running around muttering incoherently etc.  To see someone eating from a garbage can was not unusual.  People were constantly trying to wipe your windshield with dirty rags.  As a young working gal, I witnessed:

Three girls have their earrings torn off.  You would never wear dangly earrings in those days.
Six purse snatchings.
Three men expose themselves to me, on the subway.
A homicide in Bryant Park.
Two muggings.
I got hit on the head by a homeless person.
Homeless people pushing shopping carts all over the city.
Some other stuff I won't even mention here.

This was normal stuff if you were out and about in the city.  I don't miss those days (I think!).
