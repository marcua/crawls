Trundle and Capibara, I have spent a little time in Mpls and really enjoyed my time there.  When I visit, I stay with friends in Nordeast, which is a nice neighborhood - their house is beautiful!  They bought it for a song in the early-90s, those lucky ducks.  When I would visit, we would either all go out to Club Velvet to see King Kini spin, or First Ave and dance the night away (my friend ran the VIP room there).  We'd have a late breakfast the next morning at the Egg & I.  Have also enjoyed The Modern, too.  I had a few friends who ran Bitstream Underground, which also had a BBS, which was a fun virtual place to hang out.  I'm still in touch regularly with people I met on that BBS.

Loved walking around the Lake of the Isles, too.

I seriously considered moving to Mpls, as I think it's a wonderful regional center, but it's just too cold for me.
