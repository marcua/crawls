Indeed, I noticed that as well!

But it is easily explained. You see, unbeknownst to most American enjoyers of the dish, turducken is actually a long-standing French tradition. Not eaten on Thanksgiving, of course, since they do not partake of this holiday, but on Bastille Day rather. It is eaten in honor of Marie Antoinette. It is a little known fact that her infamous quote "Let them eat cake" was actually mis-translated from the French a long time ago. She actually said, "Let them eat turducken!"

And so the French have ever since...
