Are you looking for a particular nationality?  I recall having a conversation with a japanese friend (who lives in Astoria) where he said there is no particular area where Japanese people congregate.  If you're looking to meet Chinese people, perhaps Flushing.  You would find Bangladeshi people in Jackson Heights.  There is an area of Woodside known as "little Phillippines". 

If you are looking to meet other asian people, maybe there is a group you could join on meetup dot com or a similar site?  Of course, there are asian people everywhere, but I don't know how well it would go if you were to stop people on the street and ask to be friends! :-D

I'm Filipino, but most of my friends are either white, black or latino. I never really hung out with a big Asian clique, though I do have a few Asian friends.
