The thing about Queens Plaza is that it's not the nicest area.  It's where all the strip clubs are located, where the Queensboro Bridge exit/entrance ramp is located- hence lots of traffic and highway, a major transit hub with the subways, where the Riker's jail bus is let out, where a lot of construction is going on, and is very industrial.  I'm sure the Comfort Inn is nice on the inside, but it really depends if the surrounding area is that important to you.  It might not be important at all, but I think it's something you should know if you're going to stay there awhile.
