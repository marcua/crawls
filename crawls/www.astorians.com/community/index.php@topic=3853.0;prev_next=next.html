« on: November 06, 2006, 12:00:10 PM »
Just wanted to see what most folks do - if you have a big backpack on and you get on a crowded train, do you wear it on your back and try not to hit people, wear it on your back and hit people with abandon or put it on the ground by your feet?  I keep finding myself on the train next to folks who chose option 2, and am at a loss to understand what folks are thinking, of course, I've seen people kick your bag if it's too close to their feet as well, so I wonder what the best course is.  Any thoughts?

I usually option 2.  My theory is that I'm taking up less vertical space if my giant ass backpack is under my feet rather than doubling the size of my person.  I also do it so I don't hit people AND being a new yorker, I don't like having my possessions not in immediate view. 

I also only travel with bags I can reasonably handle and try to keep myself and my luggage out of the busiest spots of the train (near the doors).

Will put bag on lap if I get a seat (or tucked under feet).

Bag mentioned above is not everyday sized normal pack but euro backpack style "I am going on a trip" kind of baggage.
