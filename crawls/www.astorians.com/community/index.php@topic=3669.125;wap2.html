skoullias:
Hey all,

This is a plug to "like" the Queens Greenmarkets facebook page, and -- regardless of whether you're a Facebook user or not -- to visit Queens first year round market in Jackson Heights every Sunday 8am-3pm (vendors have been there this entire month despite the brutal cold). It's a ten minute bike ride from Astoria (I recommend 31st Ave as the preferred route to get there and back).


Support the people bringing you cellared and fresh greenhouse vegetables, fruits and juices (inc. hot cider), delicious pastries and artisan breads (the latter which is an investment in entrepreneurial activities of immigrant women), farmstead cheeses, local honey, wild mushrooms, wild local seafood, pastured poultry and meats, and wine from local vineyards. Textiles recycling, too.

And don't hold your breath or anything, but Astoria might just be getting a new market this coming season.

Fingers crossed.
