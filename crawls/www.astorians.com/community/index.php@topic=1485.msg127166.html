I have treated many bed bugs in our area. The reason why I tell clients to wrap the mattress in plastic is so bed bugs won't spread while they toss the mattress and/or after the mattress is treated.
Threw the years I noticed that even though you wrap them, rip them up, label them as bed bug infestation someone or someone with a van comes a long and picks them up and sells them for 5 dollars. In turn the companies re wrap them with new material and sell them for top dollar.
 I do hope the city gets involve to pick up infested mattresses, but I highly doubt it.
Don't forget when your dealing with bed bugs its not only the mattress you have to  be worried about its everything in the room or rooms.
