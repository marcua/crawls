Title: Questions About FanFic
Post by: Starwind51 on June 09, 2011, 04:35:42 PM
I'm hoping for some considered thoughts on these questions:

In fanfic (fiction written by fans of something), characters, whether fictional or historical, who are portrayed as being heterosexual in their original form, are often rewritten as homosexual in fanfic.  I do understand that this is just one genre of fanfic, but why do you think this is done? 


From your own experience reading/writing/participating in the creation of romantic/sexual fanfic, is there any difference between Gay and Straight fanfic in terms of whether the stories are written in the first person (he kissed me/she kissed me) or the third person (he kissed her/ he kissed him/ she kissed him/ she kissed her)?


I could understand anyone who has amorous feelings for someone they don't actually know spinning romantic/sexual stories featuring themselves and the object of their affection, but I'm wondering why people re-write two other characters this way.
