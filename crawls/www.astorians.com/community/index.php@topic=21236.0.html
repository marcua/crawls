There's a better cost effective method of reducing your bug problem...Silicone. Just seal all cracks in your kitchen with clear silicone. Any large hole should be filled with steel wool. Outside of kitchen, seal pipes in your bathroom, keep a wire mesh drain cap over your bath tub drain and more importantly, throw out trash containing food every nite before heading to bed. If that's not possible, seal any leftover food in a plastic bag until you're able to toss it the next day. I guarantee you a bug free home by as much as 98%.
