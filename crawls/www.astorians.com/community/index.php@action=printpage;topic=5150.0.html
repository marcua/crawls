Title: UFC - Korean Fried Chicken
Post by: Astoria Luv on February 10, 2007, 10:27:36 PM
Tonight, my friend and I went to the Unidentified Flying Chicken in Jackson Heights (71st Street and Roosevelt Avenue).  The restaurant was featured in a recent NY Times article regarding Korean fried chicken (link to article below).  Yes, you read it right, Korean fried chicken. If you like fried chicken, I would suggest you go there!  While the service was poor (a very nervous and disorganized woman kept on asking what we ordered!) and we had to wait for at least 40 minutes to get our food, the chicken was absolutely amazing and well worth the annoyance.  I have never had fried chicken that was so tasty!  In addition, the french fries that we had as our side were very good!

http://www.nytimes.com/2007/02/07/dining/07fried.html?_r=1&oref=slogin

I recommend that you call your order in about a half an hour ahead of time as they fry the chicken for each order individually.
