Title: Ultimate Frisbee, Tuesday evenings, near Hell Gate bridge
Post by: adamwc on April 10, 2007, 10:43:36 PM
FYI for any other ultimate players lurking here.

Had a terrific time playing ultimate tonight, starting around 6:15PM by the Hell Gate bridge. There's a great group of people playing on Tuesday evenings and they seem pretty committed to making it a regular thing. Especially with the promise of oppressively hot weather in months to come, I'm thrilled about the availability of an evening game. Also makes it so that occasional weekend getaways from NYC won't mean missing ultimate for an entire week.

There were two games (4x4) to 11 tonight. The first game, my team (Anna, Chris, Alex) won pretty handily. But Alex pulled a hamstring and had to bug out. Luckily, a dude by the name of Jonathan showed up and joined in. In the second game, the other dudes (Andreas, Jeff, Gregg(or Craig...isuckwithnames... and...the other guy over there.. :lol:)  jumped out to an early lead and actually were at game point with a 3 score lead...... but we mounted a comeback and won that game, too!  :mrgreen: F-U-N.

I hope the weather holds for this coming Saturday morning, but if it doesn't and that game falls through, try coming to next Tuesday's game.
