Title: Re: Where are you getting your Christmas tree?
Post by: artgirl on November 30, 2008, 04:45:46 PM
I've been getting my tree at the stand in front of the Rite Aid (30th Ave & 33rd St.) for at least the past 4 years, and I've always been happy with it. When I went over that way yesterday, I noticed they're open for business (they usually set up right after Thanksgiving).

I usually get a 6-foot tall tree, and I think it usually runs between $25-40 (price depends on the quality of the tree). I usually get mine in early December, and as long as you keep it watered, it should last thru Christmas. (if you let the water completely dry up, even once, it won't last)

I would suggest stopping by to check out the selection-- and if you don't see one you like, stop back in a couple days. They seem to always be getting fresh shipments in, so the stock will vary from day to day.
