Title: Re: Ever considered doing a marathon, triathlon, or century bike ride?
Post by: angledge on April 24, 2007, 01:08:59 PM
There are different lengths of triathlons, just like there are different lengths of running races or swimming races.

The Westchester Triathlon is an Olympic-distance triathlon, so the distances are:  0.93 mile swim, 24.8-mile bike, & 6.2-mile run (1.5 km/40 km/10 km).

There are sprint triathlons that are shorter & half-Ironman, Ironman, & Ultraman distances that are longer.

As always, Wikipedia knows all:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triathlon#Standard_race_distances
