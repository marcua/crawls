I've been nearly every morning since the Saturday after they opened, whenever that was. On weekdays I get a bagel to go and a cup of coffee. I think the coffee is better than at BB. I think the bagels at BB are better, though. NYCBCH bagels, to me, are good enough. Nice chewiness on the outside but maybe a bit too light and airy on the inside. I like a bagel with a bit more density and chew--like at BB. That said, the bagels are not a dealbreaker for me.

The one thing that's started to drive me crazy there, though, are the cashiers—or, really, the computer cash-register.  The cashiers are nice, friendly people, don't get me wrong. But only twice have I given my order without them asking me to repeat it. It's clear the cash register is prompting them on all the steps—from whether its to stay or to go, to whether you want your cream-cheese bagel toasted, to whether you want light- or dark-roast coffee. So no matter if I give a simple order all at once—"sesame bagel with cre cheese, not toasted, and a small dark-roast coffee"—they're fiddling with the cash register, not listening, and then ask for each detail of the order again: "What kind of bagel? Do you want anything on it? Toasted?"

I hope the owners notice this and fix the system, as it a bit annoying.

Other than that, I really like this place and am glad to have it as another option to BB, Astoria Bagels, and Bagel House.

I like this place and it is much closer to me than BB.   I hope that it gets past some of these early hiccups which are not too big of a deal.
