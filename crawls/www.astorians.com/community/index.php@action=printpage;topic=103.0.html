Sign up for this group at: http://lists.astorians.net



SUNDAY AFTERNOONS 3PM-5PM



Ever wonder how other people see you...



Come find out at a weekly "Figure Drawing for Anyone" meeting (with clothes on of course!)



Take turns modeling and drawing in a casual cafe setting slightly off the main drag (Ditmars) in Astoria.



Bring:

pencil, pen, water colors, whatever

paper

a favorite pose



Meet at the Freeze Peach Cafe at 22-00 29th St.

(Between Ditmars Blvd. and 23rd Ave.)



If you would like to see some sketches from recent sketching events check out the Freeze Peach Blog at http://blog.freezepeach.org



No instructor

No fee
