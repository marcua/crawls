OT/ Gotta love Brandford.  So open minded. The vast majority of jazz musicians wouldn't ever consider crossing over to rock.  And we wonder why people think of us as snobs?

As far as venues go, I'm much more into small intimate spaces.

Village Vanguard is my favorite.  Sitting in there just makes me think of all the history made in that room.  And I try and picture what it must have been like to see John Coltrane up on that very stage in 1961 for the very beginning of his famous quartet.  Wow.

I also like 55 Bar, Fat Cat, Smalls.  Places where there is wonderful music being made by amazing musicians and friends of mine.  The crowds are attentive, cover is cheep, and very comfortable environment.
