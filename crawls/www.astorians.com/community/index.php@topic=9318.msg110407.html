Like I posted before, and I will say again, if you actually witnessed officers drive by when there was a huge street fight, you should report it to the precinct, the Patrol Borough Queens North, the IAB.  If they don't want to do anything, call Vallone and tell him the exact time, location and details of the incident, since you had a good vantage point. 

Did you call 911?  They keep logs of the calls coming in and if it was a big fight, other concerned residents might have called.  The 911 dispatcher sends it over the air and sector cars or anti-crime should respond.  If they ignored the calls, they should be held responsible.  In the future, try to get the patrol cars number, if visible, and record it.  Unfortunately, more and more cars are coming from the generic Fleet Division (FSD), but every patrol car has some sort of way that it can be tracked against the roll call list.
