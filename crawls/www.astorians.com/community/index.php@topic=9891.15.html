Based on this thread, I recently visited the cart on 34th Avenue and Steinway (west side of the street) and I have to report that it was probably the best chicken and rice that I have ever had!  The carts in Manhattan that I have gone to have little pieces of questionable chicken, way too much rice and some wilted lettuce and tomato.  The cart on 34th Avenue had a lot of chicken, sufficient rice and to top it off, some tasty vegies and a few thick french fries.  The only downside was that it wasn't exactly piping hot when I got home, but that's not their fault, I just need to walk faster!  By the way, I saw folding chairs against the wall and people were actually eating their food on them.  I'm not really into that.

BTW, I'm going again tonight!
