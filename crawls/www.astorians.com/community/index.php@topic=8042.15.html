Experienced Drummer and Bassist ready to get a band going. We need some VOX/Lead Guitar(s) and anything else. Funky, Hippie Rock, Jam oriented sound. Definitely need some strong VOX in a musician. Bassist can sing lead on some songs and backup on all others.

Look, we have been on the road, stayed in cheap hotels and slept in a van. That was 10 years ago. We are in our early 30's now, and we are looking to get music going again and find its place in our lives. Rehearse once a week (maybe twice), with the intention of playing monthly gigs. Don't let any of this fool you though, we are obsessed, dedicated musicians. We have done mostly originals our whole careers, but here's the covers we've been jamming on right now (Grateful Dead, Phish, Allmans, etc)...please use the following song lost as a measurement of the type of musicians we are and what we're looking for :

Cream Puff War (Grateful Dead)
Cryptical Envelopment > The Other One (Grateful Dead)
The Eleven (Grateful Dead)
Cream Puff War (Grateful Dead)
Salin' Cross the Devils Seas (Allman Brothers)
Axilla (Phish)
Woflman's Brother (Phish)
Fee (Phish)
Julius (Phish)
Mike's Song (Phish)
Horn (Phish)

Plus a Few Original Tunes from back in the day, and some Blind Melon and others as well. (If you have any as well, bring 'em)

Let us know!!! We rehearse at Astoria Soundworks right now, with the occasional excursion out to the drummer's house in LI. If you want to get together and jam towards a goal, let us know.

PM me if you are interested.
Peace out.
