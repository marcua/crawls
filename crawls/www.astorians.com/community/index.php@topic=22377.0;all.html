It looks like their opening is imminent.  They are stocking the store today - lots of produce is already in place.  They are going for a warm, open look - warm wood floors, square bins with pyramids of fruit and vegetables placed throughout the middle of the store - it's not cramped.  I saw boxes with produce labeled from New Jersey, Mexico and Chile (didn't see anything organic, but I only saw a few boxes).  They also had stacks of bags of grilling charcoal in the window, and a long glass case along the left wall that looks like it may be a deli counter (although I didn't look that carefully).  I also saw boxes of Evian and Vitamin Water.

I saw someone earlier with a flier (just a regular store flier with prices of various goods rather than one specifically announcing the opening), although I didn't see any evidence of fliers at the store itself.  But it looks like they will be open by the weekend if not sooner.

Also went in on Sunday.  I was impressed overall.  Clean, nicely designed space.  Room to walk around comfortably.  The seafood section is really terrific.  Bought some giant dry sea scallops that were top quality and delicious.  The fresh fruit and vegetable section (half the store, really) is very impressive.  Very nice (organic/healthy) selection of pastas and dry goods, cans, bottles/jars as well.  Especially things like Asian stir fry condiments.  But I was disappointed that there is no frozen food section for vegetables, or single serve entrees like the Amy's Organic line.  And there is no fresh meat at all.  So it's not really a full service supermarket.  The people were friendly at the seafood counter.  And the checkout counter staff was friendly and very speedy.  I think it's still coming together so we need to give it some time.  The outside is not as nice looking as the inside.  And piling up bottled water and bags of charcoal in front of the windows definitely didn't help the appearance of the place.  I hope they add freezers and some meats. Then focus on organization and appearance and this place will be a winner.
