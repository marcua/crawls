Title: Re: Construction noise from Amtrak overpass
Post by: metallica on September 23, 2010, 04:52:53 PM
Amtrak is replacing wooden ties and maintaining the over head catenary structures/wires.

Calling 311 or even Amtrak won't stop the crews from blowing their horns. It's a federal rule that when a train goes through a work area it must sound it's horn. The workers sound theirs back as acknowledgement and to warn their co-workers of a oncoming train. This is a major project and I don't see it ending any time soon.
