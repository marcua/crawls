First off, my apologies to the ACLU who would be be happy if I had said "holiday" song.  *sarcasm*

To those of you celebrating different holidays, or not celebrating at all, start your own, or ignore this thread.

Ok, it seems lately that every artist has a Christmas album of sorts.  Haven't liked much of what I've heard.  All recycled similar sounding versions of the same old songs.  (There's only so much you can do with Silent Night and I won't even bring up Streisand *makes face*)  These however, are some of my favorites, both covers and originals.

Christmas Time -Bryan Adams
New Your Christmas -Rob Thomas
Do They Know It's Christmas -Band Aid
Please Come Home For Christmas -Eagles
Father Christmas -The Kinks
Christmas (Baby Please Come Home) -U2
Santa Claus is Coming To Town - Bruce Springsteen
Christmas Wrapping -The Waitresses  (that's right, isn't it?)

I'm sure I've forgotten some, but I have no idea where my 45's are.

Looking forward to hearing some of yours.
