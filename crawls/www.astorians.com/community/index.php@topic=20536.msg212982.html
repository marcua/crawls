Today for breakfast I made crepes with egg and ham, folded into triangles (my son loves this)
For lunch I had FAGE plain yogurt with honey
For dinner I made a Cuban dish called "Vaca Frita" translated in english its "Fried Cow", and white rice.  It's made with shredded beef, lots and lots of onions, salt, pepper, and lemon juice (lemon juice very important).  You simmer the onions first in olive oil (I use Pegasus greek extra virgin olive oil) and when the onions are tender (slice the onions thin long strands) add the shredded beef (you get the shredded beef by boiling beef that you use for stew until it's very tender then you take the pot, sit at the table, grab a plate, and one by one shred the beef by hand), add 1/2 cup of lemon juice, salt, pepper to taste and let it cook until the beef starts getting crispy.
