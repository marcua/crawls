Arguing over “the city” as in “I’m going into the city later” instead of saying “Manhattan” (I say city most of the time) seems pointless to me. As terms, they are both interchangeable.

What I don’t get, and excuse me if I sound rant-ish because I’m not trying to be, is the thought that Astoria is somehow not part of the city because it’s outside Manhattan and since it’s not part of the city, different rules apply.

Try telling the Astorians who was had their house or apartment robbed, their car or bike stolen or vandalized, who were mugged, beaten, or raped, or whose loved ones were killed in the neighborhood last year that they don’t live in “the city.” I’m sure you’ll get an earful.

Drag racing and motorcycle packs (not motorcyclists) are a nuisance because they drive recklessly, make noise at all hours and drive some people absolutely nuts.

It bothers me to think the nuisance is down to spoiling someone’s preconceived idea or image of what an idyllic or real outer borough neighborhood should be.

Now am I splitting hairs? I’d be interested in other thoughts.

Perhaps it is time to do away with the semantics (is Astoria 'the city' or not / all motorcycles vs some motorcycles etc) and get back to the original topic.

This is the simple truth -

If one bike comes past my house on 19th Street at an average speed, no biggie.  However, the regular routine is this:

At least ten bikes, if not more ride together, gain speed as they go down the street, often pop wheelies, rev their engines, set off alarms etc.  This happens over and over again in the course of the night.  I have counted up to 18 bikes doing this as a group.

This is on 19th Street, a residential street which houses many elderly people and families with children.

Is this acceptable to you?  If so, please come over and try to sleep in the front bedroom.  Try it for a couple of weeks and you will be walking around like a zombie.

Now, is the situation clear to everyone?

The actions you need to take:

Call 311
Call 718-626-9311 and ask the Officer to send cops down to the park
Call Vallone's Office

Do this consistently.  Everyone who experiences a problem with this - be relentless.

I have some experience with this and can share additional info if you wish to PM me.
