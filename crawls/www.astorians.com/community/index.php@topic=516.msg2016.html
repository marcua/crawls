Excuse me for my late introductions, I'm kind of shy,

Hi my name is Zeno, I've been living in Astoria for a little over a year now and enjoy it very much.  I am a gay (monogamous) male in my thirties who is passionate about books, computer gaming, and communication.

I grew up in a very small, conservative town on Long Island (which I believe has alienated me somewhat from society) and am very sensitive as to what people think about me. I am a pacifist at heart but have learned how to take a stand and defend myself when certain people intend violence against me. I have been in many fights (unprovoked) simply for being who I am, but firmly believe that brains, diplomacy and the power of the pen always wins out eventually against brute force. Brains over Brawn, Magic over Might.

My heroes are Mahatma Ghandi and Immanuel Goldstein as well as the folks at Looking Glass Studios and Valve Software.

I am a speculator by trade, but proudly label myself as a slacker (in the original sense of the word) who is really only interested in writing science fiction, watching movies (Star Trek...all of them) & Japanime.

Politics are anathema to me.  I just can't sleep at night when I think of the people who are suffering all around the world for whatever "reason".
In their isolated pain I see myself and how I would feel (felt) in their situation. I guess that makes me a "bleeding-heart liberal" though  my politics are striclty non-partisan.The suffering isn't so bad when you know that there are people out there who are aware of it and demonstrate empathy. There is nothing more crushing to me than to be in pain and be ignored or treated like an object by someone emulating a machine.

I am also in recovery from substance addiction (alchohol, cigarettes and cocaine) and am firmly Agnostic (religiouswise), IOW I don't subscribe to A.A. or any other "programme".

All in all I relish and enjoy life.  Simple pleasures, creature comforts, camraderie and intellectual stimulation are for me an undying source of ecstacy.

My fervent wish is for this wave of oppression to pass so I can quietly pursue my hobbies in peace.  I am very interested in learning about technology.
