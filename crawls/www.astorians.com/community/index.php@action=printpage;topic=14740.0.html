Post by: essen on April 25, 2009, 05:57:22 PM
Blackbirds, on 30th Ave and 42nd Street. They have TVs that play sports, but it doesn't have a "sports bar" feel to it. Decent beer selection and good food. The last time I went, there was a band playing and it was way too loud, so I guess look out for that if you plan on having a conversation.

Post by: Christine on April 25, 2009, 09:41:13 PM
Indigo is nice, plenty of sports tv but not overbearing, contemporary atmosphere without being lounge-y (genuine Irish bartenders too!)

Cronin & Phelan's is a good pub, local but not a dive, also good tv w/o being too much.

McCann's can get a little too sports-like for my taste, but I suppose it depends on the night.
Title: Re: any reccomendations for a cool bar?(not into places like Kavo or central)
Post by: SplishSplash on April 26, 2009, 12:18:13 AM
Rapture! It's on 28th Ave. 35th st. Happy Hour EVERY day 5-8 pm, 2-for-1 on ALL drinks! Everything is reasonably priced anyway though. It has a very chill vibe to it, with couches and big comfy chairs all over. The food is pretty good too. It's a pretty small place, and gets kind of busy on the weekends, but not crazy crowded (at least when I was there). And if you have a table, you'll have a waitress so you won't have to yell over a sea of people to get a drink. I'm actually really surprised that more people don't go there.
