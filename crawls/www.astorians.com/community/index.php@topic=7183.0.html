Actually I don't go to the one on N. Blvd anymore. There is one in East Elmhurst on 25th Ave & 72nd St. You need a car to get to it, but believe me there are (almost) no lines whatsoever. And it's spotless!

Usually wireless doorbells should work through brick or any other material, but if they don't, then maybe your landlord has to invest a little money to have the wires run through the brick. Some of the wireless ones run  on batteries, and it's better to avoid those. Better off using the plug in types, since they give a stronger signal.
