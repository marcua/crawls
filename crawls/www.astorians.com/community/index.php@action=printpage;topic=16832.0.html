Title: Wushu*Kung Fu instruction in Astoria
Post by: _vectorzero on December 07, 2009, 11:49:55 AM
Wushu is the art of stopping violence and the grandfather of all martial arts. It is best known in the US thru Jet Li, Jackie Chan, Gordon Liu

I've been attending Wushu classes for over 8 months, It has improved my strength, stamina, and flexibility. It has taught me that power comes internally and that only your insecurities pose a threat to your achievements.

We learn modern and traditional forms and weapons, self defense(Qin'Na), acupressure therapy and massage, SanDa/SanShou(ChineseBoxing) and acrobatics.

The classes are off of the N/W at Ditmars.
I definitely love the fact that I was able to find this training in the area cuz the other schools I found were located either deep in queens , lower manhattan or brooklyn.


Send me a msg if you would like to know more.

Cheers  :-)
J
