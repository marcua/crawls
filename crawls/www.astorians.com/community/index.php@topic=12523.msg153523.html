I went to Planet Fitness in upstate NY and it was a nice gym, and yes, insanely cheap. No tricks, though.  I think upstate it was like $10 a month if you signed a contract, or $15 a month and maybe a $50 initiation fee if you didn't sign a contract, but without the contract, you could leave at any time.  The gym I went to was very large and had new equipment.  Staff was mostly high school/college kids without any real fitness knowledge, but they left you alone and you just went in there and did your thing and left.  My only problem with the gym was that (maybe because of the low price) there were lots of useless people hanging around, spending more time socializing than working out.  Lots of high school students, but again, this was upstate NY and the gym was located a couple of blocks from the high school.

Basically, Planet Fitness is like the Walmart of gyms.. they are rapidly expanding, and by offering such a low price, will definitely take business away from the other gyms in the area.  That's how they make their money--volume, and as was said above, many people pay and then never show up.  I'd say it's worth checking out if the location is convenient to you.
