The queensview coop playground (it's not really "private"- everyone who lives in the area goes there) at Crescent/34th ave (the walkways lead to the playground, in the middle of the buildings). It's a nice playground, cool parents- at times a little bit of a hipster quotient.

If I'm allowed to post about this here- I belong to the astoria meetup for SAH moms (though many are not stay at home) which is a really diverse group of moms.  Pretty cool moms (though many dads go to the weekend events, too) very converse friendly.  We do a great bookclub, have dinner at astoria restaurants, and have some pretty cool meetups during the week with kids- playgroups- sure, but also trips to museums, trying out the many cuisines of queens (and we try to do some weekend activities, too).  check out the sahm meetup site- search for astoria, and if you are interested feel free to email me.

My friend Haydee has the same problem, since she and her husband moved back to NY from Miami she has had a hard time meeting people with kids. Most if not all of our group of friends are single or married without kids. So, since they stop by @ least once a week with Sammy their 2 year old son, I came up with Tuesday Night Tots n Tonic. It's a night during the week that parents who have kids from 6 months to 5 years old to come to Hell Gate Social meet other parents with kids while enjoying a libation. And since raising a child is an expensive undertaking we will do happy hour specials from 7-9pm with $4 beers, martinis, and well drinks. We will also be showing kids flicks either inside or outside weather permitting, to keep the kids occupied so that you can enjoy yourselves.  We will be starting Tuesday Night Tots n Tonic on September 12th, so tell those you know with young infants and toddlers about it.

Hell Gate Social
12-21 Astoria Blvd
718.204.8313
hellgatesocial.com
