Here's what you do...

Get yourself a George Foreman Grill.   Then hit the local supermarket and get yourself a package of "top sliced" hot dog buns (this is more key than you may think) and a package of Sabbrett or Nathan's "natural casing" hot dogs.   Grill the dogs in the GFG for about 9-10 minutes and lightly toast the buns.   Some sort of spicy mustard is best, but you can also dip the dogs in a bowl of baked beans.   (I prefer the Bush's Baked Beans with Onions)

You'll end up with two extra rolls because the natural casing dogs come in six-packs while the buns come in eight-packs.   I usually will use the extra buns to sop up soup or stew.

Enjoy your dawgs!!
