Rendevous really isn't meant to be a restaurant, it was intended to be more of a bar/lounge.  They do serve a limited menu from the same kitchen as Bartolino's, but in my opinion food isn't good.  Drinks are good, but atmosphere is lacking and so is the crowd.  We've tried it a few times for happy hour and also later on a Friday night, but left after 1-2 drinks because it was totally dead.  Service is good and Bobby, the owner, is very nice, but the place is still in great need of an jump start.
