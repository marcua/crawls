Title: Astoria CSA Opens to New Member at Q&A March 10
Post by: belle on March 01, 2011, 09:57:16 PM
Registration opens to NEW Members Thursday, March 10 at Astoria CSA's New Member Q&A, 6:15-7:45 PM.

New Member Registration & Farmer Q&A, Thursday, March 10, 2010, 6:15-7:45 PM
This event provides: New Members an opportunity to ask questions to members and our farmers; Registration opens to new members; Find a share partner; More information about Extended Payment, Low-Income Share Options and all our fabulous Add-on options!

The event is held at ARROW Community Center (35-30 35th Street, b/w 35th & 36th Avenues).


Membership is first come/ first serve. Don't forget to check other local CSAs for information/ space too: Harvest Astoria (east Astoria), Hellgate CSA (north Astoria), LIC CSA and Woodside CSA.
