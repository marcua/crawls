the DOE will probably be changing things pretty significantly for 2012--they are currently requesting proposals from testing companies for a new test.  but, pm me with any questions--having gone through the process I am happy to share what i have learned.  One thing that won't change--the best time for testing is the year BEFORE your child enters kindergarten.  entering in kindergarten you will have the most options--so set aside the insanity of testing 4 year olds.  (they have fun--and most don't even know it is a test unless you tell them).  you will need to register in September of that year (you can sign up for automatic emails with information on the DOE website.
