Although the store is notably grungy, the Trade Fair on Ditmars has the best beer selection I've seen in the immediate area. Lots of beer from other countries, and reasonably priced. I bought some Asahi there the other day and it is quite nice. I've bought a lot of Brooklyn Weisse there too. They only keep a few beers in the fridge, the rest are in the back corner of the store. I haven't noticed Steel Reserve, but I'll keep an eye out.

I usually Key Food.  They have Dog Fish Head (by my request).  They are also receptive to getting new stuff.  Have had success with submitting request in writing.

Or you might just need to discover Dog Fish Head, Raison D'Etre, the best beer ever.

Also on 23rd ave and ummm 27th street?  The bodega on that corner is well stocked.

As for bars, Fatty's at Ditmars and 24th(?), they have a nice beer selection and some good outdoor seating.
Sparrow across from the beer garden (24th Ave at 29th Street) has some good beer and can be a great mellow bar (or an odd sort of outing).
