DJ YONNY
Mixing records is an art; a talent that New York City’s DJ Yonny has infinitely mastered. By way of Delaware /Philadelphia, Yonny has quickly made a name for himself as a versatile DJ. The Colombian and Puerto Rican mixer is notorious for his ability to read crowds and respond to their energy with the perfect mix, hence his official moniker “Mixmaster DJ Yonny.
NYASIA
NYASIA has been singing professionally for the past 20 years. After being signed to Mic Mac Records in 1990, her debut song, "Now & Forever", went to the number one spots in Miami, Brazil, Chicago and Texas. Her next Single, "Who's Got Your Love", crossed over in California before hitting New York and hitting the top ten. This track was on BILLBOARD, "Hot 100 Airplay" & "Billboard Hot 100". Her "self-titled", debut album, spawned eight 12" singles with countless remixes. That album had the talent of producers/writers; Mickey Garcia, Nelson Cruz, Ellis Pacheco, Michael Berdot, Mauro DeSantis & Mike Lerello just to name a few. Duets included songs with George Anthony & David (formerly of Nice & Wild)
