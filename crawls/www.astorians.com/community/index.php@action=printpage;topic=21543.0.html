Title: Once in a while NYC makes me smile...
Post by: astoristani on August 23, 2011, 10:28:28 PM
Astorians,
Sunday evening I was coming home from a paranormal investigation of the abandoned buildings at the Rockland County (New York) Psychiatric Hospital and pondered my options of how to get home without an umbrella in the torrential downpours.
I decided to jump off of the LIRR at 125th St. and catch the M60 across the bridge. But, somehow I managed to be distracted and ended up at GCT. ACK!!!
So, I jumped on a 7 train to Queensboro Plaza and transferred to an N after hiding in the middle of the platform to avoid the rain. When I got to Astoria Stop, I waited for a M60 bus at 31st and Astoria.
I made my stop at 44th St. & Astoria Bl., Pushed open the back doors of the bus, and was ready to run the half block to my apartment.
As I was about to leave the bus, I heard someone yelled, "Buddy, you forgot something!"
 I thought what could I have left behind? I have my wallet and keys and my bag of research equipment.
The Canadian couple handed me a cheap umbrella and said "you forgot this umbrella that we found in the restaurant tonight. Good Luck!".
I stayed dry and made it home.
A New York experience that makes me smile! What's yours?
