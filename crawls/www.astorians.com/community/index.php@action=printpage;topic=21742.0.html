Post by: Dance Entropy on October 02, 2011, 09:52:46 PM
Cross Pollination
Pumpkin Cheesecake!
A seasonal event for performers, dancers, sketch artists and musicians to jam, improvise, collaborate and get to know one another!This is a unique opportunity to explore the combination of visual, motion-based and audio arts. As host, Green Space hopes to provide a relaxed environment for spontaneous, free expression and improvisation between the different forms while encouraging synergy amongst artists.


$5 at the door
Friday, October 21st 2011 7:00-10:00 pm


For each event we are including a theme based on different, unique celebrations that occur on that particular date. October 21st happens to be National Pumpkin Cheesecake day so come on in and interpret that as you like!


Also. Artists should bring simple materials: non-messy, acoustic, easy to carry, etc.
These sessions are open to artists, dancers, musicians and viewers alike.
Food and beverages allowed!

Where is this at?
Green Space 37-24 24th St.  Ring #301 and come up to the second floor!
