I know you can set the MySpace music app to not start playing automatically when you load a page.  Just go to Account Settings > Music Settings.  Embedded content like quicktime or flash stuff will play regardless.

As far as slow pages go, I think it's a couple of things that really make them awful.  A lot of people use layout editors and want to throw the kitchen sink into the mix in addition to a bunch of songs (sometimes quicktime AND windows media AT THE SAME TIME) and huge, ugly-ass graphics.  I think it's mostly all the embedded stuff that makes myspace suck sometimes.

I see a lot of really ridiculous stuff on there but it's possible to do something clean that doesn't kill your friends' computers when they look at it.  Alison's page posted earlier was clean-looking and didn't slow things to a crawl, for example.  And of course, there's always the boring default which loads up quick.
