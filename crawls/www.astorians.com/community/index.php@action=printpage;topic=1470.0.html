Welcome to the NewMusicHouse.com Beta Site

This is the beta launch of this new site all about music, the people who love it and the people who create it. This site is geared towards independent musicians and local bands (and by "local band", we mean every local band in the world). Musicians will be able to upload music they have created and recorded while music fans will be able to find and download music that interests them.

If you are a music creator, please read the Music Creator Orientation page. If you are a music lover, please read the General Member Orientation page.

Since this is a beta site, there are going to be some bugs here and there. If you find a problem with the site, please use the "Report Problem" link on the right navigation.

WE NEED BANDS, EVERYTHING IS FREE

Since this community site is brand new, we need people and bands to put up some content. To make this as easy as possible, the entire site is currently free. All normal fees are waived while the site is still in public beta. If you are a member of a band or know members of bands, feel free to take full advantage of us.
