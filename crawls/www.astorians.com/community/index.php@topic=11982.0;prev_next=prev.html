The yellowing after using "eco-soap" for laundry is from a chemical reaction between the soap and your clothes being previously washed with detergent. (Soap-based laundry suds versus the commonly used detergent.)

Check out: http://www.ecocycle.org/hazwaste/recipes.cfm

Laundry

When making the initial switch from a detergent to a soap laundry cleaner, wash items once with washing soda only. This will eliminate detergent residues that might otherwise react with soap to cause a yellowing of fabrics.

Hope that helps!
