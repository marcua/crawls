Not in the city, but Long Beach is highly, highly awesome and LIRR does a Beach Express thing that includes admission to the beach (yeah, you've gotta pay to beach yourself there.) Long Beach is a really fun/cute town too.

I've been to all the NY Beaches.  I think the trip to the beach on the LIRR to Long Beach is the prettiest, least painful.  The walk from the train to the beach is quick and fun (since most of the train is walking there).  And if you have a friend with a car, Jones Beach is great.

Rockaway was a little dirty when I went there but that was a long time ago.  Same for Orchard. Maybe they've both been infused with money and cleaned up which would be great.

Starwind is so right about the book and the week day vs weekend.  I always go the beach during the week.
