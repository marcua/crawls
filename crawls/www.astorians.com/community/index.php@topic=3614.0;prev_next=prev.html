Stout is close to where I work, what's it like?
Stout is large and frathouse like (at least during a concert night). However, they have an incredible number of beer types on tap. Where it really shines is the private room(s). If one is throwing a private party, you have access to pool tables and a nice quiet space. Plasma televisions and an ipod doc station with five or so playlist options or the ability to plug in your own ipod.

I give the private room option a resounding endorsement and only a marginal thumbs up for the loud crowded rest of the place.
