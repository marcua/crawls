Born in Honolulu, HI - lived there until I was 7 ( Kahala Elementary )
Parents moved us to San Francisco, CA - lived there till I was 18 ( West Portal Elementary, Hoover MS, Lincoln HS )

Moved to New Orleans to attend Tulane ( BA ) - Lived there for 7 years.
Moved to Denver - biggest mistake of my life - lasted a year

Moved to DC for a job - realized after 6 months I should of chosen NY instead. DC has the most unattractive, self important, boring student council geeks that I have ever seen in a city. It's like all the dorks from your hometown boarded a bus and gunned it all the way to DC. I haven't even been back to visit.

Moved to NYC - lived in the E Village for a year - loved it but wanted more space.
Moved over to Astoria found a huge 1 bedroom + den - have been in the same apartment for 3 years!!

I love NYC - I can't imagine living anywhere else. Paradise doesn't always have palm trees!!
