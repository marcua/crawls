[For any activist young whippersnappers. If my ID is not too dated, I could try to get past the velvet ropes.]

***
Please join me to celebrate my birthday with some of New York's "next generation" leaders! On April 27th, hosts including Donald Trump Jr., Sara Weinstein, Karenna Gore Schiff and Justin Rockefeller will be helping us celebrate at Spy Club in Union Square, and I would love for you to be a part of it.

As I talk to New Yorkers around the City, I am consistently encouraged by the spirit and hope of the City’s next generation. Yet too many people feel that they have no way to voice their goals, no means of interacting with their elected officials, and no opportunity to create change. I want to prove we can!

I know we can do better to harness the energy of New York and build a better, stronger City.  I am dedicated to getting people engaged and involved, and I'm asking for your support.  To that end, the minimum contribution for this event is only $10, and I hope that you will join us as we build towards the future!

The best way to RSVP for the party is on my website, http://www.ericgioia.com/.  Clicking on the invite below will take you there. You can also RSVP at 718.426.5012, or by emailing us at teamgioia@ericgioia.com.  I really hope you can be there--I know it will be a fun night and I am sure you will meet lots of new, interesting people!
