Title: Free Learn to Ride a Bike class for adults 11/9
Post by: emiliajocrotty on October 28, 2008, 01:46:05 PM
Don't know how to ride a bike?  It's not too late to learn!

Bike New York will host one final Learn to Ride class for adults this season on Sunday, November 9 in Jackson Heights. 

Bikes and helmets will be provided free of charge.  All you need to bring is a warm layer or two and a good attitude.  A few dollars for a hot drink or snack from the nearby farmers market might not hurt, either.

LEARN TO RIDE CLASS
Sunday, November 9
9am - 1pm
Jackson Heights Play Street
78th Street between 34th Ave. and Northern Blvd., adjacent to Travers Park

You must register in advance at http://www.bikenewyork.org/education/classes/learn_to_ride_adults.html (http://www.bikenewyork.org/education/classes/learn_to_ride_adults.html).

Please direct any questions to emiliac@bikenewyork.org.  Thanks!
