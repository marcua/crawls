Sweet & Sour Watermelon & Cucumber Salad

INGREDIENTS:
1/3 cup balsamic vinegar
1 cup water
1/2 cup sugar
1 bunch scallions (chopped)
3 cups seedless watermelon balls
2 English cucumbers (sliced paper thin)

PREPARATION:
Boil 1/2 cup of water. Add the 1/2 cup of sugar to the boiling water and stir until sugar dissolves. Mix together the vinegar, plain water and sugar water (simple syrup) in a bowl; season with salt and pepper if desired. Stir in scallions. Let sit in refrigerator until cool.

Place the watermelon and cucumbers in a serving bowl; pour marinade over the top. Cover and refrigerate several hours before serving. Toss gently before serving.
