Thanks for the links, they are great. I am also an avid coupon clipper and I think that Stop and Shop doubles coupons up to one dollar. I have started bringing my bags and returning my cans and bottles, which saves a very small amount in each shopping trip. I buy chicken and other meats in large packages when they are on sale and usually break up the packages and freeze so I can cook later. I buy my veggies and fruit at the bodegas and fruit markets around Astoria because I find the supermarkets to be more expensive. I also buy some products in bulk. I eat gluten-free due to Celiac Disease so it is often cheaper for me to buy a case of one product  online rather than pick it up at the local health food stores. The only problem with that is the lack of space to store the items.
