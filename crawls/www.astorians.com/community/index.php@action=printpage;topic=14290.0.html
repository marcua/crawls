Title: Re: Art Framers in Astoria?
Post by: phillygirl on March 26, 2009, 08:41:41 PM
There is a small frame shop on Broadway across the street from C-Town (I can't remember the exact location, but it's the only one in that area). We had a bunch of really old maps that my boyfriend wanted framed, plus two generic posters we really liked, and we got them framed there. The guy did a gorgeous job. He really helped us pick out appropriate mattes and frames. We weren't really in a hurry to get them back or anything, but he said he'd get them back framed to us in two weeks. (He had to order the wood.) On the day we were given to pick them up, he didn't have them ready, which we didn't care. But when we paid, he threw in a really nice picture frame, too. I highly recommend him, and his prices were reasonable.
