Title: New Website Shows you Where your NYS Tax Money is going
Post by: Billz1981 on July 31, 2008, 02:42:59 PM
A plug for a new website from the Manhattan Institute's Empire Center for New York State Policy.  SeeThroughNY, at www.seethroughny.net is a really cool site where you can, and I quote: "searchable databases of the entire payroll of state government employees, operating expenses by both houses of the New York State Legislature, the Legislature’s pork-barrel "member items" spending for 2008-09 and current teachers' union contracts and superintendent of schools's contracts for nearly all of NY's school districts."

Worth checking out for all you government transparency/good government types.  Note that it just launched today, and is close to server crash, so if it doesn't work the first time, try try again.
