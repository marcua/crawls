Great news!  I just met with the owner of Sai Organics,  36-07 30th Avenue. 

He will be opening another store. New place will be at the old Keystone diner on 30th ave between 30th and 31st streets. The kitchen in back will be run by a Vegan chef preparing fresh items each and every day. Baked goods too.  There will also be a juice & coffee bar with some seating.

He expects to be open by mid december if all goes well.  And yes the 1st store will be staying open right where it is.

I agree, Rachel. They need to be closer to where I live. My friend is always mentioning great finds at Sai Organics, but 30th ave. is sort of out of the way for me.

Books like How It All Vegan opened my eyes to how good vegan desserts can be. JustDesserts, eggs can be replaced by egg replacer or flax seed with water or bananas or silken tofu or any number of other ways. Milk can be soy or rice or some other non-dairy milk. And replacing butter is a no brainer. Of course, it's going to taste a little different, but vegan desserts can still be insanely rich.
