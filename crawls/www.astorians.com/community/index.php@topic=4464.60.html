Hats off to Il Bambino
« Reply #70 on: August 16, 2007, 02:24:00 PM »
I've been frequenting Il Bambino, my favorite restaurant in all of Astoria, for months now. Therefore I was shocked to find, upon my arrival last night, that the menu had been slightly modified. My shock quickly turned to glee however as I perused the menu and spotted all sorts of new, intriguing dishes. I went the vegetarian route and started with the roasted garlic butter and parmesan crostini, which was delicious and easily rivaled my old favorite, the oven roasted crostini. I followed that with an extremely flavorful salad of penne pasta, cherry tomatoes, olives, pesto, and Cacio di Roma cheese and I was floored. When it comes down to flat out great food this place has raised the bar so high, that the restaurants surrounding it, once serviceable, now seem stale in comparison.
