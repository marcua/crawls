June 8, 2007
I Scream, You Scream
yummers!

It’s rich. It’s white. It’s plain vanilla.

But it’s not from Connecticut.

It’s a helping of Upper East Side, the latest flavor from 5 Boroughs Ice Cream, the husband/wife Astoria team that churns out delectable city-themed pints.

Need diversity in your freezer? Try the Jackson Heights Mangodesh, a “melting pot” of mango and cardamom. Show your love for the fuzz with NYPB, New York’s finest chocolate hearts and peanut butter (part of the profits go to the NY Police & Fire Widows’ & Children’s Benefit Fund). Then take a bite out of 10021 with UES Rich White Vanilla, made with Madagascan and Indonesian vanillas.

Other flavors include Staten Island Landfill, South Bronx Cha Cha Chocolate, and Bay Ridge Amaretto Amoré. Wall Street Mint is rumored to come next.
