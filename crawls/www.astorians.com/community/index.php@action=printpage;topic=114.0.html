Title: Irish Rover
Post by: jennifer on October 16, 2005, 11:09:19 PM
It'd been a couple of years since either of us were at there - so Jonathan and I went to the Irish Rover on Saturday night.  CHEAP drinks. A friendly bartender.  A fair to middlin' cover band - a number of songs worth a sing a long.  We were probably the oldest people in the place (late 30s).  It was fun - definitely a low key alternative to all the euro style cafe/bars in the area.
