Title: Chanukah Party and Meal: Sun, Dec 17th (RSVP by Dec 8th)
Post by: abopevents on December 06, 2006, 12:24:15 AM
The Light of a Hundred Menorahs (a bit of an exaggeration, but it'll be fun!)

This year our Chanukah party will be a catered event. Kindly RSVP by this Friday, December 8th if you'd like to join us!
Help us mark the second day of Chanukah as we continue a beautiful and fun tradition we began last year. You are asked to bring your own menorah (families are encouraged to bring one per person if they wish) and candles to the center. There will be traditional Chanukah goodies, including latkes, gifts for the children, and of course, dreidels. It promises to be a delightful way to celebrate Chanukah. We'll also have a wonderful kosher catered meal by Michael's Creative Cuisine. Tickets are $20 per person (just $5 for children 10 and under). Menu choices are: Salmon w/Roasted Vegetables or Acorn Squash Stuffed w/Couscous. Latkes, salad, dessert included. Our caterer will prepare a special Macaroni and Cheese for the kids. RSVP by December 8th w/your meal choice(s) to reserve your seats: 718-278-2680 or info@astoriacenterofisrael.org. We are pleased to be co-sponsoring this year's party with Queens' Lavender Latkes.


Astoria Center of Israel Synagogue
27-35 Crescent Street, Astoria 718-278-2680
http://www.astoriacenter.org (http://www.astoriacenter.org)
Take the N or W subway towards Queens to the 30th Avenue stop. Walk west on 30th Avenue (towards Athens Square Park) for about 4 short blocks; turn right on Crescent Street and walk a half block down on the right.
