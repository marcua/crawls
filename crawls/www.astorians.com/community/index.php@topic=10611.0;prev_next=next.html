Hello helpful, resourceful board.

I am turning 25 during labor day weekend. I want to have a bday party to celebrate not only this, but the end of my fellowship and the start of my graduate school career. This will also be a joint bday party with a close friend who has a bday several days later. But being that labor day weekend is always a problem (I have almost 25 years of experience to attest to that) I will probably have the party the weekend before. I was entertaining the idea of having a party in Astoria/Long Island City, although Pete's Candy Store looks like it may be promising as well. Here is what I am thinking of:

A bar or bar friendly restaurant. I think we may all congregate in the mid-afternoon and go on into the evening.

I am expecting a minimum of 15 people, probably not to exceed 25.

Pete's Candy Store sounded cool because you can rent out the back garden and set up an open tab bar situation for a very reasonable fee (around $400 or so) but I would like to be closer to home.

I am nervous about trying for the beer garden because I want everyone to sit together.
