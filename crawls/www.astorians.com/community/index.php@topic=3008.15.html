I'm so glad to have met the members of the group.  It was a nice time.  I felt bad because I was off from work on Friday and foolishly went to Hellgate on Thursday night as well.  And, unfortunately, did a little too much celebrating.  Was not my normal self on Friday and went home relatively early.  Not usually my style.  But we had a great meal and a very nice evening.  We're pretty much there every Friday and stay much later.  I'm glad the folks who went liked the place.  The crowd is very friendly and the place is picking up by leaps and bounds.  I can picture having to wait for a table pretty soon.  From almost no one there 6 weeks ago to what it's become - pretty amazing.  The owner, Andrew, is even surprised how its caught on.  And it was pretty funny about meeting people from the group.  One man who is a regular, was a little confused.  He knows my wife and I from the bar.  When I walked in, he came over very sheepishly to tell me that "girls" were asking for me and said they met me online.   :-o  I tried to explain but he isn't someone who goes online so it just sounded bad to him.  We sat with the two ladies we met at the outset and then he came to the table during dinner to tell me that more girls from the website were looking for me in the front of the bar.  I'm still not sure what he really believes about it all but it'll be fine.  I hope to see you all there again.  And that more will give it a try.
