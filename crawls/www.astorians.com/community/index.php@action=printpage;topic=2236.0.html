For the soccer ignorants among us - how many years is it between World Cups?  I've been told 8 and 4, which one is it?  And are there other championships inbetween the World Cup?  When are those? 

4 years between world cups.....Euro cup every 4 years..next one in '08 co-hosted by Austria/Switzerland...copa America, copa Africa and who knows what else also takes place on alternative 4 or 2 year rotations but Eurocentric fans like myself do not follow some of the later due to notoriously unreliable levels of competition (sometimes top teams like Brazil/Argentina send substitute teams or do not show at all)....

in any case, to continue with the theme of "folow the money trail" the best players are signed by wealthy private clubs in Europe (Real Madrid, Man United etc.). For these clubs the premier tournament (on par with the world cup) is the transnational Champions League (only Euro teams) and it takes place from September to May annually.

so 2 new tournaments to follow :wink:: champions league and Euro cup
