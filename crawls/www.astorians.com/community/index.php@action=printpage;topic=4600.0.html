Title: More Chocolate for Sale
Post by: JustDesserts on January 05, 2007, 11:02:14 AM
Since I've been getting positive reviews about my truffles, I've definitely decided to make more candy to sell for Valentine's Day.  I realize Valentine's Day is quite possibly the world's most ludicrous holiday, however as a budding pastry cook I feel it would be foolish not to capitalize.   :wink: Again, many thanks to my December truffle customers for their orders!

I'm offering 6 flavors this time (11 got too crazy).  These are the best of the lot, based on your feedback and my own personal opinion.  (As the saying goes, the chef is always right):

Hazelnut ganache, rolled in dark chocolate and garnished with feuilletine
Caramel ganache, rolled in dark chocolate and garnished with chopped pecans
Raspberry-Chambord ganache, rolled in dark chocolate and garnished with almond dust
Chili-infused ganache, rolled in dark chocolate and garnished with spicy cocoa
NEW:
Orange ganache, rolled in dark chocolate and garnished with white chocolate drizzles
Espresso-infused ganache, with a hint of Kahlua, rolled in dark chocolate and garnished with chocolate-covered espresso bean

Choose any combination of 6 truffles.  Packaging will be the same as before, but the prices have changed as follows:
Clear bag with ribbon, 6 truffles  $10
White candy box with ribbon, 6 truffles  $12

Also, thanks to kind words from a very sweet friend of mine, I will be selling scones in the following flavors for $2 each:
Mini chocolate chip
Orange-cranberry (available vegan)
Herb
Parmesan - Black Pepper
Lemon-currant (available vegan)
I'm also trying to develop a low-fat, whole wheat variety with mixed fruit, which would also be vegan.  I'll post again if that one works out. 

They will be individually wrapped.  3 is the minimum order; mix and match. Perfect for breakfast, or for a snack with tea.   :-)

Feel free to order now; product will not be available until Saturday, January 13th.  Last orders will be taken Sunday, February 11. I'm offering delivery again in Astoria.  If you're interested in ordering, or have questions, PM or email me at michelle31980@gmail.com.

Thanks in advance!!
