Take over my membership to The Rock
« on: February 19, 2008, 06:47:03 PM »
So, I'm moving to Brooklyn April 1. At that date, I'll still have 8.5 months left on my annual membership to The Rock fitness center. It'll cost me $250 to cancel it outright, so I'm hoping to find someone I can transfer it to.

What's in it for you:

• No registration fee. I already paid that.
• No transfer fee. (They normally charge people assuming someone else's contract a $75 transfer fee. But since you're doing me a favor, I'll reimburse you on the spot.)
• A shorter commitment. The portion of the contract you will assume will be around 8 months, not a year.

About The Rock:
Well, you can get info straight from the source at the website (http://www.therockhealthandfitness.com/main.html), but I can tell you I really like it. I'm quitting because I'm moving. The Rock is pricier than some fitness centers ($83 per month), but I do think you're getting what you pay for. The gym is huge, and it's open 24 hours. It's located right at the Ditmars stop, so if you commute from that station, you can't help but walk past it. I go right after work, and I've never had to wait more than a minute or two for any piece of equipment, and usually, there's no wait at all, even at peak hours. They have child care and lots of free classes, and the membership is pretty diverse. Honestly, my only gripe might be the clubby music they play almost exclusively.

Please let me know if you have any questions. Thanks very much.

Jason
