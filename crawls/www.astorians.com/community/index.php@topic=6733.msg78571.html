I tried this place the other night and I really liked it.  I had a banana smoothie (not exactly taking advantage of all the great old-fashioned things they make, but I was in the mood).  The smoothie was good and the staff was nice.  It is a bit narrow near the counter, so don't forget to look up and around at the high ceilings and notice all the little touches the owners added to provide the all-encompassing old-school experience.  I found the old coca-cola icebox to be a great touch.  For all the classic touches, there's certainly a modern streak to the decor as well.  Next up, jam/maple syrup and a slice of pie...
