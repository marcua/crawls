June 24 - Queens Green Drinks!
« on: June 11, 2008, 08:08:49 AM »
Hey Astorians,

I thought you might be interested since this is in your neck of the woods.

**Please note change in Date**

For the month of June, Tuesday, June 24th to be exact, we have Socrates Sculpture Park hosting an after hours Queens Green Drinks featuring fresh fruit smoothies AND their internationally renowned outdoor sculpture museum. Their current exhibit, Waste Not, Want Not, features artists using primarily found, recyclable, repurposed objects to form a complete exhibit.   Socrates also organizes and plays host to a variety of programs including the Summer Solistice Festival, yoga & tai chi, an international film series and more!  For more info go to www.socratessculpturepark.org.
 
What's Queens Green Drinks again?  Very simply, it's an informal social gathering/networking event for folks that are environmentally minded.  Come and I'll introduce you to some of the other like minded folks in and around Queens.  If we're lucky (which is likely) we'll cross pollinate make new friends, catch up with old ones, and maybe even sprout an idea or three.
 
Since this is a new little experiment for our borough, please help get the word out and tell your friends about it!  Heck, why don't you bring them?
 
Queens Green Drinks
Tuesday, June 24th
6:00pm - 9:00pm
at
Socrates Sculpture Park
 32-01 Vernon Blvd at Broadway
Long Island City, NY 11106
 
BY SUBWAY
Monday through Friday take the N or W train to the Broadway stop in Queens and walk eight blocks along Broadway toward the East River. On Saturday and Sunday take the N train to the Broadway stop.

BY BUS
Q103, Q104 to Broadway and Vernon Boulevard Q19A to Broadway and 21st Street
 
Future Queens Green Drinks - Save the Date!
July Queens Green Drinks will be on Thursday, July 31st at the Creek and the Cave hosted by Triple R Events

Just to let you know, we'll have a different host each month selecting the venue of their choice.  We almost have our summer calendar complete, so keep your eyes out for more details. If you are interested in helping to get the word out, if you have suggestions for hosts, or if you'd like to donate your skills in building a website please let me know at helen@queensgreendrinks.org
 
To receive regular updates (about 1-2 times a month) from Queens Green Drinks, please e-mail lets_meet@queensgreendrinks.org.
 
-Helen
