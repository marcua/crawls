Title: Music for Queens presents "All You Can Aardvark" - Free Classes for Next 2 Weeks
Post by: AllisonTartalia on January 16, 2011, 10:59:06 PM
Dear families:

To kick off the new year, Music for Queens has decided to have a special two-week long "All You Can Aardvark" event!  For the next two weeks, please feel free to join us for as many classes as you like....for free!  The only requirement is that you RSVP.  Class sizes are limited to ensure the best possible experience for you and your child.  If classes fill up, we will add more classes to make sure everyone who wants to has an opportunity to join us.  Between our five locations, we have classes six days a week, including Saturdays.

Music for Aardvarks is a hip, rocking music class for babies and toddlers.  Classes incorporate a wide array of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling and instrumental jam sessions. The original music used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.

To sign up for your free All You Can Aardvark classes, please visit:

https://spreadsheets.google.com/viewform?formkey=dFRWZktnZ0ZfTV8yd3MzX0ZGRktCeWc6MA

If you have any questions at all, please feel free to contact me at info@musicforqueens.com.

Thanks so much!

Allison Tartaglia
Director
Music for Queens
