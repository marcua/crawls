ASTORIA MUSIC & ARTS' 5-DAY ROCK ODYSSEY EVENT SERIES

Local organization Astoria Music & Arts is continuing their 2009 event programming with a 5-day live music and performance series in Astoria and Long Island City, Queens: AM&A presents: A ROCK ODYSSEY. The series runs from Thursday, May 28 through Monday, June 1, 2009. Performers & venues are as follows:

May 28th – Club SANM @ 7pm, 35-15 36th Ave., Astoria
AM&A Short Film Festival
Dancing by Chilaires and A.O. Movement Collective
Live Music by: Billy “The Kid” Conahan, Mission: on Mars, Illimanjaro
DJ 2melo

May 29th – Hell Gate Social @ 8pm, 12-21 Astoria Blvd., Astoria
We Are Darien, Hops, Homeboy Sandman, Vector Zero, Drew P, Little Creatures

May 30th – The Quays Pub @ 8pm, 45-02 30th Ave., Astoria
Trainwreck Washington, James Call & The Erotic Photo Hunt, The Twees, Dirty Wings

May 31st – Lucky Mojo’s @ 7pm, 514 51st Ave., Long Island City
Ala Muerte, Digamy, Mr. Wolfe’s Cleaning Service, Jo Williamson, Scott Epstein (performing selections from his rock opera, “Disease”)

June 1st – LIC Bar @ 7:30pm, 45-58 Vernon Blvd., Long Island City
Jason Vitelli, Julie Corbalis, Paul Tabachneck & The Awkward Situation, Bliminal
