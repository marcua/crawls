Trattoria is great for Italian, no doubt about.  Rocco is actually doing a cooking class at Macy's DeGustibus on April 18th if anyone's interested.  He's quite the personality.  I went about a month ago when he opened the restaurant on a Monday, but only for a cooking demo and dinner and it was really fun.  I think it cost something like $50, but that included wines and a full 4 course dinner and he donated all of the $$ to charity (can't remember which one).  Supposedly he does this a few times a year.
