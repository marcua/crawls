Quote from: Alexa on October 20, 2008, 01:42:20 AM
While it might make for interesting people watching, cafe's aren't really where you go to meet a greek girl. Generally as a culture, an individual coming up to the girls to chat them up is seen as odd. The way in is through a friend, or a friend of a friend. Rarely is there another way into the group. We don't go to cafe's to meet others, but to meet with friends, and meet our friends friends. Clubbing is the same... if a bunch of girls are in a group, it is seen as sort of odd for someone not part of the group, or that they haven't been introduced to, to try and join in.

However, i'm not sure if seeking out a girlfriend based on her culture and ethnicity alone is the best way to go. And speaking as a greek girl, it can be somewhat more challenging to infiltrate this group if you aren't greek...not impossible, just challenging.

And not all greek girls are high-maintenance, but even if that were the case, why does everyone hate on the high-maintenance? Let them spend their money on hair nails and bags...what does anyone else give a s___ about that...live and let live.
