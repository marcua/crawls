OUTSTANDING!

We went here tonight.  I've been curious about it since I saw them working on the space this summer.

We had the octopus appetizer - we LOOOOOVE grilled octopus and this dish did not disappoint!  My bf ordered the spinach and veal ravioli and I had the ricotta gnocchi. 

OUTSTANDING!  Seriously, both dishes were so delicious we switched mid-meal!  We couldn't decide which we liked more.  Really really tasty.

The staff was professional and attentive without being over bearing.  This place is not large but it doesn't feel cramped (not yet anyway). 

FYI:  They DO NOT have their liquor license yet so you need to BYO if you want wine with dinner.  It should be coming soon they said.

No matter - we will DEFINITELY be back!

Aside:  Last week MexiQ, this week Bugatti's - isn't Astoria awesome that there's a new great restaurant opening almost every week?
