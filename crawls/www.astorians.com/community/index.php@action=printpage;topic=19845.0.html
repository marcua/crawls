Title: Greater Astoria Historical Society Releases New Book at Holiday Party
Post by: greentiger on November 22, 2010, 09:04:38 AM
On Monday, December 6th at 7:00 pm, the Greater Astoria Historical Society releases its latest book, Then & Now, Long Island City at their annual holiday party, which is free & open to the public.

The book features the neighborhoods of western Queens that were once part of old Long Island City.  Neighborhoods such as Steinway, Old Astoria Village, Astoria Park, Ditmars, Hunters Point, Blissville, Sunnyside, Sunnyside Gardens, Ravenswood, Dutch Kills, Broadway-Astoria, Old Middletown, and The German Settlement are all included in the book. The book will be available for sale at the GAHS for $21.99.
