I have no one to suggest, but I feel your pain. I eventually found a specialist for my own health issues but it was definitely a hassle.

I didn't do this, but I wonder if you could call up one of the other gastros that aren't on your plan and ask them if they have anyone that they could recommend? It seems that if you find a friendly doctor who knows that you can't go to them because of insurance issues, they may be able to point you to some good ones.

One thing my PCP noticed was that my now-endocrinologist was in a larger office of several endocrinologists, which implies that they are set up, both machine-wise and logistically, to take care of my issues. Perhaps you can also research groups of doctors in the same practice and find one that way.

Anyway, good luck to you - hopefully you can find a good one.
