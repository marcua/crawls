I went to Sandwich Bar 2 weeks ago for dinner with my other half.
We each had a pressed sandwich and shared fries.
I had the #23 pulled pork sandwich. It was delicious! I don't often order pulled pork from because its so hard for places to get it right, but it was right on the money. I loved it.
I forget what the other sandwich was that we ordered but i remember that we liked that too.
Bill was $20 for two sandwiches and the fries. I thought that was super reasonable for what we got.
The music that night was all 90's alternative rock type of stuff. I was singing along and so were a couple more patrons.
Our server was attentive and helpful. All in all a nice experience and def going back.
