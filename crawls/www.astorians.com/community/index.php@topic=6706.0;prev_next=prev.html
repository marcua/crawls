Hope seeks new home (from Francis's Friends)
« on: June 11, 2007, 08:43:09 AM »
Hope is a young and pretty ballerina cat who is long, graceful, curious, and pale orange/white.  She is super human friendly and loves to cuddle up on elbows and ask for chin rubs.  At least once a day she gives the human a few nose licks.  Hope loves to look out the window and is content enough to share the house with another cat.

She is looking for a good home with a few screened in windows where she can watch birds and cars (and people and airplanes and trees) and play a bit to stretch out her long legs.

Hope has been fixed and is up to date on her shots.

If you are interested in adding Hope to your home, check out Francis's Friends and fill out an application -- or PM me.  You can come visit Hope by appointment near Ditmars.

Hope on petfinder: http://search.petfinder.com/petnote/displaypet.cgi?petid=8545370
Francis's Friends: http://francisfriends.org/rescue.htm

Please remember to check cat food for the following ingredients. They are the ones now on the FDA's list of halted shipments:
Amino Acids
Corn Gluten
Corn Gluten Meal
Corn By-Products
Mung Bean Gluten
Mung Bean Protein
Protein Hydrosylates
Rice Gluten
Rice Protein
Rice Protein Concentrate
Rice Milled Products
Soy Bean Meal
Soy Bean Powder
Soy Bean Gluten
Soy Bean Protein Isolate
Soy Protein
Soy Protein Powder
Soy Gluten
Wheat Gluten
Wheat Flour Gluten
Wheat Protein

Most supermarket brands have these as ingredients. There are some fine(r)-quality brands that are available either online or in pet food marts.
