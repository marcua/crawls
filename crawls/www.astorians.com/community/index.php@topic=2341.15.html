To be honest, I am quite surprised to the fact that of the so many happy customers at Fatty's, not one has posted a response that describes the beauty that is Fatty's.  I have been going to Fatty's, and avidly recommending it, for over a year now.  Fatty's is not your all too common pretentious restaurant, nor is it a diner or fast food joint.  It stands for something that perhaps most people do not understand in this fast paced city... chilling out.  It's an experience.  A place to go and relax, unwind and have a FANTASTIC bite to eat.  All the food is prepared fresh on the spot.  It takes a bit longer for that, but the idea is to chill out, sit back, have a mojito or a top notch brew, ENJOY your company and have a good time. The staff has always been energetic, friendly and welcoming. The ambiance is minimalistic and cozy in every way, with wonderful exhibitions of local artists on the walls. The owner has been extremely friendly and attentive to me and my guests, and the food is mouthwatering and perfect every time.  If you are looking for food in 7 minutes go to Igloo, the diner, or somewhere else for some frozen patties and frozen fries or boring iceberg lettuce salads. 

If you are looking for a neighborhood place, with great food, a smiling staff , and a feeling like you have stepped out of our crazy daily city life to wind down and loosen up- then escape to Fatty's.

As New Yorkers, we feel like we are owed something. We aren't owed squat.  Truth is, we owe alot to Fatty's for bringing such a gem with a tremendous amount of character into our neighborhood. 

Thanks Fatty's!!
