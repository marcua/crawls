For all the complaining that goes on here about our lack of book stores, Astoria's lack of book stores is not really that weird when looking at just about any other town in any other outer borough. We have one indie book store, Seaburn Books. We also have a little library branch north of Ditmars on 31st Street with weird hours. The thrift shop on Ditmars has a random selection of books. Is it that big deal to go to one of the many book stores and libraries in Manhattan when you want a new book? There aren't any music stores either in Astoria (meaning stuff like instruments, sheet music) and yet people don't complain repeatedly that we have no music stores. The people who want a book store so badly should pool some money together and open one. I don't get the complaining and begging for one every time a store goes out of business.

That said, I did a Google search for book stores in Astoria and a place called "Books" came up at 21-17 29th Ave. Anyone know what this is?
