Alt side ticket is $45.


I've done exactly what you're thinking about and never had an issue, other than the ticket. I've been living here for 15+ years and had numerous tickets in that time. I've never been ticketed twice in the same day for the same offense, never heard of that before. In fact, there have been some mornings where I was running late to get to my car and already had a ticket by the time I got to it, or I had an early work issue and just didn't have time to move it, so I've just left it there with the ticket on it for another week. Never had a problem doing so.


There are also a few blocks east of Steinway and south of the Grand Central starting around 44th or 45th street where there are no alternate side rules. You could try parking over there, but it's hard to find spots.
