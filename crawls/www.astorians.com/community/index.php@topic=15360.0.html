Here is a recent article explaining away some of the rumors.

http://www.metro.us/us/article/2009/05/18/06/1001-82/index.xml

I've lived here for 4 years. I have absolutely NO bug problem and I leave my windows open.
The financials are good. you can pick up a copy from the management office. The place is maintained well. They are selling 2brs every month in the 260-325 range. And the prices keep climbing. Even through the recession. Also you can rent the units for $1800 per month.

The main issue is that there are over 600 units here and thus a few people have had bad experiences and post about it. Nobody posts about how good a place is.
Go talk to any complex with hundreds of tenants and you will hear horror stories.

The place is not the greatest in the world, but the greatest 2br in Manhattan will cost you Millions. I love it. The location is amazing. My apt is very well kept and BUG FREE. Quiet, very safe and rooftop access!
