Been listening to two artist that were recently introduced to me.

I'm really into guitarist (Satriani, Vai, Johnson, Van Halen, Vaughan, the usual suspects).  But someone introduced me to Joe Bonamassa.  Apparently, he's been judged the "best" Blues guitarist (a title which is inherrently subjective).  But after hearing some of his songs on Rhapsody, the guy is the real deal.  His song "Sloe Gin" has great tone and soul.  His CD "Live from Nowhere In Particular" is great!  If you're into good guitar and blues, definately check this guy out.

And by accident, I was listening to this song by a band called "Ra".  They have a new CD, so they were playing the first song off the CD called "Broken Hearted Soul".  One word....AWESOME!!  Not as hard as say, Metallica, but still had a nice crunch in thier sound and good melodies.  Very hard sound to describe.  The closest I can come to is "Alice in Chains, only less dark and more rythm".  If nothing else, check out "Broken Hearted Soul".   :mrgreen:
