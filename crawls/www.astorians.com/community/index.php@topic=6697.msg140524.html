On 31st and Ditmars, Woolworths, Genovese and A&P, Joyce  Leslie across  the  street. On the corner 31st and 21 Avenue, CARVEL  with  the big  'Cone'  outside  on a  Saturday night. Matty's  bar  on Ditmars where  that  Greek bar is  now , 'Billy's Bar  where  'Fatty's  is  now. The Akropolis  buildings  before  they were named  that, dark and  foreboding immigrant  apts, I grew up in from 7  to about  14. All gone or  'renovated.'  Truth is  Astoria  as  I knew  it has  vanished, very little  remains. Sure,  some of the Pizzerias, and  small shops  like King Penny, but, it's  mostly gone. I may be going soon, sadly, sell my place. It's  just  becoming too much like  Manhattan, scary  'homeless', garbage everywhere, although many of  the side neighborhoods  still maintain nice  yards and blocks, families  have left, replaced  by  'go-go' younger  folks. I guess  that's  the way it always  is in life, but, just  once  I'd like to go back and ride  my bike  down the dirt  roads  where  Steinway  Mansion used  to be  with  my Buddy  'Benny', who was  killed  in the WTC  attacks.  But, like  aging, you can't  go back.  :-(

Really fun thread.

I first moved to Astoria in the 80s and I lived over Tootsie's Bar on the Northeast corner of Steinway and 23rd Ave, anyone remember it? I think there is a different bar there now.
And where the Nail Salon is, it used to be this little Mom & Pop candy and sandwich shop. I can't remember the name. And on the Southwest corner was a big deli. I can't remember the name of that one either. Now those big old white double doors facing the corner are all boarded up.
The Southeast corner had a Greek gift shop that also rented videos.

On 31st St, past Ditmars, right past the el, on the West side of the street, there was a pet supply shop, I think the owner was Vinnie? He sold mostly pet food, but actually sold live animals sometimes. One time, I cringe to remember, he was selling a Vietnamese Pot Bellied Pig. (Adorable.) He threatened that the little pig would end up being someone's dinner if no one bought him. My partner at the time and I found someone to buy him but that is a story for another time.
