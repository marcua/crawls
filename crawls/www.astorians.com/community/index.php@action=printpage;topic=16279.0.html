Title: Re: Tuxedo rental
Post by: RayG on October 06, 2009, 11:36:47 AM
A Classic Tuxedo never goes out of style. A 1 or 2 button Tux can be had for less than $175, usually including Shirt/Tie. The best part is that most Tux pants have a slider waist band so you can adjust it in case of 'expanding girth'.
Go to 3 or 4 functions and it's saving $300 in rentals of clothes that you don't know where they came from. Be especially wary of rentals with bullet holes that have been repaired :-)

Penney's, Men's Warehouse, Sym's all carry them.
