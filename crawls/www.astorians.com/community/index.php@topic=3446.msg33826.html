Just something to remember about almonds - they're only good for you if they're raw, not roasted. I was really po'd cos I'd just bought two cans of roasted almonds.  :x

Dr. Oz's assertion that "roasting [almonds] 'turns healthy oils rancid' and 'the oils become damaged'" has been contested.  Here's a blurb.  While I always think it's important to look at the source of information (and in this case, it's the Almond Board of California who responded), there's at least one peer-reviewed study (abstract here, full text here), conducted by the Sphera Foundation (and yes, funded by the Almond Board of California), that found no difference in the health benefits of raw vs. roasted almonds.  Wegman's, the favourite supermarket of some on this board, states that USDA research also refutes Dr. Oz's assertion.
