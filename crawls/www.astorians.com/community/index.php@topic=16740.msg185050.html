Steinway Street is full of second floor residential apartments and the noise from holiday music blasting over bullhorn speakers all day and into the night is a nuisance.  When affected residents complain, Steinway Street BID ignores them because they are the same people who are members of Community Board 1.  I believe individuals have rights and they should not be captive to noise in their own apartments so that others can derive a profit.  Additionally, if I were a merchant, I would question whether the holiday music actually increases consumer spending.

As a shopper walking on Steinway Street, I can only say there is nothing as merry than the gentle hymns of Silent Night amplified over rusty Army-style bullhorns and garbage blowing on the street because the merchants can't be bothered to properly tie-down their own garbage at night.
