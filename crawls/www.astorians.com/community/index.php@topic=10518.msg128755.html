Hi all--

Consider this an all points bulletin; I am seriously concerned.  The gyro cart that sets up on the corner of 30th ave/31st street, right outside the seafood store/chase bank, hasn't been there for WEEKS (maybe I just haven't walked by at the right times?).

I love love LOVE the gyros this guy makes.  I know I am bordering on obsessive, but I think they're the best around, not to mention the fact that the guy who makes them is super nice!  I wish i knew his name...the better to wander the streets of astoria shouting it.
