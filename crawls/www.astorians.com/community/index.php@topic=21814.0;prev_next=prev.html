I think so. I've lived on 30/49th street and used that station for 3 1/2 years. Instead of walking between the development and the school (49th street), late at night I will usually walk up 48th street next to the school. Seems a little better lit.

Also late at night, I will usually just take whichever train shows up first and sometimes take the N/Q to 30th street. The walk is a little longer, but there are no safety concerns that way.

I lived in that area for 6 years and in all that time there was only one incident I witnessed.  Some kids were having a fight and there was a bat involved.  That was the only time though where I was afraid.   And that could have happened anywhere.  It's a great area - you have tons of stores just down the street  - Best Buy, Home Depot, Stop n Shop, National Wholesale Liquidators, Sports Authority, Staples, Michaels Crafts, .......
