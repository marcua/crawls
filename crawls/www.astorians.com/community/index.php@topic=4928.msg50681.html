I am very pleased and honored to announce that Freeze Peach will feature my scones, Danish, and cookies this weekend.  This is a trial run for Meesh and myself, so I hope you'll all go to Freeze Peach this weekend for coffee and goodies so that Meesh will carry my pastries all the time.  My items have my JustDesserts logo on them, so they'll be easy to spot. 

I owe many massive thanks to several people (I won't name names so as not to embarrass or anything, but they know who they are) for supporting my craft, and helping me get my name out there.  You guys are awesome!!!!  Thanks also to everyone who have ordered my candy and scones - without your orders, there's no way I would have felt confident enough to expand like this. 

An extra special thanks to Meesh for giving me a shot.  There are no words to describe what a privilege this is for me.

Sorry, didn't mean for this to end up sounding like an Oscar speech.   :roll: 

So please please please go to Freeze Peach and buy lots and lots of stuff!!!!!
