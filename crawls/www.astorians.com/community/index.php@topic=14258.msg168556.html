The best slices of good old fashioned NYC style slice pizza come from Alba's on Ditmars and 37th street and Polito's on Steinway and Broadway.  For square slices, the Sicilian and Marinara slices at Sac's on Broadway and 29th are the best.  Rose and Joe's and Rizzos have a nice homemade feel to them. Portobello's Grandma slice is also very nice. Lots of good pizzeria's in Astoria/LIC.  Absolutely none in Woodside/Sunnyside.
