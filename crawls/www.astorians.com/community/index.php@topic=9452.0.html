Most people who have a "thing" for 80's movies, like myself, are usually people who had their teen years in the 80's or thereabouts. It isn't something that can be explained easily, no matter how bad or cheesy the movie is. "The Legend of Billie Jean" is one of those "rebel" type movies, sort of like "Rebel Without A Cause" of the 80's. Of course I am in no way comparing a great, classic film of the 50's to a crappily made cheesy movie of the 80's.

 Viewing these movies today, it's obvious that the quality isn't on par with movies of today, but it's all about the nostalgia, a yearning for a part of your life that has passed.
