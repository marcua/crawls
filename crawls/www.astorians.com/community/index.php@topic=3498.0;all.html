Buying a mattress *is* one of those items that you get what you pay for.

I bought my very first bed in 1964. I grew up in a twin bed and I was/am a restless sleeper—I toss and turn all night long. So, my very first bed purchase was a king. I thought I paid way too much for it, but it was so comfy in the store.

When I bought a mattress from 1-800 mattress, it came wrapped in plastic which I guess avoids the whole old near the new issue.  I don't know if that's standard or done for a reason or simply comes that way.  In any event, if you are buying a mattress keep in mind that a particular mattress from a manufacturer will be sold under a different name at each retailer.  So you have to compare details vs price, not the mattress name vs. price. 

Sounds crazy, but I made a chart of the store name, mattress maker name, style name, coil count, type (pillow top, plush, standard, etc).  Then I found a $999 mattress from macys that matched exactly with one offered for $750 at 1800mattress and $650 at sleepys.  By giving 1800 mattress the exact name of sleepy's version and the price, they confirmed the price was legitimate and offered to match it.  I went from them because I liked the delivery options better.

Good luck with your search.  For me, it was more effort than buying a car...
