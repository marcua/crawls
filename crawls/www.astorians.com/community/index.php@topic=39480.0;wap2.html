I will not form my own opinions until I try it for myself, BUT... two good friends went last night.  They said there was a crowd and the goofy factor was off the charts.  No one allowed in.  You must leave your cell phone number and they will call you when there is room and you can come in.  The wait was about an hour.  My friends were fed up and really turned off by the over-the-top fake speakeasy play acting.  I always say, if people like this place, more power to them.  For me, if it clears more room at the bar at The Ditty, then I will stick to the more conventional bars and lounges.  (I wonder if they ask all customers to walk out carrying a Square Hardware bag so they aren't raided?)
