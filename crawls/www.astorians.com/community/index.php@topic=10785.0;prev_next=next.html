We've had significant problems with our internet the entire time we lived in Astoria. Even though we've had technician after technician come out to "fix" the problem over the last four years, it was only when the most recent one came out a couple weeks ago when our HD cable channels and on-demand channels started going out as well did we find out that we were never given a dedicated cable line and had instead been sharing with others in the building. The technician ran our own line for us and the service has vastly improved. Every apartment is supposed to have their own line, but apparently TWC doesn't bother to make sure that's actually happening.
