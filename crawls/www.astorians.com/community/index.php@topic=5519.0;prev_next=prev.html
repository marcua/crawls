For anyone that is interested the Astoria/LIC Transportation Alternatives Committee is having another meeting this Thursday.  The info's below for anyone that would like to attend.

Astoria/LIC Committee Monthly Meeting

When: Thursday, March 15th, 6:30-8pm

Where: Phillco's (a pizza place), north side of Queens Borough Plaza, upstairs, where you go from street level to the overpass to the subways (exact address will be posted on http://www.transalt.org/campaigns/astoria-lic/  shortly)

The Plan: We'll be going over the month's agenda, revving up plans for bike month and taking on whatever else is on your minds.

Email Wiley at volunteer@transalt.org or call
646-873-6008 for directions.
