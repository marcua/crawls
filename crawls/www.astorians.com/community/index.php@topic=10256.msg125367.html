SPRING PHOTO CONTEST!!
« on: April 23, 2008, 09:42:09 PM »
It's that time of year again boys and girls - for a new picture on astorians.com!

Here are the rules:
0. Two submissions per person only - show us your best work.
1. Your image should be in digital format.
2. The picture needs to be taken with at LEAST at 3mp camera. Scanned images must be HIGH quality.
3. A small version of your image should be posted to this thread with a brief description of what it is.
4. The contest starts immediately and will run until May 15. At which time the contest will be closed to entries.
5. Astorians will then vote on the submitted images.
6. A winner will be announced and the graphic will go live on the homepage shortly thereafter.

Further details:
Your image will run for a period of weeks or months. It should be a picture that captures some aspect of Astoria, and ideally, should be a spring or summer theme. On the bottom right hand corner we'll provide your name/username/link/email whatever you want so people can link or contact you. Obviously you are giving us permission to use your image on the Astorians homepage by your submission. We reserve editorial right over your photograph's use on the website.
