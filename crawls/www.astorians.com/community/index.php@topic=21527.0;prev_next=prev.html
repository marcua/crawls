International meats on 30th

Astoria fish market on 37th ave by 33rd street
Parisi bakery on broadway
Rosarios on 31st street
Cassinelli pasta on 23rd ave
Grand ave liquors on 31st street for the international selection
Alpine imports on 23rd ave
Kickshaw on broadway

Food tour was a hit!  Due to time constraints we mostly stuck to 30th ave between Trade Fair by N stop and International meats.  Lunch at Il Bambini and coffee at Kickshaw.  They loved it.  All on film too.  One of the people freaked out at all the feta choices.  They also got to see raw almonds and chic peas in pods.  Very cool.
