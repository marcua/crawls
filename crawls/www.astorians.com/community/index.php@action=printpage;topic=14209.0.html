Title: New Music for Aardvarks Location at Queensview Coops / Trial Classes
Post by: AllisonTartalia on March 06, 2009, 09:06:24 AM
Hello everyone!

As many of you know, I’m a Music for Aardvarks teacher in Queens and wanted to let everyone know about some free trial classes we’re holding in March, as well as about our new Astoria location at Queensview Co-op Apartments.  For those of you who are not familiar, Music for Aardvarks and Other Mammals is a class geared toward children aged 6 months through 4 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, rhythm training and instrumental jam sessions.

Written by David Weinstone, songs like Taxi, City Kid and Subway provide children with a positive image of their world and help them relate to their surroundings.

Here are the dates and times of our March Trial classes!  We'll be having trial classes both at the Queensview and at the Astoria School of Fine Arts.  Please phone (212) 769-6812 or email info@musicforqueens.com, to reserve your spot!  More information about our Spring semester classes can be found at http://www.MusicForQueens.com.

Thanks so much!
