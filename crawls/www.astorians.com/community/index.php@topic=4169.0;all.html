A half dozen (building) neighbors and I have organized an Orphans Traditional Thanksgiving Potluck (OTTP) dinner. So far we have shrimp cocktails, a cheese and cracker platter, crudités, marinated tomatoes, onions and mozzarella, matzoh ball soup, a roasted turkey, sausage and cornbread stuffing, turnips, a candied sweet potato casserole, a classic string bean and French fried onion casserole, garlic mashed potatoes, brown sugar and ginger baby carrots, Brussels sprouts, cranberry-orange compote, 1 pumpkin pie, 1 apple pie, 1 chocolate cream pie, 4 dogs, 2 cats, 2 cockatiels, 1 parakeet and, probably, a couple additional people, 2 more dogs and maybe even a partridge in a pear tree.

It's gonna be a lot of fun cooking and preparing in a communal kitchen with a group of nice people. Unfortunately there won't be any children running around this year.
