Title: Moon Photo, just sharing :-)
Post by: ManicMaurice on July 26, 2010, 12:13:11 AM
I've been down in FL for the past few weeks visiting with my nieces. It's been a while since I posted a photo, but I've been teaching my girls all about photography and we've been shooting thousands of photos, so hopefully I'll be sharing more in the near future.

In the meantime I wanted to share this particular pic because I'm really happy with how it turned out.

This photo in particular is for my 7 yr old niece, Kailee. We took a walk the other night and we practiced shooting long exposures. She kept commenting how pretty the halo around the moon was. So we shot a few frames of it and I told her that after her Mom and Dad came and picked her up I would process the images of the moon for her.

Goodnight Moon
(http://farm5.static.flickr.com/4074/4819700943_00d3845be3.jpg)
View Full Size Image @ Flickr (http://www.flickr.com/photos/manicmaurice/4819700943/)

It's a psuedo-hdr image made out of two exposures. One long exposure to capture the dreamy quality of the light halo made by the clouds. And a second image during a slightly less cloudy moment, so I could capture the details of the moon. I then lined up the images in photoshop and blended, dodged, and burned until I was happy with the results.

Hope you guys like it. :-)
