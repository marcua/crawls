When I was booking my last flight -- I found an interesting option with ATA connecting to southwest but flying out of LGA.  I've never seen a southwest connection as a transfer flight with a different carrier for a different segment.  It was a tempting flight option since I was flying into overly small regional airport where southwest is the only carrier that flies in biggish planes.
