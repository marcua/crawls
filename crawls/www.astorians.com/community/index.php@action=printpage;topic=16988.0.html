Title: Re: New Bar on 30th Ave
Post by: amarman on December 29, 2009, 05:02:42 PM
Its like fancy bar food - the menu is small but when I was there they had 4 or 5 specials, mostly entree type dishes. I ordered this Greek salad (special), it was about $7 and was huge, and really good. My bf ordered the burger. He said it was pretty good and he's pretty particular about his hamburger. Someone next to us ordered what looked like a caesar salad, which also looked pretty delish.

I think the menu was typical apps (potato skins, wings, onion rings) and a few entrees (burger, salad, chicken sandwhich) and one or two desserts. Hope that helps
