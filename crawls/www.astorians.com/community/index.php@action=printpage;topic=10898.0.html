Title: A Night With The Whiskey Dregs (a reading) June 26 @ Waltz-Astoria
Post by: whiskeydregs on June 16, 2008, 08:39:32 PM
I have a story to tell you but first I have to let you know that we have another reading coming up. Yeah, yeah, yeah...

Here's what happened last time:

So, Song, from Waltz-Astoria, came up with the brilliant idea of having a pre-party before the reading. The sequence of images I have of going drink for drink with everyone before I had to host this thing sends tremors of horror and delight through my exposed nerves. Before the event, Joe and I had our own private debate of what to read because some of our stuff is...well, it's not for the weak-hearted. We like to get tough with words and puff out our expressions like we were writing bad obituaries for past sexual experiences or even worse - the mistaken identity of certain appendages. In other words - just another day in the bright and sunny world of The Whiskey Dregs. What began for me as a body full of loose nerves dealing combat to my confidence ended as one of the best experiences I've ever had. It was incredibly fun and...some parts of the night are just lost in the wind tunnel of memory.
