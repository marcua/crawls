Upgrades at Google
« on: October 14, 2009, 05:11:33 PM »
I don't know if anyone looks at google street view or even cares about it being you all live there but as an old 32nd street resident from the middle of the last century now living in NJ I have been waiting for the google execs to finally send the four way view truck down my forgotten street so I can finally see where I lived as a kid.

Granted it's only a two block stretch before it ends at either the railroad trestle or Chinky Hill but people did grow up there and might be interested, like me. Thanks google.

Some nice new sharp images of the old sleigh riding hill which is now a parking lot for Staples and the old building that looks like it is next in line for demo that was possibly the button factory that put many neighborhood Czech immigrants to work.

Be warned, my grandfather will come out of the grave and shoot whoever did 23-20 32street, his beloved home, in red siding if google is available in the hereafter.  Hank
