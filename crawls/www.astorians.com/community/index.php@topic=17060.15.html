Hello,
If your ever in the city down by the lower east side stop in economy candy. They have a bunch of great old time candy like the candy cigerettes, wax lips,  ice cubes. plus plenty of choices by the pound,  Prices are very reasonable. Tried to put up a link to their website but don't think i did it right. If you type in their name I'm sure you will get the site. They are just south of houston street east of 2nd ave. Few blocks from Katz's deli. Note to Starwind loved the flying saucers ,when I was a kid we used to call them holy communion because that's what the outside tasted like.
Take care all.
