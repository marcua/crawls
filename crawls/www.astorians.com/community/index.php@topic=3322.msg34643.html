i think i just lucid dreamed right now... kind of excited about it.... hopefully this counts...

first of all, i woke up about 4am, achy, nervous, i hafta go to court this morning and get there on time. i sat up for a bit on the internet, etc. then i went to sleep... i was having an odd dream about being in a case review with my supervisor that went totally wrong. i decided to leave and go back to my 'home'. that journey was odd, my friend and her girlfriend picked me up in a car driving around in crazy traffic with people who were driving trucks with the front that looks like a cartoon character....almost like disney or something, they stop by a starbucks, i walk past it and go to my home... in my dream this was a familiar place, but when i think about it now, i've never been there, even tho it felt like a town i lived in when i was little. 

here's the odd part.... my alarm goes off, i sleepily hit snooze. i fall back asleep, and i'm back in that house. it kinda turns scary as there are random men coming into my bathroom, washing their hands, and trying to flirt with me. i kick them out, and try to lock the door. for some reason i decide to leave the home (maybe i didnt feel safe) and walked down a dirt road to a starbucks. (what? i hate starbucks. anytime i step into a starbucks is to take a piss when i need to), and the starbucks is playing music from one of my favorite bands. but it's not the most recent album. it sounds like their first, but it's a little off and fuzzy. and i'm walking around the creepy starbucks, and the band is actually there and they recognize me! (i have met and hung out with them before early in their career) so they all greet me, they get their coffees, and we go back to my 'house' and that music from the starbucks is still playing in the house.  then they say they are going to jam out in the woods.... i know what they are going to do, they are going to 'smoke'. but i really wanted to hear them play that old music, i walk out the house and walk down the dirt road, talking to my favorite guy in the band....

the alarm goes off again! i remember hitting snooze,

and going back to where i was in the dream. i'm still walking down the dirt road, and the guys in the band are talking about what they were thinking of playing, how great it's going to be, and that it's going to be a great day. the whole time i'm thinking in my head.... i need to go to court! so i continue talking to him and tell him that i really love the band and i'd really like to hang with them. but i need to go. the guy looks at me, takes his hands in mine and says 'well, it was great seeing you, i hope to see you again.' looking kinda sad. i nod my head, smile, and turn away.

i woke myself up. got out of bed, started making breakfast, and writing this. 

some comments-- again! my most vivid dreams i remember when something like this happens, when i wake up, fall asleep again, and it's a short period of time before i have to wake up. 

when i was on the internet, i was listening to the band's webpage, which ONLY has their newer stuff. the music in the dream was amazing. darn!

i seriously felt as though that house was familiar, but, i can't place it. i must have been in that house in a previous dream. 

i've never hung out with that band in that fashion. i've sat with them before and after shows. i sat and hung with them almost a whole day when they were playing a show at our university, i was 'hospitality' and got them whatever they needed. but they seemed pretty cool and didnt want much. so i sat and chatted w/ them the whole day.
