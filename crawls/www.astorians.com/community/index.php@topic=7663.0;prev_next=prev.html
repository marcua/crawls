Job Offer: Administrative Assistant, The Steinway Reformed Church
« on: September 16, 2007, 05:19:43 PM »
The Steinway Reformed Church seeks an ongoing part-time administrative assistant.

Responsibilities:
-organizing paperwork for weekly worship, other congregational activities, and communication with the denomination
-assisting the pastor with correspondence and public relations
-coordinating outside groups' rental or use of the church building
-maintaining the church events calendar
-assisting with planning and publicity for special events
-other tasks as needed by the pastor

Requirements:
-at least two years of administrative experience in a church or other not-for-profit
-literacy in Microsoft Office software, internet and e-mail
-excellent skills in writing and communication
-good time-management and priority-setting ability
-strong capacity for independent, self-motivated work

$12-15/hour, approximately 15-20 hours/week (with some seasonal variation) on a schedule to be agreed upon with the pastor

To apply, please send a cover letter, resume, and two references to
The Steinway Reformed Church c/o S.E. Holliday
19-92 78th Street
Steinway, NY  11370
or e-mail to ellholyday@gmail.com.

Please DO NOT return-post on Astorians.com.

Steinway Reformed Church, located at 41st Street and Ditmars Boulevard in Astoria, is a friendly neighborhood church where all are welcome, in a beautiful Victorian-era building hosting worship, education, community service, arts programs, and other activities.  The small but growing congregation includes everyone from young families to older folk, lifetime church members and new seekers, people born in Astoria and newcomers from around the world.  The church is part of the Reformed Church in America (www.rca.org), a historic Protestant denomination with roots in ecumenism and social justice.
