I'm going to change it up a bit and share 2 GOOD subway stories that happened yesterday.

On the R on the way to work yesterday I started feeling wicked dizzy and warm. Start peeling off the layers and hoping someone would get off at QP-no luck in my vicinity. Starting to feel weak in the knees and hoping I can just make it through the tunnel, guaranteed a seat at Lex. I can't. I feel like my face should be ghost white since I feel so clammy and like there's no blood up there. I cave-I ask a woman if she wouldn't mind if I had her seat since I really wasn't feeling well. No problem no attitute she got her s___ and got up to stand where I was and I proceeded to sit with my head between my knees til 23rd street. I really must have looked bad, because it didn't take much for her to get up.
Karma rewarded her with a seat at Lex in a few minutes and I didn't pass out thus being the sick passenger gumming up the works.

On the R train back home yesterday there was a middle seat that had a small puddle of water on it. No one decided to sit there until Lex when everyone and their mother comes in through the last door of the last car. One woman decides she'll just sit on the edge. The woman next to her takes out some tissue and wipes the seat dry for her. I thought that was super nice.

Thought I would share to temporarily put our faith back into humanity. Until the next idiot comes along...
