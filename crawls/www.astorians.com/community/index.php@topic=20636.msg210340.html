Does anyone know what's going on with Aphrodite (Ditmars & 33rd St)? They used to be Ditmars Gyro Place, but they renovated a couple years ago and changed their name (same family still ran the place though, and it was the same menu). Now they're closed up. It is my favorite take-out/delivery place up here on Ditmars, and every time my parents came to visit, we'd go there. Their chicken souvlaki = magic.

I really really really hope they're just renovating again, but since they just did a couple years ago, I'm doubtful. Someone give me hope!
