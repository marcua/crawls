Title: Amici Amore--fyi
Post by: artgirl on January 08, 2007, 10:16:25 AM
Just a head's up on some upcoming changes at Amici Amore...

I ordered dinner from there last night and when my boyfriend went to pick it up, he ended up chatting with the guys there... seems they're making some changes with the restaruant. They are supposedly going to change the name (to what, I don't know yet). It will stay Italian, but they're making it less formal, and more of a bistro-type place. The menu will be modified, but I don't have any info on what exactly the changes will be. The one thing they did say is that the prices will be lower. And they will be making delivery orders as well (though they've always made a delivery exception for me because I live in the building). Also, they've reclaimed the space that was Butcher Bros steakhouse, and have turned it back into a bar area for the Italian side.

Without knowing any more details than that, I have somewhat mixed feelings... I've always been a big fan of the place. Because of its slightly upscale vibe, it makes for a nice place to bring visitors, or somewhere to go for a "special" dinner. And I hope the changes to the menu aren't too drastic, because I love their food.

On the other hand-- I don't go there as much as I'd like to because it is a bit pricey to eat there often. So this might be a good change for them-- if it's more laid-back and less expensive, it might encourage more of a neighborhood crowd, which would be nice.

Anyway, I'll keep you posted on when they make the switch so you can check it out!
