well stated, dalty.

to each their own, i guess.  it's too bad people always have to lay such severe judgments on other people because their opinions happen to vary.  to me, it sounds as if geo has the "elitist" mentality.

chain restaurants have their place and time, as do the local establishments.  one place doesn't necessarily trump the other, and it varies from region to region, from neighborhood to neighborhood.

and it's absolutely true that you are just as likely to get crap/dirty food in a local place than you are in a chain place.  look at the postings about chinese restaurants (you can't get more local than that, even if they are predominantly take-out joints).  the restaurant business is the same wherever you go.

also, if a mom and pop restaurant does a consistently great and satisfying meal, they will eventually get a following and thrive.  it's the mom and pop places that are doing average (or bad) food that will disappear, and then good riddance.  make room for someone who can possibly do it better.  and don't you think that chain restaurants fail in certain locations too.  if they're not living up to the local standard (or someone else does it better) then they're not going to make it either.

what about those local places that start off great, then expand, and for whatever reason aren't as good anymore (ie: service and/or food quality suffers).  isn't that a shame too? 

i find that at least in the chains you get a certain amount of consistency.  you know what you're going to get, wherever you go.  if you're traveling and you're too tired to think of where you want to go for your one solid meal of the day, you want to know that the meal you're going to get is going to be ok.  i've traveled to a lot of places all over the world, and i try to frequent the local establishments as much as i can, but sometimes it's more of a hassle than a pleasure.  in those instances, i have no problem hitting up a chain restaurant (hard rock cafe comes to mind) and i know that i'll get EXACTLY what i want for the price that i want.  nothing wrong with that.
