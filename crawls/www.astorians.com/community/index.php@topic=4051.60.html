Sorry for being late on this one, but I've made at least one turducken for the Superbowl for the last 4 years (one year we made two, and I was eating turducken sandwiches for what seemed like an eternity.  This year will be the 5th anniversary of the delicious turducken bowl.  As grossed out as some people claim to be about this culinary masterpiece, I have never heard one complaint since the first year (we have switched where we order from since then).  I can't reccommend this enough. 
The crummy part of the turducken is the cost, unless you have the ability to carve the bones out of three different birds, make three different kinds of stuffing and then make it.  Personally, I order from a place called "Cajun Specialty Meats" in Louisiana.  I just follow their instructions almost to a T and it's so good.  Man, I can't wait for the Superbowl again.  Another keg and 4 dead birds, delicious.
