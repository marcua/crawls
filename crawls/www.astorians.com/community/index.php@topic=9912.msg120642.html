My fiance and I checked out the 'soft' opening last night and I have to say I was very impressed!  The owners were incredibly sweet and willing to chat and discuss their wine list with us, which was good because they didn't have a Cabernet by the glass, which is practically the only wine my fiance drinks! :-)  He wound up getting a Syrah that was absolutely delicious based on a recommendation, and loved it so much he got a second glass.  Their Montepulciano by the glass is really nice, light and refreshing.  The chef is also really nice, and walked around talking to people while they tasted his food.  They were serving food off of the small shares portion of the menu last night, and everything was delicious--if I had to pick a favorite, I think it'd be the 'gasm dip, which (and this may sound perverted) is aptly named!  I only wish they'd have served more crackers with it.  The shrimp were also absolutely delicious...they have tarragon in them which is one of my all-time favorite herbs, and the sauce reminded me of scampi.  All in all, despite the name hate going on, I think this place is a great addition to the neighborhood and I hope they stick around.  Considering the fact that it's stumbling distance from my apartment I know I'll be back!
