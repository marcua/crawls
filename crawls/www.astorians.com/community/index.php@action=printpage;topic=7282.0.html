Most interesting tidbit from this site so far (quoting the FAQ):

"Who Is Leading the Effort for Statehood?

NYC statehood is a grassroots effort. No one has taken a clear leadership position in this effort, although some prominent New Yorkers have stepped forward to lend their support. Norman Mailer started the ball rolling with his 1967 mayoral campaign. U.S. Representative Bella Abzug picked up the baton in her 1976 run for the Senate. Currently, city councilman Peter J. Vallone, Jr. is the most prominent advocate."
