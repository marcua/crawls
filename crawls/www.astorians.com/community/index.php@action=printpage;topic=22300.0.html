FREE PARKING!!
Will be holding a Wine Tasting from 5-8pm on Friday 02/17/2012
We will be tasting Wines from the CLOS LA CHANCE WINERY
Clos LaChance produces wines from their 150-acre Estate vineyard in San Martin, CA and the nearby Santa Cruz Mountains. These wines showcase the diversity of varietals that have popularized the Northern Central Coast region and excited winemaker Stephen Tebb. “We have taken considerable time and effort to study our vineyard and plant varietals where they grow best. With so many micro-climates, each wine we produce truly embodies the terroir we’re blessed to work with day after day,” says Tebb. “And, they reflect true varietal characteristics that wine lovers have come to enjoy.”
