Well.... I was limited to one small town (Gruene) for food, except for the drive down I-35 from the airport in Austin. And I used that trip to get my blessed cherry limeaid from Sonic!

But I did have some really good chow. Most notably, my dinner at the Gristmill, where I took a page from Flooz's book and took a picture of my delicious steak! It also came with a salad, but who wants to look at that when there's bacon-wrapped STEAK!



I finished all of my dinner and then proceeded to eat the sausage and green-- er, that's Gruene beans of the friend sitting next to me. Also, the other highlight was hitting an old-time soda shop for some Blue Bell Homemade Vanilla on a cone - the BEST vanilla ice cream anywhere!



I also got some tasty Tex-Mex, plus plenty of margaritas and sangria and a wine tasting of some local Texas wines. The whole thing made me rather homesick, but I'll be down in Houston in a month (for a wedding), so I have that to look forward to. My plans are to eat my way through that town as well...
