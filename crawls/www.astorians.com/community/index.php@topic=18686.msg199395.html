Offering another option...

Immaculate Conception school has openings for the fall.  I found that paying the tuition ($440/month) and afterschool ($75/week) actually ended up being cheaper than sending my daughter to the public school and having to pay for a nanny after school.

My bill for IMMAC is only ~$750 per month, versus a sitter for 4 hours a day,5 days a week at $15/hour = $1200 per month.  It sound like you're looking at $1000 a month for afterschool at Mushroom, which is more expensive than IMMAC too.

Immaculate Conception has other things going for it too, like great test scores, warm nurturing environment, small class sizes, etc., but the fact that it's inexpensive and offers care from 8:00 AM to 6:00 pm is a HUGE selling point.
