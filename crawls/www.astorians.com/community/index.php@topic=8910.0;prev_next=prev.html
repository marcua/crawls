Last Few Days to Sign up for 3-on-3 Co-Ed Basketball... And More Dodgeball!
« on: January 07, 2008, 03:16:55 PM »
Hey Astorians,

ZogSports here again to tell you that there are only a few more days to sign up to meet fellow Astorians, get involved in some friendly competition, be a team player, and hang out with your neighbors. 

Our charity-focused 3-on-3 Co-ed Basketball league is starting soon and we hope to have a few more Astorians members join us. 

Here are the details:

8 regular season matches and 1 scrimmage match
One head referee/game
A heart-wrenching single elimination playoff tournament (50% of teams qualify)
One t-shirt per person on the roster
Gym fees
"Prize Money" from our sponsors and a portion of your league fees donated to the winning team's charity of choice
League administration
Organized Happy Hours at local bars after the games

The league starts January 19 and will run on Saturday afternoons with games beginning between 12:00-3:00pm.

Please email info@zogsports.org with any questions, or visit www.zogsports.org to register.

AND... great news - we're coming back in the spring with a NEW Dodgeball league - start forming teams or getting psyched to sign up as an individual now!
