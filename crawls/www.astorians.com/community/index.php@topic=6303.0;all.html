Dr. Spyridon Katehis is one of three dentists working out of 30-89 36th Street, between 31st & 30th Avenue. (718-278-4419) I went as a walk-in 3 years ago when a filling broke off - needed immediate work. I didn't have a dentist out here. Since then I have had a cap made, had two fillings and get cleanings there. He is excellent and a young dentist (attended NYU Dental School), so he knows the new techniques.

I am a little of a fanatic (having worn braces for 4 years when growing up) - so I monitor my teeth. When I had the cap done, which is expensive (but less so than in the city), he let me pay in a couple of installments which was helpful. I recommend him, and you don't have to wait 10 weeks for an appointment.
