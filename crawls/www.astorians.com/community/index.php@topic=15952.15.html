It is a terrific place - I was there on Thursday night with a few friends and we all loved it.  Great drinks, delicious food, and wonderful ambiance.  The staff and owners are doing a great job making this place deliver on all counts, and their devotion to creating a warm, welcoming space shines through. 

We loved the drinks we had - Kelso wheat beer, the spicy cherry cocktail, and the Malbec (really anticipating this cask ale coming, too), and tried a bunch of food items:  sausage rolls (yum), deep fried pickles (SUPER YUM), mac and cheese, burgers and fries.  We devoured it all.  And the double smoked bacon was amazing.

The space is beautiful!  I look forward to hanging out in the back garden, too, once it's open.

Looking forward to my next visit!

My fiance and I went last night.  I think we grabbed the last 2 seats available.  The place was packed and getting more so the longer we were there.

The food was pretty good.  We both had the grilled cheese, but I had the caramelized onions with mine.  Although the bread was toasted nicely, the crusts were awfully dense and chewy.  I'm guessing the bread was getting bit stale.   The biggest disappointment was that because the waiting staff was overwhelmed, we had to repeatedly ask for things.  And we didn't get our side of fries until well after finishing our grilled cheese.  And unfortunately, our fries were a big soggy. 

Oh well.  I'll definitely try them again but I may go on a week day night and a little earlier in the evening.

I went with a group of friends last night too.  It was fun!  The place is already really popular though!  Very crowded.  Must have been all the write-ups about it and the buzz.  It's a really nice place, reminds me of Brooklyn but more so Dumbo than Williamsburg.  I had the mac and cheese with bacon which was to die for.  The bartenders, staff, and the bouncer were all really friendly and sweet.  The drinks are real inventive and strange, but in a good way.   If they fix up the back area a bit, it would make a nice garden area.  Right now, it feels a tad claustrophobic back there, like an air duct, it's small.    My only other suggestions would be to turn on the air or the fans because it was way too hot and uncomfortable, and the music was super loud.  We all had to shout for two hours to hear each other and left with sore throats.  Hopefully, they'll tone it down a bit for next time.  Great addition to the neighborhood!
