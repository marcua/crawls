So glad to have found this post! We had walked past this place at one point and wondered over it, but reviews here and on Joey in Astoria sealed the deal. We tried it tonight and got the prosciutto de parma, fresh mozarella, and sundried tomato pesto tapas, the speck and grana crostini, the manchego and membrillo crostini, and the prosciutto with gorgonzola dolce and fig spread panini. It was all absolutely amazing, only cost about $25, and we couldn't even eat it all! Oh, and we got it delivery, which was refreshingly quick. Oh, and they accept credit cards for delivery orders, which seems to be somewhat of a rarity around here. Anyone know why that is?
