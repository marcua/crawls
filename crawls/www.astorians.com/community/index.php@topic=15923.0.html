Serious Fun Free Trial classes this  Monday-Wednesday, August 24-26, for ages 4 and up!

Do you need after school care for your child?

Come and try our free trial classes this Monday thru Wednesday, August 24, 25, and 26.  Parents can meet the teachers and staff, as well as tour the facility.

Serious Fun

41-01 Ditmars Blvd
Astoria, NY 11105
