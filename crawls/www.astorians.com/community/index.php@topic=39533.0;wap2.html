2016 Queensboro Dance Festival

(1/1)

Karesia:
Check out a week of dance featuring only Queens-based artists!  This year's festival will showcase 28 professional Queens-based choreographers and dance companies from all corners of the borough, spanning Briarwood to Long Island City, Floral Park to Howard Beach.  Enjoy a different line-up each night, featuring everything from Indian, tap, Brazil Zouk, hip-hop, Chinese, contemporary, Mayan, ballet, and Filipino.

Shows:  Tues-Sun, Oct 18-23, 2016
Tues-Sat 7:30pm
Sun 7:00pm

The Secret Theatre:  44-02 23rd St. at 44th Ave, Long Island City

Tickets:  $15 advance online, $18 door, $35 festival 3-day pass, $5 HS Students (door only)

FULL FESTIVAL PERFORMANCE SCHEDULE + INFO:  http://www.queensborodancefestival.com
