Hey All,

My roommate is moving out and I'm in need of a new one until at least February.  I'm located on Steinway St., near 25th Ave., about a ten minute walk to the Astoria Blvd. subway stop on the N, W. 

The bedroom is a decent size, big enough to fit a double bed, dresser, and desk, and there is access to the fire escape.  You get your own closet, though not in the bedroom, and plenty of overhead storage all to yourself.  The building is old, so it has some quirks, but the neighborhood is fantastic, with a plethora of Egyptian restaurants, cafes, hookah bars, and shops, dotted with two lovely Irish bars, a supermarket 1 block away, and a laundromat literally across the street from me.  The apartment is already fully furnished, so a roommate with fewer possessions would be ideal. 

I would prefer a non-smoker, as I'm somewhat allergic to cigarette smoke, although like I said, you have access to the fire escape, plus roof access and I'm only on the 2nd floor so getting outside to smoke is not an issue.  I would prefer no pets although I'm open to a cat.  I love animals, and used to own a cat myself, the issue is really just the apartment is fully carpeted and makes for issues with cleaning up after animals.  I'm also rather partial to fish tanks, should you happen to have one in your possession...

I'm pretty laid back.  I'm not looking to be best friends with my roommate, although I do enjoy having someone to talk to from time to time.  Just someone who can go out with me for a drink once in a blue moon.  I come home to decompress after work, and tend to just curl up with my computer or a book.  I'm definitely not much of a partier, so if you're up at all hours of the night and plan to blast music or have friends over until late into the night every night, this isn't really the place for you.  I'm not particularly anal about cleaning, but I do hope to find someone who will help keep the common areas tidy.

I'm definitely a fan of this part of Astoria.  My lease is up in February so I'm considering looking for a new place, but until then, my home is your home!

Rent is $700/month, plus $10 for cable internet and an average of about $40-$50 for electricity/gas.  Heat and hot water is, of course, free.

Please shoot me a message if you're interested and I'll send you my e-mail.  I'm hoping to get someone in as soon as I can, my roommate has given me til the 17th or 24th, she's not sure which, to move out, so I need someone right after that! Thanks!
