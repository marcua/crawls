This weekend I went straight to Food Bazaar's Fb site, hijacked another thread and asked when. The reply has changed from 'early 2013' in other accounts to 'this year.' This evening a walk up to the site reveals the Dunkin Donuts now closed for renovations (worst spelling attempt on hand-written sign I've ever seen). The Food Bazaar sign has not gone on the building as the article stated it would.  Internal lighting still shows the old internal storefront arrangement and old Pathmark shelving in place, and you get the impression nothings been done. I saw a stack of about 4 used window air conditioners possibly being liberated from the site as the noise from glass bottles being removed from the metal redemption shed filled the air. I suspect the site is totally vacant except for a few parked cars (what's the Singas truck still doing there?). A mess.

FINALLY! almost 15 full months since the Pathmark's closure, Food Bazaar of Astoria is almost open. Their website claims an opening on December 12. The exterior has been painted, and the neon sign is finally up. They've advertised for help, and the one big negative this chain is notoriously non-union workforce.
