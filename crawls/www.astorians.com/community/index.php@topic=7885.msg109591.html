Hi Preggers ladies,
I'm currently on the other side of pregnancy. My son is 15+ months and it's so much fun!
Here are a couple of resources that I wish I'd had when I was preggo...

The Yoga Room in Astoria has a PreNatal yoga class. Having done a fair amount of yoga in my time, I opted to do regular yoga to keep fit while pregnant, but found that I had missed out on joining a tight-knit group of women in the area who are a great support system for eachother.
Here's the site: http://www.the-yoga-room.com/yogaroom_astoria/yoga_classes/yoga.html#gentle

I don't know if any of you are planning to breastfeed, but it really worked well for my son and for me. Part of the reason it was successful for us was that we got off to a great start. Our local chapter of La Leche League (a breastfeeding support system that is worldwide AND local!) has a couple of meetings in the area. Check it out while you're still pregnant so that you'll already have the resources you need when you need them:
http://www.lllusa.org/NYE/Queens/queens.HTM
You can also get online and get support from the comfort of your own home computer.

Another one that was so helpful was the meetup group here, in astoria for moms. It's called the "stay at home moms" meetup, but there are lots of part-and full-time working moms in it. There are playgroups and "moms night out" events. No formal mandatory meeting, just fun and social. I remember wandering around Astoria the first few months of my son's life literally going up and down streets looking for other moms of young babies. How frustrating it was! But getting hooked up with this group will give you lots of moms to get chatty with. Some of them are my best friends now! http://sahm.meetup.com/817/

And, incidentally, I have chosen to focus my health counseling practice on moms-to-be and new moms, so check me out if you have questions on which foods are best for you and your baby right now and ways to make your pregnancy a little more comfortable. http://www.angelsonghealth.com/welcome.html

Congratulations to all and good luck!
