 Welcome to Bistro Les Minots, a cozy and casual French Bistro. The joint owners, respectively from Paris and Marseille, will transport you to Paris while adding the warmth of the French Riviera. Our menu takes the better of these two beautiful regions of France so as to satisfy all tastes and desires. The cute front dining room coupled with the warm and romantic back room, provides the perfect atmosphere for any occasion, personal or corporate. Traditional regional casseroles will be served as everyday specials. Special events, from French movie night to wine tasting will be organized on a weekly basis. if that's not enough, you'll be able to enjoy all of our dishes at home any day of the week.
