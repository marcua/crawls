Please don't settle for local. Get as many recommendations as you can, and then schedule fee-free appointments to interview each of those in your health insurance plan. You need to know who it is that will be responsible for the health of your baby and not just that they are convenient to your home.

Also bear in mind that our local hospital Mt. Sinai of Queens (a.k.a. Astoria's Pre-Funeral Home) does not have any pediatricians affiliated with them. In case of an emergency you will need to go to the nearest hospital where your doctor as privileges!
