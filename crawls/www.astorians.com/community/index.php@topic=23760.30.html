This isn't surprising to me at all.  I haven't really said much since my visit there but I can say that I was far from impressed.  On one hand, I think this restaurant has been a shot in the arm for the Ditmars dining scene.  So that's the positive side of it.  But there wasn't much else I liked about this place.  From the crazy uncomfortable bar stools, to the poor service, to the even worse food.  The place definitely looks nice.  But look closer and you'll notice it's a bunch of very cheap materials thrown together.  From the awful furniture to the accoustic ceiling.  We had some raw bar items that were good but not great.  I think the raw shellfish at Psari, S Prime and Off the Hook is much, much better.  The hot Greek items were downright awful - and were everything but hot by the time they reached us.  And I decided to visit the men's room at one point - (actually, there are individual restrooms like at Studio Square) and I unfortunately chose the one where someone had thrown up all over the place - and not very recently.  I quickly chose another and that one had no paper towels and the dispenser was broken.  What I see is a "celebrity" name behind a substandard restaurant.  And it will probably be packed all the time by the people impressed with brand names, but who know little about really good food.  And that might explain the bar fights and whatever else goes on there.  Once someplace is only seen as the fancy place in town, or the ultimate date spot, things can go downhill.
