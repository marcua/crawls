One of my favs is Agnanti - the Greek restaurant on Ditmars down by the park.  They have a great menu with lots of options that you may not see at other places.  Very well priced and everything is delicious.  The only downside is that you may have a to wait for a bit on a Sat night, but I think that shows that they have a lot of true fans, since it's an out of the way location.

Agnanti is the best greek in the nabe, hands down.  We live up the block and have gone so many times I've lost count.  However, one piece of advice...don't go on a Saturday night, particularly in the warm weather.  The lines are insane, and the place is slammed.

Go on a mid-week night when you can actually enjoy the place and the waitstaff.  Really great restaurant.

I really surprised you've felt they were rude there. The first time I went there, there was only one other table filled, so it made sense that they were kind and attentive to us. However, the next time I went, they really went out of their way to be great to us. My boyfriend and I were waiting to meet some friends and they only had the six-seater table in the back open. Since there were going to be only 4 of us, we told them we were willing to wait so they could keep it open for larger parties. However, after a little bit, they had us sit down there anyway rather than wait for better business. Also, the four of us were having so much trouble deciding on an appetizer, that the chef actually offered to make us a special appetizer, which was delicious (even though we still ended up ordering 3!). Then at the end of our meal, our waiter brought us shots of sambuca for free. The whole time the chefs were quite friendly as was the waiter - I love that he is always singing, which I know is odd, but I like knowing he's having a good day. Maybe you caught them on a bad day. I haven't tried delivery yet, but we were charged the same price for the pizza eating in as is on their take-out menu.

My places:
Il Bambino
Agnanti
Michael Angelo's II
Fatty's
JJ's


Looking forward to trying Mojave, though! Plus, I've not yet eaten at Local, Bistro 33, 718, Wave Thai, or many of the other restaurants being listed on here. I'm going to have to get on that!
