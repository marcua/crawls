Kelli,
I have been a member at the Rock for years, and i enjoy it.  It's a bit more pricey than other gyms, but there is a really good variety of classes.  The X-Bike class is amazing. The boxing gym and rock climbing wall make it a unique place. 
Some positives:
Great trainers and teachers
Convenient location if you take the N/Q home from work
lots of space

Some negatives:
Small locker rooms
Price is higher than other local gyms
the weekend front desk staff can be flippant at times.

I am not sure of the pricing structure, but you might want to send an e-mail to Dora Arsenis [dora@therockhealthandfitness.com].  I get weekly Rock updates from her, so she'll be the one to ask.

Good luck
