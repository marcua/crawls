Like I've said elsewhere, the problem is that Clemens was put into a position where if he admits any use, he basically has to admit that his trainer jabbed him in the butt with the stuff all the time. He can't get off like Pettite and say "I did it once, it was a mistake, I'll never do it again." However, unlike some other athletes, Clemens hasn't bulked up considerably--he has gained weight, but that could just as easily be attributed to aging, especially considering where the weight sits--and the man claiming his involvement is facing criminal charges. There certainly is reason to want to point the finger at a big figure.

That being said, I find it hard to believe that Clemens never did them. I almost think that it's the kind of thing that almost every player has probably tried at some point. The real question is whether or not he used them as extensively as the claims say. I don't know. I hope that he didn't. I like Clemens and would like to see him come out on top in this, but I don't think I would be surprised either way.
