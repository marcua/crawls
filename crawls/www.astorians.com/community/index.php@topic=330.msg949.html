I'm writing because of an urgent threat to many of the things we care about. Republican leaders are about to change Senate rules so that George Bush can stack the Supreme Court with right-wing judges. The impact would be so huge that even Republicans are calling this plan the "nuclear option." But if we flood the Senate with phone calls, we can stop them. Will you call our Senators?

Please take a moment to call Senators Schumer and Clinton. You don't need to be an expert to call. Just tell whoever answers (or say this in a message): "Hello. I live in ______, and I would like to urge Senator _________ to oppose the 'nuclear option' and support requiring judicial nominees to have broad support in the Senate. Thank you very much." It's easy, quick, and really, really important.

Centuries old Senate rules require federal judges to win broad support--this leads to fair, non-partisan courts. But Republicans are preparing to use a parliamentary trick called the "nuclear option" to force through every one of Bush's right-wing nominees.

The nuclear option would eliminate the right to "filibuster" judges. This is basically a rule that has always allowed both parties to stop the most extreme nominees. If Republicans succeed, George Bush will be able to nominate up to four Supreme Court justices who will roll back years of progress on women's rights, labor rights, civil liberties, the environment, etc. It's scary enough that I'm asking people I know to help stop it. We're trying to get tons of calls in today. Please call right now.

Thank you very much for helping me save the Supreme Court and protect democracy!
