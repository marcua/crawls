I hit Ginza last week with some friends. We did the all you can eat deal.

$18.95 for unlimited sushi, soup salad, and appetizers. it comes with a free drink too.

PLUS, they don't have their liquor license yet, so if you ask for sake, its free.

The sushi was good. Fresh fish, big variety on the All You Can Eat menu. Definitely worth the money.

And after 3 or 4 bottles of free sake, the meal is awesome by default.

If you want great sushi, i suggest JJ's, but if you want to practice sloth and gluttony, go to Ginza.

OK, I tried this place with a few other Astorians and thought it was pretty excellent for all you can eat sushi.  We paid only $16.95 for all you can eat soup, salad, appetizers, and sushi.  We ate dumplings and sushi, with tea at the end.  There was no sake to be had, but that was ok.  The sushi was good and fresh and substantial.  There was a good amount of variety in the sushi choices, too.  We stuffed ourselves and were VERY happy with it all.  Great taste!  Great value!  Check it out. :mrgreen:
