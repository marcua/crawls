Right there with you girl - had 2 weddings last year, did the bloomies thing and came up empty thanks to being a bit more endowed than average!   :? 

I've had dresses made at Mimi's Closet on 31st just beyond the ditmars stop, across the street from Thai Elephant and she did a great job fitting me, but that takes some advance planning. 

I've tried KrisTee's on 23rd/Crescent and while they're very nice in there, I don't think I could fit into any of their tops or dresses even when I was 12!  The place on 31st that you mentioned is a possibility, but slim picking again. 

For buying off the rack, I'd recommend a place called LeeLee's Valise in Brooklyn.  It was a hell of a train ride to get there from Astoria, but jeeze was it worth it.  Here's her website - http://leeleesvalise.com/.  Sizes start at 10, but somehow this woman is able to carry lines of clothes that are specifically geared to someone with a larger top.  I typically wear a 10 pants/jeans, but need a 14-16 top and they made it work with no alterations necessary.  Website really doesn't do justice to the lines she actually carries in the store, there's much more to choose from in person. 

Good luck & let me know if you discover any other gem places I could also try!
