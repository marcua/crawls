Homeowner, Apartment Dweller, Room Renter:
 
Need your kitchen cabinets, closets, dressers, home office files, kids room, storage room, garage de-cluttered, consolidated and organized? Are things piling up and it's just too difficult or time consuming or emotionally draining to go through on your own? I'm just the person to do it for you. I can help you go through it all and advise the best way to maximize your space and put it all together for you without having to spend much on those expensive organizing tools. I initially try to do the job with what the homeowner already has, so no hidden fees. Every person needs to be organized according to their own lifestyle and needs. I want to help you achieve order and freedom from all the clutter.  After an initial meeting and run through of what the homeowner needs, we can discuss the best path to clear it all up.
 
This initial meeting will be for a $40.00 fee which includes overview, discussion, ideas, needs, suggestions. I will give you easy and inexpensive ideas on how you can even do it on your own if that is what you would prefer. (I know sometimes it's difficult having a stranger touching your things). I will write it all down for you to reference back to on your own time.
 
If after this you accept and allow me to organize your home for you, it'll be a $10.00 an hour fee until what you need is organized. You can set the time, and even stop me if you feel it's enough. You call the shots since it can be a difficult process for some.
 
If you need the help, if it's finally time to sort through it all, if you just want the suggestions and guidance, please give me a call @ 917-640-1434.
 
I truly believe that an organized home can solve many things and provide more breathing space in your life.
 
Thanks and hope to hear from you soon.
