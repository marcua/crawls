I did recently decrease the storage, however it was my understanding that this wasn't a hard limit. In other words, that by decreasing the storage of PM's that none of your existing PM's would be deleted. Let me know if this is not the case. If some of your PM's were deleted as a result please accept my apology. What was supposed to happen was your quota would say 100%, but wouldn't delete any messages that were already in your box.

Supporting the site does include getting an astorians item from the store. But we're still trying to figure out how to identify people who have purchased from the store. Currently we can't see who has purchased any items. We've put in a request to the company that runs the online store to find out if there's any way we can even have a custom message sent to let people know they should email us and let us know they are a supporter so we can update it manually. Of course the best thing would be for them to shoot us the email of the person so we could update that manually.

In the case of the $0.00 donation the reason this is set up that way is just because it's the only way the software will let you enter any amount you want. Currently if you click on the donate link it allows you to enter any amount you want to donate. The software sees this as $0.00 until the next screen where you enter in any amount you want.

PM's are currently associated with how many posts someone has made in the forum. We really need to do a FAQ, but, like the "rules" nobody has done it yet. I'd love it if anyone wanted to volunteer to put this together.

For anyone who has purchased from the store please let us know so we can add you as a supporter. I've been meaning to post that announcement but just haven't gotten around to it yet.
