Do we need Alternate Side parking 4 days a week?!?
« on: September 17, 2007, 10:17:07 AM »
One thing that always bugged me about Astoria (one of my few complaints) is the Alternate Side Parking Rules.  Where I grew up on the south side of Astoria, Alternate Side took place four days a week.  It was such a pain in the a$$ to constantly have to move your car and circle the neighboring blocks 20 times.  On top of which, the streets were never really "cleaned" anyway. 

Since I've moved to the north side of Astoria, near Ditmars, Alternate Side is done twice a week and I have yet to see any negative effects of this parking schedule (versus changing moving the car twice as much).

So others on the board feel the same way?  IS there really a need to have Alt Side four times, or should we start to complain and have this changed?  Wouldn't it save the City money by not having cleaning crews go out twice as much as they need to?  (Though I'm sure people will answer "They make their money on the tickets"; remember, there are administrative cost to issuing tickets and so there's not as much money as you think there is...which is quotas are implemented). 

I think it might be time to request that Alt Side rules be eased in order to cut City cost and make life a little easier for many Astorians.
