Title: Re: Tequila Sunrise on Northern Blvd

Yeah, tequila sunrise is solid in terms of food, but that construction is doing a number on their curb-appeal.  I asked the waiter on my last visit when it would be completed and he said: "next year."  Well that sucks.  I recommend the enchiladas poblanos.
