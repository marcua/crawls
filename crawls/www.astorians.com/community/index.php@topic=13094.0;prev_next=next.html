I've only been there on a few Saturday nights and I can say that it's pretty OK but nothing thrilling. First of all, if you don't get there either very early or very late you're in for a long wait. It gets very crowded and winding your way to the bathroom can take a good ten minutes. The music I would guess is suitable for the type of place, but it's nothing that I would ever listen to so I can't really comment. The crowd is mostly 20-40, predominantly white, a lot of good looking single women and sleazy looking guys. Never had food, drinks are neither bad nor cheap. Guess it's the closest thing to a Manhattan clubbing expirience in Astoria, but I haven't been to that many places besides Cavo. Hope that helps at least a little.
