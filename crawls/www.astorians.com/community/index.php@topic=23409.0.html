You'll have to hire an attorney, if you haven't already done so already. From there you're going to have to go to court and get a judgement of eviction.  Once you have done so, you'll have to contact the city marshal to evict the tenant out.  Be forewarned, this whole process will take a few months.  This is a list of of NYC Marshals:
http://www.nyc.gov/html/doi/html/marshals/list.shtml
