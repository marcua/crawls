We went to Arnharn Thai on 36th Ave. last night for dinner. I hadn't been in probably more than a year. A brief report. First, they sat us by the front door, and it was extremely cold. This is an ongoing problem with that restaurant. Second, we got a ground chicken salad as a first course, but apparently they threw some chicken bones in the grinder too! We complained, got it sent back, and didn't pay for it. Aside from the disturbing crunchiness and occasional bone shard, though, it was relatively tasty (I thought). Third, we got the shrimp with glass noodles and vegetables. This was good, especially the noodles. A non-spicy dish. And fourth, we got the watercress with black bean sauce which was excellent! Oh, and fifth, they were understaffed, with just one person in the front of the house, and he was new and spoke pretty dodgy English...

Hopefully issues 1 and 5 will be resolved by the passage of time (Spring!), and hopefully issue 2 was a fluke. :)
