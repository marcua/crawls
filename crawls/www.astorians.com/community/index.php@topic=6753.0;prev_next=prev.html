Here's what I did when I sublet from a person I didn't know (and things worked out well so far!)

1. Drafted a short and sweet agreement (I "so and so" at "such and such" address am subletting to "so and so" for "such and such amount" for "such and such" period of time) (send me a PM if you want the wording) which I signed and so did the guy subletting the place.
2. Gave the guy forward-dated checks for the months of the sublet (and listed the check amounts and check numbers on the above contract).
3. Gave the guy my resume, business card, proof of employment. He did same for me.
4. Gave the guy a letter and business card to give to his landlord (who was fine with this arrangement) to allay any fears the landlord may have.
5. The guy gave the landlord forward-dated checks for the months of the sublet.

In the end, both parties have reason to be uncomfortable (my forward-dated checks might not clear, ditto for his checks to the landlord, and I could trash his apartment), but the element of trust comes into play and you stand to lose 1 months rent in the end (at which point you cancel the other checks and things get more interesting). The key is in finding someone trustworthy to sublet to.

Good luck!
