Hi guys,

I've turned this off manually for privacy reasons first, and for my sanity second.

As you might imagine with 1000 registered members the board is generating emails to people all the time. When the "board" sends you a message, it comes "from" an address that I monitor (support@astorians.com). The reason I monitor this address is in case someone needs to contact me, or in case the board itself generates an error it notifies me on that address.

Now, the "problem" is that when the board includes the text of your private message, or the text of a post, about 30% of all users just hit "reply" in the email program and send a response to the post or to the private message. Then I end up getting these private messages in my email account.

I realize that it's people's own fault for not reading the warning in the message that says:

"IMORTANT: DO NOT REPLY TO THIS MESSAGE"

But, the reality is that people don't read it. So - if you'd like for people to be able to email you from the board, then the best bet is to make your email address public (it's only public to registered and active members). If you prefer to utilize the board message system, which gives you some privacy, then you'll currently need to log in to get those messages.

I wouldn't mind the other option, except that I don't really like getting your private messages or replies to posts in the server email account. It wasn't a big deal when we were small, I just deleted them. But now that we've grown it's a bit of a problem.
