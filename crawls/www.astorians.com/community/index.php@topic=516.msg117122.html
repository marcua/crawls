Hey Robynlynn,
There are so many places to hang out in Astoria. Numerous cafes along Broadway and 30th Avenue. One of my faves is Cafe Bar on 36th Street and 34th Avenue (most Astorians call it "No Name" because it doesn't have an actual name:)
It has a bohemian and low-key atmosphere.
While I usually go there with friends, I have gone plenty of times just solo and always struck up conversations with interesting and cool people.

Also Hellgate Social is a great bar on 12th Street and Astoria Blvd. It's open and friendly and doesn't usually have cliques hanging about that would make it awkward to go there and meet new people.

Island is a fairly new club/lounge that opened up on 35th Avenue and 36th Street. It is a nice place but the last time I was there (on a Saturday) it was infested with LI guidos. Also, prices are typical of a Manhattan bar.
