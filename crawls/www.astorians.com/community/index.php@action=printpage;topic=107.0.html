Title: Novel Writing Month Kick-Off
Post by: jayme on October 29, 2004, 08:37:55 AM
Join us @ the Freeze Peach Cafe Monday November 1st at 8pm for the kick off of National Novel Writing Month. We'll sit around in a circle and procrastinate for the better part of an hour in celebration! (Just kidding)

This monday we're rolling up our sleeves and getting our hands dirty as word one goes on paper. We'll let out a cheer and get to writing at 8, drink some tea, share story ideas, and enjoy the company.

 Remember:
 - Nov 1st the writing starts. No cheating!
 - Please make a BACK UP copy of your hard work. (Save, save, save.)
 - Enjoy the process. Stay up late. Work hard. Write a lot.

Sign up before by Oct 31st if you're officially participating (show up at our write-ins even if you're not).

More information about NaNoWriMo is available online at:
 http://www.nanowrimo.org/

The Freeze Peach Cafe is at  22-00 29th St. in Astoria (Ditmars & 29th, near the Ditmars stop on N/W)
http://freezepeach.org

Daily write-ins start Tuesday where you can drop in, sit down, and join me in daily dedication.  I'll be in the cafe 8pm-10pm for the month of November starting Tuesday Nov 2nd.
