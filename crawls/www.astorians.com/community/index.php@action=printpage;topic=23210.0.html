THE AMERICAN RULING CLASS
 (http://www.occupyastorialic.org/wp-content/uploads/2012/07/AmericanRulingClass2.jpg)
The American Ruling Class, the world’s first “dramatic-documentary-musical”, explores our country’s most taboo topic: class, power, and privilege in our nominally democratic republic. This feature-length satire stars Lewis Lapham, the renowned essayist and author, and a heavy-weight ensemble cast that includes former cabinet secretaries, corporate mandarins, media magnates, and at least one journalist working as a waitress. The film follows Lapham and two recent Yale graduates as they make the rounds of Pentagon briefings, the World Economic Forum, philanthropic foundations, law firms, corporations banks, and New York society dinners as they attempt to answer the question, “Who rules America?” (88 minutes)
