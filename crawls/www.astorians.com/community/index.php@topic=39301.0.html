Do you mean Casa Galicia on 31st Ave and 37 Street? It's a private club/cultural society dedicated to the Galicia region in Spain.

From their website:

Welcome to Casa Galicia of new york’s website (Unity gallega of the us, inc). since 1940 our club has been dedicated to the promotion of preservation of Galician culture, folklore and traditions. We invite you to get to know us and see how our little slice of Galicia, Spain is alive in the midst of New York city’s skyscrapers.
