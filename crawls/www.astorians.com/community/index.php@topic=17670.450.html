For the folks that have been saying how they'd like to hear more from the candidates, I have good news! Jeremiah invited his opponents to 9 debates in a letter sent to each campaign today by his Campaign Manager.

Here's the text of the letter:

In exactly three months, the people of our community will decide who will represent them in the State Assembly. It’s the first time in a decade that this district will not have an incumbent in the running, which makes this election a unique chance for the voters to choose a new direction for our government. With government dysfunction leaving our community in the lurch day after day, the stakes are high. Jeremiah Frei-Pearson feels that the candidates owe the people of our community every opportunity to hear from those who seek to represent them.
 
Political debates are a time-honored tradition that will give the voters a forum to hear the candidates discuss and defend their visions for our community. Jeremiah believes that a robust contest of ideas strengthens our democracy and helps ensure that our neighbors will make an informed decision on September 14th. With that in mind, he issues this challenge: Aravella Simotas and John Ciafone meet Jeremiah for nine debates, open to the public, before election day. We suggest each candidate choose a debate venue in the order in which they announced their candidacies. This will ensure a fair process and a diverse set of forums.
 
I’m certain the three campaigns can come to an agreement on matters such as times, dates, and other specifics. In the interests of scheduling, however, I ask that you please confirm your acceptance of this proposal by Wednesday, June 16th and that the first venue be chosen by Friday, June 18th.
 
In three months, our neighbors will choose from three individuals offering three different paths for our community. Let’s give them the chance to make the most informed choice possible.
 
Sincerely,
 
Jesse Laymon
Campaign Manager
Jeremiah Frei-Pearson for Assembly
