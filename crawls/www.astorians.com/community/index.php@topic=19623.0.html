If you want to study "real" martial arts, there is a non-profit school that meets in the basement of the Salvation Army on Broadway and 45th street.  It's called Horangi Taekwondo Dojang.  I believe they meet about 3 times a week.  If you attend all 3 classes per week it used to be $1 per class, if you attend 2 classes it's something like $3 per class.
