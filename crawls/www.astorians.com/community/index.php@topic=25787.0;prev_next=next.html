Is there still no place in Astoria to buy good whole-bean coffee?


I mean fairly-high-quality, not standard supermarket fodder. Here's what I've found:
  • Starf*cks  - I hate the place. Out of the question.
  • Euro Market - Low quality but at least you can buy by the ounce (i.e. not pre-packaged)
  • Green Bay on Broadway - Really awful quality. And the beans labeled as decaf always turn out to be regular! ALWAYS.
  • Various markets, prepackaged beans, various brands - very expensive (like, Whole Foods prices), not vacuum packed, and often the date has expired. Not okay
