Quote
I think many today  do not understand  what the first amendment  means. What does  it mean to you, folks? 

It means, in a nutshell, that the US government cannot interfere with your right to say what you want.

It doesn't stop anyone else from telling you things you don't want to hear, or mandate any regulations on anyone else's dialogue - unless, of course, the dialogue is taking place on your private property (i.e. your home, web site, or business) in which case you do have the right to control it.

Quite frankly, I think most people I meet today understand it pretty well.
