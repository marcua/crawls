I've done the yoga in Socrates, although not as often as I'd like.  I've never had a problem with bugs or the sun.  A lot of times you can position your mat under the shade of a tree.  I'd say there are usually around a dozen people.  It's more or less beginner or advanced beginner level, so it's not particularly challenging, but it's accessible to people of all levels and very relaxing.  I really love it - there's something about doing yoga outside on a beautiful summer morning that I can't really explain.
