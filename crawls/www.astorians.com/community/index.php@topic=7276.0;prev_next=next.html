hello!!!

i can teach you to knit! for free! (i sent you a PM!)

i 'facilitate' the craft group here, ACK (Astoria Crafty Knitty) and we meet every Thursday at 8pm at Freeze Peach Cafe (www.freezepeach.org).  if you wish to learn how to knit or crochet, just bring your own yarn, appropriate knitting needles or hook and i'd be happy to teach you. the lesson is free, but we always ask everyone who attends the group to support the AWESOME venue, by purchasing a tea, coffee, or snack. (Freeze Peach has EXCELLENT teas and coffees!)

i've been to knit new york. i dont particularly frequent the yarn shops in nyc, they are a bit expensive for my budget, but nice to splurge at every once in a while. there's a small, but satisfying, craft / fabric store at ditmars that i like to frequent (between 36 and 37) where you can get some good ol red heart or lion brand or bernat yarn and knitting needles and crochet hooks and all that good stuff. then, for bigger and better projects, i hit up the smileys yarns sales.

http://www.smileysyarns.com/

hope to see you in august!
