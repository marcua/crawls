Title: Re: Car Radio Reception - Terrible since DTV switchover
Post by: neo11 on June 19, 2009, 08:31:53 PM
I haven't noticed anything different about radio reception and there's really no reason why the DTV switchover should interfere in any way with radio.  If anything, because the analog stations have mostly been switched off, there's less RF "noise" which should reduce interference. 

However, this is usually the time of year that two atmospheric phenomenoa, "e-skip" and "tropospheric ducting" come into play, bringing in FM signals from a large distance which normally would not be heard in the area.  Even so, this would really be noticeable on frequencies not occupied by NYC radio stations.

Out of curiosity, which stations are you trying to receive?
