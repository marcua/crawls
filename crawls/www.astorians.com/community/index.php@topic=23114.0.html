When I want a bagel (rarely), I like these ones at Astoria Bagel Shop. I think they are great!

I have to agree.  I also don't indulge in bagels very often.  But I order the same one every time at Astoria Bagel Shop - the Whole Wheat Everything.  I think theirs is about the best.  Chewy and crisp outside and light and airy inside.  Not that dense bread that you want to tear half out.  And I like their spreads.  The Lox Spread is very good. The Olive Cream Cheese is delicious.  And if you really like hot stuff, the Jalapeno Cream Cheese is super hot.  They have an A rating but the place is definitely old school.  Looks almost dingy but it's not dirty.  And I think they have a huge beverage selection.  Astoria Finest had the same kind of bagel - not too heavy and dense.  And very good salads and spreads as well (better white fish than Astoria Bagel Shop).  But it was also a nice place to sit and had much more seating than Astoria Bagel Shop.  And they had some really good coffee choices there.  If someone follows that same model, they should do just as well.  It only closed because something very odd happened with the people who ran that shop.
