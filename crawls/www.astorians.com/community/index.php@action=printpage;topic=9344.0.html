Title: Ovelia for brunch
Post by: round2 on February 03, 2008, 03:20:11 PM
I've been meaning to check out Ovelia for months, and made it there today for brunch.  We thoroughly enjoyed it - the food, service and environment were all pretty great.  We sat in the back section which has extremely comfortable restaurant chairs (relatively uncommon I find), which are different from the ones up front.  I was happy with the music which was inoffensive and SOFT.  Nice to be able to comfortably have a conversation.  Very reasonable prices as well.

I took a look at their other menus and will definitely be back for dinner sometime soon.  Great variety, and some good options for vegetarians (like me) too. 

My hubby loved their housemade sausage, by the way.
