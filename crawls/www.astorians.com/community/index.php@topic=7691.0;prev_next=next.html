I went to eat here for the 1st time this weekend and loved it.  I ordered the grilled pork chops and they were very tasty and juicy. The only thing I didn't care for too much was that they served it with potato chips on the plate.  I would rather have had potatoes or a baked potato.  The next time I go I'm going to ask about that.  I loved the atmosphere.  They had some nice music playing in the background.  The service was also great and at the end of our meal the owner/executive chef came over to the table to ask how everything was.  I'm definitely going back and am going to try something else from the menu.  I thought the prices were reasonable.  My dish cost $14 and I got 2 porkchops.
