Yes, as everyone else said, the Sunday papers.

Also, Newsday does an "Early Sunday" edition that comes out on Saturday morning.
It's 50 cents and has all the Sunday stuff including coupons.

Even if you don't read the paper, it can be worth just to buy the Sunday Daily News
and the early Sunday Newsday (total cost for both $1.50) for the coupon savings.

I clip coupons, watch the sales and often end up saving anywhere from $5 to $8  off
my bill - even the cashiers are impressed.
