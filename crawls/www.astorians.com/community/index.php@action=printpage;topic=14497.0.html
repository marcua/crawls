8pm Waltz Classical Presents
LAURA BARGER & DAVID KALHOUS

* $10 Admission, $10 Drink Minimum


Sunday, 4/05/09
8pm

Contemporary piano virtuosi David Kalhous (Czech Republic) and Laura Barger (United States) present an exciting and adventurous program entitled "PLAYING GAMES: CONTEMPORARY MUSIC FOR PIANO 4-HANDS."

Inspired by Gyorgy Kurtag's Jatekok ('Games'), the program is centered around several of Kurtag's original works and transcriptions of J.S. Bach for piano 4-hands. In addition, the duo will present a new work written for them by NYC-based composer Joseph Di Ponio, as well a rare performance of Woldgang Rihm's intriguing Klavierstuck Nr. 3.

Program:

J.S. Bach/Gyorgy Kurtag: Three Transcriptions (1985)
         Alle Menschen mussen sterben, BWV 643
         O Lamm Gottes, unshuldig, BWV DEEST
         Allein Gott in der Hoh' sei Ehr, BWV 711

Wolfgang Rihm: Klavierstuck Nr. 3, Op. 8c (1971)

Joseph Di Ponio: Sifting the Contents of Time (2008)

Gyorgy Kurtag: Selections from Jatekok (Games)

* $10 Admission, $10 Drink Minimum
