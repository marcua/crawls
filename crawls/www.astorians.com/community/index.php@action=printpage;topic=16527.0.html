Title: For Those of us Who Love The Fall Spicy Coffees
Post by: bejessie on October 30, 2009, 12:49:27 PM
So this isnt about restaurants in Astoria, but for those Astorians who like myself love the fall spiced coffees at starbucks and the like, but want to save a few bucks for more important things like that triple smoked bacon at Euromart or Fattys Sweet Potato Fries or weekend drinks at Hellsgate.. whatever..

What I do is every sunday I make a simple syrup of 2 cups of sugar to two cups of water. I boil this down till the sugar disolves. I then add 2 tbs of vanilla, 1tbs pumpkin pie spice, 1 tsp allspice, 1 tsp cinnamon and 1 tbs cardamom. Let this boil for 5 minutes. Keep in the fridge and add a shot to your coffee in the morning. Keeps in the fridge for about a week and kicks starbucks arse. Enjoy!
