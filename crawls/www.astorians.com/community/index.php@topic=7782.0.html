My mom and I did go for dinner on her birthday Friday night.

It was very nice. Granted, we went early - 5:30 - because she was up early
for a funeral in NJ that day and wanted to go and come back early since she
was tired.

We were the only ones there at that time, but it was nice. Food was great - she
had chicken parmesan and I had grill salmon. Both were hot, delicious and well presented.

Our waiter is one that I've known for awhile and he really took good care of us, as always.

We ended up being charged the early bird prices because their early bird specials are from
4:00 to 5:45. But the early menu is varied - different appetizers and entrees to select from, not as
limited as it can be at other places.

Anyway, it was great and I hope more people go - I'd hate to see them go out of business because
it's the only decent restaurant around there. We have nothing but these snotty cafés on 30th Ave. and
I'd hate to see this turn into one of those.....
