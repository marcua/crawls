A couple weeks ago, I went over to Zabb Thai (30th Ave, betw. 33-34th Sts, I think) to order some takeout, and much to my dismay arrived to find them closed, and the dining area completely cleared out.

My first thought was that they had closed (Nooo!), but one of the guys (manager/owner?) came out to the door when he saw me sadly peering in. He said that they're just changing a little-- more sushi-oriented, but they will still have some Thai food as well. He said they should be reopening by the "end of the month".

Just FYI...
