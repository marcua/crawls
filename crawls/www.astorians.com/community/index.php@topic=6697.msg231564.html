I grew up in yorkville on East 72nd st . My dad worked for REA for 33 years until his 60th birthday.He worked there until 1975 or 1976 until he was able to start collecting his pension. When I was a young boy I remember getting the Steinway Bus on 59th street in Manhattan and get off at Northern Blvd and Steinway. We then would walk up over the small bridge to the terminal by the railroad tracks. I remember walking on the platforms and riding on the fork lift with my dad. He loaded and unloaded freight from trucks onto and off of the railroad cars. I carry my dad's REA ID to this day. He died from a heart attack in 1980 only a few years after he retired. I would sometimes drive down to the abondoned terminal to relive my memories of him at work. He helped me buy my first car at the Ford Dealership next door.
