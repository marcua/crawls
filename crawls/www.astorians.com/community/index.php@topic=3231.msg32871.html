Brasilianville Cafe & Grill is a cafeteria-type place at 43-12 34th Avenue.  There are two sections: a buffet with hot and cold food and a grill area with various cuts of meat rotating on spits; there's a counterman there who'll slice off pieces for you.  All selections change daily.  You pay by the pound and there are 3 different prices: buffet only, meat only, or meat + buffet.  It's pretty reasonable no matter what option you choose.  They also have desserts and savory pastries.

Oh, and I'll be at the 7 PM showing!
