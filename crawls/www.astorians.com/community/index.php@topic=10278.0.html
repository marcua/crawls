I found out the scoop on the place from one of their employees.  They are opening this Tuesday, June 17, 2008.  They do have the rodizio option as well as paying by the pound.  I love going to Brazilian places and I haven't paid less than $30 a head and they are charging $22.95 which is excellent for all you can eat meat and buffet.  The option to pay by the pound is really cool too because you can have one rodizio on the table and your wife/husband/etc. can eat by the pound and that dish can come up to let's say $10. They also have take out like the other Brazilian spots in Astoria.  Finally somewhere I can get fast food on the week nights where it isn't really "fast food".   :-)  Love that they'll have Sangria!
