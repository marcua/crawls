Queensbridge Park (Vernon Blvd @ 41st Ave) is a good place to see the fireworks.  It's crowded but not as sardine-like as other riverfront areas.  The only drawback is having the Queensboro Bridge between you and the fireworks.  They absolutely do not let anyone watch from that bridge, by the way. 
But why wait until the Fourth when you can see a fantastic neighborhood fireworks display this Friday, June 29 over Astoria Park?  It's an annual event, Grucci Fireworks following a Big Band concert that begins @ 730p.  They're really good!
