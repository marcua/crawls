What an odd gift!
I found this on the internet: Quince paste is sweet with a delightfully delicate floral taste. A common presentation is to serve 1/4" thick slices of Manchego cheese with a thin 1/8" slice of quince paste atop.  The sweet fruit is a  perfect complement to the pungent cheese.
So apparently if you want to make a cheese plate, you can put some quince paste on it.
