Hey Everyone!

Since ya'll helped us move into the NYTVF Pitch Contest Finals, you're invited to watch us pitch it to the executives - LIVE!

Thu, Sep 14 2:00 pm  (90 min)
IFC Center  -  FREE   ($2.50 handling fee for reserved seats)
Reserve at www.nytvf.com !!

The NYTVF teams up with MSN, IFC, and Rainbow Media to present the final round of the nationwide Pitch Contest.  This summer, TV fans from all over the country submitted minute-long video pitches presenting their original ideas for new TV shows.  Fifty of these video clips were webcast on MSN for users to watch and vote on.  Ten of the top vote-getters have advanced to the final round, which is being held live at the IFC Center during the 2006 NYTVF.  The ten Finalists will pitch their ideas head-to-head to a panel of development executives.  One lucky winner will win $8,000 from IFC and Rainbow Media to create a pilot from the winning pitch.

Hope you can make it!
