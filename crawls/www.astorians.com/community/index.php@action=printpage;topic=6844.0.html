Title: Hellgate CSA Composting Event!
Post by: gud stuf on June 27, 2007, 09:02:37 PM
On Saturday July 21st, come join our master composters from the Queens Botanical Garden http://www.queensbotanical.org (http://www.queensbotanical.org) and learn how to have fun and help the environment… with worms!

The New York City Compost Project http://www.nyccompost.org (http://www.nyccompost.org) can get you composting right away! At the seminar, you can purchase your very own bin for $10, and you also get a voucher for your first pound of worms. It’s bound to be an interesting and educational day.

All Hellgate CSA members are encouraged to attend; please RSVP so we can get an idea of the size we need for the location. It will be easy access and central to Astoria, fear not! Location and time TBD. I'd love to get an idea of how many people are interested so we can make sure to get enough bins and a prime spot  :wink:

Refreshments will be provided!
