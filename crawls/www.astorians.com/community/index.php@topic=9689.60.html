Today we tried Grand Cafe when the BF said he wanted "brunch outdoors." I did warn him that we'd be paying more for it, and get lesser quality.

Their prix fixe seemed reasonable: $11 gets you a hot and cold beverage; potatoes; toast; entree; minimuffins. I got the Mediterranien omlette and he go eggs Benedict.

Mine was okay, warm, though overcooked; his was on the cold side. Understaffed, so maybe our meal waited around too long before being served. Great place for catching up with people and lingering- we certainly weren't rushed out which is refreshing. Music was kinda lounge-y but bearable.

Grand has been growing on me since I discovered the milk-free frappe.  Who knew it could taste so good and be dairy free???  YUM.

We were also there for brunch this am.  I got the cheeseburger (I'd already eaten breakfast 3 hours earlier) with salad and asked for the wasabi dressing.  This dressing makes EVERYTHING taste amazing.  Double YUM.   I have no idea what my husband had - b/c I was too busy enjoying my cheeseburger and frappe.

The food took a long time - but it didn't matter b/c it's a great place to just sit and enjoy the people watching AND there are those mini muffins to snack on while you wait.  Aside from the wait for food - I think the staff is really awesome.  Very attentive (today) esp the busboys.  I have to say that I DON"T like to sit inside b/c I find the music annoying and way too loud (I must be really old).
