Quote from: Kate_nyc on December 28, 2010, 09:25:07 AM
I'm 9 months pregnant, too - hope I don't go into labor a week early!
Oh Kate, I feel for you. On Good Friday, March 27, 1970, while carrying a multiple pregnancy, one of my waters broke and I went into labor. It was during a rare late winter blizzard. There were no cabs, no busses, no trains and the police department wasn't answering their phone. (We didn't have 911 back then.) My late husband was stranded in Manhattan at work. I got dressed, water flowing, and walked to Crescent Street and the corner of 30th Avenue hoping to find some way to get to ANY hospital with a neonatal unit.

I got picked up, and transported by a garbage truck plowing the streets. These two hefty, wonderful men, lifted me into the tuck and they plowed their way to Jamaica Hospital while blood for the babies' transfusions was being brought there via fire truck.

Think of all the wonderful stories you will have to tell that baby . . . and all the memories that you will have.

Please, though, do try to get help before you decide to do it alone at home.
