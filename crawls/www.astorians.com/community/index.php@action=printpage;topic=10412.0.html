Title: Re: mta free transfers
Post by: Starwind51 on May 09, 2008, 12:37:16 AM
Subway to bus is always a free transfer (possible exception: express buses).  The driver should have told you to contact the MTA.  You can get a postage-free envelope at a token-booth, or whatever we call them now.  As per the instructions on the envelope, you tell them aproximately what time you got off the train and what time you got on the bus - presumably you spent less than 2 hours on the train and were therefore within the free transfer period.

As for buses - if you pay in coin, you get a free transfer, but it's only good for another bus!  I have no idea why it can't be used on a train!

http://www.mta.nyc.ny.us/metrocard/problems.htm (http://www.mta.nyc.ny.us/metrocard/problems.htm)
