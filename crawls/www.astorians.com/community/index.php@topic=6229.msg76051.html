I'm an artist and random day job holder 

Right now my day job is working customer service (funny- since I am NOT a phone person) at a company called Active Health Management. Basically we call doctor's offices all day and provide health info based on health insurance claims. Aetna just bought our company and since that happened it's been Big Brotherville here.

This last "random" job has lasted a bit longer than I would have liked! But it pays well and has benefits etc....always a good thing. However this job is about the furthest on the spectrum of "jobs that suit me" as one can get. I guess you could say I've been in a bit of a rut here for a little over a year now.

For the past 2 years I have been taking evening classes at the New School in their Creative Arts Therapy Certificate program. It's not undergrad and not grad- more like a really solid foundation in Art Therapy. I'm almost finished and have an internship next semester (hmmm...opportunity to get out of this crappy job?) LOL
Still undecided about pursuing art therapy on a graduate level- I think that I want to be more certain before embarking on that path, but I am definitely gaining a lot from the classes that I've been taking there.


I also try and find time for my personal work, which is painting and what I went to school for too!  :roll:


I'm also exploring the idea of mural painting/decorative finishes and have been looking into this increasingly. For the time being I'm not quite ready to make the leap into being self-employed in this area...but I am working on building up my portfolio and experience with the idea that someday I could follow that path.

I'm starting a mural project soon in the Village in an apartment lobby. I also did a mural at the new Bistro 33- across their bar....by the way- can hardly wait for them to open!!!
(I've been in Bistro food withdrawal).

So that's my whole long "occupation" description. I guess I should've mentioned that my other part time occupation is looking for another day job!
