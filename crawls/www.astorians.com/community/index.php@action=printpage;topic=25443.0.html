So funny, I just happened to be checking this site today after a few months.  Yes, we found a day care that we love!!!  Its Tons of Fun daycare on 36th street.  It is about the price that I didn't want to pay, but worth every penny.  Honestly after looking at nearly every day care in the area this was the one where we felt the most comfortable and I could not be happier with our choice. 


What I like - the owner, Sherry, is very reachable, she will be in touch with you all day if you need it.  She knows the first few days are harder on the parents, and she will guide you through the transition.  Her and the workers truly love the children as their own.  My son comes home happy, tired from playing all day, clean, well fed.  The day care center is clean and safe, the hours are flexible.
