Title: Re: Stroller friendly eateries
Post by: lanseaux on August 09, 2011, 02:01:59 PM
In general, I have found rebb_'s experience to be very similar to mine.

I think the car seat that snaps into the stroller is the snap and go - or just like it, anyway.  I think I've blocked out the memory.

But I've been in most places in the Ditmars area with a stroller.  When in doubt, we have asked - either by calling ahead or poking our heads in.  If the weather is suitable and they have an outdoor space, we try to sit outdoors.  We've also been to many places on 31st Ave (Il Bambino, Bareburger, Pachanga) without issues.  And while I haven't been to Vesta with one, I called once and asked if we could bring in a stroller early during their brunch hours, and they said it wouldn't be a problem.  Generally if it's a place that might be less child-friendly, we always ask, and we always go early or at other off-hours to minimize any conflicts (including Sparrow, where we find that we have been welcome for an early or late brunch, or early dinner).  We haven't had a problem yet.
