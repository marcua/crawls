No.  Lots of people think Ketchup and Mayo are either French and/or Russian dressing and I believe it's a common misconception. 

Russian dressing has horseradish and/or Worcestershire sauce and other additives.  French doesn't have mayo.  It's generally ketchup, vinegar, sugar and sometimes dry mustard.
