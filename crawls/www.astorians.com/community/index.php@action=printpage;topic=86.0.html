Title: Kerry supporters gather for a taste of South Beach in NYC.
Post by: MariaNYC on October 16, 2004, 07:39:21 PM
To all those of you who want to do something about political change, come out for an evening, meet other Kerry supporters, even republicans and the undecided who are just there for the great food and great drinks.

For a $15 admission you can have a large array of the typically fabulous Cabana food between 6pm and 8pm at the Pier 17 Restaurant at the Seaport. Sunset is between 6:15 and 6:30pm. Great view! There's also a Cash Bar with tasty drinks that start at around $6, alcoholic and non alcoholic. Entrance is on the side of the escalators, near the down escalator.

Come out for a good time.
Meet other concerned fellow citizens.
Bring your friends.
Contribute to the Democratic National Committee.
Your donation to the DNC must be a check payable to Kerry Edwards Victory 2004.
