Title: Waterfront Bike Ride!
Post by: Emily on June 21, 2005, 03:18:58 PM
BIKE RIDE!
Please join us for a sunset bike ride of the Astoria/LIC waterfront on Sunday, June 26.  While it won't quite be a  formal tour, we welcome any of you to bring your knowledge of the local land, environment, history, arts and culture of the area to  share.   On September 17, we'll be doing a shoreline cleanup, so this ride will also help us identify the spots most in need of some love and care.  We'd love to see you there!  Details follow.  Please email or call  Emily to let me know if you'll be joining us (contact info below), or just show up at Gantry Plaza on Sunday, June 26.

What:  Astoria/LIC Waterfront Bike Ride
When:  Sunday, June 26, 5 pm til sunset
Where:  Start at Gantry Plaza State Park, 48th Avenue and the East River- We'll bike along the water to Ralph DeMarco Park where we can enjoy the setting sun
Who: All are welcome!  Tour led by Astoria/LIC Waterfront Parks Catalyst Coordinator Emily Maxwell, local blogger Joanna Grossman, and all of you interested in sharing local knowledge!
Why:  To get to know our waterfront, and to identify spots particularly in need of attention
After the tour: Get refreshed at Astoria's famous Bohemian Beer Garden if you'd like to get a taste of local culture.

RSVP to emily.maxwell@parks.nyc.gov or 917-902-9165

check us out on the web: www.astoriawaterfront.org
