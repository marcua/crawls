I had a really hard time finding a passport photo place in the neighborhood recently.  I used to go to the photo place on 31st Street near Ditmars but that doesn't exist anymore.  CVS on the corner of 31st and Ditmars has a passport photo option, but they did such a poor job (using a point & shoot camera and crappy printing) that I refused purchasing it.  The closest place I found was a photo place in Greenpoint.  They did a really good job with a professional SLR camera and two photos were, I think around $10 and instantaneous.  The place is:

Kubus Photo  
102A Nassau Ave
Brooklyn, NY 11222b/t Eckford St & Leonard St 
Greenpoint
(718) 389-1339
kubusphoto.com


Hope this helps!
