Title: March 29 - Kshama Sawant on Building Alternative to Two Parties of Wall Street
Post by: Al122 on March 24, 2013, 11:16:21 PM
Last fall, economics professor and Occupy Wall Street activist, Kshama Sawant ran against the Democratic Speaker of Washington State's House of Representatives and won a historic 29% of the vote as the candidate of the Socialist Alternative party. Sawant is now on a speaking tour discussing the lessons of the 2012 campaign, her current campaign for the Seattle City Council, the city council campaigns of other Socialist Alternative candidates running in Minneapolis and Boston and how we can continue to build a left alternative to the Democrats and Republicans, the two parties of Wall Street.


Program, co-hosted by Occupy Astoria LIC will be held at the Greater Astoria Historical Society, 35-20 Broadway 4th Floor on Friday March 29 from 6 to 8 pm.
