What an excellent idea!  Rescue cats are wonderful and older ones (who are sometimes classifed as such by only a year a old!) are great cats.  You know exactly what you are "getting" as far as looks and personality and they are usually calmer than kittens and do not need constant supervision (like kittens).  Both of my adopted furry children have been rescued at 1 year from a Florida shelter that I ended up volunteering at afterward.  I've noticed that the little kittens go quickly, but the bigger guys usually stay longer and truely appreciate a loving home.  Add a cat to your life today - especially while it's free!  Any number of years spent with a loving cat are good years, for both you and the cat. :-)
