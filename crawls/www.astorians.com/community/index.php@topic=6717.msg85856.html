Darn good massage found

There's a new Chinese massage place a few doors down from Starbucks on Ditmars.  The hour massage is only 50 bucks.  It's a no frills place and it's one large room separated into three spaces for tables so it's not a place where people do a lot of chatting.  It's open 7 days a week and almost all day long.   

A man gave me my massage and he found every knot in my body and worked it out.  A few times it was too hard, but I chose not to tell him to loosen up because he was never in one spot for two long.  Afterwards, I was so zoned out, that I went to bed really early.   

I am so excited to go back in a few weeks.

There's not much privacy.  They've partitioned off, I think, about 3 cubicles with shoji screens and a curtain.  They play classical music.  I was the only one there when I went.  Don't know what it'd be like with others around.  The prices are $30 for 30 minutes, $50 for one hour but she worked on me more than an hour so you're not rushed out.  Also, they do foot reflexology for $30 a treatment and I think you can choose a hot stone treatment.

Chinese Gongfu Tui-Na
29-28 Ditmars Blvd., 2nd Floor
718-204-7580

They have some pamphlets, you can pick up one outside the door (on the street) that explain their kind of massage therapy.
