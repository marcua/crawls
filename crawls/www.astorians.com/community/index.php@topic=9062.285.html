She pretty obviously lied that it burned and another would be right out. My friend said he didn't want it now and not to bring it. She said she'd take it off the check (well of course she should). So she brings the entree after we're all mostly done with our food. My friend again tells her he doesn't want it, nicely. She insists not once or twice, but at least 7 or 8 times that he take the plate. We've all worked at restaurants before and we all know this is so she won't have to tell her manager that anything went wrong if we take the plate. If it goes back to the kitchen she's busted.
