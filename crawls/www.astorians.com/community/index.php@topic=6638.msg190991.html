I may have posted this already, but there was a bar on the south side of Queensboro Plaza, right about where you climb the stairs to reach the N/W/7 trains.   It was open for years, but at some point either in the late 1990s or sometime this century it closed.   It had morphed into a convenience store.   In any event, that is old news.   The new news, which I may or may not have posted previously, is I noticed that like much of that part of LIC the convenience store and other buildings have been demolished and now it is an empty lot.   No more memories of the fact that a little bar stood there for years and years no doubt the first stop for many who toiled in the Eagle Electric plants that used to be in that area.
