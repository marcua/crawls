Pasta Potluck Dinner and Chavurah Service: Fri, November 10th, 6:30pm
Enjoy a scrumptious homemade (kosher) pasta dinner of stuffed shells with tomato and pesto sauce followed by a Chavurah service in our social hall. All are welcome. Dinner tickets are $5 per person. Please make reservations at 718-278-2680 or info@astoriacenterofisrael.org by November 6th, if possible, so we know how much food to prepare. Dinner is at 6:30pm, services begin at 8pm. If possible, please bring a kosher dairy or pareve dish or dessert. If you don't have a kosher kitchen, you can bring a packaged kosher item (cupcakes, hummus, lox, etc.).

We hope you can join us on this Friday night or another, or for Saturday morning services. Everyone is welcome.

Astoria Center of Israel Synagogue
27-35 Crescent Street, Astoria 718-278-2680
http://www.astoriacenter.org
Take the N or W subway towards Queens to the 30th Avenue stop. Walk west on 30th Avenue (towards Athens Square Park) for about 4 short blocks; turn right on Crescent Street and walk a half block down on the right.
