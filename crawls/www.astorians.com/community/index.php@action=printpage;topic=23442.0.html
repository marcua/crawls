An Open Call for the First-Ever Astoria Art Festival
Dear Queens-Dwelling Creative Folk,
 
We are thrilled to announce the first-ever Astoria Art Festival will be taking over the neighborhood on Friday, November 9-Sunday, November 11. For one weekend, restaurants and shops across Astoria will showcase the work of emerging and well-known local artists alike, complemented by an energetic kick-off party and several boozy receptions. It’s going to be an amazing collaborative opportunity to put the growing number of talented Queens artists in the spotlight.
 
We invite Queens-based artists from all mediums (except for sculpture and large installations; unfortunately, tight spaces don’t allow for projects of this nature) to submit proposals and high-res Jpegs of their works for consideration. From photography and paintings to sketches and mixed-media collages, we want to see your creative endeavors!
 
GUIDELINES
1. The deadline for submission is September 30, 2012.
2. Along with high-res images of your work, please send an accompanying detailed description of no more than 200 words.
3. Please note the Queens neighborhood in which you reside.
4. Send all materials to the email address inbloomcollective@gmail.com  by Midnight September 30, 2012 with the subject heading Open Call.
5.  All selected entrants will be notified by October 15th, 2012
 
Thanks so much for your interest,
In Bloom Collective
