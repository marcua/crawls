CRIMINOSO, needs YOU! This Sunday @ LIC Bar: 4-7 PM
« on: September 26, 2008, 12:59:52 AM »
Criminoso is playing this Sunday, Sept. 28th from 4-7 PM in the outdoor patio of LIC Bar.  Its a very cool place with very fine beers and more (House Toasted Lager!!!). 
 
We'll be playing all original electro-jazz/samba tunes, along with a few traditional Brazilian tunes.  Musicians are welcome to sit in on our tunes, so bring your horn, or axe, or whatever.
Sunday's band: Dan - bass, Jonas - Sax, Cameron - trombone, me - guitar
 
Criminoso needs you to come to this gig!  Here is some history as to why:
 
A couple of weeks ago, I booked a month long trip to Italy in November where I am going to be studying Italian (seriously, I did...it's gonna be cool).  Well, the sinister Gustavo Rodriguez (music booker at LIC bar and member of a very good [but evil] band called The Little Creatures: http://www.myspace.com/thelittlecreatures) is determined to foil my plans and upset the European balance.  Gustavo is planning to eradicate the phrase "va bene" and pasta production throughout Italy...ruining Italian greetings, destroying the Italian economy, and totally screwing up my November trip by preventing me from completing my "How much pasta can I fit in my mouth in one sitting" research.  Think about it...everyone in Italy is going to be sitting in cafes, all up in a bad mood because the can't say, "it's going well".  Folks, there is only one way to stop him:  People enjoying drinks and having a good time while listening to live electro-jazz in an outdoor patio on the last Sunday of September.  Seriously, it totally throws of his "Chi" and reverses his moon cycle for a whole month...giving me enough time to stuff my face with mostaccioli all of November.  So, Criminoso needs you now!  This could affect you too!  He plans on going after "de nada", "oye ve!", "chez Jacques", plantains, hot dogs, crepes, fruit roll-ups, and fruit cake (fruit cake!  who's that against?  Not all of Gustavo's evil plans are well thought out, folks...)
 
So...Come on down, enjoy some great beer and music!  The gig is rain or shine, so if it rains, we'll still be playing inside.
 
Sunday, Sept. 28th, 4PM - 7PM
CRIMINOSO @ LIC Bar
45-58 Vernon Blvd
Btwn. 45th Rd. & 46th Ave.

Long Island City
