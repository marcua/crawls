I am selling my handmade, chocolate truffles for the holidays.  They make a perfectly delectable gift, and you would be helping me pay my rent. 

Varieties:

For the season-
   Mint ganache, garnished with crushed candy canes
   Hazelnut ganache, garnished with feuilletine
   Spice-infused ganache, coated in traditional cocoa powder
   Caramel ganache, with pecan garnish
   Almond ganache, garnished with toasted, sliced almonds

Specialty (dipped in dark chocolate)-
   Lime-infused ganache, garnished with white chocolate and candied lime zest
   Lavender-infused ganache, garnished with white chocolate
   Chili pepper-infused ganache, with spicy cocoa garnish
   Peanut Butter ganache, garnished with dark chocolate swirl.


Your choice of any six truffles. 
Specify packaging:
Clear candy bag with ribbon $8. 
White candy box with ribbon $9.

I am taking orders now through December 15th.  I offer delivery in Astoria only.   Delivery dates begin December 4th, and end December 21st.  I will need at least three days’ notice to have your order ready for delivery.  Please PM or email me at michelle31980@gmail.com to place an order and arrange delivery.  Cash only, please.   :-)

Thanks everyone!!!
