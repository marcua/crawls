Title: Astorians for Peace & Justice
Post by: borzoi4 on April 04, 2005, 04:35:32 PM
Astorians for Peace and Justice is holding their biweekly working meeting at the Freeze Peach cafe TONIGHT at 7:30 p.m. We always welcome new members, whether you want to join the working group or just find out about us and sign up for our e-mail list for upcoming events. Or e-mail us at astorians4peace@earthlink.net.

Tonight's agenda includes our local lobbying efforts in support of the Religious Freedoms Peace Tax Fund (an effort to allow people to be conscientious objectors when paying their taxes).

AFPJ is an independent, non-partisan, non-sectarian organization, founded in 2003 in opposition to the Iraq war, that works for peace and justice issues locally here in Astoria.
