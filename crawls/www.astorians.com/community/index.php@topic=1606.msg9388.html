Steinway Street Bridge Demolition To Begin This Weekend
February 17, 2006

The demolition of the old Steinway Street Bridge could cause confusion and a lot of traffic tie-ups in Astoria this weekend.

Demolition work on the bridge is expected to take two weeks to complete and there are plenty of changes in the neighborhood as a result.

Parking restrictions on Astoria Boulevard will be in effect until early Monday. The restrictions return next weekend.

Road and lane closures near the bridge take effect at midnight and last through the weekend. One eastbound lane and two Westbound lanes will be closed on the Grand Central Parkway. On Astoria Boulevard, the 33rd Street ramp to the Grand Central will be closed.

And on the BQE northbound, the Grand Central Parkway entrance ramp will be closed. The westbound Grand Central Parkway entrance ramp at 74th street will also be closed.

The new bridge is expected to partially open in October.
