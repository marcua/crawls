Title: Welcome to "Obese City"
Post by: Cannibal on December 02, 2006, 12:09:27 PM
My buddy Joe reported to me last night that the mailbox closest to the Cup restaurant has been tagged with the moniker "Obese City."  He also informed me of an obscure federal rule that once a locality has a name change that new moniker gets placed on the mailbox.

At first I thought "somebody with a spraycan is yet again critiquing my body image."  But then again I thought, no, maybe there was a new influx of Overweight Outerborough Refugees to compete with the svelte PYT's and hypsters.   Go figure, I hadn't heard of this name change in the local or electronic media.  But it stands to reason, what with franchise chain brick oven pizza, spiffy new supermarkets complete with hot ginger spice, "tapas" "clubs" with exclusive Manhattan bottle service All now making their appearance.  Then it dawned on me!  These were the formerly svelte who decided to come off their high horse and have a delish meal with the rest of us.  Okay!

This new name works for the uptown Astoria crowd who don't want their friends moping around the QB Plaza area looking for the lastest dive.  Maybe it also portends a new zip code and a new name at the 36th Ave - Bebbe subway stop (who will trouble themselves remembering Bebbe when you can say Obese Blvd).

So a new age has dawned for Lower Astoria!

- Cannibal
