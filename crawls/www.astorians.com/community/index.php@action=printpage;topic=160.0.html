Title: NYLXS Free Software Open House
Post by: windowsrefund on December 02, 2004, 07:21:19 PM
On Monday evening, December 06, 2004, NYLXS will be hosting a Free Software Open House. On that day, we will make the world aware of the virtues of of Free and Open Source Software (FOSS), and encourage its widespread use. NYLXS will be creating and distributing SimplyMepis2004 Linux Live CDs as well as informational fliers detailing both Free Software and NYLXS. In addition, we will demonstrate multimedia and sound, office automation, graphic manipulation, remote access via the X Windows system, GNU/Linux for kids, programming tools and general personal and business use of GNU/Linux.

Everyone is encouraged and invited to the NYLXS Free Software Open House 2004. Complimentary refreshments will be served...

What: NYLXS Free Software Open House Demonstration/Celebration

When Monday, December 06, 2004 6:30pm - 11:00pm

Where: NYLXS Educational Center
              Kings Games
              1724 East 12 Street (Corner of Kings Highway)
              Brooklyn, NY

Phone: 718 336-1955

For more info, see the NYLXS website at http://nylxs.com
