About the Mountain:
Killington features access to 200 trails and 32 lifts, plus the highest lift-served terrain in Vermont from the 4,241-foot summit of Killington Peak. They get an average of 250 inches of natural snow every season. The town is known for its nightlife!!!

Capacity 100 people and selling out fast!

Round trip transportation, leaving at 4:30pm on Friday from the upper east side, returning Sunday around 10pm.
2 nights at a great hotel with a heated pool and large hot tub!

2 breakfasts
