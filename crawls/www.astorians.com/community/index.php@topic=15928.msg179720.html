Hi Anya:

I am the Director of Serious Fun, a licensed NYC child care and arts enrichment after school center.  Please come to our free trial classes next week--Monday - Thursday, 3:30-4:15 pm, 8/24-26, featuring a different class each day (we've posted about it on the discussion board).  The program takes place at the Steinway Reformed Church,41st and Ditmars blvd (we are part of the church's community outreach but our program is not religious).  You can look over the facility and your child can take the classes to see if we are a good fit for you.

We can certainly pick up your child at the end of the school day and bring them to our program. We are open September 14-December 18 every day the public schools are open for a full day (we are closed on school 1/2 days).  The children have a light snack, receive homework help, and participate in a 45 minute arts or language program each day.  We also have quiet time and outdoor play (weather permitting).

I've included some more information below, and please feel free to call or PM me if you have any questions: 646-415-8901.

Thanks,

Sylvia

Here’s an outline of the day:

2:20-3:00         Pick ups from local schools
2:45-3:15         Drop offs at Serious Fun
3:15-3:30         Daily Routine: take off coats, deposit book bags on table, wash hands
                      Snack: crackers and choice of water or juice.
3:30-4:15         PreK-K Enrichment class (class depending on day)
                      1st-3rd Grade Homework help
4:15-4:30         Bathroom/water break and transition
4:30-5:15         1st-3rd Grade Enrichment class
PreK-K Homework help and/or story time and quiet games
5:15-5:30         Pack up bags and stuff for home and GO OUTSIDE
5:30-5:50         Recess Outside (weather permitting) or inside active games
5:50-6:00         Parent Pick Ups

Parents can choose from part-time or full-time one- to five-days a week.  Our current class schedule is:

Mondays-Hands on Art: children play with paint, mask-making, printmaking, puppetry, & sculpture—the messier the better!

Tuesdays-SocRoc: children have fun on their feet, including basic soccer skills and exercise.

Wednesdays-Everybody Dance: children learn classic dance positions, jazz dance moves, dance games and create their own dance moves.

Thursdays TBD, either Theater Arts or Spanish Language Immersion

Fridays-World Music Jam: children play instruments from around the world while learning new songs & cultures.

Transportation provided from  PS 2, 84, 85, 122, Grace Lutheran, Immaculate Conception, and St. Francis schools.
