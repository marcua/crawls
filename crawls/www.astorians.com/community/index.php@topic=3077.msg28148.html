Just a quick update here. I've heard from a couple people that they haven't been able to access the site sometimes throughout the day, or that the site looks messed up. That should be fixed by tomorrow. The reason that it does this is because in the process of moving the site to a new server the new ip address has to get linked to the domain name. It takes time for the new address to show up in everyone's internet provider's servers so that's why it looks strange sometimes. It usually just takes 1-2 days for all these to disappear.
