2 wins for animals yesterday!
« on: November 08, 2006, 12:12:41 PM »
Proposition 204 in Arizona was passed by 61% of the voters despite agribusiness spending $2.5 million to defeat it.

  Arizona is now the first state in the U.S. to ban the use of veal crates and the 2nd state to ban the use of gestation crates for pigs.  Amazing work done to get these issues on a statewide ballot.

Also amazes me  how agribusiness fights even the most reasonable measures, increasing crate size to allow an animal to turn around!
http://www.time.com/time/nation/article/0,8599,1555372,00.html
