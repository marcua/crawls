Post by: johnny4evah! on May 03, 2011, 07:41:13 PM
Try Verni's on Hazen Street and Ditmars Blvd. I occasionally walk there when the weather's nice from the Astoria park area. Now if you have a car I recommend Garden World on 46th Street and Francis Lewis Blvd in Flushing. We were there last weekend. It's a gardener's heaven! They have everything- from supplies to plants to trees, I wish I lived next door to them! I was also surprised by the variety of veggie seedlings and reasonable prices.

The closest Garden/Nursery stores in Astoria are Verni's on Astoria Blvd. on Hazen Street or the Home Depot garden center on Northern Blvd.  For a bigger garden nursery type place I would recommend Garden World or Keil Brothers in Bayside or Hicks in Westbury if you have wheels.
