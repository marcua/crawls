On the right side of the screen, find "Show unread posts since last visit". Click that and you will get a list of topics with newish messages.

In each forum's line, to the right of it's title you should see a dark rounded blob marked "New". Click that to open the topic, positioned at the first message since last time you looked.

If you use firefox, and have a 3 button mouse, the center button opens a new "tab", so my habit is to first go down the list clicking the (new) thing for each topic I am interested in, then use the browser tabs to look at them one-by-one.

There ought to be a one-click way to accomplish the same thing, but so far as I can tell, there is not.
