Brand new poster.  Hello, all.

My fiance, Tegan, and I moved to Astoria in April 2005 and have really enjoyed our time here.  (Although, a little more public green space would be great, and I'm going to guess that is common opinion around here).

Thought this was a great thread to start into, as we have still only touched the surface of dining spots in the area.  So, this has provided a bunch of recommendations.  Thanks!

I finally did see a Cavo mention.  I'm definitely a fan, with the acknowledgement that it is an expensive night out.  I'm already looking forward to sangria and pitas on the patio in the Spring.

Thai Chili (45th & Broadway) is our favorite spot, mostly for delivery, but the eating in is really nice as well.

And, if you are a vegetarian sushi fan definitely check out Bai Sushi (37th & Broadway, I think), as they have a delightful vegetarian platter.  The fish is usually pretty good too.

Matt

Without a doubt out favorite restaurant is Bangkok Tasty .  It's not much to look at, and we thought it was initially a Chinese restaurant trying to be creative by serving fake Thai food (partially because it used to be a Chinese restaurant).  But we were wrong.  Real Thai people serving excellent Thai food. It can be on the spicy side, but it's worth every tear shed.  In fact it's more of a take-out place than a real restaurant but you can sit and eat there as well if you really feel the need to.   :mrgreen:
