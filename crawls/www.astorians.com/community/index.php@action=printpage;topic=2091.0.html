Hey, so I've got a suggestion. There are a lot of vegetables to be had over the course of the summer. Not all who will be participating are likely to be expert cooks, and recipes might well be appreciated. The Madison, WI CSAs publish a cookbook called From Asparagus to Zucchini: A Guide to Cooking Farm-Fresh, Seasonal Produce (http://www.macsac.org/atoz.html). It looks like for orders of more than 10, they're $12 each, plus $1.25 shipping. Perhaps Hellsgate members would be interested in organizing an order of these? I've used the cookbook, and it's very good. (Alas, I no longer have my copy...) There are sections for each individual vegetable, plus sections for seasonal recipes that use a variety of vegetables.

Another suggestion, as Cabanird suggested, is Astorian-supplied vegetable recipes. Perhaps the already brilliant Hellgate CSA (http://www.hellgatecsa.com/) website could have a wiki (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wiki) for recipes and suggestions about vegetables?
