Hooray, sweet potato season! Here's a great recipe:

Roasted Sweet Potato Salad

From digitalchef.com.

    * 4 yams, peeled, 1/2" dice
    * 3 T olive oil
    * 1 t salt
    * 1 t black pepper
    * 2 red peppers, sliced thin
    * 1/2 t garlic, minced
    * 1 small red onion, sliced thin
    * 1 T ketchup
    * 4 oz red wine vinegar
    * 2 T honey
    * 2 c chopped parsley

   1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Put oven rack in center.
   2. On baking sheet, toss yams in 2 T of the olive oil, salt, and pepper. Bake 25 minutes, until lightly browned.
   3. Toss with other ingredients, including remaining 1 T olive oil, in a bowl.
   4. Adjust seasonings and serve.

Serves 8.
