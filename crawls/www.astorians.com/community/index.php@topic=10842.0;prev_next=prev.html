1 BR in a 2 BR apartment -Available October 15 -$875
« on: September 21, 2008, 10:29:33 AM »
AVAILABLE: October 15th/November 1.

What is offered:

1 bedroom in a 2 bedroom first floor apartment in a quiet residential area of Astoria. 10-15 minute walk to trains N/W or R and close to busses Q101 and Q18. Laundromat across street, corner store at end of block. Within walking distance of 2 good supermarkets. Great access to various shopping, pubs, restaurants, etc.

The bedroom does not have closet but the living room has a tall and wide closet to store your belonging and clothes. Apartment is cozy and warm with a medium size living room, newly renovated kitchen and bathroom.

What I am looking for in a roommate:

Respectful, considerate and responsible are three top things that come to mind. Must have good sense of humor and pay all bills ahead of/on time. Prefer Longterm individuals at a minimum of 6 months. Prefer if you stay for a year. Email me and tell me a bit about yourself, what you do and what you're looking for.

About Me:

I’m a 30 year old PR professional, working mostly 9-5 but can work later depending on clients.

I have a quirky personality, am quite sarcastic, love to laugh and have a great many friends in the neighborhood. We often go out for a pint a few nights a week to catch up and talk with other folks in the hood.

Hobbies include beading and jewelry-making, music and film, photography and reading. I’m courteous, kind, patient and pretty laid back and I have a good sense of humor. Overall, I wouldn’t say I’m the neatest person, I openly admit that I have my clutter but I do like to keep things clean and like to follow a cleaning schedule. Fairness is key to me in sharing an apartment with another person.

I also have a wonderful and playful two year old cat named Maggie, that is loving, social and very well trained. If you have a cat that is socialized and well-trained, this may be a great place for you!

I spend a lot of time with my friends and in Astoria which is a great neighborhood and my family will come to visit. My sister and bro-in-law have a baby boy so, they will come and visit for a weekend off and on, and sometimes my mother or both parents will come for a long weekend.

A few basic rules:

No Drugs. I don’t care what you do just don’t do it at home.
Smoking is cool….as long as it’s outside.
Overnight Guests are ok, just not every other night.

Bills and Utilities:
Rent 875 per month plus utilities (Cable/Internet and ConEd)
Up front: First and Last, plus security 1 months rent
