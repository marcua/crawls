I love twitter. Most people don't get it because 90% of the people on there just don't understand what to use it for. No one wants to read your daily minutiae and meaningless updates like "I'm bored." or "Just ate." Find the more entertaining people and you'll have a blast with it.

It's also a pretty interesting look into people's lives- there's a ton of famous writers and actors and directors and such on it and it's fun to be able to give feedback and get responses on their work.

Plus, you can link it to your facebook account and have everything trickle through there. It's a little weird to hear it constantly referenced in mainstream media these days but it deserves it- it's an entertaining site.
