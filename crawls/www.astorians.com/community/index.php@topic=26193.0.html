I remember when "The New York Times" used to run every Sunday articles on health department inspections. Imagine the horror of restaurant owners when they didn't fare well. The "Times" stopped it when it was revealed that some health inspectors were shaking down restaurant owners. If the didn't fork over bribe money they would give the restaurant poor marks that would then appear in the paper. It was a big scandal. Health inspectors were arrested & the "Times" stopped printing those Health Dept. reports.
