I think what people might be expressing is that there's a difference between advising the community of some threat (scammer, or other threat), and just seeking revenge. I don't think people would mind at all if Just Me posted something that was done out of community service, but I think where people might have a problem is if someone used the board just to get "revenge". That kind of writing is best left to something more personal like a blog. Here's an example of an anonymous posting about something negative and potentially dangerous.

So, it's okay to post "negative" things, and even "out" a scam (as has been done many times on this board), but people won't respond well to people that are just airing their dirty laundry.

That's my take on it anyway. As for board policy - as long as someone's individual identity isn't compromised it's okay to post things that one thinks might be helpful for the community (and general public) who reads this board.
