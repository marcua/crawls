I live at 24th Ave at 27th St in Astoria (duh) and I'm looking for a cool roommate for July 1st. This apartment really is a good deal. I mean, $625.50 near the subway, and everything else?  If you know anyone that fits this description please forward on..  Thanks, Helen


The Room:
1 bedroom available in a 2 bedroom apartment. Room size is 14' x 11' unfurnished. Would fit queen/king size bed with room for desk, dresser, and bookshelf. Bedroom has big windows with southern exposure so there's lots of light.

The Apartment:
It's an apartment with some creative style and much character. There's a separate living room, kitchen, bath, and foyer with plenty of shoe storage. Common area are mostly furnished with the exception of a couch.
Other noteworthy apartment things - there's no cable, no common area air conditioner, and there's free wireless internet. Two sweet and playful cats also come with the apartment. (I think this apt has reached it's capacity of four legged friends... so no additional cats or dogs please.)

About Me:
I'm a nice roommate, frequently not home because I work full time, have evening meetings and activities, and will additionally be starting grad school part time in the fall. I compost indoors, enjoying biking, environmental issues, and being a quasi vegetarian.

You:
Should be in your late 20's/early 30's, considerate, mature, progressive, and able to communicate well. You should also be financially stable, independent, busy, and will make time to contribute to maintaining a clean & comfortable household. No bedbugs, no smokers, and no crazy people please.

Location:
Astoria, Queens – Ditmars area. Part of the international belt of Queens, Astoria near Ditmars Bvd has a real neighborhood feel. It has an art scene, Manhattan skyline views, and the famous Astoria Beer Garden is two blocks away. It is 20 minutes to midtown and 4 short blocks to the N/W at Astoria Blvd or Ditmars Blvd. M60 to LGA Airport or Upper Manhattan. Street parking is relatively easy to find if you have a car. It's conveniently located near all things that are Greek, edible, and delicious. Nearby there are also neighborhood restaurants, bars, laundry, gym, Astoria Park, supermarkets, and a fancy deli.

Interested?
Please write me at helenshirley@gmail.com with a description of yourself.

Utilities usually come out to $60 per month spilt between two people.
Room is available starting July 1st.
Heat and hot water included.
No broker fee.
Credit report needed. First month's rent and one month security deposit to move in.
