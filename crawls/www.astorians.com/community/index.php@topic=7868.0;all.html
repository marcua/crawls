i know this was a few posts back.....but the sahm group is accepting new members (it had to shut down briefly).  Nannies aren't allowed to join the sahm - and only moms can sign up to events that are posted on our calendar (though as was said before, you can bring anyone you want with you).  But, members talk on the message board about setting up playdates between sitters, or a "meetup" for babysitters to get together, exchange numbers, etc.  lots of people in the group work part time- some full time (like me!)

also, if you're looking for stuff for your sitter to do- there's the lots of tots at the steinway reformed church- tues and thurs mornings (if it's not full) wed and thursday afternoons 3:30-5 and there are great music classes in astoria, too (music together, music for aarvarks) and i think some other playgroups around town....hopefiully someone else remembers where/when they are

that being said- it would be nice to have a working parent group......the astoria sahm group is great (if i do say so, myself- cause i help run it), but it does not do a lot of "working parent" stuff......i have to work on that! (typing w/one hand- nursing.......sorry)
