Post by: lanseaux on March 01, 2009, 12:32:34 AM
Quote from: shayne1 on February 28, 2009, 09:19:13 PM
We might be the only people that think so, but my partner and I both LOVE Golden Chopsticks on Ditmars and.. 37th?

Not the only ones.  We don't try to make it anything it's not, but Golden Chopsticks has been our go-to Chinese take-out for something like 15 years.  I've posted in other threads about them before.  And it's not necessarily greasy - they have many dishes where the decent quality of their veggies (and their chicken) comes through.  They have two different chefs/cooks - one does General Tso's so it is more or less indistinguishable from other take-out places, but the other one does bigger chunks of meat, less breading, with a darker, less sweet, substantially spicier sauce that we find really good.
