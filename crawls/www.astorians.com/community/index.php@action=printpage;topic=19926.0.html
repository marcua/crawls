Title: New Charter School Opening in Queens District 30
Post by: queensedu on December 05, 2010, 02:03:38 PM
A new charter school is opening August 2011 in Queens Community District 30! (CD30 includes LIC, Astoria, Sunnyside, Woodside, Elmhurst, Jackson Heights). Academy of the City will start with Kindergarten and first grade and will add a grade each year until it is K-5. All children are welcome, especially those learning English.  The school will feature small classes; support for English Language learners; inclusion of students with special needs; a strong math, science and social studies curriculum; an extended school day and year; and music, foreign language, arts, and physical education. Applications are due April 2011.

There will be community meetings for interested parents at the Queens Public Library Broadway branch (40-20 Broadway) on January 13th at 6:30pm and January 29th at 3:00pm; the Long Island City branch (37-44 21st St.) on February 7 at 6:00pm; and at the Jackson Heights branch (35-51 81st St.) on March 12th at 3:00pm. Contact the school for more info at: info@ownf.org and visit the website: http://owncs2.ownf.org/.
