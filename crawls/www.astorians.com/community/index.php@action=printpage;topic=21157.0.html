Hello fellow Astorians,
 New York Dog Nanny is happy to get the word out about our dog walking and boarding services to the Astoria area.  Serving this area since 2010, we are PET CPR & First Aid Certified as well as Insured.  Our approach to dog walking is simple--- we are tech-savvy individuals who cater to the working professional, first time dog owners, or long-time Astorians who've never been able to find a trustworthy person to watch their pet so that they can go on vacation.


Our dog walking services are based completely around the comfort, security, and mental/physical well-being of your favorite four legged family member.  We strongly encourage front-lead harnesses for all dogs, never ever yell at dogs , yank leashes, or use cruel techniques to walk your pet (unless they are about to be hit by a car).  We have a tried an true approach to dog walking that draws upon mental and physical agility to make sure that your pet is happy, rested and healthy for you by the time you get home.


We also offer season-based walks/playtime. Meaning-- in the summer months we are happy to play with your pet in your home if it's too hot outside.  In the winter and rain we are careful to wipe their paws and make sure they still get their exercise.
