I haven't been thrilled with a lot I've tried at Martha's, but, as I tend to say every time I talk about Martha's, I love their buttercream cupcakes. Their pastries aren't quite as divine as those from La Guli or St. Honore, but the cupcakes make me happy (they aren't as cloying as a lot of their other stuff), plus the store just looks and smells really nice, which I think adds to the dessert experience. I like getting some of the coffee drinks too. Next time you want to use up your dessert indulgence units, go for the buttercream cupcakes and see what you think. I particularly like the peanut butter one, but my roommate had the strawberry one and loved it.
