Title: Walking Tour of Astoria Park
Post by: greentiger on May 18, 2012, 06:31:46 AM
 
[font=]ASTORIA PARK AND POOL[/font]
[font=]GAHS Walking Tour in Astoria Park – Saturday, May 26, 2012 at 11:00 am[/font]
[font=] [/font]
[font=]Come join us for a walking tour of the entire Astoria Park to include topics such as the earlier inhabitants of the area, the old mansions, the East River, the treacherous Hell Gate, the two mighty bridges above the park, the great Astoria Pool, the trees, the wildlife, and a butterfly garden!  [/font]Members of Green Shores NYC will join us along the route to discuss upcoming events and plans for the park’s future. Meet at NE corner of Astoria Park at Ditmars Blvd. and 19th Street. The tour will be about three hours. Wear sturdy shoes and bring water.  There are two restroom stops in the park and light refreshments from local vendors along the way.  Call for reservations 718-278-0700
[font=] [/font]
[font=]Fee: $10.00 for GAHS, Astoria Park Alliance and Green Shores NYC members, $15.00 for non-members[/font]
