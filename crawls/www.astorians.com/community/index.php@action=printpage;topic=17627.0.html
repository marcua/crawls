My children currently go there.  (one in 4th & one in K).  The tuition that you mentioned is annual.  As someone else quoted, there are tons of fundraisers through out the year to keep the tuition down.  The fundraisers are not mandatory - you do what you can when you can.   Also, as someone else mentioned it is a good idea to register with the parish at least a year before your child is ready to go to school.  If you contribute to the parish church (with the envelope system) you do get a discount.  Hope that helps!
