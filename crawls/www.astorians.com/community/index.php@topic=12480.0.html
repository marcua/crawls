This THIRSTY THURSDAY @ Bungalo
"VP Mud Wrestling: Palin vs Biden - Take 1"



    We here at Bungalo don't officially support Political candidates. But we feel it's our moral and civic duty to provide you with a place where you and your friends can down some drinks and have a night when it's actually OK to talk about politics in a bar.
    We invite you to watch the VPILF and your standard issue old Grey haired Politician go at it to the backdrop of house beats and alcoholic beverages. This should be a very interested show down. Well, unless they replace Palin with a body double (Maybe they'll put in Tina Fey?) and Joe Biden stays sober.
    Once the dust settles and the fight is over, we're going to turn up the music and proceed with a good ol' Thursday night at Bungalo.
     
The debate starts at: 9pm. Hope to see you there!
