I thought that the credit card company had to use the three reporting agencies: Experian, Transunion, or Equifax to pull your report.  I don't understand why they can't simply run a new report.  By the way, how many years ago was your last negative credit hit?  It takes normally 7 years to get negative credit off of the report and bankruptcies usually take 10 years.
