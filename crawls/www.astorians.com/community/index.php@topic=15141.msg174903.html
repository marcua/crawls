We just moved in last week.

I never heard of that no TV thing.  The main objective is to be respectful and not blast music late at night and stuff like that.

The rules are strict mainly in regards to renovations, to ensure the integrity of the structures and that nobody is doing anything that could cause harm/damage to the building and neighbors.  Other than that, it's not bad at all.

There are a lot of kids and many people in the community are very friendly...so if you have children or are planning on it I would say that it is a good place to be.

The grounds are well kept, the building common areas are very clean, security patrols actually patrol, and the co-op is run well.
