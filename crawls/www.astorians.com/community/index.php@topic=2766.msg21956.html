Salad :
Lettuce
carrots (sliced thin)
Beets (raw, sliced thin)
(add sliced red onion, lemon juice, olive oil, salt and pepper)

QUICHE:
beet greens
swiss chard
humus (whole garbanzo beans or the spread? Humus = garbanzo in hebrew/arabic)
eggs
various cheeses

Cook rice with dried apricots and nuts (two and 1/4 cups of water per cup rice) - Instant Pilaf when ready - just add olive oil, salt and pepper to taste.

In separate pot, cook the red lentils and the brown lentils (when cooked, spice with salt, pepper, cumin and a hint of lemon juice and a dash of olive oil)

and when you're all done, don't forget to invite the neighbors over for dinner
