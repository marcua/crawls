Title: Re: Wedding dress cleaning-suggestions?
Post by: jenriet on April 12, 2009, 01:10:29 PM
I got mine cleaned in another state, so I can't speak of where to get it done, but make sure you store it in a muslin garment bag or stuff it with acid free tissue in a museum archival box. I just got mine professionally cleaned and then have it hanging in a closet in the garment bag. Do not let anyone store your dress in plastic - it could ruin it. You should get your dress cleaned ASAP too - even if there are no visible stains now, your body oils, sweat, etc. need to be gotten out of the dress before it settles into the fabric and stains come out later on. I want to use mine for a trash the dress shoot later on, so I researched this thoroughly to make sure it would still be gorgeous when the time comes.  :mrgreen:
