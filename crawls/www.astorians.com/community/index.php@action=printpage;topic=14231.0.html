Title: Re: Ballet Classes for Toddlers in Astoria
Post by: Debbie on March 16, 2009, 03:54:34 PM
Be very careful when selecting a Ballet School.  Ask the question - when do you put the girls in toe shoes?  If they give you a specific age, get out of there.  Toe Shoes should not be used until the child's back and stature can support them, otherwise they will have trouble in the future.  Also, ask about the ballet technique they teach.  Could be Cechetti, Russian, Royal Academy etc.  Many schools tend to put kids in tutus and teach them to kick a bit.  This is fine for fun, but if they begin with the toe shoes early on, it is not a good sign.

I once went to a class to see what Helen Skarla, a local school and I was shocked seeing little girls arching their backs, dancing on their toes without any guidance or structure.

I was a student of Cechetti and Royal Academy for many years and I'm a bit passionate about this.
