i used to tutor immigrants for ESL while i was an AmeriCorps*VISTA.

we went to a work shop on those who tutor/teach ESL, and one of the techniques that they showed us was to bring in a magazine, or a newspaper with lots of pictures, and start talking about the pictures.

like if it's a fashion/women's magazine and there's a picture of woman modeling clothes, etc...

Who is she?  Where is she going? How old is she?

and then other people can jump in and respond, therefore practicing their pronounciation and spontaneously asking questions.

then you can reverse and have someone else ask questions.  

and you can go on making up stories about the person etc, therefore building up vocabulary.

just a thought, if someone wants to try that in the cafe or somewhere else. of course, it would be great if there was someone who speaks fluently in the language everyone is trying to learn.
