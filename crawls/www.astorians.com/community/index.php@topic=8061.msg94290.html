Banks today no longer have security guards. They simply have various anti-theft policies and proceedures, and take their chances. 20-30 years ago every bank branch had at least one security guard. Today they pass their higher insurance premiums and actual losses onto their customers with higher fees.

Don't worry about your money though, you are insured by the FDIC in case of bank theft, or bankruptcy by your bank, unless you have over $100,000 in a single account. Pretty brazen of the thieves, they sound desperate to try to knock off a small residential bank branch. Most bank robberies today are in the way of identity thefts, ATM frauds, and check and wire frauds. Old fashioned bank robberies have too much risk for thieves.
