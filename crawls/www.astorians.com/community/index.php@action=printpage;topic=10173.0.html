Title: Arcos
Post by: Yaxpac on April 16, 2008, 04:06:45 PM
I had been meaning to hit Arcos since they opened and finally had the chance to dine there last weekend.  We arrived around 9pm and it was crowded.  I always wanted to try the suckling pig on the menu so i asked the waiter how it is...he said they just got written up in the Village Voice about that dish, their house specialty.  Well, the pig was fantastic.  I love pig roasts and this was fit the bill.  The skin was nice and browned with a perfect crisp to it.  The gave me a hearty portion with diffferent parts of the tasty swine.  It even included what i guess was part of the rib section as it had several little rib bones.  The dish is $16 and so worth every penny.  I can't wait to have it again.  They serve it with some sort of brown gravy/au jus that was great too.
