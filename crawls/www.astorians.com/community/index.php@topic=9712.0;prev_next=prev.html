THE SHOW IS TAPING IN ASTORIA ON TUESDAY, MARCH 4th

GREAT CELEBRITY GUESTS
 
FANTASTIC AUDIENCE RAFFLE PRIZES—MULTIPLE IPODS, GIFT CARDS

AND OTHER BIG TICKET PRIZES

CBS is very excited to re-introduce one of the greatest game shows in the history of American television to a whole new audience.  With new twists, higher stakes, a one million dollar grand prize and the one and only REGIS PHILBIN hosting, CBS is confident viewers will tune in to re-discover the format that helped to define the game show genre as we know it.
 
The new MILLION DOLLAR PASSWORD will feature two teams comprised of one celebrity and one contestant, each competing to guess a password revealed only to the studio audience and viewers at home. The team with the highest score will then have to decide whether or not to keep their earnings or move on to a tension-building final round, where one word can potentially win a one million dollar grand prize. Tons of fun to watch in the studio live.

The show is filmed at
Kaufman Astoria Studios
3412 36th Street,
Astoria, NY 11106
The entrance is on 34th Avenue between 35th and 36thStreet (at the blue awning labeled Kaufman).

For tickets to the show, please email milliondollarpassword@broadwayvideo.com with the following information in the subject line and body of your email. You must be 18 years or older and live in NYC or already be in NYC at the time of the show to be eligible for tickets.  (Audience members will need ID as proof of age and will be signing an audience release at the show.)

1. Your full name
2. The number of tickets that you want (max. 2 tickets unless you book as a group)
3. Your phone number
4. Email address
5. How you heard about the show (email, website, friend, school)
6. Your Age range (for example 35-40, 18-25, etc)
7. WHICH SHOW:
    SHOW A: Tuesday, March 4th, 3:30 PM (start time) to 6:30 PM (show ends)
    We need the audience to arrive no later than 3:00 PM for this show.

(Example: Helen Newton / 2 tickets / 718-555-1555 / helennew@gmail.com / friend / 25-30 / SHOW A)
 
You will receive a confirmation email with all of the ticket information within a day or two of your rsvp but no later than Saturday, March 1st. If you do not hear from us by this time it is most likely because ticket supplies have been exhausted. The audience is not large (200 people approximately) so please don't ask for tickets unless you intend to use them. Thank you.
 
Audience members will receive fantastic prizes via audience raffles throughout the show.
All prizes will be given out at end of the show.
