In reading this message thread on Astorians, I am surprised by the level of vitriol against Judy and ignorance on the issue of noise pollution.

Having founded a noise pollution awareness group, I often receive messages from desperate people who are dealing with noise problems so severe they have to have to sleep in motels at night or self-medicate.  Not everyone can afford to move away or "just get used to it."

When people complain about noise, they sometimes become the target of others (especially those who are liable for the time and cost to mitigate or abate the problem) who either attempt to summarily dismiss the issue or invalidate the complainant because it is so much easier than actually addressing the issue.

I hope the church will negotiate and an amicable solution can be reached.  For the rest of you who disparage Judy, it is cowardly and speaks volumes about your contempt for your own neighbors.
