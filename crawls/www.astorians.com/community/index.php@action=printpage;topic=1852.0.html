i've been hearing a bunch of great things about locale's brunch these days though i personally have never tried it.

went to locale w a friend last night for the first time and had the seafood specials then stayed for some drinks. the seafood was excellent -- fresh, perfectly grilled and very tasty. the dark chocolate mousse cake was so great we seriously scraped off every last bit of the chocolate. the drinks were good too (tried the french martini, the expresso martini and the chocolate martini.)

give it a try. i'll be back for brunch without a doubt.

So right! The fried artichokes are the best thing about this place. Some people think the vibe is too pretentious; I think its ok.
