I have been using http://www.retailmenot.com/ for all my online shopping for a couple of months now and I'm happy to say, most of the times I've found good deals.  When I don't find deals there, I'll google "[name of the store] coupons" and 8 out of 10 times I find a discount code.

My family and I have decided that this year we are just going to give gifts to the 17 and under crowd and for the adults we are doing an exchange where we each have to spend $30.  We did this last year and really had a blast at the family dinner, because people did all sort of creative things to complete the $30.  It was a nice alternative to the consumerism of the season and, particularly this year, it might help to ease the anxiety for those of us who have been hit with the current economic fiasco (and who hasn't?)

I also buy on ETSY because I like the idea of giving unique gifts.
