Best Yet Market has a very good selection including Majic Hat, Blue Moon, LI Blue Point (Toasted Lagers are great) as well as imports.
Almost every Key Food has Franzeskaner Hefer Weiss. And of course (Support your local Brewery) Brooklyn Beers all of which are great. BTW I recommend visiting the Brewery .

Lucky Beverage on 21 St off 30th Ave has probably the best selection of International's including Chimay and Duvel and many too numerous to mention - along with micro brews and exotics that seem never ending.

The Beer Garden of course has a great selection going out for bars and stuff. SUnswick on 35th Ave near the Studios has something like 64 beers on draft.  Hellgate Social has an excellent menu of beers for your indulgence at reasonable prices.

I'm something of a conneuesior, feel free to contact me for more info, but this is a good start.

If you want the best substitute the city has to offer, you've got to travel to Brooklyn.  Spuyten Duyvil on Metropolitan Ave. has the best selection of Eastern European beers in the city.  They also serve platters of cheese and meat that are sliced right in front of you.  The staff knows their beer like pros and match you up with what you're looking for and they'll even help you pick out the right snack to go along with it.  I don't think they'll sell you cases to go, but they may put you in contact with their distributor.
