it is indeed astoria soundworks (www.astoriasoundworks.com) and they do have practice studios equipt with pianos. i practice there weekly and I couldn't ask for a nicer bunch of people.

a word of warning about the equipment, though: it gets used a lot and generally displays a much wear and tear. it also varies widely from studio to studio (no matter what they may tell you) so if at first you get something crappy, try another room or ask for a replacement.
