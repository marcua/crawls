Hi!
I'm a newcomer to these boards, and a three-year resident of Astoria, and I just wanted to drop even MORE love on Valverde!  My fiance and I went there for the first time for dinner after ordering takeout from them several times, and we weren't disappointed.  He had the papadelle with bacon, cherry tomatoes, and red pepper and it was absolutely astounding.  I had the seafood risotto which was also delicious, and a glass of Chianti that the owner recommended which was delightful and perfect with my food.  We split the creme caramel which was so light and delicious and perfect to end our meal.  The staff is so amazingly sweet--we'd only been there once, but they remembered us when we returned to try brunch a week later before going Christmas shopping!  Brunch is a must--their coffee is phenomenal (and I love coffee), the Crostini dish with Parmesan and scrambled eggs is delicious, and their bread is out of this world!
Better than all of that, while we were there the owner told us he'd just installed a fresh pasta machine, which was kind of like telling my fiance that the Mets were going to win the World Series next year (who doesn't love fresh pasta?).  We're definitely going to be back! :mrgreen:
