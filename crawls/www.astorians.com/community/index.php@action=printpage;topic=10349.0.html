Title: Spring Math (2008)
Post by: Starwind51 on May 05, 2008, 12:31:58 AM
This year Spring will have 93 days, including the beginning and ending days that are shared with the end of Winter and the beginning of Summer.

Early Spring was from Tu March 20 to Sa April 19, 31 days.
Middle Spring started on Su April 20 and will last till Tu May 20, 31 days.
Late Spring will begin on W May 21 and go until F June 20, 31 days.

Why I Do the Math


Some years ago, the Country Time Lemonade Company ran a commercial that said, “This year Summer will be a short   #   days”.  And I thought to myself, “isn’t it   #  days long every year?  Then I realized, since the solstice occurs on different days over the years, summer would have a different number of days each year, and hoping to make the most of it, I started counting the days.  As for why I divide the seasons into 3 parts, New York City has 12 seasons a year, not 4, and it’s useful to know when they will be occurring in order to anticipate temperature changes and planning what to wear.
