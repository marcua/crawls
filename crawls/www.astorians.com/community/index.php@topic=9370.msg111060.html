I did it when I was 24. Recovery time for me was 2 weeks. Very sore and tired at first,
but after a couple days I was ok. Couldn't eat though - that ice cream myth is probably
just for kids. I don't remember getting any pain meds but since I was hardly taking in any
food/liquid, don't know if I could have tolerated them anyway.

Stock up on some good DVDs, a book or two and anything else you can do that doesn't
require talking. Took me about 8 days to sound like myself again - now that's the frustrating
part. Not being able to speak was a killer, although my family was very happy.

Good luck!
