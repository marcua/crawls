The very first place I thought of was Chez Josephine. They have a private room, a terrific Sunday brunch menu and a whole lot of ambiance.

The second place that comes to mind is Angus McIndoe. They also have a private room, a really nice Saturday and Sunday brunch menu and is full of class.

There is, of course, the New York maven of private room parties; Joan Hamburg. She does a call-in show on WOR 710 AM, weekdays from 9:00–11:00 AM. You might get lucky and get through to her: 800-321-8828.
