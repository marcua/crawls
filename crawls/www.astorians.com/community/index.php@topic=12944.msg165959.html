My husband and I actually really liked Oleput, and we're really disappointed that it closed.  It was actually our favorite place in the 'hood to grab a drink.  Their wine menu was consistently good, and we loved the relaxed atmosphere.  Admittedly, we didn't go there enough to keep it open, and I'll tell you why.  It was a little pricey...more than we could afford as a regular thing.  But actually less pricey than we find Crescent & Vine.  The inconsistent hours did bug us a bit, too.  If we were picking a place to meet people, we wouldn't choose it, because it wasn't a sure thing that it'd be open.  Just wanted to add my two cents, because I'm genuinely disappointed that it's gone.
