NYC has specific laws regarding eradication of bed bugs by your landlord.  He is required to get the place completely exterminated and in the proper way for bed bugs.  He is also required to pay for it.   Here is some information regarding the laws and who to report him to:
http://www.nyc.gov/html/doh/bedbugs/html/info/owners_tenants.shtml

I feel your pain.  I have been in the same situation and had to inform my landlords of the law before they would take any action.  But I also knew that the whole building was infested and i could not count on my neighbors to report or allow full extermination.  It was the end of my lease and I moved out.  But it was a nightmare.  Here is a thread on bed bugs in Astoria.  It explains on how to get rid of them, etc.  It's hard but doable.

If I were you, I would contact the proper authorities, and take steps to move out.  I would also take every precaution to make sure you don't bring them with you.  Since he did not properly inform you of the bed bugs, I believe you may have a right to get your rent and your security back - failure to disclose history is also an offense.

Good luck.  I will also amend your posts to read the proper address.  - mod

WHAT? This is horrible and Im so sorry you are going through it. First of all they are obligated to let you know of an infestationw within a one year time frame. Its AGAINST the LAW for them not to. Second, bedbugs are considered a healthcode violation and your landlord is REQUIRED to treat the problem. Not just your unit, but all units in the building.
 
In the meantime, take a deep breathe. Yes this sucks. BUT its not the end of the world. I promise you that even though it feels like it is, it isnt. There is an end to this and you will one day again sleep through the night.
 
I would first take all of your clothing and wash it all in hot water and keep it in a large zip bag. Get rid of your mattress and frame and box spring. If you cannot do that right now, I would cover the bed in a bedbug proof cover and tape over the zipped side. This will prevent any bugs from going in or coming out. I know. gross. But.. Isolate the bed. meaning, keep it away from the walls or anything else. On each leg, get small dishes and I would put vaseline on the legs of the bed and leave the dishes full of baby powder or water. This will help prevent more buggies from coming onto your bed.
 
Your vaccum cleaner is your friend as is a steamer. You want as much as you can isolated before you can move. So if your clothes are not all being worn, I would get them washed, dried on high heat for an hour and put in zip bags and in storage if possible. I dont know if you moved furniture here but if you are renting a room just a bedroom set is a small loss compared to possibly bringing them with you.
 
Not all of NY is this crappy and I am sorry this happened to you. UGH. What a nightmare.
