If it's computer stuff you're looking for, I recommend Digitcom Technologies at 53 - 05 Broadway http://www.digitcomtech.com. It's not a big place, but it's geared toward computer do-it-yourselfers, so they have a good selection of items at very reasonable prices. The staff is very knowledgeable and friendly, and is happy to order anything you want if it's not in stock. I've been there about four or five times, and have had nothing but good experiences with them. They even have a decent selection of Mac compatible components, and add-ons, which for me is a big plus.
