Title: Green Space Blooms Annual Dance Festival
Post by: Dance Entropy on March 06, 2014, 11:46:50 AM
Green Space
 presents the 8th annual
 Green Space Blooms:
 A Queens Festival of Dance and Music at Green Space
 37-24 24th St., Ste. 301, Long Island City, NY
 April 10, 11, 12, and 13 at 8pm
 Family Matinee April 12 at 1pm
 
 A Queens celebration of new dance and music! The festival will take place at Green Space, on April 10, 11, 12, and 13 at 8 PM.
 Admission: Presale tickets $15, Tickets at the door $20.
 Family Matinee on April 13 at 1pm, $10 for all ages.
 Tickets available online at www.GreenSpaceStudio.org. (http://www.facebook.com/l.php?u=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.GreenSpaceStudio.org.%2F&h=zAQH46vNG&enc=AZMOmQroCI852KiNcVjZBimKsfd4_japxdRfpipUq5A35I9tYjqX4jQ0mV4kdYE7HmU&s=1)
 
 Green Space Blooms will feature 28 diverse choreographers, as well as resident company Valerie Green/Dance Entropy. Completing each evening is a live music post-performance party, sharing music from a different ethnic or classical tradition each night, curated by Open Music Education.
 
 "During the ‘Green Space Blooms’ festival, you can get the best of both worlds with a diverse assortment of dancers, choreographers, and musicians." – Boro Magazine
