The annual meeting is usually of more interest to activists who care deeply about liberty.
Unlike the monthly free events, it is a fundraiser with fee details below. Unless a member, I would recommend arriving at 12:30
Peace, Liberty, New Year wishes.

January 30, 2010
Manhattan Libertarian Annual Convention
Join us at our 2010 Annual Convention. 2010 is going to be a big year for New York City Libertarians and this is the way to get it started right.

Time: 11:00am – 5:00pm
Ukranian East Village
140 2nd Ave
New York, NY


Approx. 12:30 pm Lunch and Speaker Program
* City Council Member Dan Halloran 
Queens, District 19
Republican/C/I/L
http://council.nyc.gov/html/members/members.shtml


* New York Civic’s Henry Stern
Former Parks Commisioner

* Senate Candidate Randy Credico
Quote
Randy has been recognized by his peers for his work with the Kunstler Fund and received awards from Union Square, NAACP and Drug Policy Alliance.  Charles Grodin in his recent memoir said that Randy Credico is his “hero.” The late writer Olivia Goldsmith has said that Credico has sprung more women out of prison than anybody. Credico is the subject of Laura Kitelinger’s award winning documentary SIXTY SPINS AROUND THE SUN and is featured along with Andrew Cuomo and Russell Simmons in the documentary LOCKDOWN USA.

* Potential Candidate for Governor Warren Redlich
- Redlich is an upstate lawyer and town councilman. If he runs for statewide office and is a candidate, I expect he would have my vote for liberty.


11:00 am Business Meeting
We will elect new officers and conduct any other necessary business. The business meeting is free. You must be a paid up member to vote. Membership is $15.

The Lunch/Speaker Program is $40 in advance and $50 at the door.  Students with valid ID $30.

Payments can be mailed:

Manhattan Libertarian Party


or paid online through Paypal.
