Title: Re: Astoria Newsletter
Post by: stickb0y7 on August 22, 2008, 12:27:37 AM
if something does get started, i'd be happy to submit photos for use in such a newsletter.  i'm an amateur at photography, but going out to shoot photos with a purpose gives me some direction and motivation at least.

would this be something in print, or online?

i think defining the parameters is a good place to start, you already said possibly bi-monthly, i think the following things need to be thought out:

  • purpose - informational, editorial, advertisement, etc?
  • length
  • sections - you mentioned updates, local topics, events, restaurants, maybe clearly define these and stick with a few categories
  • contributors - accept contributions from the entire community, or have designated writers/reporters for certain sections?
  • editing - someone to pull this all together, suggest assignments or topics or events to cover

i've never been involved with a newsletter before, but these are the things that came to mind.  maybe i'm making this too complicated? all of the above doesn't have to mean a lot of work or people, just direction.  it only takes a few people to write a successful blog and they write and edit their own work and decide on the topics, but it works if they are all working toward the same goal.  sorry, i tend to ramble when i'm thinking - hope some of this helps :-)
