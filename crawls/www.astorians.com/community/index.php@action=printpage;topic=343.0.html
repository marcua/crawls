Title: More Meditation Classes in Astoria
Post by: andres21 on March 29, 2005, 06:12:11 PM
Presented by Chakrasambara Buddhist Center
Eight Steps to Happiness
The Buddhist way of loving kindness
with Luis Villalon

Sundays 12:00 - 2:00 pm

In this series based on Geshe Kelsang Gyatso's book 'Eight Steps to Happiness', we will look at how to train the mind in loving-kindness.  The more we are capable of opening our hearts to others, the more a natural happiness will arise inside.  Luis Villalon will guide us in this step by step method for bringing about a transformation of the heart ($10 per class).
 
At Grace Lutheran Church
31-20 21st Ave. (at 32nd st.)
Astoria
(use office entrance to the left of the main church entrance.)

April 3 - The Nature of Happiness
April 10 - The First Step: Love
April 17 - The Second Step: Enhancing Love
May 1 - The Potential for Change

Each class is self contained and can be taken individually. Anyone can benefit from these techniques, regardless of spiritual background.

If you have any questions please call 212 924 6706 or visit our website, www.meditationINnewyork.org
