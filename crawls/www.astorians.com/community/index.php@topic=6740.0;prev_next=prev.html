Drumming in Astoria Park
« on: June 07, 2007, 10:58:20 AM »
Last night at about 7:45 I went to Astoria Park to relax like I always do in the summer and noticed that it the peace and quiet was interrupted by loud drumming.  I looked to see where it was coming from and saw a group of about 6 guys in a circle on the grass under the Hell's Gate Bridge.  They just kept drumming and blowing a whistle.  It reminded me of half time at a football game or a marching band.  It was so loud that I had to put my finger in my ear to hear while talking on my cellphone.  This was still going on by about 8:40 PM so I decided to go home.   I was really upset by this because I felt that I couldn't enjoy the park in the same was as I had been enjoying it since I moved to Astoria 13 years ago.  I remember this drumming going on last week too and am wondering if this is going to be going on all summer long.  I feel that these people are very inconsiderate.  I'm sure that I must not be the only one who feels this way.  What about the people who live in that area.  I tried walking away, but still heard it.  I like music, but this is not music to me, it's just noise.  Does anyone else feel this way? :|
