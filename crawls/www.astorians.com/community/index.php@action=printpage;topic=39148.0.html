Title: Volunteer with NYC Parks Stewardship in Astoria on 2/13
Post by: Parks Stewardship on January 29, 2016, 11:29:48 AM
Volunteer with the Stewardship Team on Valentines Day weekend in Astoria to give your street trees some love and care! This work will improve the health and lengthen the life of these street trees, and make your neighborhood greener and more beautiful. Volunteers will be trained in litter removal, weeding, cultivating, and mulching techniques. Come dressed in sturdy boots or shoes, long pants, and clothing that can get dirty.

Time: 9:00am - 1:00pm
Meeting Location: Sean's Place (https://www.google.com/maps/place/Sean's+Place/@40.7606568,-73.9206269,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x89c25f395548beb3:0x33be6d28b4cf0791) on 38th Street between 31st Avenue and Broadway.

To get more information and register, visit: https://www.nycgovparks.org/reg/stewardship/1371 (https://www.nycgovparks.org/reg/stewardship/1371).
