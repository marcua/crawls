Title: Baru
Post by: petit hiboux on September 21, 2005, 10:11:31 AM
My husband and I ended up there on a night that I'd worked until 10 PM and seriously needed some pampering, wine, and mood lighting. They've got the mood lighting down, the wine was alright if overpriced, and the pampering, well. Our waitress was like Elizabeth Shue on SPEED.

My husband had the yellowtail tuna, he really liked it. Said it melted in his mouth. My sirloin was very well prepared and tender, and medium rare just like I like it. The mashed potatoes were a little stiff, not quite moist and buttery enough. The dessert was delicious - a warm chocolate cake.

Look, we spent a hundred bucks on that meal, I won't lie. It was expensive but it was also pretty good. It's a typical greek mafia eatery and I expect the service and quality will only go DOWN over time and the music was a little too loud (although she turned it RIGHT down when I asked, granted, there were only three other tables). The service wasn't rushed, but probably not too knowledgable either.

They're the same people that own Plaza Cafe, down the street across from Athens Park, so it's basically what my husband and I lovingly refer to as the Greek Mafia - they won't ever need to close their doors so they don't have any impetus to really hit top marks with their non "community" customers.

But I will have to say, our meal was probably a 7/10.
