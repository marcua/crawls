Re: New Place on 33 and Ditmars-- Just Burgers
« Reply #12 on: May 04, 2011, 12:34:08 PM »
It opened yesterday.  We got burgers to go last night.  They were SLAMMED - line out the door at 7pm.

I'll withhold judgment until they've been open for a bit and had time to get things smoothed out.  Not bad for an opening night.  It's a Petey's-style place rather than Bareburger - thin, fast food-style burgers that can't be cooked rare or medium.  I think the meat quality and taste is better at Petey's.  I like the toppings options at Just Burgers - about a dozen free toppings and spreads, and about a dozen more for $1.  I liked that the toasted bun wasn't a generic squishy flavourless kind, but it was a little too thick and dense.

They offer beef, turkey and veggie burgers (quinoa and "daily veggies"), with or without cheese ($.90 extra).  Regular and cajun white potato fries, sweet potato fries and onion rings.  The onion rings looked absolutely fantastic and the people I asked said they were as good as they looked.  The sweet potato fries were really good - steak fry-sized wedges that were fried enough to have a nice crunchy crust.  First time I've ever had sweet potato fries that were crunchy enough for me.  Four flavours of shakes (vanilla, chocolate, strawberry and -- oreo?). 

One thing I hope they change:  their menu board lists "mini" and "regular" burgers.  I thought the mini would be like a slider and the regular would be, well, regular.  Turns out that "mini" is a single patty and "regular" is a double patty (which is how it's listed on the receipt).  A "regular" - aka double was way too much for me and I would have ordered a "mini" had I known what that meant.  They need to change or clarify their language.

Mini burgers start at $4.15 and regular burgers start at $5.15.  Fries are in the $3-4 range depending on type and size, and shakes are somewhere around there too.
