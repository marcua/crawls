Thanks for your concern but this is a legal apartment in our 2 family house.  If you are interested in our apt, please PM me and I can give you the address.

PS You can check to see if an apt is legal or not by looking at the Certificate of Occupancy (CO), available to anyone at the Department of Buildings website as part of their Buildings Information System  In order to get a CO, you have to pass a plumbing, electrical and structural inspection and have DOB approved plans on file.

To look at a CO go to:
 
http://www.nyc.gov/html/dob/html/bis/bis.shtml

On the right side of the screen type in the borough, house number and street name.

It will bring up all the permit and certificate information.  Then click on Certificate of Occupancy and it will bring up a PDF.

You can use this system to see that work was approved, also look at whether the building ever had a violation, if it passed its inspections, etc.
