Title: Music for Aardvarks Free Classes Next Week
Post by: AllisonTartalia on April 20, 2007, 02:12:35 PM
Music for Aardvarks, the hip, rockin' music class for babies and toddlers has arrived in Astoria!

Music for Aardvarks and Other Mammals is a class geared toward children aged 3 months thru 5 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, and instrumental jam sessions. The original material used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.  To hear some of the songs and learn more, visit www.MusicForQueens.com!

Next week we'll be opening our classes to anyone who would like to come for a free trial!

Classes are held at Steinway Reformed Church
21-65 41st Street
(at Ditmars Blvd.) :mrgreen:

Class times are Monday at 10am and 11am
Friday at 10am and 11:30am

Please rsvp to reserve a spot, as space is limited by emailing info@MusicForQueens.com or leaving a message at 212-769-6812.

Our Spring Session begins on April 30 and will run for 8 weeks.  For more information, to hear songs, or register for classes, please visit www.MusicForQueens.com.

Many Thanks!
