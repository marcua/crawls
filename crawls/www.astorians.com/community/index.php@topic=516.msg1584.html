Just wanted to say hello and to introduce myself here to all of you.  I will be moving to Astoria in early August from Long Island.  LI is not my native area but I have been living there for the last four years studying music at Stony Brook University.  Now that I've gotten my doctorate, I'm moving to Queens to be closer to the city's music scene.  I'm a harpsichordist and erstwhile singer that plays and sings both early music and new music; I teach as well.  I also love to eat and cook, and am really excited about the availability of good food and ingredients in Astoria.  I run, bike, and swim, so Astoria Park looks fabulous.  Don't own a car (I have lived on LI for 4 years without a car!) but walk and bike everywhere.  I think Astoria will be a good place for me.

Anyway, I look forward to exploring Astoria.  Perhaps I will meet some of you at some point, too.

Meg
