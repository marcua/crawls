Title: Young Jews Shmooze at ACI!
Post by: Your Astoria Shul on February 15, 2011, 11:53:36 AM
   YOUNG JEWS SCHMOOZE

Friday evening
Feb. 18 at 6 PM


Song, Spirit, and Supper
Welcome Shabbat among friends!

Join us for a great Friday night as we share a delicious Shabbat meal, followed by a brief, musical and meaningful celebration of Shabbat.

Families with young kids, couples, singles -- all welcome to meet, mingle, and greet Shabbat together!

Don't miss this month's Schmooze! We really do have a great time welcoming Shabbat with song and spirit. Dairy dinner of fresh, delicious home-made salads. Modest suggested donation to help defray food costs: $10 pp, $20 per family; ACI members: $5 pp, $10 per family.


Pre-register by going to the ACI office (open Mon-Fri 9am - 1pm), or RSVP'ing to astoriashul@gmail.com, or just join us at the last minute!

Looking forward to seeing you!
