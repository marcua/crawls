Astoria > Flushing Meadow > Astoria > Crescent & Vine recap:

It was a great, relaxng ride and probably the perfect weather for one. Three of us ended up riding, following the Ditmars > 23rd Ave > Ditmars route to the Promenade then cutting through the Shea Stadium parking lot to Flushing Meadow and Corona Park. While there's no dedicated bike lane and a few MTA busses nearly clipped us it's definitely doable for just about any level of rider.  We finished the ride with an ice-cold reward of Lagunitas Pils at the styling-new Crescent & Vine. Good times.

Looking forward to the next one!   :mrgreen:
