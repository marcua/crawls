Title: La Vita Mia closed?
Post by: 60decibels on July 03, 2016, 06:33:32 PM
First New Good One closes and now La Vita Mia pizza has been shuttered for at least a week. When I first moved to 24th Ave, they made pretty reliable pizza and really good baked ziti and lasagna. The quality has declined a little since then, but not enough to make me stop going. Anyone know what's going on?
Title: Re: La Vita Mia closed?
Post by: dawnlovesthierry on July 13, 2016, 02:17:49 PM
So I caught one of the guys working on La Vita Mia and asked him if they would be re-opening.  He said yes, definitely.  Right now they are renovating to make the sitting area nicer and more inviting for customers to eat in, rather than getting their order and running out.  I look forward to seeing what they do.  It definitely needed a face lift!!  As you noted, it was better a few years back.  That was when Claudio owned it.  After he sold it, it did go down hill a little.  Not sure if this is another new owner or the same.


As for New Good One, a few months back I caught one of the workers who looked like he was cleaning out some belongings and asked him why they closed.  He told me the landlord doubled their rent and there was no way they would be able to make it, so they just had to close.  Let's see how long that space remains empty.  I'm guessing a long time.
