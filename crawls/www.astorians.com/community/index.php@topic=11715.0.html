Re: Anyone try Kafene?

Personally, I really enjoy it there.  They always give me coupons for free drinks including coffee, mimosas, and wine.  I've never had a problem redeeming them.  I'm at the point where at least 1 hostess recognizes me and knows that I have an iced coffee with almost every meal, so I'm a fan of the place.

I think the food is delicious.  However, my only complaint would be that I can't expect to get the same dish twice.  Having ordered things multiple times now, I've gotten different versions of the same dish a couple times, though all of them were tasty.  Sometimes, you just want it to be what you expected, even if it is good.

Regardless, I would recommend them to anyone.  They have great coffee.  Also, the service has improved dramatically- if anything, they're over-attentive now.  The reason that the above posters may have had issues with service is because they switched menus a few weeks ago- it took the hostesses a while to learn it, but now they have it down.  The owner takes time to ask your name and what you think of the place- everyone's very warm and friendly.  Hope you enjoy it!
