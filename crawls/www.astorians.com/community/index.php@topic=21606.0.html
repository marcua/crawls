Buon Appetit Deli has been around for quite a while.  It's not a new place and it's definitely not pretentious.

The guy who owns Buon Appetit is leaving (I don't know when) to focus on a place he has or is opening on Long Island.  The guys from Joe's Pork Store are going to be combining the businesses to keep both of them going.  They will still make the same sausages they've always made, but perhaps expand some since now they have a kitchen that is bigger than their entire old store.

Don't get me wrong - it's a different vibe and I miss going into the little shop.  But I'm glad they're still around in some form, and I'm also glad that Buon Appetit isn't closing - their heros are great.
