Hi Folks,
I am currently looking for a roommate to join my awesomely located apartment in Astoria.

Here are the stats:
They are two rooms attached to one another in the front of our apartment building. There is a leafy green tree right outside the bedroom window (hey, this was a factor for me...). One room has two windows and is roughly 11x13, while the smaller room attached is 11x8 with one window and a small closet. They've both served as bedrooms in the past, but I am looking to cut down the number of roommates, so I'm hoping to rent both together as a bedroom and office. It is a really nice space.

We live above a Brazillian restaurant, hair salon, TWO laundromats, a low-key bar/restaurant, and a store that sells Grecian statues--you know, for all your statue needs. There are also two bodegas: one on either corner (the one to the right makes the best coffee in the world. Or in Astoria. Or on the block. I don't know, I'm not a fan of the hyperbole--but it's damn good coffee.). The Steinway stop is only 3 short blocks away: roughly a 3-4 minute walk for me, and I tend to lollygag, (R,V,G--34th Avenue entrance).

Price--$900 for the two rooms together.

Why is it so cheap, you ask? Because we've lived here for 6 years, and the rent has barely increased in that time. The apartment features a HUGE kitchen--the biggest I've seen in Astoria--a living room (12x11), a small back porch (we call it "the lanai" out of love for "The Golden Girls") rooftop access, and of course, a bathroom.

We control heat/hot water, and pay for it ourselves, which is kind of a perk, as the thermostat is in our kitchen. No sweltering/freezing days and nights in our apartment. Currently, utilities will run 75-100/mo per person, depending on the season (that also includes gas/electric and cable/wireless internet). Should this be too much, we are willing to get rid of cable, if you are not a TV kind of person.

Also, our landlords are noisy. Noisy in a unique way. So we ask that you be comfortable with noisy. The pro of this, clearly, is that you can be noisy as well--they're not going to complain. They will also likely try to feed you homemade Italian food, which is delicious, but fattening. If you care about fattening. Of course, you could always decline, but I've never had that kind of will power.

US: we are two lovely ladies, artists/actresses/musicians with a SERIOUS sense of play. We have occasional parties, but more along the lines of the holiday soiree, because then we get to use the word "soiree". One of us is frequently touring, so for the most part it will only be two folks sharing the space.

YOU: Please have an awesome sense of humor, and know who "The Golden Girls" are. Being nice would be helpful--we like friendly folk. So friendly and funny--together. Easygoing is helpful. Messy is fine, dirty is not (i.e., your room can be a mess, you can leave mail on the table and books on the couch, but PLEASE wash your dishes and throw out your trash. The primary goal is to avoid prevent fungus) GLBTQ Friendly, open to other races and religions--basically, friendly to everyone. Unless they suck. And then you can be mean. Non-smoker, or someone who can stick to smoking outdoors. Probably in your 20's or 30's, but if you meld with us, age is no issue. 

You would need to pay the first month's rent, along with 2 months security. However, if you need some time to come up with the security, we can negotiate.

Please call 607.368.1432, or email me at meganmelnyk@gmail.com if you are interested. :-D
