It just might be in your best interest to break that steamer trunk open, and catalog what's in it. You might be able to list them (Craigslist, eBay, etc.) and make a few well-earned bucks. Pull the trunk over to the computer, list them in Excel, and hit the alphabetize button. IMHO, no-one is going to take them *all* off your hands because they don't know what they're getting. I did the same thing a few years ago with vinyl records that I inherited from my parents and my sister. I combined the collections, cataloged them, and sold the lot to a dealer. I made enough off that sale to buy a car in 2001. Think about it.
