As some of you may know, I am an actor/producer living in Astoria and I have been developing some new concepts for television series.

The New York Television Festival had a pitch contest - my writing partner (Marissa) and I entered one of our concepts and we made it into the semi-finals! The show is called ASSISTED LIVING and its basically a cross between Golden Girls and Desperate Housewives and it all takes place in an assisted living community. Its gonna be hilarious - but we need your help.

It is now up to the public to decide so on Friday August 25th, we need you to go to http://tv.msn.com/nytvf/contest/pitch and vote for Joe and Marissa, Assisted Living.

We need your vote! As it seems right now you can do multiple votes so the more you vote the better:)

If we get into the top 2 for that day, we make it to the semi-finals where we get to pitch our concept to executives and have a chance to win a development deal with IFC!

I'll send out a reminder on Friday as well. Just wanted to give you a heads up- and remember, Friday is the ONLY day you can vote for us. So get your vote in by midnight Pacific Standard Time (East Coast has up to 3a.m. to vote) or it won't count.

Thanks everyone!!! :-D
-Joe
