Title: OPEN CALL FOR LOCAL ARTISTS - VISUAL, MUSIC, VIDEO, WRITERS, ACTORS...!
Post by: audreyLICmag on February 06, 2012, 04:31:52 PM
Hi all!

This is an OPEN CALL & announcement for the 2012 LIC ARTS OPEN festival, a weeklong festival in Long Island City in May highlighting our area's incredible talent.

If you're a visual artist, photographer, writer, video artist, musician, actor, poet, we want you to get involved!

Check out the website HERE (http://www.licartsopen.org/) for more info. Come to a meeting and network, write an email if you have questions, fill out a submission form to submit your project/art/event for inclusion, just find a way to SHARE YOUR WORK in this festival especially if you've never done it before!

**SPECIAL CALL**

I'm specifically looking for local writers - poetry and short stories/fiction/non-fiction who want to read their work AND artists who want to show their work in a special hybrid event. Email me! (audrey.dimola@gmail.com)
