Title: Green Apple Supermarket on 21st Street
Post by: Starwind51 on July 13, 2016, 07:25:49 PM
38-50 21st Street
M - Sa 8 a.m. - 9:30 p.m.
Su till 8:30 p.m.

phone number 718 707 0887
(no website, Facebook page seems to pertain to other locations & be outdated)



Formerly the site of Pep Boys


I don't get out and about often, so this place was in business for a few months before I even knew it was there.  I was passing by in a car as a passenger so I couldn't get out to investigate, and I already have my preferred places to shop.  Recently I checked for reviews on line, that's how I knew it was open longer than I would have guessed.  Most of the reviews were very favorable, but one person hoped they would increase their security because he or she observed a number of people exiting the store without paying.  Considering how close it is to the Queensbridge Housing Project, that's not a hopeful sign.


Looking through a backlogged accumulation of store flyers I realized they've been sending out flyers for a while, but I don't think anyone from Astorians has mentioned going to this store.  The flyers show famous brand names, and Krasdale products.  I've tried Krasdale foods on 3 different occasions years apart and consider it a hallmark of pure crap.  Reviews by shoppers that sounded believable praised their fresh produce, meats, and fish departments.  One of these days, when the weather is nice, I'll go over with my shopping cart, but if you have an air conditioned car, they have parking.  I'm hoping someone else will go and tell us what you think and what you're comparing it to.  As an incentive, I guess, their flyers boast smoothies & fruit salads.
