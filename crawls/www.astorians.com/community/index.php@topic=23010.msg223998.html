What's up fellow Astorians?


I recently moved into the Ditmars/Steinway area (as indicated in previous posts) and have myself quite a large backyard.


I decided to start a vegetable garden despite having 0 experience. My chances of failure are high... Despite this I am determined to make s___ grow!


I have planted four varieties of tomatoes, eggplant, red and yellow bell peppers, basil, cilantro, some type of peppers I have never heard of, shallots, bok choi, and thats about it for now.


If anyone is interested I will post pictures...


If even half of this stuff I planted grows, I will have far too many vegetables, so I am open to suggestions as to what to do with all the extras.


If none of this stuff grows, I will live in shame and never return to the Astorians Forums.




Signing out...
