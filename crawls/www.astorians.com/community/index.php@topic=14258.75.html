Alba's is decent (that's the one on 37th and ditmars).

Frankies used to be really good (at least that's what I remember) but over the last several years seems to have gotten worse and worse.

Dino's on broadway was really good until the old Italian guy (the owner, I believe) died many years ago.  Even though his wife was still around the pizza was never the same.

Most slice places nowadays tend to use cheap ingredients and put little care into making pizza, so even though new york is known as a "pizza town" I find it's actually pretty difficult to find a decent slice place.  If you work in midtown, it's like a pizza wasteland.
