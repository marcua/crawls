Title: scrambled tofu
Post by: megc on November 27, 2005, 10:52:54 AM
I needed to use up my tofu, so I made scrambled tofu (http://www.theppk.com/recipes/dbrecipes/index.php?RecipeID=110) from a recipe off the post-punk kitchen site.  It was pretty good, and dare I say....delicious!  I had forgotten about this approach to tofu; there was a great breakfast restaurant in Berkeley that had a bunch of tofu scrambles that I liked on the menu.  I treated the tofu like scrambled eggs and put a little chipotle tabasco on it, but regular salsa would be good, too. I did adjust the recipe by adding only half the nutritional yeast, as I felt a whole 1/4 cup would overwhelm things.
