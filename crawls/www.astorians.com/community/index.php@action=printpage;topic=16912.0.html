Post by: alyssa726 on December 22, 2009, 03:15:04 PM
I'm taking my family to Stove on Christmas.  28th ave and 45th street.  really really delicious and nice.

My detailed review from my last visit, Christmas '07

Quote from: alyssa726 on December 25, 2007, 06:54:02 PM
went to stove for an early Christmas dinner today, and it was awesome.

first of all, i was impressed when we walked in: really nice bar, white table clothes, simple but lovely decor, all that, but not pretentious at all.

food was amazing.  they offered a Christmas menu. i had a salad, this neat sole, crab, shrimp thing, and the sherry trifle. my dad had the soup, filet mignon, and apple pie a la mode.   both came with a melon and prosciutto appetizer and entrees were served with mashed potatoes, sweet potatoes, green beans and turnips, (yes, turnips!  i hadn't had those since i was in Ireland 5 years ago, so i was really excited... they were delish, and after chatting with our server found out that Stove is Irish owned and the chef is from Kilkenny).

here's the best part, for two complete meals and a bottle of decent wine, the bill was under $100!  the set dinner was only $30something.

i would definitely recommend stove.  great, friendly service to boot.

thanks for bearing with me through my turnip digression.
