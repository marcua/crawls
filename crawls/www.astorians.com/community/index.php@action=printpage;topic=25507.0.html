I've been taking classes since July at Tone NY on 37th Street between Broadway & 31st Avenue.


Excellent studio run by two pilates instructors who really know their stuff. They have beginner classes,
intermediate and advanced mat classes as well as reformer classes.


They also offer SurfSet, which is a wonderful workout that's really fun.


They have intro specials for new students. I really like it  - classes are tough, but fun and you really see results.


Website is www.toneny.com


You can also look them up on Yelp - that's what got me to be honest. It was the only time I had ever seen
so many positive reviews for a business without one negative one.


The studio is bright and super clean and the level of instruction is excellent. I could go on and on, but Tone is
the real deal.
