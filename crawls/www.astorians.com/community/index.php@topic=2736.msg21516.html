All call to volunteers --

We are looking for a volunteer (or a few) to fill out the ranks in helping us get the leftover veggies to the food pantry drop off at 21st Street (near 36th Ave).

Ideally you'd be able to commit to every distribution -- but even just a few would be incredibly helpful.  You'll swing by around 7:30 to pick up leftovers and drive them to Hour Children (the previously mentioned food pantry).  You get in return, in addition to the satisfaction of having done a great deed, our thanks AND an understanding that in taking on delivery, you've fulfilled ALL of your required volunteer work. 

If you are interested in being involved in part of the food pantry shuttle -- full time or part time -- please let me know!  Write info at hellgatecsa dot com or shoot me a PM.

thanks,
Jayme -- Hellgate CSA, Site Coordinator
