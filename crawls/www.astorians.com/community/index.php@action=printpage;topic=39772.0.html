Title: Our own local politicians have allowed Astoria to be ruined
Post by: yippee1999 on June 05, 2017, 12:56:31 AM

Greed.  That's what it all boils down to.  It is positively stupefying...the pace of gentrification now in Astoria.  I just shake my head...every time I turn around, more construction sites...sometimes multiple new buildings going up on just one block alone!

This wouldn't be happening without our politicians allowing it to happen. 

Today I was walking down 30th Ave near 35th Street, and I saw an entire string of businesses GONE.  I'm pretty sure it was the block that used to have what I thought was a great, divey old-school billiards place (down the stairs, and I think run by Colombians?).... and right next to it was a charming old-school music store that sold guitars and also offered private lessons.  (I had always hoped to one day get some bouzouki lessons there!)  And now...unless I missed them and they were on another block?....I think they are both gone.  And what did I see but a new sign...something about some 'mixed-use building' going up, which is just a typical total b.s. way to get around restrictions, get tax write-offs etc.

Honestly, it makes me almost physically ill, because I know that at the core of this is pure greed.  Why oh why do so many people crave $$$ so much, at any cost, and not caring about the negative effects...driving out old-school businesses, long-term residents, and completely altering the character of the neighborhood?  Money will NOT make you happier!
