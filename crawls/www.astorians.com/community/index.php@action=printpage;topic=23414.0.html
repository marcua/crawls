Title: New Neapolitan pizzeria at 36-08 Ditmars Blvd -Tufino
Post by: akuban on August 31, 2012, 10:57:04 PM
There's a new pizzeria going in at 36-08 Ditmars -- in what used to be the American Woodworking space.

Some info here: http://queensnyc.com/2012/08/more-pizza-is-coming-to-ditmars-blvd-in-astoria/ (http://queensnyc.com/2012/08/more-pizza-is-coming-to-ditmars-blvd-in-astoria/)

But I'll add more: @AstoriaHaiku on Twitter talked to someone there who said it would be "different than anything" and "Italian style."

To me that screamed Neapolitan wood-oven pizzeria. Sure enough. I poked my head in today and saw they had a Stefano Ferrara wood oven all set up and looked pretty dang close to opening. The workers in there said "next month," and @Astoriahaiku quoted them as saying 2 to 3 weeks. They've done a lot of work already. Did a good job of keeping this a secret.

A Stefano Ferrara oven doesn't guarantee great pizza, but it's considered the Cadillac of pizza ovens. Whoever is opening this place shows signs of wanting to do it right.

Title: Re: New Neapolitan pizzeria at 36-08 Ditmars Blvd
Post by: lanseaux on December 18, 2012, 07:50:48 PM
First impression: this pizza is very, very good. The crust is fantastic - it's got a nice chew and great flavour. We had two pies: margherita and quattro formaggi. Flavours were balanced and the crust held up nicely in the margherita, although the cheeses overloaded it a bit in the quattro formaggi (not in taste, but in structure). We also had a mixed greens and endive salad with fennel, goat cheese, walnuts and pears, and finished the meal with their "tirami-choux" -- tiramisu flavours in a French-style cream puff. Both were very tasty.

They had some opening night service hiccups, but were very apologetic about everything, and we are certainly not holding any of it against them - you really have to expect issues on opening night. The important thing is that the food was excellent.

We have decided that we have to try every pie on the menu, and have started a list.

Quick note - they do not yet have their liquor license but I think they will be getting it (or hope to get it) in the next week or so.
