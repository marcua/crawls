Water is generally a pressure fed system so water should be available for most buildings if the power is lost. People with those fancy touch faucets might want to store some. If the power goes out, then it's possible the treatment plants will be out of commission, so that would mean boiling the water during the outage. Gas stoves should work fine other than those with electric igniters. keep matches handy. Stay away from buying milk, eggs, etc... PBJ and a couple of loaves of bread will be on the menu at our place.
