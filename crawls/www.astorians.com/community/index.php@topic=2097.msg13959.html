I went to the greenmarket website and apparently there is a farmers market, in Astoria, on 31st Ave between 12th and 14th Streets, on Wednesdays from 8-5 beginning in July.

For those of us who might actually be working at that time, there is a farmers market at Hunter's Point on Saturdays from 8-3, and also one on Rosevelt Island, right on the other side of the bridge, also on Saturdays

I haven't been to the one in Astoria, but I have seen the ones in Hunter's point and on RI. I think RI has more selection, but I am not sure about the origins of the produce
