Title: Re: Draft Western Queens Transportation Study
Post by: geowrld on October 08, 2014, 08:30:10 AM
There are some interesting things in that study.  The shoreline is rapidly developing and really needs better transportation options than currently available.


I don't think parking is adequately addressed.  "Progressive Rate Parking" is not going to alleviate parking congestion - it will just garner more money for the city.  People aren't parking for 2 hours on Steinway instead of 15 minutes because of the cost.  They're parking there because that's how much time they need.  The few small lots in the area are grossly insufficient for the population.  They need to turn one (or more) of those lots into a ginormous - and in my fantasy world:  not heinously ugly - parking structure.  Maybe even one that could accommodate long term parking.
