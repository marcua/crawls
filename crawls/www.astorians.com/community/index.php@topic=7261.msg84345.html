I'm not a big fan of seafood, but I went to Elias Corner on 31st Street and 24th Avenue (across the street from the Beer Garden) and I was impressed!  A group of my friends sat outside on the patio and relaxed in the early evening sun, while watching all of the folks trying to get into the Beer Garden.  The prices are really good for what you get and the service, at least for our group, was really good.  I attached a thread regarding the place

http://www.astorians.com/index.php?topic=432.0

Again, I'm not an expert on seafood, so there are probably more informed reviews, but I liked the place and I think I'll go back soon!
