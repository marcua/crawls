Here's the latest (and probably my last) update on happenings at the rink.  The Adult hockey tournament which I wrote about in the original thread ("Hockey @ LIC Ice Pavilion") that was unexpectedly cancalled is now back on.  Details are as follows:

"City Ice Pavilion will be kicking the Adult Hockey League with a 4 Division Tournament starting the week of January 5th. Each Division will play on the following nights (with each team playing one game on a Sunday or Friday). All time and dates are subject to change"

D1- Monday   
D2-Wednesday     
D3-Thursday         
D4- Tuesday

For those of you who aren't sure, the higher the division, the better the caliber of players.  So D1 guys have probably played College and/or Junior.  Whereas D4 players have much less experience.

If you want to enter as a team, you get a a minimum of three games guaranteed for $1000 and free parking.
All teams must have matching jerseys (City Ice will provide jerseys for an extra $200 per team)
Rosters are limited to 16 skaters and one goalie
Each Divisionâ€™s winner will get 15% off 2009 Adult Hockey League Fees


If you're not entering with a team and are just going solo (like me), individuals can sign-up for $120.00 (includes evaluation session, and jersey).  In order to placed with a team, you need to go to an "evaluation session" (ugh), which are scheduled on the following dates:
Thursday, December 18th @ 8:30p-9:50p
Monday, December 29th @ 8:30p-9:50p

Again, an "evaluation session" consist of you doing a bunch of drills on the ice and the staff will assess which level you belong in.  I'm sure a few team captains will be there; so if they need another skater and you impress, you'll may get a phone call asking you to join thier team.


No Checking. No Fighting (only because people have to go to work the next day).
Half-Shields are permitted (as I said in the previous post, I only recommend this for advanced players, but it's up to you).
All Players MUST be registered with USA Hockey


Finally, if you need a copy of the registration form:
cityice.pucksystems.com/page/show/55822-adult-hockey


So call your friends, wake up the kids, text your co-workers...there's some hockey going on in our neck of the woods!!!!

OK....one last update.  Info on the upcomming Adult Hockey League (A.H.L.) has come out.  Info on the A.H.L. is listed below.

Oh, and the tournament is taking place starting this week.  I'm guessing that some of you may want to drop by and watch some games on Monday or Wednesday nights.

______________________________


The City Ice Pavilion's First Adult Hockey League will start on Monday, February 9th.

There will be 5 Divisions, based on skill and all teams will play 12 games, with the top teams advancing to the playoffs.

The cost for the 1st A.H.L. at City Ice is $4900.  An early payment discount of $4500 for all teams paid in full by the Captains Meeting on Monday, February 2nd at 8:30pm.


All "Free Agents"  looking to get on teams should attend the Evaluation Session on Tuesday, January 27th at 10:00pm.  The cost is $20.00.

Divisions/Game Nights
D1 (Monday nights)        - Mostly college and college club players
D2 (Sunday/Wednesday) - College club and intramural players
D3 (Thursday)               - Intramural players and youth hockey experience
D4 (Sunday/Tuesday)     - Some youth hockey experience, and some true beginners to the sport
D5 (Friday)                   - Developmental - All beginners!
