Here is my contribution.  I love Astoria!

Astoria is a culture-rich phenomenon that has the best of many worlds.  On any given residential street, you are likely to find a vast mixture of cultures, people and families.  There is truly a place in Astoria for everyone.  Amazing considering that Astoria does not seem large enough to contain all that, but in fact it does!  It seems there is always something new to discover.  Not only does it give a very nice feeling of welcomeness and variety, but somehow also affords a small-town neighborhood air as well.  Where else can you find some of the most amazing ethnic restaurants side-by-side with a beautiful park, waterfront and variety of great bars and hang-outs? Affordable too!  Many families and younger singles can actually afford to live in a part of the city that is less than 20 minutes from Manhattan.  It is also one of the safest places to live in New York and one of the more rare places in the city where neighbors still watch out for each other and say hello on the street.
