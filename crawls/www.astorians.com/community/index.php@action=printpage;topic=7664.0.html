Title: Last Call for September Concert
Post by: AllisonTartalia on August 24, 2007, 02:15:44 PM
Hello Everyone!

Just putting out a last call for performers and venues for this year's annual September Concert. 

The September Concert is a series of free concerts, organized by individuals, schools, businesses, and associations

- to bring communities together
- to reaffirm our hope for peace
- to celebrate life and our universal humanity
Concerts are held in all areas of our city, our nation, our world. A global day of music for peace, every September 11th. Anyone can participate!  For more information and videos and pictures of past Septemmber Concert events, please visit www.septemberconcert.org.

All events citywide are going to be listed in the Daily News, and Tuesday is the deadline for that, so I thought I'd put a last call out to interested participants.

In particular, I am in need of instrumentalists who don't mind performing outside without a PA, as there are a couple of neighborhood shops who would like music, but due to their size can't accommodate the musicians inside.  If you are an instrumentalist of any sort who is interested in performing in this kind of situation, please let me know.

Aside from that, if you're a venue that would like to have music or if you're a musician who would like to arrange a concert, please let me know.  If you have it arranged by Tuesday morning, I'll make sure it gets into the listings and will make sure you get a banner and other materials.

Thanks so much!  I look forward to hearing from you!
