The real Hawaiian Shave Ice is much more finely shaved than mainland sno-cones, snow cones, etc. It's piled super-high and then syrup is added.

At the better shave ice stands, they use their own flavors and offer 30 or 40 flavors. The flavors taste more like what they are supposed to than most sno-cones I've had. Think jelly belly compared to run of the mill jelly beans. In addition to cherry, orange, raspberry etc. they offer coffee, root beer, green tea, bubblegum, local fruits such as lilikoi, mango, pineapple, and flavors based on real products like coke, melona, etc. You can get ice cream and/or red azuki beans (used in Chinese and Japanese deserts) underneath and condensed milk on top.

Here's a link to someone's blog entry about my favorite place.

http://oishiieats.blogspot.com/2006/09/waiola-shave-ice.html

And here's a link to the place all the surfers go to:

http://www.matsumotoshaveice.com/

BTW, I love Italian ices too and have been looking forward to a nice hot day here!
