Title: Take Root with The Moving Architects and Yael Nachajon
Post by: Dance Entropy on December 19, 2013, 02:24:49 PM
The Moving Architects continues to explore Erin Carlisle Norton’s signature movement style through three divergent dance works. “The New” poses dangerous renditions of the Twist with Stepford-esque 60’s flair; “Jellyfish” reveals liquidy limbs in an emulsifying duet; and “Fixate” embodies complex power plays with the pulsating guitar rhythms of the company’s long-time collaborator Ian Hatcher.
 
 "Think Martha Graham meets Donnie Darko." -Zachary Whittenburg, TimeOut Chicago
 
 Yael Nachajon and company use twisting, collapsing, contact-driven movement to explore interpersonal interactions in this new work, ASAP. The piece amplifies scenarios to the absurd to get to the core of human behavior.
 
 January 24 & 25, 8pm
 Tickets $15
 Green Space Studio
 37-24 24th St. Suite #301
 Long Island City, NY 11101
 718-956-3037

 Take Root is a monthly curated series that supports dance makers by providing an opportunity to show a full evening or half evening work paired with another performer.
