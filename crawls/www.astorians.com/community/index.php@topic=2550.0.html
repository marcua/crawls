Hi. I have a 3year old and an 18 month old. Day care in Astoria for infants is very difficult. I found that I couldn't reasonably get my first into a program until he was close to 3. I don't know about waiting lists, because I haven't had that problem. We are on a tight budget and stood away from private daycares which were significantly more expensive. We were also put off by the attitude of the private centers-it was almost as if you should kiss their feet for taking your child. It was too wierd. There are plenty of church sponsored daycares that are well run and maintained. You can check out St. Markella's, Most Precious Blood or St. Rita's for a start. I don't know if they take infants though.

My pediatrician is in Manhattan for both my children. I've interviewed a few here in Astoria, but the office facilities weren't great, long waiting times, so stuck to my original pediatrician on the upper east side. Don't have much of a problem getting there.

Hope this helps.
