Title: Re: single DAD HERE
Post by: mamidesamuel on April 18, 2009, 04:51:40 PM
A couple of recommendations:
-  PS 1 in LIC has a great exhbit of a swimming pool (not an actual pool) that is so fun for kids.
-  NY Hall of Science next to flushing Meadows park (#7 to Shea station) is a fantastic science museum for kids.  A membership to the museum is highly recommended because they have reciprocity with lots of other great kids museums such as:  Brooklyn Children's museum (a fantastic kids museum), Intrepid, Liberty Science Museum and others.
-  Also I know you don't want to take the kids into the city but the NW train takes you easily to Central Park where they can enjoy the park, the fantastic playgrounds in the park, and the zoo.
-  I'd lastly recommend you join www.astoriafamlies.org.  It is a site for families to talk about raising kids in Astoria.  There are family friendly events listed on the calendar for events in and around Astoria.

best of luck exploring with your kids.
