Title: CALLING ALL ARTISTS, MUSICIANS, ACTORS, DANCERS, VOLUNTEERS.. BIG EVENT!
Post by: audreyLICmag on March 16, 2009, 07:14:45 PM
Hi everyone! Please check out this upcoming local event:

AMandA in Wonderland! a.k.a. an Astoria Music & Arts event taking place at, and in collaboration with, the Wonderland Collective in Astoria!

The event takes place on APRIL 18TH, which is swiftly approaching, and AMandA and Wonderland are calling upon all fellow conspirators: artists, musicians, DJs, dancers, performers, fire spinners, spoken word, comedians, filmmakers, organizations, collectives, volunteers, participants, and all other creative energies, to be a part of this celebration. In other words, they want YOU! Even if you are not a performer/artist/musician, etc., you can get involved with the organizations to volunteer and help plan this & future events.

Wonderland & AMandA are both dedicated to the enrichment of the artistic community in the Astoria/LIC area and beyond. Only with a network and community of artists will this come to fruition. This is great opportunity for all of us to build together, and YOU can be a part of it!

If you are interested in collaborating on the April 18th Wonderland event, the Astoria Music Now! festival in July, or any other events, or know of any other organizations or performers that would be interested in working together, please contact Mike Tummolo mtummolo@gmail.com from Wonderland directly and/or email the group at astoriamusicarts@gmail.com.

THANK YOU!!
