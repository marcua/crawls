I don't think it's tacky at all to bring your own bottle to a place, BYO, or not. There are tons of places all over the country that allow this (4 star to the most humble neighborhood restaurant), that's why most places charge a corkage fee. If you know your wine, or have a bottle you want to pair with a nice meal out, and esp if the place doesn't offer what you want or have, there is nothing wrong with bringing it in. (Though I always call first.) There are plenty of places that I WISHED would allow me to bring a bottle b/c I know their wine list is crap. That's my rant.

Otherwise, I second all the suggestions...

Leng Thai
Kebab Cafe
Arepa Cafe (though more casual)
Bambino (if he's still allowing it)

And if there is a place you like that you're not sure, just call and ask if you can bring your own bottle and they have a corkage fee.
