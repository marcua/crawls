Hey there, I hope you enjoyed a nice long weekend.

This week, I'm doing a staged reading for Ensemble Studio Theatre as a part of OCTOBERFEST, 2005.  In case you don't know (especially for those who aren't in the biz...), EST is a very prestigious theatre company in NYC.  So I'm pretty excited to be a part of it.  The info is below.  I hope I'll see you there.  Plus the ticket price is "whatever you want to pay".  

(David played Mayor Lenny on both Ghostbusters movies.  Christine, who plays my love interest, played Lisa West on ABC "One Life To Live".)
 
If you want to find out more about them or about OCTOBERFEST, 2005, these are the websites.
http://www.ensemblestudiotheatre.org/
http://www.ensemblestudiotheatrela.org (Los Angeles branch)
 
I look forward to seeing you there.

Nick
------------------------
As a part of OCTOBERFEST, 2005

"Yumee and Jack" -- the reading

Written by Bill Bozzone
Directed by Kate Baggott

With:
David Margulies ("Ghostbusters")
Christine Toy Johnson ("One Life To Live")

Nick Sakai ("Law & Order: Criminal Intent" "Third Watch")

and

Barbara Anders
 

Oct 12 (Wed) and Oct 13 (Thurs) at 8:30pm

Ensemble Studio Theatre, 549 West 52nd Street, 2nd floor
Reservation: (212)247-4982 (Or you can contact me directly)

Suggested Donation -- you can pay whatever you wish.
