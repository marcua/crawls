I just left the Rock after being a member for 5 years. I won't go into the details as to why I left, but it is the priciest gym in Astoria.
I've heard from friends of mine who are still there though that there are deals to be had, so if you do decide to join, negotiate and
don't take their first offer.


I don't have personal experience w/Matrix, but I've know a couple of people who been members over the years and liked it. You
don't say exactly where you live, but I'm assuming it's somewhere between the two clubs. I know Matrix is much less expensive,
but less expensive doesn't mean lower quality.


I joined Life-Health & Fitness on 35th Ave. & 37th St., which is significantly less $$$ than the Rock and I'm very happy.
You should get passes for both clubs and try them out - see where you feel more comfortable and which suits your needs.
Good luck.
