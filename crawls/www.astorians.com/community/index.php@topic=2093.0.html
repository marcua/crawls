Great Trader Joe's finds
« on: May 09, 2006, 10:03:39 PM »
So now that we have basked in the glory of the Trader Joe's that has decided to dwell amongst the humble people of New York City, I thought it might be a great idea to share some great Trader Joe's finds.

I'll start.

While waiting (if I remember correctly) in an obscenely long line (but still well worth it) at TJ's I grabbed a lovely jar of mustard. It was an impulse but I could not help myself. It is delicious. And perfectly spiced. My sandwiches have wondered how they could have been eaten for so long without this lovely spread.

It is Sake Wasabi Mustard.
