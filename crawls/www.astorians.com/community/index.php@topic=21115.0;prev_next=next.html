 Queens[/color] Art Express 2011   [/size]CALL FOR VOLUNTEERS

[/color]Queens Art Express is a special event sponsored by the Queens Council on the Arts.[/size]
[/color] We need volunteers this summer during Queens Arts Express (QAX) to assist with meeting visitors, art lovers and tourists as they visit and explore all that Queens has to offer in performances, art openings, music and more from   [/size]JUNE 15th through JUNE 19th
[/color] As a volunteer, you will receive a QAX T-Shirt as a token of our appreciation for helping out and a $10 Metrocard [/size]
[/color]PLUS be a HOST at art events that weekend. [/size]
[/color]Helping QAX will consist of:[/size]
[/color]•           [/size]Providing information at key subway stops, such as map and event directions
[/color]•           [/size]Assist at venues with crowd control
[/color]•           [/size]Distribute marketing materials
[/color]•           [/size]Meet, greet and network with artists and organizations
[/color] Starting JUNE 15 with Fun Kickoff Party at Kaufman-Astoria Studios through final events on  [/size]JUNE 19
[/color]with a variety of arts events happening JUNE 16-17 - 18 - 19 along the 7 train and N/Q train from LIC- Astoria to Jackson Heights and all the way to FLUSHING![/size]
[/color]We are looking for enthusiastic and reliable people with great customer service skills who have a passion for showing the world the diversity of arts that Queens has to offer. 
[/size]
[/color]PLUS helping with the Closing event at the end of JUNE at the Video and Food Festival atop Brooklyn Grange Rooftop Event in Long Island City[/size]
[/color] TIMELINE[/size]
[/color]Please RSVP - asap[/size]
[/color] [/size]
[/color]1 Training session for QAX will be held [/size]
[/color]WEDNESDAY, JUNE1[/size]
[/color]Quinn bldg - 2nd floor[/size]
[/color]at the Great Astoria Historical Society[/size]
[/color]35-20 Broadway - Astoria[/size]
[/color] [/size]
[/color]please put “QAX Volunteer” in the subject line.[/size]
[/color] [/size]
[/color]let us know if you are in town then and can help out a day or two on these fun and informative events.[/size]
[/color]Best,[/size]
[/color]Celeste Balducci[/size]
[/color]QAX Project Associate[/size]
