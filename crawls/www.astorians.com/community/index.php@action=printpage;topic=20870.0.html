Post by: pridecenter on April 23, 2011, 05:53:44 PM

Queens LGBTQ Families Info Fair

Queens Pride House and Center Families (LGBT Center, Manhattan) are happy to host Queens LGBTQ Families Info Fair!

  • Open to LGBTQ Parents, Parents-to-be, & Anyone interested in learning more about the many ways to create and expand families!

  • Talk with special guests about family planning, legal issues, ways to better protect your family, adoption, fostering, alternative insemination, surrogacy and more!

Enjoy food, games, arts & crafts, and activities for all ages!

Join local LGBTQ parents, ask questions, and share stories!

Bring your families and friends!

Childcare will be offered.

Raffle!

FREE!
