Just a note that, unfortunately, there's no real competition in the electricity distribution market, for obvious physical reasons. (You can get different suppliers, but ConEd always does the distribution.) They're a monopoly, which means that you can't really punish them by forcing customers elsewhere. The $3 credit comes from their revenue stream, meaning other ConEd customers (outside of the LIC network). I haven't heard much about their management structure, but I imagine people in management should lose their jobs over this. This said, I think that starving ConEd of revenue by forcing large rebates is just going to make the problems worse in the long run. Presumably they should be upping rates and increasing investment in infrastructure, not giving credits and short-changing maintenance. Also, those guys who were working long hours in tunnels in hot weather deserve every bit of overtime owed to them.
