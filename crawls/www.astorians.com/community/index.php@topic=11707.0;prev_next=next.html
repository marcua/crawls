Fun filled week at Hell Gate Social
« on: August 05, 2008, 05:39:33 PM »
Wednesday August 6th
Rock Band
You always wanted to be a rock star, but never made it. Well Live out your dream with Rock Band the video game. Be the lead singer, an ace on the ax, or beat away at the drums.
All the while guzzling Miller High Life or Busch and a shot of bourbon for $3.
Fun starts at 7pm

Thursday August 7th
Thursday Night Music Showcase presents:
Dirty Wings - Astoria trio performing songs of personal and political protest.

Bliminal - Local Astoria rockers taking root in a new movement of independent music fusing subculture with pop culture inspiring generations to think and dance.

Kelli Eva Ellis - Astoria singer/songwriter by way of Hawaii, Arizona and California, brings the sunny sounds of the west coast to Queens.

Friday August 8th
DJ Tark is on the 1's and2's spinning his brand of new wave, and electropop.

Saturday August 9th
Alternative  Arts Association presents "In and Out of Days", a multimedia exploration of the  dreams and nightmares we experience as we collectively pass in and out of days. This will be the fourth major performance and the second show at Hell Gate Social. The show features a number of visual and performance art pieces, including dance, photography, music, theatre, sculpture, and much much more.

Free Shuttle from Astoria Blvd stop on the N to Hell Gate Social

Event starts at 7pm

Sunday August 10th
Sunday Night Cinema presents:
Revenge of the Pink Panther  The world believes that Inspector Clouseau (Peter Sellers) has fallen victim to Douvier (Robert Webber), a drug lord who's just blown up the inspector's home. But Clouseau, very much alive, is headed for Hong Kong with the drug lord's discarded mistress (Dyan Cannon) to investigate Douvier and a heroin deal worth 50 million Francs. Can Clouseau, disguised as the Godfather, intercept the deal? And will Inspector Dreyfus see through his disguise?

Happy Hour from 7pm-9pm daily
Hell Gate Social
12-21 Astoria Blvd
718 204.8313
