« on: December 13, 2008, 09:32:42 PM »
Hello everyone! I'm a 24 year old female Astorian. My current living situation is far less than ideal so I'm looking for a new place for me and my cat to live. I'd like to move in around January 1st, which is when my landlord wants me to sign a new lease.
 
The most I can pay is about $850/month including internet, electricity, gas and ideally cable (but that's not as important). The room should be large enough to fit a queen sized bed, desk, dresser and bookshelf. At least one window.
 
Ideally I would like to move into a 3 bedroom/1 or 2 bath or a 4 bedroom/2 bath apartment with roommates (m/f) in their mid 20s to early 30s, but I could live in a 2 bedroom if you think we'd get along really well. Or a 4+, so long as there are enough bathrooms.

I'm not a huge fan of moving, so I'd like to find a place where I could stay for at least a year (provided we get along). I'm a 24 year old female (but most of my friends are guys...) employed full time as an assistant teacher at a school for autistic kids (salary is about 30,000/year) and I'm also in graduate school part time.

I'm looking for a place with friendly roommates who might occasionally want to hang out and watch tv, play video games (wii comes with me) or cook/order food, but I also value my personal space and alone time and will probably spend a fair amount of time in my own room. During the week I leave the apartment around 7:00, generally get home between 5:00 and 8:00 and try to get to sleep by 10:00 or 10:30.Therefore I do not want to move someplace where there are constantly parties or roommates who are up late making a lot of noise.

I am quiet and considerate and always clean up after myself in common areas (though I admit that my own bedroom is often disorganized. But not dirty). I pay my bills on time. I'm looking for roommates who do not smoke cigarettes (at least in common areas) but who are 420 friendly. However I want to reiterate that I am very responsible and hard working and will NOT be hanging out on the couch watching TV and eating pizza all day. In fact, chances are I'll be asleep a couple hours after you get home from work.

Along with me comes my adorable (but very shy initially) cat named Wuzzle. She loves affection and playing fetch or chasing after the red dot from a laser pointer. I'll take full responsibility for feeding her and changing her litter box. In my current apartment her litter box is in the kitchen (only place we have room) but the smell doesn't bother us because it's a covered litter box and I use the crystal kitty litter which produces almost no dust or smell.

If you or someone you know has a room available, please let me know!

Also let me know if you or someone you know is looking for a room to rent and think we'd make good roommates!

Thanks!

Tamar
