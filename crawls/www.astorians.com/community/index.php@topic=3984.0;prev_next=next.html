Thew new Vynl is great!  Very stylish inside.  The bathrooms are amazing, you have to try each bathroom while you're there.  Well, one each time you visit at least so you don't look like you have bladder problems.  But it is worth it.  The food is amazing, Shephard's Pie, Meatloaf, Chicken Pot Pie!  MMMMmm... walked by there, again, last night.  Can't miss it with the huge circle glass window in the front.  It is just south of 51st street on the east side of 9th Ave.  Seems to attract a big gay population as well.

El Centro is also a great place!  The staff is very friendly and quick, too.  Good mojitos.

You are right about the Greek Kitchen not comparing to Astoria, but hey for Manhattan?! ;)

My staff went to a neat place last night after our dance.  Renaissance Diner at 51st and 9th ave.  The food was good, I enjoyed the environment as well.
