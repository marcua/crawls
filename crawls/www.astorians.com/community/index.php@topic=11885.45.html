For every nifty thing about the 'hood, there's a trade-off:

- Relatively low-crime....assuming you take precautions. Don't leave your car windows open, even a crack; don't leave your bikes outside or locked w/a cable.

- Roomy, charming apartment.....but falling apart and needing new flooring/carpeting/refridgerator/etc.

- Cheap rent......on a very noisy corner/busy street.

- Great selection of restaurants......if you can afford to eat out! (actually, plenty of affordable choices.)

- Life-long residents who know what you're up to....this makes me feel safe, as I could care less what they think.

All that said, I'd LOVE to move to a quieter part of town, or to a better apartment, but consider myself pretty lucky to live here given my low-budget lifestyle. I happen to welcome the negative comments, as the rents seem to go up with every great review of the neighborhood! There really is a lot of hype.
