Cohen's Fashion Optical on 31st and Ditmars
« on: August 25, 2009, 12:23:34 AM »
I like to support local business, so given an opportunity I will purchase items, even if more expensive, locally. I wanted a new pair of sunglasses so headed to Cohens by the Ditmars stop (and although the name is a chain, it was bought and is now privately owned).

I wanted a good pair, and was willing so spend more. I met with the saleswoman who was very friendly, but did talk me into a higher priced pair, out of the range that I told her I wanted, but went with it. I got them and voiced my concern if they were uncomfortable; she said "don't worry about it just bring them back".

Two hours later, I came back with scars on my nose from the glasses that were incredibly uncomfortable, but that I still wanted to find a pair that was good for me. She, less energetically and with some annoyance, then showed me others, and although they were a bit more expensive (now well beyond my range) I wanted to give them my business, and got the higher priced pair. I got them. The were uncomfortable as well. Normally, I wouldn't make a big deal about it, but they were VERY expensive, and for that money, I wanted to ber comfortable.

SOOOOO, I went back, and was met with a huge attitude, and told "no returns, only exchange". I was so shocked at the change in the salesperson (naive I know) and how she barked the order at me. I asked her where there was written, and told her if I had known, I would not have spent that much, nor bought them there. She pulls a sign out from behind the register (that no one could see) and showed me where it said it at the bottom of the sign. I was furious that I was supposed to know that and was never told.

When asked to speak to the manager, I was told he was at another store and to come back on specific days. I went back twice to be told he didn't come in that day. When I finally conceded to exchange them for store credit, I was told I couldn't without the store manager (a new rule?). I went back two more days, and was told again that he wasn't there.

I was furious with this store, and the staff, but decided that I no longer wanted to spend any more of my time waiting around and going to this guy to discuss this policy, and the lack of communication. The staff were rude and condescending, and he was unavailable. I will never go to this store again and have passed the word on to as many as I can. I know that I should have known better and bought at a store that would honor a return or exchange, but I wanted to keep it local. UGH. I'm so pissed at this store and annoyed with myself. Just wanted to share the experience if it might turn even one person from going there in the future, or to at least be careful with what they get there...just in case.
