Agnanti Cafe
« on: August 28, 2012, 08:21:26 PM »
Agnanti opened up a cafe across the street on Ditmars. They're having an opening party right now, there's a huge spread of food for everyone. I chatted with a woman behind the counter named Faye about the plans for the place. They have a menu of pastries, panini, bureks, coffee, and they'll have a liquor license soon. They'll be opening at 7am, which makes it a great new breakfast stop. I think this is such a great addition to the neighborhood. And with all the foot traffic that heads down Ditmars towards the park, I think they'll do well. I'm really excited about this!
