The NYC DEP will also test your water free of charge, at the very least for lead (we had ours tested).  I can't find it online, but their Lead Unit phone number is 718-595-5364.  They send you two plastic jars and instructions for drawing the water.  You send it in, and they test it.

Our water is excellent tasting, and we don't filter it.  In the winter, especially, it is cold and clear.  I can just picture the water coming down from the chilly reservoirs.  In the summer, it usually tastes better if you chill it and let it sit a bit.

The water in Astoria is better than water in some other parts of NYC.  When my in-laws got married, they moved to the Bronx.  They used to come back to my mother-in-law's parents' house (where we live now) to fill jugs for drinking water.
