Title: Dodgeball Coming Back to Astoria
Post by: ZogSports on July 15, 2008, 03:00:24 PM
Are you looking for a way to connect with fellow Astorians? How about doing it on the court, pegging each other with red foam balls?  And then laughing about it at organized post-game happy hours?

Don't have a full team?  Don't worry - sign up as an individual or with a few friends and we'll place you on a team! 

This is our fifth season in Queens and to celebrate, we're running our most popular leagues: Dodgeball in Astoria and Touch Football in Long Island City!

WHEN: Early Registration starts July 21!  Leagues run from September-December.
HOW: Register at www.zogsports.org. For more info, email info@zogsports.org

 

About ZogSports
ZogSports, the co-ed, social, charity-focused sports organization, has spent the last four years helping young New Yorkers do something different by offering sports leagues, trips, social events, and volunteer opportunities for people in their 20s, 30s, and 40s.  To date, they have given more than $450,000 to charities chosen by participating teams.

ZogSports offers several sports each season (dodgeball, kickball, touch football, outdoor and indoor soccer, volleyball, basketball, whiffleball and football), and allows participants to sign up to play in various divisions, ranging from Players to Extremely Casual.
