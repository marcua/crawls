I was doing some research last night on the old QP's Marketplace' down at Queens Plaza. A NY Times article about it referred to it originally being called 'QP's Boxcar Marketplace', and stated there would be a few merchants selling stuff directly from box cars opened up into the building from the tracks out back. I'm not sure if I'm remembering this right, but I very vaguely recall one shop in there that was nothing more than a guy with a register at a back door of the building with a boxcar full of goods open for customers to by from. Maybe this was just my overactive imagination?

Does anyone else remember this, or have some good QP marketplace stories?  I'm writing up a story about the place and would love to hear anything you've got on it.

My parents would take me there to buy comic books.  If I recall right there were two comic shops, and the owners of one of them eventually moved to a storefront in sunnyside gardens after the rent was jacked up.
