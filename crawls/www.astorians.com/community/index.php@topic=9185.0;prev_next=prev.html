Can't say how many times I've been tempted to jump out of bed at 2 AM, get on my bike to track down the source of these sounds!  Besides "The Hum", there's also the "The Roar" which I believe has been mentioned on this forum possibly coming from either LGA or the new power plant on Steinway.  They both sound similar, but I think the LGA source is mostly likely engine "run up" to test jet engines after maintenance and lasts less than a minute, while the power plant roar can last more than ten minutes. 

Astoria has got to be the most noisey place in the city,  what with the 31st St El, the GCP, truck jake brakes on Astoria Blvd, motorcycles, car stereos and alarms, Amtrak and freight trains, the helicopters, LGA,  the loud bars and clubs, the PR doorbells, construction activity, churches and mosques with outdoor loudspeakers.   I've even heard & seen ATV's and snowmobiles on occasion!  (i know I'm leaving stuff out)  My neighbors include a rock band, a model airplane hobbyist and a carpenter.  All of which like to practice their thing at home! 

Yes, Astoria is an aural symphony.  My personal solution: earplugs.
