i am writing from the reading room of the public library since i have been without power since 10pm on monday. we had very limited power from two outlets this morning at 77th and ditmars. it felt like a rare treat to string together a couple of extension cords and have a fan blowing on me last night for the first time in days. needless to say, i did not get a full night sleep once again. all of our food has spoiled and we have no hot water.

this morning i walked around midtown waiting for the library to open. i amused myself by calling con-ed a few times, calling the city a few times, calling peter vallone a few times and trying to get the mayor on the phone. unfortunately i had no luck in getting additional info.

i have to say i feel completely let down by our fair city. at the core of what government provides for its citizens is help and relief in times of trouble and hardship. i learned today that the city is doing absolutely NOTHING during this emergency and deferring to con-ed to provide relief.

i certainly am not asking for much but a message from the mayor saying that he knows we are suffering and that he is doing whatever he can to help would be nice. after eating out for three days, a little ice and some cold water would be a kind gesture too. perhaps it is time to get the red cross involved?

i feel completely abandoned by the city i love and pay taxes to support.
