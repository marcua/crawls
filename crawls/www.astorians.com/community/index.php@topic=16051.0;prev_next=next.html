There's a Home Depot on Northern Blvd that may have some things you're looking for.

Also, check out the ABC Superstore on 31st St between Ditmars and 21st Ave, or The Bargain Stop on 30th Ave and somewhere around 34th Street (whichever store is closer to you - they have pretty much the same stuff). They've got cheapo housewares of often decent quality, depending on your budget and how fancy you want to be. We got our dish drain and kitchen garbage can there, among other things. They aren't Simple Human brand or anything, but they work and they are cheap.
