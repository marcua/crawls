Daisy - I just saw "Kooza" this past Sunday and they make it pretty easy to get to.  We took the M60 over the bridge then just walked a few steps  to the X80.  The X80 bus stops on the NW corner of 125th and Lex (the signpost actually says "Cirque du Soleil".  I would suggest getting there by 4 at the latest as the line will be quite long, plus this will give you the chance to hang out a little before the show begins.  The bus drops you off about the equivalent of 5-6 city blocks from the grand chapiteau but as soon as you get off the bus you will see it on your right so it's pretty easy to get to.

Coming back was super easy as well.

One thing to note is that you will have to buy a "pay-per-ride" MetroCard or have $5 in change.  They don't accept the monthly cards.

Have fun!

I won't say anything to ruin anything but will say this:  In my opinion, it was good but not great when compared to other touring shows they've had here over the years.  If it's your first Cirque show then you will absolutely love it.  I do appreciate the way things were done though.

Being that I've seen most of their other shows, in addition to being an amateur aerialist myself, I find myself being too criticial when watching these things so my opion is usually different than the general public.  My partner was in agreement with me on this though. 

You will enjoy it regardless and I encourage everyone to see it.  Theatermania has discount tix for 40%.  We actually got our tix for 75% off through Cirque Club which were pretty decent seats.
