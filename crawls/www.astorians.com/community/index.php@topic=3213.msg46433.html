Apple pie jam (Ball Complete book of home preserving)
This recipe follows standard canning procedures.
You can find canning directions on line.

3/4 cup raisins finely chopped in food processor
6 cups chopped cored peeled tart apple such as granny smith
1 lemon zested and juiced
1cup unsweetened apple juice
1 package (1.75oz) regular powdered fruit pectin
9 cups granulated sugar
1tsp ground cinnamon
1/2 tsp ground nutmeg

in large deep stainless steel pan, combine apples, lemon zest and juice. Bring to boil over high heat, stirring frequently. reduce heat and boil gently, stirring occasionally, until apples begin to soften (about 10 minutes). 

Remove from heat and whisk in pectin till dissolved. Stir in raisins. Return to high heat and bring to boil.  Add sugar all at once and return to full rolling boil, stirring constantly. Boil hard for 1 minute, stirring constantly.  Remove from heat and still cinnamon and nutmeg. Skim off foam.

Ladle into jars and process accordingly.

Enjoy.
