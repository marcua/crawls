My new year's resolution was to drink less often, and so far it's been pretty easy.  Maybe I'm just getting old (!),  but I don't react well to alcohol anymore - I will spare you the details :)  It's saving me money big time - which is great because I'm pretty broke these days, and it's saving me calories.  I usually just have a glass of wine when out with friends and then water the rest of the evening.  You can have fun without drinking, I still go out with my friends, whether to a bar, club or movie, I'm just not much of a drinker, and it's perfectly ok.  I still have tons of fun, and I'm better in the morning because of it.  Plus with work killing me all week, I hate to waste my weekends because of some stupid hangover.    Best of luck to you, I'm really happy I made this decision, and I hope you will too.
