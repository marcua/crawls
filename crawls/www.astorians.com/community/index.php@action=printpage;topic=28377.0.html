Title: Re: Nature's Taste on Ditmars
Post by: Yaxpac on September 16, 2014, 05:17:26 PM
ha, yeah seriously Braun just go inside and take a look.   


I went in last weekend and they were extremely welcoming inside.  Others were in the store perusing as well.   Think along the lines of Butcher Bar...but more market/butcher and less restaurant.  There are a few tables to sit and eat but i got the feel that it was more market/butcher.  they had some beautiful looking pork chops that looks as good or better than Flying Pigs at the Union Square Farmers market.  I was surprised to see that.  Also, the meat was offered both fresh or frozen.  I like that.   I was also happy to see farm fresh eggs (albeit a little steap at $6/dozen).   The pork products looked exceptionally good (pork chops, bacon, etc).  I did not buy anything but I do plan on doing so in the future.


While i was in there they gave a woman in the store a sample of their brisket.   She ate and and said that she was an admitted brisket snob...and thought it was delicious.   I did not try anything so i cannot speak for it first hand but this what i overheard. 


Overall though, I thought it was a nice store and it was well stocked for a new place.   They had fresh chickens and meats that I look forward to trying.  The stuff appeared to be the real deal too, from a true farm.   I am from the east end of long island, near Riverhead and I am happy to see this place in Astoria.   I look forward to trying their products.   


Now, if only they would carry Briemere pies...
