Sorry, yes they were sold. I hope you had a productive day at the flea market. Where was it? It it recurring?

Note to anyone thinking of having an apt sale in Astoria:

We decided to set up a table on the sidewalk in front of our apt with smaller items and it really drew people to the sale. We got rid of most things and actually made a lot of moola. I highly suggest that people should set up a table outside and direct potential customers up to your apt to see the larger items. Bonus: You get to meet a lot of your neighbors by sitting outside and talking to everyone who passes. Although I already knew it, it was reconfirmed yesterday that Astorians are the best people ever!

We had a really great day. Thanks to everyone who showed up!
