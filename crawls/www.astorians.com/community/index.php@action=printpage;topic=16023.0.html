Title: Crafters Wanted ! Eco-friendly Market coming to Long Island City
Post by: Artforqueens on August 29, 2009, 10:07:26 AM
As seen on www.discoverqueens.info

together we can help save the planet

The Hunters Point Community Development Corporation is currently accepting vendors for a new weekly Eco-Friendly market,Hunters Point Hidden Treasures.

The market will feature crafts, jewelry,
art/photography, vintage and other clothing made
by local Queens artist and others. As well as Antiques,Bic-a-Brac.

Local crafts people working with sustainable materials blend with the farmers who sell locally grown natural produce to create an Eco-friendly shopping and buying environment.

Theater groups welcome to showcase your talent
Local Fashion Designers welcome to showcase a fashion show



Dates: Saturdays from Sept. 12, 2009 through November 21, 2009
Hours: 10:00 am -- 4:00 pm. Vendor set-up time is 9:00 am
Where: 48th Ave. & Vernon Blvd. next to the Greenmarket, Long Island City, in Queens, NY
Cost: $25.00 per week, $80.00 for four weeks in advance.
Checks/money orders payable to Hunters Point Community Development Corp.

Call or email for application
Terri Adams, President
Hunters Point Community Development Corp.
49-10 Vernon Blvd., Long Island City, NY 11101
Office: 718-786-9245, Feel free to call if you have any questions.
Hope to see you there.
