You're on a magic island...
« on: November 30, 2010, 11:46:44 PM »
...And you have nothing to eat but one dish each from three Astoria restaurants.  Which establishments would you choose and what would the dish be? 

After much deliberation, here are my three:

Il Bambino - Bresaola panini
Vesta - A Warm Bankie (off the brunch menu)
Mundo - The red sonja

I could live forever on those three things.  Yum.  I'm curious about what other people think are the best things. 

(About the magic island: assume that you have a nutritional supplement and that you would never get any vitamin deficiency illnesses -- so you could potentially choose three different kinds of French fries if you wanted.)

Excellent exercise.

I would choose:

* Il Bambino - tomato fresca panini (was going to name another one involving goat cheese but it is gone, sob...)
* Burrito w/carnitas from El Rey Del Taco
* Sweet Afton's burger loaded with cheddar and smoked bacon.  And a side of those insane beer battered pickles. 

Totally agree about the Warm Bankie.  Love it.  Also-rans include: loukoumades from Athens Cafe, a whole wheat everything bagel with lox spread from Brooklyn Bagel, the spicy seafood in curry coconut broth from JJ's (it's perfect for a cold day!), the Ottoman dumplings at Mundo, the Cypriot breakfast at Cafe Bar, and skordalia from Zenon (love their baby walnut dessert too!). In its heyday I would have chosen Brick's tuna tartare.

Yep, we're pretty lucky to have the delicious nosh that we do around here.  :-)
