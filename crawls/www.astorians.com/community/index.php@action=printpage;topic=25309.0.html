Rural Route kicks things off at Strand Smokehouse
[/size][/color]
with a night of archival film projection, live music, craft beer, casked bourbon, and tasty southern barbecue!
Astoria’s own 2/3 Goat will kick out a melding of alt-country, bluesy bluegrass and classic rock that echo the core members’ diverse roots: Annalyse descended directly from the infamous McCoys of E. Kentucky, and Ryan comes deeply rooted in the S. Jersey shore beach culture. They were chosen as one ofthe top 10 bands out of over 3,500 to perform in
The Outlaw Roadshow at SXSW 2013.

Located in the southern back country of Astoria, Queens, The Strand Smokehouse is a Meat Lovin’, Beer Drinkin’, Whiskey Shootin’ paradise. All of their meat is smoked to perfection (they promise, a guy from Texas told ‘em so) and their bartenders sling only the most refreshing craft beers, and casked bourbon in a beer hall atmosphere.
