If you want information on Astoria CSA on a more timely basis, please head over to www.astoriacsa.com and on the main page sign up for our news mailing list at the bottom of the page. It's the best way to keep informed about upcoming events.

If you register you will automatically be added to this list.

An update regarding the Membership Drive the other night: our meeting with the farmers went very well. We surpassed our expected numbers (of attendance and registration) and have hit our membership goals! Thank you to all who attended it was an amazingly successful night. It's so exciting to hear everyone elses' excitment about this CSA that some of us have been working on since November!

For the time, registration is still open, we can handle about 50 more shares.
