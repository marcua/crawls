A friend and I finally tried this place the other night, and wow, is it awesome!!  The waitresses are super friendly and attentive, and the food portions are huge.  The food was so delicious.  We both tried the beef soup which is very hearty and flavorful.  I had the pork schnitzel and mashed potatoes for dinner, and had to take half of it home, it was so big.  My friend had the potato with sheep cheese dish, which is absurdly huge  - warning: do not get this as a dinner but try to get it as an appetizer if they offer that.   It's amazing but really rich, so you won't be able to finish but a quarter of it.  The beers are pretty good too.  It's a really affordable place with nice wood decor.  I'd definitely recommend it.

I love the spaetzle with sheep cheese and that bacon - instead of ordering it as an app, you can easily take half of it home and have it for lunch the next day.  The spaetzle with sauerkraut is also very good.

If the sauerkraut soup ever appears on the specials menu or even the regular menu, get it!!  It's seriously delicious - rich and hearty.  The pierogies are also great, and I the pork schnitzel is tasty as well.  I love those knaedle (sp?) round bready dumplings - so good!  Pretty much everything I've ever tasted there is really, really good. 

Glad you had such an awesome experience there, daisy!  We should totally do a group Astorians dinner there this winter.  It would be fun!   8-)

I agree with all this.  It's a great place!  And huge portions all around.  But really delicious food and friendly people.  And I love the atmosphere.  Really feels like an alpine chalet.  I love the garlic soup here.  I usually get the Boiled Beef in Dill Sauce.  It's amazing.  And try under the Snack menu, Dumpling Cubes with Eggs and Pickles.  We thought it might be a cool appetizer.  Rather, it's like a brunch meal.  But if you go with about 4 people, order it and share it.
