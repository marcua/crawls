Boy did Table 30 open and close quickly.  Don't blink or you'll miss it.

I've been to the Chirping Chicken on Broadway near 31st and it was absolutely awful both times.   The quality of those grilled chicken places fluctuates.  The best one I've been to is in Kew Gardens right outside the Kew Gardens stop on the F/E train.  It is so good that when I go out to the Kew Gardens Cinemas to take advantage of the cheap matinee prices (art films for $6.50 instead of $12.50 you would pay at the Angelika) I stop there.   The aforementioned Chirping Chicken on the West Side near the Beacon Theater is pretty good too, better than the horrible place on Broadway but nowhere near as good as the chicken place in Kew Gardens.   There is another lousy chicken place on Second Avenue around 61st Street.
