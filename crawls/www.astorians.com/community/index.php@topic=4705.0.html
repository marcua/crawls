I like Sparrow a lot.  Other drinking establishments I've liked are Stini Yiamas on Ditmars for cocktails, McCanns when there's a baseball game on (it's a bit frat-like there), Irish Rover on 28th Ave (great Irish pub/bar, which I hope will continue their Pub Quiz after annoying football is over).  There's the Albatross, which is a gay bar but friendly to all.  Fatty's also has a bar, but it's not a "bar" per se.  I'm looking forward to that new wine/beer place opening up near Fatty's.  And Waltz has a liquor license and they sell beer and wine.  It's a cafe on Ditmars near 23rd Ave.
