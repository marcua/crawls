Lentil soup with a base of scallion, garlic, and red peppers. You'd need to buy some vegetable stock for this. You could spice it with cinnamon and a bit of cumin and add the pomegranate seeds toward the end.  Add lemon at the last minute before serving, to balance the rich flavors (and adds acid b/c you don't have tomatoes on hand).  

Bok choi and spinach sauteed with lemon and garlic (I'll give you particulars if you wish, on the method that works for me).

If you wanted to, you could make a little snack of Salvadoran quesadillas (cheddar cheese), which are quesadillas (preferably with corn tortillas) fried and then fried again  having been dipped in beaten egg first.  With salsa.

Pears sauteed with walnuts and brown sugar and cinnamon (making a relationship to the soup earlier in the evening), served with yoghurt for dessert.

Just off the top of my head.
