It sounds like you've gotten great referrals, and you had said you were looking for a woman doctor, so I guess I'll just post on my OB/Gyn in case anyone else is looking, esp. anyone who has been told they are high risk.  I'm currently 14 weeks into my surpise 3rd pregnancy and I was no spring chicken when I had my first.  I'm actually more like a Christmas chicken at this point, and Dr. Silverstein has delivered all of my "high risk" (by insurance industry age standards) babies, first at NYU Midtown and I guess the next one will be at Mt. Sinai.  They are a recognized high risk delivery practice, but they see everyone.  Actually, they were the ones to calm my fears after reading all the baby books about how my risk for birth defects and other probs were scarey once I passed 40 (which I was for the last baby and this one).  All of the OBs are men, but I would never call them cold--they are extremely warm and informal--Dr. Silverstein always greets me with a big hug and remembers all my family, even those he didn't deliver.  There is one woman in the office, Dr. Gottlieb, but she focuses on Gyn only, no obstetrics, but she, too is very personable and warm. My first delivery was so much fun, the nurse told me I had to stop laughing and push!

Dr. Silverstein started this practice with two other docs who were all teaching physicians at NYU.  They have their own imaging office onsite, so you don't have to go from one hospital wing to another for your different appts. Mt. Sinai's birthing rooms aren't as pretty as NYU Midtown's, but I understand the rooms where you will be staying after delivery are more comfortable.

I really love these guys and would recommend them to anyone.  They take my insurance, which is Aetna:  http://mfmnyc.com/index.html
