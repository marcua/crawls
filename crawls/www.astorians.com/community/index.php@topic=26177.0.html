Queens Council on the Arts Presents: High School to Art School Interview Day
« on: January 10, 2014, 03:48:19 PM »
QCA’s High School to Art School Portfolio Development Program (HS2AS) provides high school juniors and seniors with high quality arts skills training including portfolio development, mentorship opportunities, college application assistance, and financial aid planning for college.

On February 15th, HS2AS will be conducting interviews for our Winter/Spring session that will meet on Saturdays from March until May.  Students submit an online application and a group of applicants are invited to interview.  Applicants must bring five drawings to the interview to be reviewed by our teachers.  Participants are then chosen based on merit and need, we hope to reach students who would otherwise not receive services.

HS2AS Interview Day for Winter/Spring Session
Saturday, February 15th, 9:00am – 2:00pm
Queens Council on the Arts
37-11 35th Ave, (Entrance on 37th Street)
Astoria, NY 11101

For more information visit: http://queenscouncilarts.org/student/
