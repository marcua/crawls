Sorry for the late posting, but we had a great time on the tour!  Along the way, we stopped at the cultural institutions/landmarks noted on the Hike NY map and made note of all the remaining "Hike NY Long Island City" signs.  We started out with 4 and picked up a 5th at lunch.  We started the hike on Broadway and 36th Street and made our way along 43rd St. over to Sunnyside, where we discovered a Greenmarket on Skillman and 43rd St.  Following the trail as best as we could, we wove through Sunnyside down 48th Avenue back into LIC.  However, due to an unmarked street on the map, we lost our way for a short while, but got back on track when we finally located Thompson Ave. and crossed over the Sunnyside Rail Yards.  After pausing at the Queens County Courthouse, we made our way to Lounge 47 on Vernon Blvd. for much-needed rest and a food break.  After lunch, we trekked down Vernon Blvd. back into Astoria for the final leg of the hike.  I left the tour at Socrates Sculpture Park, but the four remaining intrepid hikers continued for the small remaining portion of the trip.  A good time (and plenty of good exercise) was had by all!

We are planning on doing the hike in reverse hopefully sometime in the near future before the cold weather is upon us.  It would be great to have more folks join us!
