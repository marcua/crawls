With the great ingredients in our neighborhood this should be easy.  Also apps are great so you are not all sweaty in the kitchen when she shows up.

Think Astoria Tapas:

International meats for the homemade orange zest sausage. Cook it off and bias cut.  Fingers only.  Its a date right.

Semolina bread slice spread with marscapone cheese, barely drizzled with chestnut honey from Dave and tony salumeria. Lay on a few mint leaves (or basil) and wrap the whole thing in one nice wide slice of Proscuitti.  The mint is a new twist. Nice freshening flavor as well and good for the date.

Crostini with hard boiled egg slice over swipe of black truffle butter (from International meats).

Cheese board: Gorgonzola, aged Parmesan, dried figs warmed in oven to soften a bit, quince paste or jam.

Poached shrimp peeled and tail on. Lemon zest garlic aioli (olive oil mixed with mayonnaise. not kraft).

Go to one of the middle eastern shops on Steinway for vegetarian stuffed grape leaves. Ask for ones with cinnamon and raisins. Put on platter with bowl of olives and lemon peel.

Blood orange martini. Fresh squeezed blood oranges, Cointreau and bourbon. Mix with ice and into glasses.

You're a rock star.
