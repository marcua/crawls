yes, i too would suggest looking at the artist's book before getting anything.  many artists are good at one style but other styles...not so much.

I would not limit yourself to the neighborhood, that might be doing yourself a disservice.  You might want to consider going to the annual NYC Tattoo convention this upcoming weekend at Roseland.  There are tons of artists from all over the world and you can look at all of the portfolios before choosing an artist.  I think that the personalities of both the artist and the "tattooee" are very important.  You will end up with a better design if you can communicate with each other well.
