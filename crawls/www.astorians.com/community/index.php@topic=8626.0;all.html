Just for your information, CPAs who are in private practices don't have the luxury of specializing in sole proprietorships. That would be akin to a doctor who is Board Certified in Orthopaedics specializing in the big toe of the left foot.

You need to interview accountants with very specific questions pertaining to your business to determine how much knowledge they have regarding your exacting needs. And don't be afraid to ask for a fee-free consultation. If they want your business, they will be glad to offer you a half hour of their time.
