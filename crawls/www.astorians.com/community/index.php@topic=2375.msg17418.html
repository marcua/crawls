I joined Zipcar and use a pick-up location on Queens Blvd.  The daily rates wind up being the same as a conventional car rental, but they also have hourly rates (I believe that's for a rental period of less than 8 hours) which start at $10/hour.  Also, you can pick up or drop off a car anytime, which is convenient for Sundays, as other rental places I looked into either were closed on Sunday or had limited hours.  They have different locations in Astoria, LIC, and Sunnyside.
