Title: Flaming drinks rule!
Post by: geowrld on February 04, 2008, 08:40:39 AM
My bf and I went to Nuevo Jardin this weekend because we noticed they have a Flaming Volcano for Two on their menu.  The food there is good - not great, not awful.  The Volcano, however, was awesome.  It was some sort of pink frozen thing in a large ceramic bowl that had a volcano sticking out of the middle into which they had poured 151 or lighter fluid or something and lit it.  Then they gave us 2 very long straws to drink the thing. 

It was very "tiki room" - always wanted to try something like this and it was alot of fun - and we were wasted on 2 of them.

Btw, Nuevo Jardin was PACKED on Saturday night.
