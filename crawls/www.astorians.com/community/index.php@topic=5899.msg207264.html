I live on 29th street halfway between the Broadway and 36th Ave N/Q line stops and I love it! My friends are scattered all around Astoria and I've never once felt unsafe walking around. The ave's have everything you could need, gyms, deli's, your choice for grocery stores and shopping and the brunch places are endless! It's also nice to just wander around and check out the super cute houses! I also have a car but I rent a garage spot so I don't have to deal with street cleaning. I also find that anywhere in Astoria is within walking distance, some friends live on 38th street and 28th ave and from my place to theirs is only a 15 minute walk.

I <3 Astoria!
