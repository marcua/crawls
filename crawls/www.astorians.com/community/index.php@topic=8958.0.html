Thee thing to do before you die.
« on: December 18, 2007, 11:48:00 AM »
What would be the thee thing you want to do in your life before you die?  To give a little variety, lets make it one of personal satisfaction, rather then family or career orientated.

Mine:
Backpack the Eastern Mediterranian rim.  Start off at the Pyramids and go up the nice through Cairo upto Alexandria.  Then go east, snake through Gaza to Jeruselum, the Dead Sea, Damascus and Beruit.  Into Turkey, see Old Aleppo (I forget what it is called now) and then along the Southern Coast, see where the Cilclian pirates used to hang out and pass through old Lydia.  Up the coast through Smyrna and then across to Europe via Constantinople.  See Adrainople, Salonika, Athens, the Hot Gates and Kalamata and then up the Adriatic and visit the tiny towns which still have tiny Italian enclaves.  Rounding past Fiume and Venice, down the boot of Italy, go all around Sicily and then end up in Malta.

Take a whole year to do it.  Walk the whole way except where ferry rides are needed.
