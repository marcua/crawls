The practices at the Strand seem totally unsanitary to me. Refilling liquor bottles with water and leaving them on the table and then refilling them over and over? That can't be up to code. The fact that they're making their food in a bakery two doors down and shuffling it through an alley and being kept warm under heat lamps? Also can't possibly be up to code.
