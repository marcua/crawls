Re: G train at Steinway?
« Reply #6 on: March 17, 2009, 09:43:30 AM »
  Years ago, the G ran from Continental Ave to Smith/9 Streets all the time.  Ah, the good ole days.
  My rule of thumb; don't depend on the schedules, they change quicker than this winter's weather.  If an R comes first, take it to Queens Plaza to wait for an E or V, just in case.  If an E or a V comes into Steinway, take them straight to 23/Ely to transfer to the G.  If the G comes in, buy yourself a lottery ticket when you get to your destination.  If a work train comes in, spend about a half hour watching the MTA work crew smoke, goof around on the platform and talk to each other at ear-shattering volumes.  If an F or any other line happens to come in, eat the two dollars already spent on your fare, walk back up to Steinway Street and take a cab.
