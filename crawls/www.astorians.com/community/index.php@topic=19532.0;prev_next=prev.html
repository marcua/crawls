New York Cares is looking for 5,000 volunteers to give our city's public spaces a good spring greening for the 15th Annual Hands On New York Day.
Clean and revitalize 75 parks, gardens, playgrounds, community centers, and schools citywide and plant 20,000 trees.

Hands On New York Day also helps us raise funds to support New York Cares' year-round volunteer projects.
Like a walk-a-thon, we encourage volunteers to ask family, friends, and co-workers to pledge donations in honor of the service they perform that day.
Our work would not be possible without you, so in return, we do our best to thank you for your efforts by offering fabulous prizes to our top fundraisers.
