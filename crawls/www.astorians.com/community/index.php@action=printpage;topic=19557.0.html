Post by: newastorian35 on October 09, 2010, 10:14:36 PM
Natura Spa, ask for Irina or Marina.  It's $45 for a first time wax (because there's more hair and it's harder to do), $30 thereafter.  If it's your first time, I'd recommend Irina because Marina speaks mainly portuguese, and you may want to communicate that it's your first time.  They take you into a private room, and it's very clean.  I've been going there for 3 years.  You can just walk in there and they'll do it for you without an appointment.  You could make an appointment, but I find I always end up waiting just as long (or longer) with an appointment.
