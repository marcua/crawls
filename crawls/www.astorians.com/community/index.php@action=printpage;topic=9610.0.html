Unfortunately, we are limited to a certain number of shares by what the farmer can give us.  Our farmer supplies several CSAs around NYC, in addition to selling produce at a farm stand.  Right now, city-wide, there is an incredible demand for CSAs, and it's putting a real strain on area famers to supply everyone with good quality produce.  Just Food, the CSA umbrella organisation in NYC, is working hard to expand supply, but it's not easy.

To all of you who are on the waitlist, we're keeping track, and you should be hearing from us soon!  Astorians is a great place to find a share partner - threads like this are perfect.
