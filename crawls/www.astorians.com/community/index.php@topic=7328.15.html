To all of you that recommended Agnanti, THANK YOU, THANK YOU, THANK YOU!!! This was one of the most delicious meals I've had in I can't say how long. I'm 1/2 Greek and feel that I can I'm a good judge of Greek food and this was by far the best Greek meal I've ever had. I highly recommend the Saganaki, oh my God! Everything we had was delicious. We say outside and the weather today was wonderful for that. The service was ok, I've had worse, I've had better, but the food more then made up for it. The cost for 2 people, came to $70 with tip. We had shared 3 apps and the Stripped Bass special. I've spent more money on meals that in no way compared to this. We can't wait to go back and try some other items, but I think I'll always have the Saganaki. YUMMY.

I hope for those of you who try it enjoy it as much as we did.

liz

I ate at Aliada's last weekend and I don't know what all the fuss is about. The people working there are indeed very friendly and provided excellent service. But the food was decidedly so-so. My pork chops were overcooked and dry. My companion didn't think much of her combo platter. We both liked the saganaki quite a bit. Lemon potatoes were great, but I haven't met an order of lemon potatoes that I didn't like.

The atmosphere was much nicer than Uncle George's, but the bill was much higher. If I'm going to spend more than Uncle George's, I'm going to Zenon or Stamatis.
