To be honest, I've had some drinks of questionable quality there in the past half year or so.  Like, really bad coffee.  However, recently I went and had a cup of the April Spice, iced with soy, and it was probably the best cup of iced April Spice I've ever had there.  Steve also turned me on to this passion fruit flavored black tea, which I had hot and really enjoyed.  So, my recent experiences with the teas have been good.

The bad coffee I had was a mocha and it was weak and tasted like dirt.  I don't know what was up with that.

I'm also bummed about the lack of Rose Garden due to the stupid crap value of the US dollar.  Apparently this tea has shot up in price and so now they aren't selling it (understandable).  I'll miss you, iced Rose Garden! 

Despite it all, I like Freeze Peach.  The Hellgate CSA had a meeting there last week with our farmer, and it was a really nice time.  I didn't get the "cult" vibe at all, but maybe it's because I've been a patron there since 2005 when I moved to Astoria, plus I don't care how people look at me in there.  Also, the seating arrangements have changed, with big tables in the front and lounge chairs and couches in the back, where most of the laptop legion was hanging out.

With the new management, it will be interesting to see what happens!
