Sai's Organics is your best bet. They have 2 stores, the one located at 36-07 30th Avenue has the best selection of baked goods. They have muffins, brownies, and various baked goods. It also has your best choice of vegan groceries, the prices are reasonable. Their other store at 30-21 30th Avenue has a better selection of fresh sandwiches, soups, and some other food items.

Also, at most greek bakeries & cafes, pastries such as baklava or kataifi are vegan, with no dairy, or eggs.
