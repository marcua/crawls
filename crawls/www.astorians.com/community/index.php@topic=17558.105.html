  • Bangkok Tasty - I love spicy Thai food, and this place actually serves it up seriously spicy (and delicious) when requested - we're lucky to have both this & Wave Thai near Ditmars...
  • Balkh Shish Kabab House - this quirky, uncompromising place - one of my favorites - doesn't contort itself to be more accessible for non-Afghans, though they're always nice there. I've wondered if they might do more business adapting to become more of a mainstream neighborhood restaurant (no doubt there'd be some loss in any gain there, too)
  • Mango's NY - good new Middle Eastern restaurant on Steinway at 28th Ave. (used to be an Afghan place)
  • Ornella Trattoria Italiana - tasty pasta dishes there, with a great Thursday $10 special pasta night (yes, I'm a cheapskate)
  • Lefkos Pirgos Cafe - this Greek bakery/cafe continues to draw me in, even as it continues to be that I'll sometimes feel uncomfortable having the nerve to be there, asking to buy things (some good baked goods, and great late hours, help make up for the lack of warm, fuzzy customer service)
