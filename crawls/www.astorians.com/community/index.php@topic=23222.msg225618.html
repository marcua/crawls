We enjoyed a good meal there recently.  Had a small pizza as a starter and it was pretty good, especially for coming out of a brand new oven.  for entree I had arugula salad with steak and have to say i was surprised at how good the steak was.  Dressing pairing was bad so got that on the side and replaced with balsamic & olive oil.  My husband raved about his alfredo pasta dish with chicken (and he hates chicken!).  they do have gluten free pasta and whole wheat too, but it's not marked on menu.  The 'hostess' in platform heels that teetered us to our table was a bit strange and out of place, but otherwise positive experience.
