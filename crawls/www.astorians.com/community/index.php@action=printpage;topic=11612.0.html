Title: Re: Activities for 12 month old
Post by: jennifer on August 01, 2008, 11:39:48 PM
There is a meetup group called Astoria Stay at Home Moms

http://sahm.meetup.com/817/

You don't have to be a stay at home mom to join - but I think most of the members are (at least for a while anyway).
This group probably has an average of 10 activities per week for moms and their babies (or toddlers), sometimes just for moms and sometimes whole family activities.  I haven't even had my baby yet (fingers crossed that he will make an appearance any day now of his own accord) but I've been to a few meetups - have met some great families in the neighborhood and am looking forward to having lots of stuff to do this fall/winter while I'm off from work. 

The group is definitely worth checking out.
