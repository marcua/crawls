More abuse of police privileges
« on: May 11, 2008, 11:17:47 AM »
On the corner of Newtown and 41st street is a private vehicle with a police sticker on the windshield and a placard stating "On official  law enforcement business".  It's been parked illegally for 3 days now and no ticket.  In the neighborhood we all know it's an illegal spot because of the sharp corner and the building line.  Cars have been ticketed at that spot over the years but this car sits for 3 days and nothing.

I know it's illegal for sure because at a police community meeting I asked why I had been ticketed there and the officer explained a little known law that it's not just the legal curb area that counts but also the building line on that block. 

Just another instance of police abusing little laws that they kill us over with ticketing.  I bet if the police were on better terms with the public we would care less about stuff like this.

Wonder who might be watching the boards and what happens tomorrow.
