There used to be Nua on 46? and Broadway but now it's called Time Cafe?  I loved living in that area.  So close to all the great big stores on Northern Blvd, and only a 7 min walk to Steinway and a 10-12 minute walk to N train at Broadway, in case the R isn't running.  And in the summer, Cavo's outdoor garden is open, which is so beautiful and the food is wonderful.  It's not a pub but it's cool to hang out and have coffee there and fun drinks in the spring/summer.  42nd and 31Ave I think.  And the Dry Rite laundromat on 48th and 31 Ave is 24 hours, or at least it used to be.  Dillingers does have a good brunch and free mimosas!
