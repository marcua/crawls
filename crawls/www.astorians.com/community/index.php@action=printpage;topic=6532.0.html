Title: GAY MENS WRITING WORKSHOP
Post by: mondob on June 01, 2007, 03:50:10 PM
GAY NARRATIVES NYC is a small, intimate writing workshop for gay men in New York City...tell your life stories while honing your writing skills in a relaxed, fun atmosphere. Meets twice monthly in a comfy upper west side Manhattan apartment...no cost or fee...prompted exercises with on-the-spot composition, each read afterward to the group. Lots of dish and superior snack grazing too! To sign up go to

http://writers.meetup.com/578/?a=if_3562200&gj=ej4

and to read about the workshop format, sample the prompts, and read posted writing samples from members, go to

http://gay-narratives.blogspot.com/
