Title: Dodgeball in Astoria - Only 1 Team Spot Left!
Post by: ZogSports on August 19, 2008, 05:37:00 PM
Join the awesome Co-ed Dodgeball League in Astoria this fall!

Many Astorians have played with us before and we want to make  sure you have the chance to come back for another great season!  All new Astorians and friends are invited to join as well of course! 

Re-live all the fun of the childhood game, then follow it up with the decidedly adult organized post-game Happy Hours at local bars!

Only room for 1 more team and a few individuals!!
Don’t have a full team of friends who want to play? No problem! Join as an individual or with a small group and we’ll place you on a team. (Bonus: write "Astorians" in the "Comments" section and we'll put all members of this board on the same team!)

Whether you haven’t played in years or you fancy yourself a dodgeball professional, we welcome everyone to join our leagues.

The Fall season begins in September and runs into December. Dodgeball games are played on Tuesday nights.

Not into Dodgeball? Check out our Touch Football league which plays on Weekends in Long Island City!

Details and how to register can be found at www.zogsports.org Questions? Email info@zogsports.org

About ZogSports
ZogSports, the charity-focused, social, co-ed sports league, donates a portion of all proceeds to charities chosen by our teams. Join our leagues and play for a charity of your choosing! Then head out to our organized post-game happy hour to socialize and feel good knowing that the bar is donating a portion of what you spend to yours and your fellow participants’ charities!
