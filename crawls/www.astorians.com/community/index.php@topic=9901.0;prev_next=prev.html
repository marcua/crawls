I don't really care for Gyro Uno.  Plus I don't think they are in this delivery zone.

I wound up getting Mr. Gyros for lunch today.  The gyro was pretty generic, nothing on par with what they serve at a place like Gyro World in Flushing.

Their fries were fresh cut, which was nice, but they were a little on the soggy side.  Portion sizes were good but nothing like the Aliada starch fest that also includes rice. 

Tzatziki sauce was a bit "watery" - I prefer thicker.

It was okay, but not great.  I can't see myself ordering from there again unless I am really super craving gyro.  It is at least better than the pre-cut stuff they have at the Bel-Aire. 

I really liked the gyro from Pamela Pizza on Steinway.  I was really bummed when they closed.

Bayside is indeed home to several nice gyro-souvlaki places, including Fontana's, Gyro World and Gyro Corner, all near each other in the Francis Lewis/Northern Blvd. vicinity.
