We've had trouble with our toilet for years, but it's been intermittent and would eventually resolve itself.....after much plunging/flooding/washing/repeat a couple more times.

The other night, came home to find my new roommate stressed out over it. She called a plumber who used an auger to clear it up, $100 later it was working again.

This lasted a day. Then it stopped working the next afternoon.

Because neither of us are big-time TP wasters, nor do we flush anything that doesn't belong in the toilet (besides the occasional cockroach), we got tired of being accused of mistreating the plumbing.

While I've always figured this was part of the life of a renter, my roommate wasted no time Googling the subject, understandably pissed that we had to wait on and pay for a plumber when there was nothing we did wrong. In the past, we never called a plumber b/c we didn't want the expense, and it was hard to say WHAT was wrong exactly.

We'll see what happens, but after nearly seven years, it would be nice if the landlords repaired/paid for SOMEthing.  :-P The leaky windowsills, refridgerator, and cracks in the floor are one thing.....but we need the damn toilet to work!!
