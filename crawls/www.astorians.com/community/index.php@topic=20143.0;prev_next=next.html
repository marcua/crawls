Marino's Fish Market on 30th Ave. closed
« on: October 22, 2010, 04:45:01 PM »
I came Astorians to see if anyone knew why Marino's Fish Market had closed up shop, and was surprised to see it wasn't yet a topic. So I thought I'd ask if anyone knew what happened?

As the sign still says "Since 1932," so I was just surprised last week to see a different sign in the window -- one referring patrons to another fish market up toward Ditmars (I can't remember the name now..."Best" something or other?).

Now there is a FOR LEASE sign up and that's kinda sad to me. I remember being delighted when I moved to 37th St. to be so close to it and now, poof, like so many other great Astoria institutions...at least there is still a fish market one block west. How spoiled we are here (in a good way)!

ah well...

Maybe we'll get another Duane Reade there
