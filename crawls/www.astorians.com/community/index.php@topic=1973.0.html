Pub Quiz this Sunday at the Irish Rover
« on: April 19, 2006, 06:41:53 PM »
Hi everyone...    Looking for something different to do?

We will be hosting a Pub Quiz at The Irish Rover starting this Sunday at 7pm --  It's a whole lot of fun -- plus you can win some cash.  We try to keep it to teams of up to about 4.  Come and test your knowledge of sports, geography, science, history, and more.  Trivia and beer -- what more can you ask for?

The Irish Rover is on the corner of 28th Ave and 38th Street.


Hope to see you there :)

P.S.:  I'd love to see an astorians.com team!!!
