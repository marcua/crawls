Title: Re: Yet another bagel shop opening!
Post by: StephanieL on November 15, 2010, 03:19:36 PM
I was impressed with the mini-bagel I got on Saturday.  Because it wasn't fresh out of the oven, it had cooled down enough to develop a hard crust, the kind that's tough to penetrate with a bread knife.  Lest you think that's a defect, old-school bagels are supposed to give your jaw a good workout; otherwise, they're just rolls with a hole.  The taste could be lightly sweeter--I don't know if the dough has enough malt in it--but I'm otherwise quite happy.

The owner's still in the process of training employees, but it's clear that they're eager for business.  I bought one full-sized bagel and two minis, and the owner threw in another full-sized one for free.  Prices are in line with the neighborhood--90 cents for a full-sized bagel and 55 cents for a mini--and they have a full array of spreads and smoked fish.
