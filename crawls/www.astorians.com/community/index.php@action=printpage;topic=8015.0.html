Post by: Alison on September 21, 2007, 09:01:31 PM
Dude, buy frozen fruit from the supermarket (that's your "ice" - get the fruit with no sugar/syrup added - like it says only "strawberries" or "pineapple" under the ingredients) and throw some in the blender with maybe a banana and a little milk or OJ, and you have HEAVEN. My fav was some frozen strawberries, frozen blueberries, a banana, and a little bit of oj to thin it out so it's drinkable. Yum! It would make about 2 pint glasses worth of smoothie goodness.

Indoor grill? Anything that you can make on the outdoor grill! It isn't the same, but it's pretty close. I'm a big fan of grilled chicken, but that's kinda boring. Or, paninis! just get another heavy pot or dish or something to press your sandwich down so it gets all flat and melty.
