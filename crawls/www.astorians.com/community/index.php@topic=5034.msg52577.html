I tend to listen to techno/electronica with no words when I work out. The fast tempos keep me going on the treadmill/elliptical. Recently, I downloaded a few podcasts for my iPod that have set BPMs (beats per minute). Some of the songs sound the same, but the beat is really what keeps my pace. I usually aim for 150-165 BPM.

If you go to the iTunes store>Podcasts>Health>Podrunner you will find the podcasts there.
