Muncan Foods
« on: February 13, 2007, 06:27:08 PM »
Has anyone ever been to this place?  They're talking it up on Chowhound lately.  Sounds pretty awesome if you like pork products!

http://www.chowhound.com/topics/367259
Here's a tidbit:

It's not just pork, and what they have goes well beyond Romanian. They make Yugoslav cevapcici, pork, beef and lamb basturma, a couple of types of kielbasa, some German-style wursts, headcheeses, some other Yugoslav sausages, smoked ribs, slab bacon, pigs' feet and ears, pork and chicken loaves, all sorts of smoked loins, salamis, bolognas...

Just started going there and have only tried a couple of things so far, but I can say the beef pastrami is a stunner. Salty, peppery, garlicky, lean and crusty, with a great, firm texture. The one I got was straight from the smoker, still warm and giving off steam. Wow.

Broadway in the mid-40s, north side, green awning. Hours seem to be roughly 8:30 to 5:30.

Muncan Food Corporation, 4309 Broadway, Long Island City, NY, 718.278.8847
