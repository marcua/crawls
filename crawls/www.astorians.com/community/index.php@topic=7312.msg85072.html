My best friend's wife gave birth to a healthy baby girl on Friday afternoon  :mrgreen: 

I was wondering if anyone had any suggestions for a gift.  I sort of went "all out" for his first child about four years ago because like I said, he is my best friend and also the first of our little group that I grew up with to have a child.  This time, unfortunately, my budget is tight, although I want to get something that would be nice. 

Suggestions from all of you parents out there are especially welcome.  You can probably tell me best what to buy or to stay away from  :mrgreen:

Thanks in advance!

I like to get children's books that might have the child's name in the title. If it is a unique name, it might take a little longer, but my friend had a daughter ella, and we were able to find about 10 books that had her name. As a kid, having a book that has your name feels pretty cool. In each book, I dated it and put a little message for her. It is personal, and books are something that can be held onto for a while.
