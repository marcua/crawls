I had to take one at my most recent job.  It was humiliating as hell, urinating in a cup (which feels oddly hot in your hand -- not warm, hot, like a cup of coffee), with the attendant banging on the door after 20 seconds, I guess to make sure I wasn't smuggling in a bottle of baby's urine something, a la professional baseball players.  Felt totally wrong and offensive to me, an ugly byproduct of the "Just Say No" Reagan administration, which, by the way, was the last time I got high on illegal substances.  (While I don't miss pot, there'll always be a special place in my heart for magic mushrooms.)

Had two friends do the same for their jobs over the past few years, too.  One a heavy drinker, the other a pot smoker -- and they were sudden/random tests.  The drinker didn't seem particularly worried -- do people ever get fired for over-imbibing a legal substance like this?  But the pot smoker put himself into a world of self-imposed misery for the next few weeks, sure that his employers would find out his horrible secret and fire him.  (The guy's not a rasta, but he smokes pot about as regularly as most people social drink.)  Nothing ever happened, and his test had to come back positive as he'd gotten high that weekend, two days earlier.  I honestly think these things are given for a company to use as potential ammo with an employee should things get to the "need extra excuse for firing" stage.
