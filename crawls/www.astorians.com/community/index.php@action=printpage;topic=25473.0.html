The Kong Show on TV's “Spooktacular Night”

Be a part of the audience as we film for QPTV!


Monday, October 28th at 7pm

Cronin & Phelan’s Bar & Restaurant

38-14 Broadway, Astoria 11103

(Btwn 38th & Steinway St)

 (718) 545-8999



On Monday, October 28th at 7pm, at Cronin & Phelan’s Bar & Restaurant, located at 38-14 Broadway, in Astoria (Queens), Powell “Chuck E. Baby” Leonard & Miz E of “The Kong Show on TV” present “A Spooktacular Night” a 1-hour pre-Halloween show featuring comedy, music, and magic in Cronin & Phelan’s Little Theatre in the Back Garden with Stand Up Comedy Magic Irish Dave Cremin, Magic Rene Clement, and surprise guest comedians.  Plus, get ready for a special appearance of the Rock’n duo of Kevin “Bocko” Corrigan and Shane O’Connor and their rowdy band Begorrah!


Be a part of the audience as we film for QPTV! Enjoy the 1-hour show in Cronin & Phelan’s Little Theatre in the Backyard Garden, then stay for drinks at the bar, and even more fun with the “Astoria’s Got Talent” Open Mic at 9pm hosted by Cronin & Phelan’s own Stand Up Comedy Magic Irish Dave Cremin.  He’ll make the drinks appear, you make them disappear.



Wear a costume if you dare, for a chance to win fun prizes.
