Golden Brown Doggy Loose Among Golden Brown Leaves - Lost?
« on: November 30, 2009, 10:39:17 PM »
Yesterday morning, or the one before, I was standing by my window (24th Street at 36th Ave), looking at the wind blow the golden brown leaves around on the lawn when I saw a little dog, generally chihauhau shaped, the same color as the leaves, playing among the leaves.  The wind was so strong I wondered if it would blow the little cat-sized dog over.  I kept expecting someone to come and pick up the dog, but nobody did.  The dog eventually ran out of the lawn area, sniffed a fire hydrent, ran under a parked suv, tried to cross the street, ran back to the hydrent.  A man came by walking a similar shaped black dog, but it was clear the golden brown dog was not with them.  It ran away down 36th Ave.  I wasn't feeling well and couldn't go out to investigate.  Has anybody heard of dog in this are being lost?
