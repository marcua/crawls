H E L L O!
I'm Luis F Martinez - aka Lou or Louis as most people call me.  I'm an environmental consultant turned contractor.  I'm also a RE broker.  I do work in the area.  I resisted doing more business in Astoria in the past - I actually found Jackson Hts character more rooted and stable.  The more time I found myself in Astoria I realize that it was truly a dynamic place to do business.  I'm originally from the Bronx and raised in PR but now live in Holliswood.  I find myself practically in Jackson Hts, Astoria and adjacent areas on a daily basis.  I just signed lease and will be opening a small store front in the area.  I hear that Astorians are meaner, haters, immature and love to hide behind their keyboards when they talk trash...heh (I Love it!) - I think that's a byproduct of being young.  Is it me or there's see a big gap in the age groups?  I see lots of bright beautiful young people, working hard making ends meet - paying their rents - looking at the future.  Then  I see much older people - home owners, literally retired and semiretired.  Point is - H E L L O!
