I met someone at a dinner Sunday evening who just switched to Fios. He's very happy with the service.


From what I understood - it was a noisy room - he got a triple play package. This includes tv, internet and 2 phone lines. He said the price is super reasonable and so far the customer service has been excellent. The deal he got is for 2 years.
