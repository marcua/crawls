Socrates Sculpture Park Greenmarket opens this Saturday, June 1.
« on: May 31, 2013, 10:54:57 AM »

Alongside Socrates Sculpture Park's workshops, yoga classes, sculpture exhibitions and stunning views, the Socrates Sculpture Park Greenmarket is a great place to spend a Saturday morning. Shoppers will find a bountiful selection of seasonal produce from the Black Dirt region of Orange County, Mexican specialty produce from Richmond County, orchard fruit and juice, meat, poultry, eggs, and baked goods.

The market is located inside the Socrates Sculpture Park at Vernon Blvd and Broadway and will be open every Saturday from 8 a.m. – 4 p.m., June 1[size=-1]st[/size] through November 23[size=-1]rd[/size]. Cooking demonstrations will be held every week around 11 a.m.

* EBT/Food Stamps/Debit/Credit accepted at market info tent.
* Compost Collection by BIG!Compost – 10 a.m. to 12 p.m.

Visit Greenmarket's website for up-to-date information on at-market activities throughout the season:grownyc.org/socratesgreenmarket and follow us on our social media accounts:  Facebook: Queens Greenmarkets, Twitter:  NYCGreenmarkets and Instagram: QnsGreenmarkets

Brittany Ryan Market Manager Greenmarket GrowNYC[/u][/b][/url] 51 Chambers Street, Suite 1231 New York, NY 10007 bryan@greenmarket.grownyc.org[/b]
