Thought it was time for an update...

A group of fellow Astorians and I went to Wunderbar last night and had a good time.  It was fairly quiet in there for 7-9 on a Friday night but the staff said business is slowly picking up.  I'm sure its location is a bit of a turn off  (37-10 11th Street) but they have some great specials and a lengthly menu that are worth checking out.

Our group got the $45 platter of a variety of wursts which came with 2 sides - sauerkraut and red cabbage and potato salad.  We also ordered the potato pancakes and the spatzle.  Most of us enjoyed the food and we probably could have ordered a bit more since we had 8 people.  In addition we went for dessert (all were $5) and the best comments went to the apple streudel which came with vanilla ice cream.  I had the german choc. cake and others tried the choc. mousse and cheesecake - I'd say they were decent, obviously from a bakery which is fine but I was hoping for something homemade.

Service was great and they were very helpful explaining the menu items to us.  I'd definitely give it another shot as the atmosphere is unique and there is plenty of room in there.  Lunch deals look great...wurst sandwich, side, and a beer for $12 though we forgot to ask if they have it on the weekends.  Give it a try!
