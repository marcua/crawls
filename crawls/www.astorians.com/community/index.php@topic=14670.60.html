I never cared much for fireworks on Easter, it has absolutely nothing to do with the resurrection of Christ, but can appreciate it if it's done organized and appropriately. The organized fireworks are usually done just after midnight, not 2-3 hours later. Several of the greek churches in our area have banned firework celebrations for Easter, and even have police presence until after midnight mass is over to help enforce it.

I had attended my church for midnight mass, and returned home, and went to sleep. I was awaken startled from a deep sleep around 2:30am by very loud banging noises outside my house that sounded like gunfire to me, not firecrackers. It wasn't until I got my bearings that I realized it was probably powerful fireworks. By the time it stopped, maybe 10 minutes later, I was wide awake. Other neighbors were also awakened and looking out their windows. I could not fall back to sleep until after 5:00. I do not have a serious sleeping problem, however I sometimes do have trouble going back to sleep when I'm startled awake.
