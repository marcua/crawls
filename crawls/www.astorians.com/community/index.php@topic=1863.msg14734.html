Sorry Flooz.  It is on the east side of 29th st, right next to Plaza.  I think it has a green awning.  I believe you went to the place on the southeast corner of 29th and 30th Ave.  (blue awning).    Sorry for the bad directions.   You should definitely go back... good food and good selection of mexican beers.

Also another great Mexican secret of the 29th st and 30th Ave area is La Hidalguense.  The address is 25-32 30th Ave.  It is on the South side of the street near the hospital and next to a pharmacy.  This probably deserves its own post.  It is a mexican restaurant and bakery.  I am particularly fond of their Huevos Divorciados.  It is two sunnyside egges over corn tortillas with black beans one covered in salsa verde and the other egg covered in a spicy red sauce.  It is served with rice and beans.  All for just $4.  I usually get it at least once a week.
