Bit the bullet and signed up with ConEd Solutions today.  Well, I started processing through PowerMove, they need to get back to me still but I'm on my way!  I'm going to do variable rate hydro/wind.  1 cent more per KWH less 4% sales tax would have broke even on my last bill.  I'm not expecting any real change.

I really think a better job needs to be done by the power companies to promote green energy.  I was surprised to see how little of an impact it will have on my bill, yet how good it is for the environment.  I read somewhere that an average household using 520 kwh/mo of green energy instead of coal would reduce carbon output equivalent to a car driving 3,000 miles.  That's pretty significant!

I've talked a couple friends into getting green energy now too which is always a good thing.  So, if you've made the switch TELL SOMEONE YOU KNOW!  Let them know how easy it is and how little change they will see in their bill.  "Green Energy: Light on the environment AND light on your wallet!"
