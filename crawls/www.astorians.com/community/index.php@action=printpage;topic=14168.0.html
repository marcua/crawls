Title: ACK Astoria Crafty Knitty Thursday March 5, 2009 at 8pm ish
Post by: cabanird on March 04, 2009, 08:26:59 AM
ACK stands for Astoria Crafty Knitty. Our group meets every Thursday at about 8pm ish to knit, crochet, and do other crafts. The group is open to anyone who enjoys knitting, crocheting, other needle/fiber arts, or to those who wish to learn how to knit or crochet. If you wish to learn, please bring your own yarn, appropriate needles/hooks, and someone will be there to help you start learning your new craft! This group is for adults 18 years old and up.

So, with that, please join us today, Thursday February 26, 2009 at 8pm ish at Lefkos Pirgos at the corner of 23rd Ave and 31st Street underneath the N/W Train Station at Ditmars Blvd ( 22-85 31st Street 718 932-4423). We tend to sit in the back area of the cafe, so please join us!

for more info and other updates, please go to our blog:
http://community.livejournal.com/astoriacrafty

feel free to private message me if you had any other questions!
