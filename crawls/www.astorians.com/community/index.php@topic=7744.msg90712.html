I was about half a block away from the train when it happened. We were walking a friend of ours to the train. There were lots of people, but after everyone heard the shots, so many people came running down from the platform that I wasn't able to tell what was going on, other than people were pouring out of the stairs.

A Manhattan-bound train pulled in to the station in the time that it took for the cops to get there, which was a few minutes. Those who left the train walked past the crime scene (read: victim), and were quickly questioned by everyone on the street.  They said it was a guy lying on the platform, dead. 

It was random and scary. I will say that everyone on the street was super calm, I didn't see anyone panicking, and people just kept their cool.
