Title: Re: Aladdin
Post by: afarber on July 17, 2006, 10:49:49 PM
I don't know about the one on 30th ave, but I live right by the one on 36th ave, and I very rarely go there.  I certainly have been enough times to have an opinion, though, and here it is.  I'm not a big fan.  The food is good enough, and rather inexpensive, but the selection is terrible.  They have a very large menu posted above the counter, but they are only making the 3 or 4 items that are written on the white board behind the counter - and these are usually the same 3 or 4 items.  Very odd.  It may be different for lunch, but dinner time is this way.  They do have good samosas if you want a quick snack, and their deserts are good.

I always liked the place a few doors east along 36th ave...Boikali I think it was called.  They had the same menu, but would actually make what I ordered.  That's been closed for renovations for quite a while and should be opening soon under a different name (bangala garden, or something).

Hope that helps!
