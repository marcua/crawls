Motorcycle Gangs are Terrorizing Astoria

I'm all for personal freedom, let your freak flag fly...but the motorcycle gangs that terrorize Astoria during the summertime are beyond the pale. The perimeter roads of Astoria Park are treated as a race track, the law is disregarded: eight to ten riders lean into every turn, blowing through STOP signs. Pedestrians are treated as road kill. Engines are revved, wheelies are popped...all within a few feet of baby strollers. The "Quiet Zone" signs posted in the park read like absurd jokes.

I ask you, what can be done?
