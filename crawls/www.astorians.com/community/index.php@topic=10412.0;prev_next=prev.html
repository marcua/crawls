EFF UPS

Have to get it out of my system...
I ordered an excerise bike (thanks to the help of the board) and had it delivered. Usually I have things delivered to my office, but I can't exactly get that home from work. I never usually have a problem with UPS. So anyhoo....they ship it; I'm tracking it and it was supposed to arrive on Friday which was perfect because I was taking a day off from work that day anyway. And nothing happens which is really really wierd b/c it was scanned as being on the truck and out for delivery. I know they can do a false scan for when it arrives or departs a station-like an anticipatory scan but you can't fake a scan onto a truck. By Saturday it was still showing that as the last scan but a rescheduled delivery for Monday. I called UPS who basically can't explain it other than for whatever reason it went back to the Maspeth station and would be delivered on Monday. But I thought you can't return with packages unless the recipient wasn't there AND you left a door tag. So Monday rolls around and the tracking page doesn't change and now the local station is searching for it and doesn't find it and they can't guarantee it's on the truck (this after being assured on Friday it HAD to be on the truck and it HAD to be delivered). Basically they lost it. It supposedly got all the way to the truck and they lost a 50 pound box!!!! How in the hell do you lose a 50 pound box off a fracking truck?
So now the shipper has to initiate a trace (which they did today) but I have to wait until the trace is complete and can take up to 10 business days (however I've filed traces at work and it's taken waaaay longer). Then and only then can I be shipped another one, which will come-UPS ground...again. Another 5-10 days. Now I may have to wait nearly a month for it (when you factor in weekends).
I'm royally irritated and just wanted to share my horror story and just basically ask the rhetorical question-how the hell do you lose a fracking 50 pound box?

rant over...well in print anyway...

I absolutely echo the UPS is horrible sentiment. THEN... and this is the REAL thing I hate about UPS...
