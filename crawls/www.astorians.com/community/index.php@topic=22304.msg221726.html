I LOVE MINCA. Also for a more pricey, crowded, and amazing broth experience, Ippudo. Holy crap delicious. Too bad there's a two hour wait whenever I go to Ippudo. It's like the Taverna Kyclades of ramen places.

As for what exactly a ramen place is, yeah, it's Japanese ramen noodles. What makes a good ramen place special is delicious savory broth, and you get it with things in it like slices of pork as well as a hard-boiled or poached egg, some sort of long mushroom, seaweed, whatever. It doesn't sound very impressive, but when done well there's nothing like it. If this place on Ditmars is authentic and good I will be quite happy.
