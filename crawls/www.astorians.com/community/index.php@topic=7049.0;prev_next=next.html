I ate at Cinema Paradiso when they first opened in January for my birthday and
never went back.

The food is good, but the service was awful. Our waiter didn't speak very good English.
I ordered bronzini, which was on special that day. I ordered the filet - you had a choice of that
or the whole fish. My boyfriend ordered the chicken special and instead was brought chicken
marsala from the standard menu.

I actually had to argue with the waiter when he brought me the whole fish and I wouldn't eat it.
The owner came out and said the "kitchen didn't understand."

Plus they have no wine other than a house wine. On the back of the menu they list various whites
and reds, yet had none of them.

After that, I never went back.

I've looked at Valverde's menu and it's much more reasonable than CP. Plus people here
have given it good reviews.

There's thread about CP from a few months ago - read it and judge for yourself.
