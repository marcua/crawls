Oh, it's not a donut place - it's a 24/7 coffee shop called Donuts, on 7th Ave. between 8th and 9th Sts.  I don't know whether they actually sell donuts, though.  The bacon/egg/cheese sandwiches from there are way better at 3am (or 2, or 4, or 5...) than during the day -- our theory is that it's because they aren't as busy.  And they deliver at 3am -- no minimum.  It rocks, and it is consistently the best bacon egg and cheese sandwich I've ever had.

Damn it, I still miss Brooklyn sometimes.
