Title: Great Bookstore
Post by: frozenfroot on September 06, 2004, 10:56:55 PM
hi wasabisam. saw your post about the lack of bookstores in astoria. its not just you. i havent found a single one. that sells used books - hand picked by people who actually like to read - with sofas near a window somewhere to hang out for awhile. maybe pick up a conversation with someone...



although slightly out of the way for astorians, the GOTHAM BOOKSTORE in midtown, and STRAND books on Broadway, near Soho are both great. Gotham is this tiny old basement place, thats packed full of well chosen used and new books - and the kids that work there love to read. Strand is bigger, slightly less personal - but still rad.



I just pulled the following address off the net for Gotham... i would highly recommend it to anyone who  not only loves to read - but loves books, and everything that goes with them



Gotham Book Mart

41 W. 47th St. (Midtown West)

near Sixth Ave.

212-719-4448
