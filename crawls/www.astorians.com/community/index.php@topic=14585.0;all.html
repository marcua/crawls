We went tonight.

This place was awful.

First, they did not have three of the beers on their menu and the waiter didn't know until he checked at the bar each and every time. Then, with the Fish & Chips entree, they did not serve the chips until we had a lengthly conversation with one of the servers about the fried fish being the "fish" and the french fries being the "chips". The food was also severely sup-par. I didn't expect fabulous food, but the fish was clearly frozen. In some places, that would be understandable for a pub, but several bars in Astoria have fresh fish in their Fish & Chips (like Sunswick). Finally, they waited to let us know that their credit card machine was down until we got our bill and put out our card.

The final straw was that we were very clearly unhappy with this appalling service and the owner or manager avoided eye contact with us as he brought bottles of sambuca to his friends at the table next to ours.

I understand that this is a pub and I don't expect four-star service or anything resembling it, but I did not except such an awful disappointment. I will not be patronizing this establishment again, which is a shame, given the beautiful decor and great location.
