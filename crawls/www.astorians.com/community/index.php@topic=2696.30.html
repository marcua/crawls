Too funny :) We all (us and our friends) get a bit teary when the name Dynasty comes up ;( We would go for big dinners and order lotsa yummies and just sit, relax and eat and chat with the lovely waitresses and waiters. I thought I died and went to heaven when I got a job in the area as I could eat there everyday, however the dream only lasted for so long. I see someone posted about Columbia Cottage and although they are good and some of the fine folks from Dynasty went to CC, they just do not have the selection that Dynasty did. As far as Eastern on Northern is concerned, like I said we have only ordered mostly from the Japanese side of the menu and it was superb. He got the katsu bento box, which is HUGE for the price, the Miso soup is YUM and the house salad is run of the mill, yet still fresh and tasty.  I ordered a few veggie rolls and an  order of the veggie dumplings, which were tasty, however a lil on the cool side (which was refreshing during the recent heat wave). From the Chinese menu I have only ordered the Broccoli with brown sauce and it was O.K. (NHW's is much better). I will post the number on the board when I get home.
I would like to let you know of another recommendation for Chinese, however not in Astoria, it is in midtown Man. named Wu Liang Ye and it comes very close to replicating the authentic taste of Dynasty. We try to make it there once or twice a month, however they are a lil more pricey, however very much worth it and the Schezuan dishes rock! Best dishes!
