World's Fair site needs your help
« on: November 11, 2009, 10:32:39 AM »
This weekend, 11/14 and 11/15 from 9am-3pm, the New York State Pavilion's Tent of Tomorrow will get a much-needed weeding in preparation for restoration.

Back in the day, when the World's Fair was in full swing, this amazing structure included a canopy of stained glass and the world's largest terrazzo map. Designed by Philip Johnson, it was a scale road map of New York State:

http://www.nywf64.com/newyor04.shtml
http://www.nywf64.com/newyor05.shtml

Since then, the canopy glass has long been broken, and the floor has become a ruin.

http://www.nytimes.com/2008/04/07/nyregion/07map.html
http://www.flickr.com/photos/marcellinar/3218082030/
http://www.flickr.com/photos/ny_sed/480873139/
http://www.flickr.com/photos/tomspixels/620098880/
http://www.flickr.com/photos/14504460@N02/2278983052/

However, thanks to funding for restoration, this will now receive the treatment it deserves! Students will work on its restoration, but first the weeds need to be pulled out by hand (herbicide would damage the floor.)

Volunteers need to dedicate at least three hours, but it goes by quickly, plus the park itself is worth a visit. Corona is nearby, where there's a variety of awesome food to be found:

    Corona Pizza
    Lemon Ice King of Corona (right across the street from Corona Pizza)
    Mama's Italian Deli
    Mama's Bakery
    Leo's
    Empanadas del Parque
    new Mexican place the name of which escapes me, around corner from Mama's and Leo's etc.

Other attractions:

-Unisphere
-Queens Botanical Garden (though off-season, it boasts a renovated "green" lobby)
-Flushing (all kinds of Asian food, produce and gift stores- Queens' own Chinatown basically)
-Children's Science Museum
-Zoo
-Flushing Meadows Corona Park Pool & Ice Rink (brand-new and largest rec center in NYC)
-Fowler Rec Center (annual membership is about $75)
- Kissena Corridor (awesome bike or walking path that traces the former Vanderbilt Raceway)

I'll get more details later, such as if there's a need for gloves etc.
