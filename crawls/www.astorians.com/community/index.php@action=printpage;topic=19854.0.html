Title: Re: Best places to watch football on Thanksgiving in Manhattan?
Post by: KidMurdock on November 23, 2010, 01:21:46 PM
The two places I was going to recommend don't really have comfortable seating per se, but are great places to spend a day drinking, eating and watching football.
The first is the Blind Pig on 14th st near 3rd ave. Great food, good beer choices, lots of screens, all in all a good spot to watch football. The other is Brother Jimmys, where the same can be said, and there are locations all over the city.
For comfy seating and the age group I typically look for, I stick to my couch, so I'm no fun there.
