Indigo would also be a good choice. They have a big back area that they call an indoor garden/patio. There is a roof (which they open in the summer) and heating. They've accommodated large groups from this website before and have the ability to easily push tables together to give you lots of space. There's a fireplace and candles. It's really very pretty, but the front is also a bar so they have a good, upbeat vibe. The food is also pretty darn good.
