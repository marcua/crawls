I understand what you're saying, newastorian.  I found that I was very nervous about going to my first mixer.  I didn't know what to expect.  Would people like me when they actually met me?  Was there something that I posted that offended a particular member who would confront me about it at the mixer?  Will the online personality that I tried so hard to create be shattered in the real world (just kidding, of course)? 

I asked all of those questions and many more and that and my busy work schedule kept me away for a few months before I just made up my mind and went.  It turned out to be one of the best evenings I have had in Astoria. 

Since then I have organized and attended many Astorian events and made strong bonds with some of the members.  And it's all because I decided that I wasn't going to let my anxiety and doubt get in the way of meeting new people and enjoying myself.
