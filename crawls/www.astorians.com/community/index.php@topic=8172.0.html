finding an apartment with bad credit - help!
« on: October 03, 2007, 07:13:20 PM »
So I'm wondering if anyone has any suggestions on how to go about finding an apartment with bad credit.

I make a very good salary - in the $70,000 range - but my credit's bad. real bad (I've seen the report). no bankruptcies or liens, but tons of late payments, largely the result of an almost pathological inability/unwillingness to simply open up a credit card bill and pay the damn thing.

(ridiculous, I know)

anyway, paying rent's never been a problem for me. I've only recently started my search, but I feel like the credit thing is just going to make things impossible. I haven't had to confront it since I've been living at the same place for many years.

I'm looking for a one bedroom or a very cool studio. Something in the $1200-$1300 range, although I'd consider going slightly higher for a place I really loved.

so yeah, any help/ideas would be greatly appreciated.
