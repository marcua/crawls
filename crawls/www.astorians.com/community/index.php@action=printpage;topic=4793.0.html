Title: HARPSICHORD CONCERT AT WOMEN'S STUDIO CENTER
Post by: WomenStudioCenter on January 19, 2007, 07:25:08 PM
WOMEN'S STUDIO CENTER INC.
presents
a Harpsichord Concert, with Meg Cotner

Please join us for an afternoon of early and recent music by, about, and for women for the harpsichord, including a number of works by members of New York Women Composers. Music by Beth Anderson, Elisabeth-Claude Jacquet de la Guerre, Julie Mandel, Domenico Scarlatti, and others will be played, with commentary by the artist. Come for a wonderful afternoon of memorable music!

February 11, 4-6pm

TICKETS: $10.00 ea
