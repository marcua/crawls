Its terrible this is happening to you but security money cant be used for last months rent .I would never allow any of my tenants to do so . If I did and there were repairs to be made , then what ?    I know you said you left your apt in great shape , but just in  general ,  the security is for repairs .  NYS rent guidelines also says the same thing .  I have it written in all my leases cuz tenants have tried to do it in the past and then when something had to be fixed , they were gone .   If someone tries that now , they would be taken to court for non payment and their credit would be tarnished.     Im not going againt you , cuz I think its terrible what was done , but the security money doesn't have to be given on spot either  , as far as NYS says . There has to be a reasonable time period,  where in some states  rules say 30 days .NYS has this period between 30-60 days . The person checking the apt shoudve gave you something to sign saying an inspection was done and this this and that was found . Any problems should be written on the spot .  The landlord then has a time period to get the costs for those repairs .  Pin holes wouldn't cost to the amount of the deposit anyway .   So that's BS on their part .    Ive always given all my tenants back their deposit , and even small repairs were taken care of by me .  Doesn't mean in the future I would allow anyone to use a security for rent .  This is a business and each landlord has to treat it as that.     PS. next time take pics  and date stamp them.
