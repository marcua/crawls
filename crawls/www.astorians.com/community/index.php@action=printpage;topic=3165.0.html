There's really nothing to it.  Basically, for an iPod, you would want to get iTunes, which for PC's is a free download on Apple's website.  It is included with all new Apple computers.  You can upload music from your CD's easily into your iTunes library via your computers CD drive.  As far as purchasing music, you can either purchase single tracks or entire albums on iTunes.  Individual songs are $.99.  Whole albums range in price usually from about $10-15.  All this music is downloaded to your iTunes library.  And to transfer your music from iTunes to your iPod, all you have to do is simply connect the iPod to your computer using the USB cable.  As soon as it's connected, iTunes automatically starts up, and updates your iPod with all your music.  You can also pick and choose what songs you want to put onto your iPod.
