Briefly (because I have to get to bed):

1.  Three dips with pita:  hummus, baba ganoush and foul (fava beans)
2.  Ricotta and sun-dried tomato pita "quesadilla"
3.  Same as #2 but with spinach filling and garlicky yogurt-cucumber sauce, similar to tzatziki
4.  Whole spatchcocked quail, with black rice and stewed pumpkin
5.  Shrimp with white rice and stewed eggplant (which doesn't do the eggplant justice)
6.  Chicken with an Egyptian herb I've had before and am blanking on the name of that's not unlike spinach
7.  incredibly tender, stewed lamb with mixed vegetables and ?

Everything was fantastic.
