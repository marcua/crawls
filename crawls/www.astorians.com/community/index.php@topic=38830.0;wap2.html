Bilingual Sprouts is Partnering with Raising Astoria

(1/1)

Bilingual Sprouts:
Bilingual Sprouts is happy to announce our partnership with Raising Astoria. Raising Astoria is a one stop shop for parents to take workshops or Mommy and me classes. Contact us at BilingualSprouts@gmail.com or visit our website and BilingualSprouts.com or Raising Astoria at raisingastoria.com or follow the link https://clients.mindbodyonline.com/classic/admhome?studioid=25013 and register for our free  "Spanish Sprouts" trial class for our 2 and 3 age group October 6th.
Spanish Sprouts by Bilingual Sprouts is a Mommy ( daddy/ Caregiver) and me Spanish immersion program for children ages 2 to 7. Using an early childhood Spanish curriculum that strongly emphasizes and facilitates adult involvement, Bilingual Sprouts provides developmentally appropriate activities that are engaging, interactive and hands-on such as read-alouds, art, games, music and movement. Bilingual Sprouts will immerse your child in a new language while having fun
