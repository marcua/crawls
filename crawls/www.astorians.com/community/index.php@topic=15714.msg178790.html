Surf Astoria is a new Internet Cafe right off of the Ditmars N/W stop in Queens. If you're looking for a great place to use the internet by renting one of our PCs or using our free WiFi, get some work done away from home, print/scan/copy/fax, hold group meet ups, hold an event, or just try some awesome teas, then come on over. We're open 10 to 10 everyday!

We need your support and we're really interested in improving your community with this establishment, so come in with ideas for us too!
