I think that you would generally want to see a co-op that has a good amount of money in the reserve fund; how much would depend on the size of the co-op. You would also want to know about the underlying mortgage on the co-op as well. Before you buy a co-op, you would get a copy of their finances that I would strongly advise you have a real estate attorney review.
