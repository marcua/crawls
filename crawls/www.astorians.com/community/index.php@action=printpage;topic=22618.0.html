Title: Guilty Pleasures Burlesque, this Saturday, at The Secret Theatre!
Post by: AudreyJess on April 25, 2012, 01:05:00 PM
Hello, Fellow Astorian!  I am pleased to announce the debut of a new variety show as part of The Secret Theatre's new Late Night Series that is happening every Saturday night!  I know, it's technically in LIC (44-02 23rd St.) but you and I both know they're sibling towns and this theatre is awesome if you've never checked it out...they recently renovated our space with a new bar in front and the bbq will be grilling out on the loading dock!  We kick it off with "Like, Totally 80's, Ya Know?" featuring burlesque, comedy, and variety:  this time around Peter Aguero of Sunnyside will be doing what he does best and telling you some captivating 80's stories and Chaz Kangas of NYC (we still love him  :wink: ) will be showcasing his rapping talents with some 80's freestyle...start thinking up your best 80's references now because he'll ask the audience for suggestions!  Let's keep creativity thriving in the boro!  Cheers, Audrey Bloom
