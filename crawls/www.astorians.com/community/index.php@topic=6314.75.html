Quote from: krocker on October 17, 2007, 12:52:16 PM
I was wondering that too - why all the praise for Panera?  Can someone explain what the difference is between an Applebee's and a Panera that makes the former so reviled and the latter so loved? 


Oh, I'd be happy to!! First of all, they're completely different.  The menu at Panera is mainly 4 or 5 soups, salads, and sandwiches.  Applebee's has got all these more complex (not always a good thing) dishes which they never really get right.  The service at Applebee's is downright despicable.  I have not ONCE had an enjoyable experience there - every time I get attitude from either the host and/or my waiter.  Dishes are almost never served how they should be, they've been cold or lacking the main ingredients!

Panera, on the other hand, prides itself on its good service - and I can say this with some sort of authority as I have frequented the one by my hometown on LI way too many times to count, and it is definitely a noticeable characteristic of the place.  They're always doing the best they can to serve you, and are happy to get the manager to answer any questions that they cannot.  I know I sound like I work there at this point, but I'm just happy to see a chain that actually treats the customer like a guest rather than an annoyance.
