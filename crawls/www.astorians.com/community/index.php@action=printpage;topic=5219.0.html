Title: Re: Oy Vey! And then some!
Post by: Frappe on February 15, 2007, 09:51:58 AM
1. bubkes - nothing (emphatically)
2. chutzpah - Guts, audacity, ballsiness
3. futz - to fool around with
4. gonif - thief (minor, scoundrel type)
5. gelt - money; chocolate coins eaten on Chanukkah
6. gevalt - interjection of shock or dismay
7. glitch - a minor malfunction
8. kibitz - to offer unwanted advice
9. klutz - clumsy person
10. kosher - right, honest
11. kvetch - to complain habitually
12. maven - expert
13. mazel - luck
14. megillah - a detailed discourse, the whole thing (emphatically)
15. mensch - an upright man
16. meshuggener - a crazy person
17. noodge - to pester, nag, whine; as a noun, a pest or whiner
18. nosh - snack
19. pisher - an insignificant person
20. putz - an idiot, a jerk; a penis
21. schlemiel - an inept clumsy person; a bungler
22. schlep - to drag or haul; to make a tedious journey, a tedious journey
23. schlock - something cheap or shoddy
24. schmaltz - excessive sentimentality
25. schmeer also schmear - spread, bribe
26. schmo - a stupid person
27. schmooze - make small talk
28. schmuck - a contemptible or foolish person; a jerk
29. schnook - an easily imposed-upon or cheated person
30. shtick - comic theme; a defining habit/feature
31. schvitz - sweat
32. shpilkes - nervous energy
33. spiel - a sales pitch or speech
34. tuchus - buttocks
35. yenta - a talkative woman; a gossip
