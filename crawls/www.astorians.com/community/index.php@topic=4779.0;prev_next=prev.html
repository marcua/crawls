http://www.earthlink.net/highspeed/pricing/?id=cable

A lot of us here have Earthlink Cable internet service.  They are powered through Time Warner's cable lines, but your email and ISP is Earthlink.  You do not have to purchase cable channels to get Earthlink service.  If you call TWC to set up Earthlink service, you don't have to get cable channels and there is no extra fee because of that, BUT they will try and sell you cable and may give you a hard time.  Call Earthlink and set up the cable internet service through them.  They will partner you up with TWC for the installation, which should be free.  It's 29.95 for the first 3 months or so, then $41.95 after. You do pay TW.

Once you have the cable modem and are hooked up, split the cable and hook it in your tv and you will get a smattering of channels.  They are not much but they are clear and do the job.  And it's better than an antenna.
