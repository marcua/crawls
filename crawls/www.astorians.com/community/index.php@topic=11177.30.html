Re: Italian Food at Hell's Gate Bar?
« Reply #43 on: January 28, 2009, 12:39:06 PM »
I still go every few weeks, and it is still amazing. The cook takes great pride in his food, and you will understand when you taste it. The service can be slow with the food, but bear in mind there's just one guy back there making the dishes. It is worth the wait. My favorite dishes there:

1) Chicken Franchese w/ Linguini (This chicken is one of the best I've ever had)
2) Ravioli with vodka sauce. (One of the best vodka sauces I've ever had)
3) Linguini w/ White Clam Sauce (Very, very garlic-y, but delicious nonetheless)
4) Shrimp Fra Diablo (Mouth wateringly good)
5) Spaghetti w/ Meatballs (An italian classic done to perfection)

Enjoy!
