Bilingual Sprouts:
Bilingual Sprouts is a Spanish immersion program for children 24 months to 5 years of age right here in the heart of Jackson Heights. Founded and taught by two New York State certified Early Childhood Bilingual and Art Education teachers. Through interactive activities such as Spanish Literature read-alouds, art, drama, music and movement, Bilingual Sprouts will immerse your child in a new language while having fun! We offer classes  Tuesday or  Friday and have 3 sessions on both days. Adult participation is required by one parent or caregiver.Session 1 (2-3yrs old) 10:00am-10:45am Session 2 (4-5yrs old) 11:00am-11:45am Session 3 ( 2-3yrs old) Tuesday 12:00-12:45 (4-5yrs old) Friday 12:00-12:45. Contact us about our Trial classes starting June 30Th! At BilingualSprouts@gmail.com. We will be offering Saturday classes soon.We look forward to hearing from you.
