Title: New chocolate discovery
Post by: megc on June 12, 2006, 11:23:12 AM
While picking up some things at the Ditmars Trade Fair, I came across a very interesting chocolate bar:

Lindt Edelbitter Mousse Sauerkirsch-Chili

It's 70% cacao content chocolate covering a kind of chocolate mousse topped with sour cherry and chili.  It is very interesting, not overly sweet, and the sour cherry and spicy (but not too spicy) chili are a nice contrast to the sweeter mousse filling.  The entire packaging is in German and I only understand a little bit of it, but I don't see this as necessarily a bad thing.  Oh, there is another flavor, coffee.

Anyway, I think it's worth a try.
