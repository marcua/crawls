Title: Beginning of a new website-- Astoria Families
Post by: sarahespanol on September 04, 2008, 10:24:34 PM
I hope it's ok to post about another website:

A friend and I started a website to promote how awesome it is to raise kids in Astoria. 

Of course, it is absolutely not for profit (we used the free site "ning") and not for glory.   Just a labor of love--- and we hope that people use it to share ideas and get to know other families in the hood.

We JUST started it, so we need help getting it going.

There's an option to make groups if people want to make playgroups and playdates....... or arrange to meet for playdates on the forum......

One section is "Places to Go" where you can start discussions about your favorite places in Astoria (kid stuff or grown up stuff), as well as recommendations for places to shop for food, favorite books, etc.

We also have an events section with good events for the family......

Also a forum section-- though I very much like the forum section in Astorians......
