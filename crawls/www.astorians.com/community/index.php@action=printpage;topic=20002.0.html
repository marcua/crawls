Title: Free Music for Aardvarks Trial Classes
Post by: AllisonTartalia on December 14, 2010, 11:49:40 PM
Dear Families:

Just a quick note to let you know about some free Music for Aardvarks trial classes in Astoria.  Music for Aardvarks is a class geared toward children aged 6 months thru 4 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, and instrumental jam sessions. The original material used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.

If you would like to try a trial class or have any questions, please email info@musicforqueens.com, and feel free to join us at one of the following.

Thanks so much and Happy Holidays!

Allison
