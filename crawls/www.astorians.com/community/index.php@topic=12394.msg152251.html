Want to help build a new CSA in Astoria?
« on: September 23, 2008, 11:00:17 PM »
A couple of friends of mine and I are forming a new CSA and we’re looking for people interested in joining the core group of planners.  We need committed volunteers to help coordinate with our farmer, set distribution procedures, keep member records, handle outreach and education, and coordinate the distribution site and member volunteer schedules (mainly keeping track of which members will help with distribution on which days.) We’ll be drawing on information from Just Food and past experience to make this the best darn CSA ever, and we’re looking for Astorians (or others!) who want in.  Core members should plan on putting in around ten hours per week, especially during the months when we're organizing members and getting the distribution up and running.  If you're interested, let me know!
