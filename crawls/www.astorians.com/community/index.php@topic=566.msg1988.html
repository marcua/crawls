Questions and Answers as I remember them.
« Reply #18 on: August 11, 2005, 01:25:03 PM »
This is just a brief summary of the meeting.  Contact Cara for more info!!

Q: Contents of share 8/9/2005?
A: Green leaf lettuce or romaine (1 head); sugar snap peas (1/2lb); 2 green peppers; 1 purple pepper; 3 kirby cucumbers; 1lb squash (choose from 3 kinds - globe, yellow summer, zucchini); beets (1 bunch - 2 choices) -- flower share, 2 bunches of red fuzzy flower (elephant ear?)

Q: How to get to share site?
A: Take the 19A from Ditmars or 21st Ave to 35th Ave.  Share site is 1/2 block away from bus stop.  Bus runs frequently but not really on schedule.

Q: What are the hours of distribution?
A: 2:30-7:30, TUESDAY.

Q: If I don't like XX can I switch it for something else?
A: There's a swap box so if someone doesn't like something they can exhange it for something in the box (if there is something in the box).

Q: Cost?  Is there a payment plan?
A: Share is pro-rated for the summer.  Ask Cara for details.  Yes, payment plan is possible.

Issues without answers (discussions in progress):

- Let's pool our resources and figure out a way to rotate pick up.
- Possibility of members with cars making big pick up.  We would contribute gas money to offset cost.
- Possiblity of share site MOVING next year.  Maybe somewhere closer?

Not discussed but adding it here:

I'm going next Tuesday to pick up so anyone who is interested in signing up, checking out, or just keeping company can come along.  Not sure what time I'll go yet.

cheers,
jayme
