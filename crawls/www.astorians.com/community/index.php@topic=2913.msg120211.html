I had a legitimate recommendation for John at Astoria Apartments from a friend of mine. I really liked their place, too, but ended up finding something before he could show me anything.

You very well might be turning brokers off by telling them you're not ready to move for a while. The real estate market here is so quick, you can't start looking two months in advance, or even one month. It's a waste of their time (and yours) to have them take you around to places they know you won't take because you're not ready to move.

As for who you should call next, I recommend Kim at Halvatzis (she found my last place), Jay at Tai Chi (he found my current place) and Soula at Buy-Sell (she showed me a place I LOVED but my then-roommate shot it down because parking would have been awful).
