Piano Factory Apartments (off Vernon)
« on: August 15, 2010, 05:29:03 PM »
Anyone have thoughts or opinions about the Piano Factory Apartments off Vernon? I know they were originally supposed to be condos but now they are being rented as apartments. I took a tour and liked the apartments. The locations isn't the greatest but I have a car so the distance from the subway is not a deal breaker for me.

All of the one bedrooms are already taken and two bedrooms are out of range for me and my budget. At $1,450, studios were not too pricey for a brand new development with legitimate, top of the line appliances and fixtures.

So far I have not been able to find any useful information about the owner of the development, Angelo Acquista of the TTW Realty Group. I asked the broker about the owner and he told me that he did not know anything about the owner (yet) because he just recently got the contract to be the broker for the property.

Thank you I appreciate any information you can provide to a newbie.
