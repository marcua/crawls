The Post keeps lowering the bar on how pathetic their headlines can get. Either they have junior high school kids writing headlines like these, or they believe their readers have no intelligence or class.

Ike Turner was a rhythm and blues & rock and roll pioneer. He started with Ike Turner's Kings of Rhythm, played piano on the first rock/blues record to be a hit in 1951 titled Rocket 88 with Jackie Brenston at Sun Studios in Memphis, preceeding Elvis by 3 years. He later went on to be the most sought after guitar session man, and formed the Ike and Tine Turner Revue.

His accomplishments were later eclipsed by booze, drugs, and the well publicized spousal abuse of his wife Tina, who escaped from his grasp pennyless to evnetually make a big comeback as a solo artist. Ike did redeem himself in recent years, but his years of abuse of Tina left him a tarnished legacy.
