Professional sports is about money. I think these character clauses in contracts are window dressing. So the major league associations will swat all kinds of behavior on the wrist if they value the player.   If the women drop charges, recant, ask for leniency, that's just the "out" the league is going to latch on to. I'm not excusing it, it's totally bogus, just trying to explain why they get lenient treatment.   In time, despite all the outcry, the Vick case will be forgotten. I would not be at all surprised if he plays again.  I think that's why he only pleaded guilty to the dog fighting, but not the gambling. This plea deal gives him solid hope of returning after a short jail term.  It may look now that there is a bigger outcry over the dogfighting than the spousal abuse, but in the end all of them will play again.   There has been a steady drum beat of supporters crying  racism, example-making, others do worse, etc. that is setting the stage for his return.
