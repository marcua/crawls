Title: Re: Looking for a Bike
Post by: queenzbwai on May 05, 2010, 02:13:30 PM
If you want to spend less than $200 and don't want to buy it used, then your best bet will probably be Toys 'R Us or The Sports Authority.  Both on Northern Blvd.  A dedicated bike shop (Bike Stop on 38th Street and 28th Avenue in Astoria) usually doesn't carry adult bikes in that price range.
Title: Re: Looking for a Bike
Post by: LastStop on May 05, 2010, 03:01:59 PM
Craigslist is another source for used bikes. I often see bikes listed by people moving from the area, etc. that are in great condition.  Sometimes the local bike shops have refurbished bikes for sale in your price range. I know that Tony's Bikes on 23rd Av and 35th St. is one such shop. I would rather support a local bike store than a big box store like Toys R Us, but that's me.  Good luck in your search!
