I've rented from the Enterprise on 31st.  Really nice people.  They are not open on Sunday, so that may be a deal-breaker for you.  If you do go with them, do some searching online for the best rate.  There are many different rates and discounts available.  One option is to tell them that your car is being serviced at one of the local shops.  Have the name of a shop ready.  They have deals with some shops, but the shop I frequent is not one and they gave me the rate anyway without any problem.  It's a significant price difference.
