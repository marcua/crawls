It seems odd that in broad daylight people would be taking photos of Con Ed.  My thought is that if they are planning something, they would be very covert about taking photos; perhaps doing it at dawn or from their roof.  Anyway, if you see something suspicious, say something.  I would like to share a story -

Right after 9/11, I noticed a lady in Astoria Park hiding behind trees.  She looked very suspicious and was dressed in long garments and a head scarf.  I noticed her hiding behind a tree when I left home to run an errand.  When I returned half an hour later, she was still hiding.  I decided to call the police.  I did not think that she was dangerous, but if you recall, the Triborough Bridge was considered a target, so I became worried.  However, my gut told me that she was hiding from something and that she was in danger.  I was right.  She had been followed by some kids who were throwing stones at her because she looked like a Muslim.  She was frightened and began to cry when the Police came.  I came out to speak to her and the Police drove her home.

It is better to be on the safe side, so instead of wondering why these folks are taking photos, call that special number that the city advertises for this purpose.
