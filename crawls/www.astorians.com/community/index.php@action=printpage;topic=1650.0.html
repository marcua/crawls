If you can't find someone to do your taxes you should consider using TurboTax. It is something like $40 (and you can only use it for the current year), but it makes it A LOT easier - especially if you have different things to add in like 1099s, W2s, school loans, investments, boats, etc., etc. There are also free versions, but I can't remember the names... you'd probably have to research on the Internet for "free tax software."

We've used TurboTax for the last few years and are pretty satisfied considering how we're totally ripped off by local, regional, and national governments.
