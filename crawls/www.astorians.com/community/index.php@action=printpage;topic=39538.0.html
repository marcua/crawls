Title: Goodbye Western Beef Steinway Street
Post by: Cannibal on September 29, 2016, 01:53:13 PM
Story in Western Queens Gazette today says it plain as day. A new six-story, 272 unit Hotel with retail in the bottom going up on that block. Gone will be the Halal chicken joint and taxi/car repair places on the block. And just like a silent conspiracy, that store is empty today - signs up, shelves gone! Western Beef was good for certain sales ($4.99 roasted chicken All Summer, but supermarket neglected to use food service ties to keep the legs together, instead using Staples rubber bands). So this will drive more customers to Food Bazaar or Organic Met Foods. While the world still continues to pray for a Whole Foods in the new building replacing the strip joint on 35th and Steinway, I will mourn the loss of that giant cold room at Western Beef. A room so big and so cold you can store meat in it, and they did!
