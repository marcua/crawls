Baja fish tacos are by definition fried (specifically, battered and fried).  I've never seen another type of fish taco in Baja (or SoCal, although I've had many more fish tacos in Mexico).

And the fish isn't flavourless -- it's delicious, whatever is in season.  Yellowtail, cabrilla (I think it's some type of sea bass), dorado, to name a few.  If you use a lean white fish they can be flavourless, but the fish tacos I had the other night at Pachanga were pretty good.  Not as good as Mexico, but in that direction.
