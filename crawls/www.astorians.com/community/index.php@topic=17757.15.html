We've used Steinway Court Vet in the past even though I have found them to be the most expensive of local vet offices.  The last time was to have my cat's teeth cleaned about 9 years ago.  Her breath was bad and I thought maybe she had an abscess.  Because she was indoors only, her routine shots had lapsed so she needed those.  And because she was 6 (old in cat years I guess), I was told she needed full labs, EKG and a chest x-ray beforehand.  She had her teeth cleaned and 7 teeth removed.  Total cost was $1200.     
BTW, if your cat needs routine shots updated, do it a few weeks prior to taking them in for day surgery or overnight.  Vaccines don't take effect immediately so your pet can still be exposed to those diseases in the vet's office from sick animals.

I rescued a kitten from the streets and she developed a respiratory issue.

First I took her to Astoria Veterinary Group (by the Grand Central). I saw a doctor who examined her and listened to her breathing. He told me that there was nothing I can do and told me to give her treatment called Lysine. It turns out Lysine is just a nutritional supplement. I did not like the veterinarian nor did I think they cared enough. It was her first veterinary visit and it only took 5 mins. Horrible experience.

The next vet I went to is Broadway Animal Clinic on broadway and 37th Street. They also have a second location in Ditmars called 21st Animal Clinic. The owner is Dr. Bekhet. I know the doctor personally because I have worked for him at his clinics. He is very genuine with his patients and never turns down an animal in need of medical care. People have brought over animals that they couldn't keep and Dr. Bekhet always takes them as long as he has room. Dr Bekhet also cured my Dumpster's upper respiratory issues which turned out to be an infection.
Dr. Bekhet is a good veterinarian but his pricing can be high sometimes.

My favorite veterinarian of all.....is All Pets Veterinarians on Ditmars Blvd. I love love love them so much. My Dumpster is an aggressive cat when it comes to doctor visit. they were so professional and very thorough with the physical exams. And they are very affordable compared to all the other veterinarians that I have seen. I am very very happy with them.
