Title: Take Root Dance Series
Post by: Dance Entropy on September 14, 2010, 03:28:28 PM
Take Root starts up again this month!
September 25th at 8:30 pm
This performance will feature:

DeXdance

"Contemporary dance food for thought."
Diane Hubbard Burns, Orlando Sentinel

"Contemporary, to be sure, and never without enthusiasm."
-Andrew Alexander, Ottawa International Fringe Festival

DeX explores current concerns of environmentalism through movement, visual art, Romantic poetry, and original music in her newest piece, “Underbrush.”  This theme expands with the use of multi-media, spoken language, and humor, bridging together past repertory to create the hour-long performance titled Art DeXo. Ralph Gould, Noel Walsh, Lew Rosengrant, and the bizarre band Recreation Arts and Pets all contributed original musical compositions to the performance, while visual artist Molly Johnson both contributed to the concept as well as crafted the props used in “Underbrush.”


Hope you can make it!
