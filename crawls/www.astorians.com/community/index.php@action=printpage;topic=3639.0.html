Title: Re: Time-Warner Cable drop-off center
Post by: eblack on October 10, 2006, 12:19:49 AM
Don't go to Manhattan!  The boxes for Manhattan and Queens are different.  When I moved from Manhattan to Astoria, I went to the Manhattan office and they gave me a box, but it wasn't a "Queens box".  They told me to just take that box to the Queens office and exchange it for an appropriate one.  It was fine, but I had to make two trips to get the box.

Later, my DVR fritzed and I called T-W and they said it was ok to go to Manhattan. At that office, they told me I had to go to the Queens one (might have been because they didn't have the Queens box on hand), so two trips again.  It's all fine now, but unless you're just dropping off, don't go to Manhattan.
