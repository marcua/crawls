Maple Court Two Bedroom
This wonderfully appointed and extremely welcoming home evidences meticulous attention to detail. Located in the heart of The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights, this is truly unique in both design and architecture, which includes 9 foot plus ceilings and original crown moldings. The high end renovation can only really be appreciated in person, with every attention to details. This sun filled apartment with western, eastern and southern exposures has a beautiful foyer entrance that leads into an open living room/dining room. The high end eat-in-kitchen is fully equipped, with custom cabinetry, Viking stove, granite counters and a Sub-zero 48 inch refrigerator. Two bedrooms are winged in one area for privacy, with many custom walk-in closets throughout. The exquisite marbled detailed bathroom has a rain showerhead for relaxation. The building is not pet friendly, but subletting is allowed. Steps away are both new restaurants and shops coupled with the classic restaurants and bars that have been a mainstay of the neighborhood. The shopping, weekend farmers market, parks, transportation and only 20 minutes away from mid-town Manhattan makes this a well situated place to call HOME.
ASKING: $329K, MAINTENANCE: $988
