7/10 - Last Day at School Yard Flea Market
« on: June 29, 2010, 10:09:52 AM »
Saturday July 10th, 2010 will be the last Outdoor Market at PS 69Q 
Join us to closeout a great market at the school yard as construction is set to begin in July 2010.  As a vendor or fleaster, your feedback is greatly appreciated for future flea markets.  We are searching for a new location in Sept & Oct and welcome your suggestions. 

Some vendor spaces are open.  View the pics taken at the 6/26 School Yard Flea Market.  Use queensflea@gmail.com for more info.

PS 69Q Jackson Heights School Yard
77-02 37th Avenue.
between 78th Street and Roosevelt Avenue
Times 10 am - 4 pm

On the menu:  Brazilian pies, cakes and sandwiches

RAIN/SHINE - WE ARE OPEN...See you at the School Yard
PS 69Q - 77-02 37 Avenue with entrance on 78 Street

Come early for the bargains and beat the rain   :mrgreen:

 - Vintage designer brands
 - New exclusive brands (Claudia is back from the 88 Street/Queen Ann flea)
 - Sterling Silver by Rosanna Jewels
 - New kids and toddles clothing at incredible prices
 - Toys, jewelry, frames, lamps, DVDs, books and more
 - household and kitchen stuff

On the menu:  Brazilian empanadas, pot pies and sandwiches
