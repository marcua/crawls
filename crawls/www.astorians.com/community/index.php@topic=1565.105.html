The straight chocolate was a disappointment to this chocolate fiend.  You've tasted my chocolate ice cream, Jonathan, so you know I like serious chocolate.  E&M's "chocolate moose" had a sweet, cocoa powder flavour that was mildly chocolatey.  It's not a particularly rich ice cream, and so has a slightly harsh mouth feel.  Meg tasted it and suggested a chocolate syrup note as well, and I agree.

Their website describes "chocolate moose" as Chocolate Liqueur, Cocoa and imported chocolate blended to make a distinct, rich chocolate ice cream.  I have to say I didn't detect much "imported chocolate".

It may be that their specialty lies in mixes of flavours and add-ins, but I would prefer a stronger base.

Of course, the fun company made up for any of that.  Oh, and the fact that it's the first time I've ever eaten ice cream outside with earmuffs on.   :-D
