Title: Its My Park Day
Post by: marija_jane on May 13, 2009, 11:54:35 PM
Its My Park Day is a city-wide event this Saturday May 16, and here in Astoria there will be activities at three parks (that I know of), Socrates, Two Coves Community Garden, and Astoria Park.  There are many activities planned throughout the day with a theme of shoreline cleanup.  This is sponsored by the Parks Department, Greenshores, and the Astoria Park Alliance, and many groups and individuals are participating with additional events.

The newly formed Holistic Moms Network in Queens will be hosting several family events at Astoria Park for those with toddlers and young children too young to go down to the water. 

10 – Clean up the great lawn

11 – planting/filling the hole by memorial statue

12 –  family yoga

1 – clean up the playground

2 – sing along and story time

Sign in near the memorial.  Specific activity locations TBA
