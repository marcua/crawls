Title: Free Music Trial Class in Astoria this Saturday!
Post by: AllisonTartalia on March 30, 2012, 09:13:24 AM
Dear Parents,

Please join us this Saturday, March 31, for a free Music for Aardvarks trial class at 9:30am at the Steinway Reformed Church, 21-65 41st Street (corner of 41st Street and Ditmars Blvd - enter through the big wooden door on the corner).

For those of you not yet familiar with Music for Aardvarks,Music for Aardvarks and Other Mammals is a class geared toward children aged 6 months through 5 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, and instrumental jam sessions. The original material used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.  Provided with a stimulating and fertile musical environment, positive role models, gentle guidance and encouragement, young children will have enriching and vital musical experiences that help them gain a sense of themselves and a connection to the world they live in.

If you'd like to reserve a spot in the trial class or have any questions, please feel free to email me at info@musicforqueens.com.

Thanks so much,
Allison Tartaglia
