Supposedly, the TT space is bigger than it appears. In a previous thread on Astorians.com (http://www.astorians.com/community/index.php?topic=15864.0) discussing Top Tomato closing down, a real estate broker said the store is 3,000 square feet if you include the refrigerators in the back and get this, the asking price was 25K per month (or $100 per square foot per year)! Top Tomato was said to have been paying 19K per month. This was back in 2009.

According to this listing, it appears to be true, plus the space could be divided. Create wouldn't necessarily be taking all of it:

http://www.cityfeet.com/cont/forlease/ln18276308/33-15-ditmars-blvd-astoria-ny-11105


In a more recent article (from 2013) average rents on Ditmars were quoted to be in the mid-$50s to mid-$60s a square foot (per year)

http://www.crainsnewyork.com/data-lists/real-estate-deal-watch/details/12/2906079
