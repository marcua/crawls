Can you explain about the iced tea?  I ate there yesterday and ordered the iced tea after reading these comments here.  To me, it was a very good, freshly brewed glass of iced tea.  Nothing spectacular or special in any way.  I even told the waiter that I read some comments raving about the iced tea and he was surprised and laughed - basically thinking it was nothing special.  Besides that, I enjoyed the meal there very much.  The space is very nicely done.  Relaxing and very nice atmosphere.  The food is very good.  The Cream of Asparagus soup was phenomenal.  The Steak Tacos were delicious.  The cabbage slaw was very crisp and fresh.  There were hints of citrus and a very hot and spicy kick.  And a generous portion.  I the grilled prawns for the entree.  Very good but the entree was definitely not the star of the whole meal.  But the roasted vegetables were outstanding (beets, leeks, and asparagus).  I think next time I'd be inclined to skip the entree part of the menu and order more tacos and sandwiches.  Even if for dinner.  The place was packed.  It's so nice to see some serious food happening on Ditmars.  From the look of things, 2013 is going to be the year for a real dining scene to finally hit Ditmars.  It's about time!
