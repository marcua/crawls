"The Cup Building" - new resident
« on: July 19, 2006, 04:18:17 PM »
I noticed a few weeks ago that the permits for the space next to Cup claimed that it was a new restaurant.  I decided to be bold and look it up online, but didn't come up with any information other than "new restaurant".

Walking by today, I decided to be bold yet again and ask the men outside what it was going to be.  At first they said "new restaurant" (I kid you not), but when I pressed on, one of them who may have been an owner said that it was going to be Italian.

I asked if it was a chain or an independent (since we have a mix of both in that area) and he didn't really clarify but specified that "Everybody will be able to afford it". 

So I guess the news is that we're getting a new Italian restaurant next to Cup!  Looks like they still have quite a bit of work to do on it, though.
