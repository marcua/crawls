Please help! Lost bracelet near Kaufman Astoria Studios (June 22)
« on: June 24, 2013, 05:13:43 PM »
Please help! While escorting my young nephew among the kid-friendly activities at the Queens Council on the Arts (QAX) block party on 37th Street/35th Avenue in Astoria on Saturday afternoon, I lost a Chinese-style green stone (jade) and silver bracelet.

The design is of carved flat oval stone pieces mounted in silver filigree with enamel flowers. It is an old family piece and has high sentimental value; I am hoping to get the word out to anyone who may have picked it up. Cash reward -- and my undying gratitude -- for its return.

I scoured the street after the party to no avail, and it was not turned in to the Museum or to the event organizer (whom I've already contacted.) We've posted signs in the area as well, but I don't want to give up on finding it.

If you or someone you know who picked it up, please email me.  Many thanks, kind Astorians!

Found!
« Reply #1 on: June 25, 2013, 04:41:29 PM »
A happy ending!  The kind folks at Materials for the Arts -- one of the QAX Block Party participants -- found my bracelet and called me.  It must have slipped off my wrist while I was building "trophies" out of recyclable materials with my young nephew.
 
 I picked it up from their offices in LIC this afternoon.  A heartfelt thanks to John and Luisa at MFTA, and to everyone who assisted me in this search.
