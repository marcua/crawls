I can speak only about Les Enfants.  My son was there for wto years and I thougth about leaving the entire time.  The administrator is very difficult to dela with.  The classes are too big (especially for the younger years) and the administrator really fights any parent involvement.  The oyl reason we stayed the 2 years is becasue my son had a wonderful teacher, Khensee Wangmo, who now has her own daycare.  when she left, I knew it was time to enroll in PreK! 

The cost of daycare, no matter where you go, usually depends in part on the age of the child.  An infant will cost mroe than a toilet trained 4 year old.    any wehre from 900-1200 I would say.

Mrs. Wangmo has her own daycare -- it share space with Blossoms.  Blossoms is for infants, MRs. Wangmo's Montessori-based daycare starts at age 3.  Love her.

We also did very well with PReK-- full day PreK with afterschool onsite at Immaculate Cnception school worked out great for us. (and affordable) My son is still there for K now.
