Title: RMS Titanic - 100 Years Later
Post by: greentiger on March 27, 2012, 11:55:54 AM
 RMS Titanic: The Exhibit
Monday, April 2, 2012 at 7:00 pm
 
Greater Astoria Historical Society
35-20 Broadway, 4th Floor
Long Island City, NY  11106
718-278-0700 www.astorialic.orgLecture by Gary Vollo, member of the Titanic Historical Society.  Lecture includes original film of the Titanic leaving port and a piece of coal brought up from the sea floor.  Special guest speaker, “Titanic Joe” Colletti will talk about the Titanic House, long an attraction in Long Island City.  Joe will also share his personal interviews with a number of Titanic survivors whose riveting firsthand accounts promise to make this an unforgettable evening.
 
Astoria author Lindsay Gibbs, who wrote the “Titanic: The Tennis Story,” a historical novel will also participate. The book is based on a true story of two Hall of Fame Tennis players who survived the Titanic … and went on to play each other in the quarterfinals of the U.S. Open just two years later!
 
Exhibit will include original material from the Titanic House.
$5 (Free to GAHS members)
