Hi,

I'm also a Rock member and they are raising their fees by a few dollars per month.
I have this from reliable sources there - they aren't saying anything about it until the
spring. I'm also told that you can negotiate lower rates by paying cash, but I've never done that.

For this gym to really compete w/the Rock they need to be open 24 hours during weekdays.
I switched from Synergy on Broadway to the Rock in the Fall of '05 because I want the early
morning availability that NYSC doesn't have.

Plus with all that they're promising, I think that to keep the place up to standard, they'll have to
charge close to what the Rock does, otherwise they'll go out of business.

And I really wonder about the early spring opening on the website. I looked in the window last week and
nothing's been touched, at least on the ground floor.

I remember when Synergy opened back in '99, their opening was delayed several times.

But it's good that more gyms are opening in this area - keeps the existing ones on their toes since they're not
the only game in town anymore. Synergy got that way - when I complained about the mice and roaches there
they told me I was 'imagining it' and said they didn't think I'd leave over 'little stuff' like that.

Well........little stuff like that sent me right over to the Rock, which is further away for me and more $$$, but
a much better club.

My mom goes over to Broadway a lot for errands and she's promised to keep an eye out for any signs of life,
so I'll post if/when we see something.
