Title: Re: Community Association Meeting at AMMI
Post by: mcdirk on January 19, 2008, 02:37:47 AM
They do the forums pretty regularly, once or twice a year I think.  Rosemary Povoromo, who is the head of the United Community Civic Association, which co-convenes the forums with Assemblymember Mike Gianaris, gets all the agencies there - I mean, I'm incredibly impressed.  I worked in city government for seven years in Manhattan and never saw anything like it - we never got so many agencies to send a representative for three solid hours, and she gets solid responses and has them give out their cards.  She runs a tight meeting, though a few people asking questions went off on bizarre tangents.  I'm sure the next forum will get posted and we'll try to bump it up a bit the day before so more folks come out.
