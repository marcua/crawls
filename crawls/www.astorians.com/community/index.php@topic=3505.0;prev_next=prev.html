Nosebleeds - not nosebleed seats
« on: September 18, 2006, 11:48:03 PM »
I've been getting a lot of nosebleeds lately - four in the past two weeks - and none of my internet research so far can explain why they've been so frequent.  Normally, I only get one-two nosebleeds a year.  The ones I've been getting stop after a few minutes, so they're not the really serious kind.  And there haven't been any changes in my health lately that could explain this - my blood pressure is normal, no nasal injuries, seasonal allergies no worse than they usually are, I haven't been picking my nose (at least not on the subway  :oops: ).

Should I see a doctor?  Does anyone else out there suffer from nosebleeds?  Any advice is greatly appreciated!
