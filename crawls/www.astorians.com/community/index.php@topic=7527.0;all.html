I Caught Kids Spraying Graffiti Last Night
« on: August 15, 2007, 11:00:39 AM »
So last night around 8pm I heard people walking on my roof.  I live on the 3rd floor of a 3 floor building on 33st b/w 30th ave and 31st ave.  There has been a lot of graffiti going on lately and it bothers me.  Someone even sprayed F&*^ Y#@ on the outside of the roof door of my building.  It has been a growing problem. 

Well last night I did something that I now regret.  I confronted these trespassing criminals.  I listened through the door for a moment and heard them talking.  Then I heard that sound of someone shaking a can of spray paint.  This set me off fuming and I barged out there and found these kids (I would guess 18-22) sitting on my roof smoking pot with the spray can sitting right there.  I asked them what they were doing there and if that was a can of spray paint.  They said yes and I inquired if they were the punks that have been "tagging" my building and the whole block.  They did not deny anything.  I told them to leave immediately and that I was calling the cops.  I told them that I know what they look like, gave them a slow once over and proceeded to call the police. 

I have to commend the police both on the phone and in person.  They tooked a detailed account and were extremely curtious.  About 10 cops showed up to my place  :-o :-o, including 3 plain-clothed officers.  I provided very detailed descriptions of these criminals and the police requested that I take pictures of the area that was defaced during daylight hours.  I plan on doing that today and emailing them to the officer with whom I filed the report. 

My only regret is that I should not have confronted these kids.  Who knows what weapons they might have had.  I also wish that I just called the cops immediately.  There would have been a better chance of catching these kids if I did that.  Based on my description, one of the perps fit a similar description to someone who was arrested recently.
