I got a couch from Big Apple Futon and had no problems with them.  I did my shopping online, and then went to the physical store to make sure I liked the couch as much in person, and to buy it.  It's on 23rd between 2nd and 3rd, I think.  They do offer free delivery within the five boroughs, but be aware that that is curbside delivery, no setup, no taking away anything you had before.  That said, once they got it off the truck they looked at me and looked at the stairs and said, you want us to bring it up?  They did without an additional charge, so I gave them a nice tip.  IIRC, full service delivery is around $50.

There are also a bunch of furniture stores on Steinway street that probably sell cheap futons. We bought our futons (no longer have them) from J&A Furniture on Ditmars, which I think is no more, and I don't think that they were really that expensive at all. I think it gets more expensive if you get nicer ones (i.e. wood futons, etc.).

IKEA might be a good idea, but I imagine the shipping (if you do shipping) is astronomical, and more than a hassle if you have to schlep it yourself unless you have a handy dandy car or truck.

There's always craigslist, too. There are people looking to upgrade or get rid of their furniture all the time, so I am sure you could find quite a few futons listed on there.
