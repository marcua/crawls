I am disgusted at the conditions these puppies are kept in. Without detracting from the plight of pet store puppies and puppy mill puppies, I am very upset at the conditions of the parrots in these stores. Parrots are wild animals, they are not domesticated like dogs have been for centuries. I bet these jerks don't even know what an avian vet is, and their birds are probably illegal.

A few years ago the national chain Petsmart stopped carrying large parrots such as cockatoos and macaws in their stores; they have parakeets and cockatiels still. They did so because of pressure and petitions from parrot rescue groups and parrot owners who saw the deplorable conditions in pet stores, and the fact that store owners and employees are clueless about how to properly care for a huge macaw (that can live 70+ years!) and selling them to idiots who end up giving the bird away because "it bites, screams, and doesn't talk" (way to go, responsible bird owner!). This is huge for the pet industry; (of course it probably boils down to that it wasn't profitable for Petsmart to do so but still this was good news.)

All I'm saying is that people have the power to change the way pets are sold and treated in this country; the swift enforcement of legislation would certainly help. But people need to speak up.
