Kosher Potluck Dinner and Chavurah Service: Fri, December 8th, 6:30pm
Join us for a kosher potluck dinner followed by a Chavurah service in our social hall. All are welcome.

Dinner is at 6:30pm, services begin at 8pm. If possible, please bring a kosher dairy or pareve dish or dessert. If you don't have a kosher kitchen, you can bring a packaged kosher item (cupcakes, hummus, lox, etc.). Main dishes are especially appreciated, if you can, but anything you can contribute is wonderful.

Astoria Center of Israel Synagogue
27-35 Crescent Street, Astoria 718-278-2680
http://www.astoriacenter.org (http://www.astoriacenter.org)
Take the N or W subway towards Queens to the 30th Avenue stop. Walk west on 30th Avenue (towards Athens Square Park) for about 4 short blocks; turn right on Crescent Street and walk a half block down on the right.
