tried luna de juarez last night on steinway and 28th ave and liked it very much. for appetizer we had the fried avocado stuffed with shrimp, cheese, and corn. for our entrees we had the steak fajitas and veggie fajitas. i thought the steak was much better than the veggie b/c the veggie had carrots and broccoli in it (not what i think of when i order fajitas). i think the best part was the $24 pitcher of house margaritas. very tasty  and the pitcher was large enough for at least 7 drinks. not the best mexican i've ever had, but definitely a nice addition to the neighborhood!
