We have 3 more performances of the Exonerated at The Secret Theatre. Tonight and tomorrow night (FR & SAT) are close to sold out, but I do have one final block of 10 comps that I can put out for Astorians...just to get you to know our group and our theatre. These comps are for the Saturday (10/11) matinee at 4pm.

The production is by The Rep@NYU, the new NYU graduate and alum repertory company. This is the first show of the first full season and this will be one of our final mainstage productions in the neighborhood, although we plan of presenting special pieces here from time to time.

The Secret Theatre is the home of the Queens Players at the Long Island Citiy Arts Center, a funky and fun theatre at 44-02 23rd Street.

For the comp list, please email kenhailey@nyu.edu.

Tickets will be confirmed by e-mail.
