Hey there, welcome to Astoria in advance! $700 should be a good amount for a roommate situation in Astoria.  You might want to look around Craigslist in their roommate section or on this board.  I can't help you in the apartment situation but I can answer your questions about trains.  Trains in all of NYC - meaning the subway - run 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.  Being in Astoria, you would most likely live near the N/W line or the V/R line.  And at night, the trains run a little less but more like every 10-20ish minutes, so it's not so bad to get to Manhattan at night. 

And I know the perfect salsa class - but it's in Manhattan.  It does have a lot of international students in it, and I currently take classes there- have for about a year and a half.  It's with Eddie and Maria Torres - legendary dancers for Tito Puente.  They are located at 57th St, only about 20 minutes from Astoria, so it's very convenient.  They are awesome teachers!!!  I highly recommend them.
