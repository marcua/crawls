Title: Re: Broadway Station renovation progress?
Post by: sjfee on April 02, 2008, 10:06:13 AM
I collected some more intel last night (1 April) on the state of the Broadway Station bar.  The entire place was definitely gutted, but overall construction is in the rebuild stage- all the materials laying around looked brand new. Some Sheetrock has been hung on the walls- probably about 1/4 to 1/3 total coverage.  The rear wall appears to be some sort of a floor to ceiling slate stone wall- and from 40-50 feet away it looked to be pretty well done. There's no roof or floor yet, but the bar is being framed. It looks like it'll be the same depth and at least the same length as the old bar.

I'm getting the impression that the final product will be a little more modern/trendy than the old place, but ought to maintain the sports bar feel. The manager at Indigo told me (about a month ago) that they're going to put up 20-something flatscreen TVs in the new place and table-top wireless speakers that can be tuned to whatever channel you're watching will be available.  Additionally, they'll be subscribing to that trivia service that's always seen in TGI Friday's and Buffalo Wild Wings- the one where you can play all types of trivia, poker, etc. thru those blue wireless "computers".

Hope it opens sooner than later... I'm in withdrawal from not having a beer/food/sports/trivia joint a block away from me...
