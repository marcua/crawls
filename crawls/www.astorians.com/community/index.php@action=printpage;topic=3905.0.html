Title: Re: Does anyone scrapbook? If so where to shop?
Post by: Alison on December 12, 2006, 02:30:56 AM
If you're willing to venture into Manhattan, I know a couple paper places you might want to check out...

I took a bookmaking class at school last semester, and while its not a scrapbooking store, I shopped for a lot of my paper at New York Central Art Supply (3rd Ave at 11th Street). There are also a few of places on 18th Street between 5th & 6th Aves - there are a couple big ones on the north side of the street, but I forget their names. Actually, the bigger of the two (the one with a second level in the back) had a pretty big scrapbooking section from what I remember, and lots of cool paper upstairs. Since I don't shop for that stuff often, I can't really comment on the prices, but the bigger one on 18th Street did have a lot of funky papers on sale when I wandered in, and the prices seemed reasonable for the size of the sheets.

My advice would be to do your shopping at art supply stores (NY Central, Lee, Pearl, etc), rather than "scrapbooking" stores - it seems like specialty stores would charge more.
