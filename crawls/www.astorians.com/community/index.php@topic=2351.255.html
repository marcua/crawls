Brief notes from precinct meeting last night:

Police created a $2,000 reward for info leading to the two bias attacks of June 10 & 12.  Lots of political and police support behind it and openly talked about at meeeting.

Dirk gets lots of credit for making this happen, it was his connections that made it possible.

Safe haven program was talked about and people should call the 114 community liasion person to voice support for it.

We met all the community leaders, Top police from the 114, Politicians and more.  There is a lot going on in our community and they welcome our support, comments and concerns.

Last nights meeting was a fun and interesting experience. Good to see how the dynamics work and how deeply rooted the community is involved.

Next precinct meeting is in September.
