I don't know if this is a different kind of salad, but the salad that made me fall in love with Greek salads was at Agnanti. It doesn't have capers in it. What they put in it are tomatoes, cucumbers, feta, onions, olive oil (maybe vinegar too? not sure and it's been a while since I've made it over there), kalamata olives, oregano. I never knew feta cheese could taste the way it tastes in that salad.
