Without hijacking the entire thread, if you are in automobile accident, stabbed or shot, Elmhurst is the perfect place to go. They have an awesome trauma team. If you have abdominal or head pain, head to Lenox Hill. Chest pain, New York Hospital. If you suspect a broken bone, Columbia Presbyterian. Eye or hearing problems, NY Eye and Ear Infirmary. If you are having the symptoms of a stroke, don't wait for an ambulance, grab a cab and head to Mt. Sinai on Fifth Avenue.

If, however, you scrape your knee, or have a nasty hangnail, go straight to Mt. Sinai of Queens. They are great with Band Aids!
