Title: Re: Bakery at Steinway and 28th Avenue
Post by: bstewart on February 21, 2017, 02:28:28 PM
There were two bakeries on the west side of Steinway. When I moved here, Benkert’s was close to the theater (now Duane Reade,) in or near what is now RadioShack. Benkert’s and the German deli next door were my favorites for food shopping.

There was another German bakery on the same side of Steinway, closer to 28th St. My guess is that it was in a former location of Benkerts at 28-30. I don’t recall the name, but the awning said “Where Butter Makes The Difference.” I remember it was used as a location for a skit on the Conan O'Brien show on CBS.

Across the street on the east side of Steinway was the Joseph Bake Shop. Vacarro Bakery, formerly at 42nd St. & 30th Ave. has moved to the space that was Joseph.
