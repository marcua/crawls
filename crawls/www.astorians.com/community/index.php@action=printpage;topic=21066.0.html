Title: A New Session of My Smart Hands Baby Sign Language Classes Begins June 2
Post by: My Smart Hands on May 27, 2011, 06:07:32 PM
My Smart Hands sign language classes for hearing babies and toddlers begin Thursday, June 2 @ 11:00 a.m. at Seaburn Bookstore.

In this 8-week course:
  • Learn how to communicate with your child before he/she can talk!
  • Accelerate your child's language development and increase his/her vocabulary!
  • Reduce frustration and tantrums!
  • Meet other parents in your community in a fun, relaxed atmosphere with a qualified instructor!
  • Learn tricks to help your baby sign back sooner!
  • Learn songs and stories to sign along to while teaching your child sign language!
Visit www.MySmartHandsNYC.com for more information.
