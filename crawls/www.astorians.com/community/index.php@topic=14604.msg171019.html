You know who you are! This is just to let people know that Jon Genoli, founder of the seminal gay R&R band Pansy Division will be in NYC this week reading from his new autobiography, "deflowered, my life in Pansy Division".  :-D He will also do some acoustic songs at the readings. He is also doing a full set on Saturday night and will be at a showing of the new Pansy Division video that was just released on DVD(see the links below). They have a new album out as well, their 9th called "That's So Gay". Here is a rare opportunity for some good gay entertainment;I really can't believe that I am the only fan in all of Astoria.

THURS. 04/16/2009 - Barnes & Noble, Greenwich Village, NYC 396 Avenue of the Americas 7:00pm

FRI. 04/17/2009 - Bluestocking Books, NYC 172 Allen Street, between Stanton & Rivington (1 block south of Houston and 1st Ave.) 7:00pm

SAT. 04/18/2009 - The Cake Shop, NYC 152 Ludlow St, btwn. Stanton & Rivington -a full acoustic set, 10 PM (no reading)

SUN. 04/19/2009 - Monkey Town, Brooklyn, NYC 58 N. 3rd St., in Williamsburg (film screening + acoustic set: 2 shows, 7 & 9:30)
