Title: Re: Bagel Recommendations
Post by: TJT on March 29, 2016, 08:13:23 AM
It sounds like the bagels may mean something in this situation - but if not, consider getting something special and unique to Astoria.  Yaya's Bakery is between 30th and Newtown.  They have delicious items you can't find anywhere else.  My wife often brings dozens of their amazing, buttery breadsticks to her office and her colleagues go crazy for them.  They come in all sorts of flavors.  More like a cookie than a breadstick and great for a breakfast treat.  Just a thought.  I always look for an opportunity to showcase what my neighborhood has that other areas do not.
