Title: "Judaism" on QPTV
Post by: bennyt on October 05, 2006, 12:45:10 PM
When flipping the channels last night, I came across a very disturbing show (yes, more disturbing than seeing the acne-faced bloated Eric Cartman pooping into a plastic dish while playing World of Warcraft).  This show aired on QPTV (channel 56) from 9:30 to 10:00 pm last night.  It is called "Judaism."  I think I have seen bits of this before, with different hosts.  This one was hosted by a person called "Rabbi M. Friedman."

If you didn't catch this particular show, you're a better person for it.  But I did, and I became really incensed and embarrassed.  The views expressed in the show were racist and hateful.  Obviously, as somebody who pays attention to things Jewish, I know that out there on the fringe there are some extreme and hostile people (as there are with all religious and ethnic groups).  But in the case of this show, the hateful views were masquerading simply as "Judaism."  I find that totally obscene.  To think that some unsuspecting person out there might see that show, and then perhaps mistake the racism and hate for mainstream Jewish values- that scares me tremendously.  I can only hope that most people are sohpisticated enough to recognize extremism when they see it.

This isn't the place to discuss what Judaism is or is not- but I am really pissed off that a show called "Judaism" serves as a forum for hate-mongering.  This is the price of free speech, I guess.
