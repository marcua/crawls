I had to bring this Spiderman 3 premier thread back to life b/c I was there that day and wanted to see how everyone else's experience was and share mine.

It was kind of a weird day for me that day but MEMORABLE - I pretty much stumbled upon it accidentally and ended up just hanging out by the front doors with a few buddies.

Anyone remember that Seinfeld episode where George does everything opposite of his instincts b/c his instincts are always wrong? Yea...that was how I approached that day.

I'm almost forgetting the glamor of celebrities and all that, and being on TV....b/c of this ONE girl.....

Mind you, I'm not the most attractive guy in the bunch with all my friends but somehow I bumped into a beautiful young lady (smooth silky black hair, petite body, about 5'5, 17/18 yo and I was 19 at the time, Hispanic heritage...) at the end of the entrance premier, she who wore a black dress and was with her friend. We ended up chit chatting for about an hour or so but it was most unusual b/c women like that don't tend to approach me as much as I have to approach them. She took some photographs of us together hanging out. I think she was from another area in Queens though.

Anyway signals got crossed, and I lost her number :( I always held the slight hope maybe I'd run into her again....that's not happening LOL

I don't know what to call this matter, puppy love, being sprung, I guess it's not knowing what COULD have been that has me banging my head on the wall. Had I only been more responsible and held onto the contact #.
