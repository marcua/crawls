Hi elenacoln,

Thanks for setting this up for us... you beat us to it :)  We do expect there to be a good turn-out this Sunday, so I think people who are looking to split shares will have a good chance of getting paired up.  That said, it is good to have a share partner in mind before you come, if possible!

We are not officially organizing partners, but we are trying to direct people to each other when we can and will try to facilitate pairing this sunday!

Just a few clarifications (we apologize - our website is packed full w/info right now, but this information is on there):

- Our single share price is $330 (admin fee included). 
- We have a required "time donation" of one shift during the season.  The core is entirely volunteer-run and we can't function without all our members helping to contribute during distribution days.  When you sign up for Harvest Astoria, you also schedule in a time to help us out!!

Let us know if you have any more questions!

-Harvest Astoria Core
