Post by: yippee1999 on September 07, 2017, 01:11:24 PM

I think everyone in my bldg. have now come to the conclusion that all the underground street construction in the area (new plumbing pipes being installed) has worsened the waterbug issue.  In the past few days alone, I saw three waterbugs in my apt., and that’s just the bugs that I SAW (all of which I killed BTW)!

After doing considerable research, I am instituting a multi-pronged attack.  ;-)

LL is having exterminator come to do entire bldg. (esp the outside perimeter of bldg.)

I have bought Combat Roach traps (for large roaches i.e., waterbugs) and will put them throughout my apt and in the hallways and stairwell as well (since that is where I see the waterbugs most often…in the hallways).  In another apartment where I lived, I had an issue with small ‘kitchen’ roaches.  The Combat Roach traps (for small roaches) absolutely worked.  Within two weeks of my putting out the Combat traps, no more roaches, for months!  Then the roaches would start to reappear, I’d get new Combat traps, and again, within two weeks, no more roaches.  So the trick is to be sure that you indeed replace your Combat traps on a regular schedule.

I will have a handyman come by and seal up every little crack in my apt.

I will periodically spray a water solution that is strongly scented with clove or peppermint oil (both are strong scents that bugs do not like) around baseboards and under my sinks where the pipes meet the wall.

I will always have bug spray on-hand to kill any bugs on-the-spot, that I may see.

I am going to buy electronic bug repellants, which apparently CAN also help.   https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B073VJL6ZH/ref=ox_sc_act_title_2?smid=A3ORWJRNTZRT61&psc=1
https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B074DTQCSW/ref=ox_sc_act_title_3?smid=A35WK6PLQS6XXM&psc=1

...and I already keep my apartment impeccably clean...but still, clearly that does not keep bugs out...it only prevents MORE from finding something attractive to eat, etc.

A multi-pronged approach is the best tactic!!!  ;-)
