What does a Faraday cage do?

A Faraday cage shields electromagnetic radiation, such as radio waves, including cell phones. Basically, it's just a metal cage with bars closer together than the wavelength you want to shield (or maybe some multiple of the wavelength, I don't know). It's why you lose cell phone reception in some buildings and some elevators, due to parallel metal rods in some types of construction. If you build one on purpose, it can be very effective...
