Rizzos Ending Seamless & Grub Hub Orders
« on: October 15, 2014, 04:34:25 PM »
Ordered a pizza from Rizzo's and there was a letter attached to it that informed us that they will no longer be accepting ordered from online third parties as of January 1. It says the reason is because of the lack of personal interaction and relationships with customers that the online sales are causing.


So, they ask that you request delivery by phone. This confuses me, because I don't know too many times where you call a pizza place and get chummy with the person taking your order to the point where you really develop a "personal relationship," but alright. Just sounds odd.
