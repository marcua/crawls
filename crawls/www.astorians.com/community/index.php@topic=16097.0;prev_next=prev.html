If you're "handy" you can get a kit at Home Depot to make a custom sized screen yourself - requires some cutting of the frame and screen material, fitting the screen to the frame, and putting the spline in.  I did 2 of my apartment windows with that and it works well.  You can choose from several different kinds of screen material that way - I choose the animal-resistant kind so my cats don't scratch through it.

You might need some tools though - a screen spline tool and a hacksaw to cut the frame.  Also, dish soap really helps getting the spline into the frame.
