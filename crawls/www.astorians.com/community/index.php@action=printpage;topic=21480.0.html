Title: I'm fundraising for sea turtles!
Post by: megc on August 13, 2011, 05:07:52 PM

Hi everyone, those of you who know me probably know that I have a strong interest in sea turtles and conservation efforts to keep them from becoming extinct. I'm doing my part by running a 5K race in October in Brooklyn near the New York Aquarium, and am accepting donations as part of this event. Sea turtles, which used to live in abundance in our oceans, are seriously at risk. All seven species are either considered "Threatened" or "Endangered". They've lived for over 200 million years, too. I can't imagine a world without them.


If you would like to make a gift for this awesome and important cause, please visit my personal fundraising page (http://e.wcs.org/site/TR/Events/RunfortheWildTeamraiser?px=1929617&pg=personal&fr_id=1080). As I say in the accompanying note, even $10 will make a difference. I'd love for you to participate with me in this way!


Donations are tax deductible and will go toward the Wildlife Conservation Society's efforts in sea turtle conservation.


Many thanks!
