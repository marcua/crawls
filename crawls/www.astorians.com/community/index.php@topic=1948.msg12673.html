I live on 37th Street between 24th and 23rd Avenues and I love where I live!  It's a 5-6 minute walk to either Astoira Blvd or Ditmars subway stop.  And this location puts you between both the Greek/Ditmars nabe and the arab section of Steinway Street; I'm a 5 minute walk to Kabab Cafe, which is totally awesome.  You'll be near the Greek Monastery which has bells that I am in love with.  I wish I was closer so that I could hear them more often.  You're an easy walk to Ditmars, and a mile from Astoria Park.  There's a laundromat at 37th Street and 24th Ave, an easy walk with your laundry in a granny cart.  I know there are places nearby that you can drop off, too.  And you're a couple blocks from that Christos Taverna which has The Steaks.

All in all, a great part of Astoria to live in.

Used to live on Steinway between Ditmars and 23rd Ave. I think that 38th would have been better, though, considering that Steinway is a major street and a lot of trucks drive past. 38th is definitely more quiet.

As for laundry - I would suggest House of Suds on around 41st (?) on Ditmars. The people are nice, and the machines work well.

I also give a huge thumbs up to the veggie store that's next to Trade Fair. They have wonderful deals on vegetables - esp. in the summer. You can get awesome artichokes there for really cheap in the summer. Trade Fair is a little less desireable of a grocery store because of their size. They do have some interesting products though. I used to buy babaganoush in a can there all the time and loved it (of course, adding my own lemon, salt, and fresh garlic!).

The walk is not fantastic, especially in the winter, but it really isn't all that bad. You'll get used to it. The days that it sucks the most is when it is either really really cold, really really hot, or when you're carrying a crapload of stuff all the way from the subway. There is a bus that does run up Ditmars, though, so some days that can be helpful.

Good luck!
