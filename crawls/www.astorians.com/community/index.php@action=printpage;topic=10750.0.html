I am offering Acting and Guitars Lessons to people in this neighborhood. Once the class grows large enough, I have a beautiful theatre space in Manhattan which I will be using for the classes.

I have recently moved to Astoria(Ditmars Area) and I absolutely love it here. Astoria feels like home. I am and have been a working actor in NYC for that past three years. I have been playing music for 13 years and I have performed at all the famous music spots in NYC including: CBGB's, Bitter End, Cafe Wha, and Apollo Theater.
