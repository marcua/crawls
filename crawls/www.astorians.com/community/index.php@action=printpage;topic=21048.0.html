Post by: lanseaux on May 25, 2011, 03:01:24 PM
MagicPie, I went to the Astor Room with some friends a couple of months ago.  It was good - I would like to go back, but if anything I found the food to be a bit too restrained.  Food like steak tartar that has a very distinctive seasoning was a bit flat.  The oysters were delicious (although that certainly has less to do with preparation!).  Friends had the warm bacon salad, the Caesar salad and the salmon entree and said that everything was very good.  I tasted the salmon and it was really nicely done.  The desserts were over the top in a good way (and tasty), and the drinks were good (and the bartender mixed us drinks on the spot based on what we said we liked and didn't like in a drink). 

All in all, it was fun and I think it would make a good date night.
