Oh, Harlan, I think they would have let you in.  Jtrane came up all the way from downtown!  And goldfish boy sported a T-shirt, too.  No problem, as our host would have said!

I think I had:  a vegetable samosa, some rice, goat curry, chicken tikka masala, paneer pasanda (cubes of cheese in a tomato-based sauce), some cauliflower from the aloo gobi (potato and cauliflower mixture), and a spinach and chickpea dish.  With it I had a fabulous salty and sour pickled vegetable mixture, naan, and some raita.  Also a couple of extra chili peppers they provided on the side (I found them to be disappointingly mild, though).  Pleasant level of spice in everything - nothing on the buffet was super spicy, though, like their vindaloo.  I need to get me some more of that vindaloo.

Everything was delicious, and I think it was a very good deal for $10.95.  I was able to justify the amount of food I ate by the 12 block walk there and back.  8-)
