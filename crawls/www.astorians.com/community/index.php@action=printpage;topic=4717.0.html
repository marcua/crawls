Title: Free Music Classes for Babies & Toddlers: 1/17, 1/24 & 1/31
Post by: AllisonTartalia on January 13, 2007, 10:34:45 AM
Music for Aardvarks, the hip, rockin' music class for babies and toddlers is coming to Astoria!

Music for Aardvarks and Other Mammals is a class geared toward children aged 3 months thru 5 years that incorporates a wide variety of musical activities, including singing, dancing, musical story telling, and instrumental jam sessions. The original material used in class uniquely reflects and celebrates the lives of children growing up in an urban environment, as well as more traditional themes universal to all children.  To hear some of the songs and learn more, visit www.MusicForQueens.com!

For the next three weeks, we'll be offering FREE demo classes at:

Astoria Center of Israel
27-35 Crescent Street
(between 28th Avenue and Newton Avenue)
Astoria, NY

The demo classes will be held on Wednesdays, 1/17, 1/24 and 1/31 at 10am and 11am.  Classes are open to  babies and toddlers aged 3 months through 5 years, accompanied by a parent or adult caregiver.

Please call or email me to reserve a spot, as space is limited: 212-769-6812 (leave a message with your phone number) or info@MusicForQueens.com.

Thanks so much and feel free to contact me with any questions!

Regards,
Allison
