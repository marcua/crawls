Title: Re: Noisette - a new bakery on Steinway
Post by: Yaxpac on December 10, 2011, 01:27:42 PM
This place is excellent.  I am thrilled to have another top notch French bakery in Astoria.  I missed that place Le Petit Prince on Broadway and I think that this place is actually better.  They have fantastic croissant, brioche, macarons, tarts, etc.  So far, everything that i have had here has been very very good.  You can tell that they use top notch ingredients and good french butter. 

I hope that this place succeeds, it is a nice addition to the area.
