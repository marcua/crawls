I usually take the bus from Port Authority - http://www.coachusa.com/olympia/
It's fast 20 mins each way and $25 roundtrip.  The buses are real nice and comfy.  I absolutely avoid JFK as an option just because getting to NJ is much easier!!  You can buy tics online or:

Port Authority Bus Terminal (PABT)
Buses depart from the Port Authority side of 41st Street between 8th and 9th Avenue and drop off on 42nd Street between 8th and 9th Avenue. Tickets can be purchased from the Gray Line New York Ticket office located inside the 42nd street entrance of the Port Authority between 9:00 am and 9:00 pm. Other times pay on bus, cash or credit card accepted. (drivers are not permitted to sell tickets)
