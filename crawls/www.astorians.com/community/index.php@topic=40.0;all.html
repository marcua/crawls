My girlfriend and I are vegetarians. We've found the following:

- Broadway Natural: (Broadway subway stop, walk 1/2 block West along Broadway) It's pretty good for a lot of natural and organic foods, but is kinda expensive.

- Another place at Broadway and Steinway: (Harder to get to, but just to the East of the intersection on Broadway) This place is cheaper, but harder to get to. It's also a natural food store.

- Fresh Direct: (online only www.freshdirect.com ) They deliver to Astoria and cary a decent variety of organic and natural foods. They are worth a shot.

Please let us know if you find anything else.

We need a good Vegetarian/Vegan place here on Ditmars!

Some more veggie suggestions
« Reply #10 on: October 30, 2004, 05:06:39 PM »
Hi all,

Having spent a good part of my life in Astoria and some part of that being vegan, here are some more options for the herbivores among us:

Many of the middle eastern cafes along upper Steinway (bet Hoyt and 28th aves) are very veg-friendly.  Kebab Cafe rocks the hardest out of all of them.  Give my best to Ali if you go there.  

Grand Tofu, which is now JJ's on 31st ave and 35th street has a newly-printed vegetarian menu.  They're really good if you like Asian-Fusion.  

As for health food stores, earlier posts have the run down.  But Queens has a community supported agriculture coop (http://www.queenscsa.org) that distributes in LIC.  

Bon appetit.

-PB
