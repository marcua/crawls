Last night, my wife and I went to Yaar, and we were thrilled.  The inside has several beautiful mosaic hanging ceiling lamps.  The staff is super friendly and attentive.  And the food was really good.  No beer nor wine yet, so BYOB if you wish to drink.  And yes, when they hear an N or Q train pull into the station, the rush out with a tray of samples.  Smart stuff.   I hope that these guys do well.
