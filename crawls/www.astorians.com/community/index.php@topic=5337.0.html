Go to Astoria Park (it's huge and gorgeous, and right by the river) and have a lovely walk through it.  Then walk around Ditmars - totally different vibe from Broadway (which is sort of near where I live, also, but I'm partial to the Ditmars area).  Have lunch at McCann's Pub on Ditmars (and maybe a pint), then head toward Steinway and wander down, taking note of how the neighborhood changes ethnicities every couple blocks.  Possibly stop into a bakery or two, then head back home.  This is what I would do first if I were exploring for a few hours.   :-)

Oh yeah, and bring your camera, because Astoria is gorgeous!!!  Especially on this sunny day!
