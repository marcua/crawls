I had the desire to travel alone last year to Italy. I decided to join a tour group for singles ages 18-34 called Contiki. I highly recommend it if you want to be "alone". It's a great way to travel w/ people your age from all over the world (Contiki serves Europe, Canada, Australia, Africa, etc.) Yet, you are still somewhat alone as you won't know a soul. It forces you out of your shell (if you're in one) and forces you to make new friends, all while experiencing something new together.
