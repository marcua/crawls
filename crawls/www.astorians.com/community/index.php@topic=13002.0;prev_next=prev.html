Growing up, I didn't like mustard, so as a young adult, Honeycup was my gateway mustard; the proprietor of a little sandwich shop in Newtown, PA, insisted that I try just a little bit.

From there, because I like spicy food, I moved on to the Colman's powder and man, did that cause some nice convulsions.  Moved on to the various brands of prepared spicy-brown mustard.  I still am not fond of the vinegary yellow stuff, though I will take a little on a Chicago-style dog.
