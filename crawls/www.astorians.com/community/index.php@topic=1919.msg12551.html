Aggh - I cooked too.  A quick pasta sauce I saw on Chowhound today based on a Marcella Hazan recipe - an onion, peeled and halved, half a stick of butter, and one 14.5 oz. can of diced tomatoes, salt to taste.  Throw it all in a pan and simmer for 45 minutes to an hour.  I covered it for about 45 minutes and then simmered uncovered for about another half an hour, waiting for Jim to get home.  Remove the onion before tossing with pasta and serving (I used penne).  Right amount for two people - 8 oz. uncooked pasta.  Man, that was good.  And simple.
