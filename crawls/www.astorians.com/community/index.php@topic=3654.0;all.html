Halloween is a bit weird (in my book) around here.

30th ave gets packed with families looking for goodies, and the shop owners who I've talked to about it HATE IT!!!  Families come in from all over queens and walk into shops that they never shop in and put out their hands asking for candy.  Sometimes it's parents with no kids in tow!! Unbelievable.  Owners go through 100s of $$$ in candy within hours.  Some families have been known to get rather incorigible if turned down, or told that a shop is out of candy. 

As a huge fan of the holiday I enjoy stolling 30th watching the parade.  Last year when we realized that there was no way we were going to get rid of all the loot we'd bought in preparation (I think we got 2 groups of kids and then put out an "honor" bowl) we went to Grand Cafe and starting handing out candy to kids who came into the cafe looking for treats. 

I really had to work at giving away my candy -
