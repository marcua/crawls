Title: Anvil! hosts Hell Gate Social's Rock Band Night This Wednesday!
Post by: AlexR on March 30, 2009, 11:08:12 AM
You all know about Hell Gate Social's weekly Rock Band nights, but this week I'm teaming up with them to give away some great goodies through Chud.com. I've got 100 passes to give away for a free advance screening of Anvil! The Story of Anvil- a really incredible documentary that's hitting theaters in a couple of weeks. I should also have posters from the film and other goodies to hand out for people who come to rock.

If you've never heard of the movie, you're missing out. Basically it follows the current life of the members of the band Anvil, a metal band that's been around for around 30 years but somehow has never broken big- despite being huge influences on dozens of classic bands like Metallica, Guns n' Roses and Anthrax. The movie shows Anvil, now in their 50s, going on a European tour and trying to get signed to a label. It's entertaining, sad and uplifting all at the same time- really fantastic film. People are comparing it to a real-life Spinal Tap but it's so much better than that.

So come get a free ticket for it at Hell Gate this week- that's of course on top of their usual amazing drink deals (3 bucks for a beer and a shot of bourbon) and Rock Band goodness.
