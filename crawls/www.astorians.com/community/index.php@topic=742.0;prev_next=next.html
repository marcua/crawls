stone soup group?
« Reply #8 on: September 12, 2005, 05:25:49 PM »
and just for future reference here's the recipe:

1 onion, diced
2 small carrots, cut into coins
2 sweet potatoes, chopped into 1/2 inch cubes
a few handfuls of green beans
a couple handfuls of kale
some garlic, minced
garbanzo beans, soaked overnight
quinoa (about 1 cup)
3 roma tomatoes
cinnamon
cayenne pepper
salt
black pepper
1 bay leaf
dried basil

sautee the onion in some olive oil.  throw in the potatoes, carrots, garlic and bay leaf.  toss that around a bit.  add the beans, quinoa, and kale along with some water depending on how "soupy" you want the final result to be.  simmer until sweet potatoes and garbanzos are tender.  add green beans and tomatoes toward the end and cook just long enough for the beans to get tender.  throw in your spices, taste and adjust.  serve over polenta.
