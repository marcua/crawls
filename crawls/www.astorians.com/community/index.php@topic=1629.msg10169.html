I went to eat at a new Japanese Restaurant on Ditmars near 37th Street yesterday and liked it a lot.  It's small and cozy and casual.  I didn't want to be adventurous with the sushi, so I had a small salad with grilled chicken and avocado with a nice sauce and they give you a cup of green tea for free with the meal.  I went by myself and felt very comfortable there.  I'm definitely going back.  I want to try the cooked salmon and also the soup looks good.   :lol:
