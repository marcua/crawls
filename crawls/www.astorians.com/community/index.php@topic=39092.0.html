Hello to all Astorians, architecture and history lovers!


After seeing the neighborhood I was raised in so rapidly altered, I decided to begin documenting the soon to be destroyed buildings, signs and architecture. I began a project titled 'Ancient Astoria' and would love to reach out and share the page with you all!


Please feel free to share any photos you may have or ideas of any soon to be demolished buildings! This thread can also help new neighbors to see Astoria's history. I'm looking forward to sharing memories !


Please follow us for daily updates and throwbacks!

I don't have any photos or locations to offer, but the Boy Scouts used to have parades, and I'm sure some of them had family members who took photos along the route, so you might try asking the Boy Scouts, as well as schools, churches, synagogues, and businesses who have been around for a long time to see if they have any photos, and while I'd be cautious of walking up to random old people, you might try visiting senior citizen centers and talking to the people who run them to ask them to ask old people if they have any photos you could scan.  I'm guessing you're already familiar with Forgotten New York.  Good luck with your project, I enjoyed looking at your Instagram collection.
