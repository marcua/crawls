When my husband and I had our wedding in Chicago, we ordered our wedding cake from a bakery in Chinatown.  It was much more affordable than the other bakeries we looked at, and the cake was delicious.  It was very light, with fruit in between the layers.  The Chinatown bakeries will do really colorful decoration, but we asked for just white frosting.  Unfortunately, I don't have any local recommendations, but I feel like I've seen wedding cakes on display in the windows of bakeries in Manhattan Chinatown, around Canal St. - maybe there would be something like this in Flushing too.  I don't think delivery will be a problem.
