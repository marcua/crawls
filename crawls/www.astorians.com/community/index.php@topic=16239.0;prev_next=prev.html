Autumn Fest-Handmade Designers Market Sunday Oct 4th!
« on: September 30, 2009, 03:03:43 PM »
I'll be participating in this event with my jewelry collection consisting of economically priced earrings ($10-$30) bracelets ($15) necklaces ($20-$40) beaded bookmarks ($10) and original photo tile jewelry ($20) available as necklaces or keychains. I use a mix of natural and manmade gemstones, sterling silver and silver plated chains, wires and finding and some base metal beads as well. Hope Astorians can stop by!

Designers and artists will be showcasing their products: jewelry, clothing, accessories, handbags, crochet, bath/body & beauty, candles, stationery, toys, housewares & decor. There will be music and a dj all day long, food and bakery vendors on site. Don't miss this one day only shopping event of the season!
Date:    
Sunday, October 4, 2009
Time:    
11:00am - 5:00pm
Location:    
"Cretans Association of Omonia" AKA- Kritiko Spiti
32-33 31st street
Hosted by Astoria Events (www.astoriaevents.blogspot.com)
