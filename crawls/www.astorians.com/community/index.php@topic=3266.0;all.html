I love the first day of school.  I love my girls!! I keep on saying that I want to change schools and then I come back and I see my students and I realize that I love it here.

As far as hating school - I hated undergraduate school.  I couldn't have picked a more inappropriate place to go - Rensselaer Polytechnic.  I never wanted to step foot in a classroom again.  Then I went to graduate school at Antioch.  There were never two more polar opposite institutes of higher learning.  It brought me back to loving education. Antioch has no grades and no exams.  You learn for the sake of learning and you prove that you learned by writing, or even better, by "doing" something that proves that you know what you're talking about.
