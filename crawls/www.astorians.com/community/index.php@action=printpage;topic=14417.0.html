I don't know how much room/time you have, but refinishing wood furniture is surprisingly simple (And frankly, cathartic- for me at least)
It's mostly sanding and staining. I was able to do 4 solid wood dining room chairs in the span of about 3 hours of actual work (Not counting drying time), and they look good as new.

I would recommend picking up a DIY book, and seeing if it's something that would interest you, I assure you it's pretty fun!
