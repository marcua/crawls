I honestly don't know what everyone has against Starbucks.  I used to work for them in the late 90's and it wasn't that bad of a job.  How many companies will give healthcare for part time employees.  The job actually helped to build up my self esteem and work skills.  A lot of what I know now for my job I learned from Starbucks.

I know Starbucks has lost site of what they stand for over the years, but over all, they aren't a bad company.  I know some people might not like their coffee and that is fine.  I know some won't like the company and that is fine also.  But before anyone "bashes" something, find out a little about it first.
