Title: Yule Log Desecrated
Post by: Cannibal on December 21, 2008, 11:40:31 AM
Jeez!  I was all looking forward to another Eggnog-swillin’, dysfunctional family-gathering Xmas.  And what’s the backround soundtrack for any Xmas Day Morning?  The WPIX Yule Log.  But hold on, kiddies!  The Channel 11 Yule Log is now the Tribune Yule Log, and now some sanctimonious twit programming executive at bankrupt Tribune musta been watching a Maury show and heard one phrase – Makeover!  So, what are you gonna wake up to this Thursday?  Gone is the holiday music, replaced with old-tyme radio plays from the 40’s that they probably didn’t have to buy rights to.  Not that I’m at all against “It’s A Wonderful Life” radio plays, but Also gone is the beautiful Gracie Mansion fireplace with heart-and-room warming sizeable log .  In it’s place looks like something shot in a McMansion;  and you know you’re in a recession when the logs are puny and you can swear the flames are from natural gas and not cedar.

This is just more media on the cheap.  I’ll bet they really didn’t want to play the music rights because the show now airs in multiple cities.
Shame!
