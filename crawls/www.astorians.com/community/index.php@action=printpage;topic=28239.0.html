Title: Everybody Deserves a Massage Week - Open House & Healthy Hour!

Everybody Deserves a Massage Week is an award-winning national event sponsored by Associated Bodywork & Massage Professionals.


Every year practitioners across the country unite to celebrate the health benefits of massage by hosting free and low-cost events in their communities.


Come celebrate with a team of your independent local practitioners this Friday July 18.  Learn easy self-care techniques to try at home, try active isolated stretching, and sample complimentary refreshments by Runa - Clean Energy.  Come with your questions!
