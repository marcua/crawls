Title: Chilean Miners Rescue
Post by: Starwind51 on October 13, 2010, 12:30:43 AM
I think the rescue of the miners in Chile will be one of those moments that you always remember where you were when you saw the first miner brought up. 

The bringing up to the surface of the first miner took place on October 12, and the second minor was just brought up.  I hope all the miners and rescue workers are brought up just as quickly and easily and they arrive in as good condition.

This is an important event for all humanity, as we continue to explore outer space and the deepest oceans we hope and pray for safety, but science will use the experience of these miners in those explorations and in any rescues needed.

I think the period of time these miners spent underground should be given annually to "International Underground Worker Safety Awareness".
