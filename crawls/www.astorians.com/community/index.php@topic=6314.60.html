Went for lunch and wifi today. 

11:30am quiet, super clean place and very friendly staffing. 
Lots of fresh baked breads looked awesome. 

I went for a hot panini chicken, mozz and chipotle mayo sandwich that came with chips.  Very tasty. Served on a foccaccia style bread that was plump, chewy and nice touch of olive oil(???)  Sandwich came with Panera brand potatoe chips that were super crisp and not oily or too salty.  Asked for a glass of water instead of a bottle and got it with no upselling attempt, just a smile. 

Got a booth next to an outlet for my laptop and tried for the wifi.  No luck but had my wireless modem so not a problem for me.  3 minutes later my meal was ready.  Very calm and efficient service at the counters followed by a manager(???) walking around wiping tables and eventually asked to take my tray away. 

Over all I'd give them a two thumbs up.  Good price, fresh and tasty food, clean and nice environment.
