Title: (re)Cycle Plays at Socrates Park
Post by: heff on September 11, 2007, 08:47:19 AM
Confluence Theatre Company presents THE (re)CYCLE PLAYS, a green
theatrical event in a community eco-festival setting. THE (re)CYCLE
PLAYS are a series of short performances in diverse mediums with
social and environmental themes. Featuring plays, dance, music, and
more, the event will promote sustainable practices as a viable
solution to today's foremost moral issue - the ecological degradation
of our society.

When: Saturday, September 15, 1PM til dusk. (Rain date on Sunday
September 16)
Where: Socrates Sculpture Park, Vernon Boulevard at Broadway
Sponsored by: Partnerships for Parks
For more info: visit http://www.recycleplays.com  (http://www.recycleplays.com)
or email info@recycleplays.com

I know they are also looking for volunteers to help setup and breakdown the event.
