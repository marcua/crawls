Title: Re: Nursery schools?
Post by: elenacoln on February 13, 2012, 11:02:29 AM
The info below came from Yahoo! Moms online group (a much more active group as compared to Astorians.com, as far as family and parenting-related issues are concerned):

Khensee Wangmo, who runs a group family day care on Ditmars & 43rd, just expanded (opened the expansion on Feb. 1st), and I believe she may have a couple of slots still available for children ages 2-5.

Khensee is Montessori certified, and taught at Les Enfants and Blossom before opening her own place a few years ago. My son has been with her since he was 4 months old, in September 2009, and we cannot say enough good things about her. She and her staff are calm, nurturing and caring, and provide an excellent environment for the children to learn and play.

Khensee Wangmo
Group Family Day Care
42-20 Ditmars Blvd
347-449-4771
Title: Re: Nursery schools?
Post by: Busy Mom on June 12, 2012, 12:35:21 AM
We enrolled our two year old in Garden School in Jackson Heights. Its an independent school with the option of continuing on until grade 12 which was very attractive to us. It has great reviews and their curriculum is great with music and Mandarin (among other things) in nursery school, French introduced at age 4 and Spanish at age 6. She'll be starting with them in September when she's two and a half.
