Title: Guacamole
Post by: nyc8675309 on October 19, 2007, 08:36:08 AM
Last night we ordered food from the new Mexican restaurant called Guacamole.

 :mrgreen:

The space is very clean and bright.  (They opened 2 weeks ago)

The staff is very friendly and bilingual.

The food was delicious.  Very simple, but very tasty.

We sampled the chicken burrito, the beef burrito , the enchiladas rancheras and the kids ate the "kids pie"  which was basically a chicken and cheese quesadilla (came with fries and corn).

My only  complaint is that the guacamole itself, while very yummy and freshly made was too oniony for my personal tastes.

Affordable menu and good service.

: )
