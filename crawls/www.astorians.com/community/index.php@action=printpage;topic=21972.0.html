Title: Re: Elliptical Machines??
Post by: rg on November 28, 2011, 10:47:14 AM
I have a Livestrong model.  Pricey but worth it.  I find the motion to be much smoother than most machines I've ever used at the gyms.  Machines with a heavier flywheel will be smoother so that's something to look for.

The best advice I can give you is to go to a store like Dick's sports where they have many models set up and you can test them out.
