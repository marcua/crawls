Title: Re: Warique, new Peruvian restaurant, relocating to Astoria from Upper West Side
Post by: bstewart on February 16, 2016, 01:04:48 PM
Restaurants without menus to take away are annoying. This one does have their menu online (http://wariquenyc.com/menu/).

I’ve tried the place once. The Auguadita de Pollo (chicken soup with vegetables) is good and warming on a cold night. I had Lomo Saltado (sautéed beef over fries)  was well prepared and presented, but not to my taste. I had some sort of Peruvian blue-corn soda that was very sweet with a flavor of cinnamon and cloves.

Service was attentive and friendly. I find the decor attractive.

They seem to have solved the inadequate heat and cold drafts that made an earlier restaurant in the same space uncomfortable.

It seemed expensive. When I go back, I expect it will be for the lunch-special menu.
