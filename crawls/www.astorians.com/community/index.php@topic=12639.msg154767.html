I usually really enjoy this place, and have been eating there since they were in their original location.  But recently I was there for dinner and ordered the following entree:
SHRIMP 18

We've been to Bistro 33 twice now, and I've definitely been pleased both times.  The first time was for my girlfriend's birthday, and we fell in love with the lobster mac and cheese and the foie gras apps.  Her steak was great, but my roast pork was only so so.

I definitely like the place, but for us it's a splurge, and will likely be reserved for birthdays or anniversaries.
