ok, i know i'm showing my age, but what exactly is emo?  i know it has something to do with music and a kind of goth look?  is it similar to what was called "new wave" in the 80's?

I always thought it just meant "teen angst".  I had no idea that emo people actually seriously referred to themselves that way.  While I knew the look, I didn't know it was a formal movement. 

Back when I was about 15, in the early '70's, (1870's, as I now like to add 100 years to my age because I'm so Twi-lit), I was up too late one night with what I now jokingly refer to as teen angst - crying to the universe about the state of my life and how nothing interesting was ever going to happen to me, I chanced to look up at the window, and, I kid you not, a voice in my head said "Blue Wind".  This wasn't the ordinary voice-in-our-head that we all have when we think our thoughts, this was something else.  It wasn't my voice.  I was completely surprised by the experience, and I repeated the words, "Blue Wind - I like that!".  It didn't know what it meant, but it was so distinctive.  I just kept the thought to myself for a few days as I contemplated the words, and then decided to use it as my name.  For another 10 years or so that was the name I introduced myself by.  Long later I when I started going online I did get to use it as a screen name for a while, but the e-mail service with which I used the name changed and I had to use my real name, and Blue Wind was already taken on the popular e-mail services, so I became Starwind (and added the 51 for Area 51).

So, long story, but I certainly remember what emo teen angst was like!  And, after that window experience, my life did get interesting.  And gut wrenching crushes - still got them.  It's coincidental to me reading the books now, but my husband, whom I love very much but is in many ways cold to me, and has gone away and I don't know when I'm going to see him again, if ever (I only hear his voice in my head - well, it's a phone call, after all!), and he does have a very distinctive and wonderful personal smell (you can't say body odor if it's nice) that I do/did actually come right up to him and breathe deeply of!  He's not a vampire though.  Unlike Edward, "warmth" is/was a very important component of our relationship, and "warm" a very special word between us.  But it's such a seemingly impossible relationship.  I've long known, it would be bad if he stays, it would be bad if he goes!  Instead of teen angst I've got grown up angst!  Emo!

By the way, until I saw the movie I didn't pick up on Bella being emo emo.I just thought she was being ordinary insecure, but now I see, yeah, she's totally emo.  God, I love these characters!  And yeah, Rob Pattinson....
