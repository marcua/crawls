Title: Autumn Fest-Handmade Designers Market Sunday Oct 4th!
Post by: firelight79 on September 30, 2009, 03:03:43 PM
I'll be participating in this event with my jewelry collection consisting of economically priced earrings ($10-$30) bracelets ($15) necklaces ($20-$40) beaded bookmarks ($10) and original photo tile jewelry ($20) available as necklaces or keychains.
