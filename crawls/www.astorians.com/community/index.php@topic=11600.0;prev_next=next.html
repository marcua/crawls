Bellydance Classes in Astoria, beginner, intermediate and advanced instruction.
« on: November 29, 2009, 08:49:28 PM »
Creative Vibrations is a unique new yoga and bellydance studio in Astoria, Queens teaching bellydance classes every day of the week! We are the only bellydance studio in Queens offering an indepth study of this ancient and beautiful dance from beginner and intermediate to advanced choreography classes.

Bellydance is a women's dance. Its passionate undulations, hypnotic snake arms, exciting shimmies appeal to women who have found sweating it out in the gym just too boring or who would like to feel more feminine and in touch with their bodies.

Bellydance is a sexy and fun way to stay in shape. No wonder it has been around for thousands of years! It is both an ancient and contemporary dance that celebrates female power and grace. According to Dr. Weiss, bellydance is an ideal exercise for a woman's body and self-image. It makes a woman of any age feel radiant and beautiful.
  
The studio conveniently located next to "Ditmars Blvd" subway stop.
