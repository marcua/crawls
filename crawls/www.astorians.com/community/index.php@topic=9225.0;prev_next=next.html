hi junior627.

my wife and i have been living in a 3-story building for over 6 years. personally, i think the good and the bad cancel each. on the one hand, there is the noise from laguardia. on the other, there is the tree filled back yards.

if we had to do it over again, i think we would have gotten a car. most of the time we don't feel all that remote but when you need to get a lot of groceries or go to the pharmacy it kind of sucks having to take the bus or cab or walk. fortunately, there are three buses to choose from.

welcome to the neighborhood. let me know if you have any other questions.
