I live at 30th Avenue. Our regular places are Brick Cafe (33rd St. & 31st Ave.), Indigo (31st Street between 30th & Newtown Aves.), Crescent Lounge (Crescent St. just off B'way), The Irish Rover ( 28th Ave. & 37th St. - I think!),
Grand Cafe (30th Ave. & 37th St.).

718 on Ditmars Blvd. is supposed to be good for drinks too. And there's always Mojave on 31 St. right by the train
station at Ditmars.
