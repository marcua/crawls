Title: Astoria Wine and Spirits Bad Experience
Post by: randall129 on February 28, 2010, 02:01:18 PM
Last Friday night, my wife and I went into Astoria Wines to purchase some wine and enjoy the tasting they do, every Friday night. I've been in the past and my experience was always superb. However, at the most recent visit, we were not greeted by anyone and their was no tasting. Ok, not really a problem. Until I noticed 3-4 employees (?) eating food and drinking wine at the register. They did not acknowledge a single patron that came in to buy wine, only those who seemed to be friends and have some food/ wine with them. Upon choosing a bottle for the evening, my wife asked if there was to be a tasting. The reply: "We don't know, waiting for someone to come and do it."
A couple moments later, I tried to approach the register (which was essentially the dinner table for the employee group assembled there) and asked "may I purchase this wine?" One person snickered at me, and the cashier rung me up.

The wine selection here is good, and the prices are also good. They're not amazing, but I feel that it's ok to pay 10% more for personalized service and attention. I will probably go back, but if I receive this kind of (in)attention again, it will be the last time.
