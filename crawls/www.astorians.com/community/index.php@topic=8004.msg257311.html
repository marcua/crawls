P.S.5 burned down Wednesday, April 12th, 1967.
Huge 5 alarm fire.
You should have seen it!
Each grade was sent to a different school, temporarily.
I was in the 5th grade. We were sent to P.S.171 on 29th Avenue. Pretty close to where I lived so I was lucky.
Across the street the brand new P.S.17 was still under construction, but it was finished enough that we were allowed to use it for the last month of that school year. 😃 First time I ever saw green blackboards!


I.S.126 was very tough! Too many fights!
Mrs Vogt was still principal well into the 80s?
I remember Mr. Stokes! He was popular.
