A little side story:  When a KK opened the first week in Buffalo, a friend of mine was waiting in a huge line that stretched outside and down the block.  A lady came out of the store with two dozen donuts.  A person in the back of the line said "I'll give you $200 for that dozen."  The lady took the money, and got right back in line.

Flash-forward to today, and you can go in there anytime you want, and there's not a soul around.  I think they went from having like 10 stores in the area to about 1 or 2.
