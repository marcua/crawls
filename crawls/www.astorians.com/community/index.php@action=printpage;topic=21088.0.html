Title: What Time is it?
Post by: John b on May 30, 2011, 02:18:35 PM
An elderly couple is riding  an Astoria-bound Q18 bus.
 
The old gent asks his lady: "Hey, what time you got?"
 
She replies: "I got eleven forty-five."
 
He rejoins: "Well, I got eleven forty-three. So, what makes you right and me wrong? For sixty years, you been right."
 
Then it occurs to me, after a certain point in life, the only question that really matters is: What time is it?
