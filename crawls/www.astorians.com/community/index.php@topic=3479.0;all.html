Berry is definitely in the title - my guess is Fresh Berry Farms.  They have a really large section of produce (never bought any so I don't know about organic, either), a large meat department, and a pretty large fish department as well.  I bought meat there that turned out to be spoiled once (but that has happened at other supermarkets, too, and they did refund my money without question), and I wasn't too enthralled with the fish that I bought there, either.

I still keep meaning to go back and explore it a little more, but I never seem to get down that far.

Bennyt, just saw your last post -- I'd say generally no, but it's still worth exploring to see if there are specific brands of things they might carry that you prefer.  I remember finding something there I had been looking for that no other supermarket around Ditmars carries, but now I can't remember what it was.  And despite the fact that I don't think their produce is organic, it is a huge selection - much bigger than anywhere else nearby.

The place on Ditmars is called Berry Fresh Farms and it's in LaGuardia Plaza (around 44th Street maybe). It's a decent place for around here, prices comparable to Trade Fair. I find it about as grungy as Trade Fair, but more spacious and less smelly. They have a huuuuge produce area with some fruits that aren't common around here, like melons I've never heard of and some nice looking champagne grapes, and nice looking blackberries for a dollar a pack. But some aren't so good, like the moldy baby artichokes I saw there this past weekend. Interesting seafood department, and an interesting meat department that's kind of hidden through a corner next to the cash registers. Not sure if I'd buy any of that stuff there. But some of the produce definitely seemed worth buying, though I didn't because I didn't feel like carrying it back.
