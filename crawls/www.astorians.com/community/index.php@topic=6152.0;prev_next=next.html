Brazilian Music, this Friday @ Waltz-Astoria!!!
« on: April 22, 2007, 08:01:08 PM »
Hello Everybody,

Anthony Lanni and Dan Kendall will be performing Brazilian music as a duo this Friday, April 27th at Waltz-Astoria. The styles will include Bossa Nova, Samba, Baiao, and Brazilian Pop, while featuring music by composers, such as Antonio Carlos Jobim, Caetano Veloso, Jorge Ben Jor, Luiz Gonzaga Jr., and many more! The performance will mix instrumental tunes, played on two guitars, and vocal tunes sung by Dan Kendall in both Portuguese and Spanish.  We will also have a special guest or two sitting in during the performance.  A great jazz singer named Robin Aleman will be going on before us.  For more information, go to: http://www.anthonylanni.com

 Friday, April 27th, 9:00 PM
 Brazilian Jazz Duo at:
 Waltz-Astoria
 23-14 Ditmars Boulevard
 Btwn. 23rd & 24st
 Astoria, NY
 718-95-MUSIC   
 no cover, $7 drink minimum
 
http://www.anthonylanni.com
http://myspace.com/anthonylanni
http://www.waltz-astoria.com

Take the N or W train towards Queens to the Ditmars stop (last stop in Queens).  Once you get off the subway, you'll be on 31st street.  Walk North to Ditmars Boulevard, turn left on Ditmars (West), and walk to 23rd St. It's about a 5 minute walk once you get off of the subway.  Waltz is a great place to hear music!  Good wines, desserts, coffee, tea, etc...

Hope to see you there!

Anthony

Hello All,

Sorry for such very short notice, but there has been a recent change in the cover for this event.  Since this is going to be a double bill, it is going to be $5 cover and $7 drink minimum.  I apologize again for the late notice...I hope this reaches everyone on time.  I assure you, you will get your moneys worth in wonderful music!!!  Once again, Robin Aleman will be singing jazz tunes before us, and then Dan Kendall and I will be up next with a bunch of Samba, Bossa Nova, and Spanish tunes.  Plus, Waltz is an excellent place to hear live music...the sound there is great, and it is very laid back and friendly!  I hope you can make it!  If you have any questions, just let me know!
