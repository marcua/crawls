There are many "church" organizations with mysterious locations in Brooklyn or elsewhere, that leave notes on my doorstep to pick up used clothing.  I called one day and arranged a pick up, and left the bag outside.  I checked off the box for leave a receipt.  Lo and behold, the bag disappears but no receipt.  I called the so called church and the woman who answered claimed to have no idea what I was talking about, and refused to help me get a receipt.  She was extremely rude in rebuffing my attempts to get information, and then hung up on me.  They call themselves the Church of God or something.  I called back and left a message on the machine asking someone to call me, but no one ever did.  Needless to say, I will never be donating to these people again, whoever they are.  I know the Salvation Army does pickups, but they're not entirely reliable.  My scheduled pickup, when I moved last year, of furniture, etc. never came through, even though I had called the day before to confirm.  Apparently the guy cancelled his run, and the Salvation Army never informed anyone.   I ended up having a last minute giveaway to the neighbors.

A friend with a car might be your best bet.
