Title: Check out ArtSPY on QPTV!
Post by: arts1966 on February 06, 2008, 04:51:16 PM
ArtSpy is a series of 8 half-hour television shows, presenting lively conversations among established and emerging artists, authors, and representatives from arts organizations. ArtSPY was produced in partnership with the Queens Council on the Arts (QCA) and Queens Public Television (QPTV).

HOOKED
Thursday, February 7, 2008 - 6:00pm on Channel 56
Knitting artists, passionate about their work, discuss unconventional and innovative knitting projects.

ART COLLECTIVES
Friday, February 8, 2008 – 9:00pm on Channel 34
Representatives of various Queens Art Collectives discuss the resources and support they offer to artists.

HOT ART
Thursday, February 14, 2008 – 6:00pm on Channel 56
Emerging artists, excited about living and working in Queens, discuss their life and work. 

THE WRITE SCENE
Friday, February 15, 2008 – 9:00pm on Channel 34
Thursday, February 21, 2008 – 6:00pm on Channel 56
Friday, February 29, 2008 – 9:00pm on Channel 34
Provides insight on the origins and inspirations of four authors and how Queens plays a significant role in their careers.

PROJECT DIVERSITY
Friday, February 22, 2008 – 9:00pm on Channel 34
Founder of Project Diversity Brooklyn, discusses the history and importance of bringing the multi-venue exhibition project to Queens for QCA’s Project Diversity Queens.
 
THE ART OF GIVING
Thursday, February 28, 2008 – 6:00pm on Channel 56
Supporters of the arts discuss why philanthropy is important, and different ways they contribute to the arts in Queens.
