Title: Green Space Blooms Family Matinee
Post by: Dance Entropy on March 06, 2014, 11:38:59 AM
Green Space Blooms presents:
WILD KINGDOM
A family matinee, part of the the Green Space Blooms Annual Dance Festival!
Saturday, April 12th at 1pm
(Long Island City)The annual Green Space Blooms dance festival appeals to all ages.
Valerie Green/Dance Entropy[/b] will perform WILD KINGDOM: a vibrant, fun-filled afternoon of dance for the whole family to enjoy, including audience participation. [/font]
The performance will take place at Green Space, on April 13 at 1PM.  Admission: $10 for all ages. 
Tickets are available online at www.GreenSpaceStudio.org (http://www.greenspacestudio.org/) or at the door.

Dance Entropy will perform three works: Dandia, Chiquita Chiquita, and Wild Kingdom. All three works are stimulating crowd pleasers, getting audience members dancing and laughing - a hit for all ages to enjoy!
