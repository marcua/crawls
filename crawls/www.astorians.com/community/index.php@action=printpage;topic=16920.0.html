Title: Re: Any parts of Astoria with no alternate side parking?
Post by: Alison on December 21, 2009, 01:07:24 PM
Quote from: dullnboring on December 21, 2009, 11:23:15 AM
My next questions.  Is there a website which lists the alternate side parking schedule for the city?  Does it change from one block to the next, where one block has it two days a week and the next is four days a week, all on different weeks, or are large chunks of the neighborhood under the same schedule?  I’m at Ditmars & 33rd and I believe my immediate block is on a twice a week schedule.  Also, if there is going to be a snowstorm the day that one needs to move their car, what is the best plan of action?  Also if the cleaning day falls on a holiday, does cleaning get skipped over that day until the next scheduled cleaning, or do they push it to the next day?  And finally, since I foresee this becoming something I will become acquainted with unfortunately…..how much are tickets for failing to move your car?

Every block has different days and times, but in the Ditmars area, it's 2 days a week. Just read the signs.

If there's going to be a snowstorm, and you're parked on a one-way street, try to park on the left side of the street. The plows push the snow to the right. If you're on the left, you'll have less to dig out.

Alternate side doesn't always get canceled for snow - it's kind of a case-by-case (or storm-by-storm) call. If you're ever not sure, call 311 - it's usually the first thing the recording mentions. There are some holidays where it's suspended, the calendar is here:
http://www.nyc.gov/html/dot/html/motorist/scrintro.shtml (http://www.nyc.gov/html/dot/html/motorist/scrintro.shtml)

On the holidays (like this Friday, Christmas), it just gets skipped over, and not pushed to another day.

If you use Gmail/Google calendar, someone usually makes a custom calendar of the Alternate side "holidays" and I have mine set to email me 2 days before every time Alt side is canceled, so I don't have to think about moving my car for that day. But I get a little overzealous with my calendar and the auto-emailing things you can do :)

Also, be sure you check the times on the alt side signs. My area is on a Wed/Thurs schedule, and in front of my apt, I can't park from 9:30-11. Around the corner, it's 11:30-1, even though it's the same days.

It sounds like a lot to remember, but it's really not so bad once you get the hang of it. Sometimes you might have to park a little further away, but it's worth it not to get the ticket (last time I got one, I *think* it was $65, but this was a few years ago).

Post by: cyberia on December 21, 2009, 02:51:49 PM
In the Ditmars area, it's only 1-2 days a week that requires moving. Below 31st it's Monday and Tuesday. One side of the street is Monday, the other Tuesday. Above 31st it's Wednesday and Thursday. Just move it the night before so you don't forget as the meter cops will be ticketing your car at 8:30:01am in the morning.

If the weather is bad, call 311 and they will tell you if the parking is suspended or not for today and the next day.
