Bungalo is a nice new lounge on Broadway at 32nd Street.  They just did a major renovation and it's lovely.  The food is above normal bar fare and they mix strong drinks - competitively priced for Astoria, the same as Crescent Lounge.  They have a DJ some nights - mixed crowd, some younger folks, some middle aged, gay, straight, men, women, it's nice.  They do have a TV, though it's not always on loud enough for you to hear it, DJs only some nights.
