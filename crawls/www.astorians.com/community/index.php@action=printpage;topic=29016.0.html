Title: 11th NYC Musical Saw Festival
Post by: AstoriaArts on April 04, 2015, 07:08:54 PM

Do you know that the largest international gathering of people who make music with carpenter's handsaws is in ASTORIA?
Come experience it: Concert, art exhibit & workshops revolving around this 300 year old art form.
50 musical saw players from around the world, the Astoria Symphonic Choir and Trinity Handbell Choir will performdiverse music styles (classical, pop, folk, show-tunes, etc.).
Natalia 'Saw Lady' Paruz will showcase music composed by Astoria composer Scott Munson for movie soundtracks such as Richard Gere's new movie 'Time Out of Mind' (in theaters September 2015).

When: Saturday, May 30th, 2015, 2pm
Where: Trinity Lutheran Church, 31-18 37th Street, Astoria, NY
Info: www.MusicalSawFestival.org (http://www.MusicalSawFestival.org)
Admission: $10
