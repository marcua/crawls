You’re Invited: ASPCA Mega Match-a-thon Adoption Event

Spring is here, people, and it’s time to meet your match—your four-legged match! That’s why the ASPCA is teaming up with dozens of animal shelters across the country for the first-ever ASPCA Mega Match-a-thon. Next weekend (March 30-April 1), animal rescues across the country are joining forces to co-host some epic adoption events, and you’re invited!

What: ASPCA Mega Match-a-thon adoption event

Who: ASPCA
Animal Care & Control of NYC (AC&C)
Animal Haven
Picasso Veterinary Fund of the Mayor's Alliance for NYC's Animals
Sean Casey Animal Rescue

When: Friday, March 30: 2:00-7:00 P.M.
Saturday, March 31: noon-5:00 P.M.
Sunday, April 1: noon-5:00 P.M.

Where: 67th Street and Broadway
New York, NY 10023

More Info: Please visit www.animalalliancenyc.org (http://click.aspcaemail.org/?qs=09c2412ab2bc7be0673ff17d0943d2d1263564a72b6d40c725137eaf5560c0c3).
