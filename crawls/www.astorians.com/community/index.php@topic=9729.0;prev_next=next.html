Found this post on another website.  Thought it might find some interest here:



Starting Tuesday, June 17th at 10:30am, our "Who's Feeding Mommy?" series is back and better than ever!

Sai Organics at 30-21 30th Avenue at 31st Street (just steps away from the N/W station is opening it's new group space above the health food store and cafe. The door is one door to the right of Sai's (closer to 31st Street).


As a mommy, you spend a lot of time and energy making sure your family is healthy and eating well? But who puts as much care into looking after YOUR health?


If you are a "mommy" of any age or stage, join us for an hour of topic discussion while our kiddies play. Mommies-to-be and mommies of older children are welcome, too. After, we can linger and chat at Sai's delicious cafe and juice/smoothie bar downstairs.


The fee for this 4-class series is $20 per session per mommy including topic discussion notes, recipes, food samples and much more!

Call 917-697-3618 or email Megan for details. RSVP is requested but not required. Drop-ins welcome. Cash only please. Stroller parking may be tricky, so wear your babies if you can, or plan to carry the stroller up the stairs and fold it if possible.


The topics will be as follows (subject to change):

June 17th - 6 Foolproof Ways to Get Off the Sugar Rollercoaster
June 24th - 5 Family Friendly Ways to Serve Veggies
July 1st - 7 Dinners for 4 Under $15
July 8th - 10 Power Foods Every Mama Needs

Tell your friends! We'll see you there.
