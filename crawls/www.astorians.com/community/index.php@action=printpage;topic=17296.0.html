Title: NY Trifles

I see shows about twice a decade. And I likely would have missed this if not for one of the performers.

http://theater.nytimes.com/2010/02/02/theater/reviews/02trifles.html

Quote
That mood is the most absorbing aspect of this hourlong “Trifles.” In reimagining Glaspell’s play, the ensemble gambles that a host of nontraditional methods will help heighten the tension and foster compassion. Some of those chances pay off. A few don’t. And others just need to sink in.

“Trifles” continues through Feb. 14 at the Ontological Theater at St. Mark’s Church, 131 East 10th Street, East Village; (212) 352-3101.


A small experimental take on a small investigation decades ago.
Intimate, strange, but likely to stick with you.
Quite professional. Struck by the performances, even when odd, and the efficient staging. Glad to have chanced to enjoy.
