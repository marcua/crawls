Went to Sugarfreak for my birthday dinner last night, first time there and loved it. We were there with my 2 year old daughter, the waitress was extremely friendly and made all of us feel every welcomed. The decor is beautiful; very clear they put a lot of love into this place. The food and drinks were great, I had the fried chicken which was incredibly juicy. I actually dropped the final bite on the floor, which was devastating. Also had the crawfish boudin balls, mac & cheese, and my wife had the shrimp po'boy- all were great. Didn't love the french fries (which reminded me of McCain's) but a very minor issue. Had a sazerac and pimms cup which also were excellent (had never had either before). All in all, a very satisfying experience...a place like this that made me feel welcome and shows such care in its ambience and food will certainly get repeat business.
