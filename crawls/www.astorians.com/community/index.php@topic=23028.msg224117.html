Just had a great Lunch, the plain slice was crispy and the cheese wasnt too much, the tomato sauce balanced nicely. I tried another with Mushrooms and that was even better.
The prices are still within reach, thank goodness for that :) .
 
The seafood salad is a bargin for all the stuff they put in it and most importantly it has always been fresh.
 
Oh Drink Peak Organic Draft with your food, UMMM my favorite beer right now.
 
Cheers fellow Astorians.
