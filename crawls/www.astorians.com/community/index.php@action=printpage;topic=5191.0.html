Title: Sudden Fiction Workshop beginning this Saturday at Women's Studio Center
Post by: WomenStudioCenter on February 13, 2007, 01:44:58 PM
SUDDEN FICTION:
Flash Fiction, Short Fiction & Prose
How can we tap into our unique thinking, feeling and knowing processes to discover and develop our own, special voices, create words that sing, images that startle, delight, and disturb? How can we commuicate effectively what we wish ardently to express? This workshop will sharpen writing skills with a focus on process and language, language as music, language as visual art, via discussions, writing exercises, prompts, and readings by participants and authors of innovative short fictions and novels. This workshop is for intermediate and advanced prose writers interested in exploring what more they can do with language and narrative, as well as writers who find themselves blocked from expressing themselves in a truly creative way.

Carol Novack, Instructor

Eight Saturdays, 2-4pm beginning February 17.

More about Carol Novack

Carol Novack is the author of a chapbook of poems-Living Alone without a Dictionary- and the former recipient of an arts grant from the Australian government. Her prose, poetry and fusions can or will be found in The Penguin Book of Australian Women Poets, American Letters & Commentary, Anemone Sidecar, Big Bridge, BlazeVOX, Del Sol Review, Diagram, First Intensity, 5_trope, La Petite Zine, LIT, Milk, Muse Apprentice Guild, Notre Dame Review, Opium, Orphan Leaf Review, Pindeldyboz, Segue, Salt River Review, Unpleasant Event Schedule, Word Riot, Yankee Pot Roast, and other publications. Carol publishes Mad Hatters' Review and occasionally, a blog: http://carolnovack.blogspot.com. She is also co-editing an anthology of innovative short fictions. Carol is a former: adjunct instructor of English at Baruch and Queens Colleges; criminal defense and first amendment attorney; and a recent graduate of Hunter College School of Social Work, where she obtained an MSW with a concentration in community organizing and social group work.
