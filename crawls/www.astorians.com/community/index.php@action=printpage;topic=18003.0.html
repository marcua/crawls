OA stands for OUT Astoria, which is an organization for LGBT folks in Astoria, Long Island City, and Sunnyside.  We do special service projects and mentoring, as well as mixers, dinners, and brunches.  This fosters positive relationships between the LGBT community and the greater Astoria community.
