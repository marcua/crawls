Title: Re: Preferential rent?
Post by: GenXnyc on April 10, 2007, 09:30:17 AM
You have no idea what the rent hike will be at the end of the lease.  I signed with a preferential of $250 2 years ago.  On the first renewal (rent-stabilized) my rent only increased the standard amount.  On the most recent renewal, it went up the standard amount and the preferential was reduced to $150.  I'm sure next time, it will be eliminated completely, but I'm moving so, I'll be curious to see what the apartment is listed at...probably no preferential.

You should go wander around and talk to other tenants and ask what the history has been if that's possible and try asking them directly.  If they are legitimate and honest, they should tell you what they normally do (take it for what it is though).  I'd also suggest you check with the department of Housing website (I'm sure you can search for the link on the board) and enter the property addresses and see what any previous building violations have been and if they are always recurring or fixed on time.
