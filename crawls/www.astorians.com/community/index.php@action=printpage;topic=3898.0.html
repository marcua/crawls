Friday Night Services resume with a special welcome to our new members: Friday, November 3rd, 8:00pm
Please show your support for our Friday services, as we kick them off again after our summer hiatus. We offer an intimate service that often includes participation from the congregants, questions for the rabbi, etc. The services go from about 8-9:15pm; join us afterwards for refreshments at our Oneg. The Friday service is usually followed by lively discussions on various religious topics and current events. On November 3rd, our service will feature participation from our new members. All are welcome.

Astoria Center of Israel Synagogue
27-35 Crescent Street, Astoria 718-278-2680
http://www.astoriacenter.org
Take the N or W subway towards Queens to the 30th Avenue stop. Walk west on 30th Avenue (towards Athens Square Park) for about 4 short blocks; turn right on Crescent Street and walk a half block down on the right.
