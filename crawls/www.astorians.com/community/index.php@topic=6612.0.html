For some reason, almost every apartment in Astoria doesn't allow pets.  It's really frustrating.  The apartment complex on 21 Ave and 27 St down to the park, does take pets, not sure about how many pounds.  The phone number is listed on the outside of the building on 21 Ave and 27/26th St.  Otherwise try private houses, perhaps try going through Craiglist by owner?  Your rental price is very high for Astoria, you would think an owner wouldn't mind so much if they were getting that kind of money.  I know it's expensive but buying a house might be an option.
