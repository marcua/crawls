Title: Powhatan Democratic Club September Meeting September 8
Post by: costa01 on September 04, 2010, 07:26:42 AM
Dear member and friend:

Our next meeting will be held on a special night WEDNESDAY September 8th at our clubhouse located at 41-05 Newtown Road, Astoria, NY 11103 at 7 PM. 

This is our pre-primary election meeting! We will be joined by our Congresswoman Carolyn Maloney, our candidate for State Senate NYS Assemblymember Michael Gianaris and our candidate for NYS Assembly Aravella Simotas. There are important races on the ballot this... primary, come learn what Powhatan will be doing on Primary Day September 14th.

Prior to our meeting on TUESDAY September 7 at 7:30PM, we encourage you to attend the United Community Civic Association's Annual 9/11 Memorial Candlelight Ceremony. This event is done in conjunction with the Port Authority of NY/NJ. The vigil will be held at McManus Memorial Park at 81 Street/Grand Central Parkway Service Road. We hope that you can attend this important event for our community as well as our meeting.

Hoping that everyone had a great summer and we look forward to seeing you on WEDNESDAY September 8th.

Light refreshments will be served.
