Late to the party, but I wanted to add some resources, since others, like me, might discover this thread.

I'm in Queens and run a bed bug blog called Bedbugger: http://bedbugger.wordpress.com
We have lots of FAQs about bedbugs, which some may find useful,
 here: http;//bedbugger.wordpress.com/faqs

Some of you mentioned the bedbugblog.blogspot.com, which is not being updated much.  The author, Caitlin, also started a support group called Bedbugger on yahoo (which spawned the Bedbugger blog, but is separate from it).  Anyone needing support or information while they deal with bedbugs should check it out: http://health.groups.yahoo.com/group/bedbugger/

In addition, I just started a yahoo group called StopBedBugsNYC:  http://groups.yahoo.com/group/StopBedBugsNYC/
It's for New Yorkers who want to pressure local officials to do more to stop the spread of bed bugs.  It's a brand new group, but it sounds like some of you might be interested in getting involved in this, so please join us.  Alternatively, if anyone's taken any political action, let us know and we'll get behind it.
