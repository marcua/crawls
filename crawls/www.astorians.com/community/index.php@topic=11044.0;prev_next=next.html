Looking for larger apt in ditmars area for sometime in sept
« on: July 31, 2009, 03:37:41 PM »
I have a reasonably large budget (up to $2700/mo, would prefer to be around $2300-2500) and high expectations that haven't yet been crushed. I'm willing to move any time in the month of September and don't mind working with a broker. I much prefer the ditmars (21st -45st, 23av-21av) area, but would go for the right place outside of that area. I don't commute into manhattan, so proximity to the train isn't very important.

Here's what I'm looking for:

One bedroom and a second area that can be dedicated as a workspace--this could be a second bedroom that's not tiny, a mezannine, a second living area, a particularly large main living area, whatever. A third bedroom wouldn't bother me, but I don't know what I'd use it for. Basically--I'm looking for a larger space with at least one bedroom and room to work.

Any type of building is ok so long as the apartment is in decent shape inside--this means kitchen and bathroom should be recent enough to not be gross or in disrepair + there should be a dishwasher or the ability to add one. I like to cook so nice kitchens are persuasive.

Parking is crucial. In parts of the neighborhood where street parking is easy (like the 40s around ditmars), I'd be ok without a dedicated spot, but anywhere where there's going to be hunting, dedicated parking is a must.

Outdoor space is good to have. I wouldn't pass up an otherwise perfect apartment over it, but a place to put a grill would be a big plus.

Here's what I'm looking to find out:

1. Am I completely out of my mind thinking this is possible?
2. When should I start really looking? I've seen a few places already, but a lot of what's out there is still for august despite august being tomorrow.
3. if anyone knows of anything like what I'm describing (broker or otherwise), feel free to respond and I'll get in touch.
