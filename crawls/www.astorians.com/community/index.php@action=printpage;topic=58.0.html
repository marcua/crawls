Halloween Extravaganza 2004 @ The Cathedral of St. John the Divine

"The Cabinet of Dr. Calagari"

One of the Cathedral's most unique and popular events, The Halloween Extravaganza promises an evening of thrills and chills, featuring the 1920 silent film The Cabinet of Dr. Caligari. Cathedral Organist Timothy Brumfield will accompany this haunting classic.

The film is followed by the frightful pageantry of The Grand Procession of the Ghouls. Staged by Ralph Lee, the Obie Award winning creator of New York City's Halloween Parade, the Procession is a theatrical tapestry of fabulous puppets, creatures and special effects. It is a memorable Halloween experience for young and old alike.

Tickets: $15. For information about Student/Senior tickets or Group Sales, please call 212.662.2133.
