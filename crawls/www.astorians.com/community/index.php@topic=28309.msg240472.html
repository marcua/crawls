I didn't really like Astoria Coffee. The place was really cool looking, and the staff wonderfully nice, but the one beverage I tried -- a regular iced coffee -- wasn't very good. Had a weird flavor to it. I'll have to try some of their other drinks to see if maybe it was just the iced coffee that I don't like.

The first couple of times I stopped, they still weren't fully up and running, and didn't have cream for coffee. Next time I stopped in, they only had fat free creamer. Pastries looked good, though they didn't have a huge selection. Really hope they succeed.
