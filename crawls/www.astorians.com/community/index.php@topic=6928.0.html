I've never been to either gym, but my former roommate and neighbors went to Matrix, in the shopping plaza at 43rd & Ditmars. They do have shorter hours (isn't Rock 24 hours or something?), but old roomie liked it because she said it was more low-key and less meat-markety than The Rock. Also, they seem to have some good classes (the class calendar was a staple on our fridge). Like I said, I've haven't been to either, so this is second-hand advice.

Just an alternative you may want to look into if you live on that side of the N :)

Also, most gyms will give you a trial membership so you can try it out - you'll have to sit through the hard sell, but it may be worth it to help you make up your mind before you sign a contract.
