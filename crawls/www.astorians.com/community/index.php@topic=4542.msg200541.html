Please understand that I hadn't read the having-a-baby-in-3D message when I made the "isn't it romantic" remark.   :-D  I had just gotten out of the movie and needed to say something.

Maybe it's because I've already read the books and know what's going to happen, but while I loved the first Twilight Movie, I didn't think two was as good as one, and I don't think three is either.  With 3 I felt like there was just a checklist of scenes to shoot and the director just went down the list.  Summit should have kept the original director, she really understood the story.  Still, I think the actors are perfect for their roles.  I will once again point out, Rob Pattinson is not just doing an American accent, he's doing the accent of the time and place in which his character lived!  Kristen Stewart portrays Bella as being more secure in her relationship with Edward, and is Taylor Lautner old enough yet that we can freely appreciate admire discuss him?  Oooh laaah laaah!  Riley's really good, Bree's really good, but once again I gotta point out - vampires don't change!  Unless an actor dies, you don't replace an actor.  A gaff of movie trivia for the ages - it was crazy to replace Rachelle Lefevre!

While endlessly perusing Twilight paraphernalia before seeing Eclipse I had come across the Jansport backpack Bella wears in the movie, and eventually had to have it, so I bought, but the movie SCREAMS product placement with that backpack.  Kristen Stewart's practically MODELING the backpack: it has so much face time it should have gotten ACTING credit!
