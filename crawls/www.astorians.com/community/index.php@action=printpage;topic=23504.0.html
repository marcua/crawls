Title: Break Bar & Billards PARTY and Fundraiser!!!
Post by: westernqnscomposts on September 18, 2012, 01:04:13 PM
On Wednesday, September 19, Break Bar & Billards will host BORO Magazine’s Party + Fundraiser benefiting the Western Queens Compost Initiative (WQCI) (https://www.facebook.com/wqnscompost).

WQCI is a network of local composting and scrap food acceptance sites. With the support of the NYC Compost Project and Build It Green! NYC, the group hopes to expand its number of drop-off sites throughout Western Queens. Most WQCI pickup sites are green market-affiliated and include Socrates Sculpture Park, Skillman Avenue and Two Coves Community Garden. The BORO fundraiser will help launch one new site in Astoria at a high trafficked, convenient location: the Broadway NQ Subway Station. So come on out and show some love!!
