My family in Sicily always makes Fiori di Zucca stuffed with fresh ricotta and not mozzarella, which I highly recommend. Ricotta is a bit lighter.  The flowers themselves are fairly easy to obtain from the vast selection of Italian oriented Astoria produce markets in the summer. These differences in recipes are usually regional and it is quite interesting to compare the difference/alterations in ingredients.
