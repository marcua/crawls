"Tribute in Light" will be lit on September 11, 2008 beginning at sunset.  The Mahattan Bridge is situated in a rather crazy place, what with Chinatown right there, so I wouldn't recommend going there.
I would suggest that you go to either the Brooklyn Bridge or Battery Park.  As long as you're not wandering around late you should be alright. Just use your judgement.   

For Battery Park you could take the "W" train to Whitehall St/South Ferry.

You're referring to "The Cove between the Bridges" (Brooklyn and Manhattan).  This is situated in the DUMBO section of Brooklyn.  "The Cove" is part of "Brooklyn Bridge Park".  I use to live in Clinton Hill, Brooklyn and would frequently photograph the skyline from this vantage point, sometimes at 1:00 in the morning.  You'll be fine. No sweat. Would be sort of interesting if you could capture the waterfall, the bridge and the two beams of light in the distance.

You could also go to Pier 1, located on the southern side of the Brooklyn Bridge.
