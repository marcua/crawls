I went yesterday for the first time this year and was somewhat nervous based on what people have written here lately.  Needless to say, my fears were all for naught as it turned out to be a pretty pleasant experience.

We got there around 2 and were able to choose our own table.  There was a $10 cover due to the Czech Slovak festival going on but we knew this in advance and decided to pay since one of the people in our group was Czech.  Needless to say, the cover was actually worth is as the band played nearly nonstop for the four hours we were there and the gymnastics demo was pretty entertaining.

As for the service, it was surprisngly good as well.  We went up to the bar to get our own pitchers but ordered food directly from the waitress, who couldn't have been nicer.  There were people ordering directly from the grill but we were too lazy to do so.  Even though we ordered from both the grill and regular menu the food only took about 20 minutes to come out for a party of seven people.  We never had trouble locating our waitress when we needed something and overall the experience was much better than expected.

Given it's close proximity to my house I'm a little more inclined to keep patronizing this place as Studio Square gains popularity as it hopefully will mean that the locals can enjoy the Beer Garden we've come to love over the years.
