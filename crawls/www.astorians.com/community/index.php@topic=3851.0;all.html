Local favorites that aren't very expensive:
Mee Noodle (53rd and 9th) for cheap, tasty, and amazingly fast Chinese food
Burrito Box (9th between 57th and 58th) for inexpensive, tasty, and amazingly fast burritos and tacos and such
Vynl (9th and I think 51st)for New American/Thai food that costs a little more than the other places. I really like their cobb salad wrap.
Casabianca (10th and 57th) for general Italian stuff like pastas, they have particularly good side salads too
Chanpen (9th and 51st) for Thai that's a little more expensive than the other local Thai, but they have much better pad thai than the other places
Kiran (53rd between 9th and 10th) for Indian

Let me know if you need more options. ;)
