Yax Pac, pronounced Yaash Posh...Yax Pac was the last great King of the Maya sight Copan, which is in Honduras.  I was a Maya archaeology major in college and I went on a dig in Honduras at a place called the "Cave of the Glowing Skulls".  They actually filmed us working and the story was on Discovery Channel a few times.  It was an amazing experience.  At the end of the trip we drove to the wonderful Maya sight of Copan which I had studied about in my classes.  I was blown away by the sight.  It is one of the most amazing places I have ever been.  There is an altar their called "Altar Q".  This is the picture in my Avatar.  To sum up the story, the altar is a symbol of the cyclical cosmology of the Maya.  I have always held a rather strong belief in this cyclical cosmology as well.  All things in history and life seem to happen in cycles...not everything of course, but many many things.  At least in my world.  When I came home from the dig I had this altar tattooed on my leg.  It is my tribute to the Maya and their influence on my life.
