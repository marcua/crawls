In no particular order, if you want take out:

Michael Angelo's (thoughts are with them after the chef passed away - truly awesome guy) on 23rd Avenue and 29th Street
Brother's Pizza on 14th street and 30th - a rare gem west of 21st
La Vita Mia on 24th Ave and 27th street - my local place. Horrendous meatballs (and I truly love meatball pizza), but the chicken and tomato and regular pizza was magnificent. But one thing that truly stands out: Buffalo Chicken pizza. It's one of the finest pieces of comfort food you'll ever come across, just be warned that each slice appears to weigh close to a pound, so either plan on eating all weekend, or get some friends in.

And Vesta's thin crust pizza kicks ass - especially the hangover pizza on the brunch menu.
