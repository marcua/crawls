If you're walking down the street or having a conversation and you stop to look up at a firetruck or ambulance flying by wailing their sirens, then youre not a NYer.

If you get off an elevator, escalator, or reach the top of a set of stairs and then stop to look around instead of continuing to walk, you're not a NYer.

If you think that a 10 minute walk from Point A to Point B is too far, you're not a NYer...I've had friends who were visiting suggest we call a cab to get a ride to the subway. What?? Its really not far. Maybe 2/3 of a mile.
