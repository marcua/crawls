Title: Re: Small business banking?
Post by: MagicPie on January 18, 2011, 03:53:05 PM
After much research, I decided to stick with Chase.  Turns out, free checking is becoming a thing of the past with most other banks.  And banks that offer it free for a year are only delaying my hassle of finding a new bank in a year.  Chase bank has "Business Basic" account which is free IF you use the debit card 5 times in a month (OR have direct deposit over a certain amount OR have a min. daily balance of $5,000 -- neither of which applies to me.)  You can then link your "premier" personal account for free.  I think I can manage that.

The hassle annoying part is that you have to go into the bank and switch all the accounts up.  I went in to explain this to Chase and they are aware of it and how annoying it is and really worked to make it as painless for me as possible.  And I must say, the customer service I got today was really good.  My original annoyance was directed at one person at one branch so I decided to try another branch and had a MUCH better experience.  (Just for them to admit that it's annoying helped a lot.)  In the end, a major selling point was the bazillion ATM's and branches around the city. 

That's my two cents.
