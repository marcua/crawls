Hello, Astorians. Queens Bike is a new organization I am a part of. We are working to increase the number of Queens bike lanes, primarily in Jackson Heights, Elmhurst, Corona, East Elmhurst and Astoria.

If anyone is interested in joining or helping, please go to any of the following:

https://www.facebook.com/qnsbike?_rdr=p (https://www.facebook.com/qnsbike?_rdr=p)
https://twitter.com/qnsbike (https://twitter.com/qnsbike)
http://qns.bike/ (http://qns.bike/)

Also, we have just created a petition to build a network of bike lanes connecting Queens neighborhoods. If interested, please take a moment to sign --- it takes less than a minute, you don't need an account, and you don't need to publicly identify yourself if you don't want to.  The petition is here:

https://www.change.org/p/amazing-map-bike-lane-could-cut-travel-time-in-queens-in-half-with-no-emissions-and-minimal-cost-sign-the-petition (https://www.change.org/p/amazing-map-bike-lane-could-cut-travel-time-in-queens-in-half-with-no-emissions-and-minimal-cost-sign-the-petition)

Thank you.
