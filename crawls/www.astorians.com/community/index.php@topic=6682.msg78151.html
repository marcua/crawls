Hi guys,

Sorry if some of you find the ads to be annoying - the thing is that the ads don't work if they are in the upper right hand corner of the screen for the very reason pointed out - they are difficult to see. Ads are only worth while if occasionally someone finds something interesting about them and visits the sponsor site.

That being said I'm sensitive to the opinions and preferences of all members, but it's an illusion these days that running an internet site is free - it takes a lot of work, and the more the site grows, the more it costs in hosting and maintenance.

I don't really feel that the ads are being "forced" on anyone though - the internet (and television, and radio) are all paid for by advertising - even our subway rides are partially paid for by advertising. That being said, I do like the model of giving people an option to disable the ads if they choose, and this is why the ads don't display for supporters. You can become a supporter by contributing any amount, and that is good for one full year.

I'm still trying to find the right balance needed to make this thing self-sustaining - so over the next few days/weeks you may see less, more, or different ads or ideas as I try and figure that out. I do appreciate the feedback, and also the support of everyone who uses the board. Whether that's by tolerating some advertising, or supporting the site directly.
