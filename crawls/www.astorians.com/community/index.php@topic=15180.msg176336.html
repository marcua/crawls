Art show @ the Renaissance on 21st St. tonight
« on: June 05, 2009, 05:00:08 PM »
Want something cheap, elegant and fun to do tonight?

Check out the art show in the Renaissance building tonight, 8pm.

It's on the ground floor of that apt building on 21st Street across from White Castle (near Broadway.) Right next door to the post office.

There's usually some light food and red/white wine at these things.

The art is unpretentious, contemporary. The space will make you go, "Holy crap, I had no idea this was right here!!" It's a hidden gem.

Anyway, it's sponsored by Art-Trope, and if you're an artist or in the art industry, this should be of particular interest. Art-Trope is my French artist friend's collaborative that seeks to help artists make connections worldwide.

So if you're getting cabin fever from all the rain, treat yourself to a cab ride over there (and maybe dinner at White Castle afterward  :lol: )
