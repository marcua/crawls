Hi everyone!  I'm new to the boards and just wanted to introduce myself.  I've actually been a lurker around here for some time, but figured I'd finally take the plunge and just sign up for the forum.  I've been living in Astoria for just over 4 years, and I currently live near Astoria Park, just off of Astoria Park/Hoyt Avenue South.  Prior to that, I lived over on Crescent Street, near Broadway.  I've lived in Queens most of life, and spent most of my formative years in Whitestone.  I really enjoy living in Astoria.  You really can't beat the amazing restaurants and energy in the community, and the proximity to the city is perfect.  The park is AWESOME!  I spend a lot of time there in the spring and summer running on the track, swimming, and just hanging on the lawn.  I'm looking forward to jumping in on some threads and making connections on these boards!  Hope everyone has a great night!
