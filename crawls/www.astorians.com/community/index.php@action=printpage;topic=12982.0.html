Title: Re: Best Liquor Store
Post by: LHT on November 11, 2008, 10:42:15 AM
If your focus is $$ then you should check out the liquor store next to Costco.  Excellent prices, but service recommendations for wines can be a bit spotty.  You don't need to be a Costco member to go here, they're not technically affiliated.  I've found that their price on hard liquor is great, especially if you're not shy about buying the 'family size' bottles.  Totally cheaper than Grand Liquor or Dominick's.

Good part about Grand Liquor is that you can call in an order in the morning & have it delivered to your door in the afternoon.  Joleson's (sp?) on 31st Street (near Ditmars train stop) also delivers and I've seen a sign at Dominick's about delivery, but haven't actually used it. 

So stock up & let us know where the party will be  :mrgreen:
