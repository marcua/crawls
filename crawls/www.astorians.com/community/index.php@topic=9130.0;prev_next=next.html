Organic Cup-a-Joe was recently started by a member of Astorians, right here in Astoria.  It's internet-based, but she has indicated that she will deliver it within Astoria.  She said she doesn't do the roasting, but the coffee is shipped to her the day it is roasted.  I'm sure you could contact her for more details.

I have friends who roast their green beans (er, coffee beans, not string beans) in a bread machine.  It is supposed to produce a larger quantity of better, more evenly roasted coffee than a popcorn popper.  Hmmm - it may also require a heat gun.  Bread Machine/Heat Gun Roasting FAQ.

I was also going to link to Sweet Maria's as Bentley's Dad has done for bean info as well.  It's a great site.  Unfortunately, I don't know of a source in Astoria for unroasted beans.
