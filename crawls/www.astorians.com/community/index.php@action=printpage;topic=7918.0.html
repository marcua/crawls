Okay, I know you were all on the edge of your seats about this one.  8-)

The old passport came back, stamps intact, in a separate mailing from the new passport.  There's a "cancelled" stamp on the front page of the old one.  The whole process only took 15 days from when I mailed the old one to when I received the new one.  I guess the backlog is not so bad now. 

The new passports are stiffer and VERY patriotic.
