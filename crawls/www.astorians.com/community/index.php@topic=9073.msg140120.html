No one's mentioned El Boqueron?  Never had so much as a bad dish there.  Everything's fresh and perfectly prepared.  Service is great.  Really, if you haven't been...34th Avenue and 31st Street.
I was just about to point out this omission myself :)

Love El Boqueron. Great tapas. One of my all-time favorite places.

The quality is always top-notch because they won't serve food if it's not up to par. One time I ordered the squid in garlic sauce (love it so much, I order it just about every time-- perfectly tender calamari with sauce that begs to have every last drop sopped up with bread), and he told me that they weren't completely happy with the shipment of calamari they received, so they weren't going to serve it. Which I totally appreciated-- I'd rather not get my favorite dish than eat a mediocre version.
