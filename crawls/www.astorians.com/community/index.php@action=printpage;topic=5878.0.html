Title: Re: Busking the rails
Post by: vena_cava on April 11, 2007, 05:51:54 PM
I've debated busking in NY but often wondered what the permit entailed. I did it in San Francisco and London. If you're busking in stations you have to go through the MTA/transportation service and if you're doing it in the street you have to go through city & county. That seems to be the basic rule no matter where you go. In London you need a permit and I noticed that a lot of NY is modeled after London so its no surprise to me that you'd have to wait forever to get an audition. I'm still waiting for my London audition after 3 months.
