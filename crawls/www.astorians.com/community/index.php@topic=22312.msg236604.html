Astoria School Symposium
« on: February 20, 2012, 05:07:20 PM »
 Astoria, Queens presents Astoria School Symposium 2012 to take place on Saturday, March 3rd 9am-12pm, and features local Daycares, Nurseries, Universal Pre-Kindergarten & Kindergarten Schools & Programs (private, public, and Charter schools)
Astoria Moms Meetup Group and Queens Holistic Moms Network are proud to bring the first ever School Symposium to Astoria. This will give Astoria families the opportunity to make the most informed choice for their child(ren)â€™s education.
This two part event consists of a Fair and Forum. The Fair will allow families to gain the needed information for school registration and have a chance to speak with school representatives. The Forum will provide families the opportunity to connect directly with other families experienced with the Astoria education system. The Symposium will also cover select topics such as special education, gifted and talented, and afterschool programs.
 The Symposium will take place on Saturday, March 3, 2012 from 9am -12pm at 21-65 41st Street Astoria, NY 11105 (Steinway Reform Church on the corner of 41st and Ditmars Blvd.).

If you wish to attend the symposium, please register through our website at www.astoriamoms.org
If you have any questions you may contact us at events@astoriamoms.org
tel: 347-94-MAMAS (347-946-2627).

I'd like to encourage all parents of young children to early school-aged in Astoria to attend this event.  Have an infant and want to get the low-down on schools in Astoria?  It's never too early!


We will have representatives from many area daycares and preschools in Astoria and the immediate area, as well as representatives from
many schools- both parochial and public (if we could not get an official from a public school, we asked a well-informed parent with children at that school to be a representative).  All area charter schools will also be represented. 



We hope that this becomes an annual event (though much earlier in the school year next time).  This is a free event- Holistic Moms of Queens and the Astoria Moms Meetup Group feel that it "takes a village" to raise kids and we are interested in gaining and sharing information about education opportunities here in Queens.


Hope to see you there!
