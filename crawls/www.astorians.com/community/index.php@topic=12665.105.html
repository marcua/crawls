PETEY'S BURGER
« Reply #106 on: March 29, 2009, 12:36:29 PM »
Petey's Burger is a great addition to the hood. Once you try this place you will not look at burgers the same.
My kids and I love this place but since it opened I feel my cholesterol rising. It is a true guilty pleasure.

I have had many a burger in my day including in-and-out which seems to be the closet comparison.
Petey's double is phenomenal but don't go there without trying the"Petey Melt"...the toasted bun gooey cheese
and grilled onions are TDF...

The Shakes are great and the hand cut home made fries are top notch. And for a fast food place the place is clean and has a great look.
I am and a huge fan and a regular, and unreal fast delivery service.

I'm not much of a meat-eater, but can appreciate fast food done well. My fiance tried Petey's last night for the first time, after reading in Time Out NY that it aspired to be In & Out Burger....you know, the way McDowell's aspires to be McDonald's :lol:
