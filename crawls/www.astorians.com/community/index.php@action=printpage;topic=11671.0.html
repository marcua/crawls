Title: Sigh...Is it just not safe to park in Astoria??
Post by: afarber on August 05, 2008, 02:56:56 PM
First someone pulled the rubber out of my windshield wipers in the middle of a blizzard.

Next a plow took off my driver's side mirror.

Followed by my passenger front window being smashed and my radio stolen.

That was nothing.

Last night at 10:30, my neighbor (thank god!) rang my doorbell.  She had recognized my car around the corner and said I needed to go check it out...there was an accident.  I closed my eyes, took a deep breath, and headed out.  My car was parked on 36th Avenue at the corner of Crescent.  By the time I got there everyone was gone except two police officers filling out paperwork.

Apparently, someone blew a red light, hit another car, which spun around and hit mine.  It looked bad.  My car is, for all intents and purposes, totaled.  Luckily, both drivers stopped (or couldn't drive away?) and both were insured.  I now have to go after someone's insurance company.  Sadly, they are likely to give me much less than it is going to cost to replace my car.

My car is undrivable.  It's still sitting there on the side of the road.  I can't pick up the accident report until Thursday.  With street cleaning tomorrow (Wednesday), I'll probably get a ticket.  I guess that's still cheaper than having it towed to a lot somewhere.  I'll let the insurance company do that.

Anyway, thanks for reading my rant.  If anyone has some grotesque interest, I'll try to post some pictures soon.

And now the search for a new used car.  Anyone selling?

Adam
