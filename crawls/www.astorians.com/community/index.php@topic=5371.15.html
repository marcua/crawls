MTA To Suspend 7 Line Construction For St. Patrick's Day
March 01, 2007

The MTA said Thursday that work on the Number 7 line will be suspended for Saint Patrick's Day weekend.

Since St. Patrick’s Day falls on a Saturday, thousands would have been forced to find another way into Manhattan for the parade due to the ongoing weekend repair work.

To make up for the lost time, construction work will now extend one extra weekend, finishing on April 2.

According to the MTA, the change in construction schedule will come at no significant cost.
