A bit of history for anyone who wasn't around last fall - several members of astorians.com, through conversations on the board, discussed their concern for victims of hurricane katrina and decided to do something to help. Musicians, cafe owners, designers and others all contributed to 3 events in Astoria that raised a sizeable sum to donate to America's Second Harvest for the purpose of helping hurricane victims.

I personally loved being involved with the project and would like to do fundraising/volunteer work with my neighbors by reincarnating our old group and welcoming new members. I think we could do a lot of good in the world and also enjoy getting to know each other and working together. Thoughts?
