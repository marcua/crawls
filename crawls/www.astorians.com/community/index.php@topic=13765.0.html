The bus is really easy.  I took it on Saturday and it was less than 30 minutes from the time I left my house in the Ditmars area until walking into the Delta Terminal.  From 125th Street just tell them to wait in front of the big Pathmark/Stop-n-Shop type food store that is on the southeast corner and it will be going in the right direction.  Bank of America is directly across 125th from the bus stop.

It's pretty easy to navigate around the airport on the bus as well provided you know your airline.  Plus, the driver usually announces the airlines as you go.

Here is a good tip though that will save a little bit of time:

If your parents are flying out of the "central terminal" they can actually get off at the first stop at LaGuardia and just walk across the street rather than going through two other terminals first.  The airlines at this terminal are American, Frontier, United, etc.

The second stop at LGA is Delta/Northwest

The third stop is the US Airways Shuttle (can also get off here for regular US Air service)

The fourth stop is US Airways (still the same terminal)

The Fifth stop is the "Central Terminal" (same as the first)

The Sixth stop is the Delta Shuttle at the Marine Air terminal.

Hope this helps!
