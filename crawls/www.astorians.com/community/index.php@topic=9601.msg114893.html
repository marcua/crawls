My name is Abby.    I have completed a 200 hour yoga teacher training program and I am certified to teach students of all levels.   My program was quite extensive.  We studied what is refered to as both the “subtle” and the “physical” body and thus gained an understanding of yoga as both a mental, spiritual, and highly physical practice.  My teacher training program, therefore also focused on anatomy and taught us how to modify each and every posture for those with certain injuries or lack of flexibility

Last year, I taught yoga at my law school.  This was a great opportunity to see yoga’s benefits at work.  Students were pressed for time, stressed, and needed a quick way to get fit.  Our one-hour yoga classed focused on flexibility (sitting and studying is very hard on your body) and stress reduction.

I am interested in teaching a class that offers a great mind-body workout.  I am able to teach all levels and enjoy working with both beginners and more advanced practitioners.
I am available weeknights and weekends.
