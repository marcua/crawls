Alternate Side Parking Rules are Suspended on Monday, February 26.

The New York City Department of Transportation (DOT) announced that Alternate Side Parking Rules are suspended on Monday, February 26 to facilitate snow removal. However, parking meters will remain in effect throughout the City.

The 2007 Alternate Side Parking Regulations Suspension Calendar is available on
the DOT Web site, along with other alternate side parking information, at
http://www.nyc.gov/dot/asprules (http://www.nyc.gov/dot/asprules)
