Just a note that Transportation Alternatives has a new version of their pedestrian/bicycle-vehicle accident data tool available: CrashStat.

If you believe the numbers, the number of pedestrian-vehicle accidents has fallen rather sharply in recent years. (They have data through 2005.)

Here in Astoria, there hasn't been a pedestrian accident at Crescent and Broadway since 2001, apparently. For the 10 years they cover, 30th Ave., Steinway, and Broadway seem to be the most dangerous areas, unsurprisingly. But in the last few years, there have been so few accidents reported that you can barely see any patterns. Interesting.
