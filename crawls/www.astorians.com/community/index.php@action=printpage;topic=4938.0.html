Title: March on Washington
Post by: Frappe on January 28, 2007, 01:38:07 PM
Marched yesterday.  Alexa should be posting some of the pictures of her, grotto and I there.  It was a well done event.  I think a hundred grand was topped.  After we marched around the capitol dome and swung back, we started heading down the hill on Indepedence Avenue (I think).  We saw throngs of people ahead of us and throngs still not even past the starting point!  Excellent!

Three tips to anyone who marches in DC in the future:
1.  If you are going to use the WMATA train for any reason, purchase your tickets ($2.35 each way) ahead of time online.  The wait to buy tickets at the Greenbelt station was a half an hour. 
2.  Afterwards, eat the "hangover burger" at the Austin Grill on Avenue E and 7th.  Greasy burger topped with jalapenos, cheese, bacon, chili and a fried egg.  I think you get to eat five of those in your life before you die!
3.  For those such inclined, I understand that smoking bashing is really the only PC sport out there where some frustrations can be worked off, but for crying out loud, keep them to yourselves at a peace march!  Getting narc'd on by a fellow marcher is just plain sad (happened to our group twice.  It was outside and it wasn't even 420)
