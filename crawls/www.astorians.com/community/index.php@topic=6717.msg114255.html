4%?

Yes.  The NYC sales tax is a combination of a 4% tax levied by NYC, a 4% tax levied by NY State, and a .375% Metropolitan Commuter Transportation District tax.  I think salon services -- hair cuts, facials, massages, that sort of thing -- are not obligated to charge the state or transportation tax.  So the tax rate on those services is 4%.
