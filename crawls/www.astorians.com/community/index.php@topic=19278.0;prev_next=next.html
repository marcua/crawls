Christine:  I know you hate realtors, but you don't know me so sarcasm isn't necessary. 

Anyway, The Acropolis is a tough sell.  When I took on a few listings I received so much interest until people did their research and called to cancel.  It was odd, but true.  Apparently there are many rumors about the Acropolis that are not true, but some are.  What I heard from buyers was really weird:  bedbugs, garbage not removed for days, roach problems, loud music playing until all hours of the morning, and the list goes on.  Negative feedback from buyers:  The units are very small, hard to fit in a comfy bed, stairs are narrow, some of the gates are not secured, etc.  Positive feedback from buyers:  No board approval required, 10% downpayment only, pets allowed, immediate subletting allowed, some buildings allow access to the roof, close to all.

Best of luck,
