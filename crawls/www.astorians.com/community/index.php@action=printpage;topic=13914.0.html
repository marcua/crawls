Cross Pollination is a monthly event for sketch artists to draw dancers as models-in-motion and for dancers to improvise with one another, while being inspired to move by the art in the making! This is a unique opportunity to explore the combination of visual, motion-based and audio arts. As host, Green Space hopes to provide a relaxed environment for spontaneous, free expression and improvisation between the different forms while encouraging synergy amongst artists. These sessions are open to artists, dancers, musicians and viewers alike.
Please, come and join us!

Saturdays every month:
Feb 7 Mar 7 Apr 4 May 9 Jun 13 Jul 11 Aug 8

Admission: $5

Time: February 7, 2009 from 6pm to 9pm
Location: Green Space
Street: 37-24 24th St #301
City/Town: Long Island City
Website or Map: http://greenspacestudio.org

(Artists- Please, bring simple materials : easy to carry, non-messy, acoustic, imaginative, etc.)
