Title: Reading @ Waltz-Astoria Tomorrow Night (May 29) @ 8PM
Post by: whiskeydregs on May 28, 2008, 08:55:17 AM
I'm happy to report that A Night With The Whiskey Dregs is tomorrow. Please be sure to sign up for our limited open mic during our pre-party at 8PM. Beer and wine for all who want.
 
THURSDAY MAY 29
A Night With The Whiskey Dregs
@
Waltz-Astoria
23-14 Ditmars Boulevard (23rd Street and Ditmars Boulevard)
N or W train to Ditmars Boulevard
Astoria, Queens

Pre-party at 8PM. Readings begin at 9PM

This event features several writers from The Whiskey Dregs - a collective of writers whose works fall on the grittier and more open side of literature. The Whiskey Dregs have just released a journal and will be doing more events througout the community.
 
There will also be a limited open mic so if you're a writer, bring something to read and join in the festivities. Make sure to sign up at the pre-party.

Come, have a drink with us at 8PM, listen to some gutsy short stories and poetry, have some fun, have a drink again and get information on how you can participate in Astoria's newest creative movement.

Some of the writers who will perform a reading are:

Mundo Rivera
Karly Maurer
Ellen Donbeck
YH Etheart
Carlos Detres
