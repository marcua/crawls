I walked over last night to check it out.  It looks like it's coming along and can be really great.  There was a decent chunk of empty shelf space; which I'm assuming is for more stock not in yet.  The owner came up to us when we came in; he was very nice.  He talked about Whole Foods a lot, so people who have compared it are right.  He said that a lot of people were surprised at the produce cost, so he was thinking about getting a blend of  conventional/organic stock.  It was a smaller store, so a little less variety, but enough that I was really happy.  My partner did notice that there weren't many vegetarian products (no tofu, not a great variety of veggie "meats"), so I hope that they will get more.  Also, they didn't have a lot of "fat free" options. 


All said and done though, I like independent grocery stores and living off of the Trade Fair on Ditmars now where the roof leeks, there are bugs, and I have witnessed the staff do some pretty gross things; I'm pretty happy.  I'll be moving to 21st and Astoria Blvd. at the end of the month and my only MAJOR issue was the grocery store lack, and this fills the gap nicely.
