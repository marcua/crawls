Title: Re: 23rd Ave and Steinway?
Post by: sarah23 on October 15, 2008, 07:27:37 PM
I'm on 37th st and 23rd ave, right next to the train. Literally my bedroom window is 20 feet from the trestle, and the train never bothers me. It sounds like a Mack truck going by for 10 seconds a couple times a day, no big deal at all. I think Steinway/23rd Ave is fine, certainly going south a couple blocks from Ditmars it's a little "grittier" for lack of a better word, with some empty storefronts and not-as-nicely manicured homes, but I never feel unsafe. My vet is down there. I think it's a great area. Plus if you have kids there's a playground right there.
