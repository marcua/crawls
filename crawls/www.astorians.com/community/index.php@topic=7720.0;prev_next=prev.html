I suggest you check out Zipcar.com.  I use their cars all the time.  The way it works is you can either rent by the hour, for $8-14. or by the day $69 and up for a maximum 125 free miles per day. The beauty is that THEY PAY FOR THE GAS.  Each car has a company gas card in it and all you do is make sure the car has at least 1/4 tank when you drop it off.  Zipcar has cars parked in garages and lots all over the city.  You go online, pick out the car you want.  Show up at the designated time, activate the rental by holding your issued card up to the EZ-pass-looking box on the windshield, the locks pop open, and off you go.  Super simple.  It's great for errands, and they have fun cars like Mini Coopers and Scions in addition to more practical ones for shopping.  In Astoria, they park them in a lot at Vernon Ave. at, I think, 30th Ave, and also over in L.I.C. and Sunnyside. It's not for everyone, but they do fill a niche for we drivers without cars in the city.
