I began with craigslist, found it to be very disappointing and decided to just walk around Astoria looking for signs..  While walking down Steinway Street, I stopped to write down a phone number on an "apartments for rent" sign and a very nice man who was working in the building inside came out and told me that he was pretty sure the one I was writing down was gone, but that he knew apartment owners.  He was a plumber, and had lived in the area for his whole life and had many friends in the area.  I rented my current apartment from a friend of his that very day, an apartment that had never been listed.  Because it was a private owner, I didn't have to pay any kind of fee.

So, my suggestion is--speak to plumbers, people doing construction on buildings, people who do any kind of work that would allow them to work with building owners.  It's the last thing I ever would have thought to do, but it worked for me.
