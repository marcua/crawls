You're only saying that because there are so many horrible Chinese restaurants! Real Chinese cooking is not notably greasy (especially compared to, say, Southern American food!), and has a wide variety of excellent flavors. Take-out Chinese is not authentic, it's an invention created specifically for American tastes, using a few sauces and techniques from only a couple of regions of China.

Helen Fong on real Chinese food (http://www.personal.psu.edu/users/h/x/hxf140/assign7.html)

This said, I love Thai food too, so I'm not complaining about a new Thai place, only that there aren't good Chinese places in the neighborhood!
