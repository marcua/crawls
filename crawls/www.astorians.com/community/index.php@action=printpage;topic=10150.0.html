Post by: paratactical on April 18, 2008, 09:36:36 AM
I believe in signs because I believe that the human mind is trained to recognize its own needs. You get cravings for strawberries when you need vitamin C or feel the need to be outside in the sun if you body needs vitamin D production. You know when you're too tired, cold or wet. I believe that we see signs because our subconscious knows what it is that we need and it tries to communicate this to us based upon what we notice in the world around us. There are hundreds of things around you every moment that you don't notice. "Signs" that you don't pick up on. But the ones that your brain does take note of and process are the ones that I believe are important. I think the recognition and realization of signs is like that of dreams.

Just my $.02.

Also, on the tattoo part: I carried the designs for both of my tattoos around in my wallet for 30 days before I got them done. The guy who did them both for me was a close friend and it was on his recommendation that I did it. He said if you still loved it and didn't question the design or want to change something after a month, there's no way you wouldn't just grow to love it more and more once you got it done.
