People can be freaks - a rant on waiting in line behavior
« on: March 30, 2008, 10:28:16 PM »
What is it with some folks when a number of folks are waiting in line?  Moving closer to the person before you does not make them move faster, it just means you are violating their personal space.  Sighing and exhaling your breathe into their hair, especially if they are shorter than you doesn't make the line move faster, it just makes folks uncomfortable around you and makes folks think you're acting like a jerk.  I just had to pick up two diet cokes and never have I wanted to get away from a person so badly - it wasn't even that he smelled or had any disgusting personal habits, just pushed into my personal space and kept blowing in my hair - what the heck?!  Is that supposed to mean something?  I think some people are just jerks.  The man behind the counter, not paying any attention, decided to bag our items together, just what I want, to get to spend more time with someone who has made me incredibly uncomfortable.  All this behavior not only didn't make the line move faster, it added a few minutes to our wait as the counter guy had to undo his lack of attention. 

Seriously, does blowing in someone's hair mean something?  I know I'm getting older - maybe it's some next generation thing?
