here's what I do when I'm really jonesin and can't get to ishkabibbles or Jim's on South st. 
  Go to your favorite butcher shop.  I like the butcher block in woodsite on 41st and qns blvd.
  Buy a rib steak, ribeye, whatever.  Go home and cut it into extremely thin strips.  saute in butter or oil.
  Toss in some sauted onions and fold it all up with some white american or whatever, then put them on a   toasted roll OF YOUR CHOOSING!  Then get after it! 
  Honestly the best cheesesteak I've ever had, I made at home.
