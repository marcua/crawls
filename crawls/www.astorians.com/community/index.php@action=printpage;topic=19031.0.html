I was excited to see this place open.  However, I was disappointed when I went there this past Friday afternoon.  It was around 2PM and they were out of ground beef.  I know that new places usually have service problems...but running out of ground beef when you're a burger joint?  I commented, why not just go to Costco to get some ground beef?  This apparently is not an option.  I like supporting local restaurants, so I ordered a hotdog.  Nothing spectacular...for a $3+ hotdog.  What's odd also is that they grill the hotdog as they are ordered...while I was eating, I noticed another customer ordering a hotdog...it took almost 10 minutes for this hotdog to get done...I like that they have root beer on tap...that's a nice touch.  They also serve sweet potato fries.  I had them...they were OK...kinda soggy.  The regular french fries were also OK.  IMHO, this place will probably survive just because of Citibank and other businesses in the area.  They also what way too many people working behind the counter.  I really want to try their burger to compare it with the ones serve up by Five Guys or Jackson Hole.
