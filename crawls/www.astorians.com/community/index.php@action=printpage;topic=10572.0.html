Title: Not coming to Astoria anytime soon
Post by: 28Grand on May 23, 2008, 12:45:36 PM
Several weeks ago, my favorite spot for fruit and vegetables – Sunshine, on the corner of Ditmars and 38th Street -- closed. Confronted by large rent increases and spiraling electricity bills, the family who owned Sunshine for a few generations decided to pack it in.

Sunshine was, in my opinion, the best produce spot in the neighborhood, a Ditmars institution, a member of the community, and will be sorely missed, at least by me.

There have been several threads on this website moaning about the lack of this or that in Astoria, which baffles me especially in regard to food and food-related businesses. I admit the supermarkets are not tops (honestly, where in this city are there any good local supermarkets?) but I want to ask: is there any other neighborhood as blessed as Astoria when it comes to food choices?

Here’s my moan: I wish we as a community would recognize the many terrific, high-quality food stores, bakeries, and shops and give them your business more often, instead of wishing for the arrival of corporate retail giants that will put them out of business.

I know most of you reading this do support local shops but everytime I read yet another thread on this site about the desire for Fairway, Trader Joes, or Whole Foods and the like (and I have nothing against them whatsoever), I feel sad.
