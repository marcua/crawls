Come to the Freeze Peach each Tuesday at 1:00PM in the afternoon for the Astorian Sandwich Society group "meeting."

Membership qualifications: You must really like sandwiches.

The group will either order, or send someone out to purchase the sandwiches. Then everyone will return to the cafe to admire and consume their sandwiches.
