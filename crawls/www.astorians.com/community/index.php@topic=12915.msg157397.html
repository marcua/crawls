Hockey @ LIC Ice Pavilion
« on: November 05, 2008, 04:36:03 PM »
This is not an ad.  In no way am I affiliated with the rink.  I'm just spreading the news.

Just got an e-mail from the director of Hockey @ the Ice Pavilion (I've known him for a while...really nice guy).  Innagural Adult hockey tournament is going to start next week (the little leagues will probably start sometime later).  If you want in on a team, there will be two "evaluation" (i.e. - tryout) sessions.  The first one is this Thursday - Nov 6th - at 8:30.  The second is next Tuesday - Nov 11 - at 7 p.m.

As you can imagine, you have to supply your own gear.  I don't know if they have a pro shop on site, so if you do go, I suggest you get your skates sharpened prior to.  Insofar as I can tell, all levels are invited.  For those of you who don't know, they basically make you do drills and assess which level you're best suited to play.  No one wants to see someone get hurt if they're playing over thier head.

I will most likely be there on Thursday between the pipes and protecting the twine...depending on how the knee holds up.

I'll laugh if I show up and anyone tries to go high glove side on me.  Unless you have a cannon, good luck with that.
