As a mother who's lived in Astoria for 4 yrs. I felt it my duty to let you know the scoop of what's going on for kids in Astoria. Not to mention I've got family in Houston so your post caught my eye. I would recommend you be near Astoria park. There's is lots going on there for kids; pool, tennis, soccer, track and a great playground. This means you'd want to be on the N/W train, near Ditmars Blvd. station or Astoria Blvd. station. This means you want to be north of 30th Ave & west of 36th Street. There are several parents groups in Astoria. They are easy enough to find. Good luck and welcome!
