Where I'm coming from on this as 1. a huge Oprah fan - I'll admit it. and 2. someone who hasn't seen the shows she's done on the Secret but has read a bit about it on the web (including that salon article).

I like the idea behind this - and I get why it's something that Oprah would be all over. What I'm worried about is that it's making people spend money on a DVD or book or whatever it is - with the money making someone incredibly rich (and who is this person anyway)?

I feel like if you're going to buy into the idea that positive thoughts will bring you positive things in life, you can start doing that right now, without a DVD. I just hope she's not actually pushing people to go buy this thing.

And so long as you're not depending on these positive thoughts being the key to you getting whatever it is you want (in other words, recognizing that a lot more is involved like hard work, and by my experience, good timing). Because if you totally depend on these positive thoughts being the key, and then find you're not getting anywhere, or if something bad happens to you, it takes away your entire base of faith in what's guiding your life. Which can be, er, really upsetting.
