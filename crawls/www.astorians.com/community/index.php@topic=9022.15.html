I had two luna de juarez experiences.

First time.  Ordered two margaritas.  Got them about 20 minutes later without alcohol (it's easy to tell when you order a rocks margarita....100% mixer).  Sent them back.  Then they took our orders.  10 minutes later, new drinks (yes, that's 30 minutes for drinks).  No alcohol.  Food arrived about 20 minutes later: cold, completely unspiced, tasted like boiled chicken and beef.  An utterly miserable experience...and they weren't particularly cheap either, especially considering you can fill up at the utterly amazing Taco Cart on 30th for 10$ a person.

Second time.  Same experience with the drinks; perhaps we were unlucky, but LdJ seemed to serve the weakest drinks in Queens.  Decided to order off the 'special' menu rather than get tacos and fajitas.  Similar problem: the food was over-salted and completely lacked any other flavor.

IMHO, at their price point, they had no reason for existing.  I was really looking forward to their opening, and the food, service, and all-around experience was probably my single biggest restaurant disappointment in 5 years of living in Astoria.
