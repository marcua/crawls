I bought mine from the Mediterranean Foods II shop next to Artopolis. Anyway the way I make it, and the way i made it when I was living in Israel is like this:fill up the ibrik about 2/3 with water, bring to a boil on a medium to low flame add as much coffee as you feel necessary. Usually I add 2 teaspoons since I usually drink 2 cups. As soon as it gets to the boiling point and is about to foam over take it off the flame and stirring it. Put it back on the flame and let it come to a boil again and repeating the removing it from the flame. At this point add the sugar, again this is all taste based and I like mine very sweet, like 3 spoons for each cup. Put it back on the flame and let it bubble for about 1 minute. Take it off the flame, pour into cups, drink. Enjoy. And this is the usual recommended way of doing it. Boiling it 3 times. Some do it only once, some do it twice.
