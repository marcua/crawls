Title: Firelight Jewelry's Final Summer Lovin Market of the Summer this Sunday !
Post by: firelight79 on July 29, 2009, 03:56:48 PM
Just wanted to let you all know that this Sunday will be, as the topic says, my final appearance at the Summer Lovin Vendor's Market at the Astoria walkway on 31st street just off Ditmars. I'll be set up between 12 and 1 pm and will be sticking around until 6:00. The walkway is really starting to pick up speed but can always use some more traffic. There's been a lot of great local vendors selling there and we can all really use the support of the local community to keep up the momentum. Hope you can find your way to the walkway while out and about. Though I won't be there on Saturday the market will be underway with more great vendors.
