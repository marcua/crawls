New to the board but native Astoria resident. Was catsitting/home sick last week for my folks - we live in a nice quiet block in the Astoria Blvd/Ditmars area. Washing dishes when I noticed a guy in a hoodie jumping over our neighbor's fence into ours, and lurking in the basement apartment stairs in our backyard. He saw me looking and ran off into the other adjacent neighbor's yard. Spooked, I went to the front door to look and check the house, a few minutes later he appeared.


Avg build, hoodie, couldn't see much. We just got an alarm system installed and the locksmith is coming over to deadbolt everything as a result of this. I hope that everyone in the neighborhood keeps their eyes peeled and takes extra precautions!


I'm extremely saddened and scared by this turn of events. I love my neighborhood, have mixed feelings about the newcomers/gentrification, but I also know that there's been an increase in crime and that's extremely disturbing.  About two years ago I was taking a walk through a nearby block after having a run in the Astoria park track - some pervert came up behind me and grabbed my behind/crotch area - hard, before running away. It was more of a deep grope. I honestly felt raped. Reported it to the police and they did nothing.
