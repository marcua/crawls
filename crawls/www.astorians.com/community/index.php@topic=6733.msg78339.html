This Thursday we will get a blast from the past with this character filled corner on Ditmars and 32 street.
Oleput, Tupelo spelled backward is the brainchild of Helen her brother Emmanuel, Evan and perhaps some other creative people.
It will be a Soda fountain and old fashioned candy store type place with a juice bar and cheeses.
I welcome them warmly to our Boulevard. Best of Luck. Geo :mrgreen:

I stopped in on my way home and turned out to be their very first customer!  It's really cute inside, and in additon to coffee, ice cream, NY State jams and jellies, pies and other baked goods, they've got old-fashioned candy and a small selection of "general store"-type goods.

I tasted the chocolate ice cream and it is very good.  The pies are indeed Briermere's, and I am very excited.  Briermere Farms is in Riverhead, on the North Road (Sound Avenue), heading out to Orient.  Their pies are really excellent.

I'm very excited they are open!
