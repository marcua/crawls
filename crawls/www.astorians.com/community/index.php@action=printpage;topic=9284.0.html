Title: Dodgeball and Touch Football - Registration Opens Monday, January 28!!!
Post by: ZogSports on January 23, 2008, 03:10:38 PM
Hey Astorians,

This coming MONDAY, January 28, registration opens for the Spring season of Dodgeball in Astoria!

Our current teams have been having a great time, playing at PS 235 Academy for New Americans (30-14 30th St) on Tuesday evenings and then celebrating at Broadway Station. 

Want to join in the fun? Sign up on Monday as an individual or a small group and we'll place you on a team.  Or you can sign up a whole team!

Dodgeball not your scene?  No problem!  We're also running a Touch Football league in Long Island City.

Get all the info at www.zogsports.org.  Questions?  Email info@zogsports.org or message us on Astorians.

About ZogSports:
ZogSports is a charity-focused, co-ed, social sports club that promotes charity and social action amongst young professionals in New York (Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens).

ZogSports organizes sports leagues (touch football, outdoor and indoor soccer, volleyball, kickball, softball, dodgeball and basketball), trips, social events and volunteer opportunities for people in their 20s and 30s.
