Astoria Restraunt Wasting Water
« on: April 22, 2007, 03:59:57 PM »
My husband and I were  walking down Steinway Street between B'way and 34th Avenue and we saw something that really annoyed us.  I can't remember the name of the restaurant.  I'm talking about the place that burnt down and reopened (the one with all the weird manikins).  Anyway, there was a manikin on the street outside the place and a shower head over it.  Basically, it was a weird display of this manikin taking a shower (fully clothed).  The water wasn't going into a drain and being resused.  The street was getting drenched.  What a waste of water any day, but they're doing it on Earth Day too.  I think that's ironic.  I am disgusted.  I walked by an hour later the water was still going.  The manikin was getting water logged.  It's just so odd.  I said something to an employee I saw walk out about wasting water and earth day (even though I don't think they should do something so wasteful any day).  She just ignored me.  I just think the whole thing is really odd.  It really annoyed us.  I have never ate there, and now I never will.
