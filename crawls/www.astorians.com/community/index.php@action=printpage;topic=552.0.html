Title: Amateur bike mechanics out there?
Post by: Christine on July 29, 2005, 03:17:59 PM
If you could possibly make it to Ronkonkoma (easy by way of LIRR), get over to the Freewheel Collective!

It's a place that offers free bike repair, workshops, and access to tools so that you can do-it-yourself. They exist for the purpose of providing free bikes and repairs to low-income members of the community, but welcome people who want to practice their wrenching skills as well.

Do a Google search on them- I don't have their exact address/web info handy at the moment.
