Title: Circus event at Astoriafamilies.org
Post by: mamidesamuel on May 11, 2009, 08:03:41 PM
The Circus is Coming to Town!
The Big Apple Circus will be at Cunningham Park in Queens

The Big Apple circus features the world's finest circus artists under the Big Apple Big Top performing in the theatrical focus of one ring with no seat more than 50 feet away. There is a total of 16 rows in the tent so every seat is a good seat.
Seating for these tickets are general admission.
The cost is $15 a person for members of Astoriafamilies.org

Children under 3 are free when they sit on an adult's lap. One child per lap.

The performance is 2 hours and 15 minutes long, including a 15-minute intermission.
Doors open 1 hour before the performance.

visit astoriafamilies.org for more details.
