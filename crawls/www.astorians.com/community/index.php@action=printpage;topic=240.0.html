Title: Bike commuting to Manhattan
Post by: jodiemim on January 26, 2005, 10:22:40 AM
i used to ride into the city pretty often, but not during rush hour. the easiest way is to head straight down crescent street, all the way to the base of the queensboro bridge. once you hit it, you'll see pretty much straight ahead the bike entrance. the bike route over the bridge has a beautiful view, but i'm not sure how much fun it would be after dark or in the freezing cold. any questions, feel free to ask. good luck!
