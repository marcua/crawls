Ladies and Gentlemen, the Mega Million jackpot is $340 million! 

I don't normally play any of the lottery games, or gamble, for that matter (I am the worst person to take to Atlantic City  :-) ) However, I sometimes buy a dollar or two worth of tickets if the jackpot gets over a $100 million!  For the next drawing, I think I will break my piggy bank and buy $5, yes $5 worth of tickets! 

Anyway, I was wondering what you people would spend that sort of loot on!  Mainly, what would your FIRST purchase be? 

To begin with, I would probably purchase a nice house in Westchester County (the groans and boos are so loud, I can almost hear them in my office).  I fell in love with the area when I was going to college at SUNY Purchase, and I would like to return there at some point, when I want to "settle down."  And of course, I would keep an apartment in Astoria  :-)
