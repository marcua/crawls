Hi Friends,
 
As you know Asia got hit by the biggest tsunami we have ever seen.   Thousands of people have been taken by it.  It is the biggest natural disaster that we have ever experience and the numbers of lives taken keeps on rising two fold everyday since it hit. More people could die from diseases and hunger.  

This is a disaster that we can't ignore or depend on other people to give.  This is a world wide disaster that is going to effect us all.  
 
I've provided a great link (see below) for the Relief Organizations that are or will be working in the disaster areas.  Please check out the page and do your own research on the organization and please please give to the organization of your choice.  Any help is welcome but in this case money is the best and most effective donation you can make.  Because it's more direct and can be put to work faster.  Unlike food and clothing which have to be ship out to the disaster area.  That can take weeks even months plus money to ship supplies to the disaster area.   The American Red Cross lets you make a donation as little as $5 and you can use your credit for it.  It's easy by phone or online.  
 
Thanks for taking the time to read this.
 
Happy safe and healthy New Year  to you all.
 
Fidel Ramirez
 
http://www.usaid.gov/locations/asia_near_east/tsunami/ngolist.html
