Iraq for Sale: The War Profiteers
a film by Robert Greenwald

This film takes you inside the lives of soldiers, truck drivers, widows and children who have been changed forever as a result of profiteering in the reconstruction of Iraq.  Iraq for Sale uncovers the connections between private corporations making a killing in Iraq and the decision makers who allow them to do so. Mimi LaValley from the War Resisters League will facilitate a discussion after the film.

Wednesday, November 29th at 7:30pm
