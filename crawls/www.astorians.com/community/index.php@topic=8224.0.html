Quote
I'm not so much pro applebees than I am against the people on this board that appear to view it as the 8th sign of the Apocalypse.

It's not the 8th sign of the apocalypse, (maybe the 12 or 13th, :-D) but let me offer my perspective. I relocated from New York to Knoxville, TN 4 years ago. Down here, in a newspaper sponsored poll this year, Pizza Hut was voted Knoxville's Best Pizza! PIZZA HUT for Gods sake!  Could Pizza Hut ever be voted Astoria's best pizzeria? I don't think so. The Pizza Huts in Knoxville have little competition, so there's no one else to vote for. And that's what chains like Pizza Hut do, they put smaller competitors out of business, until they're the only game in town, and you are left with a marginal product rather than exceptional.

Sal, Kris and Charlies Italian Deli used to have great hero's I remember, and I guess they still do from what I have read here on this forum. What would happen if Subway, Blimpie's, or Quiznos decide to open up next door to them? You'd eventually have no choice for a hero other than eating at Knoxville's top-voted sandwich place, Subway! should they knock Sal, Kris and Charlies out of business.

In conclusion, I am living in a food wasteland here, and don't want Astoria to turn into the same thing because of chains.
