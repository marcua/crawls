July 26 Astoria Music Now! Music Festival in Astoria Park
« on: July 21, 2008, 12:14:59 PM »
We're talking all day and night. Read on:

The Astoria Music Now! Festival in Astoria Park, Saturday July 26 from noon to 9PM. There will be many RocknRoll bands on 2 stages, drum circles, hula hoopers, comedy, live paintings...all sorts of goodness going on, including readings from yours truly in the traditional Rock n Roll sense of artists of days past.

Our new art director, Chris Hingel will be doing live paintings...he does beautiful work and we're happy to have him with us. Contributor and poet for the Whiskey Dregs, Mundo Rivera will also be performing some of his work. He is a very talented poet from Astoria who also does an excellent performance - not to be missed. As always, go to www.whiskeydregs.com for more info about The Whiskey Dregs. I'm MC'ing this event so please don't forget to say hi to me...my name is Carlos.

We also have an after-party that will be announced during the event. More fun and silliness...so let me just give you the info and we'll call it a day:

ASTORIA MUSIC NOW! The First Annual FREE Music & Arts

Festival of its Kind. For one day, on July 26th, people will be treated to some of the finest local music our area has to offer. This event will highlight and benefit the arts in one of the most culturally diverse communities in NYC. Come enjoy nine hours of local music and arts absolutely FREE!

WHERE: Astoria Park - The Great Lawn - Ditmars & 19th St. Astoria, NY 11106

WHEN: Saturday, 7.26.08 - 12-9pm

MAIN STAGE: Bliminal Little Creatures The Tarantinos Gutter & Spine Kite Operations The Swedes Dirty Wings Bayard Russell and Friends We Are Darien! Drum Dharma Drums Pop Mafia Hula Hoops, Magic, Comedy Tent, Face Paint, and Much More!!!

SECOND ACOUSTIC STAGE (JUST ADDED!) El Jezel Jesse Pagano Janine Otten Joe Young Sean Wiggins Gustavo Rodriguez
