Title: Open Hockey @ World Ice Arena
Post by: NYCGoalie on June 11, 2009, 03:44:06 PM
Even though this isn't a "Astoria/L.I.C." facility, I guess it's still close enough to us to be considered a "local" facility.  I have nothing to do with this facility.

So the City Ice Pavilion is now officially done till September.  For those who may want to still play a little hockey during the summer, there's going to be ice at the World Ice Arena in Flushing.

There will be three open hockey sessions during the week:
Friday nights @ 10 p.m.
Saturday nights @ 10 p.m.
Sunday mornings @ 9 a.m.

There haven't been a lot of people going to the open hockey sessions; so I figured I'd pass along the info to people who may be interest, or who may know other people who may be interested.

I went last Sunday with a small group of friends and I must say, it's a really nice facility.  The ice is good, very few bad spots and the humidity was kept well in check (which, I was surprised, considering it's right next to a huge pool).  There's free parking across the street and there's a bus that stops a block away on College Blvd.

I'm probably going to be consistent with the Sunday mornings.  I may do Friday nights as well if I can't make it on Sundays.

They'll also be having a 4-on-4 tournament this summer. 

Hope to see some of you out there!
