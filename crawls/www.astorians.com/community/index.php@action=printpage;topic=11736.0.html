Title: Dissapointed with Watawa - a rant
Post by: t3knoid on August 09, 2008, 06:52:45 AM
I cannot deny Watawa's good sushi.  It's usually where my family and I go to eat sushi locally.  However, after last night's debacle I will no longer spend my money here.  Here's my rant....

We decided to go to Watawa last night for dinner.  We got to the door around 8:20.  There were three women managing the front and we were told that it would be a 20 minute wait....we waited......we had a large party..five...large for this restaurant...waiting for 20 minutes is not bad.  It was a nice night out and it looked like Ditmars was having a good night.  Of course, Taverna Kyclades had their usual line, and tonight it was extraordinarily long.  Like many who waited for a table we speculated on which tables might free up for us to sit down.  They had tables outside so that they can accommodate more patrons than usual.  After 25 minutes, tables are finally clearing.  While we waited, more people came to the restaurant.  Mostly parties of 2 and a party of three.  There were two adjacent tables for 2 that were clearing so I go inside and ask if we can have those tables and just bring out another chair.  They emphatically said OK...after another 5 minutes, one table has been cleaned and the other one was paying their check.  I was psyched that we were going to get some good sushi in a few minutes.  To my disappointment, they sat two people who came after us on the table that was just cleaned. 

I am a very patient person and will give people the benefit of the doubt...so I assumed that a table must've opened up inside to accomodate us.   I went inside to inquire why the table outside was given to someone else instead of our party after I spoke with them.  Their excuse was that they did not have an extra chair.  This excuse should have clued me in to what happened with the rest of the evening...but like I said I am very patient...We waited some more and finally one of the larger tables inside enough to accomodate us was getting cleaned up.  At this point, every party who came in after us has been seated except for the party of three.  The next move by the restaurant baffled me...they sat the table for three (btw, I saw the look of one of the person in that party, they looked surprise that they were seated first)!  At this point, I am waiting for someone from the restaurant to come out and give us some reassurance that they will be sitting us soon....5 minutes come...nada!  It's now 9PM...now I am thinking of all the times that I've eaten here and give 25%+ tips every time...I go back into the restaurant and asked politely why the party of three was seated before we were...the manager's excuse...they did not have an extra chair and that they are just waiting for another table in the back to free up....I patiently walk outside...but after 5 minutes I thought about it some more and told my family we are leaving.  Before I leave, I go back in and told the manager about how disappointmented I was of their mistreatment of the situation and that not having an extra chair is inexcusable when they have a bar where they can get the extra chair from, but instead sat a party of one before us...that they sat a party of 3 before us and others who came in 15 minutes after we did.  I also told them that I will no longer partronize the restaurant.  With that said, all I got was a blank stare.  Ugh!  You don't tell someone 20 minutes and make them wait 40 minutes and not once make an effort to explain what is going on.

We decided to go to Wave Thai instead.  We lucked out and it wasn't busy.  I was a little apprehensive that Wave Thai may have "gone downhill' from some of the posts here.  I am happy to report that it has not.  Peter (the owner) was there and greeted us.  We got our food quickly and it was great!

I don't know if my situation is unique or not.  The restaurant is good for small parties (2) and has undeniably has some of the best sushi in this area but it does not excuse our mistreatment.  Service for me is a huge factor in a good restaurant.
