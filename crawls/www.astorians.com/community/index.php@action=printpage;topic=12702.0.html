Title: Re: Good coffee and ambience?
Post by: Pulled pork on October 19, 2008, 06:21:45 PM
Welcome to your new home. The best advise is to find a Greek/Euro cafe where you can sit for hours and just have 1 cup of coffee.  But most of these places are around Broadway and 30th ave.  Believe it or not but Panera breads on 35th ave and 38th street has comfy seating, decent coffee and foods, free wifi and plenty of room. I've seen kids studying, people on laptops working and so on.  Another great place is cafe Bar on 34th ave and 36th street.  More Bohemian and a local establishment (non franchise). Not sure about free wifi or hanging out policy but during summer months people stay at outside seating all day and no one complains. Should be that same inside.

Good luck and enjoy the neighborhood, it's wonderful here.
