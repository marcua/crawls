Mail service in Astoria is poor - period.

For years, I had battles w/my local post office on 21st Street re: magazines
that were left in my lobby that were being stolen. Some are too large to fit in the
box and rather than leave the required call slip for me to pick them up at the P.O.,
they would just be left for anyone to take.

I wrote, called, visited them - to no avail. I finally got the name of the postmaster general
from a friend and started invoicing him every time one disappeared. Finally it stopped.

But we still have problems. Recently we've had entire days where my building gets no
mail delivery. When I inquire, I'm told "you must be mistaken." No, when you can see
that all the boxes are empty, it's a little suspicious.

Is there a way for Netflix to track your orders? Can you find out when they send out
something to you to track how much time passes until its arrival? That's what I did w/the
magazines - put tracers on them.

Good luck.
