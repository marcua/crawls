attempted car theft in College Point
« on: July 11, 2008, 10:06:27 AM »
As we got into the truck yesterday around 8pm after shopping @ the College Point Home Depot, the BF was about to start up and go....until he noticed the car in the spot in front/to the left of us.

Sporty red car (Pontiac?) with a guy in the driver's seat, bent over, working on something. "That guy's trying to jack that car!" John said with concern. "What? How do you know?" I asked, oblivious. "He's trying to break open the steering column."

The sun wasn't even down yet, and the parking lot was pretty busy. I dialed 911. Another guy came strolling along, and got into the blue car next to the red one. The guy jacking the red car saw BF staring at him, got out and jumped into the boxy compact light blue car, and the two guys took off.....not before BF got the licence plate # (DT4-44N)

The 911 operator said that nothing could be done, b/c the perps had left, and it wasn't even our car. I went into the store to report the incident, and the guy couldn't even get a hold of any security guards.

Meanwhile BF called to say the owner of the red car had come back. I left a description of the perps and their car w/the HD guy, who said "this happens all the time." Well, no wonder! Security AND police presence in that lot are non-existent. 

We stayed with the car owner for about 20 min, even drove around the block to check out a guy who resembled the accomplice. BF was clearly in vigilante mode; both of us were frustrated and felt helpless that we couldn't do more.

Anyway it was a bit surreal. Be careful out there guys!
