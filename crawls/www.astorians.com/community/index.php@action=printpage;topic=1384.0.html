Title: Re: Locale?
Post by: Annwyn on January 30, 2006, 10:36:07 PM
I have been to Locale twice and I absolutely love it!
It is simple yet elegant as well as classic with a tinge of old world.     

The food is super, the aesthetic is beautiful.  In addition to tables thee is a very elegant bar area as well as a small elevated lounge with couches. 

The music was varied.  I enjoyed mellow electronica, and faster ambient beats, as well as some jazz and a little bit of the classic , sexy r & b like Sade. They also played the entirety of Depeche Mode's new album and some euro-centric electro.  Unfortunately they did throw some current pop in there, but I was happy regardless, as I sipped my Sauvignon Blanc and savored a huge plate of fried calamari.
