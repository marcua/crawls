I don't have a Dr rec for you.  I didn't like mine. 
NY Presbyterian is ranked #1 in the city for obstetrics.  Chances are though if you go there you will have a C- section.  It seems to be their specialty whether or not you really needed one.  If you're high risk, you might want to go there.  Otherwise St. Luke's-Roosevelt (http://www.nywomenshealth.com/) and NYU (http://obgyn.med.nyu.edu/) have gotten great reviews from my friends. 

You probably won't find an OB/GYN in Astoria and you don't want to give birth (if that's even a possibility) at our local terribly rated hospital.  So you'll most likely end up going to Manhattan.  If you have a car and want to go east instead of west http://www.northshorelij.com/NSLIJ/lij this place has good reviews as well.
