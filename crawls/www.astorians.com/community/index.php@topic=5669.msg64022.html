In a place like that, table moving is just part of the business.  That being said, it sounds like the restaurant handled it poorly, and if the table wasn't needed, then it sounds like they needlessly created a confrontation.

As a waiter, over the years, I've asked dozens of tables to move.  If a party wants a table that is bigger then it needs, I'll still seat them if I can manage it, but making it clear that I might have to ask them to move.  In other times, when a two top is in the middle of tables I want to put together for a bigger party, then I'll ask and grease the wheels with a drink.   Usually they'll pre-empt me and volunteer to move.  If you establish a good rapport with a table, its never really a problem.
