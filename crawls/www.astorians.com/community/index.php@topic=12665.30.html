I just ate there and was pleased with the results.

I ordered a cheeseburger and fries. The cheeseburger was real nice. juicy and filling. There was the usual assortment of toppings. plenty of onions (big bonus for me), tomato, lettuce and some sauce which tasted like mayo mixed with ketchup. Also I think the bun was coated in butter. All in all, an excellent cheeseburger. Reminded me of Joe's best burger in Flushing which is a high compliment.

The place looked nice and clean. My only complaint was that it took awhile for my food, but it's their first day so I can understand.

Very welcome addition to the neighborhood.
