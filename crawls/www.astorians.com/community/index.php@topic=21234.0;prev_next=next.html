  UCCA SHREDDING & RECYCLING EVENT TO
COMBAT IDENTITY THEFT AND INCREASE PAPER RECYCLING

DATE:
Saturday, June 18, 2011
TIME:
1:00 p.m. – 4:00 p.m.

WHERE:
Augustana Lutheran Church
69-05 Ditmars Boulevard
Astoria, New York

CONTACT:
Rose Marie Poveromo, (718) 932-5415, rmpucca@aol.com

DESCRIPTION:

The United Community Civic Association, Inc. (“UCCA”) will host a free shredding and recycling event to help combat identity theft and increase paper recycling. The event will be help at Augustana Lutheran Church, 69-05 Ditmars Boulevard, Astoria, New York. Refreshments will be provided by The Brooklyn Bagel & Coffee Company.

The UCCA is a not-for-profit organization formed in 1992 that actively participates in the community affairs of Western Queens in New York City. As part of its several goals, the UCCA provides an open forum for its citizens by organizing events to (1) provide information on issues and activities affecting the community, and (2) define and solve problems regarding the community’s quality of life.
