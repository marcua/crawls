RANT - kids and petty crime
« on: August 27, 2008, 03:44:42 PM »
So a short time ago, a bunch of kids -- about half a dozen, maybe 12-13 years old, came down our block.  They smashed an Absolut vodka bottle in front of our house, yelling and shouting.  We yelled at them out of the window and they just took off, shouting and laughing.  My husband and a neighbour took off after them (turns out they smashed another bottle up the block), caught up with them, and at first it appeared that a couple of them agreed to come back and clean it up.

As they approached our house, though, they changed their minds and took off again.  A few minutes later, a guy driving a white Range Rover came down our block.  One of the kids was in the car with him - his son, who had been particularly disrespectful to my husband and neighbour.  The father proceeded to threaten our neighbour, telling him he would come back, beat him up, and bite his ear off.

Our neighbour said he recognised the kids -- they hang around our area (off of Ditmars between 31st and Steinway) and will go into people's back yards - I don't know if they've stolen things, but we've been aware of their presence for a couple of years.

So this is to the father:  Be a man.  Stand up and acknowledge that your son did something wrong.  You are such a coward and an arrogant bastard, and obviously you're teaching your son to be the same.  Shame on you.
