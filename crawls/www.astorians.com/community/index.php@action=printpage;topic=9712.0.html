Title: ¡Sacapuntas! | New Monthly Comedy Show Hosted by Astorians | Mar 6th | THU | 9PM
Post by: DanAllen on March 04, 2008, 04:42:54 PM
Dan Allen and Ray Devito (Astorians since 2001) are not sure what ¡Sacapuntas! means but they do know that they are hosting a fantastic show that blends polished comedians, erudite writers and downtown humorists who are sharpening their acts at this monthly showcase of heavy hitters in an intimate setting.

March 6th, 2008 | THU | 9PM
Stage No. 43 at Jimmy's 43

Limited seating please email to get on list:
RSVP@SacapuntasShow.com

$5 in advance through the website and $7 at the door:
http://SacapuntasShow.com

The first show is going to be magical. Dan and Ray have NYC's finest storyteller Tom Shillue, the fabulous VH1’s BestWeekEver blogger Michelle Collins, the always inventive, downtown favorite Patrick Borelli, the awesomeness of Andrés du Bouchet.

Jimmy's 43
"Located the heart of the East Village, known for handcrafted beer on draught with local & organic food."

Located downstairs at 43 East 7th Street between 2nd and 3rd Avenue.

By Subway: Take the 6 to Astor Place Take the R/W to 8th Street Take the F train to 2nd Avenue
