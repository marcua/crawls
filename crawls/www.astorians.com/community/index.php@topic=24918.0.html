I walked by here last night and Mars looked nice.  Time for a little history lesson, though.


I remember when I first moved into Astoria back in '87 there was nothing on that area (34th Ave. I think). I used to walk to the film museum and there was nothing between Broadway and 36th Ave. Then a Greek Tavernia opened in that space where Mars is and was there for years. It was the first place to open in that part of Astoria. I can't remember if it closed or just changed owners/names, but a new space -- another/different Greek Tavernia I believe -- opened in that space before going under and being closed for a number of years, while restaurants & bars opened up all around it.


I was always sad to to walk by there and see that space closed because I remembered when it first place to open around there. It is nice to see it open and thriving and again.
