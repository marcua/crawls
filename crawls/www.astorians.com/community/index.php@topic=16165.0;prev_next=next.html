Re: Fastest Way to get to the 59th St Bridge / Northern Blvd from Ditmars (by car)
« Reply #1 on: September 14, 2009, 04:30:39 PM »
To get to the bridge, go down Crescent.  You can also try going down 21st St., but the traffic can be a pain there, too.

To get to Northern Blvd., just go down one of the streets paralleling Steinway.  My preference is to cross the Grand Central on 37th St. or Steinway.  If you're on 37th St., make a left onto Astoria Blvd. South and then a quick right onto 38th, and go down 38th.  If you cross on Steinway, again make a left onto Astoria Blvd. South, and turn right onto 42nd.  Both 38th and 42nd bring you directly to Northern Blvd.

I take 21st St to get to the Queensboro upper level. It can be horrid if there's alot of traffic, but that right turn entrance ramp is pretty sweet.
