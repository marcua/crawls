TONIGHT: Monday, January 14th @ 7pm
Cronin Phelan's Bar & Restaurant
@ The Little Garden Theatre in the Back
38-14 Broadway, Astoria, NY 11103
(R-Train to Steinway)

Astoria Producer Chuck E. Baby (aka Powell Leonard) and  Miz Ella are hosting a screening of episode 1 of "The Kong Show on TV!"
 
 The TV show, edited by the very talented Tom Bibla, features many Kong Show favorites, including stand up comedian Rob Gorden, musician Dan Tighe the Sensitive Guy, Magic Rene Clement, Stand Up Comedy Magic Irish Dave Cremin, The LionHearted Players Sketch Comedy "Love in an Elevator", Andrew Cordle, Lorraine Klimowich, Adam Murphy, Michael Picardo, Cathy Schramm,  and introducing Rick Sansone, as the villainous billionaire Maroney Sparks, owner of the wacky KNG TV studio. 
 
 Meet them live, or on screen, at Cronin and Phelan's here in Astoria in the 'theater space' in the back at 7pm this Monday, January 14th.


After the showing there will be a brief Q&A, then it's to the front for drinks at the bar.


 :wink: Then at 9pm, there's Stand Up Comedy Magic Irish Dave Cremin's open mic, so stick around for that too if ya want to cuz it's free admission! Just like our viewing. See ya'll then and by all means, tell your friends. 'Til then... :azn: :azn:
