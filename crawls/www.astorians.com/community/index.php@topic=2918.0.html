Unfortunately the only macaroni and cheese I've ever bought that I would call "good" was in Brooklyn.  But here is a really easy recipe which I have adapted from the Naomi Judd Home Companion Cookbook (which is awesome.)

Naomi Judd Mac and Cheese

Ingredients:

4 tbsp. Butter
1/4 cup Flour
2 cups Milk
16oz Cheddar, cubed (I like to use 8oz Cheddar 8oz Smoked Gouda)
1 box pasta (I like Ronzoni's Cavatappi)
Salt and pepper.

-Melt butter in a large pan.
-At flour to make a roux.
-Slowly add milk and bring to a quick boil.
-Slowly stir in cheese cubes.
-Add salt and pepper to taste.
-Combine sauce and pasta, pour into baking dish.
-Sprinkle with shredded cheese and bake to desired level of browness.

Voila!
