Title: Re: NYC Community Colleges?
Post by: Eyerish on November 26, 2007, 09:49:32 AM
I went to BMCC, Borough of Manhattan Community College and really disliked it. I found the staff, including most instructors disagreeable and far from helpful.

After getting my AA at BMCC, I went to Lehman College (also CUNY) in the Bronx for my Bachelor's degree and had the exact opposite experience. It was an amazing school and often consider going back there to take random classes because I loved it so much.

You may be able to get directly into a 4 year CUNY college instead of going to a CC first. Look into it, I think it'd be worth it. Hunter and Baruch are also supposed to be amazing schools.

Note: At Lehman many of my professors were also teaching at Columbia so I was getting an Ivy League education at a city school.
