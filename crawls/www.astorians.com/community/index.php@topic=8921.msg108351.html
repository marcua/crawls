I love Aveda's student program. You get a student who'll listen to what you want and give you a scalp and neck massage with your cut, all for $20.  :mrgreen:

I did not have a good experience with my haircut at SPAce. I went for a cut and color. The color was great, but the woman who cut my hair got booked up while I was getting the color and was really short with me when I came out front for my cut. I can understand getting booked up, so I told her I just wanted a quick trim of the ends and bangs, that I was happy with the style I had, I just needed it cleaned up, no need to blow dry or do any complicated cutting. She decided that what I wanted wasn't what I really wanted, and cut layers into my hair, "shaped" my bangs by cutting more hair into them and just generally ruined my hair. She also blew it out to giant proportions, which I hate and protested during the process. She told me the style would look better blown out and that it wouldn't take a long time. Considering that I asked for a trim of split ends, and I came out with the same length and razored ends (which split faster than cut hair), I was really upset. I haven't been back since. At least at Aveda, the students listen to what I want and make suggestions about what will work for my face instead of deciding to do whatever they want without consulting me at all.
