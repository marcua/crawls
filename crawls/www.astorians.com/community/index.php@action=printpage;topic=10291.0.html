Title: Used Bicycle Sale starts May 5th at Spokesman Cycles
Post by: SpokesmanCycles on April 27, 2008, 09:54:06 AM
Our annual sale of "slightly used bikes" starts on Monday, May 5th!

These are all bicycles that have been used as rental bikes and maintained by our mechanics.  Most of the bikes are just a year or two old.  Some have only been ridden once.  Some have a few dings or scratches. Some come in any color you want as long as it’s black.  But they’re all sturdy and safe and ready to ride, and are much cheaper “slightly used” than brand new.

For more details on specific bikes and prices see http://www.spokesmancycles.com/usedhome.htm (http://www.spokesmancycles.com/usedhome.htm)

Spokesman Cycles is located at 49-04 Vernon Boulevard in Long Island City. We're just half a block from the Vernon-Jackson stop on the 7 train and are open every day from 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM.

For more information,  email us at mail@spokesmancycles.com
