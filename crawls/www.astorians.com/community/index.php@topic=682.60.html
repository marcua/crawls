My head is spinning.  So much has happened today.  Lanseaux and I met with a local business owner who very graciously offered to donate two great spaces, plus free buffet.  I have bands lined up for each venue.  They will iron out details with the venues.  I am not counting chickens until they are hatched, so I will wait until the bands set dates with him before I post names publicly.  If you can't wait for the scoop come to the meeting tomorrow night!!!  

I am very excited about this development because it enables to reach out to a new segment of the population, it has the capacity to make an excellent financial contribution, and it's very low-maintenance on our end.  We market.  We show up and collect cover charges.  The space and the food are donated.  The bands are more than willing to donate their time and skills.  I'm overwhelmed with the generousity that people are showing, and my faith in the human race is beginning to re-emerge.  People get a call from a stranger and say "Sure, I'll give my time, my space, my skills, to help you help people."
