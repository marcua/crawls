I love El Boqueron. Great tapas and the staff is wonderful. I've been going there for years and have never been disappointed.

Don't know if they do anything special for New Year's Eve-- I know that some holidays like Mother's day require reservations, and they may have a special menu. Actually, come to think of it, my boyfriend & I happened to wander in on Mother's day once, and we just ate at the bar and chatted with the staff, they didn't mind.

Anyway, do check it out. All of the tapas dishes are very good, but my personal favorites are the squid in garlic sauce, and the baby rack of lamb.

Oh, and don't forget the sangria :)

I haven't been to El Boqueron in about three years but I once went there with someone who knows Spanish food well and we had a very lovely dinner there.  Wish I remember what all we ate since it was so tasty.  I think we might have had white anchovies as a tapa before moving on to main courses.  That would make sense since I've been told boqueron means anchovy in Spanish.  I recall the waiters being very friendly and a bit old school with the white linen cloths hanging over their forearms.  I'd recommend the place.
