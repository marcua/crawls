I live on 21st Ave near Steinway.  Have a shiso plant that produces leaves non-stop.

Shiso is great for wrapping around sushi or sashimi.  Some consider its taste to be somewhat like lime.  Either way I LOVE shiso with my sushi.  Shiso leaf is only sold in Japanese markets and can be a bit pricey.

So...I'd love to share the wealth with any neighbors.  If you know you will be having sushi/sashimi takout one night, or over the next few nights, hit me up for some shiso.  You'd just need to come here for pickup.

Some people also use shiso to make something on the idea of a mojito, or to simply add to a salad.
