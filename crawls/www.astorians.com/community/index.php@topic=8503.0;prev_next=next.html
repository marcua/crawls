Tour de Queens Planning Mtg
« on: October 29, 2007, 01:27:55 AM »
I know this is a little last minute but if you'd like to get involved with planning the actual full scale Tour de Queens ride (scheduled tentatively for the second or third weekend in September 2008), the Transportation Alternatives of Western Queens Committee (TAWQ) is having a planning meeting today, Monday evening, October 29th. 

At the planning meeting we'll be discussing all the different logistical aspects of the ride that need to be planned!  This includes everything from networking with local elected officials, press, and the bike world to fundraising and identifying potential sponsors to actually mapping out the route and finding marshals for the ride. If this sounds like it might be of interest to you, please join us!  If you want to make sure we save you a seat please RSVP at heffronmike@gmail.com   Info below:

TAWQ Tour de Queens Planning Meeting
Monday, October 29th
7:00pm
At Sparrow on 24th Ave and 29th Street, Astoria
(directly across from the Beer Garden)

p.s. Also we've started a google group for everyone that wants to stay in touch with TAWQ info.  You can join at http://groups.google.com/group/TAWQ
