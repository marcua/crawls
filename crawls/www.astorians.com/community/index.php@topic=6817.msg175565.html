We've had a DeLonghi Alfredo Ultima - like this one, but in black, since the summer of 2001.  It's great for a lot of things, and like erpotown, we like to use it in the summer when we can to avoid turning on the oven.  It's not great at toasting (you have to flip the toast to get the underside evenly done), but it's very good for baking and light broiling (veggies or browning cheese as opposed to a good broil for meat).  I even roast small chickens in it quite successfully.
