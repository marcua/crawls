Title: Jackson Heights Momo Crawl
Post by: JeffSayYes on November 22, 2021, 10:19:49 AM
Just wanted to share an event I am organizing this Sunday called the Momo Crawl.

A momo is a Himalayan dumpling that has an incredible presence in the Jackson Heights area. (I think Astoria is devoid of them at the moment)

There are currently around 30 places selling the momo here and on Sunday the 28th, most sell them individually for $1 each. We have a trolley, vintage cab, and pedicab to usher you around with dancing performances and, at the end, we vote on a winner and march the championship belt to their doorstep. There's a lot to it and it's a fun, low cost event.

You can see more info at momocrawl.com
