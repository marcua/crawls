Went last Saturday to "Ethos", the Ploes replacement. It's actually the 2nd new name, but I forget what it was between those two - it only last for a couple of weeks. The menu has changed, but not for the worse. The prices were higher, but it seems like that's been the case throughout Astoria. The food was good, but still shy of Ploes from a couple of years ago (to me, food and service nose-dived towards the end). The problem was the service. It took the six of us 2 hours to get out, and we were actually in somewhat of a hurry. Drink orders weren't taken for 10 minutes, entrees another 20 minutes after that, and we knew there was trouble when they asked another guy at my table what kind of steak he wanted (he orders steak at every restaurant he goes to) at least 20 minutes after taking his order. It was comical by the end, I asked for the check, and asked for it again, and again. Once I got it, I walked it to the register in the back and handed it to her, telling her that we were in a big hurry by this point. I worried I was being too emphatic, but no - we didn't get the credit slips back for another 10 minutes. So, to summarize, food is good, prices are slightly high, wait staff was essentially non-existent.
