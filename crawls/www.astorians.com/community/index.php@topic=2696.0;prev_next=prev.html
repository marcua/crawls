I got Pita Pan delivery last night and was pretty happy for the most part, actually. Granted, I hadn't eaten for about 10 hours when I got my food, so that may have something to do with it, but I really liked my burger and it was quite large. I hadn't read this thread yet, so I got onion rings instead of fries, but they were really good too. My boyfriend got the wings, which were actually just the drumettes fried and then you poured the buffalo sauce over them, but they were decent too. My only complaint is with the Energy Elixir smoothie I got. It was really watery and bland - NOT tasty at all. I don't know if that was just because they delivered it and it had started to melt or what, but would definitely not recommend it as it was. One thing to be aware of though, is that they are quite busy so delivery takes awhile. It took us forty-five minutes to get our food last night, but they did warn us it would take that long because of the rush they were having. I personally would order from them again.

I had a chance to finally go to the new Pita Pan today and was very disappointed.  The euro-techno music was blasting, loud enough that the ground was literally vibrating.  My friend and I couldn't even speak to each other, the music was that loud, let alone enjoy a meal.  When I spoke to the manager, who was behind the counter, about this, his reply, pretty rudely, was "this is my business and this is the kind of vibe I want for it." 

I ended up leaving and going up to Pamela's on Steinway, where I had a delicious souvlaki without the, ahem, "vibe."  It's a shame, as the old Pita Pan was a nice place to sit and enjoy a meal, especially in their garden in the back.  The new version seems to want to rival the trendy euro-trash cafes on 30th.  Very unfortunate, unless you're into that whole scene.
