The lead that brought me to Astoria turned out to be false. However, there was a different woman - spanish, short, long black hair with an 8 year old daughter who went into Discount Pet Shop on Steinway and Broadway and said she found a chihuahua and asked for help - wanted to know what to do with it. The woman who works in the pet shop suggested she bring it to the vet or shelter. The woman said she might keep it. So please, if you see a woman walking a black chihuahua in astoria, ask her if she found it and let her know her owner is desperately looking to have her home again. There have been no chihuahuas turned in to the vets in that neighborhood, and obviously I'm constantly checking all the shelters.
