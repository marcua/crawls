There's a new pizzeria going in at 36-08 Ditmars -- in what used to be the American Woodworking space.

Some info here: http://queensnyc.com/2012/08/more-pizza-is-coming-to-ditmars-blvd-in-astoria/

But I'll add more: @AstoriaHaiku on Twitter talked to someone there who said it would be "different than anything" and "Italian style."

To me that screamed Neapolitan wood-oven pizzeria. Sure enough. I poked my head in today and saw they had a Stefano Ferrara wood oven all set up and looked pretty dang close to opening. The workers in there said "next month," and @Astoriahaiku quoted them as saying 2 to 3 weeks. They've done a lot of work already. Did a good job of keeping this a secret.

A Stefano Ferrara oven doesn't guarantee great pizza, but it's considered the Cadillac of pizza ovens. Whoever is opening this place shows signs of wanting to do it right.
