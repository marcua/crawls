Title: Free Event - Baby & Toddler Clothes and Toy Swap (Sept 17)
Post by: jorli on September 10, 2011, 12:56:16 PM
 
Saturday, September 17th
@ the Broadway Library
4020 Broadway, Astoria, NY
in the Basement Social Room
Time: 11:30 AM – 1:30 PM

What is it? A community event to share maternity, baby and toddler clothes and toys. Everyone is welcome!
To Browse and "Buy" – Just show up anytime between 11:30 AM and 1:30 PM.  Browse the stuff and take what you want.
To Donate – Bring your gently used items to swap/donate to the library by 11:00 AM.  If you can't be there by 11, you can schedule an early drop by e-mailing astoriaparents@gmail.com
For more info about us, visit www.AstoriaParentsMeetup.com (http://www.astoriaparentsmeetup.com/)
Please note: Any leftover items will be donated to a local charity at the end of the event.  We will not accept items that are badly stained, broken, torn, or just plain not useable.  Thanks!
