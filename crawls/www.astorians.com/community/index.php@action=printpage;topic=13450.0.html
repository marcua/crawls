Title: Re: Parking Lot in Astoria?
Post by: holyfrjole on December 22, 2008, 08:27:42 PM
Last time we went away (end of October) I parked at LaGuardia from a Wednesday afternoon to Sunday morning and it was $90. Ridiculously expensive, but we figured that we would have spent $20 each way on cabs (including tip), and if we had left the car (we have alternate side 4 days/wk) it would have cost us a $45 ticket. It was good to have our own car waiting for us when we got back, and it was pretty easy to get from our terminal to long term parking on their internal bus system (I made the miserable trek from long term to the Delta terminal on foot going because we were super late and I couldn't wait for the bus to loop around).
