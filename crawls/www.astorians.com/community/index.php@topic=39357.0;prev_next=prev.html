My wife and I went Saturday night.  It was probably around 7-8.  Place was pretty empty, but it was pretty cold out.  Food and service were excellent.  I think the bus staff must challenge each other to see who can refill the water glasses the most.


They threw in a free appetizer and a sample of their rice pudding for dessert.  All in all it was definitely on point and worth the trip.  Especially so if you live near enough that Seva was always "too far away."


They do need to do something to the space though.  They have the beginnings of some decorations, like hanging lamps and a mural on the back wall.  But there is a lot of empty white space throughout that needs some dressing up.
