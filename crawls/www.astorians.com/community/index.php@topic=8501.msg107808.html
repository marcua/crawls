Gotta try it! I used to buy tzatziki from Christos, but alas no more! I don't think it was Fage. This brand is imported from Greece. I wonder how it compares to freshly made?
For all of you wanting to make your own tzatziki:
If you use Fage plain yogurt you don't have to put it in cheesecloth over a bowl to drain the excess liquid (unless you want to) because it's already thick.
After you've shredded or chopped the cucumber salt it lightly and let it sit in a strainer over a bowl for a few minutes (30 minutes). Later squeeze out all the water from the pieces with your hand. The salt leaches out the water from the cucumber. Now your tzatziki will be nice and thick-not watery!
Don't forget the garlic and a bit of vinegar. Optional-a little chopped dill.
