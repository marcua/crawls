Quote from: lanseaux on June 01, 2010, 12:30:31 PM
Their menu says "all burgers cooked medium unless specified" - which indicates that they are willing to cook burgers ordered another way.  It sounds, however, from people's experiences, that they are reluctant or unwilling to do so, which is disappointing and makes it seem to me that despite their lip service to it on their website, they don't truly care about their ingredients -- or their customers.

Having my burger cooked the way I request is very important to me (fast food-style burgers excepted), and their inconsistency in doing so is the main reason I haven't been to Bareburger yet.  I've eaten all the "exotic" meats on their menu, so they don't have anything I have to try.

I completely agree. If you won't cook your burgers to order, it means you don't trust your meat. I have a real problem with a place that doesn't flat-out say they will not cook something below a certain doneness, especially when they will let you order that way but then just send out something cooked more. That isn't the right way to do business and from the reviews, sounds like how Bareburger runs their restaurant - unless, of course, it's that they just have incompetent cooks. Not sure which one is worse in the end. Either will keep me from giving them my business, however.
