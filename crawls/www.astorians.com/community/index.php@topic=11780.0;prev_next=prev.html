Welcome to the neighborhood!
There is a library on 31st between Ditmars and 21st Ave.  And the Post Office is on 31st between Ditmars and 23rd Ave.

As for movie theaters, the closest is the kaufman astoria on Steinway and.. maybe.. 35th Ave? I'm sure someone will correct me.  But if you're into movies, for the rest of the summer there are a variety of foreign movies showing at Socrates Sculpture Park on Wednesday nights at sundown. Tonight is the Red Balloon and Persepolis.  It's an awesome place to see movies.  Take the N/W to the Broadway stop, then walk west till you hit the river -- the park is right there.

Your fellow former Midwesterner, Rachel
