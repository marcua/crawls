How do I make a post with the picture in the text body? That's all I ask. I am only able to post it as a separate attachment.

you do have to have the photo online somewhere like photobucket or flickr.
within the photo hosting site you can grab your photo' url to post.
then when you are composing a post in astorians you click on the button that looks like it has a photo in it, underneath the symbol for italics. you get this: (http://) in between the ][ you put the url you got from your photohosting site and bob's your uncle betty's your aunt (sorry, been reading some irish lit lately...between that and john i've been spewing lots of irish slang) you have the photo in the body of the message. just make sure to preview before posting to make sure the link worked, you may need to get an alternate link or you may need one that corresponds to a smaller size (in case it comes out ginormous). I had problems my first few times doing it and our friendly neighbors helped me out by explaining it this way.
