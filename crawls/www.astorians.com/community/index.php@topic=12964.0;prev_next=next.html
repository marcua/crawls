Prefer to rent or to own >?
« on: October 21, 2008, 08:56:38 AM »
How many people here just dont want to own their home and would rather rent instead ? I recently was talking abt this with some friends from work and a couple of them said they would rather rent then own . They made some good points that i really never thought abt on the renting is better side .

Renting is better:

1. If u intend to move every couple of years /job is on the go
2. If u have a great deal on low rent or rent stabilized apt.
3. Dont have to pay property taxes , maintenace or home owners insurance
4. Are able to invest your money elsewhere , mutal fund, ira or somewhere else.
5. Dont need to spend on closing costs , interest on mortgage .Those things are money u wont see again .
6. Stress and pressure knowing u can lose the house if u dont pay the bank every month


Owning is better :

1.If u have a rental property u can deduct interest on mortgage and property taxes
2. Home value will eventually go up
3. After 15/30 yrs u live free( other than maintenance ,taxes and insurance).
4. Its yours and u can flip it for a profit one day
5. Have more room for your family in a bigger place than an apt.
6. Alot of upkeeping , mowing lawn , garbage , repairs  and so much more to keep the house going and in good shape yr after yr.
7.Rent will eventually go up , but monthly mortgage rate monthly wont(unless its not fixed)


Each has its pros and cons i guess . You can look at it from both views and compare many other points and see what fits you better .
All that interest paid to the bank is crazy for some . Ask anyone with a home at the amount of interest they pay monthly , regardless if they got a great mortgage rate of not . My friends mortgage is $4000 a month and $3400 of it is interest and he has a great rate too . So litttle goes twds the principal .

There is much more flexibility with being a home owner, being able to be a pet owner with no problems . Painting your walls  and being able to crank your favorite song ( in some cases). Calling it your own does have alot of responsibilities also and for some people that might not work out .
