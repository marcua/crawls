Am I the only one still IN college? (Yeah, I'm taking my time.) I graduate from the School of Visual Arts this May with a BFA in Photography. Woo woo!  :rock:

Right out of High school, I went to the University of CT. I knew it wasn't for me, but I went because I was "supposed" to. Quit there after a year, then took a few years off, got a full-time job, and later, took classes part time at Housatonic Community College in Bridgeport, CT. Took an intro to Photo class there with an awesome teacher (HCC really did have a good art program, especially considering that it was a community college!), got along great with the professor, did an independent study, got my associates from HCC (after taking, like, 2 classes a semester, haha) and then applied to SVA.

I'm so, so, so glad I did it this way instead of staying at UConn. Nothing against it, but it (and that accounting major I chose bc I was good at math) just wasn't my style. :)
