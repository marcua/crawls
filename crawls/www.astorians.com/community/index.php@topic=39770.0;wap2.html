Funding for Queens Artists & Nonprofit Organizations

(1/1)

QueensCouncilArts:


Queens Council on the Arts (QCA) announces the 2018 Queens Arts Fund (QAF)



The QAF Grant support Queens–based cultural projects by individual artists, collectives, artist–nonprofit partnerships and nonprofit organizations, meeting eligibility requirements, that offer distinctive cultural programs for the Queens community.



Application Deadline on October 26, 2017.  Information Sessions begin on June 8, 2017.



For Guidelines, Applications and Information Session Dates visit: http://www.queenscouncilarts.org/qaf/
