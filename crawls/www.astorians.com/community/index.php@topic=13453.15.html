I work for the corporate offices of the online division and I love the company I work for.  This is direct from the website:

How do I cancel an item from an order?

Once your order has been accepted and scheduled to ship there are limited modifications that you can make to your order. Items that are listed as available to ship in 24 hours or 2-3 days cannot be canceled once the order has been scheduled to ship.

It says in black and white that once it's scheduled to ship, there are limited modifications you can make.  You called and demanded that changes be made and they were... yet you still aren't pleased.  BN has the largest inventory of books on the web and receives an incredible amount of orders every single minute.  Therefore, sometimes a book you just ordered that was in stock becomes out of stock.  I know it was frustrating to find out the book would arrive after the holidays but instead of looking at the situation and acknowledging an honest delay, you want to think the company is manipulating you and just trying to get your money.  As I'm sure the other employee on here can back up... BN only cares about books and getting you the books you want.  There is no master plan to knock you over the head and steal your money.  If you want to boycott them and shop at Amazon, who really does only care about your money, have at it.  We'll miss your business.

Oh, and I do agree that LaserShip is awful.  The reason they use them is because they can fulfill same day deliveries.  It's all about getting you your orders and most of the time, it does work... and quite well.

One more thing... free shipping is on all orders over $25.  It wasn't a one off, that is every single day.

Now, I'm not here "representing" the company.  I'm just surfing on my Astoria site so there's no need for anyone to bring a hammer down on my head.  For those who had bad experiences shopping at BN.com, I'm truly sorry.
