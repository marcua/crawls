If you or a friend have a Costco card, they have great shrimp platters: already cooked with cocktail sauce included.  Close to 40 medium sized shrimp for about $9 in the smaller platters.  Plus they have other platters that you could just buy and put out including sushi platters.  I've found their platters to be freshly prepared each day and really well done.  They also have great baguettes in their bakery.  And their pesto sauce is really good too.  If you wanted to just make a pot of pasta and add pesto with the shrimp platter, get some olives and the bread. 

My family would be happy with that.

Although I love Indian food myself so like pp's suggestion a lot.
