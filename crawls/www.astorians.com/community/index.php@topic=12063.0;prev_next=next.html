This place is really good.

I've had good tacos from Los Portales on Broadway, good tacos and other food from Original Mexican Food on 29th St. just off of 30th Ave.  It's fun to go to Original Mexican Food on a Saturday morning, sit at the tiny counter, and order a really awesome breakfast that they cook up on the rickety stove right in front of you.  Their daily specials are also very good and authentic. Tacos Mexico is also pretty good.  But what I really miss is good Baja-style fish tacos.  I've never had a good fish taco in NYC.

That said: I actually LIKE Fresca Tortilla! I think it's because there is such a disconnect...it bears no resemblance to real Mexican food, but it's still greasy, and covered with cheese, and flavorful in a fast-food kind of way. Much like McDonald's is to burgers.   :-D

I kind of agree with that, too -- if you stop expecting Mexican food, it's not bad for what it is and for the price.  I think the price is key.

Los Amigos is inconsistent.  The worst meal I've ever had from there was something I got one day when I got back from Mexico (maybe I didn't want the trip to end?).  To this day I don't know if it was bad because they were off just that time, or they're generally bad in comparison to what I had been eating for the past 10 days.  But one time I had really excellent chile rellenos from there.  I order from them mostly because they're pretty much the only option around Ditmars aside from Fresca.
