Meditation Classes in Astoria

'Transforming Painful Emotions'

learning to enjoy life

We are to offer classes in Astoria on behalf of Chakrasambara Buddhist Center on Monday Evenings 7:30 - 9pm and Sunday Afternoons 12:30- 2pm. Locations below.

The new series is called 'Transforming Painful Emotions' is starting Monday June 2 and Sunday June 8.
The delusions are agitated and distorted states that disturb our mind and are the main obstacles to fulfilling our wishes.  Each week we will explore a different delusion and learn practical methods to identify, transform, and overcome these inner enemies, the source of our problems and difficulties. These classes are self-contained so please feel free to drop in at any time.

Talks and guided meditations suitable for everyone. The emphasis is on integrating Buddha's teachings into our lives. Beginners are welcome. Classes can be taken individually or as a series. No pre-registration required. Everyone is welcome.

The cost is $10 per class.

Here are the class titles by week. Classes are every week unless otherwise posted.

June 2 & June 8      Identifying the Inner Cause of Unhappiness
June 9 & June 15    Dealing with Painful Emotions
June 16 & June 22  Jealousy vs. Rejoicing
June 23 & June 29  Creating Inner Wealth
June 30 & July 6     Letting go of Attachment
July 7 & July 13      Reducing our Anger
July 14 & July 20    Escaping the Ordinary

Monday Evenings:
7:30 - 9:00 pm
at Sai Organics
30-21 30th Ave
at 7:30 pm
(feel free to come early to relax if you wish)

Sunday Afternoon:
12:30 - 2:00pm
at Grace Lutheran Church
31-20 21st Ave.
between 31st and 32nd st
(please use office entrance)

See you soon!

Chakrasambara Buddhist Center is a 501-c3 non-profit, volunteer-based organization that provides meditation classes, retreats, and workshops to thousands of students and practitioners throughout the NYC region.
