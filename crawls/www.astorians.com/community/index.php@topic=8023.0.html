Advertise in the 2007 Queens International Film Festival Program
« on: September 21, 2007, 05:59:48 PM »
From November 8-11 2007 The Queens International Film Festival returns to the streets of Astoria with plenty of events for all who share the love of the silver screen.  Our mission is to not only create an event that appeals to the industry, but an event for the general public and everyday film lovers of Queens.  More importantly, however, we want to spur much needed economic activity in our neighborhoods.  What better way to support local businesses than to bring people in from near and far for an unforgettable event? 

In this, our fifth year, we’re anticipating over 20,000 attendees from all areas, all backgrounds, and all ages.  There is always something for everyone at the Queens International Film Festival, and this year is bigger and more jam packed than ever.  This year’s festival will include a very special youth program designed to expose at-risk kids from the five boroughs to the inspiring and expressive medium of film.  Our Music Lounge will be featuring local, as well as, international singing sensations.  We have seminars such as Women In Film, which will be hosted by the legendary Chazz and Gianna Palminteri.  Cultural diversity and the great mix of Queens ethnicities will be celebrated as we honor minority achievements in film.  Currently, we are in talks to award Queens Natives John Leguizamo and Lucy Liu, for their contributions to the silver screen.  We will also be having book signings, screenplay readings, gala parties, and over 300 film screenings including several Academy Award entries, and several films made right here in Queens. 

Now we recognize that times are tough and that there is a great deal of uncertainty weighing on everyone’s minds.  However, the support of our local organization’s is especially integral in our continuing to celebrate the magic and rich history of Queens and Astoria.  We have dedicated ourselves to creating an event for both the film community and most importantly our community.  On behalf of our organization and the people of Queens, we would like to invite you to be a partner of the Queens International Film Festival.   

If you have any questions you can reach me directly any time at (908) 310-1659, or email me at jmlara@comcast.net. 

I truly hope that we can count on your support as we work to make the star of the boroughs shine brighter than ever.

All my best,

Joseph M. Lara
Senior Vice President
Queens International Film Festival
