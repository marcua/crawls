A two man robbery team is robbing bodegas in our neck of the woods

The complete list is below:
1) Sunday, June 14, 2:15 a.m., the J & L Fruit and Vegetables store located at 21-003 45 Ave.
2) Sunday, June 14, 4:03 a.m., the 7 Eleven located at 30-01 30 Ave.
3) Tuesday, June 16, 12:01 a.m., the Mobile gas station located at 12 Boody St.

5) Thursday, June 18, 12:35 a.m., the Northern Boulevard Gas Corporation located at 34-17 Northern Blvd.
6) Thursday, June 18, 12:40 a.m., the SAS Deli & Grocery located at 38-02 31 Street.
7) Saturday, June 27, 04:08 a.m., the 7 Eleven located at 61-19 Northern Blvd.
8) Sunday, June 28, 1:00 a.m., the bodega located at 146 Nassau Ave.
9) Sunday, June 28, 3:20 a.m., the 7 Eleven located at 101-04 Metropolitan Ave.
10) Tuesday, June 30, 1:18 a.m., the Bodega located at 50-52 69 St.
11) Thursday, July 2, 12:30 a.m., the Bodega located at 66-04 Queens Blvd.
12) Wednesday, July 8, 12:00 a.m., the Bodega located at 21-37 21 Ave.
13) Wednesday, July 8, 12:56 hrs, the Bodega located at 61-25 Woodhaven Blvd.
Investigators said in each case the suspects entered the location armed with a gun and removed money from the register.
There were no reported injuries in any of the robberies.
In all the incidents, one suspect wears a red "USC" sweatshirt.

I think I might have seen these guys get taken down late last night, although it's just idle speculation and what I saw could have been totally unrelated.  I was out with a friend at around 1 AM walking to 7-11 (had a jones for a Slurpee).  My friend and I noticed that there were a lot of cop cars (about five or six) parked in the area of the subway/Carvel that is around 30th ave and 32nd street.  There were also cop cars waiting in the intersection right by the Tastee Corner diner and some cops on foot hanging out in the area of the 7-11. 

We went in to get our Slurpees, and I commented to my friend that there were so many cops and that I wondered if it had to do with the string of robberies.  As we were leaving the 7-11, something to the tune of 20 police cars, marked and unmarked, converged on the general area of Indigo.  They had a van stopped on the other side of the street, close to the fish market.  There were tons of cops, at one point a helicopter overhead with the search light, an ambulance or two, the ESU truck, marked cars, unmarked cars, uniformed cops, plan clothes anticrime guys, etc.  It was like something out of a movie.  The cops all had kind of a happy mood and the took away three separate guys in cuffs, a white guy and two black guys.  All of them looked to be in their 20s.

It's on the map at Gothamist, but not no details other than "pursuit": http://gothamist.com/map/
I heard a drunk woman who spilled out of the bar talking with a police officer and then telling her friends that it had to do with the bodega robberies, but obviously I don't take this as authoritative info.

Did anyone else see all this?  There were lots of people milling about and gawking.  Did anyone hear something on the news I might've missed?
