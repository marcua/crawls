FDNY visited my building today. They gave out tickets to people who had plants on their fire escapes. They removed the plants, and stuck tickets under the apartment door. They also gave tickets to people who had air conditioners in a fire escape window, and a 48-hour notice to remove them. They aren't fooling around anymore.

Check with Consumer Reports on those a/c units that don't require window installation. They might be more expensive, but it is certainly better than trying to sleep in sweltering 90°+ weather.
