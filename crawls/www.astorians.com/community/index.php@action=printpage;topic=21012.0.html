Not a lawyer here, but you might have squatter rights. I had a roomie who refused to leave once I gave her notice to get out, and she was able to stay for 3 extra months (rent free might I add) due to squatter rights. (if you live in an apartment for over a certain amount of time, I believe it is 4 months, then there are legal;proceedings that must be had in order to remove the "tenant" aka squatter). Not sure if the same applies to month to month though. i.e, the roomies may not have to leave when the landlord wants them out. His problem, not yours.
