Well, I did elaborate, but it got lost in the either when I was typing... :-o

Let me be the first to blow my own horn here...

VAMPIRE LESBIANS OF SODOM & SLEEPING BEAUTY or COMA. June 18-29 at the Secret Theatre, Long Island City Arts Center, 44-02 23rd St. (right under the 7 tracks near the Citibank Building).

I'm DAMNED proud of this production. It was done in April and sold out it's run and we're bringing it back in a modified 3/4 round format. The show is Great fun, if you don't know it. The original production became the longest off-Broadway play (and held the record for quite awhile) and launched the career of the life force that is Charles Busch (TALE OF THE ALERGISTS WIFE, PSYCHO BEACH PARTY, DIE, MOMMIE, DIE!.)

It features a cast of 7 in multiple roles, 39 flashy costumes, and one of the best 5 casts I've EVER worked with. I did a production in Provincetown 15 years ago that ran 2 summers...this one is miles better. Charles Busch actually had some contact with the cast and loaned us the original "virgin sacrifice" wig (if you know the show, you know that's actually a big deal). The audience reaction and feedback was amazing. If nothing else, I'll gurantee that you will laugh.

You can get show info at www.secrettheatre.com or direct tickets at http://www.theatermania.com/content/show.cfm/show/144311

If you saw the first one, please post a note!

Oh, yes! 4 of the company are Astorian nabies!

GBS IN LIC
Auditions for Shaw's MRS. WARREN'S PROFESSION opening 9/11/08 with the Queens Players at The Secret Theatre in LIC will be on July 21 & 22. This is one of those great little theatres that are so hot in the nabe now.

There are roles for 4 men & 2 women.

Directed by Ken Hailey

Information on the play and a link to the full script are available:   http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mrs._Warren%27s_Profession

It's a 105 year-old play that you would swear was written last week...full of the wit that Shaw's plays are known for. It tackles some pretty spiky issues, too.

Requests for audition slots should be e-mailed to info@secrettheatre.com
