Have they gotten better about cooking the burgers to the requested temperature[?]

Short answer - yes, they have, at least in my experience.

I had serious issues with them early on because of they way they consistently mis-cooked their burgers (usually over- rather than under-cooking).  When I finally went there, my elk burger was practically raw (cold inside).  I actually like burgers that way, but I rarely (no pun intended) order them that way unless I absolutely trust the source of the meat, and mine was much rarer than I had ordered.  But on subsequent visits my burgers have been much more appropriately cooked, and my friends have also been happy with their burgers.
