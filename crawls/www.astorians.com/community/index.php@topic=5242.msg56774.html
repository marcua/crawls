Astorians Sponsors Pilot Program
« on: February 17, 2007, 08:04:34 PM »
Greetings,

Tonight you'll see something new on your screen while browsing the site - an Astorians.com sponsor. In short, two Astorians have agreed to be part of a pilot program to sponsor two of the categories within the site in the form of a small banner advertising their service or product. The first Astorian to participate in the pilot is our own JustDesserts, who will be sponsoring the "Restaurants & Food" category with her pastry products. The second Astorian to participate in the pilot will be announced shortly, but is a well-known local musician on the board.

The hope is that Astorians.com will be able to provide a valuable service to local artisans, musicians, artists, and entrepreneurs that make this community strong - while also providing an additional way for individuals to support this site. The intention is to keep the advertiser-sponsors local, and evaluate them on a case-by-case basis. Your ideas and feedback are welcome.
