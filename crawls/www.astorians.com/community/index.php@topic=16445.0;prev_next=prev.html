To say that the rezoning plan is 'finalized' may be a bit premature.  Rezoning an entire Community Board (CB)or even a part thereof must go through a review process called ULURP (Uniform Land Use Review Procedure).  All currently open ULURP applications for Queens CB1 are listed here:

http://www.nyc.gov/html/dcp/pdf/lu_apps/ulurp_q01.pdf

Other than the proposed Hallet's Cove development, I don't see anything that covers more than a couple of tax lots.  So this rezoning hasn't entered the formal review process yet.  Probably what's happening is that the Dept of City Planning (DCP) has completed their studies and presented their findings and recommendations to the CB for input prior to starting ULURP.
