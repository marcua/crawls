* frat boy quotient

Has anyone else noticed how bad the smoking is at Sparrow? I was there on two Sunday evenings this past summer, and could not stay due to the level of smoking going on in there.  It reeked, and I couldn't even breathe.  I have no idea if this goes on during the week, or if it's a weekend thing.  I do know it's where all the beer garden hopefuls/rejects (I don't mean this in a bad way) go when the line is too long on the weekends.  But during the week, I've heard people say they really like the place.  Both times on Sunday when I went, it was relatively empty, except for a few people, and the lone drunk girl asleep all over the chairs.  Yet the smoke was intense, there's no ventilation, and the bartender could care less.  It's definitely a hole in the wall, I think I may have liked the old Tupelo's actual design of a bar better, but that's a personal opinion.
