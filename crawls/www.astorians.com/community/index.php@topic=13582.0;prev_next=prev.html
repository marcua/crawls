Has anyone found great gym rates recently?  I'm interested in joining Synergy and was hoping to take advantage of a crazy special.  :lol:

I'm a member of synergy.  They're always advertising a $35 a month special, I think.  But when I joined, I looked into Globalfit first.  I think the global fit website said something like a $59 initiation fee then $27 billed every 2 weeks.  Of course the people at synergy (or any gym) would rather sign you up themselves, because then they get the commission.  So, I went there, told them about the globalfit deal and said if they couldn't match it, I would sign up through global fit.  They agreed to $30 a month and didn't charge any initiation fee.  I just paid them in full for the entire first year (but they never said that was a requirement.  You can bill monthly, paid through a credit card or checking account).  No matter what they tell you at any gym, the fees are always negotiable, especially the ridiculous "initiation fee".

I joined Astoria Sports Complex (ASC) last week after being a member of NYSC for 10 years.  I did it for economic reasons, obs.  So far I like ASC. The way membership works there is you have a choice of yearly packages: $159 ($99 plus misc) for either the cardio OR strength training floors, another price for the pool and either cardio or strength, another price for everything.  I did the $159 for cardio and have found the place clean with a good number of machines and reasonable variety of machines.  The machines are dated, like what NYSC had 6 years ago, but completely usable and reasonable.  Everything is pretty bare-bones compared to NYSC, but, again, for $159 quite acceptable.
