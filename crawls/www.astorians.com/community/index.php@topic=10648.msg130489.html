Hi,

I just thought I'd start a whole new thread about Freeze Peach, the new Freeze Peach that is.   If you haven't been there lately you should drop by.  In addition to new tables and chairs, they are now offering panini sandwiches - cubans, ham & cheese and grilled cheese - and their usual fair of 27 teas and a world of coffees. There are also more edibles such as candy bars, biscotti, etc. 

Starting Sunday there will be new ongoing hours of 10am-11pm seven days.   The new partners have a mind to bring in Emack & Bolios so stay tuned.   

The address is: 22-00 29th Street, off of Ditmars.  Visit the website: www.freezepeach.org for information and updates.

Hi,

I'm glad to see there is so much concern about Freeze Peach.  Obviously we all care about the place.   Panini's are a new thing they're trying out.   I haven't personally tried it out yet but may soon.   Several people have given them rave reviews though.

About Emack & Bolios, they are seriously looking into it.   I think it's definately going to happen.   Probably around July 1.  I'll post here the minute I see it so stay tuned.

I think that Freeze Peach does tea really super well which is probably why it's my hangout of choice.   I maxed out on coffee a while ago and my stomach can't handle it when I drink even decaf.  Somehow it can handle tea.  I personally love the Chai's.   The only place I know of that offers Chai without sweetener.  Have you ever tried it at Cosi or Starbucks?   You might as well order a sugar latte or an iced sugar latte.   I'm trying to cut out sweeteners so it's a help but you can also get it with honey at Freeze Peach.

Keep watching here for new developments and stop by the cafe to see what's going on.
