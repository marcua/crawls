I was the one who had the unfortunate experience of staying at the Seacrest Inn.  Ugh, how awful.  As far as the type of people on the island -- There are definitely people with money, but there are also many nature lovers there.  The island is so beautiful, cool birds, lovely ocean, rocks, sand, lighthouses.  You can rent a bike - but be a biker, as this can get arduous and I was so very out of shape, it was pathetic.  You can rent mopeds too.  It's a great island to explore, and so relaxing.  I wouldn't worry about feeling out of place.

Block Island is not cheap.  Do your research on trip advisor and other such sites, and go with a group of people.  It saves on a room.  I have no idea how long you should book in advance.  Probably a lot.  You could also just make a day trip out of it, many people do that.  And yes, the booking of the car takes a long time.  I would just rent the moped.  It's an island with not much technology so the bad websites don't surprise me.  But it is a beautiful place to visit if you can swing it.
