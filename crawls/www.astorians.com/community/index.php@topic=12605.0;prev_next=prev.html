I just finished reading Company by Max Barry.  If you have ever worked in an office, company or the corporate world, you will relate to this hysterical book.  It's fabulous and well-written.  I second anything by Augusten Burroughs (start with Running with Scissors), and I personally love Chuck Palaniuk - his stuff is VERY strange but well-written and dark.  (He wrote Fight Club, which was made into a movie).  If you're new to his work, I recommend starting with "Survivor", one of his best novels.
