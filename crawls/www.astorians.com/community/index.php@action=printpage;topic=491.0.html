Title: paint your own ceramics night
Post by: longa on June 23, 2005, 05:57:23 PM
Hi,
I am organizing a small group to have a "paint your own pot night" at my art studio.
We are meeting on Tuesday nights, starting June 28th, around 7ish pm, we will be there until 10:30pm.
There will be around 4 or 5 of us, all locals from Astoria.
You can either paint something that is already made (there will be some cups, bowls, plates, mugs to choose from... or  handbuild it out of clay. What ever you feel more comfortable with. There are plenty of references to get ideas from, books, magazines, fabrics, etc.
Do not feel intimidated by other peoples artistic skills, just take your time and you will amaze yourself.
Once the piece is painted, I will glaze and fire it, it will be food, microwave, and dishwasher safe, and you can pick it up in about a week.
Cost will be from $10-15 depending on size of piece, that includes all materials, the piece and the firing.
The address of the studio is: Astoria Soundworks 38-01 23rd Ave Buzzer #35, Astoria NY 11105
If you have any other questions, contact sandra @ 718 932 7885 or email at astoria_artworks@yahoo.com
