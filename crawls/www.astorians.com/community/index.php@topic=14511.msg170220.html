As you may have discovered in your search, out of our local public schools, neither PS 84, PS 85 or PS 2 have an after school program. They all try for spots in the Beacon program.

Just spoke to Champions at PS 122 and they will not have a Kindergarten program next year, unless families have an older child in Champions. So that means there is no public program for UPK or K in the area. Grace Lutheran offers private after school care for those ages and LIC kids and Serious Fun do as well. Other private schools require that your child be enrolled in the school to take advantage of after school care, that was my understanding for the Immac program.  Have they opened it up to parents outside of their school?

Steinway Church is holding a meeting aout this on Saturday, sponsored by thier program, Serious Fun, which I run for them.  I've posted it on the board: Sat Jun 20 at 11:00.  I'm gathering info on all programs I can think of and would love to hear suggestions and experiences from everyone.  If you can"t come but want to contribute, please PM me.
