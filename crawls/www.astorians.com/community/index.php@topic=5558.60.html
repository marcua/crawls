Speaking of taco crawls, we're diggin' Athens Grill on 30th Ave across from Trade Fair. Don't let the name fool you--it's AUTHENTIC Mexican, which is not always pretty, but tasty nonetheless. Anyway, we stopped in the other night around 10:30 on our way home from Zlata Praha (sp?), our fav neighborhood Czech bar, and the place was PACKED. The restaurant part was filled with, from what I could tell, all Mexicans (a good sign) and the kooky, pink-lit bar in the back, blasting the Tejano music had a number of guys sitting at the bar. Their homemade sauces are good, though they give you a runny green concoction they refer to as "guacamole." Not sure about that. Anyway, we'll usually sneak back and order a few shots of Cazadores--my fav tequila--from the bartender in the back.
