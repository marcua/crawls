Title: Re: nail salons
Post by: ladydi on June 04, 2008, 08:33:15 AM
Yes, you tip - you're getting a service so you tip according to the price of the service.

Most of the nail salons in Astoria don't require an appointment - I would only make one if I wanted
a certain technician or was getting something other than a regular, basic manicure, like
silk tips for instance.

If you're going to one of 'day spas' like Anasa, Hydra or maybe Space, I would make an appointment -
I don't think they take walk-ins.

I've gone to Eve's Nails, on 30th Ave. between 30th and 31st Streets. Small, but very clean, good service.
The downside is that the wait can be long, esp. the busy days like Thursday, Friday, weekend.
Their regular, basic manicure is $8.

Have fun!
