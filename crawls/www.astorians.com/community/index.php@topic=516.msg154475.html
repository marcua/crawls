We are both in our 30s. I am 33, an illustrator and artist, and specialize primarily in graphics for children's books, wedding invitations,  and customized coloring books for kids, businesses, weddings and various other special events. I also paint on stuff I find, like furniture or doors or anything really. I love retro graphics, and my idols are Mary Blair, Kevin Kidney, and Jim Flora. I'm also crazy about kids, and love to paint fun illustrations for them to decorate their rooms with n stuff.
