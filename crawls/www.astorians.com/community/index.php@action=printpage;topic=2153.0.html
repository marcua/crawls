Title: Queens CSA (35th Street/Avenue) Membership Open!
Post by: aaronf on May 18, 2006, 12:27:49 AM
The Queens CSA in Astoria is reaching out to new members for the 2006 season. Our members receive hand-picked organic produce every week from June through December. The fruits and vegetables are locally grown at the Green Thumb Farm in Water Mill, Long Island -- this is the same farm as the Hellgate CSA.

Pickups are Tuesdays at the ARROW community center on 35th St. For more information call 718-937-1264 or visit our website: http://www.queenscsa.org (http://www.queenscsa.org)

The season begins June 6th with sweet and juicy organic strawberries. Hurry up and get healthy!
