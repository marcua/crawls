Hello Mae,

I have lived in Astoria for over 15 years and I think it is one of the best neighborhoods in NYC. I think that of all the areas here the most convenient, safest and least congested is the Ditmars Blvd.\31st street area (also known as Upper Ditmars area). It is the last stop on the N\W in Astoria, has some of the best schools, shopping, restaurants, parks, lounges, bars, shops and some the most interstesting people. It is very child friendly and their are plenty of apartments for rent. There are a a lot of 3 family homes and small apartment buildings however, you have to act fast because it is an area that people snatch up apartments very quickly.

Let me know if you need some more help and I would be glad to direct you. Good luck neighbor!
