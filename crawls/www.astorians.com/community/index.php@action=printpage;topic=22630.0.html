Title: Thanks from Us at Green Space!
Post by: Dance Entropy on April 28, 2012, 01:21:25 AM
We just wanted to throw out another big thank you to everyone who made it out to Green Space Blooms this year! It was the best so far with amazing attendance and strong performance work.
And for those of you who may have seen all the posts and ignored them as spam we just want you to know that we have been posting religiously on the Astorians board trying to get the word out to you neighbors that something extraordinary is happening at Green Space. That no, you don't have to go all the way away to Manhattan or Brooklyn to find quality performing arts when we have this sweet little purple box venue with ongoing series (http://www.greenspacestudio.org/GreenSpacePerformances.html) right in Queens!
If you love performing arts then come on by! You all are just around the corner, up the road and a few blocks away! We'd love to see you!

Valerie Green & Theresa tm
