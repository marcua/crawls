Welcome!  I'm always near that intersection for one reason or another.  There are lots of great places nearby where you don't have to go too far and get too wet.  Broadway Station is perfect for a burger n brew n game.  Shima is my favorite Japanese restaurant in the entire city.  Sac's has a great brick oven pizza (especially welcoming in this cold weather).  And you can't live in Astoria with out going to Omonia for dessert.  All within a 2/3 block radius of you.
