Recently formed Astoria women’s soccer team is looking for interested women, between the ages of 18-32, with some soccer experience, to play on our Division B team.  Spots available in all positions, including GOALIE!!  Players should have played competitively on a High School or club team.  The season begins in September and all games are played on Sundays in the NYC area (Brooklyn, Astoria, Manhattan), fall practices take place on Wednesday evenings.  Our team is comprised of friendly and fun young women and we’re looking to add more players to our soccer family.  The summer practices have already begun and take place every Sunday morning in Astoria.  For more information please contact: Sam Armstrong (samlarmstrong@gmail.com)  In your e-mail, be sure to include your name, soccer experience, preferred position, and contact information.
