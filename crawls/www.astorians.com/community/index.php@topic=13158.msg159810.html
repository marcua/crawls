There is another well-known option offered by AT&T that's made by a company named after a fruit, which you've maybe heard of. I have a suspicion said company and product will lead to a long bickering thread similar to any mentioning of the "h-word" (h*pster).

From the bit I've paid attention, most reviews at this early point find the Bold a better choice right now. Lots of complaints of the Storm software feeling unfinished or worse (slow, freezing, etc.).

Overall though, it will depend on what your primary usage is likely to be. If you bang out a million emails/sms's, you'll probably want to stick with a physical keyboard. If you're likely to do a lot more web-viewing, music/video watching and game playing, full touch is probably a better bet.
