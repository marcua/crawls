No matter how much research I've done through the years, I still can't tell the difference between waterbugs and roaches. Whatever - they're both disgusting. I had a pretty big problem with roaches last spring. (An exterminator said that they were, in fact, roaches.) Once he came through and dusted, caulked up some gaps around cabinets and whatnot in the kitchen - I was free of them.

I was hoping to be free of them this season, no such luck.  The sticky strips really do work - I manage to catch quite a few. I leave two or three strips under both the oven and the fridge. I killed one with spray just last night. And killed another a few weeks ago. The other two apartments in the house don't have issues with them. I'm OCD clean - not sure why they keep coming back, and to MY apartment. They're repulsive.

We never had roaches in my house growing up (Ditmars area), other creepy crawlers yes, but not roaches or waterbugs.


But about 15-20 years ago, waterbugs appeared and now everyone I know has them in their house or apartment to some degree. It's less a problem in the colder months but when it gets warm and humid, they tend to go inside buildings.


One time, I was out all day and when I returned home late on a particularly hot and very humid night and flicked on the light, there were about 10-12 of them flying around my bedroom. I'm not usually freaked out seeing one or two but 10+ was really gross.

I've lived in NYC for 15 years now.  Never saw any waterbugs at my place in Harlem for 7 years.  In Glendale, Queens, in a REALLY nice totally gut reno'd house, I saw one waterbug in 5 years.  Was in a new luxury bldg. on 21st Street in Astoria for 2 years and had no waterbugs.

Now...I'm living by Steinway and 21st Ave in a small apt building that is pretty nicely kept BUT I suspect the LL who lives on the first floor has lots of 'stuff' in his apartment and I also know the basement levels garages are FULL of stuff...which I'm sure doesn't help matters.  Here in this building I've noticed that in the summer months, we seem to get waterbugs.  I'd say over a three month period in the summer, I might see a total of 10 or so in the hallways.  I think I saw one in my own apt last year, and I saw one about a week ago.  Otherwise it's not a real problem in my particular apt. as I paid a handyman guy last year to seal up all the tiny cracks along floor/baseboards, under sink, behind stove, behind fridge, etc.  I also put grey felt weather strips under my apt. door to help fill the space.  I think all this helps.

I also keep my place super clean.... though I understand that when it comes to water bugs they will often show up regardless...
