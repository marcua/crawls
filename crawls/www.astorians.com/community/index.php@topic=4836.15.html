The Oscars are very much a fashion show.  And a popularity show as well.  But I don't think all awards whether for movies, music or books are bad.  And in much of the entertainment industry, your success can depend as much on networking as on your talent.  Achieving an award can bring someone who was previously unnoticed to the forefront and kickstart their careers.  The Oscars are all glitz and glamor but there are plenty of other awards programs in film, music, tv, books, etc. - most not even televised - that really do recognize talent.  I know plenty of people with awards under their belt, most of them filmmakers, and the majority are very deserving of the honor.
