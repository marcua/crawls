The food here was very tasty.  Two friends and I split 4 mezes: the assorted dips, grilled pork, house sausages, and grilled octopus.  Everything was great, especially the octopus--so sweet and tender.

BUT!!!!

We made the mistake of going yesterday around 6 PM, right after all the Greek Independence Day festivities.  This might have been the first nice day they had, with the outdoors tables set up, and they were completely slammed.  We waited 45 minutes for the first plates of food to come out (we suspect the waitress had forgotten to enter our order) and were well into eating before we got napkins.  We saw drinks and food going to wrong tables, and just a general air of cluelessness among all the servers.  Hopefully, this won't be a trend as we move into warmer weather.  Also, the Greek dance music was blaring.  I've seen live jazz players there during the week, so I hope this was only because of the parade and won't be a regular weekend thing.
