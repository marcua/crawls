I'm on wind power and have been for... 18 months or so?

There are 2 charges -- cost and supply.  Supply cost is cost per kwh for wind (locked in rate) and service charge fluctuates (I think) and is charged by coned for delivery.

Bill for 7/2/07:

used 169 KWH (over 31 days) -- $0.068580/per KWH ($11.59) << service
used 169 KWH (over 31 days) -- $0.185000/per KWH ($31.27) << supply cost for wind

It's a little confusing, I guess.
