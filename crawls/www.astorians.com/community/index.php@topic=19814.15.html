I've become a fan of Last Stop located directly under the ditmars station where Yolo use to be (same owners, just new name/style)... especially on Sundays for the wing & beer specials they have while watching football.

If you're looking for really spicy, then they won't impress, but every time I've gone, the wings are a good size w/ plenty of meat, cooked perfectly and the sauce has good flavor.

If you've overlooked this place, then it might be worth a try on Sunday since the wings are on special (10 for $5).  :mrgreen:
