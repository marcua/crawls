it's definitely not one of those type places - it's the next generation, which is a welcome change.  We stopped in for drinks before Christmas and while it is Croatian-owned, they're serving up drinks and food like any American place and very welcoming regardless of your ethnicity.  We actually went back over the weekend for dinner and were blown away by the quality of the food.  Not cheap, and not fast, but holy cow was it delish!  Stuffed mushrooms were some of the best I've ever had and my husband had a really light and tasty fish soup for appetizer.  I had gnocchi with veal sauce and he had veal cutlets amazingly tender and served with the most buttery roasted potato on the side.  Walk in the door & give it a try, I don't think you'll regret it.
