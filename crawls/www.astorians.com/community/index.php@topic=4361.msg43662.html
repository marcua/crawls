YEAH!  Hell's Kitchen turned us on to huitlacoche too.  Coincidentally, we just ate at Tacos Mexico on Sunday night and had none other than the huitlacoche quesadilla there - very good!  Another good place for it is supposed to be Itzocan (two locations: east village and east harlem).

We didn't end up going ANYWHERE - weren't so hungry at 10:00 (we had wine and cheese before we headed into the city), and figured we would grab a bite when we got back to A-town.  Big mistake- we waited like 20 minutes for the N, got back to the Ditmars stop at around 10:45, and found almost everywhere a) closed, b) getting ready to close, or c) somethign that we didn't want to eat.  Ended up at Mike's Diner.  Translation: we failed miserably.  Next time, we will build up an appetite by shlepping to ninth avenue and go to Hell's Kitchen or E.  There's also a Mexican place there in the 50's (El Azteca) which is not very good, but I reserve a special place in my heart for it because my friends used to live across the street from it and we'd chow there while intoxicated at 1am.
