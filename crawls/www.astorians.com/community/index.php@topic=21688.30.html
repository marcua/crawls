Finally made it over to Bear last Friday evening.  Was somewhat surprised to find nearly every table full - plus seats at the bar.  This was one of the best restaurant meals I've had in Astoria in a while.  And I don't have to tell you what great food we have all over town.  First of all, the owners and staff could not be more kind and attentive.  We were there just as they were trying out their new winter menu.  We had the scallop ceviche appetizer along with an appetizer of roasted mushrooms with a lightly poached egg on top.  When the semi cooked yolk blended with this mushroom dish, it was amazing.  The scallop looked as good as it tasted.  A very nice combination of flavors from wasabi to the salmon caviar, and the delicate scallop.  I had the rabbit sausage over buckwheat (kasha) pilaf.  I couldn't get over how delicious it was.  Perfectly seasoned and cooked.  A light, poultry like flavor but not dry like chicken can be.  I really enjoyed it.  My wife had the short ribs and loved every bite.  Portion sizes are just right to rather generous.  Prices are very reasonable for a menu like this.  I realize this location seems odd to some people but there are more and more people moving to this part of town.  And a number of large real estate projects about to come online right near there.  They are positioned well.  From what we saw, they have no trouble at all filling tables.  I look forward to going back soon and bringing friends.
