So we have been watching all the ground floor activity around the newish apartment building on 33rd Street a couple of buildings behind MP Taverna over the last few weeks.
 
First there was a sign of a daycare opening and then we noticed that another business was going in.  Last weekend we walked up to the door(the lights were on) and assumed it would be an acupuncture place or something of the like.  Then this morning I noticed the dreaded papered over door and the words "Happy Spa" in bold letters.
 
Personally, if I were paying over $2k a month for a 1 BR apartment in this building I wouldn't be happy about a "spa" like this opening up in one of the retail spaces.  Even more disconcerting is that it's about 30 feet from the incoming daycare.
