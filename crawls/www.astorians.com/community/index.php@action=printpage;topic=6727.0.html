Title: Marriage Equality Legislation Advances
Post by: mcdirk on June 19, 2007, 02:34:36 PM
Did folks see that the New York State Assembly Judiciary Committee voted along party lines yesterday to support the marriage equality bill?  It proceeds to the Rules Committee today and may have a vote late this afternoon.  It's likely to be a tight vote, but hopefully we prevail.  Our local Astoria Assemblymember, Michael Gianaris, is a co-sponsor of the bill, as is Assemblymember Cathy Nolan, Assemblymember Jose Peralta and Assemblymember Jeff Aubry.
