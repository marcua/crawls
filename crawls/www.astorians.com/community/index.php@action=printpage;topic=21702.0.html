Title: A Potpourri of Song — NOT Bach! Sunday October 9 at 4:00 p.m.
Post by: mvsqueens on September 25, 2011, 05:35:10 AM
A Potpourri of Song℠ presents “NOT Bach” on Sunday, October 9 at 4:00 p.m. in St. Cecilia’s Church, 80 Henry Street, Greenpoint/Brooklyn, New York.

Gina Haver, Soprano and Astoria resident Mark Victor Smith, Organ join to perform Mozart’s Exultate Jubilate for soprano and Fantasy in F minor for Organ, organ works by Buxtehude and Mendelssohn, and Schubert’s The Shepherd on the Rock.

A reception follows with food and drink for all to meet and greet the artists and each other.  The suggested donation is $15 or “as you are able.”  Visit the website at http://www.stcecilia-bklyn.org/ for directions and more information.  A tithe of the concert receipts will be donated to charity.

This concert was first presented at Trinity Lutheran Church (31st Avenue and 37th Street) last February.
