Next Sunday then.  If you can throw a frisbee and run, you can play ultimate.  If you can't throw a frisbee, I will teach you.  There are only ten simple rules you need to know, and half of those are related to the field dimensions, so don't worry about that.  I learned the game at college in the Midwest, my school was really big into it and now every time I get together with old friends, we always play.  Its cerebral and athletic.

Ultimate is an exciting, non-contact team sport, played by thousands the world over. It mixes the best features of sports such as Soccer, Basketball, American Football and Netball into an elegantly simple yet fascinating and demanding game.
