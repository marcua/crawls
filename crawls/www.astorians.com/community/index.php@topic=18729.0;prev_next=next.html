To be honest, HANAC were probably the least helpful - all of their buildings are full and not taking any applications. Same with the Catholic Charities housing.

The best resource I found was NYFSC, who manage Riverview Gardens senior housing in the new Avalon condo building by the waterfront in LIC. They have waiting lists, but it does turn around quickly.

http://www.nyfsc.org/services/housing_buildings.html
