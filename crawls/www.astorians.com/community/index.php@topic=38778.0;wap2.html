Free Read Aloud Stories for Small Kids

(1/1)

GrowNYCgreen:
EVERY SATURDAY IN AUGUST, 2015 | 2 – 4 PM

Location: Socrates Sculpture Park
Vernon Boulevard and Broadway
Long Island City, NY 11106
Presented in partnership with Urban Libraries Unite
Meet at the park’s “Mini Library” and join Urban Librarians Unite for Guerrilla Storytime in the park. These fun, pop-up readings with real librarians will offer families a chance to hear stories and sing songs in the park. Drop in anytime to share the love of reading with your family as we take the library into the park!
FREE. NO RSVP REQUIRED.
For further information visit: http://socratessculpturepark.org/education/kids/guerrilla-storytime
