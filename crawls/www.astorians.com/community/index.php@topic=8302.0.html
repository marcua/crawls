« on: October 16, 2007, 09:03:11 AM »
maybe this belongs in the rants section....

i'm becoming increasingly annoyed at the Q101 bus.
i live east of steinway street. and above astoria boulevard.  i work in Jamaica. so i take the Q101 bus down steinway to get the R and take the trains to Jamaica. 

it doesnt get me to work on time if i get to the bus stop one hour before work begins (because the bus seems to be very unpredictable). so today, i got to the bus stop an hour and a half before work begins. that bus was stuck at the intersection of steinway and astoria boulevard for more more than 5 light changes. it's quite frustrating.

and then of course, when we get to subsequent stops, there are tons of people waiting. one by one get on the bus. and then people fight to get on because it's crowded. and people fight to get off because it is crowded.

is my annoyance at the Q101 justified?
is it just the traffic at Astoria Boulevard that is the problem? the construction (STILL!!!!) going on at that overpass?
is there a better way to get to Jamaica that i don't know about?
