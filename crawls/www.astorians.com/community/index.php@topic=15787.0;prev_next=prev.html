Another recommendation for the future:  I love Mexicana car service.  I never used it when I lived in Astoria, but now that I live in Woodside, I use them all the time.  They are always perfectly on time or a few minutes early, and sometimes I use them when I want a ride into Manhattan for  night out, and when I call, they usually say "7 minutes" and then they are there (cost is $30 to Manhattan-I usually end up somewhere around midtown west).  Most recently, I had them pick me up at 430am to go to JFK, and they were a few minutes early and the cost was $28.  I took a yellow cab home from JFK, and the cost was about $35 (although we did hit some traffic).

Mexicana is always on time, professional and yes, they do speak English.  Oh,and I love that they keep your address in their system, so that when I call, I only have to give them my phone number and they know exactly where I live.  My only gripe is that they do not take credit cards. 

Mexicana
718-446-8700
