Title: Re: Car stereo installation
Post by: byron on June 27, 2007, 07:27:17 AM
Just a heads up, and you may already have this stuff, but you also need the proper installation kit(the pastic mount for the stereo) and wiring harness for your particular car.  It's really a straightforward process, just take the dash apart enough to get the old stereo out, put in the new mount, plug the wiring harness between your cars wiring and the new stereo(it's just male-female plugs), plug in the antenna, put the new stereo in the mount, reassemble.   Usually the hardest part is finding all the right screws to loosen on the dash, but one of those 20 dollar service books from the auto parts store can usually tell you exactly how to do it.
