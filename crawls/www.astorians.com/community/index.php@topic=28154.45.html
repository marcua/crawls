I'm really disappointed to hear so many people saying negative things about the World Cup revelers. The vast majority of the revelers are out there having fun, not destroying anything, and not hurting anyone. Yes, there are a few bad eggs who do stupid things but let's not be so pessimistic and negative. I love the celebrating in the streets and I worry that in four years the police will crack down on the celebrations because of a few idiots.

I found myself in the middle of the Greek and the Brazil/Colombian celebrations and the police handled each of them differently. There were not enough police presence at the latter one. For the Greek celebration, the police were there within minutes, keeping people out of the streets, and allowing traffic to flow down 30th avenue. For the Brazil/Colombian one, the police just sat on the sidelines until it got out of hand. I think they were not prepared for that level of street activity. Hopefully next time they will be more aware and station themselves on the streets before the games end.

There is no reason to spoil the fun. It can just be handled a little better.
