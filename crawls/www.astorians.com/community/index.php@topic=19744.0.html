Thanks for the advice everyone. After doing a little research I realized I was probably pretty screwed. I ended up just settling the tickets, which you can do online, for a reduced fee. If you ever get any kind of parking ticket and don't have the time/energy to fight it or you know your guilty and won't win, follow these steps to get a reduced fine.

1. Go to http://nycserv.nyc.gov/NYCServWeb/NYCSERVMain
2. Click GO button next to "Request Hearing for Parking/Red Light Tickets
3. Enter your violation number (usually a ten digit number with a hyphen  on the side of the ticket) and click search
4. The next screen will ask you if you want to plead guilty for a lesser fine. Follow the directions.   

This turned my $65 tickets into $43.  It still sucks but at least it is something less than full price and I didn't have to waste a day in traffic court.

Thanks again for all the advice.
