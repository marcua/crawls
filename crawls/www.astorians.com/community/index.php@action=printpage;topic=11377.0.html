ASTORIA MUSIC NOW!
The First Annual FREE Music & Arts Festival of its Kind.
For one day, on July 26th, people will be treated to some of the finest local music our area has to offer.
This event will highlight and benefit the arts in one of the most culturally diverse communities in NYC.
Come enjoy nine hours of local music and arts absolutely FREE!
 
WHERE: Astoria Park - The Great Lawn -  Ditmars & 19th St. Astoria, NY 11106
WHEN: Saturday, 7.26.08 - 12-9pm
