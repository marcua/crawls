Fresh - Steinway Street and 30th Ave.
« on: June 21, 2008, 08:56:03 PM »
This place has been opened for a few months now and I'm giving it a big thumbs up. 

As the name implies, the food is fresh and deelish!  They say the food contains no artificial sugars, fats, carbs, chemicals, additives or preservatives.  They use food that is "all natural, organic, antibiotic free, heart healthy..." etc.  They have a great menu.  They have really good food and smoothies and they deliver.

http://www.freshnyjuicebar.com/
