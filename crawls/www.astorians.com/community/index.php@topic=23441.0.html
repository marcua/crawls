A new fishmarket opened on Steinway between 28th and 30th Aves a couple of months ago, the guy used to work at another fish market in Astoria for many years, and finally decided to set up shop for himself.  He's invested his life savings in the place and works his butt off to give you great service.  His prices on some items like shrimp and Little Necks are the best in the neighborhood and the place looks spotless, you can tell he is trying hard.  I'm happy to see a new fishmonger in this neck of the woods.  I wish him luck, and as long as his quality keeps up, I think I'll keep going there.
