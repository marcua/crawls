Title: Women's Choir concert - this Saturday at 8:00 p.m.
Post by: jlcak on May 15, 2009, 10:07:42 AM
Hey Astorians!  If anyone is interested in hearing a FANTASTIC women's choir this Saturday, the Melodia Women's Choir is performing at 8:00 p.m. at St. Ignatius Loyola in Manhattan (http://melodiawomenschoir.org/concerts.php).  Not only is it a great group of singing ladies, but we are performing with a full all-female orchestra as well.  We will be singing some fabulous works: "Messe a troix voix" by Andre Caplet, "Ave Maria" by Gustav Holst, "Cantique du Jean Racine" by Gabriel Faure, and a rare performance of "Trois petites liturgies de la presence divine" by Olivier Messiaen.  Tickets are available online through the Melodia website (www.melodiawomenschoir.org) or by phone at 1-800-838-3006.  There are several Astoria locals in the group, so come out and support some singing Astorians!  It's great for date night - about an hour and a half long which leaves plenty of time for dinner and chatting  :-)

Melodia Women's Choir presents "Visions of Eternity"
Saturday, May 16th, 2009, 8:00 p.m.
St. Ignatius Loyola
980 Park Avenue (at East 84th Street)
Tickets - $20 in advance, $25 at the door

Hope to see you there!  And for the Messiaen fans out there, there WILL be an ondes Martenot in the orchestra   :mrgreen:
