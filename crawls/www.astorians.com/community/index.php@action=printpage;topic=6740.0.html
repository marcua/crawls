Title: 19A Diverted for St. Antonio Abate Street Fair
Post by: Starwind51 on June 20, 2007, 04:36:59 PM
Starting today the 19A is being diverted between 31st Street and Steinway.  The temporary 31st Street stop (going towards Jackson Heights) is in front of Eckerds on the 31st Street side.  The bus will go along 21st Ave with no scheduled stops, so if you need an interim stop, discuss it with the bus driver.  If you need to stop at Steinway (going towards LIC) you will have to get off at the stop before.
