Post by: GreenEyedGirl on February 06, 2007, 06:52:05 AM
Hmmmm... everyone always raves about American Eye Care, but I had a pretty bad experience there. (That is why I am looking for new eye doc in my other post.) When I was pregnant, I noticed that my vision changed and my glasses and contacts weren't doing the job that they used to. (I consulted with my OB and they said that pregnancy hormones and water weight gain sometimes cause vision changes.) The doctor at American Eye Care insisted that this wasn't true and there was no way my vision changed that much, even though he did all the eye tests and saw that there was a change. I was literally arguing with him about it. He was pretty obnoxious about it and treated me like I was crazy. At any rate, I ended up having crappy vision all through my pregnancy because of this place, when it could have been corrected. I decided I would never go back there.

On the insurance thing-you just pay the bill with your credit card or cash. I have eye coverage, but I still pay the bill and then submit it to my insurance later.
