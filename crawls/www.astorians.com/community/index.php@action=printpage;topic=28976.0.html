Title: Grantwriting Essentials: LOIs & Artist Statements
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on March 10, 2015, 11:29:39 AM
Join Queens Council on the Arts Wed, March 18th & 25th as Courtney Harge gives insights on the “Best Practices for Writing Letters of Inquiry and Artist Statements”, a hands-on BYOB Intensive workshop designed for artists who wish to better communicate about their work.

Participants in this workshop will not only learn how to write clearer, more concise letters and statements, but also will learn tips and tricks to think more clearly about their process. This workshop will cover creating a strong opening paragraph, answering all of the questions included in a request, creating a context that connects with your work samples, how to avoid repetition, and other tools of the trade.
