We tried Vest last night for dinner. Very nice!

We arrived a little before six. There were a couple of other tables occupied and a nice
atmosphere. We were seated right away and given menus, water, etc.

I had the cracked wheat salad and the roasted chicken. Both delicious. Chicken wasn't hot
enough, but they were great about taking it back and it was perfect on the second try.

Boyfriend had calamari and lasagne. He loved both. He's a calamari connoisseur and he
ranks this up there with Mojave - theirs is great. His lasagne was piping hot and looked/tasted
homemade. He's pretty picky and gave it two thumbs up.

Only drawback - no Corona on their beer menu and then they were out of Heineken.
He went with Amstel light and that was fine. I had Pinot Grigio which was great.

Overall - very nice place with good food and a friendly feel to it. We've already decided to try
stopping in for a drink after work. The bar looks comfortable and it's so close to both our homes.

Hope it works for them in this economic climate.
