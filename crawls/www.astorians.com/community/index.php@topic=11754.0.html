best pizza in astoria?
« on: August 11, 2008, 01:38:10 PM »

I guess it's impossible for one person to have tried every single pizza joint in astoria, but of all the pizza places I have been to, my vote goes to Sac's on broadway and 29th street.

they have these amazing marinara slices with such a nice crispy crust.
and I have to say I am a little biased towards Sacs and their marinara slices since I am just slightly lactose intolerant.
but their regular slices are kick-ass as well!

an honorable mention goes to Rizzo's on steinway and their paper-thin slices, which also have great sauce. not to mention the really nice peeps who work there.
