The wife and I finally ate here last week.  Our experience was the complete opposite of what I've read.  This was the worst dining experience we've had in years!  We both chose a different type broth but found them equally distasteful.  The broth was more akin to mopwater then a stock to feed humans.  The noodles were undercooked and the pork was grey, fatty, and nasty.  Oddly enough the gyoza was excellent.

You couldn't pay me to get me back in this place.
