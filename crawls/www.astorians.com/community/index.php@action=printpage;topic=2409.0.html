Title: Directions to Green Thumb Farm
Post by: kickingchicken on June 21, 2006, 12:23:51 PM
Hi all,

Before yall head out in droves to Green Thumb Farm this Saturday for strawberry picking and farm tour, you need to call the farm in advance to let them know how many are in your party. 

631-726-1900

The 10:30AM already has 11 families that have signed up.  1pm has 4 families signed up so far.  There is no quota, but just to let you know that most people seem to be leaning towards the morning tour.

These are the driving directions and train directions I got over the phone.  (I'm not a driver, so I hope I wrote them down right!)  It's about a 2 hour drive to the farm.

From Queens:

Get onto the Long Island Expressway.  Go to Exit 70S.  Follow South (bear right) and get onto Route 27 Sunrise Highway.  It becomes Country Road 39.  Follow to the end.  There will be a traffic light, with a car dealership nearby.  Turn left at the light onto Montauk Highway, Route 27.  Pass the Princess Diner on your right.  Go through the town of Water Mill.  There will be a windmill on your right.  Go a quarter of a mile east, and on the right hand side will be the farm.

By Train:

Take the Long Island Railroad to Southampton.  Take a cab from Southampton to Green Thumb Farm, in Water Mill.  If the driver doesn't know where it is, say that it's a quarter of a mile east on Montauk Highway.  It will cost about $10-15 one way.  The woman with whom I spoke kindly said that she could drive people back to the train station from the farm.

I don't know how many people will be in my party, so I don't know how I'll get there.  I'm leaning towards the public transportation. 

Just an FYI from your friendly neighbor,
