Title: Re: Nice Architecture/Bldgs
Post by: Frappe on February 07, 2007, 12:15:07 PM
I love Astoria, but I can appreciate the comments about its architecture.  Best area I know of outside Brooklyn is 44th Drive on the other side of the bridge.  Super charming, staid string of well proportioned brownstones, you'd think you're in the north slope.

The home pricing there is high, but I dont think it has topped out, so they might not be a bad investment.

One of the most architectually pleasant areas, if not the most sophisiticated, was 36th Street just south of 30th Avenue.  For three quarters of a block such pretty well balanced housing, with even sightlines, front gardens.  Unfortunately, a developer built out to the maximum a couople of years ago, and now its only a matter of time before the eyesores remove any sense of charm the house might have had.  Same will happen to Ditmars between Crescent and 29th Streets.
 
On the west side of 21st street, just south of Astoria Park, has some scattered gems.
