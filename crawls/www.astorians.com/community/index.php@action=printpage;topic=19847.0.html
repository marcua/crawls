Title: Steinway Piano Factory Tour
Post by: greentiger on November 22, 2010, 11:24:44 AM
The Greater Astoria Historical Society is hosting The Steinway Piano Factory Tour on Monday, November 29, 2010 at 9:00 AM. This is an exclusive behind the scenes look at how a piano is made. Donation is $25 per person payable to “The Greater Astoria Historical Society” All payments in advance – no money will be collected at the factory. The tour is limited to 20 people.

The tour will be at the factory on Steinway Place (38th St. and 19th Ave.) and starts at 9:15 AM and ends around Noon.  Please arrive at 9:00 AM. The factory is quite large and includes walking 3-4 flights of stairs so wear comfortable shoes. info@astorialic.org or call 718-278-0700
