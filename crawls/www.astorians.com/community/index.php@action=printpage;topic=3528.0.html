Well, you can't. Netflix chooses to use the usps as their shipper. However, I think that the problems that a lot of people have with their mail has to do with individual mail carriers, so if you wanted to invest in a PO Box, you can do so for about $6 a month (http://www.usps.com/receive/businesssolutions/poboxfees.htm). The boxes that are 1.3" x 5.5 " should be able to fit a Netflix envelope.

I know, you'll still have to go to the Post Office to pick up your mail and that's inconvienent, but unless Netflix starts using another shipper (which they probably never will), you'll have to work with the Post Office.
