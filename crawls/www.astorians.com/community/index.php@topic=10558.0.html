I think the Fairway would be nice.  But I would be happy with Gristedes or a Food Emporium as well.  And would love a Whole Foods - and Trader Joe's would also be great.  Best Yet is a very good market.  Great selection of produce, seafood and groceries.  But I agree that we could use a standard more American market if you will - more in the heart of Astoria.  Like where the Euro Market is.   The Trade Fairs and Key Foods are okay but don't hold a candle to a really nice market.

I agree with his statement about the Water Taxi slip.  It would be nice if something like that were incorporated into the Costco property.  The one near Water Taxi Beach is too far away for Astorians.  I guess the Astoria waterfront is probably also too far for most Astorians.  And they certainly won't put it by Astoria Park.  The N, W and 7 will start to look like the L in a few years.  And freaking out about development is not the answer.  More mass transit options is the right answer.
