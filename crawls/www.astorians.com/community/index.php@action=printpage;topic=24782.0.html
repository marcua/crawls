Title: Cordwainers--shoe and leather repair
Post by: StephanieL on April 22, 2013, 01:57:23 PM
I wanted to start a separate thread rather than post in my thread for leather repair recommendations.  I ended giving my leather coat to Cordwainers on Ditmars Boulevard.  They not only do shoe repair, but all types of leather work.  I read about them on Nancy Ruhling's blog and decided to get them a shot.  Well, they did a great job.  It was a nasty tear and they made it practically as good as new.  There's a small "scar" but I expected that, and it's pretty small to see.  They charged $50 and delivered it to my office in Manhattan for free.  I'd definitely use them again.
