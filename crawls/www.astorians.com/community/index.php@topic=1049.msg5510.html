Manhattan-style deli and buffet?
« Reply #2 on: November 03, 2005, 11:58:56 AM »
I don't know what part of Astoria you're in, but the food place attached to the Rock on 31st just off of Ditmars offers one of those "build your own salad" bars.  It's not super cheap, though.

I really like the souvlaki lady on the corner of Ditmars and 33rd, right by the Thessaloniki jewlers, for cheap eats.  A chicken or pork souvlaki on a stick is $2, and on a pita with all the toppings and sauces, it's $3.  If you get it on a pita, it's easily a full meal.

Buon Appetit deli makes very large sandwiches for about $5.50, and you can get the same filling on a large bed of greens for $6.50, I think.  Each sandwich or salad lasts me two meals, plus a soda or chips or whatever for another dollar or so.
