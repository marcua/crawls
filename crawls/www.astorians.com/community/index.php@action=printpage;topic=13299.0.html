Yes, I do. But now that I've gotten older, I have a list of requirements.

1) 20 pounds or less
2) spayed/neutered
3) wee-wee pad or newspaper trained
4) socialized
5) no food aggression
6) Must be up-to-date on all vaccinations
7) Should be licensed (ASPCA)

Fee for Astorians is always negotiable, but usually I don't charge for short periods of time.
