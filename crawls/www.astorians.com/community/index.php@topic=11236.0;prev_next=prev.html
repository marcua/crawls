Went up in the balloon (twice) in Central Park, very cool.  The wait on Tuesday was about 2 hours (got there at 10 am), went back the next morning at 7 am and waited about 45 minutes.  It's 25 bucks for about 8 minutes, which is way cheaper than what Mr. Spitzer spent.  Seriously though, you will get a view of the Park which not many people have seen.  It's located over by West 72nd street on Cherry Hill.

Re: Central Park Balloon Ride

It's 25 per person.  The basket holds 4 people (plus 1 operator).  When it starts to get windy (it doesn't take much), they go to 3 people + 1 operator; more wind they go to 2 people + 1 operator.  If you go, just count the number of people in front of you on line and you can see how long your wait will be.  They let media on at the front of the line and people that made reservations online (you can't reserve anymore), but the girl at the desk said that 80% of the people that made reservations don't show.  Oh, one more thing - if you are the next to go up and the wind increases and they cancel all the flights for the rest of the day, you are out of luck.  They stressed that to us when we were waiting. If you can, get there very early, the first ride is at 0700, so if you show at 0630 you should be fine.  I think they operate until August 22 or somewhere around that date.  The balloon really spins and moves with the smallest of breezes, so if you get motion sickness....
