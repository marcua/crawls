Title: Re: RECIPES for week 6
Post by: enigmacat on July 18, 2006, 01:47:03 PM
Radicchio is good split into quarters, coated with olive oil and grilled.

You can also separate the leaves from the head and put 1 tablespoon of blue cheese and a bit of toasted walnut in each leaf. Broil for about 5 minutes, until the leaves wilt, and then roll them up and secure with toothpicks. Serve with red grapes. Yum.

It also makes a great stirfry with snow peas and olive oil - I think anzubird showed me that. The sweetness of the peas balances the bitterness of the raddichio.
