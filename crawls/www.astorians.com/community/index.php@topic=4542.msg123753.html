Bravo! I had a life changing experience many years ago. I got all stressed out because my division was lagging on a fixed deadline for a major project. I decided to take an offensive posture rather than wait for my needing to take the defensive. I made an appointment with the (then) CEO of my major Fortune 100 company. I made my case in person, and he was wonderful about it. We hugged, and as I was leaving his office, he said, "Ahem . . .
