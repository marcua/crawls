Title: Team Trivia League
Post by: NYC Social Sports Club on November 05, 2010, 04:09:43 PM
Join NYC Social Sports Club for a 7-week Team Trivia league.

Teams of 4-6 players compete weekly in a 50-question quiz that includes Audio, Visual, and Social rounds.  This league has been running for a year in Manhattan...this is the first foray into Queens.  We're planning on having a fantastic time building a new community of fun people, Trivia lovers, and folks who just want to get together and party!

January 25th - March 8
Tuesdays at Studio Square (inside, of course)
Game Time - 7:30

Registration is $50 and includes team-color t-shirts, online schedules & standings,
and drink specials - Blue Moon pitchers are $12; all other pitchers are $14!

You may register with a full team (4-6 players), a friend or two, or as a Free Agent.  We match many players up before the season...a great way to meet people.

Register today!
