Title: Singer/Songwriter Pete Tufel and Band at the Sidewalk Cafe

Singer/Songwriter Pete Tufel will be performing with his band at the Sidewalk Cafe (Avenue A & 6th Street) this Saturday, October 16 at 8pm.  There is no cover charge.

Pete and the boys (Moppa, Owen, & Brian) are Astoria residents, who play an exciting brand of roots, folk, & alternative rock n roll--all original music.  The lineup is:

Pete- Guitar & vocals
Moppa Elliot-Upright & electric bass & vocals
Owen O'Mahony-lead/rhythm guitar
Brian Czach-Drums/percussion

Check out the website for pics & music:

www.petetufel.com

Thanks for your support!
