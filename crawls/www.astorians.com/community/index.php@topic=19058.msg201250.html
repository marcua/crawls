Location:  20th AVE and 20th Street

The story so far:

SUV meet car.

Car meet brick-framed metal garage doors.

Estimated damage:  SUV required a flatbed to haul it away,  Car bound for the compactor,  two garage bays trashed.  Contents of garages unknown.

No apparent personnel casualties.

Note:  This is the second time this year an accident has sent a vehicle screaming into the garages that line 20th Avenue west of 21st Street.  This is also the stretch of road where a motorcyclist died about a year or so ago.
