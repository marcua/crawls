Title: Greater Astoria Historical Society Walking Tour - Mott Haven
Post by: greentiger on March 16, 2013, 04:23:01 PM
Saturday, March 23, 2013
Join Forgotten New York‘s Kevin Walsh and GAHS’ Rich Melnick through Mott Haven and see its collection of former piano factories, iron works, eclectic architecture and historic St. Ann‘s Church and its churchyard, where a signer of the Declaration of Independence is laid to rest.
 
Start Time: 12 PM Noon
 
Start Location: Meet at the 3rd Avenue #6 train station outside 40th Precinct Building, Alexander Avenue and East 138th Street.
Time: approx. 3 HRS
 
Fee: GAHS members $15, $20 non-members
