Okay, so you can all laugh at me for this gripe. I'm a social worker, so I get this is a ridiculous thing to complain about, but almost all the Diet Coke and Diet Pepsi in Astoria is past, or just nearing, it's expiration date! Oh well, I'll just carry my addiction soda in from Manhattan where I work.  I otherwise love my newish neighborhood around 21st street/astoria blvrd (Have been there about 14 months now) but this is my ONE gripe.
