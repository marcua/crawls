Title: Protest in NYC to Stop Worker Abuse - Friday 3/30
Post by: Sambarella on March 29, 2007, 04:58:18 PM
EVENT NOTICE: MARCH 30 WORKERS’ RIGHTS BOARD PUBLIC
HEARING IN NYC TO SUPPORT WORKERS AT SMITHFIELD HOG PROCESSING PLANT

On March 30, Help Kick Off a National Week of Action To Stop Worker Abuse:

SMITHFIELD WORKERS’ RIGHTS BOARD PUBLIC HEARING IN NEW YORK CITY

A national campaign is underway to support the 5,500 workers who workat the Smithfield operation in Tar Heel, NC — the world’s largesthog processing plant. Each day on the job these workers endure poverty wages, brutal conditions and crippling injuries. Conditions there represent a national disgrace. The Smithfiled company is breaking the law to stop a unionization drive. The company’s behavioir has been cited by Human Rights Watch for violating international human rights standards.

Come hear about Smithfield’s USE OF INTIMIDATION and IMMIGRATION
RAIDS from workers who seek a voice on the job.

when: 11:00 AM to 12:30 PM on Friday, March 30

where: THE COMMUNITY CHURCH OF NEW YORK
40 East 35th Street (between 35th Street & Park Avenue)
Manhattan
