Title: Re: Light Meter
Post by: jwphotonyc on June 22, 2008, 11:09:23 AM
What and how you're shootin defines what you need. 

Personally, i love my sekonic L-358, as it does it all.

You can check out the used sections at B&H or Adorama--the clerks are really knowledgeable and can probably steer you in the right direction for how you'll be using it.  Plus, they'll probably have something that'll match your camera very nicely.

Sunny 16--if you're shootin outside on a sunny day, use F16 and your film speed as the shutter speed.  Shady F5.6 & film speed as shutter speed.  Those are estimates, remember.
