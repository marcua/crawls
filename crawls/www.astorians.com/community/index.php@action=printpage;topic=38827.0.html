Take Root presents Cat Wagner / Collaborations and DanceAction
 
 Cat Wagner / Collaborations: Welcome to the Real Rosie Radio Hour!  Set within an old-fashioned radio show, “Rosie, Really.” distorts Rosie the Riveter as a cultural feminist icon.  Two women dressed as Rosie Riveter characters dance and sing their way through a collective identity crisis and propose a wide array of women's roles and representations in American popular culture.
 
 DanceAction: Game Night is a dance work that explores the behavior of human beings in relationship with the elements of chance and of choice. People meet and interact; their relationship takes shape according to the circumstances, as well as the characteristics of each one of the individuals, and their personal choices. This piece explores how the element of chance affects human relationships, creating somehow the perfect configuration for a specific form of interaction between two people. Following this idea, this work is based on Board Games, the rules of the games and once again the elements of choice and chance present at a game.
