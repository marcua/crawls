It's really easy:
-Bring books that you want to give away or lend. (If you want the book back, put your contact info inside the cover.)
-Snag books that other people bring. (If someone wants a book back, arrange to return it at a future swap.)
-After the swap is over, we donate any remaining/unwanted books to a local thrift shop, one block away.
-Hang out, talk about what you're reading, have fun!
