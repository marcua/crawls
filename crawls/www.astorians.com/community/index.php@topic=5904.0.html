Cronin and Phelan is great for food but I don't really think of it as a bar to hang out in. But that is just my opinion. That being said they have awesome shepard's pie and good pints. The brunch is good too. I am not a big sports person- but I can tell you they have a lot of tv's and there are always sports on in there.

Doyle's Corner is ok. I've been there a few times. I prefer The Irish Rover and the Quays. I live over on lower Broadway and when its nice out don't mind schlepping over to either location. I love Barry the bartender/owner of the Quays/Pulpit and Jerry the bartender from the Irish Rover is pretty awesome as well. Both places are great about buy backs and have a decent jukebox.
