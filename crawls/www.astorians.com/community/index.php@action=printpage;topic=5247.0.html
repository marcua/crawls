Title: Re: Waxing / sugaring places for guys?
Post by: megc on February 18, 2007, 07:12:30 PM
Sugaring differs from waxing, in that it only sticks to the hairs, whereas waxing sticks to  the hair and the skin.  Therefore, it's less painful.  It's also better environmentally, as sugar is a biodegradable, natural product; wax often contains mineral oil which is a petroleum product, a byproduct of gasoline distillation.  It also cuts down on razor waste (2 billion disposable razors end up in landfills each year).

For more info:  Sugaring (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sugaring_%28epilation%29).
