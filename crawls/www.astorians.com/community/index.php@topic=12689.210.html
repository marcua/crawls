I went with my wife last week to try out Vesta on our 'date night'.  The staff was super friendly, and we liked the ambiance but the food didn't measure up.  I liked the people and space so much that we will give them another try, but what we had that night was not good.

The cracker pizza was deplorable.  That crust was like eating pizza on Matzoh.  I ordered a fish entree that was so dry I could not eat it.  But as I said, the staff was super nice and handled my disatisfaction perfectly.  I didn't ask for them to redo it (I was full) and expected nothing, but they comped all our wine and this was greatly appreciated.

It is amazing how proper costumer service can make up for such a subpar meal.  The food was not good, but we had a nice time.

Until we return to Vesta we will stick to L'Incontro for a different but truely amazing Italian dinner.  If you watch how you order it isn't much more expensive either.
