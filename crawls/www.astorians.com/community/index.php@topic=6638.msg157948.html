I never knew the "Astoria Park Tavern".  I always assumed it was a restaurant.  I've been by there a million times, but never went in.   I haven't been down the block in several years at least but there used to be a little neighborhood place on Ditmars just west of 21st Street.   Is that place still there?

I also got off the train on Sunday at the 36th Avenue stop to walk over to the multi-plex.   I still think about that bar that had been on the northwest corner of 31st St. and 36th Ave.   One day I walked by and it was gone.   You walk by there now and there is no trace that a bar existed on that spot.   The supermarket must have expanded and taken up that space.

And another long-tme Astoria bar is still going strong on 36th Street -- Veronica's.   I can never remember the exact cross streets, but it is around 36th, 37th Street.  That place reminds me of Kelly's, that bar on the corner of Crescent and 31st Avenue.
