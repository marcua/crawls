It looks like their opening is imminent.  They are stocking the store today - lots of produce is already in place.  They are going for a warm, open look - warm wood floors, square bins with pyramids of fruit and vegetables placed throughout the middle of the store - it's not cramped.  I saw boxes with produce labeled from New Jersey, Mexico and Chile (didn't see anything organic, but I only saw a few boxes).  They also had stacks of bags of grilling charcoal in the window, and a long glass case along the left wall that looks like it may be a deli counter (although I didn't look that carefully).  I also saw boxes of Evian and Vitamin Water.

I saw someone earlier with a flier (just a regular store flier with prices of various goods rather than one specifically announcing the opening), although I didn't see any evidence of fliers at the store itself.  But it looks like they will be open by the weekend if not sooner.
