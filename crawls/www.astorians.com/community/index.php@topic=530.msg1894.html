I enjoy listening to John Sterling's commentary. I listen to the games on WCBS a lot since I don't have YES or ESPN. I think John's corniness (and sometimes just plain weirdness) is off-putting to some people, but for me it makes listening to the game on the radio more entertaining. I think Suzyn does well to balance him out.

Some Sterlingisms that amuse me...

"It is high! It is far! It iiiiiiiis....... caught at the wall"
"A thrilla from Godzilla!"
"Back to back, and a belly to belly" (when back to back HRs are hit)
And of course: "Yankees win! Thuuuuuuuuuuuuu Yankees win!"

Also, whenever it's a clear, sunny day he makes a point of referring to the sky as "azure blue"
