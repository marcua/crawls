Crescent and Vine on Ditmars and Crescent is a splendid wine, beer and cheese bar. Winegasm is hit and miss. I've been there about a half dozen times. Half of those experiences were absolutely amazing, and the other half were very disappointing.

I think it boils down to the following: if the owner/ manager is working and wants to spend time on your table, then you're in for a treat. Special wines that they just got in, amuse bouche for tasting, excellent pairing ideas. Otherwise, it's pretty much just choose your wine and your app and that's that. The decor is pretty nice, but the street view is wanting.

DiWine can be similar, but I've had a lot of luck sitting at the bar earlier in the evening. The bartender(s) are rather keen to try out new cocktails -- and they're often pretty good.

I'd add Brick to the list, it's not a "wine bar" but the bar has a decent wine selection, and they have a couple decent cheese plates.
