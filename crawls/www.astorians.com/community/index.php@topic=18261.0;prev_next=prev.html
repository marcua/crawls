Taking the train, Borough Hall stop has a ton of choices.  One of my favorites is getting Vietnamese sandwiches and bubble tea from

Hanco's
85 Bergen Street
(between Smith St & Hoyt St)
Brooklyn, NY 11201

One of the last time's I was in Ikea, I tried the food for the first time and for the price point, it wasn't bad.  I split a meatballs platter and princess cake.  It was a Tuesday so I paid a total of $3.50 including coffee.  If you want to be adventurous, you should give it a try and if your not satisfied, it won't break the bank and you could get something else around the area.

Hope it's not too late, but I can highly recommend the lobster roll from Red Hook Lobster Pound, 284 Van Brunt St, tel: (646) 326 7650, from 12-7, and for dessert a swingle (mini key lime pie covered in dark chocolate) from Steve's Authentic Key Lime Pie, 204 Van Dyke Street, tel: (718) 858-5333.
In case you're still there after 5.30pm you can head over to this place for dinner: The Good Fork, 391 Van Brunt Street, tel: (718) 643 6636. Love this place!
Enjoy!

I almost forgot... you can have excellent latin-american food (tacos, huarache, pupusas and more) at the red hook ball fields: bay st at clinton st. Cheap and delicious.
