I think you should seek to help, but you may want to be careful in the way you do it. The best way to help may be to call a homeless organization and then make a donation to them. You just don't want to get into a situation where he lives outside your window solely because he knows you'll take care of him when he may need access to a lot of other things like health care, mental care, etc.

I am all for helping, but I favor being cautious in that kind of situation too.
