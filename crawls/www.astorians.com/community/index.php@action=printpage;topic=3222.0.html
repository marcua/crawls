Post by: lanseaux on August 30, 2006, 10:43:18 PM
I love Casa Mono.  I've been a bunch of times, had reservations some of those times.  Despite the crowding, if you go at an off hour or are willing to sit at the bar, you can almost always get a seat.  Fantastic wine list, and the tapas orientation of the menu lets you try a nice assortment of dishes.  If you - or anyone - go there, get the duck egg, served sunny side up, with cured tuna loin and truffles over roasted fingerling potatoes.  Truly an oh.my.god kind of dish.
