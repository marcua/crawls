Re: Milkflower
« Reply #20 on: October 13, 2013, 11:20:44 PM »
Positives:
- Outstanding quality. Blows everything else in Astoria if not NYC out of the water. If your idea of pizza is half a pound of mozzarella, a half gallon of red sauce out of a jar and a pool of oil, go to any old pizza joint. You won't like this.
- Terrific starters. Do not skip them.


Negatives:
- Insanely stingy on ingredients on some pizzas. Arugula pizza had 6 or 8 little arugula leaves arranged so there's one on each slice - that is, about 5 cents worth of arugula. That's what you do with truffles that cost $150 a pound.   There was zero detectable truffle oil on the pizza that was supposed to have it. I call bullsht.
- Several pizzas are bland. I love subtle foods but there's a difference between subtle and bland. I've been a few times and still no pizza had any herb or black pepper.
Summary:
- Definitely worth a try. But next time I'm sending it back if they skimp on ingredients.
