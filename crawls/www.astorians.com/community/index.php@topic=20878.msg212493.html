Abandoned vehicles can be reported to the city: www.nyc.gov/html/dsny/html/collection/vehicles.shtml

The Department of Sanitation encourages you to help keep NYC clean by reporting abandoned vehicles.

Abandoned vehicles may be reported by calling the New York City Citizen Service Center at 3-1-1.

When calling to report an abandoned vehicle, be prepared to give the location of the vehicle and its description including the make, type (2 door, 4 door, station wagon, etc.) and color.

Abandoned vehicles without license plates may also be reported by submitting a Derelict Vehicle Removal Request. http://www.nyc.gov/html/dsny/html/contact/vehicle_form.shtml

Abandoned vehicles with license plates may also be reported to your local police precinct.
