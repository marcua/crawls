Title: Single Cut Brewery - Bad Beer, Worse Attitude
Post by: Yaxpac on December 06, 2013, 09:25:23 AM
So, I had been very excited when the news about Single Cut Brewery made the rounds.   I had some guests in town over the holidays so I decided to take them over to Single Cut.  What a mistake.   The attitude of the staff there was so better than thou that we left half filled beer glasses and walked out.   The beer was not very good either which made the decision to leave that much easier. 


We all got flights of various beers.   2/5 were flat, and the other 3 were just pretty much tasteless and watery.  Not one beer had lacing.  I should add that the bartender (if that is what you call the guy there) spilled almost every glass and just handed us a dripping wet tray of beer.   the whole experience was a joke.   It was like they were doing us a favor by serving us their beer.   


To top it off, we took their tour which was rather lame but the kicker was when the tourguide started knocking local breweries (Kelso in particular) and saying that they had inferior product.   So unnecessary.   These places should support each other not knock them.  Kelso is far superior to Single Cut anyway. 


If you pay attention on the tour you will see that they do not use proprietary yeast like they say they do and they order hops (non-fresh, compressed pellets) as needed.   It is basically a beefed up home brew operation that uses poor/cheap ingredients.  They store their lager barrels in the hot main room when they should be in refrigeration.   


The prices for the growlers are through the roof too and work out to $4/beer.  directly from the brewery??


We left and went to MP Taverna where they had far superior beer and friendly bartenders.   

This place does not know what they are doing or how to make beer and their customer service sucks.  It exudes pretentiousness and...I hate to use the word but it fits...is complete hipster garbage.  This is Queens, not Brooklyn.
