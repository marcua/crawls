Frank Sinatra School Of The Arts-2nd Annual Alumni Concert
Friday January 7, 2011 from 7:00pm - 9:00pm
Tony Bennett Concert Hall-Frank Sinatra School Of The Arts
3512 35th Ave
Astoria, New York 11103
http://maps.yahoo.com/dd?taddr=3512+35th+Ave&tcsz=Astoria%2CNY%2C11103&tcountry=us

Since its first Graduating class in 2004, alumni from the Frank Sinatra School Of The Arts in Astoria, Queens have gone on to pursue careers in Vocal and Instrumental music, Classical and Modern dance, theatre, Opera, Comedy, Visual Arts and more. Come watch as a select group of alumni perform a concert in the state of the art Tony Bennett Concert Hall at the Frank Sinatra School Of The Arts, located on 35th street and 35th Avenue in Astoria, NY. Easily accessible by car, by bus (Q101, Q66), or by subway (Yellow line-R, Orange line-M). Tickets are only $12 for adults and $7 with a student i.d. Tickets can be purchased online at:
