after being a fan of Rizzo's and Sac's the past few years, my new favorite is Lorusso's.

Finally checked out Sac's, after wanting to go there for years.  Not impressed, though could be persuaded that our mediocre (at best) pizza was at least in part our own ordering mistake.  We got a Grandma's Old Fashioned pizza and added garlic and fried eggplant.  There just wasn't one part of it that was good - too much (flavorless) cheese, too little sauce, the fried eggplant pieces seemed "re-purposed", and flavorless too, and the garlic was just some chopped up raw garlic seemingly tossed on (overloaded) at the last second.  A doughy and boring crust too (though not burned as someone else suggested).
 
Could there be a delicious pie that would have come out of that same kitchen?  Did we miss that?
