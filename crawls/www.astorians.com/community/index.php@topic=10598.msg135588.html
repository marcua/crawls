I went back last week for more take-out.  Bypassed the appetizers and ordered lamb vindaloo and a side of dal.  The vindaloo sauce was VERY delicious, as complex as the green chili dish I'd ordered last time; not as spicy as I'd hoped but spicier than most NYC Indian places will make it.  The lamb was kind of tough, but flavorful.  I'll probably stick with chicken next time, or try one of their shrimp or fish curries.  The dal was the most delicious dal I'd ever tasted.  They put a couple pieces of tomato in it, but they were easily removed and they'd kept their structural integrity rather than leeching their tomato-iness into the dal.  If you're accustomed to the bland, I'll-eat-this-because-it's-legumes-and-it's-good-for-me dal that most places serve, you'll be surprised.

So I was happy with it again.  And on my way out I ran into Leah, so that was another plus.
