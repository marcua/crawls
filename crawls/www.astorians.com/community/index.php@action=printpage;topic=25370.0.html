Title: Italian Classes for Kids
Post by: veatnomeat on August 24, 2013, 09:27:14 PM
Starting September 28, the Cultural Association for the Molise Region is starting total immersion Italian classes with hands-on activities for kids.  The classes are taught by a master teacher and native Italian speaker.  The classes will be held on Saturdays from 9 to 10 am and the cost is $150 for 10 one hour sessions.  The address is 31-16 36th Avenue, Long Island City (right off of the 36th Avenue stop on the Q/N line.)


My husband has been taking Italian here for the last few years and the instructors are wonderful.  We're looking forward to having our kids take the class and thought I would post it here to see if any other parents are interested.  For more info, you can check out their website camr-uco.org where you can also register...
