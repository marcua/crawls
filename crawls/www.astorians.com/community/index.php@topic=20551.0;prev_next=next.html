I've ordered from Peapod two previous times and am waiting on my third order to arrive this week.

I can confirm there is now a $60 order minimum.

The first time I was able to use the $15 discount and the second time I found a code online for $5 off (better than nothing I suppose).

There was a new code I just found to discount my third order as well (just google peapod coupon codes).  Keep in mind the free delivery for 60 days after your first order only works with a minimum order of $100 so it's a little misleading.

One more caveat, their delivery windows are NOT guaranteed.  I once almost missed a delivery because I had a friend picking me up for an engagement 20 mins past my delivery window and was told on the phone with customer service that I would be charged a penalty if I was not present even after the delivery window.  Luckily the truck showed up and I didn't have to deal with that situation.

Anyone else have experiences?  Please do share.
