It usually depends.  Some seats you can tell how many people will fit.  The older trains with the obvious seats are simple to figure out, but the N trains usually fit 6 "average" sized people.  By average, I mean people who aren't HUGE.  Sometimes I get in and look at the seat and if there are 5 people, I'll find the biggest little space and say "excuse me" and start to sit down.  Usually, it opens up.

However, if 5 of those 6 people are small, it might appear as though someone else could fit, but they really couldn't comfortably and I don't bother.

Totally depends on the situation.  I'd say the rule of thumb is, if you're not going to be more comfortable sitting than standing, don't sit.
