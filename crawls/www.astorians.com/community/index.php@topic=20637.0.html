Went here for the second time last night.  Just can't believe how good it is. Phenomenal house made pastas. The Pork rib ragu and the Alla matriciana were out of this world. Quiet and pleasant dining room plus thoughtful and charming waitstaff only added to a great night out.

Four things stood out for me.
The waiter, Sergio, knew the ingredients and history of the food.
Salt was never over used.
Spice never stood out more than intended. 
Care during prep and cooking of recipes really shined.

Went tonight, and my wife and I both really enjoyed it. Had the penne con pollo which was very rich and flavorful; creamy with sun dried tomatoes, peas, asparagus, etc. She had linguine with clams...had the chocolate eggplant dessert which was quite unique. Owner was very friendly without being annoying; asked how we heard about the place, and let us know about the weekly specials, where we lived, and hoped we came back. Really made us feel welcome. Place was quite crowded; lots of people there seemed like regulars. Glasses of wine are absolutely huge. As far as prices go, I found it reasonable. Two people with apps, drinks, and dessert (and including tip) was less than $100. not cheap, but a nice place for a night out. hope that they find continued success here.
