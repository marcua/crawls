Title: seasonal deliciousness
Post by: megc on October 30, 2009, 09:11:42 PM
Throughout the year, so many tasty things appear in conjunction with a particular season or holiday.  It's fall now, and a day before Halloween.  Pumpkin pie has my number these days.  I discovered this afternoon that Shake Shack has a seasonal dessert, "Pumpkin Pie Oh-My (http://newyork.seriouseats.com/2009/10/sugar-rush-pumpkin-pie-oh-my-shake-shack.html)" and it is seriously good.  I tried it tonight and was really happy I did.  It's basically vanilla frozen custard impaled with a slice of pumpkin pie, then mixed together, and topped with a pile of (real) whipped cream.  So simple and so good.  One cup of it is enough for two - or even three - easily.

Run, don't walk, to get it! 8-)
(it's good prefaced by a Shack burger and fries, too.)
