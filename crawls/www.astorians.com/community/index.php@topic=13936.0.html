First of all although Blvd. Gardens mailing address is Woodside, if you cross the street you are in astoria - hence calling it astoria (not to mention the Q18 bus that you would take up and down 30th avenue has a stop there and people who live there use steinway and the rest of astoria as their neighborhood).

Second, i think thanos made it seem like it is in the ghetto. Yes the projects are close but with astoria projects, ravenswood, woodside projects, queensbridge and the terrace low income housing (or at least it used to be) most everyone in astoria can say they live near the projects.

Blvd. Gardens is a wonderful place, a little community with private playground, gardening club, and lots of other perks. There are lots of kids that have the ability to run around and play outside which is something you rarely see these days. Yes, we have our problems, parking is a bitch, etc. but overall i love the sense of community and getting to look out of my windows and see grass and trees and children playing...and having the perks of "astoria" right down the block.
