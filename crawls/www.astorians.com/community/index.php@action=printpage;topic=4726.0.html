please join us for ACK - Astoria Crafty Knitty group every Thursday from 7pm (ish) to whenever at Freeze Peach Cafe (www.freezepeach.org). we've been known to close the the place on thursdays, but no one is obligated to stay a certain amount of time. it's just a relaxing nite being together with crafters. we just ask that you support the business by ordering a cup of coffee, tea, etc. we are very informal...show when you can, stay as long as you want.

we have a great little group going on of crocheters and knitters. we'd love for you to join us!!! check the calendar, i should post up our meetings every thursday... i'm an avid crocheter, i know how to knit basic stitches such as casting on, knit, purl, other stitches not so much. but i'm willing to learn from others. i'd like for this to be a group where we can learn from eachother and have a good time. be supportive.

pm me if you want to be on the ACK emailing list.
