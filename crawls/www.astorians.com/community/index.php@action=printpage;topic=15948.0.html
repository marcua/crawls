If I may jump in...Monthly maintenance is a combination of the actual common area maintenance costs and underlying building mortgage. The mortgage interest payment is tax deductible which would commonly comprise 35% to 65% of your total maintenance In the Coop you don't own the apartment but you own the actual building or complex...well, you are a partner.  Unlike a condominium that you get title and deed you will get shares and a proprietorial lease which basically gives you the right to occupy the apartment you live in.  Your Mortgage is totally separate than your maintenance and just like in any real estate property you will have the benefit of writting off your finance charges as well.
