Title: ** PARTICIPATE IN INS&OUTS MAGAZINE'S FALL PHOTO ESSAY! **
Post by: audreyLICmag on August 13, 2008, 05:29:56 PM
hey everyone!

it's audrey from ins&outs magazine, a long island city-based arts/music/culture quarterly print magazine (www.licmagazine.com). just inviting you to participate in the photo essay for our next issue: falling in love in LIC!

for our soon to be released vol.II, issue 2, we did a photo essay about falling in love in paris, featuring full-page photographs of lovers, artists, musicians, and general residents of paris with a quote about who or what they have fallen in love with, and why.

out of love for LIC, we decided to do the same thing but in queens, and ask our readers/LIC residents/EVERYONE (!) for help!

what do YOU love? your pet, your park, your view, your job, your art, your music, your favorite restaurant, the gantries, the pepsi sign, vernon blvd., the library, your family, friend, partner...? let us know!

feel free to participate -- the more, the better! send a short quote about what or who you love to stephanie (stephanie@licmagazine.com) ASAP :) we'll review the submissions and get in touch with our favorite stories to take a full-page photograph to be featured in our next issue.

please help us with this great project -- thanks so much!
