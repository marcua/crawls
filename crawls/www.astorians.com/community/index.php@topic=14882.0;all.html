Dr. Maria is very nice and has a good manner about her though.  I really wished her dentistry worked out for me because of it --it's rare to find a doctor/dentist that pleasant.  I just don't realize why everyone else I know has had a root canal completed in a few visits and mine has taken almost half a year. Also, I don't know why I never experienced pain from dental work until I started going there.
