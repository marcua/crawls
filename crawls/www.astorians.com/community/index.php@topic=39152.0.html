I think the streetcar is a great idea. Here are some reasons why:


1. 21st road is awful and it needs to be changed. The waterfront from Sunset Park to Astoria is rapidly changing and 50 years down the line, the waterfront will continue to develop, become nicer, and the population will increase. Putting a transportation line along the waterfront is forward-thinking and will fit in perfectly to the long-term development process.

2. There are currently ZERO transportation options, other than buses, available on the waterfront. This helps.

3. NYC is becoming less Manhattan-centric. As more neighborhoods are being developed in Brooklyn and Western Queens, more people will need to travel between the two boroughs to get to work. A streetcar like this is a great way to help this inevitable shift be more smooth.

4. A streetcar is super-cool and will draw lots of attention. It will draw more people to the neighborhoods.

5. Streetcars are easier to build than subway lines. It will happen sooner.

6. The waterfront along Bklyn and Queens is becoming wealthier. You can't stop this. You can try but you will fail.
