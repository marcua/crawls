Jennifer,

i just wanted to send a quick note about your post in regards to Little Angels. The gentleman who answers the phone is a very reserved person by nature. He is born in a different country, in a different time, and, no, he does not have the polite, cheerful phone manners that Americans expect. But he is really sweet. He is not the one running the daycare. His wife is, and she is the one who is a Montessori-certified teacher, with over 7 years of teaching and caring for children. However, her husband always helps with the strollers, with other little tasks of getting children in and out of the house. He is very gentle. My son is extremely suspicious and cautious by nature, but he loves both the teacher and her husband, asks about them when he is not there.

I recommend that you go to the open house and meet the teacher herself. It is a family daycare, and it may be possible to set the hours that are convenient for you. So far they have been very accommodating. But it is best to talk to them in person.

Also, if you have a Facebook account, you can look up Little Angels Montessori, Inc. there. You will see the pictures of their place, of the backyard.

I am not sure how old/young your son is, but for a small child, this is a very good option. your child will get individualized attention, but you will also get individualized attention. the teacher even helps with potty-training (if that is a concern).

Good luck!!
