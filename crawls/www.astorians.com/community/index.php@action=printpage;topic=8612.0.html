Title: Re: Visiting LA
Post by: Harlan on November 15, 2007, 01:51:28 AM
I'm in LA right now! (Well, Long Beach, now.) My sister and her husband live in Pasadena, where I was yesterday, then today we stopped briefly in Koreatown to see where she works, then went to Santa Monica. Spent the afternoon on the pier (cool, not crowded on a Wednesday afternoon), and on the 3rd St. promenade. It's sorta like the South St Seaport, but about 10 times larger, and not as historical. Now I'm in a hotel, facing away from the beach, preparing for 3 1/2 days of mostly work...

Oh, and apparently, downtown LA is fine during the day, but at night it's deserted, like downtown NYC used to be, and somewhat dangerous.
