Look at that stretch of Shore Blvd between Ditmars and 21st Ave. Yes, it's nice and quiet there, but if I saw a group of suspicious characters there, and a group of suspicious characters near a heavily trafficked main stretch of Shore Blvd, I'd be more comfortable walking by the main area of Shore Blvd.
