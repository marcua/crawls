Take Root presents Mersiha Mesihovic / CircuitDebris and Underground DanceWorks

Mersiha Mesihovic / CircuitDebris: Presents VOID, from a three part Suite entitled The Trilogy (of Survival).  The Trilogy examines the struggle against social conformity and the individual struggle for self-determination in our vastly standardized society that makes a constant effort in shutting down what is different or uncommon.
 
Underground DanceWorks: In a collection of diverse pieces, Artistic Director Charles Tyson Jr, seeks to embark on a journey through the soul and society - continuously searching, exploring forgiveness, regret, escape, acceptance, truth and answers - all things we as a society struggle with on a daily basis. Through his signature movement style known as EclectiFunk, Underground DanceWorks (UDW) shows that "in order to truly move forward, we must learn to acknowledge respect and embrace the differences that make our society such a unique one - make intelligent commentary without claiming to know the answer.  Being Quiet doesn't get the point home.  Just because the truth isn't pretty, doesn't make it any less the truth."
 
 January 22nd & 23rd, 2016 at 8pm
 Admission: $15
 Green Space Studio
 37-24 24th Street, #301
 Long Island City, NY 11101
 718-956-3037
 
 Tickets are available at the door or online at http://www.greenspacestudio.org/TakeRoot.html (http://www.greenspacestudio.org/TakeRoot.html)
 
 Take Root is a monthly curated series that supports dance makers by providing an opportunity to show a half evening work paired with another artist.
