Since 1988 the rent has gone up...TWICE. I'm paying $125 a month- granted it's 6 blocks away, but well worth it to keep the car safe and out of the street cleaning shuffle. Unfortunately, the owner passed away last year but his wife is staying and I (and my wife) do what we can for her- especially when it snows. Even pick up some Irish sausies for her once in a while from the Butcher Block.
