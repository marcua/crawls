I have definitely smelled "skunk weed" and it does smell a lot like a skunk. (BTW, other varieties of pot include the stuff that smells like sweet burning grass and the stuff that smells like armpits. I've never actually smoked any of it myself, but you get to know these things when you work night shifts at recording studios.) Incidentally, skunks are generally nocturnal so you're more likely to smell a skunk at night than during the day. If you smell this stuff at various times during the day or night, I would say it's skunk weed.
