We are done with the newborn diapers (and I have no idea where that package of preemie diapers came from), I was given a breast pump that I don't need, and then there's just those extra gifts - we just don't need 50 newborn onesies, or 7 blankets (though somehow my mom convinced me today to hang on to #6), or 85 bibs. 

I felt really bad today - my mom returned all the new baby clothes that she'd bought for us months ago - because I told her we had an over abundance already.  So today she showed up with a snowsuit and then I pulled an almost identical one out of the drawer that someone gave us last week.  People LOVE to shop for baby clothes b/c they're SOOO cute.  And then people LOVE to give (and receive) baby hand-me-downs -b/c most of those clothes are never worn more than a couple of times (if at all).  I think if baby gap (babies r us, etc) stopped selling clothes NOW - there would still be enough newborn clothes in circulation to keep babies in newish clothes for years.
