King Kong - the 1976 Dino De Laurentiis remake.

Look at the scene where Dwan (Jessica Lang) and Jack Prescott (Jeff Bridges) escape from Kong by running from Astoria Boulevard South on 33rd Street to the Astoria Boulevard (Hoyt Avenue) station (BMT line).  They run up the stairs to a subway that just pulls in.  They board the R train (that used to run on the Astoria line at the time before it was changed to the N train).

The empty lot in the background was a junk yard, later developed into a nightclub.
