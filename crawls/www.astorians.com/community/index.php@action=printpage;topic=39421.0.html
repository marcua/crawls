Title: Astoria Aroma, No More?
Post by: 28Grand on June 07, 2016, 09:46:57 AM
In case you missed this tidbit yesterday, the ever present, ever controversial Astoria Aroma has been abated, at least around Steinway and Ditmars (hopefully). The Ward's Island plant remains to be seen, or smelled.

As reported in Gothamist:

"The DEP and City Council Member Costa Constantinides announced today that the $3 million project to install "aluminum covers and odor control units" has been completed."
