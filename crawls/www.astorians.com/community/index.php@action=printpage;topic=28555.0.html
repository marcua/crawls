Title: Community Supper at Trinity Lutheran
Post by: mvsqueens on November 04, 2014, 11:46:58 PM
Community Potluck Supper / Cena Comunitaria
 November 14  (The second Friday of every month)
   6:00p.m. to 8:30 p.m.

 Trinity Evangelical Lutheran Church
   31-18 37th Street, at 31st Avenue
   Astoria, NY
 
 Bring a dish and a can of food for our pantry, if you can.
   No food to bring? Don’t worry: Just bring yourself. No one goes away hungry!
   Make new friends of all ages — Meet your neighbors — Network a bit.
   Enjoy your Friday night quality time with folks from Trinity and the neighborhood.
 
 All are Welcome!
