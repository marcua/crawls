I can confirm 100% that, the establishment located at 31-29 Ditmars Blvd, formerly doing business as Mezzo Mezzo, will in fact become a Michael Psilakis Restaurant. I met with both the former owner,as well as the new owner yesterday ( October 21 2011) and the so called rumor is now hereby confirmed.
 I am a musician who organized and operated a weekly Music and  Belly Dance  event, every Saturday night for nearly the past 6 years at Mezzo Mezzo, in Astoria. And  while it may be a bit sad to see things coming apart at one end, it will probably be a great pleasure to see the end result of this new proposed venture, forthcoming, which promises to bring a new high tech, gourmet restaurant in Mezzo Mezzo`s place,at this location. From  what I could gather, the said location will undergo a renovation lasting a good five months and we should all see the final results of the total transformation by sometime in  March or April of 2012.


Looking forward to bigger and better things always,
