Just to clear up confusion on the construction:  The rumored C-Town construction site is actually on 34th Ave, behind the Kaufman studios.  (For awhile it was speculated to be a Food Lion, then hoped to be a Whole Foods, but the construction workers reportedly have confirmed it as C-Town).

The construction on 35th Ave, according to the sign, is going to be the Frank Sinatra School of the Arts.  No idea how Kaufman Astoria is supposed to be "expanding" when all of their buildings are being converted, but maybe it has something to do with all the exterior shots that "Pride and Glory" has been doing lately.
