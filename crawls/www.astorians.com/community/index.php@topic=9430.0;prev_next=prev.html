Ok, kids...I just want to give you folks a heads up. I've been directing MRS. WARREN'S PROFESSION with Queens Players/Secret Theatre (44-02 23rd St.).

There's this girl...Savannah
Her dad's the artistic director so, in the name of avoiding nepotism, she never gets cast. She just graduated from Frank Sinatra school and is AMAZING! She just gives a breathtaking performance that will blow you away...you will be hearing from this girl, so check this one out.

The rest of the cast is great as well. If you don't know the play, you should. George Bernard Shaw is just great smart fun, and this is one of his best...a really great blend of comedy and drama.

It opens Wednesday (the 10th) and runs weekends for the rest of the month.
