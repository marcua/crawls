(Long Island City) Dance Entropy presentsthe seventh annual Green Space Blooms, a Queens celebration of new dance and music.  The festival will take place at Green Space, on April 12, 13, 14 and April 19, 20, and 21 at 8 PM.  Admission: Presale tickets $15, Tickets at the door $20.
Family Matinee on April 13 at 1pm, General Admission $10 all ages.
Tickets available online at www.GreenSpaceStudio.org (http://www.greenspacestudio.org/).
GREEN SPACE BLOOMSIn response to the growing community of choreographers and dance artists in Queens and a lack of a specific Queens borough dance festival, Dance Entropy inaugurated the Green Space Blooms Dance Festival in April 2007.  Growing each year, the seventh annual Green Space Blooms will feature 40 diverse choreographers as well as resident company Valerie Green/Dance Entropy. Completing each evening is a live music post performance party, sharing music from a different ethnic or classical tradition each night, curated by Open Music Education.  The festival brings New York City-wide attention to Long Island City as a destination for quality, thought provoking dance and music.  Green Space a cutting-edge dance studio space in Long Island City  - Queens Ledger  Artists by date: Friday April 12th, 8pm    Kate Corby & DancersKatherine MooreKate Bishop OPT 1Ryan McNallyxodus dance collectiveValerie Green/Dance EntropyBarbara Maher Makes DancesPost Performance Music: Pedro de Alcantara, cello Saturday April 13th 1pm: Family MatineeValerie Green/Dance Entropy will perform WILD KINGDOM a vibrant fun filled afternoon of dance for the whole family to enjoy including audience participation.
