Hmmm..........when I think of a hipster, the irony that comes to mind is how they're so pretentious in their non-pretentiousness. If that makes sense.

Yuppies would wear the blingy symbols of their success; hipsters are too cool for the obvious labels. They'd rather spend a lot of money on a hard-to-find retro collectable t-shirt than on Brooks Brothers.

So I guess a hipster is a cross between a hippie and a yuppie. Somebody with the money and the brains but with different values (yuppie = steakhouse; hipster = Veggie Castle.)
