On Sunday my husband was walking through Astoria Park when he saw a cardboard pet carrier sitting open on the ground near the pool. The only reason we can think of for it to be there is that someone abandoned their cat or dog in the park. I guess I'm posting this to ask people to please be on the lookout for a stray cat or dog in the area, and maybe bring it to a shelter or a vet if you find it.
