Yes, I have Colicchio's Think Like a Chef book. It's very good. Not a comprehensive cookbook at all, and not really a traditional celeb-chef "these are my best recipes" book, but basically its title. The core of the book are several chapters where he takes a trio of ingredients and does a series of variations on them. I definitely recommend it. It's helped me get more creative with recipes.
