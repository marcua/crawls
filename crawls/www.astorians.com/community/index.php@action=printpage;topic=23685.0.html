You should check out the following (in no particular order):

  • Polito's (Broadway between Steinway & 38th Street).
  • Alba's (Corner of Ditmars & 37th Street).
  • Sac's (Corner of Broadway & 29th Street).
  • Rizzo's (Steinway between 30th & 31st Avenues).
  • Vesta (Corner of 21st Street & 30th Avenue).
  • Porto Bello (Ditmars between 43rd and 45th Streets; in the shopping center).
That should keep you busy for a while.

I might be the only native Astorian who doesn't like Rizzo's. It was good back in the day, but I don't really think it's that great anymore.
