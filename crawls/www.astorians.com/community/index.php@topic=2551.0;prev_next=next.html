We walked past the new place over the weekend.  Looks more casual and was full of people.  two good signs already.

Menu seems to be a mix of what was, what sells in the hood and all day cheap breakfast.

Cant say i blame the guy for trying to cover all the bases in terms of food and pricing.  We actually plan on visiting sometime soon.

apps range from $5 - $14
salads: $6-$9
entrees: $12-$25
Pasta: $8-$12
sandwiches: $6-$9
wraps: $6-$9
angus burgers/grilled chicken $5-$10.25

mostly american with some bistro, greek, cafe wraps & salads thrown in for good measure.  AHHH a true Astorian restaurant.

I just had a terrible experience with this place...

My boyfriend & I decided to order in from Park Kafe on Valentine's day. (We don't make a big deal over Valentine's day, and decided to just chill at home rather than going out.) We hadn't ordered from Park yet, so thought it would be fun to try a new place.

When our food hadn't arrived an hour after ordering, I gave them a call to check on it. She said "oh yes, your order has just left". I live a block away, so I expected it would arrive in the next 5 minutes. Nearly another HOUR later, still no food. Fed up, I called to cancel the order (by this time it was approaching 10:00). First they were confused that we hadn't gotten the order because the delivery guy said he had delivered it and come back with money. Then they told me they'd mixed it up with another order, and ours hadn't even been cooked yet. Then they put me on hold for a while, so I had to hang up and call back. The girl was very apologetic, and said that if we came to the restaurant we could have dinner on them, but by that point I was too pissed off. Too little, too late.

Unfortunately I can't even comment on the food, since I never got to eat it. Too bad, since it looks like a decent menu and is so close to my apartment.

Isolated incident of bad service? Maybe. But I will never patronize the place, unless I hear some seriously good reviews by you folks...

I guess this wasn't an isolated incident. 

My wife and I decided to try Park Kafe on the advice of one of her college students.  We went for brunch this morning and the menu looked good.  We were seated outside and it seemed like a cute place for brunch.  The waitress took our order and the food came in a reasonable amount of time (delivered by another woman), but I realized that my order was wrong.  I ordered an egg white omelette and it was clearly made with yolks.  I pointed this out to the woman who brought it and she said she didn't know what I ordered, but she'd take it back to the kitchen and check with our waitress.

In the next 20 minutes, our waitress hadn't come back to us once to check on us or apologize for the error and my food still hadn't come out.  My wife at about half of her french toast, but was waiting for me to get mine.   We also hadn't gotten refills on water or coffee the whole time.  My wife is 9 months pregnant, so water is generally necessary in large quantities on hot days.

I got the attention of the woman who brought the food, because it was impossible to get the waitresses attention.  I explained the situation and my wife said that we were contemplating just walking away.  She said she would check on my food and get us water and coffee and hoped we wouldn't leave.  She then proceeded to not bring us water or coffee.  The waitress brought out my food about 5 minutes later and said, "sorry about the mistake" and then disappeared again after we asked her for water. 

Five minutes later we asked another waitress for water and coffee and she brought us water and then the first girl brought us more water and coffee.  Then we just sat there after finishing our meal (a fourth person took this away, so clearly, they weren't understaffed) to see how long it would be until someone came by to check on us in any way, shape or form.  After a half hour of nothing, we asked the second waitress, who was very nice, but not our waitress for the check.  She brought it to us, so our waitress didn't even come back for that. 

It's really a shame, because the food was good, but the staff is just braindead.  We might go back and try dinner if we see a completely different staff working, but I doubt it.
