Two other great sourdough options:

1. FreshDirect has Sourdough Pullman loaves (good for sandwiches).  It's not Amy's-bread good, but it's good.

2. If you are a member of the Hellgate CSA, you can order sourdough bread through the meat and dairy purchasing program.  It is from Rock Hill Bakehouse (I think it sometimes is sold at the Union Square farmer's market), and it's reallllllllllllllly delish. 

But I only wish that Rose & Joe's would carry it.. their bread is great.. but sourdough is my only true love..
