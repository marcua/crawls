Title: Stitchin' Queens Biweekly Meeting (July 8th)

Welcome Astoria-area crafters! The next meeting of the Stitchin' Queens (a group for knitters, crocheters, and any other crafters) will take place on July 8th at 6:30 at Panera (38-01 35th Avenue, just off Steinway Street).   New to the group? We'll be the women knitting and crocheting. We're generally there until 8:30/9:00.


If you do plan to attend, please RSVP at danielleb726@gmail.com, so I can alert you if there are any last minute changes. Email danielleb726@gmail.com to be added to the mailing list. You can also check out our Ravelry page at: http://www.ravelry.com/groups/stitchin-queens (http://www.ravelry.com/groups/stitchin-queens) or our Facebook page at https://www.facebook.com/stitchin.queens (https://www.facebook.com/stitchin.queens).
