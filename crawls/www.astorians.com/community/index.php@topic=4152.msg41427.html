It is very good.

Magdi (the owner) is super duper pleasant and lovely.  The Friday and Saturday night dinner clientele is fun -- and if you want to go play cards, they know some rocking good games.  Very good place for eating or just relaxing. 

I haven't been in a while (and was publicly admonished on the street for my absence) but I used to regularly play cards on Fridays.  Something a little like rummy in concept but much more difficult (and addictive). 

Chicken dishes are really my favorite.  Blackened/roasted, good.

Several flavors of hookah (shesha?).

Try the lemon drink.  Yum.  Also good, turkish coffee.
