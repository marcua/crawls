Dear parents,
 
Long Island City Kids is pleased to announce our partnership with Club Z! Tutoring.
 
Club Z! is the world's largest in-home tutoring company, offering educational services for students of all ages in all core subjects at affordable rates. Club Z! operates in over 400 locations across the United States and Canada. All tutors are state certified teachers or degreed professionals with a passion for teaching.
 
LIC Kids offers Club Z! 1-on-1 tutoring at your convenience in the comfort of your home. Flexible schedules are available and no long-term contracts are required. Tutoring for small groups of children is also available.
 
You can call Club Z! customer service at 718-392-2457 to learn more. Please visit our website: www.clubztutoring.com (http://www.clubztutoring.com)
