What are these stations?  The ones with numbers after a decimal point?  What kind of television service uses those numbers?

Those are the new Digital channel numbers. It was mandated 2 or 3 years ago for the local broadcasters to send a digital signal instead of/or along with the analog. They can expand the number of channels quite easily because they don't interfere with each other. If you have one of those OTA Digital to Analog converter boxes and do a scan every so often, it will pick up those new channels when they become available. But frankly I get pretty bored with that one channel that constantly shows figure skating or something. 4.1 or something?
