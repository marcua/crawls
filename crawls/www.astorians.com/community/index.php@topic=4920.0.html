Spicy Mina is probably about a 20 minute walk for you guys, Jonathan!

In Astoria, I've never thought Ghandi was as bad as other people have said... But it's by no means great...

If you want to try something totally different, go get dosas at a South Indian place! There's some good little holes in the wall in Jackson Heights, or I rather like Saravanaas in Curry Hill too!

Or go to Delhi! My sister and her fiance just arrived there for a couple months of backpacking around before they move on to elsewhere in Asia. Here's part of what she said:

"the four of us left the center area and went to get dinner at a tiny place somewhere under a graffiti covered bridge, where i had by far the best indian food i've ever eaten (for a quarter of what i'd pay for indian back home). we talked over a din of barking stray dogs and endlessly honking horns."

(OK, that last isn't very practical...)
