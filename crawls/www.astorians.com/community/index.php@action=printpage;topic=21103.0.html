El Sitio - Cuban Restaurant (718) 278-7694
 
I've ordered a whole roasted pig for the holidays many times and they're incredible, the pig is seasoned with lots of lemon, garlic, oregano, and it's succulent, it's like butter, it literally melts in your mouth.
 
They're right under the N train, 36th avenue stop.
 
You have to order in advance, and if you want suckling...you need to let them know.
