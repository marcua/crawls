Post by: Bentleys Dad on July 02, 2008, 10:38:25 AM
If you plan on going to LIC to watch the fireworks, plan on going very early. We went two years ago  ( or was it three) and went to Gantry Park. We figured it was going to be full, but we didn't count on it being as packed as it ended up being. We showed up around 6:30, if I remember correctly, and by 8 o'clock it was PACKED, wall to wall people as far as the eye could see. And when I say packed I mean that they were limiting the amount of people that they were letting onto the piers, and certain areas were for residents of the  Rockrose Towers only.
