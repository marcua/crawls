Title: Family Carnival and BBQ (benefit for the Steinway Reformed Church) 9/6/09
Post by: sarahespanol on August 17, 2009, 12:07:56 PM
Ladies and gentlemen... boys and girls... Astoria Moms is proud to present our... Family Carnival Picnic and BBQ to benefit the Steinway Reformed Church, from 2 p.m. - 6 p.m on Sunday, September 6th.  (location:  Steinway Reformed Church-- 21-65 41st Street)

We are teaming up with the Steinway Reformed Church to bring you a day of fun, games, and food! Many of our local eating establishments are happily donating some of their yummiest dishes, and there will be hot dogs on the grill. Kids' games will include potato sack races, monster toss (like a bean bag toss but scarier!), and ring toss! We will have a face painter, and a sno-cone or cotton candy machine. We'll let you know what's what closer to the date. The event will be held in the Church Garden. Be sure to bring your picnic blankets!

There is a donation of $5 per family at the door. Five bucks gets each family five tickets, which you can use to "buy" hot dogs, a face painting, a balloon, a sno-cone, cotton candy, or a game. Once you use those up, we'll be happy to sell you more tickets!

We'll have a raffle (tickets will be $5), which includes:
2   Barefoot Books packages - value $28 
1   Six-week pottery class, benchtime, a locker and a tool kit - value $285
1   3 day pass to Fitness Point Gym
1   Pampered Chef basket - value $50
1   Gift certificate to Bistro Les Minots Restaurant
1   Free week pass to CrossFit Queens 
1 $50 off & free boxes Coupon from Available Movers
1 Basket of Creative Memories Scrapbooking Materials - value $30
1 Gift certificate from Infinity Day Spa
1 Session of classes from Astoria Preschool Dance
1 Gift certificate from Rejuvenate Spa
1 Gift certificate for Lecons Francais (French Lessons)
1 Book, "The Diaper Free Baby" and a $10 gift certificate for EC Wear

We'll also have a small bake sale in the Large Room. All funds raised will go to benefit the Steinway Reformed Church.

 
We hope to see you there!   Please contact the church if you have any questions!
