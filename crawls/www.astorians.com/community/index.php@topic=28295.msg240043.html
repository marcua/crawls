Tried this place today, and was so charmed by the decor, the latte and the nice service. Was pleased that even though it's small, there were even some spots to sit with my laptop and work a while -- the staff even brought me a glass of ice water and offered me the wi-fi password. I've been spending a lot of time at NY Bagel & Coffee and have been so thrilled to have that, but this is kind of a pleasant, quiet, lower-key alternative. They didn't have much in the way of pastries and things to eat when I was there -- but everything else was so solid, and I imagine things will just be getting better. Very happy to have this in the 'hood!
