Astoria Park Meeting
Tuesday, March 13th, 6:30pm
Steinway Reformed Church
41-01 Ditmars Blvd at 41st Street

All are welcome!

Come join us for our continuing discussion regarding Astoria Park and the formation of an Astoria Park Friends group. Our last meeting brought together some nearby residents from throughout Astoria who are interested in working together to ensure the overall health of the park.  But we need you, too!

We, a collective group of Astoria Park users, are interested in clean ups and gardening days, advocating for physical park improvements, partnering with other programs and events that take place in the park, environmental education, and creating literature/brochures. Please come join us to further refine these thoughts and add your own ideas at our second meeting.

If you have questions, please call Helen Ho at 718-520-5913 or e-mail at helen.ho@parks.nyc.gov.

Here's part of the Minutes from the last meeting:

Astoria Park Alliance Meeting
Tuesday, March 13, 2007
Steinway Reformed Church

1. Introductions made by participants, which represented various elements that use Astoria Park: Hellgate dog owners, tennis players, Astoria/LIC Waterfront Parks Association, ARROW, Astoria Moms/Dads, Steinway Reformed Church, Hellgate Roadrunners, Hellgate (CSA) Community Support Agriculture, soccer.

2. Brief introduction of Partnerships for Parks by Helen.

3. Brief introduction of Astoria/LIC Waterfront Parks Association and ARROW by Clare.

4. Friends of Astoria Park introduction by Lucille DiBiase.

5. Helen mentioned that the previous meeting was featured in the Queens Chronicle. She’ll bring the article to the next meeting.

6. We voted on a name for the newly formed group. It will now be called Astoria Park Alliance.

7. Group Mission statement. A mission statement has yet to be established. A subcommittee is recommended to accomplish this.

8. Projects and activities upcoming in the Park.

9. It was asked if the LIC Farmers Market could move to Astoria Park.  Athens Park is being considered instead.

10. Helen discussed the Capacity Fund Grants Program.

Next meeting will be Tuesday, April 10th at 6:30pm at Steinway Reformed Church.
