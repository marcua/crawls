Movies- As a kid/preteen- Sixteen Candles, Pretty in Pink, The Outsiders, Legend of Billie Jean, Say Anything, Tapeheads, Real Genius, Better Off Dead, Stand By Me and Quadrophenia.

TV- The Muppet Show (the Swedish Chef!!), CHIPS (terrible I know), Carol Burnett Show (even though I was pretty little when this show was on it would crack me up), Little House on the Prairie.  I didn't watch a lot of TV in my teens.

Music- I started out loving heavy metal (Def Leppard, Quiet Riot etc) and eventually turned to New Wave, Punk and Goth Music. Favorite band - The Cure. But with a sister ten years older - I had a healthy appreciation for The Who, Pink Floyd and Jimi Hendrix.
