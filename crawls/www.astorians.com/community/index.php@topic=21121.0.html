We just got back from dinner and I couldn't wait to write.
 
WE LOVED IT!!!!  Seriously, it was great.  Love the decor.  Everyone is really friendly.  The owners are there and really cool.
 
We had a boudin ... something to start.  We chose the pork sausage.  There were 3 fried balls of deliciousness.  Well seasoned but not over the top.  Then we had the jambalaya (I think my boyfriend is going to leave me for this dish), and the fried oyster po'boy (half) with a side of collards.
 
For dessert we had SMACK.  I don't know what it was - dump pie?  with cherries and pineapple and some sort of sauce. 
 
We really really liked it and can't wait to go back.  Beer selection is nice and they have a full bar.
 
What's great is that there isn't anything even remotely like it anywhere in Astoria.  It feels fresh, and different, and I hope they succeed.
