Title: Sabor de Cuba
Post by: geowrld on January 03, 2015, 10:13:06 PM
Went here tonight - it was great!  It's in the old Burger Bistro location on 31st Ave btw 37th and 38th Streets.


We had these "chicharrones" made of wheat with a phenomenal spicy sauce - those come on the house (like bread at Italian restaurants).  We ordered the chicharron de pollo - basically fried chicken breast pieces with another amazing sauce.  I had the churrasco (sp?) - grilled skirt steak - and my husband had the ropa vieja.  And we had a side of maduros.


All was great.  We had a great conversation with the owner - William - and we highly recommend this place.  It's high time a nice cuban restaurant opened in Astoria.  I hope they succeed.  The location is great (we live around the corner) and the music is all the great cuban tunes I love.  They're in their "soft opening" phase and are having a grand opening next weekend.
