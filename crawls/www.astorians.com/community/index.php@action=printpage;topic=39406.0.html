FYI - the Opossum/Possum are harmless and are related to the kangaroo/koala family.  They will NOT attack you.  If you hear them hissing at you and/or showing their teeth, it's only because they are FRIGHTENED   They are solitary mammals and prefer to be left alone and try their best to avoid confrontation with humans.
Title: Re: OctoPossum
Post by: Simplymei on May 31, 2016, 03:33:15 PM
FYI:  they are harmless and will not attack.  Should you hear them hissing at you and/or showing their teeth, this means they are FRIGHTENED and may even play dead by foaming from the mouth (at times) and even secrete a foul smell.  They will lie in a comatose state EVEN IF YOU TRY TO MOVE THEM - remember that!!!  They try avoid confrontation with us humans and are active at night.  So the video that circulated with the mom carrying her babies on her back may mean that "someone" or something threatened her during the daylight hours.  I actually felt sorry for her when i saw that video because she most likely was headed to a new shelter for her family.


They are non-destructive. They benefit the environment by keeping it clean and healthy. They eat roaches, rats, slugs and dead animals. They also eat over-ripe fruit,vegetables,grass, etc.,   Many refer to them as "Nature's Little Garden Sanitation Engineers".   That's my 2-cents.
