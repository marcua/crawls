It's a Greek bar with excellent musical performances at night.  Think of it like a scaled down Zodiac.  Very scaled down.  The main reason we don't go anymore is because we quit smoking.  But it was always a lot of fun.  I can see it being a bit unwelcoming to non-Greeks or people not into the music and dance.  But I always found the people to treat us nicely.  But by no means is this a normal after-work kind of place.  It's totally Twilight Zone until 11pm.  :-o
