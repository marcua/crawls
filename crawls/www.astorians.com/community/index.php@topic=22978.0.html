Hi West Coast!

My husband and I are looking to rent our two bedroom apartment in the Astoria/edge of Woodside. Our Apt is located in the Boulevard Gardens-Woodside New York- complex, which you can google to see their site. The apt is located on beautiful garden grounds, five stops on the R train to midtown Manhattan (20 minute ride!), the train is three blocks away. We lived there without a car for 6 years, it is close to everything: stores, restaurants, shopping. The apt has lots of windows, closets, eat in kitchen, living room is about 19x12, two good sized bedrooms, great neighbors. We have a laundry room, bike storage and storage rentals in the basement, elevator to this quiet 6th floor apt.

You would have to meet with the board for approval, lease is for one year, can extend one year more.
