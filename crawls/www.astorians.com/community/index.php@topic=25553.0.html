Wanted to let everyone know that we ended up getting a Turkey from Sage General Store in LIC.  Decided that the restaurant wasn't the best idea for us with our newborn.  It was a Spit Fire Roasted Turkey.  We picked it up the day before and had to put it in the oven for about two hours.  It was unbelievably good.  I was amazed at how juicy it was.  I would recommend and also purchase one there again if I don't have the time to do one myself.
