Smell of Human Feces in Astoria!
« on: September 25, 2008, 09:32:56 AM »
Hello all,

For all of those who are new to the area, or those lifelong residents like myself who smell human feces all day, we have an issue. That smell you are inhaling all day, everyday, is human waste from the Bowery Bay Sewage Treatment plant located near 19th avenue and 46th street. I don't know about you, but I cannot keep my windows open during the day because if I do, when I get home, my apartment will smell like I have a backed up toilet. This is a serious health hazard that needs to be attended to immediately. If the city can put up two unnecessary new baseball stadiums in less than a year, they can certainly resolve a simple problem that is affecting the lives of so many hard working citizens.

I have called Vallone's office to complain about the smell of human feces that is destroying the air quality in our neighborhood. His secretary, Anne, is writing a letter to the Bowery Bay sewage treatment plant to ask them the status of the repairs of the domes. The domes that usually cover the pools of human waste, are damaged and some are alltogether missing. This is the reason the fumes of your neighbors toilet messes are wafting into your windows. If you enjoy smelling human excrement everyday, then don't do anything. If you value your health and quality of life here in Astoria, please do your part. Call up Vallone's office, 718-274-4500, make a complaint. They seem to be willing to write a letter to the Bowery Bay on our behave. Call up the News and ask them to do a story on it. Call 311 and complain. Do something!
