A small correction on the Q101, the Manhattan bus stop is at 2nd Avenue & 61st Street. When the trains to Queens are out though, the queue here gets very long and it's not unusual to wait over an hour before you can even manage to get on a bus. When I see a very big line here, I find it's better to either try to catch a cab, or if you're brave it can be actually quicker to walk over the bridge than to get home by bus.

Awesome post! You can also add that if you're coming from Brooklyn you can take the G train to Court Square and transfer to the Q69 to Astoria. If the N, Q, R, E, M, F, 7 lines are really messed up because everyone's thinking the same thing or the trains are rerouted to the point where they run at a snail's pace, this is actually a decent option. You'll have to pay another fare but it sure beats the L train.
