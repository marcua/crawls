Title: Astoria Bookshop event: November 27, Bohemian Hall
Post by: zlo on November 26, 2012, 05:16:38 PM
Just a heads up that the fabled Astoria-Bookshop-to-be is hosting an event at the Bohemian Hall on Nov 27 at 7pm.


Jen Adams, who lives in Astoria, wrote a book called "The Books They Gave Me"--all stories about books given and received. She'll be reading and talking with the manager of Word in Greenpoint. (The book is based on a tumblr (http://thebookstheygaveme.tumblr.com/) she started a while back.)


The woman behind Astoria Bookshop is running these events (she has a couple more in the pipeline I think) to gauge interest while she's looking for space here. So if you're curious about the bookshop, this is a great opportunity to learn more and show your support.
