Title: Young Adult Mass ~ Astoria Beer Garden ~ Fellowship
Post by: cfnyc on October 05, 2009, 08:05:05 PM
Join us, The Catholic Fellowship (http://www.cfnetwork.org) for the Young Adult Mass on Sunday October 18th at 5pm at Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church (www.mountcarmelastoria.org) located at 23-25 Newtown Avenue, Astoria, NY 11102.

Following the mass, please meet the group in front of the church to head to the beer garden. The beer garden is located at:

The Bohemian Hall Beer Garden
29-19 24th Avenue
Astoria, NY 11102
http://www.bohemianhall.com

Note: We will be outside in the garden, so advise that you dress warm. If the weather is too cold or rains, we will go indoors at the Beer Garden.

Directions to the church: Take the N train uptown to Astoria and get off 30th Ave Station. Walk down 31st Street and turn left on Newtown Ave. The church is located at the corner of Newtown Ave./Crescent St.
