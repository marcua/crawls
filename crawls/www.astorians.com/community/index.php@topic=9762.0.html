« Reply #1 on: March 07, 2008, 06:25:01 PM »
What a great subject.
Piccola Venezzia continues to serve classic Italian cuisine, it's the type of dishes that you find in good restaurants for the last 20 years. Very good quality. There's no change. It appeals to the older crowd.
Tratoria L'Incontro serves Italian cuisine with a twist, it's very modern, and with great influences from seasonal foods. Chef is very creative. It appeals to a much younger crowd.
Ex. Piccola will always have classic chops, L'Incontro will add a twist and serve it stuffed with spinach and mascapone cheese.
Both are wonderful places, but I personally prefer L'Incontro.
