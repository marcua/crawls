Title: [BK] May 7th Fort Greene, open house tour
Post by: TRX on May 05, 2006, 03:42:19 PM

http://www.developdontdestroy.org/

The Fort Greene Association
in Historic Downtown Brooklyn
is pleased to announce its 28th House Tour

Transition/FortGreene

Sunday, May 7th, 2006
Noon to 5 pm, Rain or Shine

The last public opportunity for the public to experience the breathtaking views from the top of the landmarked Williamsburgh Bank Tower (since 1929, the tallest building in Brooklyn).

A special exhibition of 21st Century architecture currently being realized in Fort Greene, including structures by Enrique Norten, Hugh Hardy, and Arquitectonica.

And a fascinating selection of spectacular Victorian era buildings, with interiors of a wide array of styles.

Tickets ($20 in advance, $25 day of tour) are available (cash only) at:
BAM Box Office, 30 Lafayette Avenue
Yu Interiors, 15 Greene Avenue,
Tillie's of Brooklyn, 248 DeKalb Avenue
BAM Triangle Park (day of tour only)

Or through PayPal link at www.HistoricFortGreene.org

For further information, please contact:
