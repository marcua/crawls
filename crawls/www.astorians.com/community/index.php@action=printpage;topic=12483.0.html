Title: Rockaways Bike Ride 10/18
Post by: heff on October 01, 2008, 03:43:28 PM
Join the Transportation Alternatives Queens Committee (http://www.transalt.org/takeaction/queens) for our 2nd annual Rockaway Ride, which will be held on Saturday, October 18.

I know, I know, it's the day before the Tour de Bronx.  Just consider the 45 mile round-trip Rockaway Ride your pre-Bronx warm-up!  Here are the details:

Saturday, October 18

Queens Meeting time: 8:30am
Queens Meeting place: Steinway Municipal Lot, Steinway Street between 31st Ave. and Broadway (next to Starbucks), Astoria

Brooklyn Meeting time: 10:15am
Brooklyn Meeting place: Grand Army Plaza, outside Prospect Park

Rockaway Meeting time: 11:30am
Rockaway Meeting place: entrance to Fort Tilden on Beach 169th Street and Murray Road

Cost: current TA members ride for free!  All others are asked to join TA at the discounted rate of $20 (all member benefits apply).

Route: We'll ride from Astoria to the Rockaways and back, traveling through quiet neighborhoods, Prospect Park, and on Shore Blvd. Parkway and the Marine Parkway Bridge.  When we arrive at the Rockaways, we'll enjoy a guided tour of some of Fort Tilden's very active community buildings, courtesy of the Rockaway Waterfront Alliance.  We'll have a snack and then turn around, perhaps stopping at Di Fara Pizza in Midwood on the way back.  45-ish miles total, relaxed pace.

RSVP: Please email emiliajocrotty(at)gmail.com to sign-up and receive a cue sheet and information in advance.
