Title: Jazz Fest in New Orleans
Post by: rene0226 on March 24, 2005, 01:23:21 PM
I'm originally from New Orleans and visit there often.  Jazz Fest is a great time of year to go but you may have trouble getting a place to stay anywhere near the city as hotels are booked almost a year in advance.  Also, everything is doubled in price for those two weeks -- food, hotels, etc. -- actually, they double the price everytime they have a festival.  :)

Some websites to check out for info are:

http://www.frenchquarterfestival.com/index.html -- this is April 1-8, I think.

http://www.bestofneworleans.com -- like our New York magazine

http://www.nola.com

The other big music festival is the Essence Jazz fest in mid-July.

And a great little hotel in the Faubourg Marigny (kinda like the East Village) is the Frenchmen Hotel:  http://www.frenchmenhotel.com.

Before or after the Jazz Fest -- all through May or early April is a good time to go weather wise and you can still hear just as much music -- not so many crowds, a little cheaper.  Best of New Orleans . com has the places to go and who's playing.  Have fun, Cher!
