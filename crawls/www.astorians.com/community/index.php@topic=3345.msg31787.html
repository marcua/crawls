Unfortunately, like jewelry cleaning, shoe repair, and TraderJoe products, I go into Manhattan for the waxing as well.
The CHEAPEST [good] Brazilian is in Chelsea (go figure) at the Beauty and Youth Spa: 6th Ave between 11th and 12th streets. It'll run you like $25. And interesting conversation with a random Russian woman (not the gentlest of folk). Walk-in service.
The BEST and most expensive is the legendary J Sisters in midtown. This is where I went for my first Brazilian because of their reputation and renown. But at $80, it'll set you back some, but the waxers there are top of the line. Make reservations a few weeks ahead of time to get your desired time slot (probably not as hard to get an appt now as when bikini season was just launching in Spring)
