This Friday December 1st, 2006
You are invited to Come and Experience the Grand Opening of
the Hottest Lounge in New York

WWW.KABALALOUNGE.COM

 

From the creators of some of the 
Most Exclusive Venues in New York City, Miami & L.A

 
With the absorption of the international pats and mystical roots from Barcelona, London, Bogota, Athens & Ibiza, Kabala Lounge has arrived to NY, with its unique European and International V.I.P taste. The design of this hot and distinctive lounge in Astoria was the uphill struggle of recognized designers & creators of some of the most exclusive venues in New York City, Miami and L.A
 Kabala Lounge will merge two rooms surrounded by wood, plants and a lighting experience only delightful by the exclusive and unique clientele who is ready to experience a new taste of exclusivity.
 

To be on our Grand Opening Guest list This Friday Dec 1st E-mail us at: 
    rsvp@kabalalounge.com
Doors open at 10pm
