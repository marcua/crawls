Title: Disappointed with the 2012 Hellgate CSA
Post by: okkichan on October 22, 2012, 11:33:45 AM
Hi fellow Hellgate CSA'ers,

Is anyone else disappointed with the shares this year? We keep getting things like lettuce and mini tomatoes well into October. Where is the winter squash? Where is the kale? Where is the variety? I know farms are not guaranteed, but weather wise we've had a pretty great year.

Looking back over the years:

Tomorrow's share:
October 23, 2012
- arugula
 - scallions
 - sweet potatoes
 - lettuce
 - mini tomatoes
 - string beans

Last year:
October 25, 2011
 - Red lacinato kale
 - carrots
 - green string beans
 - curly cress
 - broccoli
 - scallions

Two years ago:
October 19, 2010
- sweet potatoes
 - lettuce
 - string beans
 - winter squash
 - diakon
 - senposai

Maybe my memory is failing me. But overall, I have felt that the shares this year lack variety and are noticeably smaller. Does anyone else feel this way?
