There is a patch of grass in the front where you can garden (Im srsly thinking of growing veggies there next summer)

All in all its a great block. The apt is totally redone, and I have looked at apartments in astoria and honestly can tell you this is a great deal. I mean who has a formal dining room anymore?!?!? Right up the street from 122. You can walk to the park and your neighbors are great. Bike lanes that go through the park start right up on 20th avenue and you can ride right through. Car service opened right on the corner and there is a corner store on the corner of the block. Your landlord is really sweet.  Ive honestly really really loved living here and hope to help find someone who will love living here too.
