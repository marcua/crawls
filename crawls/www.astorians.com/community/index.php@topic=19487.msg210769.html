I felt it was necessary for me to update this thread because of what happened to us when we moved into our apartment in the Acropolis.  We basically moved in on a friday and was moved out by sunday. It was a horrific experience and I swear to you I will shudder every time I walk past that complex.

I have many friends who live there who are happy and do not have any sort of bug problems. One of my friends has THE most adorable apt ever and never seen a bug. But there are just too many units in there with bedbug problems and while the management does spray when called, the whole towers are not treated.

I moved in on Friday. When I spoke to the landlord I asked specifically about bedbugs. He said the apartment didnt have them. After all my crap was moved in, I was cleaning and noticed the baseboards and outlets sealed. This usually happens after some sort of bug outbreak. Its the correct thing to do to try to keep bugs out. I called the landlord and flat out asked again if there had ever been bedbugs. He admitted a year ago tehre were but they were treated and hadnt returned. The apartment had been vacant for 6 months. I freaked out about how he was legally obligated to disclose this to me.

Saturday rolls around and there is a stench in my apartment that we cant figure out where it came from. Like cat pee. Super nasty. My daughter comes from her aunts house and sleeps in her bed (covered with bedbug covers) the next morning she notices a bug on her pillow.

Now to be fair, none of us were bit and my daughter is EXTREMELY sensitive to bedbug bites. She got bit once at my dads house when his apt got infested a few years back and it was so bad she wound up at the doctor. Im not 100% sure it was a bedbug. It could have been something else. But that with the fact that the situation wasnt disclosed to me sent me into a panic. I panicked and called the landlord and flipped out. I called my old landlord hwo basically told me we were like family to them and said that we should return home. We did. Our old new apartment is getting a huge renovation.

I had to throw out my daughters bedroom set, and every box was opened and sprayed. I had every fabric item removed from the apartment in sealed trashbags and then washed in hot water and dried on high.  I steamed everything and had to vacum every nook and cranny to make sure that I didnt bring anything. Im STILL in a panic. Im so petrified of bringing anything from that place into my apartment.

With that said, this is the second time I have experienced some bedbug type of situation at the acropolis. Like I said I know people who live there and are totally happy. But this was a nightmare. I will NEVER advise anyone to go to that complex again. My former landlord who owns the unit is so upset that he wants to sue the management company. I got my deposit back. He claims they assured him the problem had been taken care of. Second time Ive heard that.  He is going to have a hard time selling his unit which is actually a pretty cute little unit.

So as much as I am embarressed that this happened, I even posted in the bedbug forum stating this had happened to a "friend" I felt it was important to give this information to anyone considering the complex. Its a great location. Which is why I wanted to live there. But I was never so happy to come back to my old block. Seriously one of the worst experiences of my life.
