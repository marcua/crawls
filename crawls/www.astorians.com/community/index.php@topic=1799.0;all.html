I found this place after comparison pricing at all the salons in the Ditmars area.  This salon offers the most competitive price, more services than others, better hours, a more intimate feel and the people are really nice.   Serives include massage, waxing, facial, nails, perms, dye (multi-process avail.), body treatments, corsets, makeup and spa treatments. 

I got a great hair cut on 31st between Ditmars and Astoria BLVD.  It's a full service salon with corsets in the window. 

The price was fantastic, hair cut (I went from long hair to super short), dye and eyebrow wax for $75.

My hair was longer than shoulder length.  I had it cut very short.  The woman who did my hair got the style I wanted just right.  I'm a very low maintanence woman, so having only to slap a little gel in my hair is my primary goal for a style.  She got it completely right!
