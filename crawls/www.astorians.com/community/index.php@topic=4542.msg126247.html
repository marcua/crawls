I managed to make it all through the day on no sleep.  I got a couple of things accomplished, I remembered to bring my bottle of water with me, I bought a salad, and walked further than I had planned - my medications for Achilles tendonitus and Sciatica were working well.  I arrived at work relatively cheery, and very nearly on time.  I was helpful, I smiled. I told people I worked with that I hadn't been to sleep.  I watched the clock.  And then I did the one thing I said I would not do.  I put my head back in my chair.  I don't know how long I was out.  I don't know if I snored.  I don't know if I had my mouth open.  But I fell asleep.  I work in one big room, I face out into the room.  The possibilities that people saw me are many.  I always say I'll never do it again, but I do.  I stay up too late, some nights I don't go to bed.  I suffer the consequences, but I don't seem to learn.
