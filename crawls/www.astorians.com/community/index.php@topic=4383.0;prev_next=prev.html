I meant to do this on my 2000th post but got distracted.

I would like to encourage my fellow astorians to join me in a New Year's resolution that I have not yet begun (as January was a penny-pinching month). For the remainder of the year, I intend to give to a good cause every month - even if I can only manage to give $5 or $10. If just 10 astorians did this, by the end of the year we would have collectively have contributed over $1000 (assuming we all made contributions of at least $10) to making the world a slightly better place.

On my list are:

Heifer International
Doctors Without Borders
Partners In Health

Other causes I would like to contribute to include Darfur and human slavery, but I still have a bit of research to do on the various groups addressing these concerns.

Please consider joining me (really, most of you are probably way ahead of me on this ;)), and please share your favorite charities.

Cheers!
