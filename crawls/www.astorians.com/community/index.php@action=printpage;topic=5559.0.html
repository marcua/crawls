When
Sunday, March 25th, 2-4 pm

Where
FreezePeach Cafe
22-00 29th Street
just off Ditmars Boulevard
map (http://www.google.com/maps?f=q&hl=en&q=22-00+29th+Street.+astoria,+ny&ie=UTF8&om=1)

1. Bring books to lend or give away.
2. Take books to borrow or keep.
3. All books must be removed from the cafe at the end of the swap.
4. (Most important) Have fun and meet fellow bibliophiles!
