There may be no point in asking this question.  In addition, this is going to come across as very very snobby, so I apologize in advance 
Does anybody know if there's a place in Astoria that sells good smoked fish (and knows how to cut it properly, in the case of smoked salmon)?  I'm warning you that I'm sort of a maven (translation: complete snob) when it comes to this particular thing, so don't send me to Brooklyn Bagels or something unless you think it can stand up to Zabar's (well, perhaps I'm being a bit too ambitious there, but you get the point).  I tried some of the oil-cured herring at one of the Greek stores- not what I'm looking for.  I just don't want to have to constantly make a shlep to the Upper East Side to get my appetizing on....

This is what you have to do:

On Fridays between 8am and 1pm, you have to go to the warehouse of the Acme Smoked Fish Company - that's right, the folks who make most of the lox, etc. that you see in the supermarkets, and also the company that smokes the house brand for places like Zabar's.  You can get it whole or sliced, although I recommend the whole.

The texture and taste is like nothing I have ever experienced.  I guarantee it is better than Zabar's.

Here are the details.  Take the Pulaski Bridge into Greenpoint, go straight on McGuinness to Meserole, turn right on Meserole, and they are at the end of Meserole on Gem St.
