"The Warblehead Union" from Boston appears at Astoria's wonderful Freezepeach Cafe this Saturday night, Jan. 20th, starting at 8 pm.

Jungle-space jazz, performed mostly on, in the words of Mickey Hart, "the Original Instruments: the Drum and the Voice" -- but also with electric guitar, acoustic bass, bamboo flute, bells, percussion, etc.

All about a feeling.
