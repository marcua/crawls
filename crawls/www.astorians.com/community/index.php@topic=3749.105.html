Not to stray too much from the topic of conversation (bagels), but I just thought of one more thing that is very New York - "youse".  Of course, people in other (East Coast) cities say this too.  But if you hear someone in New York say this, chances are, they're not NOT a New Yorker.

Also, I tell you one funny story about bagels.  One time when I was visiting my parents, I woke up in the morning and went downstairs to the kitchen.  And what did I see?  My sister sitting in the breakfast room eating a bagel sandwich with something unrecognizable on it.  Upon closer inspection, I realized that it was the leftover cashew chicken from our Chinese food the night before.  Let me say that again.  My sister was eating a cashew chicken sandwich on a bagel for breakfast.  Unbelievable.  It sounds so wrong and yet so New York and yet so wrong (but so good?).  It's another one of those New York things run-amock.
