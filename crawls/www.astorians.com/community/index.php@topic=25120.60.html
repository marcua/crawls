According to the pdf just posted, they are clearly in violation of the noise code if the decibel reading is above 42 deceibels:

Commercial establishments that play music must limit the level of unreasonable or disturbing noise that escapes into the streets or is heard in nearby residences by requiring that sounds levels may not exceed:•42 decibels as measured from inside nearby residences, AND•7 decibels over the ambient sound level, as measured on a street or public right-of-way 15 feet or more from the source, between 10:00 pm and 7:00 am [size=0px]k[/size]Sometimes residents are disturbed by pervasive bass sounds that resonate and can be felt physically by a person. •Bass sounds measurements are weighted in the “C” scale and may not exceed 6 dB(C) above the ambient sound if the ambient sound is greater than 62 dB(C).
