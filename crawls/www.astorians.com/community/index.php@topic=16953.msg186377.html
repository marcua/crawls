There are more to these cuts than just the W train.  The proposal talks about taking away free metro-cards from students.  It also talks about eliminating door to door service for Access a ride customers and dropping them off at "accessible" train stations.  Both of those practices hurt a large segment of our community (children, elderly and disabled riders). 

Families are already are cutting back to make ends meet.  Asking them to pay $4.50 per day to send their kids to school will lead to choices that aren't tenable.  Some parents may just say, we can't afford for you to go to school this week. 

The Q won't be available on weekends either or from what I understand during non-peak hours. 

A rally will help bring these issues into focus, help better educate the community as to what these cuts mean and organize the neighborhood to move forward against the cuts.  Public displays of dissent are useful and can get results. This is why I support this rally.

  Cutting student metrocards is just wrong - I hope that proposal is removed.

I've been conflicted about this. As one article in the NYT's noted, Student transportation is traditionally paid for by the Education portion of a government budget not by the transportation agency.

It seems to me, and I could be wrong about this, that the Student card was introduced so the City wouldn't need a fleet of buses to take kids around the city. And that makes sense to me. if the infrasructure already exists, why not make use of it.

However, the city and state has cut their portion of the expense and I do feel that MTA has a valid beef in not being happy about that cut. And ultimately, if the state and city is not making a fair contribution to the expenses of the student cards, we, the users of the transit system have to pick up the difference. Or cut the expense. As one article noted. MTA's stance on this is probably a bargaining tool for what the state and city pays.

Overall, I don't think it's a bad expense. But, it does mean that we could have to pay more to keep the program going. Is it good or bad. I don't know. aAnd I'm curious what others here think.
