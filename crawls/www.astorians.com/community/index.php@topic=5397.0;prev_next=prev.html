There's a thrift place on Astoria Blvd right off 31st Street. (Visible from the N/W stop.) If you can squeeze your way thru the teetering mounds of miscellania, interesting things may be found.
Drawback: It's so crowded you can hardly turn around. The staff will frequently have to move half the store onto the street so that a customer can look at one item. But there's certainly furniture available.

Also check out the Salvation Army on the corner of Steinway St and (I think) 35th Ave (someone help me out please?). It's a HUGE warehouse space and the entire rear is full of furniture.
Drawback: Prices are pretty high for everything, including used furniture. There are no returns, and I'm pretty sure there's no delivery!

At the Furniture Market, did you venture into the basement? All the discards (read: cheaper stuff) can be found down there....

Hope that helps!
