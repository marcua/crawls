Post by: Simplymei on August 11, 2016, 12:53:22 PM
clearview821 ~ my recommendation of a person is "Cadillac Man."  He never gives his real name.  He has been around forever and has seen the changes in the Astoria/Ditmars area.  He is/was homeless.  For years he was stationed under that Amtrak passage when you pass the Park and Ride at the Ditmars Station.  Very polite and just about everyone in the area who has lived there long enough knows of him.


Lately in the early am you can find him hanging out in front of that Italian sandwich place along 23rd Ave (Sal, Kris, Charlie Deli) .. oh, that's would make a good collection for the work you're doing.  That place is very very popular.


Good luck.  C'iao for now ~
