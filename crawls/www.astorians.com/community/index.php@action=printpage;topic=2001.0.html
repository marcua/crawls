Title: 2006 Astoria Street Fairs start this Sunday!
Post by: erpotown on April 25, 2006, 09:52:58 AM
It's that time of year to weave your way through your fellow Astorians and enjoy street meat, grilled corn on the cob, cheap sunglasses, loud Greek/Croatian/Brazilian techno music, and live infomercials for super absorbing towels.  :-P

Yes, the 2006 Astoria Street Fairs are kicking off this Sunday on Steinway Street.

Here is a complete list of the 2006 Astoria Street Fairs courtesy of http://centralastoria.org/news.htm


April 30
Central Astoria LDC
Steinway Street between 28th & 34th Avenues

May 18-21
St. Demetrios Cathedral
30th Drive between 30th & 31st Streets

May 29
Broadway Astoria Merchants & Professionals
Broadway between Crescent & 47th Streets

June 22-25
St. Antonio Abate Society of Castrofilippo
Ditmars Boulevard between 35th & 38th Streets

July 16
Friends of Community Board 1
30th Avenue between 29th & 42nd Streets

July 27-30
St. Irene Chrysovalantou Monastery
23rd Ave. from 35th to mid 37th St. & 36th St.from 23rd Ave. to under the Amtrak overpass

July 30
114th Precinct Civilian Observation Patrol
Broadway between Steinway & 47th Streets

August 3-6
Hellenic Orthodox Traditionalist Church (St. Markella)
26th St. from 23rd Ave. to the Amtrak Trestles

August 13
Steinway Street BID
Steinway between 28th & 34th Avenues

August 17-20
Borgetto Cultural Association
Steinway Street between 25th & 28th Avenues

August 19
Dutch Kills Civic Association
36th Avenue between 29th & 35th Streets

September 4
30th Avenue Business Association
30th Avenue between 29th & 42nd Streets

October 1
Broadway Astoria Merchants & Professionals
Broadway between Crescent & 47th Streets

ALL FAIRS RUN 11AM TO 6PM
