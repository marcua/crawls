Gleason - why do you assume that I (and probably a lot of us) have not actually attended those meetings?  I call them LAME, which is why I have absolutely no interest in wasting my time.  I have attended the 114th and with all due respect, I was the youngest person there (and I am not that young) and when I posed a question about issues in the area, it was ignored.  The Civic Associations Meetings are the same old, same old.  I went for the first time when I was in my 20's and guess what - 20 years later I saw the same people, not doing very much.  I then attended a number of Community Board Meetings and I won't even go into those experiences.  What a total waste of my time!!!

That is exactly why seven years ago, I started the Astoria Rennaissance Committee.  The majority of the people who came to our meetings were from the Astoria Park area.  We were able to:

Fix the broken equipment in the kiddie park in Astoria Park.
The 114th began conducting sweeps of motorcycle racers the year we held our first meeting.
Purchase 20 garbage cans for the Park.
Organize clean ups in the Park.
Get speed bumps installed on 19th Street (I am proud of that accomplishment as it was something I managed to do on my own.)

I do not know what ALWPA stands for but I cannot attend one more useless, waste of time meeting.

If you pick up some old copies of the Gazette and the Tribune, you will see so many of the wonderful accomplishments we achieved in this area.  I was driving this, but work became much more involved so I slowed down.  Astorians.com has inspired me to start up again.

If I receive 30 commitments, we will have our meeting and you are free to attend or not to attend!
