Jayme, when I made mine it was a bit spongy on the outside and dense really in the center.  But it was substantial and didn't fall apart or anything when I cooked it in the cast iron pan (the pan really does make a difference).  I did not knead with excess flour, because it just was not sticking to the counter.  So I am guessing that perhaps kneading with flour helped you get the density you want.

Also, how long did you knead it the first time compared to the second time?  That also might make a difference.
