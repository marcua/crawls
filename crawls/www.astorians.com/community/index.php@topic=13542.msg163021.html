Arts Classes for Kids--Free Trial classes at Serious Fun
« on: January 02, 2009, 09:29:15 PM »


Hi parents:

Just to let you know Serious Fun, Steinway Reformed Church's new after school arts program in Astoria begins their Winter 2009 session on January 5, and we're offering free trial classes the week of January 5-9, so if you'd like to have your child try a class while you observe, we would love to have you.

The first 20 families to register will get a second day of classes free!

We are a registered School Age Child Care, so tuition for classes is tax deductible.

The classes are offered each day, M-F, 4:00-5:30 pm, at Steinway Reformed Church, 41-01 Ditmars Blvd (co of 41st/Ditmars).  Our Winter classes will include:

Mondays:  Hip Hop Dance: children have fun learning basic dance technique and tumbling  set to an urban beat.

Tuesdays:  Hands on Art: children play with paint, mask-making, mural-making, printmaking, puppetry, & sculpture--the messier, the better.

Wednesdays:  World Music Jam: children play instruments from around the world while learning new songs & cultures, featuring the performers of Song and Beat: www.songandbeat.com.

Thursdays:  Improv theater: children use improvisation, storytelling, and music to create their own plays, featuring performers of Chicago City Limits www.chicagocitylimits.com.

Fridays:  Spanish Language Immersion: children learn Spanish using original music, songs, games and with native language speakers from the Language Workshop: www.thibauttechnique.com.

In addition, for the Winter 2009 session we are offering a full after school program until 6:00 pm, including transportation from local schools, a light snack, 45 minutes of homework help, and 45 minutes of the featured arts class and free play/socializing.

As always parents can choose to register for a daily or full weekly program, and we will work with parents to arrange a payment programs that meets the needs of our community.  Transportation from local schools is available.

For more information, visit our our website: www.SeriousFunNYC.com, or give me a call at 646-415-8901.

I hope you will take this opportunities to check out our program,

Sylvia Ramsaywak
Serious Fun
