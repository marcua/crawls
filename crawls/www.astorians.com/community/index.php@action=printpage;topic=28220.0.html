Title: Participatory Budgeting NYC Info Session in Sunnyside tonight (7/8) @ 6:30 pm
Post by: mronda on July 08, 2014, 12:50:09 PM

How would you spend $1 million in our neighborhood?


Council Member Jimmy Van Bramer's office will hold the first of two information sessions tonight about the Participatory Budgeting (http://www.astorians.com/community/pbnyc.org) process beginning in the 26th District. As his email explains: Residents come together at public meetings to discuss community needs (e.g., school repairs, park improvements, sanitation needs), and brainstorm ways to address them. Volunteers will take these ideas and develop them into concrete proposals this fall. In spring, a public vote will be held to determine which projects will be funded.


Info sessions:
Tuesday, 7/8 from 6:30 - 8 at Sunnyside Community Services, 43-31 39th Street and
Wednesday, 7/9 from 6:30 to 8 at Jacob Riis Neighborhood Settlement House, 10-25 41st Avenue


Contact JVB's office for more info and to RSVP: 718-383-9566 or ngulotta@council.nyc.gov
