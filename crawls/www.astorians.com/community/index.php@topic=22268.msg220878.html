Sadly an elderly man was hit by a car this evening while crossing 21st Ave, pushed across the intersection and reportedly killed as a result. According to witnesses the driver of the Mercedes was a man in his early sixties who failed a Breathalyzer at the scene. He might have gunned his accelerator to beat a yellow light. None of this is confirmed but that was the word outside as to why the intersection was completely closed down with blood on the street this evening. Very sad.

My comment: people drive far too aggresively through these densely populated neighborhoods. You can see it in action at almost any time of day on main streets and side streets. Less meter maids and more speed traps.
