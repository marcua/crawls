We went Saturday evening.  Second night of business and staffed to the gills. They get lots of leeway here since they are so new.

Nice bar area with comfortable seats.  Nice table set up and good comfortable seating.  Tables not crowded together. :mrgreen:
Soft lighting & quiet background music.  Unheard of and quite welcome here in Astoria. :mrgreen:
Friends joined us later at a table for drinks and we could actually hear each other without leaning in and screaming to each other.
One of the owners stopped by to say hello and talk about the place.  Nice guy and our second round was on the house.
:mrgreen:
Excellent margarita for $6 served in a tall Pilsner glass.  Strong on booze yet smooth going down. :mrgreen:

Food was very fresh and clean.  All obviously made on premises.
Very light on seasonings but hey, they just opened and we will most definitely go back for another meal.

Prices were not cheap.  Apps were around $8 to $12 and Entrees/meals were around $14 to $26.  The apps we had could have been a bit more substantial for the price.  Also the menu seemed a bit scattered.  Looking forward to seeing how it gets refined over the coming months.

Over all two thumbs up for decor, service, atmosphere & drinks. We know the food will be perfected shortly because of all the good people working there to make your experience a memorable one.   Welcome to the neighborhood.
