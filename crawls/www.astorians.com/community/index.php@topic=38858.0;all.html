Hi there,

Nice to meet all of you! I've been living in Astoria for the past 12 years and love it here. I've never posted on the board before, but do read it from time to timeâ€”mostly to get great restaurant recommendations. :)

So, the reason I'm posting is because I've been having an ongoing noise issue with Escape Penthouse (formerly Sunset Lounge) on Steinway St. It's a club that's located on the top floor of an office building. I live on 38th St. and every weekend for more than a year, they blast their music so loud that I can feel the thumping bass and hear the music in my apartment from 10:00pm - 4:00am. I have tried nearly everything I can think of to end the problem, but it still persists. Here's what I've done:
  • Call 311 every single time. Most of the time, the police ignore it. Sometimes, they even say "there was no evidence of a violation" while I can hear and feel the noise myself. So they're not really doing anything or even necessarily responding to the complaints. Once in a blue moon they will say that they took actions to resolve the situation.
  • Talked to Community Affairs at the Astoria police department. They haven't done anything.
  • Emailed the business owner directly, while CC'ing the Community Affairs detective. The business owner doesn't bother to rectify the issue.
  • Gone to the Police Community meetings. I plan to go to the next one and complain again.
I know this isn't just my problem as there are a lot of neighbors who complain as well. Since January of this year, there have been over 300 separate noise complaints. So it's a legitimate community issue. Still, nothing has been resolved.

So, my question is, is there anything else that you can recommend? I really don't know what else to do. Also, if any of you live in that area and are having problems, please let me know. I'd like to get together a large group to complain at the next Police Community meeting.

Thanks,

Neil
