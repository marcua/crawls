At long last, my passport is expiring.  Since my life's goal was to get as many stamps in it as possible, I am hesitant to part with it because it's represents 10 years of traveling abroad.  I'm sentimental.  Does anyone know if The Man returns your old passport when you apply for a new one?  The website vaguely says they do but I want to hear from someone: "Yes, when I sent in my expired passport to get a new one, both came back in the mail." 

And if they don't, would be easier to just say I lost it and apply for a new one altogether?  Does that take much longer?  Is that legal?  It is much more of a hassle? 

I've already been to the website but for some reason, I don't trust it.

The new passports are stiffer and VERY patriotic.
