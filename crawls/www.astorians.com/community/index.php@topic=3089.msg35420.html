I have little to no computer skills.  I would insert pics of my tattoos...however, you can see some on my pics page of myspace:  www.myspace.com/leahberman

I started getting my ink in my 20's.  I used to be into piercing but went attended some tattoo conventions (to get pierced) .  When I went to one convention in Orlando (or somewhere north of S. Florida)  to visit a friend who happened to be sharing a booth with an amazing artist, Frank Lee, I finally decided to get my first one -- a snake and dagger on my right upper arm.  After that, I got my second (a Coop Devil on my lower back), the day I graduated college.  Then I got hired at the tattoo shop Frank Lee was working at in S. Florida and got a free swallow tattoo on my ribcage by Dave Poole.  Next, when I moved to Denver, I got a quarter sleeve on my left arm containing all luck symbols (by Brad Lee and Jef Kopp).  Then, when I moved to Jersey and hated it and everyone, I got my "Love Thy Neighbor" tattoo on my inner right arm.  Lastly, years ago, I got my Oni Mask at the NYC Tattoo Convention by Jef Kopp.  Jef got asked to work in Japan after completing it (the artists watched him tattoo me and saw the completed piece).
