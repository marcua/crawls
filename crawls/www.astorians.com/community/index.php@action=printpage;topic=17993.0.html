Title: Free Trial Class - Chinese Language through Theatre, Storytelling and Movement
Post by: strangecandy1 on April 20, 2010, 03:40:51 PM
Hi Everyone.  I've organized a Chinese language class for kids 3+ on Saturday mornings.  We use songs, storytelling, theatre and art projects to introduce Chinese language.  We have a Chinese actor performing and interacting with the kids at every class.  If you'd like to come take try a class for free, you can send an email to info@funwithchinesecharacters.com.  More info is available at www.funwithchinesecharacters.com.  (fun with chinese characters) Younger siblings are welcome to tag along.  The class is at the Community Church/82nd Street Academics at the corner of 35th and 82nd in Jackson Heights.  We are in the middle of Spring semester.  Summer starts June 12th.
