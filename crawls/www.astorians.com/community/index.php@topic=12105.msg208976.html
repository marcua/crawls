Quote from: Valentin Mosnegutu on January 23, 2011, 04:04:27 PM
and is it true that the less you take your dogs for walks or just let it around your back yard that's its more likely for them to get arthritis ?
No. There are actually over 120 forms of Arthritis, and exercise has nothing to do with it. The Primary causes in dogs are: ligament, tendon, or muscle disease; fractures involving the joint; developmental disorders; congenital disorders;  dietary and hormonal disease; metabolic disorders; cancer; degenerative joint disease; inflammatory joint disease and degenerative spinal joint disease. In fact in many types of Arthritis any form of exercise is contraindicated.

Quote from: Valentin Mosnegutu on January 23, 2011, 04:04:27 PM
and how can you make a nonsocial dog into a social one ?
There are two separate and equal socializations that a dog must be taught: human and canine. Both are very important to the well-being of the dog. It is somewhat more difficult to teach an older dog, but rehabilitating an antisocial dog can be done by a patient and loving pet owner. The proper time to socialize a dog is during the brain imprinting months of puppyhood.

If you have an unsocialized or an antisocial dog the time to start is NOW.
