Salade Nicoise is one of my favorite summer dishes--some heat is involved in boiling eggs and searing tuna, but both can be done in advance. Google it and you'll find lots of nice recipes. I often make a summer entree of a chicken breast, avocado and Belgian endive salad, served chilled or at room temperature.  The original recipe was an avocado and endive salad from the Barefoot Contessa, and I found that adding cubed grilled or poached chicken breast gives it enough heft to make it an entree.  I'd use 1/2 chicken breast, 1/2 avocado, and 1/2 head endive per person.
