Title: Crafty? Knitty? DYI-er? Let's get a group going!
Post by: jayme on November 07, 2004, 08:47:17 AM
It's an afternoon (or evening) in early December. The Christmas rush isn't upon us just yet but it looms ahead on the horizon.  You're knitting a scarf for your brother's ex-mother in law's nephen when...

You've just moved into a great new apartment, a little bit of a fixer upper but great non-theless.  You've got great ideas for faux-fresco walls but aren't quite sure what to do with the lingering remnants of 50 year old wallpaper all but superglued to the plaster underneath when...

You cook, you clean, you know all about Martha's tips and tricks, and you've just put the icing on your seven layer butter cream molten chocolate fudge cake.  The party favor packets are assembled next to a cleverly set table and you want nothing more than to show it off pre-party for some ooo's and aaa's when...

You've got to head out the door at head to the Freeze Peach where the group for clever crafty dyi-ers (and some knitters too) is getting together!  

We'll meet once a week to share tips, tricks, and new ideas.  We'll sympathize with your glue gun burns, give you pointers on how to pick up those dropped stitches, and do a little show-and-tell.

Interested?  Email me jayme@nurbn.com include please (if you'd like) project ideas.  I'll be sending around an annoucement as to the particulars to interested parties.
