The best place I know of is in Jackson Heights/Corona...details below.

GREEN FIELD +
(718) 672-5202; 108-01 Northern Boulevard, between 108th and 109th Streets; $$; no smoking; all major credit cards.


This enormous Brazilian churrascaria, which has seating for 400, specializes in grilled meats. There is no menu, only an all-you-can-eat buffet for $19.95, with a salad bar, more than 10 side dishes and a variety of soups.

The buffet's main dishes include pork tenderloin, short ribs and garlic chicken. Rice dishes, yucca and plenty of raw and slightly cooked vegetables are on the side. At the end, a simple piece of fruit from the salad bar is just right for dessert. ($25 and Under: 12/15/95)
