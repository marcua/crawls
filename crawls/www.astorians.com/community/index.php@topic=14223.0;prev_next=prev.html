I'm going to give Rite-Aid a try after work but i'd also like to have a backup plan.  I live around 30th Ave and 31st St.  Any suggestions for a mom and pop pharmacy in that area would be great.  I'd also take the walk up to ditmars or down to broadway if anyone had other suggestions.
