Title: Meeting about new charter school in Astoria
Post by: queensedu on February 01, 2011, 09:50:59 PM
Come for a community meeting about Academy of the City, a new charter school opening in Astoria in August 2011. The meeting will be held at the Long Island City branch of the public library, at 10-43 41 Avenue, on February 7th at 6pm. Learn about the school's learning model, admissions details, and meeting some of the school's founding board members.

For any questions, you can email the school directly at info@ownf.org.

Admissions lottery for the school take place in April 2011.
