Hi everybody,


It's not a scam. I am a grad student reporter from the CUNY Graduate School of Journalism and am just trying to use all the means available to me to get in touch with locals and folks from Astoria. In addition to posting on here, I attend community board meetings and speak with district leaders about issues that are important to those in your community and am only trying to lend an objective local voice on bigger topics, be it trash, bedbugs, smashed car windows, or transit issues (I am actually working on an article on the QM22 bus line right now).


I appreciate any feedback you guys give me, and of course if you don't want to give out a name I can honor that. But I can't guarantee you'll make it into my article.


If you have any additional queries you'd like to speak about personally, please let me know! I promise, I'm not trying to get money from you guys or scam anybody. Just a journalist trying to make it in the world :)
