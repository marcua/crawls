Back in 2009, when Top Tomato closed, there was a robust discussion on Astorians about what people would like to see open in that space. Speculation was that for 3,000 square feet, which I believe included the basement area, the rent was a whopping $25,000 per month.


That's an outrageous price, even now. Average commercial rents currently in the Ditmars area run about $65.00 per square foot per year. At $25,000, this works out to $100 per square foot per year, which was why I thought the space was empty all this time.


Anyway, good luck to what/who ever opens up a business in that space. It's been boarded up for far too long.
