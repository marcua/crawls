Title: Go from artist to creative entrepreneur!
Post by: QueensCouncilArts on January 06, 2014, 01:52:34 PM
Financial literacy is critical to managing one’s art.  Without it you can’t successfully budget for a theater production, tell a potential investor your funding needs or cover your personal bills. Learn how to prioritize needs over wants and appropriately manage cash flow with Tricia M. Taitt, a professional dancer and a certified Finance & Accounting consultant.
In this next edition of BYOB in Brief, part of QCA’s Build Your Own Business (http://queenscouncilarts.org/professional-development-workshops/) (BYOB) Workshop Series, Tricia teaches the fundamentals of personal finance, demystifies the art of budgeting and promotes good business management practices.
