French Bakery
« on: January 01, 2007, 05:04:24 PM »
Speaking of all the new places opening on B'way in the 30's...  My friend told me about a new (a few weeks ago) French bakery, Le Petite Prince, on B'way b/w 33rd/34th (I think) (north side).  (The sign is hard to see-- nothing in the window and it almost looks like a salon.)

I just had a chance to try it the other day and it was great.  I'm right near Frank's (on 36th) but find when I want a good crusty baguette they fall through (but their other bread is great!).  Le Petite Prince has real French bread-- closest to France I've found here.  Great Pain au Chocolat as well-- really flaky (though could use a tad more chocolate).  Their pastries look great, though a little pricey for the area, but I'm willing to try them...
