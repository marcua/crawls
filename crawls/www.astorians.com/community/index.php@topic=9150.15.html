So, after having an awful experience trying to put together a reasonably priced party at Indigo, I turned to one of our favorite neighborhood restaurants, Leng Thai.

We wanted a casual summer afternoon party in a garden/patio space with appetizers and drinks.  Leng Thai's owner and staff went all out for us.  Yesterday's party was perfect.  Everyone loved the food and were floored by their gorgeous patio space.  Oh, and for some reason I had no idea that they served pitchers of Asian Sangria. MMMMM...we had many, many pitchers of those.  So good!

Anyway, if someone is thinking of having a smallish party (under 30 ppl) I HIGHLY recommend having it at Leng Thai.
