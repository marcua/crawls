Hello Everyone!

OK...unfortunately I haven't had much luck finding willing participants for the interviews required by my classes so I've decided to loosen the purse strings.  I am willing to pay $30 for what should amount to about 2 hours (give or take) of your time.  This is what I need to do for this assignment:

1. Take a full developmental history of your child (questions like "When was his first word? When did he starting walking? etc.)

2. Do an interview to gather information about your child's adaptive behavior.  Adaptive behaviors are behaviors that help an individual to take care of him/herself and get along with others.  So there are questions about communication, daily living skills, socialization, and motor skills. 

3. Last I need about a 1/2 hour to speak with your child and conduct a primary interview.  Basically, I'll ask questions about interests, feelings towards school/teachers, friends, very routine stuff.

So if anyone would like a little extra cash and really help out a busy graduate student, call me or reply! I pay cash.  Also keep in mind that whatever you tell me is strictly confidential (you don't have to give me your last name or anything anyways). I'll keep it brief and professional and will really appreciate it!
