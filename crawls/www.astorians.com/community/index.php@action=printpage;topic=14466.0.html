Post by: NYCGoalie on April 07, 2009, 09:56:02 AM
The roller hockey rink is still in use.

The local YMCA runs a league out of the rink in the summertime.  The season hasn't started yet, which is why you don't see any games there.  The games are usually on the mornings during the weekends. 

Most of the guys who play in that league are still playing ice hockey in the new L.I.C. Ice Pavillion Rink.

Since I don't live close to that rink anymore, I don't play there that much.  Although I'll probably start too since my daughter loves the adjacent playground.  During the summer, I usually spend my weekend mornings skating in the park next to the Astoria Blvd/Hoyt Avenue train station.  The floor is pretty smooth and is easy to get to and find parking. 

I see a lot of people around there playing pick-up there lately.  I even saw one guy there put on full goalie gear!!  (I wouldn't do it though).
