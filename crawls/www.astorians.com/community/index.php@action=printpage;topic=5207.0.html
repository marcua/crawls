Title: Opera Appreciation
Post by: enigmacat on February 14, 2007, 10:51:48 AM
I didn't like opera at first, but when I was in college I thought maybe I just hadn't given it a chance and needed to learn more about it. I spent a lot of one summer listening to or watching various operas in the university library, with 2 or 3 librettos. And by gum, I started to like it.

I sang some opera with my voice teacher, did a paper on Shostakovich's Lady Macbeth of the Mtsensk District for music history, did an internship with an opera company and worked there after graduating.

Some of the singers now at City Opera and the Met I have personally driven to rehearsals, taken to the doctor, and assembled gift bags for :)
