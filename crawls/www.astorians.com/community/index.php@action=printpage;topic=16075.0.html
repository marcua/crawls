There's definitely no Sri Lankan food in Astoria. You can get good South Indian food in Jackson Heights or Curry Hill, but for real Sri Lankan, I believe Staten Island is the place to go. I've never been, but you might poke around on Chowhound for suggestions.

Of course, from Astoria, it takes almost as long to get to Staten Island via public transportation as it would take to fly to Sri Lanka. :(

Title: Re: Sri Lankan Food?
Post by: round2 on September 06, 2009, 11:28:18 AM
I came across a restaurant in Flushing yesterday that offers "Authentic Sri-Lankan & South Indian Cuisine," called Bownie Restaurant at 143-05 45th Ave near Bowne Street: (718) 463-8621.  Their hours are described as 10am - 10pm on their take-out menu.

Bownie is across the street from Southern Spice, where I had a tasty lunch yesterday, and around the corner from the renowned Ganesh Temple Canteen, and the Dosa Hutt.
