Okay - so let's get a clear idea of how many of you out there would be willing to attend a meeting focused specifically on the issues regarding Astoria Park and Shore Blvd, namely Safety, Trash and Noise?  No need to pontificate at this point, I think we have enough discussions out there regarding these topics and we have all expressed our frustrations.

We would invite our Councilman, local precinct representative, someone from parks and if we can pull it off, someone from sanitation.

So - when do you think it would be a good time and would you attend?
