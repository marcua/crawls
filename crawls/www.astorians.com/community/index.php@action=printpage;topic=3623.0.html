Title: Re: Anyone know a good cure for Laryngitis?
Post by: mc on October 01, 2006, 09:42:03 AM
I had a nasty case of laryngitis a few years ago.  My doctor told me to take plenty of fluids - lemon and honey in hot water really helped.  I also got a humidifier, which made a surprisingly big difference.  Aside from that, definitely rest your voice - if you must speak, talk in a normal tone of voice because whispering strains your voice even more.

Get well soon!
