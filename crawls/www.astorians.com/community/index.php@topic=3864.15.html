I use Adorama on a regular basis.  I find it's best to buy in bulk from them, as the print prices go down when you order 25 or more of any size (can be different images).  Also, their shipping is a flat price of 4.95 for 1 print or 100.  One thing I really like, is if you live (not most of us) or work in Manhattan, they will deliver the prints to your home/office same day.  Just get your order in by 10AM (or the night before) and you will have your prints by 5ish - all for the same 4.95 shipping.  I do this all the time.
