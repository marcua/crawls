Title: Re: What are the Laws concerning skateboarding?
Post by: barrister15 on January 29, 2007, 08:10:48 PM
Title 19 of NYC's Administrative Code prohibits only the "reckless operation" of roller skates, in-line skates and skateboards on public streets. By "reckless operation" they mean skating on a public street, highway or sidewalk in a way that endangers the safety or property of another. It's a violation of the Vehicle and Traffic Law -- they could get a ticket, but that's pretty much it.
