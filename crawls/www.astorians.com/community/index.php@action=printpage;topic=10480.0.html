Somebody asked a question about a fencing club in Astoria, but it got eaten by the Great Backward Server Glitch... Here's what I said in response...

No, there isn't one. :( Here's the Metropolitan Division's list of clubs (http://www.metrodivision.com/clubs.html). The nearest one, in Sunnyside, is Seriously Not Good -- do not go there. Aside from that, everything decent is far away. New Amsterdam is great, but they're over by Columbia. Fencers Club is world-class, but expensive, and in Chelsea. Brooklyn Fencing Center is in Cobble Hill. Manhattan Fencing is near Times Square, and seems to have good coaches, but I (regretfully) stopped fencing before they opened, so I don't know much about them...

You would think in a neighborhood with as many Europeans as Astoria that we'd have a club here... Wanna start one? :)
