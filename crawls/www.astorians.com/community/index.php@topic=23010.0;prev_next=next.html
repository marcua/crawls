Back in the old days, crying babies were just another "sound of the city" in the neighborhood, especially in the summer with the windows opened. Back then, everyone knew who the family was and who the baby was. Families congregated on the stoops and sidewalks and often just dropped each others kids off for a few hours of baby sitting here and there. I agree and understand you being upset about the child services call, and your building may be close-knit, but in this day and age, I would prefer children be more safe than sorry. The person who made the call I'm sure had the best intentions at heart. I know that newborns can be trying at times, but I would understand a quick visit and inspection, especially nowadays, could be life saving for an infant.
