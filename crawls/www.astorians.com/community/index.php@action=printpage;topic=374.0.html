What is Grouper?
Grouper is a new Windows based application that allows users to share their personal media within private groups. Grouper uses P2P technology to connect you directly to your friendsâ€™ hard drives where you can share large media files in a safe, encrypted environment. Grouper is a simple install and requires no firewall reconfiguration.

How does it work?
All interaction with your friends and family happens within your groups. Start your own group or accept an invitation to join a friends group. Once inside a group, you can stream music, download personal files, and IM/chat in a private setting with other group members.
