The Dole/Kemp sign has been there for so long - I think it's still a Republican club that has the space in that building, but they aren't very active.  The local Republicans had hopes of picking up the local Assembly seat when former Assemblymember Butler was facing primaries from Kimon Thermos every two years, but since Assemblymember Gianaris won the seat, they haven't really been fielding candidates, not really for anything.  I do wonder why they don't at least update the sign - they could have had Bush/Cheney up, though I don't think that would be very popular in Astoria (maybe that's the reason?)

I don't know the background of the window/frames shop owner, but there are a lot of white faces in his window.  I'm not sure that means anything though, he could be a person of color who just likes to have white faces in his window.  I've found the displays so tasteless over the years that I've never gone in the store.  I don't mind his pro-military stance (in fact, that I agree with) and his support for Bush is his own business, though I disagree with him (or her) there.  What I find offensive is his general nastiness to immigrants and the jingoism, all the "Love it or leave it" sloganeering really turns me off and some of the stuff he's had in the window was worse.  Considering how many immigrants live in Astoria and how hard they work to acclimatize and be productive, I just think he's being rather callous and mean-spirited.  He certainly has the right to say it, but I think the rest of us have the right to be offended and to refuse to patronize his store.
