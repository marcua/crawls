Flooz, glad you brought this up!

Yes, we can put in an order at Oscar's Adirondack Smoke House, a place near Lake George with amazing bacon and other smoked products.  Sliced bacon has a five pound minimum, but it comes in one pound sealed packages, so you could put in as little as a pound order (as long as our final number is a factor of 5).  A 5 pound package is $24.95, which comes out to $4.99/pound - the exact price of a pound of Boar's Head bacon at Key Food.

We can say - a week for people to put in their order?  Jim and I will order it (as we've done before), and then people can come pick it up when it comes.

Shipping, of course, goes down as the price goes up - if we put in an order of two 5-lb. units, shipping will be $5.00 each.  If enough people are interested and we get 5, shipping will be $2.89 each.  So we can't really figure out the full price until everyone's order is in.

And of course, you're not limited to bacon.  They've got lots of other smoked meats, as well as smoked cheeses, NY maple syrup (don't think that's smoked!), and mustard.

So to begin:

Flooz:  1 5-pound bacon
L&J:    2 5-pound bacon  (we have family members to buy for, too.  :-D)
