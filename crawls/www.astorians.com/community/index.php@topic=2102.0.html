There are still are a couple of places in Astoria with outdoor patios to have a drink with friends. Astoria has Cavo, Central, and Hell Gate Social to name a few. If you are not into the cheesy Euro-trash scene Hell Gate Social is the best by far.
 I went 2 Thursdays ago, after reading about it in the Village Voice. The place was kinda hard to find but once we found it  we were glad we came.  The female bartender was a hoot, and the freak show that was going on was totally fun. But the best part about it was the backyard. As you step out you are greeted by a grass bed, which the owners allow to sit on. If you don't want to stain your pants they have tables and chairs too. Plus the drinks are way cheap,my favorite was the Hell Gate Social Lemonade. I believe it was tequila, vodka, and lemonade after a couple of those i was feeling real good.
