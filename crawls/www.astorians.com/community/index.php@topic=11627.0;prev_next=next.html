Michael Pollan, author of "The Omnivore's Dilemma," this Friday at PS1
« on: August 06, 2008, 06:13:04 PM »
Michael Pollan, author of "The Omnivore's Dilemma" and "In Defense of Food" is going to be speaking this Friday at 7pm in front of PF1 (public farm one) at PS1.  The lecture is called "Taking the Plant's Point of View."  There's a suggested donation of $5.

I just found out about this talk because of an advert in Time Out.  Pollan's work is amazing--both informative and very well written.  The PS1 website says: "With this productive urban farm as a backdrop, Pollan will discuss the power of taking the plant's point of view, what that does for us, and why it is important to solving our environmental problems." (http://www.ps1.org/calendar/view/52/)

This should be an amazing talk.  Pollan is like 50s Elvis for CSA members!  :lol:
