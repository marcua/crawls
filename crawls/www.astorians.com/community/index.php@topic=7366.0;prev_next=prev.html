I have the same trouble with WFUV, and I'm a fan of Woody's Children from its WQXR days.  But my own favorites are:

Yahoo LaunchCast Radio, which is like Pandora except that in addition to member stations they have artist fan stations and their own preprogrammed genre stations.  My current favorite stations are the Buddy Holly and Paul Simon fan stations, but there are so many more.

http://www.abc.com.au/classic/  Classical radio from Australia.

http://www.wwuh.org/  They have an amazing classical music program in the early evening.

And any station on which I can get Fresh Air and All Things Considered with good sound quality.  Yes, ATC and WWUH Evening Classics air at the same time so I have to choose every day.
