Haven't been here in about 2 years.  Went for dinner last Thursday around 6:30pm.  We were the first ones in the restaurant area but the bar was pretty busy.  The bartender (Alice i think) came over and said "pick a table"...we sat, she gave us menus, cut some fresh bread & took our drink orders. We were 3 adults, 2 kids (11 and 15)

We took a look at the menu.  It's your basic Italian entrees with 3 appetizers (2 salads and shrimp cocktail)  The kids skipped apps but the adults wanted salad, she suggested getting a family style salad for the table which was a great.  I ordered the eggplant parm with a side of meatballs, the others had chicken parm and linguine with white clam sauce.  My eggplant was amazing. The others raved about their meals as well.  The salad was great too, classic garden salad with fresh mozzarella, balsamic & oil...nothing fancy but very fresh.  The whole vibe is very casual, red/white checkered table cloths, very laid back.  When our entrees arrived the restaurant area started filling up.  I did notice that a table across the way had ordered Fried Calamari which was not on the menu for appetizer.  Would have been nice to know if that was an option but no big deal, maybe next time.  :-)

Our waitress/bartender was great, friendly & energetic...a really nice lady. Overall we had a delicious inexpensive dinner.  I'll be going back very soon.
