Quote from: BigDogLover on June 19, 2009, 09:19:29 AM
Anyone have experience with cable cards?  From what I've read I can use cable cards in my Tivo to get HD service without a box...

That's what I have with RCN. You do have to have someone from RCN to come over & install the card (though I could have probably done it myself) and so they will charge a fee if you are already getting service with them. I don't know how they handle it with new customers - maybe they will waive it, I don't know.

I really like not having to deal with an extra box cluttering things up, it makes channel changing *much* easier and reception has been great. Every once in a while the Tivo restarts on me and I don't know why. I should probably look it up on the Tivo Community Forum: http://www.tivocommunity.com. If you don't know about that site, they have information on using the Tivo with just about any cable provider in the nation.

Good luck!
-Deborah
