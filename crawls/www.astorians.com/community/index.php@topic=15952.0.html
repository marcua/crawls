I know I said I'd post tomorrow, but I just got back from Sweet Afton and I'm so enamored with the place, I had to post right away!

This place has everything that I could possibly want in a bar. They have a small, but varied tap selection which is rounded out with a great selection of bottled beers and a cider, all for mostly $5/6 with a few more expensive selections (i.e. Chimay on tap - which they were out of or don't have quite yet - and Saison du Pont by bottle). They have a great cocktail list (which I didn't try, but everyone around me seemed blown away by) featuring house infused liquors and an amazing alchemist bartender and part owner, Mike.

I saw that they had dark rum and when I asked Mike about Ginger Beer, he immediately knew I was hoping for a Dark & Stormy. They don't have ginger beer yet, but this amazing bartender alchemist muttled some ginger and made me an amazing dark rum drink.

They also have Kelso beer on tap (a Brooklyn brewery) along with some tried-and-true favorites like Ommegang and the soon-to-be-Chimay tap. They will also soon have a cask beer.

The food is limited, but amazing. It comes from NYC people (basically all of it) and is phenomenal. I had the macaroni and cheese with bacon added in. It was sooooooo delicious, but it was a pretty large portion for a bar (and for the price) and I just couldn't manage to finish it.

The decor is wonderful and they have a garden they're putting the finishing touches on. I took some pictures I hope to post tomorrow.

All in all, it was a wonderfully friendly, open, great spot (thanks Mike and Greg for being amazing and gregarious bartenders) that I will be back at again and again!!
