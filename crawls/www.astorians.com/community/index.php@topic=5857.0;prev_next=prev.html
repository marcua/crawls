Korea or Iran).  Stopping the atrocities that go on in these places do not benefit us, cost too much, and cause whoever is in office to commit political suicide if American resources and lives were spent there, so why do it?

It's BS.  We either need to be world cops or get the hell out of everyone's business.  I honestly don't care which way, but we need to commit to it.  If we're going to save the world, back the hell out of the UN and do it the right way.  If we're going to leave everyone alone, stop meddling with countries we "think we can take" and flexing our military muscle.

I just don't want to be lied to anymore.  I'm strong enough to hear that things are dire, that we screwed up, that things must change.  That's the only thing that's going to make them change.  Farting roses and pandering to the public is only going to bring about more trouble.  Overextending ourselves and trying to take over the world, through force or friendship or democratic puppets, is only going to send us down the path of Greece, Rome, the Ottomans, Spain, the Dutch, the UK, the....hell, I could go on.
