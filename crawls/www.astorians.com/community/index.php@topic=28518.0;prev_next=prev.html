3br - $825 room in huge 3br apt on Steinway St
« on: October 22, 2014, 11:54:50 AM »
ABOUT THE APARTMENT: Large, 3 bedroom 2nd floor apartment in lively and trendy section of Astoria on Steinway Street. Enormous common area- living room and dining room with lots of sunlight and plenty of communal closet space. Kitchen is spacious with ample counter and cabinet space and storage, as well as door access directly to PRIVATE ROOF!! Hardwood floors throughout (aside from kitchen and bathroom, naturally), nice ~crown molding~ if you're into that (you will be once you see it), easy access to public transportation (plethora of bus stops nearby, as well as M/R trains (Steinway St) and N/Q train (30th Ave). [/size]Short walk to grocery stores, laundromats, bodegas, banks, bars, hookah lounges, delicious food- all of that. We have Apple TV all set up with Hulu and Netflix. ABOUT THE ROOM: The bedroom is at the front of the apartment, with a window facing the street. The room can be accessed either through a door facing the living room, OR through your Own Private Entrance that goes into the foyer so you can be as sneaky as you like when it comes to entering and exiting the apartment. There is one window, and while there is no closet in the room, there is a closet in the dining room that is all yours. It will also come furnished with a twin bed, which we can remove if you have your own bed that you'd prefer to use. ABOUT US: We are both females in our late 20s who have been roommates for 3 years. One of us is a photographer and creative type with a full time 9-5 job while the other works in the restaurant industry (where as you can imagine, the hours are more staggered and tend to be late night). There is also a very friendly 8 year old cat living here, so it would be ideal for our next roommate to not be allergic or have any particular aversion to cats. ABOUT YOU: Looking for someone who is an all around Decent Person in every sense of the word, is clean and responsible (just asking for standard contribution to the common areas as far as keeping it tidy and replenishing household items like toilet paper and trash bags, etc.) While we do have a cat, we can't accept any additional pets into the apartment. No late night loud partying so if you're a big drinker and tend to enjoy having all of your friends over late night fairly often, this is probably not a good match. We don't necessarily need to spend a lot of time together, but it would be nice for us to live with someone that we enjoy being around when it happens. TERMS: This is a 'sublet' technically so you wouldn't be on the lease- but our lease is currently signed through Oct 1 2015, with option for renewal if we so choose at that point. We would ideally like to find someone who plans to stay for the duration of the current lease, but if it needs to be a shorter amount of time we can discuss that as well. There are additional bills that are paid monthly- Time Warner Cable internet bill which amounts to about $16 per person, and ConEdison electricity bill which is typically around $30 a person. If you are interested- please send us a note with a little about yourself (where you're from, what you do, general interests, when you're looking to move, etc.) and we will be happy to send along photos of the room and apartment and answer any questions you might have. MOVE IN DATE: As early as Nov 1, no later than Dec 1. Thanks!![/color]
