Real reason why rent is too damn high.
« on: June 09, 2012, 10:16:06 PM »
I have an awful suspicion that rent is really increasing because of these damn real estate brokers. last few months a 2 bedroom apt on the Ditmars side of Astoria was averaging around $1700. I currently live in a 2 bedroom apt and pay $1600 in front of Astoria Park. Now all of a sudden the average has increased to around $2000, for shi**y apartments none-the-less.

I visited a few places this week and all of them either had either mold in the apartment or was broken down. Keep in mind that most real estate brokers tell landlords "I can rent this out for $1900... don't worry about it". Meanwhile the only thing the broker is looking out for is that extra months paycheck. They are trying the best to squeeze out as much as possible without any justification. I've literally heard the most dumbest logic come out of one brokers mouth about why I should rent his apartment quick. I had one douchebag named Kosta from Century 21 tell me "Oh you're looking to move in Aug 1st?... Oh that only leaves you with uhm... 10 days from now. You need to rent this quick or you'll miss it." WTF? My blood is boiling at this point because of his fake ass aggressive tactics. DOUCHEBAG, it's not even July yet... What a freaken moron this guy is. This is a good one too: "Barely anyone in Astoria allows small dogs in Apt's, so you're best bet is to rent this as soon as possible." Meanwhile I know for a fact that Astoria is one of the most dog friendliest neighborhoods in NYC. I've found plenty of places that contradict his bullshit. He was also saying how "You know next month I can rent this out for $2000 because it's August 1st." WTF? Ultimate douchebag logic right there.

Look, these douchebags only want that extra months paycheck. What's happening is called the sheep effect. One broker sees an other broker's agenda, the rest follow the trend. This is fucked up because the average Manhattan apt cost's around $3000 I believe. Not far off from Astoria prices.

These douchebags need a lesson in coming up with logical conclusions and justifying their aggressive pseudo brain wave patterns with common sense.
