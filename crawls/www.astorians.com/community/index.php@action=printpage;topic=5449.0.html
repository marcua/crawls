Glad to find out about this board.  I'm a painter who has lived in this neighborhood for a long time, and somehow have had a hard time linking up with other artists.  (maybe because I'm shy and lock myself in my studio??)  Anyway, I hope you'll say hi and introduce yourself!

I know at sixteen degrees the summer seems a LONG way off, but:
I have a great art studio on 34th St and 36th Ave that I will be subletting for five to six months starting in July as I've been awarded a residency out of the country.  It's 300 sq ft with freight elevator, separate common area, kitchenette, shower, etc. and a killer rooftop.  It is work only, which means no, unfortunately you can't live there.  $700/month - which is the true rent.

Thought I'd mention it now, in case anyone is thinking this far ahead with a "what if...?" scenario.  Maybe you and a friend (or two) have always wanted to do THAT PROJECT but just haven't had the space?  Maybe you want to commit to really focusing on your art this summer, but don't want to commit to a long lease?  I've held group figure drawing sessions here, had small shows, etc, etc.

Ok, so I'm sounding like a saleswoman.  I don't mean to - I would just love to find someone cool, reliable and local who could USE the space (there aren't that many artist studios for rent in Astoria) and not have to deal with a trail of broken craigslist appointments this time around.

Stay warm!!  :wink:
