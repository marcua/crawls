Ask and thou shalt receive.  Ok folks, I've decided to post this on the calendar for all to see.  Moment of madness I suppose.

Let's do this for the early birds among us.  11AM sharp.

One picnic at St. Michael's Cemetery made to order.  I will bring some chips and some sammich fixins.  I would like to make this a sharing event, so I will bring extra fixins for those who don't want to bring something, can't, can't afford etc.  Bring what you will, even if it is only yourself.  You can share a story with everyone even if you don't bring eats!

I do ask if you come that you pick up all the trash you bring plus one extra item of trash.  I feel it always makes a place better.  You'll feel better about it and if everyone did this just once every time visiting a public place, I'd like to think we would make a big dent in removal of litter through our collective efforts.
