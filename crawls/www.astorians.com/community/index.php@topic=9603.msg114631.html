I'm 43, my boyfriend is 42. When we stay around the neighborhood, we like
Brick for drinks on the weekends. The crowd there is pretty mixed, relaxed and
never any trouble. We also go to Indigo once in awhile after work and the Rover too -
great crowd there and they had a fun band last year on St. Patty's day.

We don't eat out a lot, but when we do we'll go to Mezo Mezo on
32nd St. & Ditmars, L'Incontro, 718, Grand Cafe (30th & 37th St.). Sunday afternoons
we like this Mexican place called Gonzalez y Gonzales on Broadway near Prince Street.
Decent food and drinks, good service and always very laid back on a Sunday.

I've never gone to the Beer Garden -  I think I've always had the impression that it
was really crowded in nice weather and we thought the crowd might be too young.
But we'll try it out one day. We also go to a lot of movies, either at the Kaufmann or
in the city, plus theatre and concerts.

We've been planning to try Mojave - maybe this weekend!
