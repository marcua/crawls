It's up!

Here is some quick info on the nod thing:

You can't "nod" on any post that is more than a week old. Also, you must be logged in to nod.

The number you see in the index (the %) represents the "popularity" of that thread based on all the posts for that particular thread. You can only nod once on any given post.
