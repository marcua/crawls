Quote from: jayme on May 03, 2007, 11:53:47 AM
NYCMac -- do you know anything about low cost spay/neuter clinics in queens?
To the best of my knowledge there aren't any. The only free one I know of is on Staten Island. I believe, that 2 or 3 of the larger Manhattan hospitals do offer low cost spay.neuter, but it usualy is done on a sliding (income/assets) scale. I personally would not look for inexpensive. I would look for good and less expensive. The new formula for spay/neuter is 2 months or 2 pounds, whichever comes first. My vet of 30+ years refused to spay my Affen because she was only 2.3 pounds and it would require a microscopic surgical procedure. He referred me to a Manhattan vet who wanted $850 to do it. I found out that he donated his service at the ASPCA Hospital 1 day a week, arranged to have her done there, and the same $850 vet did her for $345. I would only have wished he would have done one of *my* surgeries. There is no evidence of a scar.

Good luck on finding someone you trust. Be sure to build a relationship with your vet. It always pays off in the end.
