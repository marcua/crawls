In terms of clean up, if this is a public area I would call 311 and make a complaint. They will also be able to provide you with a graffiti removal form which can be given to the owner of the building. The removal is free, but only if the owner signs the form. If it is a private area, I am not sure what calling 311 will do but it doesn't hurt to call.
