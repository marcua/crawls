My girlfriend went yesterday, I opted out.  There was a line outside, but apparently they kept everything moving well.  I expect them to be quite busy every weekend, its best to go on a weekday before people get off work if possible.

I used to shop at TJs often when I lived in San Francisco.  For those who haven't been to one, rather than staples its best for things like frozen prepared foods (the frozen chicken masala and thai curry dinners are very good for a quick lunch at work, and the frozen enchiladas are good too), good cheap vacuum packed frozen fish, and snacky things .  The produce is usually hit or miss.  The store I went to in San Francisco was nearly half wines and liquor, but I'd expect that to be different here because of the different laws.

--bob
