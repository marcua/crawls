perhaps one of you has picked it up and are just waiting until I am home to call me and drop it off.. well, I am home!   Call away.

If anyone happens to see it, please contact me.

Thank you.

thanks all.

my monkey wallet is blue.  one big julius.  zippered all around to close it.

here's the whole story

yes, this restores... no it supports my view of the integrity of my great neighborhood here in NYC.


Apparently it was a 15 year old kid who was afraid to be at the station since he thought the cops might think he stole the wallet in the first place. He happened to get a wonderful cop who came into the lobby to talk to him and said well hey, does she live near you? The kid said he hadn't even opened the wallet.. just walked it into the station. The cop opened the wallet, read my address from my license.. the kid says oh - that's 3 blocks from my home... and the cop.. hands the kid back my wallet and says, well, why not bring it to her....kid walks all the way to my apartment, rings buzzer (which is broken) and then walks wallet all the way back to the precinct. Cop decides to take down kids info in case of reward... then cop has to INVENTORY everything in wallet since it is now in official police possession. Decides to flip through all the junk in it.. comes across my CM business card and calls me.


Is this Jenny L...? Jenny L.... the "consultant".... and that's when I know that whomever is calling me has my wallet in their hand and I start to do the happy dance....

He asks my permission to not inventory everything if I promise to come in within the hour. He tells me there is only a $20 bill in it. (If there had been more then the kid would have put money IN!!!) I rushed over and retrieved it.

I am so happy to have everything back. Have to wait for the new credit cards and bank card to arrive but hey...

thanks again to all of you for your good wishes.

AND YES!!!!   I have sent the $20 to the young man.
