Unfortunately that situation there has been out of control for years.  I used to live right on the corner of Ditmars and Crescent, and got to see this up close for several years.  I would often see fights start in the middle of the street, with big crowds forming a circle around those who were fighting, egging them on.  The trash, the noise, going into people's yards, sitting on people's cars, it was all a mess.  But perhaps the memory that stands out to me the most was one morning, when I was in the deli that's on one of the corners of Ditmars and Crescent.  A student from St. John's Prep comes in with a friend of his, and one of them grabs an Entenmann's cake and walks out, with his friend following him.  The deli owner was behind the counter and told them to stop, that they hadn't paid for it.  The second guy turned around, said "yes he did" and both left the store.

We complained a number of times to the principal of the school, but ultimately, the nasty and rude behavior never stopped.
