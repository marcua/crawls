Title: upcoming Art Show--Arte en Fuego!
Post by: J on November 26, 2007, 12:32:32 AM
Feel the fire and get in the groove at Arte en Fuego! This exciting event will feature artwork by emerging artists from around New York and smooth grooves by some of the city's hottest DJs at Cafe Fuego in Manhattan!
 
You can see some of my pieces on display and then get your groove on to the sounds of  Sambarella (voted best Latin DJ by the New York Post in 2005) and the Gruv Gallery. It all takes place at Cafe Fuego on Wednesday, December 5th at 7:30 until....you get tired of dancing! See you there!
