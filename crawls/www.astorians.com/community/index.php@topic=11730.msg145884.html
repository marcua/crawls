Beautiful Women, Lonely Men (Salon/ Broadsheet)

Quote
studies show that the more men see extraordinarily beautiful women, the less attracted they become to the ordinary ones who might actually date them.

Hmm. You men who look at a lot of really beautiful women, do you find this to be true? And women, does seeing a lot of really handsome men raise your standards unrealistically?

Here's the article that the broadsheet post is in response to:

Why I Hate Beauty

Quote
There's an extraordinarily high concentration of gorgeous females in Los Angeles .... As the head of a public relations agency, I work with these women day and night. You might expect that to make me feel good, as we normally like being around attractive people. But my exposure to extreme beauty is ruining my capacity to love the ordinarily beautiful women of the real world, women who are more likely to meet my needs for deep connection and partnership of the soul.
