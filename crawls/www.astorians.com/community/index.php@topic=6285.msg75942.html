2007 Benefit - 2nd Annual Taste of LIC
Wednesday June 6, 2007 6-9PM

Buy now and save a bit of dough! Individual tickets $35 (will be $40 after June 1); "Bring a Friend" tickets $60 (will be $75 after June 1).

Join us to raise funds to support year round theater, dance, music and visual arts programming at The Chocolate Factory and to celebrate Long Island City's best restaurants, bars, shops, spas and more!

Food from all of the best LIC restaurants. Great beer, wine and soft drinks. Great raffle and auction prizes courtesy of Alcone Cosmetics, City Dog Lounge, Dance Theater Workshop, Signature Theater, Dynamic Outdoors, Kika Hair Design, M&J Florist, Patrick Neal, Skov Silk, Spokesman Cycles Bike Shop, Subdivision, Vernon Wine and Liquor, warrug.com and many many more! All this plus performance highlights from our 2007 Season artists.
