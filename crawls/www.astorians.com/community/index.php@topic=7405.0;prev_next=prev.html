I used to be a hardcore regular at the Beer Garden and they basically just leave the garden open year round. As in, if you want to go out and have a smoke, go ahead. If it's 30 below and you want to sit outside, go ahead. They also put up a tent and heat warmers in recent years to extend the use of the garden. They sort of play it by ear there. As long as its warm and there is a crowd, the garden will remain open with staff.
