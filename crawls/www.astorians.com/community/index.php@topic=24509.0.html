Two family houses and water bills

Hello Homeowners:

My house does not have a water meter. As a result, I am paying a 100% fine on top of a frontage bill of $1500 per year. I have read the horror stories about huge bills from the new meters (and tenants not reporting running toilets which is something I have experienced) and I am struggling with the question of whether I am better off paying frontage or getting a meter installed.

I realize it is anyone's guess, but I am not sure how realistic NYC's estimate of annual water bills of $1200 for a two family house is? There are no dishwashers, washing machines, jacuzzis or swimming pools here, but I do take baths and have a modest garden.

How much are you paying for water bills ?

Thanks.

Our experience is about the same - two family house, including 1 dishwasher & 1 washing machine, and charges of about $150 per quarter. One nice thing about the remote meter is that you can check an hour-by-hour report of your water usage online to make sure that it's accurate. We can see that our usage goes down to zero overnight, so I feel comfortable that there are no undiscovered leaks, etc.


Seems like a meter is probably in the best interest of the homeowner!
