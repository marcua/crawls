Title: A BLUES OF THANKS!!!!
Post by: brownbird on March 06, 2005, 11:11:04 PM
Thank You to those of you that came out to freezepeach to join in the hollering revelry of my brownbird blues. Whether trampling percussively on a crowded subway platform or erratically exercising personal demons in the corner of my dimlit apartment; I have mainly existed in the howling world of an existential blues existence; detached from the “business-side” of music, embracing the practice, ideas, and regressive rites of passage that is the music I have chosen (or has chosen me).

With that said, it is refreshing to be among friends, away from the perfunctory performance pressures of “gigs” and “shmoozing”. It is a rare gift to find warmth the in embrace of obscurity, to share this thing I do for the sake of the music with all of you and have a friendly receptive audience. I had a blast, a night of music that was just that: music. Passion in infancy untouched and unharmed. Thank you.

A Special thanks to Matt & Meesh for creating the conduit.

Brownbird
