So I went bowling at Maric Lanes yesterday.  It's 4.75 per game per person on the weekends, and 2.50 for shoes (I think?). Much cheaper than Bowlmor in Manhattan.  (It's a hike in the boondocks of suburban Queens.  You should see these homes!  Wow.  I want to move.)  The place is so retro, and definitely has not been renovated since before I was born.   The balls kept getting stuck behind the pins and piling up, and the attendant had to fetch them out.  If you have your own ball, bring it, there are a lot of bad balls to choose from :)  That being said, we had a great time, and really enjoyed ourselves.  I think they have leagues all week, so I have no idea when it's not busy.  You might want to call and find out.  We waited about 20 minutes for a lane because some bowling masters were practicing with two lanes to themselves each, and there was a birthday reserved as well.  Still, this was better than the normal 2 hour wait at Bowlmor.
