Just thought I'd let you all know that we went to this on the opening day. It was really fascinating. I really enjoyed it. Most everyone who was there at the same time as us were very respectful - quite a few seemed to be med or biology students, and it looked like a few doctors/professors might have been there as well.

The most interesting things there to me was the room that focused on pregnancy, babies, and fetuses. Also the breast cancer was very interesting, as well as the specimen that showed the effects of obesity on the body.

It was really interesting to see all of these bodies up close, but to be perfectly honest, it really wasn't shocking at all for me. It is a pretty clean environment, so it didn't feel a whole lot like we were looking at real people, but rather, specimens. I do admit that I wondered a few times who the person was or what they did in life, etc., but overall it was not creepy or weird at all.

Anyway, if you have the chance to go it is well worth it.
