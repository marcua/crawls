Title: Out to Work 2008
Post by: mcdirk on September 08, 2008, 06:01:02 PM
Looking for work?  Know someone who is?  Come talk with LGBT friendly companies at Out to Work 2008, the second annual LGBT Career Fair, sponsored by the Greenwich Village - Chelsea Chamber of Commerce and the LGBT Center on Thursday, September 18, from 12 noon until 7 p.m.  The job fair will take place at the LGBT Center, 208 West 13th Street in Manhattan, easily accessible via subway.

This event is made possible by three outstanding corporate sponsors - HSBC, NYU and Balducci's.  Out to Work has four community co-sponsors, the National Gay and Lesbian Chamber of Commerce, the Empire State Pride Agenda, Out Professionals and the Human Rights Campaign.  Our media sponsors, who have been instrumental in helping spread the word about the event, include Next Magazine, The Villager, Gay City News and Chelsea Now, Metro, The Men Event, Out Impact.com and Bleu Magazine.

Admission is FREE and No pre-registration is needed, but resumes are required to attend.  Inquiries should be directed to Sean Oakley at sean@villagechelsea.com or Dirk McCall at dirk@villagechelsea.com.

Hope to see you on Thursday, September 18!

PS - if you know any companies seeking to target the LGBT community - have them contact us as well!  (this event is open to heterosexuals as well - just targeted to the LGBT community - so anyone looking for work should feel free to attend, no questions asked!)
