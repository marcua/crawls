I kinda feel about the beer garden like Mario Batali does about seared scallops.  Jeffrey Steingarten asked him why he would serve something as over-popular as seared scallops, and he replied that something truly good never goes in or out of style - it's always worth doing.  I love the beer garden because as popular and crowded as it is, it's the genuine thing -- it's not an ersatz 21st century replica.  It's the only remnant of what was once a huge part of NYC life, and it's ours.  And besides, you cannot beat that kielbasa!
