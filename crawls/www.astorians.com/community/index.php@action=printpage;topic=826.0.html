Title: rides
Post by: jennifer on September 29, 2005, 10:12:33 PM
take the bridge from 36th Ave to Roosevelt Island.  Circle the whole island -make sure you make it all the way to both ends - they're both beautiful and both very different.


also - there's a footbridge in the north east corner of Astoria that will take you to the waterfront park that connects the airport to flushing meadows.  once you hit flushing meadows theres lots of greenway to follow.

Pick up a free bike nyc map at either of the local bike shops.
