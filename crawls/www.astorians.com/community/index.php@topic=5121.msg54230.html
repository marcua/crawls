There are no loud speakers between Astoria Blvd and 39th Ave Stations. Never heard an annoncement in my life on any of those platforms.

It was important I be in at work on time by 9:00am Thursday for a meeting ,so I left home 7:50 to leave plenty of time for delays. After waiting almost 30 mins, I went down to ask the token booth clerk at 30th Ave what was wrong, a crownd had already gathered. She wasn't very articulate, but the trains weren't running, she didn't seem to know what to say to the passengers who needed directions. I knew there were only 2 obvious options; take the bus to Queensborough Plaza for an the 7 train, or walk to the Steinway Str Station for the R train (a 3rd option was to go home). I ended up walking from 30th Ave Station to the Steinway Station. Needless to say I was late for work and the meeting, arriving at 9:25am, getting dirty looks from management.

Friday morning there were more problems, no trains at all for over 20 mins, then several Ditmars bound trains start showing up back to back. The first train that arrived bypassed 30th Ave as usual, and the next train packed like sardines. I was late again today, third time this week. Once again they gave no reason for delays. Very frustrating to work in midtown and not be able to make it to work in under an hour.
