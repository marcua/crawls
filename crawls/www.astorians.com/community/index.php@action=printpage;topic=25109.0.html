Title: Rural Route Film Fest seeks volunteers for next month's fest!
Post by: ruralroute on June 24, 2013, 01:23:44 PM

Call for Vols!:
The 2013 Rural Route Film Festival is coming right up - right here in Astoria, July 27 & Aug. 1-4 at Museum of the Moving Image, Queens Library, Strand Smokehouse, and Brooklyn Grange Farm! We have an incredible line-up of international films, restored classics, live music, and food vendors, but are in definite need of a few more good volunteers!!


We've got about a month to go, and need help with promotions and help at the festival itself!I


If you love independent film & live in the NY/Queens area, but find yourself longing for that crisp mountain air, a faraway adventure, or peaceful cornfield (along w/top quality cinema), PLEASE REPLY TO: filmfest@ruralroutefilms.com with letter of interest.
