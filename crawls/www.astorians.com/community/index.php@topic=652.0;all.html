Bleu Drawes is Jamaican with a few other things thrown in.  For instance, this summer Steve's been offering a chilled soup; once it was a gazpacho with a whisper of scotch bonnet, and another time it was a cucumber-based soup, if I recall correctly.  Both were fantastic.

Some of the apps:  sweet/sour wings, Jamaican pattie (beef, chicken or callaloo), codfish cakes.

Some of the entrees:  jerk chicken, pork chop in brown sauce, cod and ackee (can't remember if it's an app or an entree), often a hanger steak as a special, and often, but not always, curried goat.  I called and confirmed that he'll have it tomorrow.
