Post by: anniecalzone on May 04, 2009, 07:10:21 AM
Most Precious Blood has a very reasonably priced day camp. It starts June 29th and goes through August 7th.
They do all sorts of fun summery things with the kids.
If you are thinking full time its $800 for 6 weeks (if paid by 6/26) for one camper. If you have more than one kid its a better price for 2 or more kids. They also have other price packages...
They also have a per day ($40) or weekly price ($180) and the hours are from 7:30 am to 6 pm. (you can take your child home earlier or drop off later.) 4 week session is $500 if paid by 6/26.
call 718 278 4081. There is a $25 registration fee.
