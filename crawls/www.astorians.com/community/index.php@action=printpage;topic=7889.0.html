Title: Dead Store Walking - CompUSA
Post by: Cannibal on September 13, 2007, 08:29:12 PM
Hey, when CompUSA spashes a three-day sale having begun today, you get the idea that they'd like customers to come by and actually buy something.  I had the urge to finish another WinBox, so I trudged over to the Columbus Square location to get the advertised Video Card.  And you'd think they'd have at least one item of the two different cards advertised (AGP or PCI-e).  Well, they have neither there.  So I trudge down to 37th and Fifth Avenue and they don't even acknowledge the item's existence.  To quote the snarly salesbuffoon "If you don't see it there, we don't have it."  There are so few items for sale in that sale, but the best answer this lummox can come up with is "don't see it, don't have it."

The other tell-tale sign of near-death was the almost vacant sales floor at the Columbus Sq. location.  I swear, there's so much open space that even with more of it occupied for large flat screens there's almost nothing being hawked there.   Even after closing half their stores you get the sense the other shoe will eventually drop.
