If the dog was having a fit (seizure) she would be either carrying the dog flat, on its back or on its side, or not be able to carry the dog at all, as it would be contorting, hard to carry, and likely to fall.  If she was just in a hurry to get somewhere she'd scoop up the dog, hold it on its feet with one hand and support its chest with the other or wrap her arm around it to prevent it from jumping.  Carrying the dog by its collar is torture. 


If a someone sees a person doing something like that, the easiest and fastest thing you can do is take a deep breath so you can project your voice very strongly and loudly yell "STOP TORTURING THAT DOG!".  It lets the person know right right away 1) they've been seen 2) their behavior is not socially acceptable and 3) alerts everyone within earshot that something is going on that they should be aware of.  More eyes and ears on the situation mean more people are able to help, and more likely to help.
