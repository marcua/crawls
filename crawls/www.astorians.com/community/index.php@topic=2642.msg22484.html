We've ordered from them a few times now and each time the food has been very good. We've mostly gotten the appetizers and the rolls, but we haven't been disappointed. The rolls have been very fresh and tasty. The lady that takes the order is very sweet and she always puts some Japanese candy into our bag, free of charge. (Free candy is always a plus!!) 

Is it the best Japanese I've ever had? Probably not, but it's definitely a welcome addition to the neighborhood. Now with this place and JJ's, I'm finally happy with the sushi options in the area.  :-)
