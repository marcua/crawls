Restaurant Bargains
« on: October 14, 2006, 03:54:47 AM »
Okay. So my insomnia struck again and I went surfing. I've had it with politics, medical information, Macintosh and blogs, so I started looking for a restaurant to go to when one of my kids comes to town.

You may already know about the place, but it was a surprise to me.

restaurant.com

You can search by location or type of food. I found a couple of really wonderful bargains in really nice restaurants.

Now here is *my* trick. I searched google for a discount code to further enhance the bargain and I found one that worked. I put two $25.00 dining certificates in my shopping cart, and when requested used the discount code 99923 and it worked. So, I am now the proud owner of two $25.00 dining certificates (that's 50 bucks, folks) and it only cost me $10.00

Now that's a nice deal.

Until further notice, the best code available is: TREATS. It is worth 80% off any gift certificate!

As soon as you pay for what it is in your cart, your gift certificates become printable.

When I am looking local, I search 5 miles from my zip code. When I am looking for a particular cuisine, I search 100 miles and narrow it down to the type of cuisine.

Play around, you'll be surprised how many restaurants you know and like(d) that you have to choose from. Like I said, I love the site. Except for some really fine-dining steakhouses, I've always found something to fit the occasion. For those fine-dining steakhouses, I use my credit card's free reward points for a redeemable dining gift card.
