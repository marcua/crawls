Title: New Moms Expo
Post by: sweetlilyg on April 08, 2011, 12:30:08 PM
Hello Astoria New Moms and Moms-to-be!

Queensmamas.com is hosting the first-ever New Mamas Expo on Sunday, May 22nd. Join us for an afternoon of learning, socializing, & shopping. Enjoy snacks and refreshments, mingle with other moms, win awesome prizes, and take home fabulous VIP goody bags! You can also bring your kids and let them hang out in our kiddie section hosted by Moon Soup . Experience the mamas expo and sign up now!


The Expo will feature:
• Breastfeeding tips from local lactation consultants

• Product demos of the newest in strollers and carriers

• Shop the hottest new baby clothing companies

• Samplings of new baby foods and treats

• Information on local mommy and me classes

• Tips for getting body back into shape

• On-site entertainment from MoonSoup to keep older siblings engaged

• The opportunity to speak one-on-one to representatives from top vendors and local businesses including Britax, iCandy, Stokke, Metro Minis, Magnificent Baby, Baby ChicNY, Petunia Baby, Zinnias and much more!

The first 200 moms to attend will receive a gift bag jam-packed with sample baby products, mama products, and discounts and special offers. The event will also feature a variety of raffles throughout the day.

Date: May 22, 2011

Time: 2:00 PM – 7:00 PM

Location: Forest Hills Jewish Center (106-06 Queens Blvd. Forest Hills, NY 11375) Enter on the side (on 69th Road).

Tickets: $15.00 at the door or can be purchased online at www.queensmamas.com/expo


A portion of all ticket sales will be donated to Baby Buggy, a non-profit organization dedicated to providing New York City’s families in need with essential equipment, clothing, and products for their infants and young children. To learn more visit babybuggy.org.

Hope you will join us!
