Powhatan Democratic Club October 4th Meeting with Queens DA Richard Brown
« on: September 26, 2011, 11:26:02 PM »
Our next meeting will be held on Tuesday October 4th beginning at 6:45pm in a special location while we work on having our building repaired.  We will be meeting this month at the Greater Astoria Historical Society 35-20 Broadway 4th Floor. 

Our guest speaker will be Queens District Attorney Richard Brown.  Judge Richard Brown has been working in public service for over 50 years.  He has been serving as our Queens District Attorney for 20 years.  In addition, we have invited NYPD officers from the 114th community affairs unit to report on events in Astoria.  Come hear these important speakers inform us about things happening in our community and ask them questions. 

In addition, representatives from Astoria for Obama will be giving us an update on what’s happening locally with the President’s re-election campaign. We also will be planning our activities for the fall which includes our upcoming Atlantic City day trip, our plans for renovation, our 3rd annual canned food drive/toy drive kicking off in November for the holidays and our holiday party.

We hope everyone will join us for our October 4th meeting at the special location Greater Astoria Historical Society 35-20 Broadway 4th Floor with guest speaker Queens DA Richard Brown. 

All are welcome. Refreshments will be served.
