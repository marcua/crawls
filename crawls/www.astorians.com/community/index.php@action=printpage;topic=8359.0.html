Title: Re: "Women's" Warehouse?
Post by: lanseaux on January 17, 2008, 03:40:46 PM
Back when I worked in NJ and had to go through Penn Station every day (and wore suits!), I was addicted to buying suits at Macy's on my way home from work.  They have an excellent selection of petites in all price ranges, and I averaged about $100/suit.

I have found generally that Kasper/ASL make really well-cut petite suits, so anywhere that has an extensive petites selection and carries them should work.  I have not had good luck finding suits at Bloomingdales, though.

Talbot's, when they have sales, is a good place to check as well.  The quality is excellent and they do a petite cut well, too.
