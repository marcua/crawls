Laundromats are self-serve in addition to having wash/dry/fold service.  So you could have them do the whites, and you could do the rest and dry it on low or permanent press.  Or, if you generally don't dry your stuff, you can have them wash it and then bring it home wet and hang it.  It requires a bit more thought on the timing to do it that way, but I've done it and it works.

Basically, you'd use it like you would laundry in the building, it's just half a block or a block away instead of in the basement.
