For what it’s worth, as a possible explanation, not an excuse, on why there are pricing problems at Key Food -- it may have to do with the fact that Key Food is a co-op, with individual stores or groups of stores owned by different companies.

Key Food merchandising may do the buying, but it’s the individual owners who set the prices. Different items may be on sale at different Key Foods depending on who owns the store.

Note, if you click on the link that round2 provided and then click ‘weekly circular’ you can see that each store has their own circular and though most stores have reductions on the same items each week, some do not. This is probably why the discrepancies are creeping in.

Regardless, obviously, there’s a problem that needs to be sorted out in the computer systems.

The Key Food on Ditmars and the one on 30th Avenue/Newtown are owned by Mandell Food Stores and a different response may be given if they are contacted directly:

info@mandell.com
