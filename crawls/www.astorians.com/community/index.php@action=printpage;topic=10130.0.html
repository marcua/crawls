Title: 5k run/walk (Asha for Education)
Post by: subanerjee on April 12, 2008, 10:17:59 PM
Asha for Education New York/New Jersey chapter is proud to announce its plans to host the fifth annual 5K Run/Walk on Saturday, May 3, 2008 at Riverside Park in New York City.  We would like to invite you to be part of this amazing event.  Mark your calendar and join our efforts to raise funds for the projects we support in India to help provide education to the underprivileged.

Register online:http://www.ashanet.org/nycnj/5k/

Visit our website http://www.ashanet.org/nycnj/5k to learn more about the event.

For further questions, contact:

Kiran Begari - kiran_begari@yahoo.com

Remya Thomas - thomasremya@gmail.com


Asha for Education is an all-volunteer worldwide secular non-profit organization that works to provide basic education to underprivileged children in India. With 66 chapters world-wide, Asha supports over 380 projects across India. The NYC/NJ chapter currently supports 18 projects in various parts of India and organizes several fundraising events in the area such as the 5K event which will directly benefit such projects. As a zero overhead organization, 100% of net proceeds go towards the education related projects for underprivileged children across India.
