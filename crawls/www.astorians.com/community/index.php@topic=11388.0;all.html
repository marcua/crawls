There was a TacoBell on 21st St, near Broadway.  It was part PizzaHut, part TacoBell, and had the best air conditioning in Astoria.  Hot day - it would be delightfully freezing in there.  AND, they had rootbeer, always a bonus in my book.  Unfortunately, almost every time I went in there would be little assholes from the new LIC high school hanging around starting fights with each other.  I say they were one of the main reasons the place closed, but in another discussion someone else said the high schoolers were one of the main reasons the place stayed in business.  

I think the biggest factor in the place closing was a lack of a traffic light at either 33rd Rd (where it should have been - corner of Queenview) or 33rd Ave - it's about a fifth of a mile between the existing traffic lights, and major parts of the local population (1/2 of Ravenswood, all of Queensview) would have to risk their lives crossing the heavy traffic of 21st Steet for a spur of the moment decision to eat there.  

Anyway, the site has been empty for a while now, and I would use that as a bargaining tool on rent if you're interested in opening a franchise.
