Jayme will attest to the fact that we make a good, clean slaw, nothing fancy, bit of a kick but not over the top.

2 Tbsp mayo
2 Tbsp distilled white vinegar
4 tsp Dijon mustard
sugar or sweetener to taste - start with 1 tsp
salt/pepper
1-2 scallions, finely chopped
4 cups shredded cabbage or slaw mix

Mix and enjoy.   :-D
