Hey y'all.  I'll be playing be performing with my quintet on May 27 at Detour.

349 East 13th Street, at 1st Ave, in the East Village
Take the L to First Ave.

There is a miniscule $5 cover charge, and 2 drink minimum, which is tiny considering the band I have for this show.

Josh Irving - saxophones
Alan Ferber - Trombone
George Dulin - Piano
Ike Sturm - Bass
Francisco Mela - Drums

For those of you who are familiar with Jazz, Francisco Mela is the drummer in Joe Lovano's band, so if you come down, you're in for a real treat.
