Title: Re: 30th Birthday party!!!
Post by: C-J on July 13, 2007, 11:25:14 AM
I did my birthday last year at the Beer Garden and had about 30 people there.  It turned out pretty well as we did it early on a Friday night (started getting there around 5:30) so we could get tables.  Then people could come and go as they pleased although some had to wait on the inevitable long line.  Plus there's plenty of food from the menu and the grill.

If you are looking for a smaller outdoor space I'd suggest talking to the people at Fatty's on Ditmars as they have an outdoor space.

Title: Re: 30th Birthday party!!!
Post by: daisy on July 13, 2007, 12:01:37 PM
Cavo probably wouldn't have a minimum charge per person, but the night would be expensive either way.  If it was a smaller group, I would say go for it, but with 20 people, that bill could get out of hand real fast.  I would recommend Beer Garden but cannot stress enough about having everyone there real early.  The line that forms on the weekends can be very long indeed.  Why not try Stini Yamas or Hell Gate Social? Both have outdoor areas and will allow you to order in food.  Very friendly staff and usually not very crowded.
