Zuzies is a great addition to the neighborhood, although it is difficult to find if you aren't paying close attention. It is part of Mix and the only Zuzies signage I spotted was something small in the window. I am not a big fan of the decor, but the food is outstanding. The lasagna salad is like a work of art.
