How To Find FreezePeach
-It is just off the southwest corner of Ditmars Blvd and 29th St.
-It is 1 storefront away from Ditmars Blvd. A nail salon is on the corner.
-There is no big sign over the door, just a discreet plaque.
-On sunny days there's a sandwich sign on the sidewalk with amusing chalked messages about the benefits of drinking tea.
-The cafe looks like a renovated garage, with big glass windows.
-If you are on heading south on 29th St and get to the alleyway or houses, you missed it.
