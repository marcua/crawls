Title: "Taste & Tour" spotlight on Astoria
Post by: LHT on July 20, 2006, 11:02:58 AM
See clip below for details.  Other LIC is also an option for visitors/tour groups to choose.


"Taste and Tour," a new campaign from NYC & Co., offers food-and-sightseeing itineraries for each of New York's five boroughs. With programs for Astoria, DUMBO (Down Under the Manhattan Bridge Overpass)/Brooklyn Heights, Riverdale and Staten Island, groups experience the diverse offerings of different neighborhoods. One itinerary suggests a visit to City Island, located off the Bronx's northeast shore, for food at Sammy's Fish Box restaurant, City Island Lobster House or the Crab Shanty, and sightseeing at Pelham Bay Park and Orchard Beach. For more information, call 877/NYC-TASTE; www.nycvisit.com/tasteandtour (http://www.nycvisit.com/tasteandtour).
