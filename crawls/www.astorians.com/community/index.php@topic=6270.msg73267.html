today i scouted out those places as promised and i found two possible venues near 30th avenue.

The first one is Park Kafe, on 30 avenue and 30 street and is right across the street from Athens Square. they have outdoor tables but the inside has a lot of loungy chairs and the space is nice and wide open. it wasn't crowded at the time and they were blaring any looud music and looked pretty relaxing. the outdoor section probably isn't as nice as the indoor section; it seems kind of cramped the sidewalk.

The second place that is pretty much across the street and is also across from Athens Square is Plaza Lounge. It has a better outdoor cafe scene and a better view of the plaza. It's also pretty roomy out and has a nice relaxing atmosphere. It looks pretty conducive to chilling out and talking. that's on 30th avenue and 29th street.

Let me know what you guys think and then we can set a date!
