Title: Update - GLAAD Media Awards
Post by: nycmermaid on March 05, 2007, 11:02:13 AM
Hi OUTAstorians,
My name is Rachel, a fellow Astorian and Director of Volunteer Management at GLAAD (the Gay and Lesbian Alliance Against Defamation). I wanted to let everyone know about the volunteer opportunities we have coming up at our New York Media Awards! We already have a few Astorians volunteering but I would love to see more!


The 18th Annual GLAAD Media Awards - New York City

The GLAAD Media Awards celebrate media projects and media professionals that fairly, accurately and inclusively portray the LGBT community. Held in four cities across the country (New York - March 26, Los Angeles - April 14, San Francisco – April 28, Miami - May 10), these celebrity packed events are planned and run by over 800 volunteers! Volunteer opportunities are numerous and exciting – signup now to hear more.

This year’s New York honorees include Patti LaBelle, Tom Ford, and Kate Clinton!
2006 Photos: http://www.glaad.org/events/gallery/2006/0306_NY_media_awards.php

How to Volunteer:
 RSVP and attend an assignment meeting, and then volunteer at the event! It's easy!


How to RSVP:
Please reply to this email or send a message to oleary@glaad.org. Please include your full name, contact phone number, name, email and phone of other volunteers attending with you.

The only awards show if it's kind, portions of the GLAAD Media Awards will be again be televised on MTV's LOGO. More then 5000 people are expected to attend our four ceremonies in NY, LA, SF and Miami.
