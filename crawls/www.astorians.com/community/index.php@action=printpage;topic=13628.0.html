Come as you are Mondays @ Bungalo   
Astoria's premier Gay-Les Party                                           

Satisfy your senses and quench your every thirst with us at Bungalo...

Feel: Cozy and at home with us. Experience Manhattan Quality at a Queen's Location. It's a place where you can shake it like no one is watching.

See: Astoria has such great diversity that there is surely something that will catch everyone's eye ;-)

Hear: Musical Selections by D.J. RandomSyn + B.T. LiLy.  They play the music that gets the party started and going strong all night long.

Lily and I have a bunch of LGBT friends who've been looking for a place in Astoria to hang out + party so we cooked up "Come as you are Mondays" especially since those who've come visit us at Bungalo have fallen in love with the place.

As the night grows we'll probably get a colorful Waiter/ress to provide table service and maybe add a few DJs to the roster to keep things fresh. We figured we start it off with just us 2 for now and scale as the demand grows (it was the only way to get the owner to agree since times are tough).
