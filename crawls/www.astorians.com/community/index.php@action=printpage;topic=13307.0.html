Pottery classes in Long Island City: BrickHouse Ceramic Arts Center. www.brickhouseny.com. For registration fee you get weeks of lessons. Environment is open, friendly, supportive - artists share their expertise with beginners. AND you get to make next year's Christmas presents now!
Good luck!
