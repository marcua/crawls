IANAL, but I don't think you'd have a case. I don't think they're liable for damage due to spikes or dropouts on a power line. They may be liable for not supplying electricity, which is why people can get food replaced, but I don't think they're liable for imperfections in the power. If you've got that kind of equipment, I definitely recommend a good UPS!

On the other hand, I don't think there were many spikes, just dropouts, so I suspect all your equipment will be just fine...! (IANA Electrical Engineer either, though, so don't blame me if I'm wrong!)
