Title: Holiday Kids' Bazaar In Astoria
Post by: kazukistan on October 04, 2012, 03:18:11 PM

We are looking for talented local artists, designers, crafters, and artisans who create items for children, for Holiday Kids’ Bazaar In Astoria, which also aims to raise fund for Queens Library.It will take place on December 1, Saturday, noon to 5pm, near Ditmars Blvd in Astoria, NY. We are looking for 25 fabulous artists who do wonderful hand-crafted items & edible goods for children. Each artist gets a 5’ long space on a table (or an equivalent space for rack, etc.) for $55, which includes a $10 tax-deductible donation to Queens Library.If you are interested in participating, please email us at store@babynoir.net . I will send you the details and the application form. We are careful to avoid overlapping of creators within a category, so it could become the first-come, first-serve type deal. I look forward to hearing from you.Thank you.
