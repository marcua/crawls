The area you want is around Broadway and 46th Street (46th Street - R train stop). I don't know exactly what the rules are around there, but I know at least some of the streets had no alternate side parking. I lived there for a couple years and liked the neighborhood a lot. But I didn't have a car. Also, uh, in the time I was living there, on two separate occasions, I saw a completely burnt up car along the stretch of 46th Street between 31st Ave and Broadway. I do not know if someone set these cars on fire or if they spontaneously combusted. Take that as you will.
