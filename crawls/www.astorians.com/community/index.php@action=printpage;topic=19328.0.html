Title: Re: Restaurants around 30th ave
Post by: Harlan on September 11, 2010, 05:15:44 PM
Vesta is a terrific choice. My favorite place for Greek is Philoxenia, on 34th Ave and 33rd St. Vesta is Italian-influenced American locavore, and might be slightly "Brooklyn" in feel. Philoxenia is a rather traditional family-run Greek menu, focused a little less on fish than some other neighborhood Greek spots, in an elegant but homey space.
