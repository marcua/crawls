I'm another Dr. Smith devotee. My dog is 13 and she's been caring for him for about 5 years now.
I used to go into 59th Street to the Humane Society, but getting a ride in to there became difficult and
it's much easier to get a friend to just go to Steinway.

Anyway, she's very good with him. My only complaint is their prices - I always ask for an estimate before
I do anything and get an itemized bill.

Incidentally, pet meds are a lot cheaper online. I get Rimadyl (arthritis drug) and Cosequin (a glucosamine supplment)
for my dog. At Dr. Smith's office, I watched a woman pay $80 for the same Cosequin strength and size that I pay
$49.95 for online. Unless the drug is being prescribed after surgery and time is of the essence, always check prices
online. Even w/delivery fees, you can still get the same drugs at much less. Your vet can give you a prescription
that you can e-mail or fax to the online pharmacy.

Good luck.
