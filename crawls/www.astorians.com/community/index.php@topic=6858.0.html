Yes there are overhead electrical cables here. The exploding manholes last year were feeder cable which are usually very large cables that carry many thousands of volts and feed, hence feeder, the lines that run overhead. Usually when you see overhead cables in the backs of homes, those only have telephone, cable wires and not electricity for safety reasons obviously, even though the poles and overhead lines are always grounded.( too technical to explain at this juncture) But if you should ever hear sizzling or popping sounds coming from an overhead, don't hesitate to call 311!
