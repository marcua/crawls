"Chris": The bearded homeless guy on 31 St, near the Post Office:Dead?
« on: August 14, 2012, 08:10:10 PM »
He is the guy who used to sleep/stay under the Hellgate RR viaduct on 31 street by the Ditmars Blvd. station. Long beard and graying hair. 40's to early 50's?? More recently near the Sovereign Bank/Parking lot short cut on the opposite side of the street. I just saw him over there last week but not in the last 2 days. There is some sort of ad hoc memorial over there now with a bouquet of flowers, a mellon, jar of peanut butter and a rather rambling note scrawled on cardboard about essentially "Here today, gone tomorrow" kinda thing, but no mention of name.
I was wondering if it was him. There is a rather gross pile of clothing, etc right at the corner of the Sovereign Bank and the shortcut, wet from runoff from the bridge, I believe belonged to him. He had a thick eastern european accent, possibly Greek. I gave him some flannel shirts a few years back, it was winter. He said: "These are YOUR possessions?" I said: "yes they are, and they're clean." He smiled and said: "Then I will take them!" Not one of those druggie/drunks from the Ditmars corner... he was afraid of them and stayed clear. I hope he's okay.
