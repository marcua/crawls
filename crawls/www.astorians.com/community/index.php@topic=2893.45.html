In my continuing, futile attempts to counter the negative hostility on this board, I would like to point out the positives of this development.   I feel that anyone browsing through the threads deserves a balanced picture of this place.  They have certainly heard plenty about the drawbacks.  Not everyone agrees the building is the hellhole most posters here make it out to be.   

Positives:

- Large square footage in apartments - much larger than anything else we looked at when shopping    around Astoria.

- Location, location, location:  Right on Astoria Park!

- 2/3 bedrooms have 2 bathrooms - and start at around $500k.  (I do agree that some of these units are overpriced for this neighborhood.  But not all.)

- Many have balconies

- Many have nice views - the better the view, the higher the $$ of course

- Bus stop directly in front of the building

- Doorman

- Exercise rooms

- Zoned for PS 122  - an excellent k-8 school with the middle school having a reputation as one of the best in the city

- Time Out NY named 19th street along the park one of the "top 50 blocks" to live on in the city.  The building is not on that exact block, but pretty darn close!

- Lots of good restaurants/shops within walking distance

- Nice neighbors:  we have met some of the others who have bought units in the building and everyone is very friendly, smart, nice, professionals - many have been in Astoria for a long time.   In our case, I am a television writer/producer and my husband is a chemical engineer.  We have been renting in Astoria for 7 years.
