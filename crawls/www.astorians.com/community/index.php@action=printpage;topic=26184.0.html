Title: NYC Doghouse
Post by: TJT on January 13, 2014, 08:08:08 AM
We had a really nice experience at this new place last Friday night.  The place was hopping.  Great wine, beer, whiskey, and bourbon selection.  The mussels were exceptional.  We all tried different dogs and wursts but everyone in our group loved what they ordered.  The servers were definitely a bit overwhelmed but they were doing the best they can and couldn't be more helpful and understanding.  A second bartender just for Fri and Sat night might help.  I highly recommend this place.  It was a good time and a really tasty meal.  http://www.newyorkdoghouse.com/ (http://www.newyorkdoghouse.com/)

We went this afternoon and had 2 very good dogs, the Far East and the Relish.  I liked the Far East dog (pork with garlic and ginger) better than the Relish one (a standard brat--tasty but not as novel as the num pang), but liked the Relish toppings better.

Groupon has several deals going on now, for either 2 or 4 people.  We just bought the one for 2 people: for $23, you get 2 of the special dogs, 1 order of fries, and 2 cocktails, beers, or glasses of wine, good Sunday-Thursday.  Considering that the dogs alone range from $7-9, and the cocktails are $10 each, it's a heck of a bargain.
