Title: Stonewall Chorale March 31st Concert
Post by: StephanieL on March 05, 2007, 05:19:28 PM
Just wanted to spread the word about our next concert.  The Stonewall Chorale is the oldest gay and lesbian chorus in the US, and we're celebrating our 30th anniversary this year.  Tickets can also be bought directly from me--PM me for details.

Announcing the Stonewall Chorale in concert with award-winning composer Meredith Monk and Vocal Ensemble, in the world premiere of the choral version of music from Monk’s recently re-released film “Book of Days”.  Also, Ralph Vaughan Williams’ "Dona Nobis Pacem", a timely, hard-hitting setting of Walt Whitman’s poetry on the futility of war, with piano, chamber orchestra and percussion.

Conducted by Cynthia Powell, with Kyung-A Yoo, piano and the Stonewall Chamber Orchestra

Sat., March 31st  at 8 p.m. at Merkin Hall, 67th St bet Broadway and Amsterdam.

Tickets are $30 in advance through our secure website, www.stonewallchorale.org or the Merkin box office http://www.kaufman-center.org/tc/0607/stonewall_033107.php or by calling 212-971-5813

Tickets at the door are $35.
