Title: Musical Theater Camp starts in 2 weeks
Post by: arthouse on July 12, 2010, 11:57:39 AM
Art House Astoria is conveniently located on Broadway between Crescent and 23rd st.  Call 347-738-4148 for more information or email info@arthouseastoria.org.

July 26-30 Musical Theater Camp,  Ages 12-18, 1pm-5:30pm, FEE $275,

Art House Astoria is excited to offer such an exciting camp! We have recruited professional performers, composers, coaches and more. In this week long camp, students will have the opportunity to hone their acting, singing and dancing skills as well as learn the art of stage make-up and audition techniques. Included in the fee of this camp there will be nightly concerts and master classes. We are particularly excited to have Andrew Byrne, one of New York's top vocal coaches, joining us for a master class!
