Adios Pathmark LIC
« on: June 01, 2012, 06:15:19 PM »
Copies of the Astoria Times May 31 issue bear the front page story "Pathmark in LIC may be closed." I'd include links to it but all pages of the story that google up have inoperative links).  So today I walk in and ask my checkout person what the story was.  He confirmed it's true, and the timeline would be two - three months.  Rebecca Henely's article states "But the lease for the building has either expired or is set to expire, which has led the union to consider that the store will be moving out."  As a chain Pathmark and the other A&P brands returned to television with ads saying how they've improved everything and we should all come on back.  Since they went into bankruptcy reorganization they've been very firm on which leases they would keep and which they'd move out of.

So, if we're about to double-dip into extended recessionary conditions I ask which of the known grocery chains would be tempted to take it over.  The store is fairly large, but doesn't qualify as a big-box store.
