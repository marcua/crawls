L'Artiste Closed :(
« on: December 28, 2012, 11:46:36 AM »
After trying to get a gift certificate for Xmas, I learned that L'Artiste has closed down.


This disappoints me greatly. This had become not only my favorite Astoria restaurant, but my favorite in the entire city, even beating out my favorite expensive seafood spots in the city. Their food, the service...always an amazing dining experience for a reasonable price and so close to home as well!


I really hope they open a new location or do something new. Does anyone have any info on why they closed?

They had a few issues:


1) Unfocused menu- first they were just French, then they wanted to integrate Moroccan food.
2) They had a social media presence that fizzled.
3) Small, cramped space. Servers would frequently bump my chair, and I wasn't far from the table.
4) French food is not popular in Astoria. Every French restaurant has closed in the past few years- Bîstro Boîte à Café, Bistro Les Minot, Pomme Cafe.
