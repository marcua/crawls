Title: Calling all multilingual Poets
Post by: Hardkore on October 27, 2009, 11:08:04 AM
Dear Poets,

I am working on a multi-lingual poetry slam for the Bowery Poetry Club in downtown Manhattan. The desire behind such an event is to give our highest respect and regard to languages that are outside the canon of Romance (latin-based) languages, and to shed light on languages that due to globalization/imperialism/modernization and simply the passage of time, are starting to disappear. So! If you are a poet who would like to participate in such an event by reading your own poetry/singing your own song in your native tongue, please let me know and we can work out the details of what will be expected. Each poet, of which we will have possibly 15+, will have about 5minutes of stage time, but it is more a celebration of everybody versus a singular spotlight. This is a great event for aspiring and mid-level aspiring poets because Bob Holman will be heavily involved. Thanks much!

Molly
