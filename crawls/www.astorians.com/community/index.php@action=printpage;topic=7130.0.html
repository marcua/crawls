Title: Brooklyn Matters - Important (and free) film
Post by: welling on July 17, 2007, 05:16:20 PM
The Long Island City Alliance would like to encourage one and all to check out the Queens premier of Brooklyn Matters, a documentary on the Atlantic Yards development plan threatening Brooklyn. It's free at the Quinn Building, 36th and B'way, 4th fl, at 6:30, Wednesday the 18th. We need to raise our awareness about this kind of invasive development, because it's happening here too!  You can go to www.astorialic.org for more info.
