I have seen so many but I can't remember them all...

A couple of slightly interesting stories, though.

When we lived in CT we commuted in on Metro North. One afternoon when we were on our way home a guy in our train car starts having a heart attack. Everybody is trying to figure out what is going on, and the conductor calls for an ambulence to meet us at the next stop. Anyway, I turn back around and look ahead of me, and one of the people standing up and gawking is Joe Pantoliano with his trademark hat and glasses. It took me a while to figure out who he was, but then it dawned on me that it was "the guy from the Matrix." Since then he's become a little more well-known, but at that time I only knew him as "that guy." I think I googled him later to find out his real name. :)

Also while we commuted on Metro North... When we switched to our local Metro North train I often saw an actor that I grew up watching on either Days of Our Lives or Another World. I finally figured out who he was and that he lived in CT, but it was so weird for me to see him on the train. Seemed like a nice guy.

Hmmm... I have seen others, but not as interesting as the first story. Saw Law and Order being filmed with Jerry Orbach and the black guy (can't remember his name) at the UN. I saw that guy (I know - a lot of that guy, that girl) from Hellboy (the lead) when I worked on 5th Ave. Saw Steve Martin filming the Pink Panther (I think) also on 5th Ave. I saw another actress on 5th Ave. who I remember from Meet Joe Black (she was the sister who wanted the father's attention). She is actually pretty well known, but I can't remember her name.

Others, too...but I won't try to remember them now.
