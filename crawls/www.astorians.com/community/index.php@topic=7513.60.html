Hillary! Some of your choices make me nostalgic! "Tangled Up in Blue" is my favorite song on Blood on the Tracks (Dylan's best album, I think). "Shelter From the Storm" is also a great track on it. Blue is a fantastic Joni Mitchell album. "All I Want" is another wonderful song from that album. Back in the early 70's I used to listen to my older brother's albums all the time. He grew up listening to the Beach Boys and I think he and his buddies were the only surfers in Astoria! We were always California Dreaming! I thought he was so cool- he had an Easy Rider poster on his bedroom wall, and did the whole cross-country trip thing in a VW bus and brought back cases of Coors beer, which the locals considered really exotic! Anyway...
Alittle OT: Any of you old-timers remember the little record store up Ditmars on 31st Street? I think it was called "Bobbi's Beat" It had a sparse collection, but once in a while I'd buy a 45 from them.
