MAGNIFICENT TOP FLOOR TWO BEDROOM AT THE GRISWOLD

The Griswold has a magnificent top floor two bedroom co-op unit located in The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights. This pre-war sunny unit features a formal dining room; large living room; huge master bedroom, with a second bedroom, which is comfortable in size; a renovated eat-in-kitchen and bathroom; original hardwood floors throughout; and southern and western exposures. This property has a lovely interior garden with a children's playground and a dog run. A bike room, storage room and laundry room is located in the basement for your convenience. The building allows subletting, has a live-in super and is close to all transportation. COME AND SEE THIS BEAUTY!
