One place that's nice for a drink if you're in the area is the new Hilton on 42nd between 7th & 8th Ave.
It's one or two doors down from the AMC movie theatre. They have a bar/lounge when you take the
elevator up to the main level. It might be a little pricey for drinks, but when my boyfriend and I first
started seeing each other, we used to go up there after a movie.

It's got a great view, comfortable and you can also get something to eat if the evening progresses well and
you want to linger a little.

Plus the location is good for mid-town.
