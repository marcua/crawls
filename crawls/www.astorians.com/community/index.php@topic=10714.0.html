This was sent to me today from someone in our accounting dept. Yes, this person is responsible for paying the bills, and communicating to accounts but writes like this all the time.

Jenn,
Please order the breakfast for the Friday Accounting meeting,
Delivered Time: 8:20 am June 6, 2008
List
Clean cheese
Jelly
A dozen of bagels(mix)
Napkins
butter
plastic knifes and plates
Orange juice
Contact person:
Numbnuts

Please let me know their phone number for safe bet

Thanks

I'd like to point out:
a) the meeting is for 5 people, can we say carb overload on the bagel request?
b) we have plates and knives and all that in house already
c) breakfast orders come with all the fixins (butter, jelly etc)
d) and finally i really resisted the urge to call him and say, as opposed to dirty cheese?

I can't read this kid's emails anymore-they make me cringe because of the spelling and grammar mistakes. There's your average common mistake, and then there is an email from numbnuts. Now, he is from another country (China) so I get that this isn't his first language, but come on! How can you be allowed to send emails like this? And he sends company wide ones like this too.
More grating than this is that he got promoted ahead of me (he's been here 1 year I've been here 5...his family knows the owners.). Granted I'm not in accounting but I can write correctly and people can understand me on the phone. Instead I get to suffer downsizing and i'm in a long term temporary situation at my old position-reception. You can't get any lower than that. And now, he has a hoity toity attitude that I'm stuck down here.

End result of all this is I will go bat s___ crazy on my boss in about a month.
