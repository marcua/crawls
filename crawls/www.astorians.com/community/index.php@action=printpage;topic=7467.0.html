Post by: NYCMacUser on August 10, 2007, 08:36:13 AM
One other thing that I feel the need to say. Tell her other friends that she is having 2 babies. No matter if they are same sex or opposite sex, fraternal or identical, they are 2 separate little human beings. Don't buy them matching outfits. Let them learn right from the start that they are individuals and hopefully their Mom and Dad will learn to treat them as such. My boys have such different tastes in everything from food to clothes because I worked at allowing them their individuality.
