Title: Astoria Symphony SEASON FINALE! The Triumph of Love - Saturday May 8th
Post by: AlexJL2009 on May 04, 2010, 01:58:01 PM
On Saturday May 8th, the Astoria Symphony presents an evening of music inspired by triumphant love in a performance of Tchaikovsky's "Romeo & Juliet Fantasy Overture" and Hanson's "Romantic Symphony." The Frank Sinatra School of the Arts Orchestra, under the direction of Ken Lieberson, will join the Astoria Symphony for the finale, including excerpts by Shostakovich, Bizet, and Stravinsky.

Saturday, May 8, 2010, 8:00 pm (7:30 lecture*)
Frank Sinatra School of the Arts
35-12 35th Avenue, Astoria, NY 11106 [map]

Program:
Tchaikovsky Swan Lake Suite
Howard Hanson's Symphony No. 2 ("Romantic")
Mahler Adagietto from Symphony No. 5
Bizet Habanera from Carmen
Shostakovich Finale from Symphony No. 5
Stravinsky Berceuse & Finale from Firebird
            Silas Huff , Grant Gilman, & Ken Lieberson,
                                conductors

The Astoria Symphony presents an evening of music inspired by triumphant love in a performance of Tchaikovsky's Swan Lake Suite, a perennial favorite by Mahler, and Hanson's "Romantic Symphony." The Frank Sinatra School of the Arts Orchestra, under the direction of Ken Lieberson, will join the Astoria Symphony for the finale with excerpts by Shostakovich, Bizet, and Stravinsky.

Tickets are $15-$20.  This concert is FREE for kids and FSSA students!
For tickets and information, please visit: http://www.astoriamusic.org/concert8.html

* Join Maestro Huff in the concert hall 30 minutes before the concert begins for an insightful glimpse into the evening's performance. These "Meet the Maestro" talks delve into the lives of the composers, the meaning of the music, and the techniques musicians use to bring music to life.
