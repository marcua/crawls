Hi all,
I needed my pre Christmas card haircut today (per my wife). I don't know if my fellow astorians know about this secret gem especially if you are in a rush. It's the barbershop at 42-05 Broadway. I was in and out quickly. I never have to wait long and and they aren't too busy to take a walk-in. Also the best part is I don't have to listen to blaring rap music while getting a cut or other patrons using foul language like the majority of shops have become here. This is definitely a family type shop where I take my kids too. The funny thing too if you go to Google Street view of the shop you can see him giving a haircut.
Raphael is nice and friendly and remembers your name. Just sharing my hidden Astoria find. Happy holidays everyone.
