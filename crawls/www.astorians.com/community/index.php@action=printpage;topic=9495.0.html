Title: Party at Irish Rover Tuesday Night!
Post by: ZogSports on February 15, 2008, 04:47:25 PM
Hey -

We’re throwing a PARTY at Irish Rover (the same place Astorians.com had the meet-up) on Tuesday, February 19! 

ZogSports Queens is hosting the party to get to know Astorians and help you get to know us and what we're all about.  We’ll have a ton of great people in attendance, drink specials, a raffle, and hopefully all of you there to party with us!

$50 OFF REGSITRATION!
Already know you want to join us for Dodgeball in Astoria or Touch Football in Long Island City in the spring?  Great!  Show up to the party with a check and we’ll give you $50 off your team registration (Early Registration fee)!  That means Dodgeball in Astoria is just $650 (down from Late Reg. price of $800) and Touch Football in Long Island City is $945 (down from Late Reg. price of $1,095).  Come with your checkbook to take advantage of the discount.

WIN A FREE INDIVIDUAL REGISTRATION!
Grab a raffle ticket when you walk in the door (Free!) and you just might win a free individual registration for either Queens league!  That means you could join Touch Football (a $105 fee) or Dodgeball (a $75 fee) for free!


• When: Tuesday, Feb. 19. 8:00pm-???
• Where: Irish Rover, 3718 28th Ave (@ 38th Street)
• What: A party to celebrate ZogSports Queens leagues (past, current, and future). Drink specials, raffle, $ off team registration!  No cover!
• Who: You!! And any friends you want to bring!

Questions? Email info@zogsports.org
