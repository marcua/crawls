2 Women Killed in Queens Livery Cab Accident
Last Edited: Sunday, 16 Nov 2008, 8:17 AM EST
Created: Sunday, 16 Nov 2008, 8:17 AM EST
http://www.myfoxny.com/myfox/pages/News/Detail?contentId=7875772&version=1&locale=EN-US&layoutCode=TSTY&pageId=3.2.1

NEW YORK  --  Two women are dead after riding in a livery cab that was struck by an SUV whose driver is under arrest for suspicion of drunken driving.

The accident was reported shortly after 3 a.m. in Queens' Astoria neighborhood.

Police say a white Range Rover driven by a 23-year-old man was going west on 31st Avenue when it collided with the cab going south on 34th Street.

The women were pronounced dead at Astoria General Hospital.
