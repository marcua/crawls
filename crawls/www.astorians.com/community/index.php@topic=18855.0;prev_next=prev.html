I went there about 3-4 times. 

Besides echoing the above sentiments (excellent tea, decent-ish food, bad service), the decor was a real issue for me.  Maybe this is just me, but my vision of a 'tea house' is a calm, relaxing place you'd want to hang out in.  HTH was quite cold, verging on unpleasant...not what you would expect at all, and not somewhere I ever felt like hanging out.  Admittedly they had an outside, but the only time I was there (tea not being a summer drink for me) the neighbors were having a LOUD party, and I was attacked by mosquitoes. 

So while I agree the business model was good, I think they should have seriously rethought the decor.  Maybe something like Rapture, with the couches but without the aggressive color scheme.
