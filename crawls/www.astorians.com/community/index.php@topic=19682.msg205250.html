I don't have much to add to this in regards to A-1 delivery service.

However:

Never use your debit card to shop online.  The protections that credit cards have are applicable to debit cards in a lot of cases.  In the case that someone does obtain your number, they could clean out the account the card was associated with leaving you with little recourse.

ONLY shop online with credit cards.  Never use a debit card.

A1 Courier Service is garbage... that is all!  Actually I have been complaining about this company to anyone that will hear me (including Amazon) for the last few years.  Anytime there is a Saturday delivery through Amazon, I cringe because I will most likely get stuck with A1 (not delivering my package).

I have gotten so many orders replaced through Amazon these last few years.  Not only for different orders but multiple replacements for one order!!  One time there was 2 blu rays that were re-shipped 3 times!  Yes... you read that right.  Horrible service and A1's customer service is the worst.  Someone nasty just answers and they don't have any info.  Amazon had informed me that as long as there are enough complaints on file then (maybe) they would consider dropping them.  I didn't hold my breath back then.

The best part is when you track your stuff and it says that it has arrived... it really hasn't.  The package mysteriously ends up in my mailbox at 10pm.  At this point after 7:30pm I had already spoken to Amazon about a replacement.  Amazon must be losing so much money in merchandise and my Prime shipping alone still dealing with this shady character.  I say character because it is truly a beat up white van (as confirmed) with a young kid in the passenger's side that drops runs to the mailbox and drops it off.

How do I know that?  I saw it last week with my own two eyes.  Luckily, the delivery was not for me but for someone 2 doors down.  The kid (maybe 11?) ran to the mailbox and dropped the Amazon box in.  I asked my husband who they were and he said, "Oh, that's A1 Steak sauce"...  I laughed and questioned if he was sure and he explained that, that is the guy who (if we are lucky) always drops off our packages.

Are you kidding me?  So... it is a tiny operation that does a horrible job.  YUCK to steak sauce.
