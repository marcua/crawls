Re: Italian Food at Hell's Gate Bar?
« Reply #4 on: July 02, 2008, 02:28:06 PM »
I think price is very fair for quality of food, but that's my personal opinion.  Entrees I think go up to like $15, maybe a bit higher for the most expensive items.  Spaghetti & meatballs are probably like $10 and those meatballs are amazing.  Again, don't expect a formal printed menu.  Basically the waitress or barkeep will walk you through what Emilio's making tonight, so be flexible. 

If you go in saying it's your first time there and how you heard great things about the food, they'll jump right in there with you and be friendly.  They're an older, very local crowd of good ol' boys.  Reach out & be friendly to them and they'll totally reciprocate.  Play a little sinatra or shirley bassy on the jukebox and you'll likely make yourself a friend for life.  Hover in a corner like a wallflower and you probably won't get a very warm reception. 

As for physical layout, bar is in front on the left with a few high-top tables on right.  Very casual dining area towards the back and a nice patio area outside in the backyard where you can eat too.  They'll serve food at all of the areas.
