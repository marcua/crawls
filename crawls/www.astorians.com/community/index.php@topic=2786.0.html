I had it in my head that the A train was how you get to JFK, until I Hopstopped it and realized just how mistaken I was.

Do this:  Go to Steinway (or the nearest R stop).  Take the train out to the express stop (Roosevelt?) and switch to the E.  Take the E to the Airtrain, and voila!  I think the Airtrain might have a $4 fee?  Still cheaper than a car.

When I took a taxi for my Christmas trip out of JFK, it was around $40.  I'd shoot for the E train next time.   :roll:

The AirTrain is $5. Yeah, I'd suggest meeting your friend at the shiny new Jamaica train station (so you don't have to pay AirTrain!). It takes a bit longer to get out there from Astoria than you might think, about 30 minutes from Queens Plaza. When I went to JFK on Monday, it took about 1:15 to go from Ditmars, to Queensboro, hiking 3 blocks to Queens Plaza, to Jamaica, to JFK.
