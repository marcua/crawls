Trinity Lutheran struck by lightning!
« on: August 13, 2006, 06:49:10 PM »
Did anybody hear about this?  During the thunderstorm on Thursday evening, lightning struck one of the steeples on Trinity Lutheran Church on 31st Avenue and caused a lot of damage to it.  Lots of pieces of concrete fell down, and the front part of the church is still cordoned off.  No one was hurt, and we had Sunday worship as usual this morning, but we had to enter and leave through the side door near the chancel.  The story was on Channel 4, and one of the parishioners videotaped the story and we all watched it after worship.

At the time, I was deep in the bowels of the building where I work, absent-mindedly surfing the Net.  I didn't hear a thing.  When I asked my boss the next day about the storm, and I told him about what happened to the church, he asked if anyone at the church had been doing bad things.  And yesterday a man looked from across the street and said to me, "The church is going down."  I said, "Lightning struck it."  He said to me real seriously, "That was the Devil."
