Re: New Feature Coming - Nods
« Reply #10 on: November 07, 2006, 02:55:14 PM »
I think it will feel different from the "endorse" or "impeach" feature because you're not voting for the poster, you're agreeing or disagreeing with the content.

It doesn't really serve any "purpose" in that it's goal is not "utility" but more just fun. The idea is inspired by some sites like digg that use this kind of method for readers to react to the content. This idea is content focused, not poster focused - that's the difference.

And yes, you'll be able to make a negative nod as well. Also, on the topic indexes you'll see the overall "nod rating" for an entire thread based on all the posters that participate there. So you'll not only be able to see how "popular" a thread is based on pageviews (the burning sticky note), but also get a feel for how users feel about the content of that thread overall.

Overall it's just for fun and a way to see what threads, and what content is of most interest to our community. It shouldn't be taken too personally or seriously.

The idea is to just see what happens and how people use it. There are a couple features built in to keep people from abusing it and just voting down any posts by a particular person as well. I'll discuss those more later once we get it working. But the point is to nod at content, not just engage in a popularity contest.
