Queens Culinary Bike Tour
« on: April 30, 2007, 10:00:16 PM »
Get a taste of Queens...literally!  Join us as we experience the flavors of Astoria,the most diverse county in the United States, and let your taste buds travel (by bike!) from Latin America to East Asia and everywhere in between.  Bring cash for purchases and a backpack or panniers for take-home treats.


Queens Culinary Bike Tour
Start: Queens side of the Queensboro Bridge
End: Bohemian Beer Garden, 24th Ave.
Saturday, May 19th
12:00pm - 3:00pm
